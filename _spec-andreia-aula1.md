## GRAMÁTICA-FOCO (1–2 estruturas) — Regra 2
1. **Present simple vs. Past simple** (fato/rotina agora × ação terminada no passado)
2. **Question formation** (Wh- para reuniões: What / Where / Who + do/are)

### Discovery (3–4 exemplos, destaque accent):
- "I **work** at Hospital Albert Einstein." (present — now)
- "I **studied** nursing for five years." (past — finished)
- "I **am transitioning** into robotic surgery." (change in progress)
Pergunta: "What do the highlighted words have in common?" → Reveal rule.

### Common Mistake (o gap EXATO da aluna — passado):
- ❌ "Yesterday I **work** with Medtronic."
- ✅ "Yesterday I **worked** with Medtronic."
(Conecta com a fala dela: "na hora de falar, eu falo tudo no presente… Eu não falo no passado.")

### Question formation:
- "What do you do?" / "Where are you based?" / "Who do you deal with?" / "What are you responsible for?"

## CCQs prontas (2–3) — escritas, não delegadas
1. "When I say 'I worked at the ICU', is that now or finished?" → finished/past.
2. "If I'm 'responsible for' the program, do I manage it or just watch it?" → manage it.
3. "Is a 'supplier' someone who buys from us or sells to us?" → sells to us.
