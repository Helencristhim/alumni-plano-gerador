## GRAMÁTICA: Present simple + adverbs of frequency
### Discovery (destaque accent nos advérbios):
- "I **always** check my schedule in the morning."
- "I **usually** coordinate the surgical team."
- "I **often** monitor the equipment."
- "I **never** skip the safety check."
Pergunta: "Where do the frequency words go?" → before the main verb (after 'to be').
Reveal: scale always(100%)→never(0%); order subject + adverb + verb; 'to be' → be + adverb; 3rd person + -s.

### Common Mistake:
- ❌ "I check always my schedule."
- ✅ "I always check my schedule."
(advérbio de frequência vem ANTES do verbo principal). Reforço 3ª pessoa: "She coordinate" → "She coordinates".

### Questions: "How often do you...?" / "What time do you start?"

## CCQs
1. "'I always check' — does 'always' go before or after the verb?" → before.
2. "'She coordinate' or 'She coordinates'?" → coordinates (3rd person -s).
3. "Always = 100%. What is 0%?" → never.
