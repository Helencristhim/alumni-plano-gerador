Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

These are the key words for this lesson. Tap Listen to hear the pronunciation.

Passport -- an official document for international travel (documento oficial para viagem internacional)
"Could I see your passport, please?"
Customs -- the area at the airport where officers check your bags (alfândega)
"We went through customs at JFK."
Declaration -- a form saying what you bring into the country (formulário de declaração)
"I have nothing to declare."
Carry-on -- a small bag you take on the plane (bagagem de mão)
"My carry-on is in the overhead bin."
Boarding pass -- the ticket to get on the plane (cartão de embarque)
"Here is my boarding pass."
Reservation -- booking a room in advance (reserva)
"I have a reservation under the name Percival."
Front desk -- the reception area at a hotel (recepção do hotel)
"Could you call the front desk?"
Room key -- the card to open your hotel room (chave do quarto)
"Here is your room key."
Luggage -- bags and suitcases for traveling (bagagem, malas)
"Could you help me with my luggage, please?"
Floor -- the level of a building (andar de um prédio)
"My room is on the tenth floor."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Past Simple + Going To

Grammar

Past Simple = completed actions in the past (Use o Past Simple para ações concluídas no passado)
Going To = future plans and intentions (Use going to para planos futuros)
Structure: Past Simple = Subject + verb (past form) | Going To = Subject + am/is/are + going to + verb

TypeRegular (arrive)Irregular (go)
AffirmativeI arrived at the airport.He went through customs.
NegativeI did not arrive late.He did not go to Miami.
QuestionDid you arrive on time?Did he go through customs?
Going ToI am going to stay for ten days. / He is going to visit Times Square.

Common Mistakes:

"I goed to the hotel."

"I went to the hotel."

"I going to stay ten days."

"I am going to stay for ten days."

Lembre-se: verbos irregulares têm formas próprias no passado (go → went, take → took). Para planos futuros, use am/is/are + going to + verbo base.
