Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Adverbs of Frequency = words that indicate HOW OFTEN something happens

AdverbFrequencyExample
always100%"I always check my schedule."
usually80%"I usually attend the 9 AM meeting."
often60%"She often briefs her team."
sometimes40%"He sometimes works late."
rarely10%"She rarely skips meetings."
never0%"I never leave without wrapping up."

Position Rule:

Before the main verb: "I always review my emails."

After the verb BE: "She is usually on time."

Describe a real deal or project you closed at Microsoft. How did you confirm the agreement and finalize the paperwork? Report what people said using reported speech ("He said that...", "She told me that..."), and use new words like contract, sign off, commitment, finalize and kick off.

Survival Card -- Lesson 14

1Let me confirm the terms, so we are on the same page.Deixe-me confirmar os termos, para ficarmos em sintonia.
2He said the terms were perfect, and he told me procurement would sign off.Ele disse que os termos estavam perfeitos, e me disse que compras aprovaria.
3Once procurement signs off, we'll finalize the paperwork.Assim que compras aprovar, vamos finalizar a documentação.
4We have a clear commitment from both sides.Temos um compromisso claro de ambos os lados.
5Going forward, we'll kick off the project in early March.Daqui em diante, vamos dar início ao projeto no começo de março.
