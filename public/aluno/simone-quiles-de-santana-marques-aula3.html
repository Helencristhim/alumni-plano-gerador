Aula 03 -- Pre-class

Email Essentials -- Corporate Emails

Write and answer a corporate email: subject line, recipient, attachment, regarding, forward, reply, follow up, best regards. Structures: "Please find ... attached.", "Could you please ...?", "I look forward to your reply."

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Subject line -- the short title that says what the email is about
"Write a clear subject line." (assunto)
Recipient -- the person who receives the email
"Add the recipient’s email address." (destinatário)
Attachment -- a file you send together with the email
"The contract is the attachment." (anexo)
Regarding -- about / concerning (formal)
"I am writing regarding the contract." (a respeito de)
Forward -- to send a received email to another person
"I will forward it to the team." (encaminhar)
Reply -- to answer an email
"Please reply by Friday." (responder)
Follow up -- to send a second message to check progress
"I will follow up next week." (dar seguimento)
Best regards -- a polite, formal way to close an email
"Best regards, Simone." (atenciosamente)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the email and answer the questions.

Simone writes an email to a client. The subject line says "Service Contract -- Telefonica". The recipient is Mr. Carter. She writes, "Dear Mr. Carter, I am writing regarding our contract. Please find the signed document attached. Could you please reply by Friday? I look forward to your reply. Best regards, Simone." Later, she will forward the email to the legal team and follow up next week.

1. Como Simone diz o assunto do email? / How does she say what the email is about?
A "I am writing about regarding our contract."
B "I am writing regarding our contract."
C "I writing our contract."
2. Como ela informa o anexo? / How does she mention the attachment?
A "Please find the signed document attached."
B "I send you the document in attach."
C "The document is in attachment."
3. Como ela fecha o email? / How does she close the email?
A "Bye bye, Simone."
B "Best regard, Simone."
C "Best regards, Simone."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Opening, Body, Closing

GRAMMAR

As três partes de um email corporativo. / The three parts of a corporate email.

Part / ParteUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
Opening / AberturaCumprimentar e dizer o assunto / Greet & state the topicDear Mr. Carter, I am writing regarding the contract.
Body / CorpoAnexar e fazer o pedido / Attach & requestPlease find the document attached. Could you please reply by Friday?
Closing / FechamentoDespedida educada / Polite sign-offI look forward to your reply. Best regards, Simone.

Tip: use "Please find attached" (não "I send you in attach") e "I look forward to your reply" (não "I wait your reply").

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Choose the best option for each situation.

You want to say the email is about the contract. / Você quer dizer o assunto.
A "I am writing regarding the contract."
B "I write for the contract."
C "This email is the contract."
You attached a file. How do you say it? / Você anexou um arquivo.
A "I send you in attach."
B "Please find the file attached."
C "The file is in the attach."
You ask the client to answer. / Você pede uma resposta.
A "Could you please reply by Friday?"
B "Reply me until Friday."
C "You reply Friday."
You close the email politely. / Você fecha o email.
A "I look forward to your reply. Best regard, Simone."
B "I look forward to your reply. Best regards, Simone."
C "I wait your reply. Bye, Simone."
