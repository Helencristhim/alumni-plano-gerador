Revise o Present Simple em detalhes: afirmativa, negativa e interrogativa.

Present Simple — Deep Dive
FORM STRUCTURE EXAMPLE
Affirmative
Afirmativa		I/You/We/They + verb
He/She/It + verbs		Milton exports jewelry.

Negative
Negativa		I/You/We/They + do not + verb
He/She/It + does not + verb		He does not sell directly.

Interrogative
Interrogativa		Do + I/you/we/they + verb?

Regra do -s		export → exports

FORM STRUCTURE EXAMPLE
Affirmative
Afirmativa		Subject + am/is/are + verb-ingMilton is attending a trade show.

Grammar Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Revise os Modal Verbs para negociação: could, would, should.

Modal Verbs — Polite Proposals, Suggestions, and Advice
MODAL USE EXAMPLE
Could
Poderia		Polite requests and suggestions"Could we discuss the terms?"
"Could you consider a lower price?"
Poderíamos discutir os termos? / Você poderia considerar um preço menor?
Would
Gostaria / Faria		Hypothetical/polite offers"Would you accept a larger volume?"
"I would suggest a 10% discount."
Você aceitaria um volume maior? / Eu sugeriria um desconto de 10%.
Should
Deveria		Advice and recommendations"We should review the deadline."
"You should consider our proposal."
Devemos revisar o prazo. / Você deveria considerar nossa proposta.
Can vs Could: "Can" expressa habilidade, "Could" expressa polidez:
"I can offer 5%." (direto, fato) vs "I could offer 5%." (educado, sugestão)
"Can you lower the price?" (direto) vs "Could you lower the price?" (mais polido)

✗ "Can we discussing the terms?" → ✓ "Could we discuss the terms?" (modal + base form)

Grammar Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Revise o Present Perfect para experiência e eventos recentes.

Present Perfect — Experience and Recent Events
STRUCTURE USE EXAMPLE
have/has + past participle
Forma afirmativa		Experience / Recent events"I have attended many board meetings."
"Milton has exported jewelry for over thirty years."
Eu participei de muitas reuniões. / Milton exporta joias há mais de trinta anos.
have/has + just + pp
Evento recente		Something that happened very recently"We have just approved the budget."
"The board has decided to increase fees."
Acabamos de aprovar o orçamento. / O conselho decidiu aumentar as taxas.
Have/Has + ever/never
Perguntas de experiência		Asking about life experience"Have you ever presented at a board meeting?"
"I have never missed a meeting."
Você já apresentou em uma reunião? / Eu nunca faltei a uma reunião.
Present Perfect vs Past Simple:
"I attended the meeting yesterday." (tempo específico = Past Simple)
"I have attended many meetings." (experiência geral, sem tempo específico = Present Perfect)

✗ "I have attended the meeting yesterday." → ✓ "I attended the meeting yesterday." (tempo específico = Past Simple)
✗ "I attended many meetings." (sem contexto de tempo) → ✓ "I have attended many meetings." (experiência = Present Perfect)
