Grammar Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Leia o texto abaixo. As estruturas gramaticais estão em negrito. Depois, responda as perguntas.

Milton Sayegh is a luxury jewelry exporter from São Paulo, Brazil. He runs a company that exports precious stones and gold pieces to the United States. His headquarters is in São Paulo, but he also has an office in Miami. Milton travels overseas several times a year for business.

Right now, Milton is working on improving his English. He is taking lessons because he wants to negotiate without an interpreter. His company is expanding operations in the American market, and he believes that clear communication is the key to avoiding misunderstandings in important meetings.
1. What does Milton's company export?
A Technology products
B Precious stones and gold pieces
C Coffee and agricultural goods
2. Why is Milton taking English lessons?
A He wants to move to the United States
B His company requires all employees to speak English
C He wants to negotiate without an interpreter
3. Which verb forms describe CURRENT/TEMPORARY actions in the text?
A "runs", "exports", "travels"
B "is working", "is taking", "is expanding"
C "is", "has", "believes"

Quiz Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situação profissional.

You meet a potential business partner at a jewelry trade show. How do you introduce yourself?
A "Hey, I sell some stuff from Brazil."
B "Good morning. My name is Milton Sayegh. I run a luxury jewelry export company based in São Paulo."
C "I am Milton. I have jewelry."
Your American distributor speaks too fast during a call. What do you say?
A "I don't understand your English."
B Nothing — just pretend you understood.
C "Could you say that again, please? I want to make sure I understand correctly."
Someone asks: "How long have you been in business?" What is the best answer?
A "For over thirty years. We specialize in luxury goods for the international market."
B "I have 30 years of business."
C "Since many time ago."
You want to describe what your company does right now (temporary expansion). Which sentence is correct?
A "We expand operations in the U.S."
B "We are expanding operations in the U.S."
C "We expanded operations in the U.S."
