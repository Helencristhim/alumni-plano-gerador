Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Simple vs Present Continuous

Grammar
Structure Present Simple Present Continuous
Use Facts, routines, habits Actions happening now, temporary situations
Form Subject + verb (+ s/es) Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing
Example "I work at a diesel engine company." "I am working on three new deals."
Signal words always, every day, usually now, right now, currently, at the moment

Tradução: Use o Present Simple para rotinas e fatos permanentes. Use o Present Continuous para ações que estao acontecendo agora ou são temporárias.

Common Mistake:
"I am work at a diesel company."
"I work at a diesel company." (habits = Present Simple, NOT continuous)

"I work on a big deal right now."
"I am working on a big deal right now." (happening now = Present Continuous)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip — Past Simple vs Present Simple

GRAMMAR
Past Simple Present Simple
Use Completed actions in the past Facts, routines, current role
Form Subject + verb-ed (regular) / irregular form Subject + verb (+ s/es for he/she/it)
Example "I studied engineering." / "We achieved our target." "I lead the sales team." / "He negotiates contracts."
Signal words in 2014, last year, yesterday, ago, then every day, usually, now, today
Tradução: Use o Past Simple para ações completas no passado (formação, conquistas, mudanças de carreira). Use o Present Simple para o que você faz hoje como rotina.
Common Mistakes:
✗ "I lead the team since 2019." ✓ "I have led the team since 2019." (duration from past to now = present perfect, but for this level focus on: "I started leading the team in 2019.")
✗ "Yesterday I negotiate a new contract." ✓ "Yesterday I negotiated a new contract." (past = past simple)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip — Present Simple + Adverbs of Frequency

GRAMMAR
Present Simple Adverb Position
Use Facts, routines, habits, schedules Before main verb, after BE
Form Subject + verb (+ s/es for he/she/it) S + adverb + verb / S + BE + adverb
Example "I update Mr. Zhang every Monday." "I usually wrap up at six."
Frequência always (100%) > usually > often > sometimes > rarely > never (0%) every Monday, twice a week, once a month
Tradução: Use o Present Simple para descrever rotinas e hábitos. Advérbios de frequência (always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never) vão ANTES do verbo principal e DEPOIS do verbo BE.
Erros Comuns:
✗ "I always am updating Mr. Zhang." ✓ "I always update Mr. Zhang." (rotina = present simple, não continuous)
✗ "I wrap up usually at six." ✓ "I usually wrap up at six." (advérbio vai ANTES do verbo principal)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip — Can/Could for Requests + Team Introductions

GRAMMAR
Structure Example
Could + subject + base verb?"Could you introduce me to the manager?"
Can + subject + base verb?"Can you assist me with the report?"
This is + name + role"This is Maria. She is in charge of customer service."
Subject + specializes in"Carlos specializes in generators."
Subject + is based in"The team is based in São Paulo."
Tradução: Use "could" para pedidos educados (mais formal que "can"). Para apresentar colegas: "This is [nome]. He/She is in charge of / handles / specializes in [função]."
Erros Comuns:
✗ "Could you to help me?" ✓ "Could you help me?" (sem "to" depois de could)
✗ "She is in charge for the team." ✓ "She is in charge of the team." (in charge OF, não FOR)
