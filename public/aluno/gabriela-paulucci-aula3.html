Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip — Present Simple for Daily Routines

Grammar

Present Simple — I / You

Subject Verb (base form) Example
I wake up I wake up at 7.
I eat I eat breakfast.
I go I go to work.
You wake up You wake up early.
You eat You eat lunch at noon.

Time Expressions / Expressões de Tempo:

  • in the morning — de manhã
  • in the afternoon — à tarde
  • at night — à noite
  • at + time — at 7, at noon, at 8

Dica: No Present Simple, o verbo NÃO muda com I e You. É sempre a forma base: I eat, you eat, I go, you go.
