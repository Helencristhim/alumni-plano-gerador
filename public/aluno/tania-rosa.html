Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto com atenção. As palavras em negrito são a gramática desta aula.

Tânia Rosa lives in Brazil. She travels to many countries every year. She loves visiting new destinations and learning about different cultures. Her family does not speak English at home, but Tânia wants to improve. Does she enjoy visiting new places? Yes, she does. She loves learning about cultures.

1. What does Tânia do every year?
A She stays home.
B She travels to many countries.
C She studies French.
2. Does her family speak English at home?
A Yes, they do.
B No, they do not.
C Only on weekends.
3. Does Tânia enjoy visiting new places?
A Yes, she does.
B No, she does not.
C She prefers staying home.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Aqui está a regra. Leia com calma e veja os exemplos.

Present Simple — Presente Simples

Usamos o Present Simple para falar de fatos, hábitos e rotinas. / We use the Present Simple for facts, habits, and routines.

FormStructureExample
Affirmative (afirmativa)Subject + verb (+s/es)She travels every year.
Negative (negativa)Subject + do/does not + verbShe does not speak French.
Question (pergunta)Do/Does + subject + verb?Does she like traveling?
Common Mistake / Erro Comum:
"She travel every year." ✗
"She travels every year." ✓
He, she, it = verbo + S. Sempre!

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situação.

You meet someone at the airport. They ask: "Where are you from?" What do you say?
A "I want go Brazil."
B "I am from Brazil. Nice to meet you."
C "Brazil is hot."
D "I no speak English."
Someone asks: "Do you travel often?" You love traveling. What do you say?
A "No, I does not."
B "I travel much yes."
C "Yes, I do. I love to travel."
D "Travel is good."
You want to ask someone about their job. What is the correct question?
A "What is your work?"
B "What do you do?"
C "You work what?"
D "Does you work?"

Stage 5: Think About It

Reflection

Aqui não tem resposta certa — é pra você pensar em inglês. Toque no microfone e responda como vier.

Imagine you are on a plane to your dream destination. The person next to you asks: "Tell me about yourself." What do you say? Talk about your name, where you are from, what you love doing, and why you travel.
