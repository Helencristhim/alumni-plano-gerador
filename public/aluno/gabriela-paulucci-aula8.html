Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context — At the Store

Leia e responda.

Gabriela is at a clothing store. She says: “I’m looking for a dress.” The assistant asks: “What size are you?” Gabriela is a medium. She likes a blue dress and asks: “Can I try it on?” It fits! She asks the price: “How much is it?” It is forty dollars — a good price. Gabriela says: “I’ll take it!”

A A dress

A Small

3. How much is the dress?

B Forty dollars
C Four dollars
GRAMMAR

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip — Shopping

As frases para fazer compras:

EstruturaExampleTradução
How much is it? (1 coisa)How much is the dress?Quanto custa?
How much are they? (2+)How much are the shoes?Quanto custam?
I’m looking for + itemI’m looking for a jacket.Estou procurando uma jaqueta.
Can I try it on? / I’ll take it.I’ll take it!Posso provar? / Vou levar.

Dica: is para 1 coisa, are para 2+ (shoes, pants são sempre plural). Para comprar: I’ll take it.

Quiz

Stage 4: Choose the Best Sentence

1. Ask the price of one dress.
A “How much it is?”
B “How much is it?”
C “How much are it?”
2. Ask the price of shoes (plural).
A “How much is the shoes?”
B “How much are the shoes?”
C “How many the shoes?”
3. Say what you want.
A “I looking for a dress.”
B “I’m looking for a dress.”
C “I look a dress.”
4. Decide to buy it.
A “I take it.”
B “I’ll take it.”
C “I’m take it.”
Reflection

Stage 5: Go Shopping

Imagine que você entra numa loja. Faça uma compra completa: procurar, perguntar o preço, provar e comprar.

Sugestão de resposta
“I love shopping. I’m looking for a blue jacket. I’m a medium. How much is it? I’ll take it!”
Grave você mesma fazendo compras numa loja. Use I’m looking for / How much is it? / Can I try it on? / I’ll take it.

Survival Card — Lesson 8: Shopping

As 5 frases-chave para qualquer loja:

2How much is it?Quanto custa?
3Can I try it on?Posso provar?
4I’m a medium.Eu uso tamanho médio (M).
5I’ll take it.Vou levar.

Checklist — Lesson 8

  • Sei as roupas (shirt, dress, shoes, jacket...)
  • Sei dizer “I’m looking for ...”
  • Sei perguntar “How much is it / are they?”
  • Sei dizer meu tamanho e pedir para provar
  • Sei dizer “I’ll take it” para comprar
  • Consigo fazer uma compra completa numa loja
