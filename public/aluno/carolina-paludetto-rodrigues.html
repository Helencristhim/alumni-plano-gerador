Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip — Adverbs of Frequency

Where do we put adverbs like always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never?

AdverbFrequencyExample
always100%I always wake up at seven.
usually80%She usually has breakfast at home.
often60%We often walk to school.
sometimes40%I sometimes practice on weekends.
rarely10%He rarely stays up late.
never0%I never skip breakfast.
Position Rule 1: BEFORE the main verb
"I always wake up at seven." / "She never skips homework."
Position Rule 2: AFTER the verb "be"

Key point / Ponto-chave: com "every day, usually, always", use o Present Simple. Com "now, right now, look, listen", use o Present Continuous (am/is/are + verbo-ing).
