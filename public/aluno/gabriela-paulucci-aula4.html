Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip — Present Simple: 3rd Person

Grammar

Present Simple — 3rd Person (He / She / It)

I / You / We / They He / She / It
I work He works
I live She lives
I eat He eats
I speak She speaks
They eat She eats

The Rule / A Regra:

  • I / You / We / They = verbo na forma base (work, live, eat)
  • He / She / It = verbo + S (works, lives, eats)

Dica: Quando falar sobre outra pessoa (ele/ela), SEMPRE adicione S no verbo: work → works, live → lives, eat → eats.
