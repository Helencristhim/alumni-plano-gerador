Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

These are the key words for this lesson. Tap Listen to hear the pronunciation.

Priority -- the most important thing to do first (prioridade)
"Compliance is our top priority right now."
Issue -- a problem or topic to discuss (problema, questão)
"We are dealing with a compliance issue."
Update -- new information about a situation (atualização)
"I need to provide an update to the board."
Delay -- when something takes longer than planned (atraso)
"We are facing a delay in the audit."
However -- but, on the other hand (no entanto, porém)
"The project is on track. However, we need more time for compliance."
In addition -- also, plus (além disso)
"In addition, we are reviewing the risk matrix."
Therefore -- so, for that reason (portanto)
"The deadline changed. Therefore, we need a new action plan."
Furthermore -- also, and what is more (além do mais)
"Furthermore, the stakeholders requested an extra review."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

This month, Percival's team is conducting a full compliance review at EGEA. First of all, they identified several issues in the operations department. Then, they developed an action plan. However, they are facing a delay because the stakeholders requested additional documentation. Therefore, compliance is the top priority right now. In addition, Percival is preparing an update for the executive board. Furthermore, they discovered a new risk that requires immediate attention. The main challenge is the tight deadline, but Percival's team is working hard to meet it.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Discourse Connectors

Grammar

Discourse Connectors = words that link ideas and make your speech more professional (palavras que ligam ideias e tornam sua fala mais profissional)

FunctionConnectorExample
Additionalso, in addition, furthermore"In addition, we are reviewing the risk matrix."
Contrasthowever, on the other hand"However, we need more time for compliance."
Cause/Resultso, therefore, that's why, because of that"Therefore, we need a new action plan."
Sequencefirst of all, then, finally"First of all, we identified the issue."

Common Mistake:

"However we need more time."

"However, we need more time."

Lembre-se: SEMPRE use vírgula depois do conector (however, therefore, in addition, furthermore)!
