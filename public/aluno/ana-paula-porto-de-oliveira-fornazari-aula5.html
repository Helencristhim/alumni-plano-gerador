Chapter 3: The Story Tense

Was Doing vs Did

The background · The moment

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I was walking on the beach when I saw a whale."

"While we were driving, the sun rose over the coast."

"It was raining when we arrived at the hotel."

"While I was taking photos, I met a group of travelers."

Which words show the long background, and which show the sudden moment?

FormUseExample
Past continuous
(was / were + -ing)		A longer action in progress — the backgroundI was walking on the beach.
Past simpleA short, finished action — the momentI saw a whale.
when & whilewhile + the long action (was doing); when + the short one (did). "While I was taking photos, I met a friend."

The past continuous sets the scene; the past simple tells what happened in it.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

"When it started to rain, I walked on the beach."
"When it started to rain, I was walking on the beach."
"We was hiking when we saw the lake."
"We were hiking when we saw the lake."
"While I took photos, I met a friend."
"While I was taking photos, I met a friend."

Use the past continuous for the long background action, match was with I/he/she and were with we/you/they, and put was/were + -ing after "while".

Chapter 4: A Trip Remembered

The Postcard, Then the Story

Artifact

A Postcard from the Coast

Greetings from Lisbon! POSTCARD

Dear Ana Paula, what a journey! Yesterday, while I was walking along the coast, the sun came out and the scenery was breathtaking. Later, I got lost in the old town, but I bought a lovely souvenir. In the evening, while everyone was watching the sunset, I took the best photo of my life. It was unforgettable! See you soon, Sarah.

1. What was Sarah doing when the sun came out?
She was walking along the coast.
2. What happened in the old town?
She got lost, but she bought a souvenir.
3. What was everyone doing in the evening?
They were watching the sunset.

Dialogue

A Friend Tells His Story

A
Mark, what was the most memorable journey of your life?
M
Chile, two years ago. One morning, while we were driving along the coast, the sun rose over the mountains. The scenery was breathtaking.
A
That sounds amazing. What happened next?
M
While I was taking photos, I met a group of travelers from Brazil. We wandered through a fishing village together and bought some souvenirs.
A
Did anything go wrong on the trip?
M
Yes! On the way back, we got lost in the forest. It was getting dark, but we found the trail and watched an unforgettable sunset. What an adventure.
