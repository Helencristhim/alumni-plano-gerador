Adverbs of Frequency

They tell us how often something happens

FrequencyAdverbExample
100%alwaysI always check my emails first.
80%usuallyI usually start at 8 AM.
60%oftenWe often have tight deadlines.
40%sometimesI sometimes work late.
10%rarelyI rarely miss a meeting.
0%neverI never leave before 5 PM.

Position Rule: Before the main verb: "I usually start at 8."
After "be": "I am always on time."

Common Mistake

"We have always a meeting on Monday."
"We always have a meeting on Monday."

The adverb goes before the main verb, not after it.
