Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto. Ele usa TODAS as estruturas das aulas 1-4 juntas.

Anna Flávia works at Usina Cururique. She is from Minas Gerais. She wakes up at six thirty every morning. She commutes to the office by car. She does not take the bus. There is a computer on her desk. There are many folders on her shelf. Her manager is Ricardo. His office is next to hers. Their team has five people. Do they work on weekends? No, they do not.
How does Anna Flávia commute to work?

Stage 1.4: Grammar Review

Grammar

Revisão de todas as regras gramaticais das aulas 1-4.

All Grammar Rules -- Todas as Regras
AulaStructureExample
1Present Simple (+)I work. She works.
2Present Simple (-/?)I do not work. Does she work?
3There is/There areThere is a desk. There are folders.
4PossessivesMy team. His office. Her name.

Most Common Mistakes / Erros Mais Comuns:

I works. ✗ I work. ✓ (sem -s com I)

She do not. ✗ She does not. ✓ (does com she)

There is two. ✗ There are two. ✓ (are com plural)

He name. ✗ His name. ✓ (possessivo, não sujeito)

Stage 3: Pronunciation Practice

Speaking

Agora frases mais longas, combinando tudo. Respire fundo e tente!

My name is Anna Flávia. I am from Minas Gerais. I work at Usina Cururique in the financial area. Our company exports sugar.
Meu nome é Anna Flávia. Sou de Minas Gerais. Trabalho na Usina Cururique na área financeira. Nossa empresa exporta açúcar.
I wake up at six thirty. I commute by car. I check my schedule every morning. I do not work on weekends.
Acordo às seis e meia. Vou de carro. Verifico minha agenda toda manhã. Não trabalho nos fins de semana.
There is a computer on my desk. My manager is Ricardo. Her name is Marina. Our team has five people.
Tem um computador na minha mesa. Meu gerente é Ricardo. O nome dela é Marina. Nossa equipe tem cinco pessoas.

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Situações mais completas. Você consegue!

You meet a new partner and need to introduce yourself completely. Which response includes identity + job + company?
A "Hi, I am Anna Flávia."
B "Hi, I am Anna Flávia. I work at Usina Cururique in the financial area. We export sugar."
C "I work at Cururique."
A partner asks "Tell me about your team." Which answer uses possessives correctly?
A "He manager is Ricardo. She name is Marina."
B "My manager is Ricardo. Her name is Marina. Our team has five people."
C "I manager is Ricardo. We team has five."
