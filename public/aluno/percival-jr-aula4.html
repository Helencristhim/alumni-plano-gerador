Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Continuous + Contrast

Grammar

Present Continuous = actions happening NOW or during this period (ações acontecendo AGORA ou neste período)
Structure: Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing

Present Simple (routine)Present Continuous (now)
II manage the team.I am managing a crisis.
HeHe reports monthly.He is reporting today.
WeWe review risks.We are reviewing risks now.

Common Mistakes:

"I working on a project."

"I am working on a project."

Lembre-se: no Present Continuous, você SEMPRE precisa do verbo auxiliar (am/is/are) antes do verbo com -ing.
