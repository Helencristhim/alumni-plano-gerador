Chapter 3: Would Like To & Hope To

Talk About a Dream

would like to + verb · hope to + verb · would love to + verb

Discover the Code

Listen and Find the Pattern

"I would like to live abroad." (would like to + verb)

"I hope to become fluent." (hope to + verb)

"I would love to see Japan." (would love to + verb)

"I wish to start a business." (wish to + verb)

FormUseExample
would like to + verba polite wantI would like to travel.
hope to + verba hope for the futureI hope to learn fast.
would love to + verba strong, happy wantI would love to live abroad.

After would like, hope, would love, and wish, use to + verb. They all talk about a future dream or want.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"I hope become fluent."

✓ RIGHT

"I hope to become fluent."

✗ WRONG

"I would like to traveling."

✓ RIGHT

"I would like to travel."

Chapter 4: Sharing Dreams

Tânia Shares a Dream

What she hopes to do someday

Dialogue

What Do You Dream Of?

F
So, Tânia, what is your big dream for the future?
T
Someday I would love to live abroad, maybe by the sea. And I hope to become fluent in English.
F
That is a wonderful goal! What is on your bucket list?
T
A long, slow trip across Europe. And I would like to retire early, so I can travel all year.
F
Big dreams! Do you think you can achieve them?
T
Yes! Every small step is a new opportunity. I really believe I can achieve my dreams.
