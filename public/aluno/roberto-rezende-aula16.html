Chapter 3 -- The Code

Grammar Discovery

Reported speech: said / told + backshift

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

What do the orange words have in common?

"Amy said the order was on track."

"The client told me they needed a change."

"HQ said the new part would be ready."

"They told me they could wait a week."

Grammar Rule

Reported Speech

Direct (what they said)Reported (you on the call)
"The order is on track."Amy said the order was on track.
"We need a change."The client told me they needed a change.
"It will be ready."HQ said it would be ready.
"We can wait a week."They told me they could wait a week.

Key idea: use said (no person) or told + a person (told me, told HQ) to report. The verb moves one step back: is/are → was/were, present → past, will → would, can → could. Say "told me", never "said me".

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"Amy said me the order was ready."
"Amy told me the order was ready."

Use told + a person ("told me", "told HQ"). Use said with no person ("said the order was ready"). Never "said me".

Conference Call -- HQ & Shanghai Team

Item 1: Status of the big order · on track, engines ready

Item 2: Client change · behind schedule

Note (client): "We need one part redesigned." / "We need it next week."

Goal: give a clear status, then report what the client and HQ said using said / told + the verb moved back. Speak on behalf of the team.

Dialogue

The Status Update

A

"Good morning, everyone. The Shanghai team has dialed in. Roberto, headquarters wants the status of the big order. Could you give us the latest?"

R

"Sure. Let me highlight the main point: the order is on track and the engines are ready. But the client told me they needed one part redesigned."

A

"I see. And did they say when they needed it? HQ is worried this puts us behind schedule."

R

"They said they needed it the following week. And the team in Shanghai told me the new part would be ready in time."

A

"That is a relief. So we are still on track overall. Could you confirm the next steps for the whole team?"

R

"Yes. On behalf of the sales team, I will send the redesign to Shanghai today and confirm the new date with the client. I will share the latest by Friday."
