Listening

Market Transaction

Listen to the conversation and answer the questions.

1. What does Roberto buy at the market?
Olives (large box) and Manchego cheese.
2. Why does he not buy the expensive cheese?
It is too expensive (eighteen euros). He asks for something cheaper.
3. How does Roberto pay?
He pays cash because the vendor does not accept cards.
Listening

Souvenir Shop

Listen to Roberto at a souvenir shop.

1. What two things does Roberto want to buy?
A magnet and a t-shirt.
2. How much is the total?
Twenty-two euros (7 for the magnet + 15 for the cheaper t-shirt).
3. Does this shop accept cards?
Yes, the shop accepts cards.
Comprehension

Did you understand?

1. How much does the large box of olives cost?
Five euros.
2. Why does the vendor say "sorry, only cash"?
Because the vendor does not accept credit cards.
3. What polite phrase does Roberto use to refuse the expensive cheese?
"That is too expensive. Do you have something cheaper?"
