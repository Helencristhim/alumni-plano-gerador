Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

At the strategy meeting, Eduarda uses everything from this block. She opens by breaking the ice: "Nice to see you all again." She gives her opinion: "In my view, we should act now," and backs it up: "The data suggests the market will recover." She argues a consequence with a first conditional: "If we wait, we will lose the deal." When David jumps in to ask about the risk, she answers and then yields the floor: "Anna, please go ahead." Finally she reports and sums up: "Daniel said that we will move now. To sum up: act now, review in two weeks." Everyone is on the same page.

1. Which skill does Eduarda use to OPEN the meeting?
A Reporting verbs.
B Breaking the ice.
C Summarizing.
2. Which sentence is a first conditional?
A "In my view, we should act now."
B "If we wait, we will lose the deal."
C "Nice to see you all again."
3. How does she report what Daniel said?
A "Daniel said that we will move now."
B "Daniel said me to move now."
C "Daniel told that move now."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

The four structures of the block, in the order of a meeting (os quatro skills, na ordem de uma reunião):

SkillStructureExample
Question form (A6)Wh-/aux + subject + verb?"What do you do?"
Turn-taking (A7)Sorry to jump in, but... / Go ahead"Sorry to jump in, but..."
First conditional (A8)If + present, ... will + base"If we wait, we will lose."
Reporting verb (A9)said / told someone (that) + clause"He told me that..."

Top mistakes of the block:

"If we will wait..." / "He said me that..."

"If we wait..." / "He told me that..."

No IF use present simple (sem "will"); use "tell" com pessoa, "say" sem pessoa.

Materiais Complementares -- Aula 10

Tema da aula: Review & Simulation -- Strategy Meeting (integra as aulas 6-9). Em cada conteudo, preste atencao em COMO uma reuniao inteira flui: abrir, dar opiniao com dados, gerenciar os turnos e fechar com um resumo. Marque como concluido ao terminar.

Filme
Moneyball (2011) -- as reuniões do front office

Assista às cenas de reunião dos scouts e do Billy Beane: ele DEFENDE uma posição com dados ("the data suggests..."), as pessoas discordam e fazem push back, e ele resume a decisão. É o arco completo da aula: abrir, defender com dados, gerenciar turnos e fechar.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês. Anote cada opinion frame, cada first conditional e como ele resume ("So, here's the deal...").
