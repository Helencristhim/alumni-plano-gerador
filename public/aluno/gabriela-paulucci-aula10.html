Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context — A Day Out

Leia e responda.

It’s Saturday. Gabriela loves shopping. First, she goes to a cafe. She says: “I’d like a coffee, please.” Then she asks: “Where is the mall?” A local says: “Go straight and turn left.” At the store, she finds a dress. “How much is it?” It’s a good price, so she says: “I’ll take it!”

1. What does Gabriela love?
A Shopping
B Running
C Cleaning
2. How does she get to the mall?
A Turn right
B Go straight and turn left
C Go back
3. Does she buy the dress?
A Yes — "I'll take it!"
B No, too expensive
C She doesn't like it

A “How much is the shoes?”
B “How much are the shoes?”
C “How many shoes?”
4. Ask where the bank is. (L9)
A “Where is bank?”
B “Where is the bank?”
C “The bank where is?”
Reflection

Stage 5: Tell Your Day Out

Conte a história de um dia fora: o que você gosta, onde come, o que compra e como chega lá.

Sugestão de resposta
“Yesterday I had a great day. I love shopping. I had lunch at a restaurant and I ordered a coffee. Then I bought a dress for a good price.”
Grave você mesma contando um dia fora completo: gostos, comida, compras e direções.
