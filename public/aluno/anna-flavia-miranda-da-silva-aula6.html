"Please find the attachment below."
Reply -- to answer a message / Responder
"I will reply to your email today."

"I will forward the report to my manager."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o email. As palavras em negrito mostram Can/Could para pedidos educados.

Dear Mr. Tanaka,

My name is Anna Flávia from Usina Coruripe. I am writing to request the sugar export report for March. Could you please send it to me as an attachment? I would also like to confirm our meeting on Monday. I am available in the afternoon. Could you please reply with a time that works for you?

Thank you.
Best regards,
Anna Flávia Miranda da Silva

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Aqui está a regra gramatical da aula.

Can / Could -- Pedidos Educados

Usamos Can e Could para fazer pedidos. Could é mais educado e formal, ideal para emails de trabalho. (Can = informal, Could = formal/polite.)

TypeStructureExample
InformalCan you + verb?Can you send the report?
FormalCould you please + verb?Could you please send it?
PermissionCould I + verb?Could I have the attachment?

Common Mistake / Erro Comum:

I want the report. ✗ (rude)

Could you please send me the report? ✓ (polite)

Regra: em emails de trabalho, NUNCA use "I want". Use "Could you please..." ou "Could I...?"

Stage 3: Pronunciation Practice

Speaking

Ouça, depois grave. Frases de email profissional!

Could you please send me the March export report?
Você poderia me enviar o relatório de exportação de março?
Please find the attachment below. Could you confirm you received it?
Segue o anexo abaixo. Você poderia confirmar que recebeu?
I am available on Monday. Could we schedule a meeting?
Estou disponível na segunda. Poderíamos agendar uma reunião?

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta.

You need the export report from a partner. What is the most polite way to ask?
A "I want the report now."
B "Could you please send me the export report?"
C "Give me the report."
You received an email with a file. How do you tell your manager?
A "I got a thing in the email."
B "I received an email with an attachment. I will forward it to you."
C "Someone sent something."
