Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Past Perfect (Narrative Tenses)

Grammar

Quando duas acoes acontecem no passado, use o past perfect (had + particicio) para a que veio ANTES, e o past simple para a que veio depois. Para uma acao anterior que estava em andamento, use had been + verbo-ing. / When two actions happen in the past, use the past perfect (had + past participle) for the EARLIER one, and the past simple for the later one. For an earlier ongoing action, use had been + verb-ing.

For the past, use must have been / might have been / can't have been + past participle.
