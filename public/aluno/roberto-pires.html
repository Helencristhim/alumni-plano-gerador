Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Present Continuous = ações acontecendo AGORA, neste momento / actions happening NOW, at this moment

Structure: Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing

FormStructureExample
AffirmativeSubject + am/is/are + verb-ing"I am staying" / "He is waiting" / "They are checking"
NegativeSubject + am/is/are + not + verb-ing"I'm not waiting."
QuestionAm/Is/Are + subject + verb-ing?"Are you staying at this hotel?"

Contractions: I am staying → I'm staying | He is waiting → He's waiting | They are checking → They're checking

Present Simple vs Present Continuous:

Present Simple (hábito/rotina): "I stay at hotels when I travel."

Present Continuous (agora): "I am staying at Hotel Arts tonight."

Common Mistake / Erro Comum:

"I staying at the hotel."

"I am staying at the hotel."

Precisa do am/is/are antes do verbo + -ing. Nunca omita o verbo auxiliar.
