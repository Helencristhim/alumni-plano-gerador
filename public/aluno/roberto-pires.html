Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Present Continuous = ações acontecendo AGORA, neste momento / actions happening NOW, at this moment

Structure: Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing

FormStructureExample
AffirmativeSubject + am/is/are + verb-ing"I am staying" / "He is waiting" / "They are checking"
NegativeSubject + am/is/are + not + verb-ing"I'm not waiting."
QuestionAm/Is/Are + subject + verb-ing?"Are you staying at this hotel?"

Contractions: I am staying → I'm staying | He is waiting → He's waiting | They are checking → They're checking

Present Simple vs Present Continuous:

Present Simple (hábito/rotina): "I stay at hotels when I travel."

Present Continuous (agora): "I am staying at Hotel Arts tonight."

Common Mistake / Erro Comum:

"I staying at the hotel."

"I am staying at the hotel."

Precisa do am/is/are antes do verbo + -ing. Nunca omita o verbo auxiliar.

Stage 1.5: Fill in the Blanks

Practice

Complete each sentence with the correct word.

"I a reservation under the name Pires."
"I am at Hotel Arts in Barcelona."
"Where is the ?"
"What is the password?"
"My room is on the fifth ."
"The air conditioning is not ."

Stage 2: Put in Order

Order

Organize these hotel check-in events in the correct order.

? "She gives him the room key."
? "He says: I have a reservation under the name Pires."
? "He takes the elevator to the fifth floor."
? "Roberto walks into the hotel lobby."
? "The receptionist checks the computer."

Stage 3: Pronunciation

Speaking

Ouça, grave e compare sua pronúncia.

I have a reservation under the name Pires.
Tenho uma reserva no nome Pires.
I am staying in a double room on the fifth floor.
Estou num quarto duplo no quinto andar.
The air conditioning is not working. Can you help me?
O ar-condicionado não está funcionando. Pode me ajudar?

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz
1. You arrive at the hotel. The receptionist asks your name. What do you say?
A "I want a bed."
B "I have a reservation under the name Pires."
C "My name is Roberto. Give me a room."
2. You want internet in your room. What do you ask?
A "Where is the internet?"
B "I need a computer."
C "What is the Wi-Fi password?"

Stage 5: Think About It

Reflection
Imagine you just arrived at your hotel in Barcelona after a long flight. Record yourself: check in, ask about your room, ask about breakfast, and report one problem (the Wi-Fi is not working). Use present continuous: I am staying, I am looking for, it is not working.

Survival Card -- Lesson 6

1 I have a reservation under the name Pires. Tenho uma reserva no nome Pires.
2 What time is breakfast? Que horas é o café da manhã?
3 The air conditioning is not working. O ar-condicionado não está funcionando.
4 What is the Wi-Fi password? Qual é a senha do Wi-Fi?
5 I am staying for three nights. Vou ficar por três noites.
