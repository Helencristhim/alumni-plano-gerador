Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Quiz

Leia a historinha do safari e responda.

Look! It's a big dinosaur. It is green. The big green dinosaur can run. Run, dino, run! Look! A tree. The tree is big and green too.
Is the dinosaur big or small? (O dino e grande ou pequeno?)
A It's a big dinosaur.
B It's a small dinosaur.
What color is the dinosaur? (Que cor?)
A It's blue.
B It's green.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

It's a + word + dinosaur

"It's a" = "E um". Usamos pra dizer o que uma coisa e.

It's a big dinosaur. (E um dinossauro grande.)
It's a small dinosaur. (E um dinossauro pequeno.)
It's a green dinosaur. (E um dinossauro verde.)

Stage 1.1: Palavras do Espaco

Vocabulary

Toque em Listen, ouca e repita. Faca o gesto! (Ouca cada palavra e repita.)

Rocket -- foguete
"The rocket goes up!" (O foguete sobe!)
Star -- estrela
"I can see a star!" (Eu vejo uma estrela!)
Moon -- lua
"The moon is up." (A lua esta la em cima.)
Planet -- planeta
"It's a red planet!" (E um planeta vermelho!)
Sky -- ceu
"The sky is dark." (O ceu esta escuro.)
Up -- pra cima
"Go up, up, up!" (Sobe, sobe, sobe!)
Go -- vai / ir
"Ready? Go!" (Pronto? Vai!)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Quiz

Leia a historinha da decolagem e responda.

The rocket is ready. Let's count! Three, two, one... go! Blast off! The rocket goes up, up, up! Now I can see the moon and one, two, three stars in the sky!
What do you say before "go"? (O que voce diz antes de "go"?)
A Three, two, one!
B Big, small, green!
Where does the rocket go? (Pra onde o foguete vai?)
A Down.
B Up!

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Numbers 1 to 10

Contamos de 10 a 1 pra decolar. Os numeros em ingles:

1 one 2 two 3 three 4 four 5 five 6 six 7 seven 8 eight 9 nine 10 ten
