Chapter 3: The Code

Whose World Is It?

my · your · his · her + have / has

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"My family is small. My home is in Pinheiros."

"David has a daughter. His daughter is a student."

"Ana loves her kitchen. Her apartment is near the park."

"I have good neighbors. David has two children."

WordUseExample
my / yourI → my · you → yourMy home. Your family.
his / herhe → his · she → herHis son. Her kitchen.
our / theirwe → our · they → theirOur neighborhood. Their apartment.
have / hasI-you-we-they have · he-she-it hasI have a son. She has a daughter.

he and she change everything: he works, he has, HIS · she works, she has, HER.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"He house is big."

✓ RIGHT

"His house is big."

✗ WRONG

"She have two sons."

✓ RIGHT

"She has two sons."
