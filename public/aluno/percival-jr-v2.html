ConnectorUsageExample

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary

Vocabulary

Click on each word to listen and learn. All 8 words are essential for this lesson.

However·used to introduce a contrast or exception
"The meeting was productive. However, we still need to finalize the budget."
Therefore·for that reason, as a consequence
"The audit revealed several issues. Therefore, we need to take immediate action."
Moreover·in addition to what has been said
"The project is on schedule. Moreover, we are under budget."
Furthermore·in addition, besides (stronger than moreover)
"The team exceeded targets. Furthermore, client satisfaction increased by twenty percent."
Nevertheless·in spite of that, even so
"The deadline is tight. Nevertheless, we are confident we can deliver."
Consequently·as a result of something
"There was a system outage. Consequently, all operations were delayed."
On the other hand·used to present a different perspective
"Working from home saves time. On the other hand, it can be isolating."
As a result·because of something that happened
"We invested in new technology. As a result, productivity improved significantly."

Chapter 2: Vocabulary

Quick Check

Which connector introduces a contrasting point?
However -- "The meeting was productive. However, we still need to finalize the budget."
Which connector shows a consequence?
Therefore / As a result / Consequently -- all show cause and effect.
Chapter 3: Grammar

Three Categories

However / Nevertheless / On the other hand = Contrast
Therefore / Consequently / As a result = Cause & Result
Moreover / Furthermore = Addition
CategoryConnectorsUse when...
ContrastHowever, Nevertheless, On the other handYou want to show the opposite side
Cause & ResultTherefore, Consequently, As a resultYou explain a consequence
AdditionMoreover, FurthermoreYou add more information
Chapter 3: Grammar

Connector Structure

PatternExample
Statement. However, ...The report was positive. However, we need changes.
Statement. Therefore, ...We found risks. Therefore, we must act.
Statement. Moreover, ...Results are good. Moreover, we are ahead.

Always use a period before and a comma after the connector.

Chapter 3: Grammar

Common Mistake

"The results are good, moreover we are under budget."
"The results are good. Moreover, we are under budget."

Use a period (.) before the connector and a comma (,) after it.
