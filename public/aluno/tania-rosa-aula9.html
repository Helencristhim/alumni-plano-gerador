Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Aqui está a regra. Leia com calma e veja os exemplos.

Making Suggestions (Sugestões)

Usamos estas estruturas para SUGERIR algo a outra pessoa. "Let's", "Shall we" e "Why don't we" vêm seguidos do verbo na forma base. "How about" vem seguido do verbo com -ing. / Use these structures to make suggestions. "Let's", "Shall we" and "Why don't we" are followed by the base verb. "How about" is followed by the verb + -ing.

StructureExampleTradução
Let's + verb"Let's visit our grandparents."Vamos visitar nossos avós.
Shall we + verb?"Shall we go sightseeing?"Vamos fazer um passeio turístico?
Why don't we + verb?"Why don't we book the hotel?"Por que não reservamos o hotel?
How about + verb-ing?"How about visiting the museum?"Que tal visitar o museu?
Common Mistake / Erro Comum:
"Let's to go sightseeing." ✗ → "Let's go sightseeing." ✓
"How about visit the museum?" ✗ → "How about visiting the museum?" ✓
Lembre: depois de "Let's / Shall we / Why don't we" use o verbo na forma base (go, visit). Depois de "How about" use o verbo com -ing (visiting, going).
