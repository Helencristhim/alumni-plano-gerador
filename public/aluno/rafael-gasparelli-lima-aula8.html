Grammar Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Leia o texto. As frases de telefone estão em negrito. Depois, responda as perguntas.

Rafael's phone rings at 9 a.m. He answers: "Hello, this is Rafael Gasparello Lima speaking." The caller asks, "Could I speak to the legal team?" Rafael says, "Could you hold on a moment, please?" and dials the extension.

Later, he joins a video call with Sarah. The connection is bad, so he says, "Sorry, can you hear me? Let me unmute." At the end, they do not hang up until everything is clear. If the call drops, Rafael will call Sarah back right away.
1. How does Rafael answer the phone?
A "Who are you?"
B "Hello, this is Rafael Gasparello Lima speaking."
C "Yes, hello?"
2. Why does Rafael say "Let me unmute"?
A Sarah cannot hear him on the video call
B He wants to end the call
C He is leaving a voicemail
3. What will Rafael do if the call drops?
A Send an email only
B Call Sarah back right away
C Wait until tomorrow

Grammar Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

As frases de cada etapa de uma ligação + Can/Could.

Phone Phrases & Can/Could
ETAPA FRASE
Answer
(atender)		Hello, this is + nome + speaking.
Ask for someone
(pedir alguém)		Could I speak to + nome?
Wait
(aguardar)		Could you hold on a moment?
Audio check
(checar som)		Can you hear me? / Let me unmute.

✗ "Wait!" / "Who are you?" (direto demais)

✓ "Could you hold on, please?" / "Who is calling, please?"

Quiz Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situação de chamada.

You answer the office phone. What do you say?
A "Yes? Who?"
B "Hello, this is Rafael Gasparello Lima speaking."
C "Talk."
You need a moment to find a document. What do you say?
A "Could you hold on a moment, please?"
B "Wait there."
C "Stop talking."
On a video call, the other person cannot hear you. What is the likely problem?
A "I hang up."
B "I am on mute — let me unmute."
C "I dial the number."
The connection is bad. What do you say?
A "Your phone is broken."
B "Sorry, the connection is bad. Could you call back?"
C "I cannot."

Reflection Stage 5: Free Production

Grave sua resposta livremente. Não há certo ou errado — o objetivo é praticar.

Record a short phone call out loud in English. Answer professionally (Hello, this is...), ask to speak to someone or take a message, make one polite request (Could you...), and end the call politely (Thank you for calling. Goodbye).
