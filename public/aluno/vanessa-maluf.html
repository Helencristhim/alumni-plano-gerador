Lesson 1

Diagnostic Session + Personal & Professional Introduction

Objetivo Depois desta aula, voce sera capaz de se apresentar profissionalmente em ingles, descrevendo sua funcao na Braskem com vocabulario corporativo adequado.

Vocabulario

deal with lidar com "I deal with corporate contracts on a daily basis."
liaise with fazer interface com "I liaise with our international partners regularly."
oversee supervisionar "I oversee the compliance department."
ensure compliance garantir conformidade "My primary role is to ensure compliance with regulations."
stakeholder parte interessada "We report directly to key stakeholders."
accountable for responsavel por "I am accountable for all corporate governance matters."
regulatory framework arcabouco regulatorio "We operate within a complex regulatory framework."

Professional Identity in English: Talking About Your Role

Objetivo Depois desta aula, voce sera capaz de descrever suas rotinas profissionais e projetos em andamento usando present simple e present continuous com precisao.

Vocabulario

in charge of responsavel por "I am in charge of the compliance team."

current priorities prioridades atuais "My current priorities include contract reviews."

Lesson 3

Listening Lab 1: Tuning Into British English

Objetivo Depois desta aula, voce sera capaz de identificar padroes de connected speech no ingles britanico e melhorar sua compreensão auditiva em contextos corporativos.

Vocabulario

connected speech fala conectada "British speakers use a lot of connected speech in meetings."
linking ligacao entre palavras "Linking makes words flow together naturally."
elision elisao "Elision is when sounds are dropped in fast speech."
weak forms formas fracas "Function words often have weak forms in natural speech."
catch (a phrase) captar/entender "I couldn't catch what he said in the meeting."
gist essencia/ideia geral "Try to get the gist before focusing on details."

Lesson 4

Meetings in English: Opening, Managing and Closing

Objetivo Depois desta aula, voce sera capaz de conduzir uma reuniao em ingles com naturalidade — abrindo, gerenciando e encerrando com linguagem funcional adequada.

Vocabulario

set the agenda definir a pauta "Let's set the agenda for today's meeting."
move on to passar para "Shall we move on to the next item?"
wrap up encerrar/concluir "I think we can wrap up here."
address the issue abordar a questao "We need to address the issue of compliance delays."
reach a consensus chegar a um consenso "Can we reach a consensus on this matter?"
action items itens de acao "Let me summarise the action items from today."
take the minutes redigir a ata "Who will take the minutes today?"

Pronunciation Deep-Dive 1: Vowel Sounds & Word Stress

Objetivo Depois desta aula, voce sera capaz de identificar e produzir contrastes vocalicos-chave e aplicar padroes de acentuacao em vocabulario corporativo.

Vocabulario

minimal pairs pares minimos "Ship and sheep are minimal pairs."
word stress acento tonico "Word stress can change the meaning of a word."
sentence stress acento frasal "Sentence stress helps emphasise key information."
vowel sound som vocalico "English has many more vowel sounds than Portuguese."
intelligibility inteligibilidade "Good pronunciation improves your intelligibility."
intonation entonacao "Intonation patterns differ between UK and US English."

Survival Card — Frases Progressivas

  • Basico"Could you repeat that, please?"
  • Basico"I'm sorry, I didn't catch that."
  • Inter."Could you rephrase that? I want to make sure I understand."
  • Inter."Let me check I've understood correctly..."
  • Avanc."I'd like to come back to the point you raised about..."
  • Avanc."If I may, I'd like to add a legal perspective on that."
  • Expert"To summarise the key takeaways from a compliance standpoint..."
  • Expert"I'd suggest we align on the regulatory implications before proceeding."
