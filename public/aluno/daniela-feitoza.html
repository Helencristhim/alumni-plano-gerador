5 Frases de Emergência para Eventos

Essas frases salvam qualquer situação em eventos internacionais. Ouça cada uma e tente repetir em voz alta.

Hi, I'm Daniela. Nice to meet you.

Sorry, could you say that again?

Vocabulário — Articles & Rhythm Vocabulário

the (definite)·o/a (específico)
“The IT department is on the third floor.”
a/an (indefinite)·um/uma (geral)
“I need a new laptop for the project.”

“The stress falls on the second syllable: im-PLE-ment.”
rhythm·ritmo
“English has a different rhythm than Portuguese.”
syllable·sílaba
“The word 'technology' has four syllables.”
intonation·entonação
“Rising intonation at the end makes it a question.”
