Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Giving Opinions

Grammar

Para dar opiniao, comece com I think + frase, ou In my opinion + frase. Para concordar, use I agree. Para discordar, use I don't agree (nunca "I am agree"). / To give an opinion use I think or In my opinion. To agree, I agree. To disagree, I don't agree.

ConcordarI agree. / You are right.Eu concordo. / Voce esta certo.

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Comparatives

Grammar

Para comparar dois numeros, adicione -er a palavra e use than: big -> bigger than, high -> higher than. Palavras curtas como big dobram a ultima letra (bigger). Nunca use "more big". / To compare two numbers, add -er and use than. Short words double the last letter.

WordComparativePortugues
bigbigger thanmaior que
highhigher thanmais alto que
smallsmaller thanmenor que
ExampleThe total is bigger than last month.O total e maior que o mes passado.
