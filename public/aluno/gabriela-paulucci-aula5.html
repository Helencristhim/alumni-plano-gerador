GRAMMAR

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip — Everything You Know

Todas as estruturas que você aprendeu até agora:

Structure Example Lesson
I am + noun/adj I am Gabriela. I am 33. 1
This/That is + a + noun This is my phone. 2
I + verb (base) I wake up at 7. I eat breakfast. 3
He/She + verb + s She lives in Jaú. He works. 4

Dica: Você já sabe 4 estruturas em inglês! Isso é mais do que muita gente que estuda há meses.

A “I wake up at 7.”

C “My mother lives in Jaú.”
2. Someone points at your phone and asks “What is this?” Which is correct?
A “This is my phone. It is black.”
B “That is phone.”
C “I am phone.”
3. Which sentence about your father is correct?
A “He work at a hospital.”
B “He works at a hospital.”
C “He am working at a hospital.”
4. Which time expression is correct?

B “I eat breakfast at the morning.”
