Chapter 3 -- The Toolkit

Yourself, your experience, your strengths, your goal

The Toolkit

Your Answer Formula

QuestionHow to answer
Tell me about yourself.My background is in... and I have worked in...
What have you done?I have managed... / I have built... (present perfect)
What is your strength?My biggest strength is communication.
What is your weakness?To be honest, I am too dedicated.
Why do you want this job?Because I love a challenge.

Key idea: for your past work, use the present perfect (I have managed, I have built). Be honest, stay confident, and give one clear example for each answer.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I have did many projects. I am strength in communication."
"I have done many projects. My strength is communication."

For past work, use the present perfect with the right participle (have done, not "have did"). Strength and weakness are nouns: my strength is, not "I am strength".

Practice
