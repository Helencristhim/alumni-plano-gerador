SubjectVerb "to be"Example
IamI am Elaine.
You / We / TheyareWe are travelers.
He / She / ItisShe is from London.
Common Mistake

Watch Out!

"I have 61 years."
"I am 61 years old."

In English, we use "to be" for age, not "to have."
