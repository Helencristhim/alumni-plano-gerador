Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto abaixo. As palavras em negrito são o vocabulário desta aula. Preste atenção em como dizemos horas e preços em inglês.

Marlene is at the airport. She checks the clock. It is half past one. Her flight is at a quarter to three. She needs to find gate fourteen.

She stops at a shop. "How much is this?" The price is twenty-five dollars. She also buys a coffee for three dollars and fifty cents. The total number is twenty-eight dollars and fifty cents.

At gate fourteen, she hears: "Flight 472, boarding in fifteen minutes." One hundred passengers are waiting. Marlene is ready!

1. What time is Marlene's flight? (A que horas é o voo da Marlene?)
A At half past one
B At a quarter to three
C At three o'clock
2. How much is the coffee? (Quanto custa o café?)
A Twenty-five dollars
B Three dollars and fifty cents
C Twenty-eight dollars
3. How do you say "2:45" in English? (Como se diz "2:45" em inglês?)
A A quarter to three
B A quarter past two
C Half past two

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Telling Time + Asking Prices (Como dizer as horas + Perguntar preços)

Time (Hora)How to Say (Como dizer)Example (Exemplo)
3:00three o'clock"It is three o'clock."
3:15a quarter past three"It is a quarter past three."
3:30half past three"It is half past three."
2:45a quarter to three"It is a quarter to three."

Asking Prices (Perguntando preços):

Question (Pergunta)TraduçãoAnswer (Resposta)
How much is this?Quanto custa isso?"It is twenty-five dollars."
What time is it?Que horas são?"It is half past two."
What time is your flight?A que horas é seu voo?"My flight is at three o'clock."

Números importantes (Key numbers):

10 = ten, 15 = fifteen, 20 = twenty, 25 = twenty-five, 30 = thirty, 45 = forty-five, 50 = fifty, 100 = one hundred

Erro comum / Common mistake:

It is fifteen to three. ✗

It is a quarter to three. ✓ (15 min = a quarter)
