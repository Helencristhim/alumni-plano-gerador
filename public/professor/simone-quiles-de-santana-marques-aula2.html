Grammar Discovery

Present Simple + Adverbs of Frequency

AdverbFrequencyPosition
always100%Subject + adverb + verb
usually80%Subject + adverb + verb
often60%Subject + adverb + verb
sometimes40%Subject + adverb + verb
rarely10%Subject + adverb + verb
never0%Subject + adverb + verb

Key rule: Adverb goes BETWEEN the subject and the main verb. With "be": Subject + be + adverb ("She is always on time.").

Chapter 3 -- The Code

Watch Out!

"She reviews always her emails."

adverb after verb = incorrect

"She always reviews her emails."

Subject + adverb + verb = correct

With "be":

"She always is on time."

"She is always on time."
