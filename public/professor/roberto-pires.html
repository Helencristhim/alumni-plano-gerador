Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Past Simple -- Irregular Verbs = Verbos irregulares NÃO adicionam -ed. Cada um tem sua própria forma no passado.

Base FormPast FormExample
gowent"He went to the desk."
taketook"Someone took the wrong bag."
leaveleft"He left it on the plane."
comecame"The bag came on the next flight."
saysaid"The agent said they found it."
bringbrought"They brought it to the hotel."
loselost"He lost his suitcase."
findfound"They found the bag."

Negative: did not + base form (verbo na forma base)

"He did not find his bag." (NOT "He did not found")

Question: Did + subject + base form

"Did he find his bag?" (NOT "Did he found")

Common Mistake / Erro Comum:

"He didn't found his bag."

"He didn't find his bag."

Depois de did/didn't, o verbo volta para a forma base. NUNCA use a forma irregular depois de did.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Present Continuous = ações acontecendo AGORA, neste momento / actions happening NOW, at this moment

Structure: Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing

FormStructureExample
AffirmativeSubject + am/is/are + verb-ing"I am staying" / "He is waiting" / "They are checking"
NegativeSubject + am/is/are + not + verb-ing"I'm not waiting."
QuestionAm/Is/Are + subject + verb-ing?"Are you staying at this hotel?"

Contractions: I am staying → I'm staying | He is waiting → He's waiting | They are checking → They're checking

Present Simple vs Present Continuous:

Present Simple (hábito/rotina): "I stay at hotels when I travel."

Present Continuous (agora): "I am staying at Hotel Arts tonight."

Common Mistake / Erro Comum:

"I staying at the hotel."

"I am staying at the hotel."

Precisa do am/is/are antes do verbo + -ing. Nunca omita o verbo auxiliar.

Present Simple vs Continuous

Simple vs Continuous

HABIT: "I stay at hotels when I travel."
NOW: "I am staying at Hotel Arts tonight."

Simple = always, usually, every day | Continuous = now, right now, at this moment

Common Mistake

Watch Out!

"I staying at the hotel."
"I am staying at the hotel."

You need am/is/are before the -ing verb. Never omit it!
