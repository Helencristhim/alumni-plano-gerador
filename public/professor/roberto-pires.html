A He pays with his card.
B He does not pay.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Present Simple for Transactions = frases do dia a dia para comprar e pagar

EnglishPortuguêsExample
It costs...Quanto custa..."It costs ten euros."
How much is...?Quanto é...?"How much is this scarf?"
I want... / I need... / I have...Eu quero / preciso / tenho"I want to pay in euros."
Polite RefusalsRecusas educadas"No, thank you." / "That is too expensive." / "I will think about it."

Common Mistake / Erro Comum:

"How much it costs?"

"How much does it cost?"

Em perguntas com "how much", use "does" + verbo sem "s". A ordem é: How much + does + it + cost?

Pronunciation Tip / Dica de Pronúncia:

thirTEEN (13) = stress no final / THIRty (30) = stress no início

A diferença entre -teen e -ty é a sílaba tônica. Pratique: fourTEEN vs FORty, fifTEEN vs FIFty.
