Speaking

Oral Drilling

1. You arrived at the airport. What did you do first?
"I checked in at the counter."
2. The agent needed identification. What happened?
"I showed my passport at the counter."
3. Security was slow. How long did you wait?
"I waited in line for twenty minutes."
4. After security, where did you go?
"I walked to gate B7."
5. The gate changed. How did you find out?
"They announced a gate change."
6. Did the flight leave on time?
"The flight departed on time."
