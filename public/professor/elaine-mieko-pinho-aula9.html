Promessa do Programa

"De 'me sinto muito insegura' para 'eu não desisto -- e agora eu consigo.'"

Forças

  • Persistência -- não desiste facilmente
  • Auto-monitoramento -- sabe onde erra
  • Clareza diagnóstica -- identifica suas dificuldades
  • Vocabulário de busca ativa -- pesquisa por conta própria
  • Engajamento social -- gosta de interagir com pessoas

Pontos de Melhoria

  • Pronúncia e entonação -- dificuldade com sons do inglês
  • Vocabulário de viagem -- limitado para situações reais
  • Fluência oral -- demora para formular frases
  • Experiência com aula individual -- primeira vez em formato 1:1
  • Confiança ao falar -- sente-se insegura ao se expressar

Restaurant Basics -- Ordering Food

Read a menu and order food and drinks politely: menu, waiter, appetizer, main course, dessert, the check, tip. Structures: "I would like..." and "Could I have...?"

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Menu -- the list of food and drinks you can order
"Could I have the menu, please?" (Cardápio)
Waiter -- the person who brings your food
"The waiter brings the menu." (Garçom)
Appetizer -- a small dish you eat before the main dish
"For the appetizer, I would like soup." (Entrada)
Main Course -- the biggest and most important dish
"For the main course, the chicken." (Prato principal)
Dessert -- something sweet you eat at the end
"For dessert, the chocolate cake." (Sobremesa)
The Check -- the paper that shows how much you pay
"Could I have the check, please?" (A conta)
Tip -- extra money you leave for good service
"I leave a tip for the waiter." (Gorjeta)
Order -- to ask for food in a restaurant
"I am ready to order." (Pedir)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Elaine arrives at the Grand Central Bistro. She has a reservation. The waiter brings the menu. Could I have a sparkling water, please? she asks. For the appetizer, I would like the tomato soup. For the main course, I would like the grilled chicken. At the end, she orders a chocolate cake for dessert. The food is delicious. Could I have the check, please? she says. She pays and leaves a tip for the waiter.

1. Qual estrutura Elaine usa para pedir o prato principal? / Which structure does Elaine use to order the main course?
A There is the grilled chicken.
B I would like the grilled chicken.
C Could you the grilled chicken?
2. Como Elaine pede a conta? / How does Elaine ask for the check?
A I would like the check.
B Could I have the check, please?
C There is the check.
3. O que Elaine deixa para o garçom no final? / What does Elaine leave for the waiter at the end?
A A tip.
B A menu.
C A reservation.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Two Ways to Order

GRAMMAR

Duas formas educadas de pedir em um restaurante. / Two polite ways to order in a restaurant.

Structure / Estrutura Use / Uso Example / Exemplo
I would like + noun Pedir comida e bebida / Order food and drinks I would like the soup, please.
Could I have + noun? Pedir o cardápio, a conta, água / Ask for the menu, the check, water Could I have the menu, please?
please Sempre no final, para ser educado / Always at the end, to be polite I would like a coffee, please.

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

A "Could I have the menu, please?"
B "There is a menu."
C "I am the menu."
The waiter asks: "Are you ready to order?" You want the soup. What do you say? / O garçom pergunta se você quer pedir. Você quer a sopa. O que você diz?
A "There is the soup."
B "I would like the tomato soup, please."
C "Could you the soup?"
You finished your dinner and you want to pay. What do you say? / Você terminou o jantar e quer pagar. O que você diz?
A "I would like to pay the menu."
B "There is the check."
C "Could I have the check, please?"
You want a glass of water. What is the most polite option? / Você quer um copo d'água. Qual é a opção mais educada?
A "Water."
B "Give me water."
C "Could I have a glass of water, please?"
