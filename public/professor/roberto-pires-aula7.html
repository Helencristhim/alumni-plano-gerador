Dialogue

Roberto meets Maria on the street

R
"Excuse me, could you tell me how to get to the Gothic Quarter?"
M
"Of course! Go straight for two blocks."
R
"Two blocks straight. And then?"
M
"Then turn left at the big intersection. You will see a church on the corner."

Spot the Error

Find the Mistake

"Go to straight for two blocks."
"Go straight for two blocks." (no "to" after "go")
"The metro run every five minutes."
"The metro runs every five minutes." (runs = 3rd person -s)
"How I get to the station?"
"How do I get to the station?" (add "do" for questions)
"Turn to left at the corner."
"Turn left at the corner." (no "to" before "left")
"Where I get off?"
"Where do I get off?" (add "do" for questions)
