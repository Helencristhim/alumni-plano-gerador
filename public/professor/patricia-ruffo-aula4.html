Chapter 3 -- The Code

Grammar Discovery

What do the green words have in common?

She said: "The results are promising." → She said that the results were promising.
He told me: "We need more data." → He told me that they needed more data.
They mentioned: "The deadline is Friday." → They mentioned that the deadline was Friday.
Patricia reported: "We have completed phase one." → Patricia reported that they had completed phase one.

Reported Speech -- Backshift

Direct SpeechReported SpeechExample
Present SimplePast Simple"are" → were
Present PerfectPast Perfect"have completed" → had completed
willwould"will present" → would present
cancould"can do" → could do

Key rule: Reported Speech shifts tenses back: present becomes past, past becomes past perfect, will becomes would. Use reporting verbs: said (that), told (+ object), mentioned (that), reported (that).

Chapter 3 -- The Code

Watch Out!

"He told that we need more data."

told without object + no backshift = incorrect

"He told us that we needed more data."

told + object + backshift = correct

Reporting Verbs

VerbStructureExample
saidsaid (that) + clauseShe said that the results were promising.
toldtold + person + (that) + clauseHe told me that they needed more data.
mentionedmentioned (that) + clauseThey mentioned that the deadline was Friday.
reportedreported (that) + clauseShe reported that they had completed phase one.

Chapter 4 -- The Meeting
Project Update Email
From: Patricia Ruffo (p.ruffo@abbott.com)
To: Abbott Stakeholders
Date: November 28, 2026
Subject: Glycemic Control Project -- Progress Update

Dear Stakeholders,

I would like to update you on the glycemic control project. Last month, we reached an important milestone: we completed the first phase of our clinical trial with 120 participants.

Dr. Nakamura reported that the preliminary results showed a significant correlation between supplement dosage and glycemic response. The feedback from the review committee was very positive.

The next deliverable is a comprehensive progress report. The deadline is December 15th. I will summarize our findings and highlight the key results for all stakeholders.

Best regards,
Patricia Ruffo

3. When is the deadline?
December 15th.

Chapter 5 -- Practice

Spot the Error

Click each sentence to see the correction

"She said that the results are promising."
"She said that the results were promising." (present → past in reported speech)
"He told that we need more data."
"He told us that we needed more data." (told needs an object + backshift)
"They mentioned that the deadline will be Friday."
"They mentioned that the deadline would be Friday." (will → would in reported speech)
"Patricia reported that they have completed phase one."
"Patricia reported that they had completed phase one." (present perfect → past perfect)
"He said me that the feedback was positive."
"He told me that the feedback was positive." (said doesn't take a personal object)
