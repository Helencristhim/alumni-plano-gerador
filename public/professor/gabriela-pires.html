Cracking the Code

Demonstratives: this, that, these, those

The Code

Look at the pattern

This is my book. (one book, near me)
That is your book. (one book, far from me)
These are my books. (many books, near me)
Those are your books. (many books, far from me)

What do the pink words have in common?

Near (close to me)Far (over there)
Singular (1)this + isthat + is
Plural (2+)these + arethose + are
The Code

Common Mistake

"This are my books."
"These are my books."

THIS = singular (one) + IS   |   THESE = plural (many) + ARE

The Code

Complete with this, that, these, those

___This is my pen. (near, one)
___That is my school over there. (far, one)
___These are my friends. (near, many)
___Those are my old photos. (far, many)
___This is a good school. (near, one)
The Code

Articles: a, an, the

I am a student. (any student -- first mention)
I have an English class. (before vowel sound)

SubjectVerbExample

Common Mistake

Always100%
