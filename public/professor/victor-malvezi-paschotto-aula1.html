Chapter 3

The Grammar Engine

Present Perfect vs Simple Past in your career story

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I joined Brink's in 2022 as a Growth Manager."

"I have overseen multiple growth projects since then."

"Last quarter, we exceeded our target by twelve percent."

"We have made headway on the new security platform."

StructureUseExample
Simple PastCompleted action at a specific timeI joined Brink's in 2022.
Present PerfectExperience/result connected to nowI have overseen multiple projects.
Key signal: Pastin 2022, last quarter, yesterdayWe exceeded our target last quarter.
Key signal: Perfectsince, for, already, recently, so farWe have made headway since January.
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"I work here since 2022."

✓ RIGHT

"I have worked here since 2022."

✗ WRONG

"I already carried out the project."

✓ RIGHT

"I have already carried out the project."

✗ WRONG

"I worked at Brink's for 2022."

✓ RIGHT

"I joined Brink's in 2022."

Use "since" with present perfect for a starting point. Use "in" with simple past for a specific time. "For" describes duration, not a specific year.

The Boardroom

Meeting your new international colleague

Dialogue

Meeting at Brink's

S
Hi there! I am Sarah Mitchell, the new Regional Director from our US headquarters. I just flew in from Dallas.
V
Welcome to Brazil, Sarah! I am Victor Paschotto. I am the Growth Manager here.
S
Great to meet you! I have heard a lot about the Brazil operation. What exactly does your role involve?
V
I oversee growth initiatives for the LATAM region. My role involves developing strategies and working closely with stakeholders across different markets.
S
Impressive! Before Brink's, I spent eight years at Garda World. How long have you been with the company?
V
I joined in 2022. Since then, I have carried out several expansion projects, and we have made headway on new KPIs for the region.
S
That is exactly the kind of leadership we need across regions. I look forward to collaborating.
V
Likewise, Sarah! Let me know if you need anything while you are here. I will send you the key deliverables for Q3.
