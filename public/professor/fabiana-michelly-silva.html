Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Look at each word, its definition, and the example. Tap Listen — hearing the word helps your memory.

Supply Chain·The network of companies involved in producing and delivering products — Cadeia de suprimentos
"Our supply chain covers five countries in Latin America."
Vendor·A company or person that sells goods or services to a business — Fornecedor
"We work with fifteen different vendors for our equipment."
Procurement·The process of finding and purchasing equipment or supplies — Aquisição/Compras
"The procurement process takes about three weeks."
Stakeholder·A person who has an interest in a company's decisions — Parte interessada
"Every stakeholder received a copy of the quarterly report."
Responsible·Having the duty to deal with something — Responsável
"She is responsible for the entire logistics operation."
Currently·At the present time; right now — Atualmente
"I am currently managing a new project."
Global Operations·Business activities that happen across multiple countries — Operações globais
"Bloomberg has global operations in over 170 countries."
Deal with·To handle or manage a situation or task — Lidar com
"I deal with vendor contracts every single day."

Grammar Tip

Grammar

Present Simple for Job Descriptions

FormStructureExample
AffirmativeSubject + base verbI work at Bloomberg. / She works in supply chain.
NegativeSubject + do/does + not + base verbI do not deal with clients. / She does not work in marketing.
QuestionDo/Does + subject + base verb?Do you work with vendors? / Does she manage the team?

Key expressions:

  • "I work at..." — descrever onde trabalha
  • "I am responsible for..." — descrever responsabilidades
  • "I deal with..." — descrever com o que lida no dia a dia
  • "My role involves..." — descrever o que o cargo envolve
  • "I report to..." — descrever para quem reporta
Note: Use "am responsible for" (NOT "am responsible to do").
Note: "My role involves + -ing" (gerund): "My role involves managing..."
