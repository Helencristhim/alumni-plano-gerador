Chapter 3

The Engine

Present simple: am, is, are, have, do

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I am an organized person."

"I have a calm morning routine."

"I do not like to speak fast."

"She works with people every day."

Subjectto beExample
IamI am organized.
He / She / Itis (verb + s)She works with people.
You / We / TheyareWe are ready.
Negative / QuestionI do not like... / Do you like...?
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"I have 46 years."

✓ RIGHT

"I am 46 years old."

✗ WRONG

"She work with people."

✓ RIGHT

"She works with people."

✗ WRONG

"I am agree with you."

✓ RIGHT

"I agree with you."

For age, use "I am ... years old." For he/she/it, add "-s" to the verb. "Agree" is a verb, so we never say "am agree."

Dialogue

Nice to Meet You

D
Hi! I am Daniel. I do not think we have met. What is your name?
V
Hi, Daniel. My name is Vanessa. Nice to meet you.
D
Nice to meet you too, Vanessa. So, what do you do?
V
I work with people, and I usually plan my day in the morning. I am quite organized.
D
That sounds great. And why are you learning English?
V
Well, I sometimes feel nervous when I speak. My goal is to feel confident and speak clearly.
D
That is a wonderful goal. And honestly, you sound very clear to me already.
V
Thank you, Daniel. That means a lot to me.
