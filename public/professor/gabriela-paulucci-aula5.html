Chapter 3

The Code

Everything Together

Grammar Review

All Four Structures

Lesson 1: I am Gabriela. (to be)
Lesson 2: This is my phone. (this/that)
Lesson 3: I wake up at 7. (present simple I/you)
Lesson 4: She lives in Jaú. (present simple he/she + s)
LessonStructureExample
1I am + nounI am Gabriela. I am 33.
2This/That is + nounThis is my phone. That is your bag.
3I + verb (base)I wake up at 7. I eat breakfast.
4He/She + verb + sShe lives in Jaú. He works.
