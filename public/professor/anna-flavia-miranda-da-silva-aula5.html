Stage 1.4: Grammar Review

Grammar

Revisão de todas as regras gramaticais das aulas 1-4.

All Grammar Rules -- Todas as Regras
AulaStructureExample
1Present Simple (+)I work. She works.
2Present Simple (-/?)I do not work. Does she work?
3There is/There areThere is a desk. There are folders.
4PossessivesMy team. His office. Her name.

Most Common Mistakes / Erros Mais Comuns:

I works. ✗ I work. ✓ (sem -s com I)

She do not. ✗ She does not. ✓ (does com she)

There is two. ✗ There are two. ✓ (are com plural)

He name. ✗ His name. ✓ (possessivo, não sujeito)
