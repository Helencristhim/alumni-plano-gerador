Chapter 3

The Code

Where is ...? / Go / Turn

Spot the Pattern

Asking & Giving

Where is the bank?
Go straight.
Turn left.
It's next to the pharmacy.

How do we ask and give directions?

UseStructureExample
Ask whereWhere is + the + place?Where is the bank?
Give: forwardGo straightGo straight on this street.
Give: turnTurn left / rightTurn right at the corner.
Give: positionIt's next to / on the corner / nearIt's next to the bank.

Rule: ask with Where is the ...?; give directions with Go / Turn + left / right / straight.

Careful!

Common Mistake

Where is bank?
Where is the bank?
Turn to left.
Turn left.
