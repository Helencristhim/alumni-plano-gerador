The Code

Discover the grammar of routines

Look at the Pattern

I usually check my emails in the morning.
She always arrives early.
I am always busy on Mondays.
We never skip the morning meeting.

Where do the blue words go in the sentence?

PositionRuleExample
Before main verbSubject + adverb + verbI usually check
After BESubject + BE + adverbI am always busy
Frequency scalealways > usually > often > sometimes > rarely > never

Frequency Scale

always (100%)
usually (85%)
often (70%)
sometimes (50%)
rarely (15%)
never (0%)

Common Mistakes

"I go always to the office."
"I always go to the office."
"He never is late for meetings."
"He is never late for meetings."

Grammar Practice
