Present Continuous

We use it for actions happening now or around now

FormStructureExample
Affirmativeam/is/are + verb-ingI am working on a project.
Negativeam/is/are + not + verb-ingShe is not staying long.
Questionam/is/are + subject + verb-ingAre you enjoying it?

Key Rule: For questions, put am/is/are BEFORE the subject.
"Are you working today?" / "What is she presenting?"

Common Mistake

"What you are doing this week?"
"What are you doing this week?"

In questions, put am/is/are before the subject.
