Chapter 5 -- Practice

Spot the Error

Click on each sentence to see the correction

"I work here since 2005."
"I have worked here since 2005." (L1: Present Perfect for duration)
"If we will change the formula, results improve."
"If we change the formula, the results will improve." (L2: First Conditional)
"If we would have more data, we would publish."
"If we had more data, we would publish." (L3: Second Conditional)
"She told that the deadline was Friday."
"She told us that the deadline was Friday." (L4: told + object)
"The moderator asked each delegates to present."
"The moderator asked each delegate to present." (L5: each + singular)

"I see your point, but based on the evidence, our current methodology has been peer-reviewed and validated."
