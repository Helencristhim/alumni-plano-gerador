The Code

What Do You Notice?

I wake up at 5 AM. I have breakfast at 6:30.
She wakes up at 5 AM. She has breakfast at 6:30.
We drive to work. They have meetings.
He drives to work. He has a meeting at 9.

What do the teal words have in common? Look at I vs She.

SubjectVerbExample
I / You / We / Theybase verbI wake up
He / She / Itverb + sShe wakes up

Common Mistake

She wake up at 5 AM.
She wakes up at 5 AM.
He have breakfast.

For he/she/it, always add -s. Special: have → has
