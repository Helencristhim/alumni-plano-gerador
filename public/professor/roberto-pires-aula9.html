Find the Mistake

"Barcelona is more cheaper than Paris."

"Paris is the most beautiful city in Europe." (superlative = most)
"French culture is eleganter than Spanish culture."
"French culture is more elegant than Spanish." (long adj = more + adj)
"This is the more worst hotel I have ever seen."
"This is the worst hotel I have ever seen." (bad → worst, no double)

Cultural Contrasts

Spain vs France

Tipping: In Spain, tipping is optional. In France, service is included but small tips are appreciated.
Compare: "Tipping in Spain is more relaxed than in France."
Restaurant service: Spanish waiters are casual and friendly. French waiters follow formal service rules.
Compare: "French waiters are more formal than Spanish waiters."
Dining pace: In Barcelona, dinner starts at 9-10 PM. In Paris, restaurants close earlier.
Compare: "Dinner in Barcelona starts later than in Paris."
