Grammar Rule

Future Plans with be + -ing

FormExample
Plan (I)I'm meeting Lucas at four.
Plan (we / they)We're going to the movies.
Plan (he / she)Lucas is coming too.
Suggest (idea)Let's meet up at the cafe.
Suggest (question)Why don't we get together?

Key idea: for a future plan you already arranged, use be + verb-ing with a time or day. To suggest an idea, use Let's + verb or Why don't we + verb?

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I meet her tomorrow. Let's to go to the cafe."
"I'm meeting her tomorrow. Let's go to the cafe."

For a future plan, use be + verb-ing. After Let's, use the simple verb (Let's go).

Grammar Practice

Future or Suggestion?

"I _____'m meeting Lucas at four." (a plan, the verb meet)
"We _____'re going to the movies at six." (a plan, the verb go)
"_____Let's meet up at the cafe." (suggest an idea)
"_____Why don't we get together on Saturday?" (suggest with a question)
