Chapter 3 -- If, Unless, Provided

If & Unless

How to say on what condition something happens

Listen and Notice

What Do They Share?

Look at the highlighted words. What do they tell us?

"The contract terminates if a party does not pay."

"We wait, unless the partner agrees."

"We accept the waiver provided it is in writing."

"If the partner agrees, we enforce the contract from June."

The Condition Code

Conditional Clauses

WordMeaningExample
ifthe condition"the contract terminates if a party does not pay"
unless= if not"we wait unless the partner agrees"
providedonly if this is true"we accept it provided it is in writing"
tensepresent, not will"if a party fails" (not "if a party will fail")

Key idea: a conditional clause says ON WHAT CONDITION the result happens. Use the present after if, unless and provided -- never will.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"The contract terminates if a party will not pay. ... We wait unless the partner does not agree."
"The contract terminates if a party does not pay. ... We wait unless the partner agrees."

After if, unless and provided, use the present. And unless already means if not.

Comprehension

About Daniel's Briefing

1. Which provision worries Daniel?

The provision that says the contract ends if either party fails to pay within thirty days. He thinks it is too strict.

2. What does he want to change about the governing law?

The draft follows German jurisdiction, but he would prefer Brazilian courts if they can agree.

3. On what condition does he accept the waiver?

Only provided it is in writing. If the partner agrees to these points, they can sign; unless they agree, they wait.

Artefact

The Conditions Section

SERVICE AGREEMENT -- CONDITIONS

DRAFT

Clause 12 -- Termination

This contract terminates if either party does not pay within thirty days. The contract stays in force unless a party gives written notice.

Clause 14 -- Governing Law

The governing law is German law, and the jurisdiction is the courts of Berlin, provided the parties do not agree on another forum in writing.

Clause 16 -- Waiver and Amendment

A party may amend this contract provided the change is in writing. A waiver is valid only if both parties sign it.

Each party must comply with every provision. The parties enforce this contract from June 1, unless they agree on a later date.

Dialogue

The Conditions Negotiation

S

"Thank you for the draft. The provision that terminates the contract if a party does not pay in thirty days is too strict for us."

P

"I see. What would work for you? We can change it, provided both parties still comply with the payment dates."

S

"Sixty days, provided we add a small interest. And the governing law -- we prefer Brazilian jurisdiction, unless you have a strong reason."

P

"We can accept Brazilian courts, provided the contract is in English. And any waiver is valid only if it is signed by both parties."

S

"Agreed. We amend the contract in writing, and we will not sign unless every change is clear."

P

"Perfect. If we agree on these conditions, we enforce the contract from June. I will send a clean version by Friday."

The contract terminates if a party does not pay within thirty days, and she agrees with Daniel that this is too strict. She will ask for sixty days.

Brazilian courts, unless the partner has a strong reason for German jurisdiction.

About the Negotiation

1. On what condition does the partner agree to change the termination?

Provided both parties still comply with the payment dates.

They enforce the contract from June, and the partner sends a clean version by Friday.
