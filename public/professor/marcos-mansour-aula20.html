Chapter 3

Reported Speech

said / told me / asked if

Grammar Discovery

What Changes When We Report?

He said he would call back.

She told me the meeting was on Friday.

He asked if I was available.

They said they had sent the report.

Direct wordsReported speech
"I will call back."He said he would call back.
"The meeting is on Friday."She told me the meeting was on Friday.
"Are you available?"He asked if I was available.
"I can hear you."She said she could hear me.

say = no person. tell = + person (tell me). For questions: ask if / whether. The verb steps back: is→was, will→would, can→could.

Common Mistakes

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"He said me he was late."

✓ RIGHT

"He told me he was late." (tell + person)

✗ WRONG

"She told that she was busy."

✓ RIGHT

"She told me that she was busy." (tell needs a person)

✗ WRONG

"He asked if I am available."

✓ RIGHT

"He asked if I was available." (verb steps back)

Use tell with a person (told me, told him); use say with no person. In reported speech, the verb usually steps back one tense.

Chapter 4

On the Call

A real phone call, message and all

Dialogue

Passing on a Message

R
Hello, could I speak to Marcos Mansour, please?
M
Marcos speaking. Hi Rachel, good to hear from you. How can I help?
R
I'm calling because David asked me to pass on a message. He said he couldn't join our call today.
M
Oh, I see. Did he say why? Sorry, you're breaking up a little.
R
Can you hear me now? He told me he was stuck in another meeting, and he asked if we could move our call to Friday.
M
Friday works for me. Let me share my screen so we can check the calendar together.
R
You were on mute for a moment, but I can hear you now. Yes, I can see your screen clearly.
M
Great. I'll message David and tell him we've moved the call to Friday. Thanks for letting me know.
Comprehension

Did You Catch It?

1. What message did David ask Rachel to pass on?
He said he couldn't join the call today.
2. Why couldn't David join, and what did he ask for?
He was stuck in another meeting; he asked to move the call to Friday.
3. What two technical problems happened during the call?
Marcos was breaking up, and he was on mute for a moment.
