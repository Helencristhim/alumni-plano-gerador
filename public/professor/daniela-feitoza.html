Series
The Office (US) — any job interview or review scene

Characters constantly talk about their careers and experience. Listen for "I have worked here for..." (experience) versus "I started in..." (exact time) — exactly the contrast from Lesson 11.

Clear, friendly explanations of when to use the present perfect and when to use the past simple, with many real examples about life and work experience.
