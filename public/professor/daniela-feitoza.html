4. Confidence Building

Progressão gradual de situações controladas para espontâneas. Cada aula termina com um “quick win” para reforçar confiança.

5. Simulação de Eventos Reais

Role-plays baseados em conferências SAP, networking events, reuniões com equipes internacionais e entrevistas de emprego.

Grammar/Structure — Article Rules 15 min

WHEN TO USE EACH ARTICLE:

A/AN — First mention, general, one of many
“I work at a company in Sao Paulo.” (any company, first mention)

THE — Specific, already mentioned, unique, superlatives
“The company distributes Nike products.” (specific company, already known)

NO ARTICLE — Plural general, uncountable general, proper nouns
“I work in information technology.” (general field, no article)

AN before vowel SOUNDS — an IT manager, an hour, an MBA
(It is about SOUND, not spelling: “a university” because /j/ sound)

DANIELA'S CONTEXT EXAMPLES:

“I am an IT Manager at the SPF Group.”

“Centauro is the largest retailer in Latin America.” (superlative = the)

“I work in __ Brazil.” (no article with country names)

“I need a new server for the project.” (a = first mention, the = specific)

Grammar/Structure — Superlatives + Company Description 15 min

SUPERLATIVE RULES:

Short adjectives (1-2 syllables): the + adjective + EST

“the largest” / “the biggest” / “the fastest”

Long adjectives (3+ syllables): the + MOST + adjective

“the most important” / “the most successful”

Irregular: the best / the worst / the most / the least

COMPANY DESCRIPTION TEMPLATE:
