Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Aqui está a regra gramatical da aula.

Possessive Adjectives -- Adjetivos Possessivos

Usamos possessivos para dizer DE QUEM é algo. Eles vêm ANTES do substantivo. (We use possessive adjectives before nouns to say who something belongs to.)

SubjectPossessiveExample
ImyMy manager is Ricardo.
YouyourYour office is nice.
HehisHis name is Carlos.
SheherHer department is finance.
WeourOur team is great.
TheytheirTheir desks are here.

Common Mistake / Erro Comum:

He name is Carlos. ✗

His name is Carlos. ✓

Regra: NÃO use o pronome sujeito (he, she) antes de substantivos. Use o possessivo (his, her).

Common Mistake

"He name is Carlos."
"His name is Carlos."

Before a noun, use the possessive (my, his, her),
NOT the subject pronoun (I, he, she).
