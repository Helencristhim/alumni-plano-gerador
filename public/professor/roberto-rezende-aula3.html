Where do the highlighted words go? What is the rule?

PositionRuleExample
Before main verbSubject + adverb + verb"I usually wrap up at six."
After BESubject + BE + adverb"Mondays are always busy."
Frequency scalealways > usually > often > sometimes > rarely > never
Grammar Rule

The Full Picture

Present Simple + AdverbsTime Expressions
UseFacts, routines, habits, schedulesevery Monday, twice a week, once a month
FormI/you/we/they + verb
he/she/it + verb+s		+ adverb before main verb
Positive"I usually update Mr. Zhang.""He coordinates deliveries every week."
Negative"I never postpone client meetings.""She does not commute on Fridays."
Question"Do you always wrap up at six?""How often do you visit clients?"
Common Mistakes

Watch Out

"I always am updating Mr. Zhang on Mondays."
"I always update Mr. Zhang on Mondays." (routine = present simple)
"I wrap up usually around six."
"I usually wrap up around six." (adverb before main verb)
