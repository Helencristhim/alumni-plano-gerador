Chapter 3

The Code

He works, She lives

Grammar Discovery

Find the Pattern

Look at the purple words. What changes when we talk about he or she?

I work at Itau. → She works at Itau.
I live in SP. → He lives in Jaú.
I eat breakfast. → She eats breakfast.
I speak English. → He speaks English.

I / You / We / They = verb   |   He / She / It = verb + S

SubjectVerbExample
I / YouworkI work at Itau.
He / SheworksShe works at Itau.
We / TheyliveThey live in Brazil.
He / ShelivesHe lives in Jaú.
Common Mistake

Watch Out!

“She work at Itau.”
“She works at Itau.”

With he / she / it, ALWAYS add -s to the verb.
I work → She works  |  I live → He lives

Grammar Extra

3rd Person -S Rule

Remember the pattern:

I work → She works
I live → He lives
I speak → She speaks
I eat → He eats
I wake up → She wakes up

With I / You / We / They = verb base (no change).
With He / She / It = verb + S.
