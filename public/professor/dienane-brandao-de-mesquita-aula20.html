Grammar Rule

Phone Phrases

WhoPhraseWhen
CallerCan I speak to + name?To start the call
You answerThis is + name + speaking.To say who you are
You waitHold on, please.To wait a moment
Person is awayCan I take a message?To write the message

Key idea: the caller says Can I speak to + name. You answer with This is + name + speaking. To wait, say Hold on, please. If the person is away, say Can I take a message?

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I am Dienane." (on the phone)
"This is Dienane speaking."

On the phone, say This is ... speaking to say who you are. To ask for a person, say Can I speak to ...?
