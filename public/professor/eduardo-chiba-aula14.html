Chapter 3 -- Passive & Effect

The Demo Code

Describe the product, then link it to a result

Discover the Pattern

What Do the Orange Words Share?

"The account is created automatically."

"The whole flow was designed to be seamless."

"Reports can be exported with one click."

"The data is synced, so nothing is entered twice."

The Rule

Passive & Cause-Effect

ToolFormExample
Passive (present)is / are + past participle"The report is generated automatically."
Passive (past)was / were + past participle"The flow was designed for speed."
Passive + modalcan be / will be + past participle"Reports can be exported in one click."
Cause & effectthis means / as a result / so that / which leads to"It is automated, which leads to faster onboarding."

Key idea: the passive keeps the spotlight on the product, not on the team that built it. Then cause-and-effect language turns a feature into a benefit — the client hears the result, not just the function.

Watch Out

Common Mistake

The account is create automatically.
The account is created automatically.

The passive always needs the PAST PARTICIPLE after be: "is created", not "is create". So it is "is generated", "was designed", "can be exported".

Practice

"The dashboard _____was built around your workflow." (passive, past)
"Files _____can be shared instantly." (passive + modal)
"It is fully automated, _____which leads to fewer errors." (cause & effect)

Eduardo's Demo Script

Quinto Andar — B2B UnitDemo Script

My Rule

Feature, then effect

Never show a button without naming the result it drives

Open: "Let me start with a use case your team knows."

Show: "Every lead is scored in real time."

Link: "This means you always know what to prioritize."

Close: "It is fully integrated, so nothing is entered twice."

Dialogue -- Rehearsing the Demo

Eduardo & Sarah

S

"Let us rehearse the demo. Pretend I am the client. Open the dashboard and show me one thing."

E

"Here on the dashboard, every lead is scored in real time. This means your team knows what to prioritize first."

S

"Good. But does it work with my current system, or do I have to start over?"

E

"It is fully integrated with your CRM, so the data is synced both ways. As a result, nothing is entered twice."

S

"Now that is a good demo. You used the passive to keep the focus on the product, and you linked each feature to a result."

E

"Exactly. Feature, then effect. I show what it does, and then I say what it means for them."
