Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Present Simple = facts, habits, routines (fatos, hábitos, rotinas — coisas permanentes ou regulares)
Present Continuous = actions happening now or around now (ações acontecendo agora ou no período atual)

TenseStructureExample
Present SimpleSubject + verb (base/s)"She works at Houlihan Lokey."
Present ContinuousSubject + am/is/are + verb-ing"She is working on a deal."

Common Mistake:

"She is working at Houlihan Lokey." (para fato permanente)

"She works at Houlihan Lokey." (present simple para fatos permanentes)

Use Present Simple para fatos permanentes e Present Continuous para ações temporárias ou em progresso.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar
Present Perfect / Presente Perfeito

Structure / Estrutura: Subject + have/has + past participle (3rd column)

Affirmative / Afirmativa"We have closed the deal."
Negative / Negativa"We have not closed the deal."
Question / Interrogativa"Have we closed the deal?"
Time Markers / Marcadores Temporais

already = já (frase afirmativa) | yet = já/ainda (frase negativa ou pergunta)
just = acabou de | recently = recentemente
since = desde (ponto no tempo) | for = por/há (duração)
ever = alguma vez | never = nunca

Common Mistake / Erro Comum

"We closed the deal since January."
"We have been working on the deal since January."
Com "since" e "for", use present perfect, nunca past simple.
