Chapter 2: Packing Words

Key Vocabulary (1/2)

A section of an organization responsible for a specific area
Department
A division of a large organization that handles a specific area of work.
“I manage the compliance department at Corinthians.”
The tasks and duties that are part of your job
Responsibilities
The duties and tasks that someone must do as part of their role.
“My responsibilities include enforcing FIFA regulations.”
To be in charge of and make decisions about a team
Manage
To be in charge of and make decisions about a project, team, or department.
“I manage a team of five compliance professionals.”
To have someone as your direct supervisor
Report to
To be directly supervised by someone; to have someone as your boss.
“I report to the General Director of the club.”

Key Vocabulary (2/2)

To organize people or activities so they work together
Coordinate
To organize different people or activities so they work well together.

Chapter 3: The Code

Quick Check

1. “She coordinates with FIFA.” Does she do this one time or regularly?
Regularly. Present Simple = habits and routines.
2. “Does he manage?” Why no -s on manage?
Because DOES already carries the third-person marker.
3. “They investigate violations.” Temporary or permanent?
Permanent. It is part of their job responsibilities.

Chapter 4: Getting There

FIFA Networking Dialogue

L
So, Nilo, what does a typical day look like for you at Corinthians?
N
Well, I manage the compliance department. We have a team of five people who work directly with me.
L
That sounds like a lot of responsibility. Who do you report to?
N
I report to the General Director. I also coordinate with the legal department on investigations.
L
Interesting! And where is your headquarters?
N
Our headquarters is in the Parque Sao Jorge area. But I work from Barra Funda.
L
What is the hardest part of your job?
N
Investigating potential violations is challenging. We enforce FIFA regulations at the club level, and sometimes that creates tension.
