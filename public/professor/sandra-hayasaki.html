Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Comparatives, Superlatives & as...as

Para comparar DUAS coisas, use o comparativo: adjetivo curto + -er than (cheaper than) ou more + adjetivo longo + than (more expensive than). Irregular: good → better / the best; bad → worse / the worst.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Relative Clauses (who / which / that)

GRAMMAR

Para descrever melhor, juntamos duas ideias numa frase só. Usamos who para pessoas, which para coisas e lugares, e that para os dois. A cláusula vem logo depois do substantivo, e não repetimos o sujeito (he/she/it) depois do who/which/that. / To describe better, we join two ideas into one sentence. Use who for people, which for things and places, and that for both. The clause comes right after the noun, and we do not repeat the subject after it.
