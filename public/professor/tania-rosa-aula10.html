After this final lesson, Tânia will confidently handle a full trip from start to finish in English — checking in, getting around, ordering, shopping, talking culture, handling emergencies, and planning with family — combining every structure from the course.

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Read the text and answer the questions.

Tânia is on her last trip of the course. At the airport she says: "Here is my passport. Can you help me with my luggage?" At the hotel: "I would like to check in. I have a reservation." In the city she asks: "Could you tell me the way to the museum?" At the restaurant: "Could I have the menu, please?" She buys a souvenir and asks: "How much is this?" She has been to many landmarks. When she feels sick, she says: "I need to find a pharmacy." Finally she tells her friend: "Let's plan our next trip together!"

1. What does Tânia say at the hotel?
A "Give me a room."
B "I would like to check in. I have a reservation."
C "Where is my passport?"
2. How does Tânia ask for directions?
A "Where museum?"
B "Could you tell me the way to the museum?"
C "I want museum now."
3. What does Tânia say when she feels sick?
A "I want doctor."
B "I need to find a pharmacy."
C "Give me medicine."
