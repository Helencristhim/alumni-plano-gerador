Dialogue

At Lost and Found

R
Excuse me, my luggage is missing. I need to report a lost bag.
A
I am sorry to hear that. Can you describe your suitcase?
R
It is a blue medium suitcase with a black handle.
A
What flight were you on?
R
I was on flight AB4821 from São Paulo.
A
Let me check. We found a bag matching that description.
R
Where did you find it?
A
Someone took the wrong bag by mistake. We brought it to Lost and Found.
R
Thank you so much.
A
You are welcome. Here is your suitcase.

Speaking

Oral Drilling

1. Tell the agent: your bag is missing.
"My luggage did not arrive. I need to report a lost bag."
2. Describe your suitcase.
"It is a blue medium suitcase with a black handle."
3. Someone took your bag. Explain.
"Someone took my bag by mistake."
4. Ask when you will get your bag back.
"When will it come? Can you bring it to my hotel?"
5. Tell the story: what happened at the airport.
"I went to baggage claim. My bag was missing. The agent said someone took it by mistake. They found it at Lost and Found."
