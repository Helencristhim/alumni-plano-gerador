Grammar Discovery

Find the Pattern

Look at the purple words. What do you notice about the verbs with "I"?

I wake up at 7.
I eat breakfast.
I go to work.
I finish at 6.

Present Simple with I / You = verb base (no change!)

SubjectVerbExample
Iwake upI wake up at 7.
IeatI eat breakfast.
IgoI go to work.
IstartI start work at 9.
IfinishI finish at 6.
Common Mistake

Watch Out!

“I am wake up at 7.”
“I wake up at 7.”

With action verbs (wake up, eat, go), you do NOT use "am".
Just: I + verb.
