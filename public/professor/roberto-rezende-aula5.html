Pronunciation

Say It Right

1. How would you update your manager on the pipeline?
"We are currently working on eight active proposals. Three are in the final stage."
2. How would you describe your targets?
"We are targeting five new companies in the agricultural segment this quarter."
3. How would you report on a specific deal?
"The mining company proposal is progressing well. We are estimating a close by end of June."
4. How would you describe your progress?
"We are making good progress. We have converted twelve prospects so far."
5. How would you ask for a report?
"Could you send me the updated pipeline report by Friday?"

The Full Picture

Present ContinuousSimple Present
UseActions happening now or around nowHabits, routines, permanent states
Formam/is/are + verb-ingbase form (+ s for he/she)

Common Mistakes

Watch Out

"We close a deal right now."
"We are closing a deal right now." (current action = are + -ing)
"We is targeting new companies."
"We are targeting new companies." (we = are, NOT is)
