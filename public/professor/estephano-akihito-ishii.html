Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Simple Present -- We use the Simple Present to talk about routines, habits, and things that happen every day.
Presente Simples -- Usamos o Simple Present para falar de rotinas, hábitos e coisas que acontecem todo dia.

SubjectVerbExample
I / You / We / Theyverb (no change)I wake up at 8.
He / She / Itverb + -s / -esHe wakes up at 8.

3rd person -s rule (regra do -s na 3a pessoa):
I study → He studies (y → ies)
I play → She plays
I watch → He watches (add -es after ch, sh, s, x, z)
I have → He has (irregular)

Time expressions (expressões de tempo): in the morning, in the afternoon, at night, every day, usually, always, sometimes
