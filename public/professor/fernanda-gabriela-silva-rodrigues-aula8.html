Chapter 3: The Code

a / an / the & Plurals

a contract · an invoice · the documents

Discover the Grammar

a, an, or the?

"There is a contract on the desk."

"There is an invoice from Brazil."

"The contract is on my desk."

"The documents are ready."

ArticleWhenExample
aone thing, consonant sounda contract
anone thing, vowel soundan invoice
thea specific one we both knowthe contract

a/an = uma coisa nova; the = aquela que ja conhecemos. / a/an = new; the = known.

Wrong vs Right

Chapter 4: On the Desk

What's There?

Daniel asks, Fernanda describes

Dialogue

The New Transaction

D
Hi Fernanda. What is on your desk for the new transaction?
F
There is a new document and there are two invoices.
D
Good. Is the shipment large?
F
Yes, it is large and urgent. We work with a reliable partner.
D
Is there a certificate for the warehouse?
F
Yes, there is. There is also one contract for the local client.
D
Is the contract complex?
F
No, it is small and simple. There is nothing missing.
Comprehension

On the Desk

1. How many invoices are there?
There are two invoices.
2. Is the shipment large or small?
It is large and urgent.
3. Who is the contract for?
For the local client.
