The Code

Past Simple -- regular and irregular verbs

What Do These Have in Common?

I started my career at a small company.
She graduated from university in 2008.
They hired me five years ago.
I transferred to Novartis in 2020.
Base FormPast Simple (+ed)Example
workworkedI worked there for three years.
managemanagedI managed four products.
startstartedI started in 2008.
graduategraduatedI graduated from university.
hirehiredThey hired me immediately.
transfertransferredI transferred to Novartis.
applyappliedI applied for the position.

But Some Verbs Are Different...

These verbs do NOT follow the -ed rule. They are irregular.

Base FormPast SimpleExample
gowentI went to the interview.
havehadI had a great manager.
makemadeI made a big decision.
taketookI took the opportunity.
getgotI got my first job in 2008.
becomebecameI became a project manager.
leaveleftI left my first company.
choosechoseI chose technology.
speakspokeI spoke to the director.
runranI ran the department.

Negative and Question Forms

Negative

I did not apply for the job.

She didn't graduate in 2010.

They didn't hire him.

did not / didn't + base form

Question

Did you graduate in 2008?

Did they hire you?

When did you transfer?

Did + subject + base form?

Common Mistake

I did not applied for the job.
I did not apply for the job.
Rule: did + base form (NOT did + past form)
