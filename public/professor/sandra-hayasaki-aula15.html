Chapter 3 -- The Big Picture

Four Ideas from This Block

The Rule

The Four Ideas, Side by Side

IdeaWhen we use itExample
Could you / Would you minda polite request"Could you clear up this doubt?"
used to + verba past habit that stopped"I used to give one-word answers."
have been + -ingstarted in the past, still going now"I have been practicing every day."
If + present, will + verba real, possible future result"If I keep going, it will grow."

Tip: used to is for the past only. have been + -ing connects the past to now. if + present, will + verb looks to the future. Together, they let you tell the whole story of your journey.

Common Mistake

Past Habit vs Still Going

"I used to practice every day, and I still do."
"I have been practicing every day, and I still do."

Use used to only for a habit that stopped. For something that continues until now, use have been + -ing.
