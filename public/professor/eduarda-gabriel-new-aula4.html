Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Eduarda has been working on a major restructuring case at Houlihan Lokey. The company has filed for bankruptcy under Chapter 11. Eduarda's team has conducted due diligence and has submitted a preliminary proposal to the creditors. They have negotiated the terms for three weeks, but they have not reached an agreement yet. The board wants to raise capital, and the advisory team has already issued a report with three scenarios. Eduarda tells James: "We have closed similar deals before -- this time will not be different."

1. What has the company filed for?
A The company has filed a report.
B The company has filed for bankruptcy under Chapter 11.
C The company filed for a new proposal.
2. Have they reached an agreement yet?
A Yes, they reached an agreement last week.
B No, they have not reached an agreement yet.
C They do not want to reach an agreement.
3. Which verb form appears most in this text?
A Past simple (filed, closed, negotiated).
B Present perfect (has filed, has conducted, have negotiated).
C Present continuous (is filing, is conducting).

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar
Present Perfect / Presente Perfeito

Structure / Estrutura: Subject + have/has + past participle (3rd column)

Affirmative / Afirmativa"We have closed the deal."
Negative / Negativa"We have not closed the deal."
Question / Interrogativa"Have we closed the deal?"
Time Markers / Marcadores Temporais

already = já (frase afirmativa) | yet = já/ainda (frase negativa ou pergunta)
just = acabou de | recently = recentemente
since = desde (ponto no tempo) | for = por/há (duração)
ever = alguma vez | never = nunca

Common Mistake / Erro Comum

"We closed the deal since January."
"We have been working on the deal since January."
Com "since" e "for", use present perfect, nunca past simple.

We have conducted due diligence and have issued the preliminary report. The company has filed for Chapter 11 protection.

Next steps: We need to raise additional capital before we can close the deal.

Best regards,
Eduarda Gabriel
