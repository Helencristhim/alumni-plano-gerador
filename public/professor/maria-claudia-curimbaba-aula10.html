Chapter 3 -- Grammar Deep Dive

Common Mistakes -- Block 1 Highlights

"She oversee the team every day."

Missing third-person -s in present simple

"She oversees the team every day."

Correct: he/she/it + verb + s

"The report was write yesterday."

Wrong: must use past participle in passive

"The report was written yesterday."

Correct: be + past participle

"If we will invest, we will grow."

Wrong: never use will in the if-clause

"If we invest, we will grow."

Correct: If + present simple, will + base verb

Your team finished a project ahead of schedule. Give a summary using "To summarize..." (Present Simple)

"Let's integrate the new platform and evaluate its impact on our workflow."

"If you invest now, you will see a return within six months."

"I would recommend that we evaluate the composition of the current strategy before proceeding."
