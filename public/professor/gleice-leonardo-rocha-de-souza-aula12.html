Chapter 3 -- Future Plans

Talk About Your Plans

Going to -- am / is / are going to + verb

Listen and Notice

Find the Pattern

Look at the orange verbs. What three words come before them?

"I am going to launch my idea next month."

"She is going to present her pitch tomorrow."

"We are going to solve a real problem."

"They are going to help many customers."

The Rule

Going To

SubjectExample (am / is / are + going to + verb)
II am going to launch my idea.
He / She / ItShe is going to present the pitch.
We / You / TheyWe are going to solve the problem.
NegativeHe is not going to wait.
QuestionAre you going to present today?

Key idea: use going to for a plan in the future: am / is / are + going to + verb in the base form. Say "going to launch", not "going to launching". Never forget the verb to be and the word to.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I going to launch my idea. She is going to presenting tomorrow."
"I am going to launch my idea. She is going to present tomorrow."

For plans, use am / is / are + going to + the verb in the base form.

Practice

Say It with Going To

"Next month, I _____am going to launch my idea." (launch)
"She _____is going to present her pitch tomorrow." (present)
"We _____are going to solve the problem." (solve)
"They _____are going to help new customers." (help)
