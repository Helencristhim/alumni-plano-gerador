Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Urgent -- very important and needing fast action
"It is urgent, so please call me today." (Urgente)
Message -- information you leave for someone
"Can I take a message?" (Mensagem / recado)
Schedule -- to arrange a time for something, like a call
"Let us schedule the call for Friday." (Agendar)

Giving a Short Presentation: Describing Your Company & Trends

Preview da aula V4 (reading): o vocabulário de apresentação (increase, decrease, growth, launch, target, highlight, overview, figures) e a linguagem funcional para apresentar números e tendências -- "Let me give you a quick overview", "Sales increased by ten percent", "I would like to highlight...", "Our target for this year is...". Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

Increase -- to go up; to become bigger or more
"Sales increased by ten percent this year." (Aumentar)
Decrease -- to go down; to become smaller or less
"Costs decreased after we changed suppliers." (Diminuir)
Growth -- the process of getting bigger or more successful
"The company had strong growth this year." (Crescimento)
Launch -- to start or introduce a new product; a product start
"The launch of the new lamp was a big success." (Lançar / lançamento)
Target -- a goal or result you want to reach
"Our target for this year is to grow again." (Meta)
Highlight -- to show or point to the most important part
"I would like to highlight our new launch." (Destacar / destaque)
Overview -- a short, general description of a subject
"Let me give you a quick overview of the year." (Visão geral)
Figures -- numbers, especially results or amounts in business
"These figures are better than last year." (Números)

GRAMMAR

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Describing Trends & Presenting

GRAMMAR

Como apresentar números e tendências. / How to present figures and trends.

Structure / EstruturaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
increased / decreased by + valorDescrever alta ou baixa com númeroSales increased by ten percent.
Let me give you a quick overview.Abrir com uma visão geralLet me give you a quick overview.
I would like to highlight...Destacar o ponto principalI would like to highlight our launch.
Our target for this year is...Dizer a metaOur target for this year is to grow.

Atenção: depois de increased/decreased use "by" + o valor -- "increased by ten percent" (nunca "increased in ten percent"). Uma overview é geral; um highlight é um ponto importante.
