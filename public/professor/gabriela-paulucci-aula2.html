Common Mistake

Watch Out!

“This is phone.”
“This is a phone.” / “This is my phone.”

Always use "a/an" or "my/your" before the object.

Grammar Extra

Adjective Order

In English, adjectives go BEFORE the noun:

a big bag
a blue pen
a small red book
a big brown bag

Size comes before color: "a small red book" (not "a red small book").
