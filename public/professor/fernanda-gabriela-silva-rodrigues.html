Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

1. Why do we say "My name is Fernanda"? (Por que usamos "is"?)

2. In "I work at Sucafina", what does "work" tell us? (O que "work" diz?)
A Where she is from. (De onde ela e.)
B Her company / her job. (A empresa / o trabalho dela.)
C Her age. (A idade dela.)
3. "Daniel is a partner." What kind of person is a partner? (O que e um partner?)
A A person who buys from you. (Quem compra de voce.)
B A person you work with. (Quem trabalha com voce.)
C A coffee drink. (Uma bebida de cafe.)
