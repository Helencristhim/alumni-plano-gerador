Chapter 3: Happening Now

Routine vs Right Now

We visit · We are visiting

Grammar Discovery

Routine or Right Now?

"We visit doctors every week." (routine)

"This week, we are visiting doctors in São Paulo." (now)

"The team tracks results." (always)

"Right now, the team is tracking the launch." (now)

Same verb, two times. What word tells you it is happening now?

TimeUse it forExample
Present simple
(we visit)		Your routine and facts that are always true. Signals: every day, every week.We visit doctors every week.
Present continuous
(we are visiting)		What is happening now, this period. Signals: right now, at the moment, this week.This week, we are visiting doctors in São Paulo.
The formam / is / are + verb + -ingI am monitoring · she is running · we are adjusting

Nothing brand new today — you met the -ing in lessons four and six. The skill now is to choose it fast: routine or right now?

The Form

am / is / are + -ing

I · am"I am monitoring the results in real time."
he/she/it · is"The team is running three campaigns."
we/you/they · are"We are adjusting the plan this week."

The -ing never changes. Only am / is / are changes with the subject.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

"We are visit doctors this week."
"We are visiting doctors this week."
"Right now, I monitor the numbers."
"Right now, I am monitoring the numbers."
"My team are running the campaign."
"My team is running the campaign."

For NOW, use am / is / are + -ing: "We are visiting". With "right now", never the simple form. And "team" is singular: "My team is".
