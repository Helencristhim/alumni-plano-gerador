Pronunciation

Say It Right

1. How do you introduce a colleague?
"This is Carlos Oliveira. He is my colleague in sales and specializes in the generator segment."
2. How do you describe who handles logistics?
"Ana Lima handles logistics. She assists with all international shipments."
3. How do you describe where the technical team works?
"We have a branch in São Paulo. The technical team is based there and supports our sales proposals."
4. How do you describe what your department does?
"Our department handles sales for the Brazilian market. We specialize in diesel engines for industrial and agricultural clients."
5. How do you politely ask to introduce someone?
"Could I assist you with that? I can coordinate with the technical team in São Paulo."

Grammar Rule

The Full Picture

Can / Could for RequestsIntroductions
UseRequests, offers, abilitiesPresenting colleagues, describing roles
FormCould/Can + subject + base verb (no TO)This is + name. He/She + role verb
Request"Could you tell me about your department?""This is Maria. She is in charge of..."

Common Mistakes

Watch Out

"Maria is in charge for customer service."
"Maria is in charge of customer service." (in charge OF)
"Could you to introduce me to the team?"
"Could you introduce me to the team?" (could + base form, no TO)
