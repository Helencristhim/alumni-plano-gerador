Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip — Present Simple

Grammar

We use the Present Simple to talk about facts, habits, routines, and professional identity.

Usamos o Present Simple para falar sobre fatos, hábitos, rotinas e identidade profissional.

FormStructureExample
AffirmativeSubject + verb (base/-s)"I work for Braskem." / "She handles contracts."
NegativeSubject + don't/doesn't + verb"I don't work in criminal law."
QuestionDo/Does + subject + verb?"Does she handle compliance?"

Keywords: every day, always, usually, often, sometimes, never, regularly

She work at Braskem.

She works at Braskem.

Na 3ª pessoa (he/she/it), sempre adicione -s ou -es ao verbo.

Do she handles contracts?

Does she handle contracts?

Com does, o verbo volta à forma base (sem -s).
