Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Simple for Professional Routines

Grammar

Present Simple = facts, routines, and habits (Use o Present Simple para fatos, rotinas e hábitos)
Structure: I/You/We/They + verb base | He/She/It + verb + s/es

SubjectVerb Form
I / You / We / Theywork, report, oversee, handle
He / She / Itworks, reports, oversees, handles

Useful Professional Expressions:

  • "I am responsible for..."
  • "My team handles..."
  • "I work closely with..."
  • "I report to..."

Common Mistakes:

"He oversee the process."

"He oversees the process."

Lembre-se: quando o sujeito é He/She/It, o verbo no Present Simple recebe -s ou -es (he works, she manages, it handles).
