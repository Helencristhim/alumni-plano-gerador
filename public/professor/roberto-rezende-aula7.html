Grammar Rule

The Full Picture

Comparative (2 things)Superlative (top of all)
Shortstrong → stronger thanstrong → the strongest
Longsignificant → more significant thansignificant → the most significant
Irregulargood → better thangood → the best
Irregularbad → worse thanbad → the worst
Roberto"Q2 was better than Q1.""Agriculture was the best segment."
Common Mistakes

Watch Out

"Q2 was more stronger than Q1."
"Q2 was stronger than Q1." (NEVER double comparative)
"Agriculture was best segment."
"Agriculture was the best segment." (THE before superlative)
Grammar Practice

Fill in the Gaps

1. Q2 was _____stronger than Q1. (strong, comparing two)
2. Agriculture was _____the best segment this quarter. (good, top of all)
3. The growth was _____more significant than expected. (significant, comparing two)
4. Brazil had _____the highest revenue of all offices. (high, top of all)
5. Generators performed _____worse than mining. (bad, comparing two)
6. Our projection for Q3 is _____more optimistic than Q2. (optimistic, comparing two)
