Present Simple vs Present Perfect

TenseUseExample
Present SimpleFacts, routines, job descriptionsI am a researcher. She works at Abbott.
Present PerfectExperience, achievements, duration (for/since)I have worked here for 10 years. We have published 3 studies.

Key signals: for + duration = present perfect / since + starting point = present perfect / always, never, ever, yet = present perfect

Chapter 3 -- The Code

Common Mistake

"I work here since 2015."

Present simple + since = incorrect

"I have worked here since 2015."

Present perfect + since = correct

Chapter 3 -- The Code

Grammar Practice

Click to reveal the correct answer

I ___ (conduct) clinical research at Abbott.
I conduct clinical research at Abbott. (Present simple -- permanent fact)
We ___ (collaborate) with three universities so far.
We have collaborated with three universities so far. (Present perfect -- "so far")
My team ___ (publish) the results last month.
My team published the results last month. (Past simple -- specific time "last month")
I ___ (work) with functional ingredients for over ten years.
I have worked with functional ingredients for over ten years. (Present perfect -- "for" + duration)
The trial ___ (not finish) yet.
The trial has not finished yet. (Present perfect -- "yet")

Patricia Ruffo, MS, PhD

Clinical Nutrition Researcher -- Abbott

Patricia Ruffo is a clinical nutrition researcher at Abbott, based in Sao Paulo, Brazil. She is responsible for leading clinical trials focused on functional ingredients for metabolic health. Her current research involves testing myo-inositol in Glucerna for glycemic control in type 2 diabetes patients.

Patricia has been working in nutrition research for over fifteen years. She collaborates with CROs such as Axia Bio and universities including Santa Casa de Misericórdia and Unicamp. Her team has published multiple studies on the relationship between functional ingredients and glycemic outcomes.

Recent publication: "Effects of Myo-Inositol on Glycemic Control in Type 2 Diabetes" -- Journal of Clinical Nutrition, 2024.

Functional ingredients for metabolic health, specifically glycemic control in diabetic patients.
2. How many years has she worked in nutrition research?
She has been working in nutrition research for over fifteen years.
