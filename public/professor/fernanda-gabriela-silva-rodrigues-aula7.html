Chapter 3: The Code

am / is / are + -ing

I am working · She is reviewing · We are waiting

Discover the Grammar

What Is Happening?

"I am working on the report."

"She is reviewing the contract."

"We are not shipping this week."

"Are you joining the meeting?"

SubjectHelperExample
Iam + verb-ingI am working.
he / she / itis + verb-ingShe is reviewing.
you / we / theyare + verb-ingWe are waiting.

Now = am/is/are + verbo-ing. Routine = present simple. / Agora vs rotina.

Now vs Routine

Two Kinds of Present

ROUTINE (every day)

"Every morning, I check my emails."

NOW (right now)

"Right now, I am writing an email."

ROUTINE (every day)

"We ship coffee every week."

NOW (right now)

"We are not shipping this week."

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"I working on the report."

✓ RIGHT

"I am working on the report."

✗ WRONG

"Right now, I review the contract."

✓ RIGHT

"Right now, I am reviewing the contract."

Chapter 4: The Status Update

What's in Progress

Daniel asks, Fernanda updates

Dialogue

The Monday Update

D
Hi Fernanda. Quick check: what are you working on right now?
F
Hi Daniel. I am currently reviewing the new contract.
D
Good. And the prices from the supplier?
F
We are still waiting for them. I am following up on two emails.
D
Are you attending the call at three?
F
No, I am not. I am coordinating with the export team instead.
D
Perfect. Are you updating the spreadsheet today?
F
Yes, I am. I will send you the full report tomorrow.
Comprehension

In Progress

1. What is Fernanda reviewing?
The new contract.
2. Why is she not attending the call at three?
She is coordinating with the export team.
3. What are they still waiting for?
The prices from the supplier.

Chapter 6: Your Turn

Give Your Update

Guided > Semi-free > Free

Role-Play

What I'm Doing

Keywords:

I am currently... reviewing coordinating
Role-Play

Pending & Not Today

Scenario: Tell your manager one task you are still waiting for, one task you are not doing today, and ask if a colleague is joining the call.

Keywords:

We are still waiting... I am not... Are you joining...?
