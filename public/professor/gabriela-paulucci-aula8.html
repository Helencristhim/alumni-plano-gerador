How do we shop in English?

UseStructureExample
Ask the price (1 thing)How much is it?How much is the dress?
Ask the price (2+ things)How much are they?How much are the shoes?
Say what you wantI'm looking for + itemI'm looking for a jacket.
Test / buyCan I try it on? / I'll take it.I'll take it!

Rule: is for one thing, are for two or more (shoes, pants). Decide = I'll take it.

Careful!

Common Mistake

How much it is?
How much is it?
I looking for a shirt.
I'm looking for a shirt.
