Chapter 3

The Past Continuous

Background actions and interrupted moments in the past

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"At nine, the whole team was working normally."

"While we were working, the system crashed."

"She was not taking notes; she was making a complaint."

"Were you presenting when the alarm rang?"

FormUseExample
was / were + verb-ingAction in progress in the pastThe team was working at nine.
while + past continuous, past simpleBackground + interruptionWhile we were working, the system crashed.
negative: was / were + not + verb-ingNegativeShe was not taking notes.
question: Was / Were + subject + verb-ingQuestionWere you presenting at that moment?
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"At nine, I presenting the report."

✓ RIGHT

"At nine, I was presenting the report."

✗ WRONG

"They was meeting when it happened."

✓ RIGHT

"They were meeting when it happened."

✗ WRONG

"While I presented, the alarm rang."

✓ RIGHT

"While I was presenting, the alarm rang."

Use was/were + -ing for the longer background action. The short action that interrupts it goes in the past simple.

Chapter 4

In the Meeting

Recounting an incident and what was happening

Dialogue

What Happened on Friday

M
Hi Liam. I heard there was an incident on Friday. What was happening when it started?
L
At nine, everything was normal. I was reviewing the payroll, and two managers were interviewing candidates.
M
And then? What went wrong?
L
While we were working, the system suddenly crashed. It was a total breakdown. Three people were in the middle of a handover.
M
That sounds stressful. What were you doing while IT was fixing it?
L
While the IT team was fixing the problem, I was handling the complaints. People were calling from every department.
M
You stayed calm. How did it end?
L
It took two hours, but we resolved it. Nobody panicked, and the crisis is over now.
Comprehension

Did You Catch It?

1. What was the team doing at nine o'clock?
Liam was reviewing the payroll and two managers were interviewing candidates.
2. What happened while they were working?
The system crashed; it was a total breakdown during a handover.
3. What was Liam doing while IT was fixing the problem?
He was handling the complaints while people were calling from every department.
