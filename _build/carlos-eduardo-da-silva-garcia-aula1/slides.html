Chapter 3: The Code

Who You Are

my name is · I am · I work

Discover the Grammar

Listen and Notice

"My name is Carlos."

"I am an analyst."

"I work at Sucafina."

"I work with coffee producers."

UsePatternExample
Your nameMy name is + nameMy name is Carlos.
Your jobI am a/an + jobI am an analyst.
Your workplaceI work at + placeI work at Sucafina.
Who you work withI work with + peopleI work with producers.

With I we use am and work (no s). / Com "I": am e work.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"My name Carlos."

✓ RIGHT

"My name is Carlos."

✗ WRONG

"I have 25 years."

✓ RIGHT

"I am 25 years old."

Chapter 4: Meeting Emma

Meeting Emma

The welcome begins

Dialogue

Hello, I Am Early for the Meeting

E
Hello! I am early for the meeting. I am Emma, a visitor from the Switzerland office.
C
Welcome, Emma! My name is Carlos. Nice to meet you.
E
Nice to meet you too. What is your role here?
C
I am an analyst. I work at Sucafina with sustainability.
E
Great! Do you work with coffee producers?
C
Yes. I work with producers in southern Minas Gerais. I am from Varginha.
E
Wonderful. Thank you for the welcome.
C
You are welcome. Please come in. The meeting starts now.
