Lesson 20 -- Checkpoint 4: The Full Story

Series / Business
Suits -- a job interview scene

Choose one interview scene and watch a candidate talk about their experience and career. You will hear the present perfect (I have worked...), the past simple (I started...), and for and since for duration -- the whole Experiences block together.

Watch with English subtitles. Write 1 sentence for each tool: a life experience, a finished moment, a duration with for, and a start point with since. Then say them out loud.

The speakers practice a job interview: a candidate talks about their experience, their background, when they started, and their biggest strength. A perfect model for your own interview answer.

YouTube / BBC
BBC Learning English -- "Present perfect vs past simple, for & since"

A short, clear review that compares the present perfect (I have visited) with the past simple (I started in 2012), and shows how to use for (a duration) and since (a start point) -- the whole Experiences block toolbox in one place.
