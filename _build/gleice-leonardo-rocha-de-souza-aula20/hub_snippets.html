Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Experiences Toolbox

GRAMMAR

Revisão do bloco. Present perfect: have/has + particio para experiências da vida sem tempo definido (I have visited). Past simple: verbo no passado para um momento terminado, com data (I started in 2012). For: duração, um período de tempo (for ten years). Since: ponto de início (since 2018) -- nunca "for 2018" nem "since ten years". / Block review. Present perfect for a life experience with no time; past simple for a finished moment with a date; for for a duration; since for a start point.

ToolExample
Life experience, no time -- present perfectI have visited many clients.
Finished moment, with a date -- past simpleI started this job in 2012.
Duration -- for + a periodI have worked here for ten years.
Start point -- since + a point in timeI have been a manager since 2018.
