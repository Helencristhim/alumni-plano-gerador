Chapter 3

The Code

Simple past: regular, irregular, and questions

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"Last week, we closed two big deals."

"I sent the report yesterday."

"We did not finish the tax restatement."

"What did you do last week? When did they leave?"

StructureUseExample
Regular verb + -edMost verbs add -ed for the pastWe closed the deal.
Irregular verb (special form)send → sent, come → came, run → ranI sent the report.
did not + base verbNegative — no -ed after "did not"We did not finish it.
(WH) + did + subject + baseQuestions always use "did" + a subjectWhat did you do? When did they leave?
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"We didn't closed the deal."

✓ RIGHT

"We didn't close the deal."

✗ WRONG

"What you did last week?"

✓ RIGHT

"What did you do last week?"

✗ WRONG

"He send the shipment two days ago."

✓ RIGHT

"He sent the shipment two days ago."

After "did not / didn't," use the base verb (no -ed). Every past question needs "did" and a subject. And irregular verbs change their form: send becomes sent.

Chapter 4

In the Room

Giving your weekly recap on the Thursday call

Dialogue

The Weekly Recap

A
Good morning, Izabel. Before we start, could you give us a quick recap of what happened last week?
I
Of course. Last week we closed the deal with the new supplier, and the first shipment arrived on Monday.
A
That is great news. Did you resolve the customs problem from the previous order?
I
Yes, we resolved it on Wednesday. I followed up with the logistics team, and everything matched the invoice.
A
Perfect. And what about the tax restatement? Did the team finish it?
I
Not yet. We did not finish it, because the new rules changed halfway through the quarter.
A
Understood. When did the partners leave after the visit?
I
They left last Friday. Before they left, we delivered the full cash-flow report by hand.
Comprehension

Did You Catch It?

1. What does Andy ask Izabel to do at the start?
He asks her to give a quick recap of what happened last week.
2. What does Andy ask about after the good news?
He asks whether she resolved the customs problem from the previous order.
3. What does Andy want to know at the end?
He wants to know when the partners left after the visit.
