ModalMeaning
must / have toobligation (a rule): you must / you have to
shouldadvice (a good idea): you should
canpermission / possibility: you can
can't / mustn'tnot allowed: you can't / you mustn't
Grammar Recap

Rules, Advice & Permission

At the airportModal phrase
Rule"You must be at the gate on time."
Obligation"You have to show your boarding pass."
Advice"You should check that big bag."

Common Mistake

Watch Out!

"You must to show your passport."
"You must show your passport."
"I have to checking in my bag."
"I have to check in my bag."

After must / should / can: base verb (no "to", no -ing).
