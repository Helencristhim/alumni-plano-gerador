Extra materials to reinforce your learning outside class. Mark as done after watching or listening.

Lesson 3 -- Looking Back -- Past Experiences & Career Milestones

Podcast
How I Built This with Guy Raz (NPR) -- any founder episode

In every episode, a leader tells the story of their career and company: where they started, the milestones they reached, and the hard moments they went through. The whole narration is in the past simple. Direct connection to Lesson 3: telling a professional story.

Tip: write down 5 past-simple verbs the guest uses (regular and irregular), then use them to tell your own story.

Listen on NPR ↗
Series
The Founder (2016 film) -- the story of how McDonald's grew

A business drama that follows a real career story full of milestones, a takeover, and big decisions. Listen for how the characters describe what happened, when it happened, and what they did. Connection to Lesson 3: career milestones and the past simple.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Note 3 sentences that describe a past milestone or a big change.

View on IMDb ↗
YouTube
"Past Simple Tense -- Regular and Irregular Verbs" -- BBC Learning English

A clear lesson on the past simple: how to add -ed to regular verbs, the most common irregular verbs, and how to make questions and negatives with did and didn't. Connection to Lesson 3: the exact grammar you use to tell your career story.

Tip: pause and build your own past-simple sentences with started, led, launched, went, and got.
