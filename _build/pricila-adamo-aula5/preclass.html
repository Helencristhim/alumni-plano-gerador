Stage 1.1: Words for Wellbeing

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. These are rich B2 phrases for talking about stress, rest and bouncing back.

Burnout -- esgotamento fisico e mental
"After two years with no break, she was close to burnout."
To unwind -- relaxar / aliviar a tensao
"I unwind in the evening with a quiet walk and some tea."
To recharge -- recarregar as energias
"A weekend with no screens is how I recharge."
Downtime -- tempo livre para descansar
"I now treat my downtime as seriously as my work."
Overwhelmed -- sobrecarregado / sem conta com tudo
"With three deadlines at once, I felt completely overwhelmed."
To set boundaries -- estabelecer limites
"Learning to set boundaries at work changed everything for me."
To slow down -- desacelerar
"My doctor told me firmly that I needed to slow down."
Self-care -- autocuidado
"For years I saw self-care as selfish -- now I know it is essential."
Resilience -- resiliencia / forca para se recuperar
"Resilience is not avoiding stress; it is recovering well from it."
To bounce back -- se recuperar rapidamente
"It took months, but she bounced back stronger than before."
Sustainable habits -- habitos que se mantem por anos
"Small, sustainable habits beat one dramatic change every time."
Work-life balance -- equilibrio entre trabalho e vida
"Since I retired part-time, my work-life balance has finally improved."
To keep up with -- acompanhar o ritmo de
"I exhausted myself trying to keep up with everyone else's schedule."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- should / ought to have + past participle

Grammar

Para falar de um arrependimento ou uma critica sobre o passado (voce fez ou nao fez algo, e hoje ve que era o certo), use should have / ought to have + participio passado. Para um conselho forte ou aviso sobre o presente / futuro proximo, use had better + verbo base (sem have). / To talk about a regret or criticism about the past, use should have / ought to have + past participle. For strong advice or a warning about now or the near future, use had better + base verb (no have).

Forma / FormEnglishPortugues
Regret (affirmative)I should have slowed down earlier.Eu deveria ter desacelerado antes.
Regret (ought to)She ought to have set boundaries.Ela deveria ter estabelecido limites.
NegativeI should not have ignored the signs.Eu nao deveria ter ignorado os sinais.
Advice now (had better)You had better rest this weekend.E melhor voce descansar neste fim de semana.
Common mistakeNOT "I should slowed down." Say "I should have slowed down."Sempre have + participio passado.
