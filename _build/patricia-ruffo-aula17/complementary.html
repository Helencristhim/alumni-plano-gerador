Lesson 17 -- Networking at Conferences

Series
The Office (US) -- any conference or party episode

Watch how characters mingle, make small talk, build rapport and exchange contacts. Notice the open questions that start conversations and the polite phrases that close them.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down three open questions someone uses to start a conversation, and one polite way to end one.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Networking and Small Talk in English"

A focused episode on the language of networking: opening lines, building rapport, exchanging contacts and following up after an event.

Record a 1-minute networking conversation: open with a question, build rapport, and close by exchanging business cards.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "How to make small talk and network in English"

A clear lesson on the phrases for professional small talk: open questions, building rapport, exchanging contacts and the polite phrases that close a conversation.

After watching, write a short follow-up message to an imaginary contact: thank them, mention something specific, and suggest a collaboration.
