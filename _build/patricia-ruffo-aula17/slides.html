Grammar Rule

Open, Build & Close

FunctionExample
Open (Wh- question)What did you think of the keynote?
Open (yes/no + follow-up)Are you presenting this year? Oh, what is it about?
Build rapportOh, we work in the same field! Tell me more.
Exchange contactHere is my business card -- shall we exchange details?
Close politelyIt was great to meet you. Let's keep in touch.

Key idea: open with a question that invites more than yes/no, build rapport by following up on what the person says, and close with a fixed polite phrase before you exchange contact details.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"Do you like the conference?" -- "Yes." (conversation ends)
"What did you think of the keynote this morning?"

Open with What or How, or follow a yes/no question with a real one. Closed questions stop the conversation.
