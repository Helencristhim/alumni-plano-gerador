Stage 1.1: Words for Smart Spending

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. These are rich B2 phrases for shopping and prices.

Value for money -- bom custo-beneficio
"At that price, the jacket was real value for money."
Overpriced -- caro demais para o que vale
"The souvenirs in the square were badly overpriced."
A rip-off -- um roubo, preco abusivo
"Twenty euros for a coffee? What a rip-off!"
Pricey -- meio caro (informal)
"That boutique is lovely, but a bit pricey."
To haggle -- pechinchar / negociar o preco
"In the market it is normal to haggle a little."
To talk someone down -- convencer a baixar o preco
"I managed to talk him down by ten euros."
A knock-off -- falsificacao barata de marca
"That 'designer' bag is a knock-off."
A steal -- uma pechincha, muito barato
"At ten euros, this jacket was a steal."
To browse -- dar uma olhada sem pressa
"I love to browse the stalls before deciding."
To shop around -- pesquisar precos em varias lojas
"It pays to shop around for the best deal."
An impulse buy -- compra por impulso
"The scarf was a total impulse buy."
Top of the range -- o melhor e mais caro da categoria
"This is the top of the range model."
On a tight budget -- com orcamento apertado
"We are on a tight budget this trip."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Comparatives with Modifiers

Grammar

Para mostrar uma diferenca grande, use far / much / considerably + comparativo. Para uma diferenca pequena, use slightly / a bit + comparativo. Para dizer "muito menos que", use not nearly as + adjetivo + as. / To show a big difference, use far / much / considerably + comparative. For a small difference, use slightly / a bit + comparative. To say "much less than", use not nearly as + adjective + as.

Ideia / IdeaEnglishPortugues
Big differenceThe market is far cheaper than the mall.O mercado e bem mais barato que o shopping.
Small differenceThis one is slightly pricier.Esse aqui e um pouco mais caro.
Much less thanThe copy is not nearly as good as the original.A copia esta longe de ser tao boa quanto a original.
EmphasisIt's considerably better value for money.Tem um custo-beneficio bem melhor.
Common mistakeNOT "far cheap." Say "far cheaper" (modifier + comparative).Modificador + forma comparativa.
