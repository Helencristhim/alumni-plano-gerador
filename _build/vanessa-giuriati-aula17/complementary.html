Aula 17 -- Explaining & Giving Reasons

Series
Ted Lasso (Apple TV) -- Any episode

A show full of characters explaining their decisions and feelings clearly and kindly. Direct connection to Lesson 17: listen for how Ted gives reasons ("I did it because...", "...so I decided...") and turns tense moments into calm conversations.

Tip: pause when someone explains a choice and rebuild the sentence with because, since, so, or that's why.

Podcast
TED Talks Daily -- Any explanatory talk

Speakers explain ideas by connecting causes and results clearly. Direct connection to Lesson 17: hear how good communicators always say why, using "because", "since", "so", and "that's why" to keep listeners following their thinking.

Tip: after one talk, record 60 seconds explaining the speaker's main point, giving the cause and the result.

YouTube
"Because, Since, So & That's Why -- Linking Words" -- BBC Learning English / English with Lucy

A clear lesson on connecting cause and result with because, since, so, and that's why, including word order and the common mistakes. Direct connection to Lesson 17: exactly the grammar we practiced for explaining reasons.

Tip: pause after each example and make your own cause-and-result sentence about your week, out loud.
