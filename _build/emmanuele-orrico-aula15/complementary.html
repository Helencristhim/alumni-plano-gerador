Lesson 15 -- Have You Replied Yet?

Series
Suits — Season 1, Episode 1 (Netflix)

A busy law firm runs on emails, follow-ups and deadlines: "Have you sent it?", "I've already replied", "I haven't finished it yet." It is exactly the fast, professional English you use on a call with the Global team.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Treasure hunt: write down 3 sentences with the present perfect (have sent, have just called, haven't done it yet). Do not try to understand 100% — look ONLY for have / has + verb.
