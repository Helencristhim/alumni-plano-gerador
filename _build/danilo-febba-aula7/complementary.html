Lesson 7 -- Asking & Answering Questions

Series
The Office (US) -- Season 1, Episode 4: "The Alliance"

Characters constantly interview each other and ask short questions in the office. Direct connection to Lesson 7: listen for question words (who/what/where/when/why) and the do/does/did word order in real, slow American English.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down 5 questions you hear and the question word each one uses.

Podcast
BBC Learning English -- "6 Minute English: Small Talk"

A short, clear episode at a friendly A2 speed. The two hosts ask each other questions and give answers with examples and details. Direct connection to Lesson 7: it trains your ear for natural questions and the words "reason", "example", and "information".

Tip: listen twice. Pause after each question and try to answer it yourself before the host does.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "How to Ask Questions in English (WH Questions)"

A short, practical lesson on building questions with who, what, where, when, why, and how, plus the do/does/did word order. Connection to Lesson 7: ready-to-use question patterns for running a Q&A at work, at exactly your level.

Tip: pause and repeat each question aloud. Then make your own version about your project 3 times.
