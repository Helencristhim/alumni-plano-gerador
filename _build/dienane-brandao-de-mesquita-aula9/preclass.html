Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Every morning, Dienane goes to work. She can take the bus to her office, because the station is near her house. She cannot drive a car yet, but she wants to learn. Sometimes she can walk, because her office is not far. On the weekend, she takes the train to visit her family. She buys a ticket at the station.

1. How do we say Dienane is able to take the bus?
A "She can to take the bus."
B "She can take the bus."
C "She cans take the bus."
2. How do we say she is not able to drive?
A "She cannot drive a car."
B "She cannot drives a car."
C "She cannot to drive a car."
3. Where does she buy a ticket?
A She buys a ticket at the station.
B She buys a ticket at home.
C She does not buy a ticket.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Can and Cannot

GRAMMAR

Usado para dizer o que você consegue ou não consegue fazer. / Used to say what you can and cannot do.

Form / FormaStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
afirmativocan + verbo (forma base)I can drive a car.
negativocannot + verbo (forma base)I cannot drive the train.
perguntaCan + sujeito + verbo?Can you take the bus?

Key point / Ponto-chave: depois de can e cannot, o verbo fica na forma base -- sem "to" e sem "-s". Na fala, "cannot" vira "can't".
