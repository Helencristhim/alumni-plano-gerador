Aula 7 -- Reading Adventures

Tema da aula: Reading Adventures -- talking about a book you read with the Past Simple of regular verbs (-ed). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
BookTube -- a short book review or "books I read this month"

Watch a young reader give a short review of a book. Listen for the Past Simple of regular verbs ("I started it", "I finished it", "I enjoyed it", "I recommended it").

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote toda frase no passado que termina em -ed. Quantas você encontrou?

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "Past Simple: regular verbs and -ed pronunciation"

Short, clear videos and quizzes that practice the -ed ending and its three sounds (/t/, /d/, /id/). Perfect review of today's grammar.

Dica: pause após cada verbo e diga em voz alta a forma do passado. Qual som tem o -ed: /t/, /d/ ou /id/?

Series / Film
Matilda (film) -- any library or reading scene

A friendly story about a girl who loves books and the library. Listen to the characters talk about the books they read and the stories they discovered -- great input for reading vocabulary.

Dica: escolha uma cena na biblioteca, ouça duas vezes e anote três frases no Past Simple sobre o que os personagens fizeram.
