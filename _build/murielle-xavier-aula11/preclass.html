Lesson 11 -- Pre-class

Happening Right Now -- Present Continuous

Describing actions happening right now in live meetings with the present continuous (am/is/are + verb-ing): affirmative, negative, and question forms, plus stative verbs that are not used in the continuous. New words: facilitate, attendee, presenter, poll, recap, breakout, host, chime in, walk through, multitask, sidebar, screen-share.

Walk through -- to explain something step by step (explicar passo a passo)

"They are multitasking as usual."
Sidebar -- a short side conversation during a meeting (conversa paralela)
"Two colleagues are having a quick sidebar."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Continuous

GRAMMAR

O present continuous descreve uma ação em andamento agora. Forma: am (I), is (he/she/it), are (you/we/they) + verbo com -ing. Marcadores: right now, at the moment, currently. Verbos de estado (know, want, need, like) normalmente não usam o -ing: "I know the answer" (não "I am knowing").

FormStructureExample
Affirmativeam/is/are + verb-ing"The presenter is sharing the screen."
Negativeam/is/are + not + verb-ing"Nobody is multitasking too much."
QuestionAm/Is/Are + subject + verb-ing?"Is she facilitating now?"
Stative verbpresent simple (no -ing)"I know the answer." (not "am knowing")

Atenção: use am para I, is para he/she/it, are para you/we/they. Nunca esqueça o am/is/are antes do verbo-ing.
