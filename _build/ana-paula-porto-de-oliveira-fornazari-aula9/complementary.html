'Have To' and 'Must' -- 6 Minute Grammar (BBC Learning English)

A short, clear video on the exact grammar from today: how to use must for strong necessity, and how it compares with have to. It shows the base-verb pattern and gives simple, natural examples. Connection to Lesson 9: this is the difference between friendly advice (should) and a strong rule (must).
