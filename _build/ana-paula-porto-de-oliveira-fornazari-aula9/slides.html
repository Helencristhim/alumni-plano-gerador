Chapter 3: The Advice Code

Should vs Must

A good idea · A strong necessity

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"You should drink more water."

"You shouldn't skip your meals."

"You must see a doctor about that pain."

"You mustn't ignore these symptoms."

Which sentences give friendly advice, and which show a strong necessity?

ModalMeaningExample
should / shouldn'tadvice — a good ideashould rest / shouldn't skip
must / mustn'tstrong necessity or rulemust sleep / mustn't ignore
After the modalAlways the base verb: no to, no -s — "She should drink water" (not "drinks").

Should is a suggestion; must is much stronger and feels almost like a rule.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

"You should to rest more."
"You should rest more."
"You must drinking more water."
"You must drink more water."
"She should drinks less coffee."
"She should drink less coffee."

After should and must, always use the base verb: no to, no -ing, and no -s.

Choose the correct verb form after the modal

Chapter 4: A Friend Needs Advice

The Plan, Then the Talk

Read, then listen

Artifact

A Weekly Wellness Plan

Weekly Wellness Plan ANA PAULA

SHOULD (good habits)

  • Go for a 20-minute walk every morning
  • Eat a balanced lunch, not just coffee
  • Take three mindful breaks a day

MUST (non-negotiable)

  • Sleep at least 7 hours
  • Book the yearly checkup
  • Cut down on caffeine after 6 p.m.
1. What should Ana Paula do every morning?
She should go for a 20-minute walk.
2. How many hours must she sleep?
She must sleep at least 7 hours.
3. What must she cut down on after 6 p.m.?
She must cut down on caffeine.
Dialogue

What Should I Do?

T
Ana Paula, I feel exhausted all the time, and I get these bad headaches. What should I do?
A
First, tell me about your routine. How much sleep do you get, and how is your stress at work?
T
I sleep about five hours, and my job is very stressful right now. I drink a lot of coffee to cope with it.
A
Okay. You should cut down on coffee, especially at night, and you must get more sleep. Seven or eight hours is important for your wellbeing.
T
That makes sense. Should I change anything else? I want to feel fit again.
A
Yes. You should eat a more balanced diet and take short, mindful breaks. But if the headaches continue, you must book a checkup. You must not ignore them.
