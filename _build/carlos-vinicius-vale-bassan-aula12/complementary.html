Aula 12 — Block 3: Leading Change & the Change Team

Series
Industry (HBO) — any town hall or leadership-under-pressure scene

Watch how leaders set expectations during a change: they own the message, handle resistance, and answer hard questions in the open. Notice how often they use "must", "have to", "should", and "need to" to tell people what is required and what is just advice.

Tip: pick one town hall or all-hands scene and write five expectations in English: two rules (must / have to), one piece of advice (should), and two needs (need to).
