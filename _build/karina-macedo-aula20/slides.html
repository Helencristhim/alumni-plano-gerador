Chapter 3 -- Review the Key Phrases

The Four Steps

Offer -- Answer -- Close -- Follow up

Phrase Review

Listen and Remember

One key phrase for each step of the deal.

1. Offer: "I would like to offer you a good price."

2. Answer: "Let me explain. The reason is the new engine."

3. Close: "We have a deal. Please sign here."

4. Follow up: "Thank you. I am grateful for the deal."

The Deal Map

Four Steps, Four Phrases

StepKey phrase
1. Make an offerI would like to offer...
2. Answer a questionLet me explain. The reason is...
3. Close the dealWe have a deal. Please sign here.
4. Follow upThank you. I am grateful.

Key idea: a deal has four steps. You offer, you answer the questions, you close the deal, and you follow up. One key phrase opens each step: I would like to offer, Let me explain, We have a deal, and I am grateful.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I explain you the price, and it is more cheap. I am agree."
"Let me explain the price, and it is cheaper. I agree."

Say Let me explain (not "I explain you"), cheaper (not "more cheap"), and I agree (not "I am agree").
