Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- The Business Deal: Key Phrases

GRAMMAR

Os quatro passos do negócio e suas frases-chave. / The four steps of the deal and their key phrases.

Step / PassoWhen / QuandoPhrase / Frase
1. OfferFazer uma oferta / Make an offerI would like to offer you a good price.
2. AnswerResponder uma pergunta / Answer a questionLet me explain. The reason is...
3. CloseFechar o negócio / Close the dealWe have a deal. Please sign here.
4. Follow upAgradecer depois / Follow up & thankThank you. I am grateful.

Key point / Ponto-chave: um negócio tem quatro passos. Você oferece, responde as perguntas, fecha o acordo e faz o follow-up. Uma frase-chave abre cada passo: I would like to offer, Let me explain, We have a deal, e I am grateful.
