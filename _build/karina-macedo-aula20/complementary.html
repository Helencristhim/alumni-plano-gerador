Lesson 20 -- Review + Confidence Check: The Business Deal

Series
Suits -- any full deal scene (offer to handshake)

Watch a whole business deal: someone makes an offer, answers tough questions, closes the deal, and follows up. You hear the four steps you reviewed today, all in one scene.

Watch with English subtitles. Find one phrase for each step: the offer, the answer, the close, and the thank-you.

Podcast
Speak English Now -- "Closing a Deal from Start to Finish"

A slow, clear podcast that walks through a full deal: making an offer, answering questions, closing, and following up. Perfect to review everything from lessons 16 to 19.

Repeat each phrase out loud. Then record your own two-minute deal, using all four steps.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Business English: Making and closing a deal"

A short, practical review of the whole deal language: making an offer, answering questions clearly, closing with "We have a deal", and following up politely.

After watching, write the four key phrases of the deal and read them aloud. Then tell the story of your deal with Robert.
