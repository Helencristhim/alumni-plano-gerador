Aula 4 -- Talking About the Past

Series
This Is Us (NBC / streaming) -- Any early episode

A warm family drama that constantly moves between present and past, with characters telling personal stories. Clear American English at a natural pace. Direct connection to Lesson 4: listen for simple past verbs and how people narrate memories.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down 5 past verbs you hear (regular and irregular).

Podcast
The Moth -- Any storytelling episode

Real people telling true personal stories from their lives, mostly in the past tense. Natural, emotional, and clear. Direct connection to Lesson 4: hear how native speakers structure a story with a beginning, middle, and end.

Tip: listen once for the story, then again for the connectors (first, then, finally).

YouTube
"Past Simple Tense" -- BBC Learning English (English In A Minute)

A short, clear explanation of the simple past with regular and irregular verbs, modeled by native speakers. Direct connection to Lesson 4: the exact grammar we practiced, in a quick and friendly format.

Tip: pause and repeat each example out loud. Then say one true past sentence about your day.
