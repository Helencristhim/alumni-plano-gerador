Chapter 3: The Code

was / were & -ed

It was · we were · I worked

Discover the Grammar

was or were?

"It was a great achievement."

"I was happy with the project."

"We were a small team."

"The results were excellent."

SubjectPast of "to be"Example
I / he / she / itwasIt was easy
you / we / theywereWe were ready

was = passado de is/am; were = passado de are. / was for one, were for you/we/they.
