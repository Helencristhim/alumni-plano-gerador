Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Career -- carreira (vida profissional)
"I started my career in 2016." (Comecei minha carreira em 2016.)
Background -- trajetoria / experiencia anterior
"I have a background in coffee trading." (Tenho experiencia em comercio de cafe.)
Project -- projeto
"Last year, I helped with a big project." (Ano passado ajudei num projeto grande.)
Responsibility -- responsabilidade
"My first responsibility was the new contract." (Minha primeira responsabilidade foi o novo contrato.)
Achievement -- conquista / realizacao
"It was a great achievement." (Foi uma grande conquista.)
Training -- treinamento / capacitacao
"The training was difficult but useful." (O treinamento foi dificil mas util.)
Hire -- contratar
"The company decided to hire two people." (A empresa decidiu contratar duas pessoas.)
Promote -- promover (dar um cargo melhor)
"The company promoted her last year." (A empresa a promoveu ano passado.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

Ana started her career in 2015. Before Sucafina, she worked at a small coffee company. In 2018, she joined the trading team. Last year, she helped with a big project, and the company promoted her. The new contract was signed in March. It was a great achievement for the team. The training was difficult, but the results were excellent.

1. "She worked" and "she joined". Why do they end in -ed? (Por que terminam em -ed?)
A Because they are in the past. (Porque estao no passado.)
B Because they are plural. (Porque sao plural.)
C Because they are questions. (Porque sao perguntas.)
2. "It was" but "the results were". Why different? (Por que a diferenca?)
A They mean different things. (Significam coisas diferentes.)
B "was" = one; "were" = many. (Um vs muitos.)
C "were" is the future. (E o futuro.)
3. "The company promoted her". What does "promote" mean? (O que significa?)
A To send an email. (Enviar um email.)
B To give a better position. (Dar um cargo melhor.)
C To start a project. (Comecar um projeto.)
