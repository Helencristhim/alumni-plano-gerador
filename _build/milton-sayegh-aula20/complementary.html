Lesson 20 -- Looking Ahead

Series / Film
The Founder (film) -- the vision and ambition behind a brand

Watch how one man turns a small operation into a global brand: the objective, the aspiration, the momentum, and the ambition to scale. A vivid model of the goals vocabulary in a real business story.

Tip: pause when a goal is set and rephrase it as "His objective was to..." or "His vision was...".

Podcast
How I Built This -- any founder reflecting on the journey

Founders look back on how they used to work, the breakthrough that changed everything, and the vision driving them now. The exact "looking back, looking ahead" language of this lesson, told by real people.

Tip: note one thing the founder "used to" do, then say a true sentence about something you used to do.

YouTube
TED -- "How to set goals" / "The power of believing you can improve"

A clear, practical talk on setting an objective, sustaining momentum, and turning a vision into a plan. Native speakers model confident, forward-looking language about growth and ambition.

Tip: after watching, record yourself naming one objective and the vision behind it: "My objective is..., and my vision is...".
