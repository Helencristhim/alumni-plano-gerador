Chapter 3: Present Continuous

Happening Now

am / is + verb-ing · talk about now

Now

Talk About Now

"I am calling" · about the meeting

"He is working" · right now

"She is not" · available now

"What are you doing?" → "I am checking the schedule."

am / is + verb + -ing = happening now

Discover the Pattern

The Form of Now

"I am calling about the meeting."

"He is working now."

"She is checking the schedule."

"They are holding on the line."

To talk about nowSay
I + am + verb-ingI am calling about the meeting.
He/She + is + verb-ingHe is working now.
Negative: is + notShe is not available.
They/You + are + verb-ingThey are holding on the line.

am / is / are + verbo + -ing = acontecendo agora · I am calling = Estou ligando · He is working = Ele esta trabalhando

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"I calling about the meeting."

✓ RIGHT

"I am calling about the meeting."

✗ WRONG

"He working now."

✓ RIGHT

"He is working now."
