Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

Gleice has a big day today. Marc, a manager from Sanofi France, visits her office. Gleice is ready: "I am Gleice. I work at Sanofi Medley. I am the commercial excellence manager." She presents her team: "My team has five people. Ana works in logistics." She talks about her routine: "I always review my schedule in the morning." And she connects: "Marc, what do you do at Sanofi France?"

C "Ana is work logistics."

A She reviews her schedule.
B She visits Sanofi France.
C She has lunch with Marc.
3. Como Gleice pergunta sobre o trabalho do Marc? / How does Gleice ask about Marc's job?
A "What you do?"
B "What do you do?"
C "What is you do?"
