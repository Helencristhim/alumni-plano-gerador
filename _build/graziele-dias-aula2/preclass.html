Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Coordinator -- coordenador(a) (quem organiza as equipes)
"I am the coordinator for the medical groups." (Sou a coordenadora.)
Team -- equipe / time
"I work with the cardiology team." (Trabalho com a equipe de cardiologia.)
Department -- departamento / setor
"Cardiology is a big department." (Cardiologia e um setor grande.)
Colleague -- colega (de trabalho)
"My colleague Ana works with me." (Minha colega Ana trabalha comigo.)
Role -- funcao / papel (o que voce faz)
"My role is to coordinate the teams." (Minha funcao e coordenar as equipes.)
Office -- escritorio / sala de trabalho
"I share an office with two nurses." (Divido uma sala com duas enfermeiras.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

My name is Graziele, and my role is special. I am a coordinator. I work with many teams and many departments. I do not work in only one department. My colleague Ana works with me. She manages the office. She does not work in cardiology -- she works with the surgery team.

1. Why do we say "she works" but "I work"? (Por que "she works" leva "s"?)
A With he/she/it the verb adds -s. (he/she/it usa work + s.)
B Because "she" is plural. (Porque "she" e plural.)
C It is a mistake. (E um erro.)
2. How do we make the negative with "she"? (Como fica o negativo com "she"?)
A She not works. (She not works.)
B She does not work / doesn't work. (She doesn't work.)
C She don't works. (She don't works.)
3. Does Graziele work in only one department? (Graziele trabalha em um setor so?)
A Yes, only cardiology. (Sim, so cardiologia.)
B No, she works with many departments. (Nao, com muitos setores.)
C No, she does not work. (Nao, ela nao trabalha.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Simple

Grammar

Como falar do que as pessoas fazem (present simple). / How to say what people do.

Subject / SujeitoAffirmative (+)Negative (-)
I / you / we / theyI work with teams.I don't work here. (do not)
he / she / itShe works (+ s).She doesn't work here. (does not)
Key idea / Ideia-chavehe/she/it ganham -s no afirmativo; o negativo usa don't ou doesn't + verbo sem "s". / Add -s for he/she/it; use don't / doesn't for the negative.
