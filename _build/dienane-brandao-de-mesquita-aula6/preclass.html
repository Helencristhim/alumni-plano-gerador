Aula 06 -- Pre-class

Places in the City -- Prepositions of Place and City Vocabulary

Prepositions of place (in / on / at) and location words (next to, between, across from). New vocabulary: bank, pharmacy, supermarket, bus stop, square, corner. You will learn to say where a place is and to give simple directions.

Supermarket -- a big store where you buy food
"The supermarket is across from the pharmacy." (Supermercado)
Bus stop -- a place where you wait for the bus
"I wait at the bus stop every morning." (Ponto de ônibus)
Square -- an open public space in the city
"There is a small park in the square." (Praça)
Corner -- the place where two streets meet
"Turn left at the corner." (Esquina)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Dienane works in a small office. The office is in a quiet street. The bank is on the corner, and the pharmacy is next to the bank. The supermarket is across from the pharmacy. The office is between the supermarket and the square. Every morning, Dienane waits at the bus stop in front of the square. Everything is close, so she walks to work.

1. Which preposition do we use for the bus stop?
A "in the bus stop"
B "at the bus stop"
C "on the bus stop"
2. Where is the office?
A Between the supermarket and the square.
B On the corner, next to the bank.
C In front of the pharmacy.
3. Where is the bank?
A On the corner.
B In the square.
C At the supermarket.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- In, On, At

GRAMMAR

Usado para dizer ONDE um lugar fica. / Used to say WHERE a place is.

Word / PalavraUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
indentro de um espaço / inside a spaceThe office is in a quiet street.
onem uma rua ou superfície / on a street or surfaceThe bank is on the corner.
atem um ponto específico / a specific pointI wait at the bus stop.
next to / between / across fromposição perto de outros lugares / position near other placesThe pharmacy is next to the bank.

Key point / Ponto-chave: dizemos at the bus stop (ponto), on the corner (rua) e in a street (espaço). Nunca "in the corner".
