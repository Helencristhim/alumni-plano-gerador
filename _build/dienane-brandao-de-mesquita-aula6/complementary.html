Lesson 6 -- Places in the City

Series
Extra English -- Episode 1: "Hector's Arrival"

A simple comedy made for English learners. The characters move around the city and talk about places, homes, and directions in slow, clear English. Perfect for A1 listening.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down every place word you hear (bank, shop, street). Then say where two of them are using "on", "at", or "next to".

Podcast
Coffee Break English -- "Asking for and Giving Directions"

A short, friendly lesson on directions: turn left, turn right, go straight, and prepositions of place. The hosts explain everything slowly, with many examples you can repeat.

Pause after each example and repeat it out loud. Then describe the way from your home to the nearest supermarket using three directions.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Places in Town and Prepositions of Place"

A clear video that teaches city vocabulary (bank, pharmacy, square, corner) and the prepositions in, on, and at, with pictures and simple sentences.

After watching, record a 60-second description of the places near your office. Use at least three new words and the prepositions in, on, and at.
