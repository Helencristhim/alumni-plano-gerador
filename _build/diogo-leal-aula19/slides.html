Chapter 3 -- The Polite Ask

The Polite Ask

How to ask for what you need -- warm and clear

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

What do the orange words have in common?

"Could you please send the report?"

"Would you be able to confirm the date?"

"I would appreciate it if you could review the draft."

"Could you please attach the slides?"

Grammar Rule

The Polite Request

OpenerThenExample
Could you please+ base verb"Could you please send the report?"
Would you be able to+ base verb"Would you be able to confirm the date?"
I would appreciate it if you could+ base verb"I would appreciate it if you could review the draft."

Key idea: A polite ask softens an order. "Send the report" becomes "Could you please send the report?" The verb stays in its base form -- send, confirm, attach, review. Add please and you sound warm, clear, and professional.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"Could you please to send the report? I appreciate if you confirm."
"Could you please send the report? I would appreciate it if you could confirm."

After Could you, use the base verb -- never "to send". And it is "I would appreciate it if you could" + base verb.

Grammar Practice

Make the Ask

Make it polite -- send the budget: _____"Could you please send the budget?"
Ask softly -- confirm the date: _____"Would you be able to confirm the date?"
Be warm -- review the draft: _____"I would appreciate it if you could review the draft."
Polite ask -- attach the slides: _____"Could you please attach the slides?"
Polite ask -- forward the message: _____"Would you be able to forward the message to the team?"

Comprehension

About the Brief

1. What are the four parts of an executive email?

The subject line, the greeting, the body with one clear ask, and the close.

2. What two polite asks does Sophia give as examples?

"Could you please send the report" and "Would you be able to confirm the date."

3. Why should the email be short?

A busy manager reads on the phone, so three short lines beat one long paragraph.

Artefact

The Executive Email

Subject line

Project Atlas -- summary and next step

Salutation

Dear Maria,

Body -- the ask

Thank you for your time today. The migration is finished, and costs dropped by fifteen percent.

Could you please confirm the budget for phase two by Friday? I attach the slides for your records.

Close

Best regards,
Diogo Leal -- IT Project Manager, Oracle
