Lesson 17 -- At the Airport -- Part 2

Series
Easy English -- "At Passport Control" (street interview, slow)

A short, slow video showing the arrival at a new country: passing immigration, talking to the officer, and answering "Where are you going?" and "How long are you staying?" Clear, real English -- perfect for today's words.

Watch with English subtitles. Each time you hear an arrival word (immigration, officer, passport control, vacation), say it out loud.

Podcast
Speak English Now -- "Going Through Immigration"

A slow, clear podcast about arriving in a new country: passport control, the officer's questions, and how to answer about the purpose of your trip and how long you are staying.

Repeat each phrase out loud. Then practice answering: "I am going to..." / "I am here on vacation." / "I am staying for one week."

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "At Immigration: Answering the Officer"

A short, practical lesson on today's language: the officer's questions at passport control and how to answer them with "I am going to..." and "I am staying for...".

After watching, say the five Survival Card phrases out loud. Then imagine you arrive in another country and answer the officer's questions.
