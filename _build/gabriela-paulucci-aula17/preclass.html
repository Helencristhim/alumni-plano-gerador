Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Immigration -- the place where you arrive in a new country
"I am at immigration in London." (Imigração)
Officer -- the person who checks your passport
"The officer looks at my passport." (Agente / Oficial)
Passport control -- the desk where the officer checks passports
"Please go to passport control." (Controle de passaportes)
Arrival -- when you come to a place after a trip
"The sign says Arrivals." (Chegada)
Visa -- a paper that says you can enter a country
"Do I need a visa for this country?" (Visto)
Stay -- to be in a place for some time
"I stay for one week." (Ficar / Permanecer)
Purpose -- the reason why you do something
"The purpose of my trip is vacation." (Motivo / Propósito)
Vacation -- a trip where you rest and travel for fun
"I am here on vacation." (Férias)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Are you ...-ing? / I am ...-ing

GRAMMAR

Na imigração, o agente PERGUNTA com "Are you ...-ing?" e você RESPONDE com "I am ...-ing". Sempre mantenha o "am" na sua resposta. / At immigration, the officer ASKS with "Are you ...-ing?" and you ANSWER with "I am ...-ing". Always keep "am" in your answer.

Function / FunçãoStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Officer asks / Agente perguntaWhere are you + verbo-ing?Where are you going?
You answer / Você respondeI am + verbo-ing + lugar.I am going to London.
Officer asks / Agente perguntaHow long are you staying?How long are you staying?
You answer / Você respondeI am staying for + tempo.I am staying for one week.

Key point / Ponto-chave: na resposta, nunca esqueça o am: diga "I am going", não "I going". / In your answer, never forget "am": say "I am going", not "I going".
