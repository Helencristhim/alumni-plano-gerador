Chapter 3 -- Feature to Benefit

The Secret

Turn a feature into a benefit -- and say it with power

Discover the Pattern

Listen and Notice

Link: "Our service is fast, and that is why it will save you time."

Emphasis: "Not only is it reliable, but it is also easy to use."

Value: "This is not a cost, it is an investment."

You: "You will win back two hours every day."

Persuasive Language

Four Tools

ToolPatternExample
1. Linkfeature + that is why + benefitIt is fast, and that is why it will save you time.
2. EmphasisNot only ... but also ...Not only is it reliable, but it is also simple.
3. Valuenot a cost, it is an investmentThis is not a cost, it is an investment.
4. Youtalk about what the client getsYou will win back two hours a day.

Key idea: A feature is what the product is. A benefit is what the client gets. Always link the two with that is why, add power with Not only ... but also, sell the value, and speak about you, the client.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"Not only it is fast, but also simple."
"Not only is it fast, but it is also simple."

After Not only at the start, the verb comes before the subject: is it, not it is. And never stop at the feature -- add the benefit with that is why.

Grammar Practice

Complete the Pitch

Close: "This offer is really _____worth it." (a smart use of money)
