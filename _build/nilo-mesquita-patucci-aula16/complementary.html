Lesson 16 -- Negotiation III: Signing the Agreement

Series
Suits -- any contract review or signing scene

Lawyers read a draft clause by clause, find the clause which is too wide, ask the other party to amend it, and only sign when the agreement is valid. Listen for how they describe one clause among many and how they talk about liability, breach and termination.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down one defining relative clause you hear ("the clause which...", "the party who...") and one contract word from today's lesson.
