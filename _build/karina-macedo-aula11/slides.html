Chapter 3 -- The Code

Grammar Discovery

phone phrases -- say who you are, ask to speak

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

How do we start and run a phone call?

"This is Karina speaking."

"May I speak with Robert, please?"

"Please hold for one moment."

"Can I leave a message?"

Grammar Rule

Phone Phrases

PhraseWhen we use it
This is + name + speakingSay who you are on the phone
May I / Can I speak with + name?Ask to talk to a person
Please hold for one moment.Ask the other person to wait
Can I leave a message?Leave words when the person is away

Key idea: on the phone we say This is Karina (not "I am Karina"), and we say speak with a person. Add please to be polite: May I speak with Robert, please?

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"Hello, I am Karina. May I speak Robert?"
"Hello, this is Karina. May I speak with Robert?"

On the phone, use This is to say your name, and speak with to ask for a person.
