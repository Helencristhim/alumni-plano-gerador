Lesson 8 -- Mexico City: Pre-trip Immersion

Series / Documentary
Street Food: Latin America -- "Mexico City" (Netflix)

A beautiful episode about the bustling markets, street vendors, and affordable food of Mexico City. Perfect for hearing how locals describe their neighborhood and traditions.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down two sentences in the present perfect (I have seen...) and two in the past simple (Last year they...) about what you watched.

Podcast
Zero To Travel -- "Planning the Perfect Trip"

A slow, clear episode about building an itinerary, choosing a neighborhood, and getting ready for sightseeing in a new city. Full of useful travel vocabulary.

Pause and repeat each travel phrase aloud. Note three new words and use each one to talk about your own Mexico City plan.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Present perfect vs past simple"

A clear, practical video on the difference between the two tenses: experiences with no exact time versus finished actions with a clear time. Exactly today's grammar.

After watching, record a 60-second description of a real trip you took, then describe a place you have always wanted to visit. Mix both tenses.
