Chapter 3 -- The Patterns

The Four Key Patterns

Can I have... How do I get to... I have a... My ... hurts.

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

Look at the orange words. Each sentence is from a different situation.

"Can I have a coffee, please?"

"How do I get to the station?"

"I have a fever."

"My head hurts."

Grammar Rule

Block 2 Patterns

PatternSituationExample
Can I have...?at the storeCan I have a coffee, please?
How do I get to...?asking directionsHow do I get to the station?
I have a + problemat the doctorI have a fever.
My + body part + hurtsa part is painfulMy head hurts.

Key idea: use the right pattern for the right situation. At the store: Can I have. For directions: How do I get to. For health: I have a and My ... hurts.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I am fever."
"I have a fever."

For a health problem, use I have a. Say "I have a fever", "I have a cough".
