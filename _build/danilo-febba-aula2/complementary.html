Lesson 2 -- My Role & My Team

Series
The Office (US) -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

A workplace comedy full of team meetings, daily routines, and managers delegating tasks. Notice how the characters describe what they do every day and how the team works. Direct connection to Lesson 2: present simple and frequency words to talk about routines and responsibilities.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down 5 sentences in present simple about what the team does.

Podcast
BBC Learning English -- "6 Minute English" (work & productivity episodes)

Short, clear episodes on everyday and work topics, including routines, time management and teamwork. Direct connection to Lesson 2: hear how speakers describe regular habits with frequency words, and keep training your listening, which is our main focus.

Tip: pick an episode about work or habits. Listen twice and note every frequency word you hear.

YouTube
"Adverbs of Frequency & Present Simple -- Daily Routines" (English grammar)

A short grammar lesson on present simple and where to place adverbs of frequency (always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never), with everyday and work examples. Connection to Lesson 2: exactly the structure you need to describe your week clearly.

Tip: pause after each example and say a sentence about your own routine using the same pattern.
