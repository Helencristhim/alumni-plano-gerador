Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. A tradução em português está entre parênteses. (Listen and read the example.)

Summarize -- to give the main points briefly (resumir)
"Let me summarize the key results."
Conclude -- to end with a final point (concluir)
"I conclude that the North is our best region."
Highlight -- to draw attention to something (destacar)
"I want to highlight one important trend."
Emphasize -- to give special importance to (enfatizar / ressaltar)
"I emphasize that we should act quickly."
Articulate -- to express ideas clearly (articular / expressar bem)
"She can articulate her ideas clearly."
Coherent -- clear and logical (coerente)
"The report is clear and coherent."
Concise -- short and clear (conciso)
"Keep the summary concise."
Persuasive -- able to convince others (persuasivo / convincente)
"Her argument was very persuasive."
Takeaway -- the main message to remember (conclusão / mensagem principal)
"The main takeaway is that turnover fell."
Standpoint -- a point of view (ponto de vista)
"From an HR standpoint, this is good news."
Overall -- considering everything (no geral / de modo geral)
"Overall, our results improved."
Clarity -- the quality of being clear (clareza)
"I present the data with clarity."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Block 2 Review (5 Structures)

GRAMMAR

Esta é uma aula de revisão (milestone). Você junta as 5 estruturas do Bloco 2: comparativos (lower than), superlativos (the best), quantifiers (many/much), past continuous (was/were + -ing) e modais (should/could/might). Use todas para apresentar dados e recomendar decisões.

EstruturaFormaExemplo
Comparativo-er than / more ... thanTurnover is lower than last year.
Superlativothe ...-est / the mostThe North is the best region.
Quantifiersmany/much, a few/a littleWe have many teams and little budget.
Past continuouswas/were + verbo-ingWe were losing staff last quarter.
Modaisshould/could/might + verbo baseWe should invest now.

Atenção: numa apresentação, misture as estruturas com naturalidade: compare, ranqueie, quantifique, conte o que estava acontecendo e recomende a decisão.
