Chapter 3

How to Ask

Ask where a place is and follow the answer

Discover the Rule

Listen and Notice

"Where is the museum?"

"How do I get to the station?"

"Go straight and turn left."

"It is next to the bank."

StructureUseExample
Where is + place?Ask the locationWhere is the museum?
Go / Turn + directionGive directions (imperative)Turn left at the corner.
It is + next to / across from / betweenSay where a place isIt is next to the park.

To give directions, use the simple form: Go, Turn (no "you").
