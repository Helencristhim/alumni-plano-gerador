Aula 19 -- Pre-class

Closing the Deal -- Finalizing and Reported Speech

Aprenda as palavras-chave de fechar um negócio (finalize, confirm, witness, effective date, enforce, valid, mutual, proceed) e o reported speech para relatar o que alguém disse: said (sem pessoa) e told + pessoa, com o verbo recuando um passo (is → was, will → would) -- "He said the price was final".

Stage 1.1: Key Words

Vocabulary

Eight words you hear when a deal is closing. Listen to each one and notice how it is used.

Finalize -- finalizar (deixar pronto para assinar)
"We are ready to finalize the agreement."
Confirm -- confirmar (dizer oficialmente que aceita)
"Please confirm the terms in writing."
Witness -- testemunha (quem confirma a assinatura)
"We need two witnesses for the signing."
Effective date -- data de início (dia em que o contrato começa a valer)
"The effective date is the first of next month."
Enforce -- fazer cumprir (obrigar a seguir as regras)
"A court can enforce a signed contract."
Valid -- válido (legalmente bom e em vigor)
"The contract is valid for two years."
Mutual -- mútuo (compartilhado e aceito pelos dois lados)
"This is a mutual agreement we both want."
Proceed -- prosseguir (continuar, seguir em frente)
"If you confirm, we will proceed with the signing."

Grammar

At the meeting, the client said the price was too high. Our director told them the quality was worth it. Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Reported Speech

Grammar

Para relatar o que alguém disse, use said (sem pessoa depois: he said) ou told + pessoa (he told me, she told us). O verbo recua um passo para o passado: is → was, will → would, need → needed. Nunca diga "he said me" (errado) -- é "he told me" ou "he said". Nunca use "told" sem uma pessoa depois. / To report speech, use "said" (no person) or "told" + a person, and move the verb one step into the past: is to was, will to would.

Direct wordsReportedPortuguês
"The price is final."He said the price was final.Ele disse que o preço era final.
"I will sign Monday."She said she would sign on Monday.Ela disse que assinaria na segunda.
"We need more time."They told us they needed time.Eles nos disseram que precisavam de tempo.
ErradoHe said me / She told that...(use "told me" ou "said", nunca "said me")
