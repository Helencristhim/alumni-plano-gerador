Chapter 3: He Said, She Told

Reported Speech

How to report what people said.

Discover the Code

Listen and Find the Pattern

Direct: "The price is final." → He said the price was final.

Direct: "I will sign on Monday." → She said she would sign on Monday.

Direct: "We need more time." → They told us they needed more time.

Direct: "I accept the terms." → He told me he accepted the terms.

Direct wordsReportedChange
"The price is final."He said the price was final.is → was
"I will sign."She said she would sign.will → would
"We need more time."They told us they needed time.need → needed

To report what someone said, use said or told and move the verb one step into the past: is → was, will → would, need → needed. Use said alone, but told + a person (told me, told us). NEVER "he said me" — it is "he told me" or "he said".

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"He said me the price is final."

✓ RIGHT

"He told me the price was final."

✗ WRONG

"She told that she will sign Monday."

✓ RIGHT

"She said she would sign on Monday."

Use "told" with a person (told me, told us), and "said" without a person (he said). Move the verb back one step: is becomes was, will becomes would. Never "said me" and never "told" with no person after it.
