The Question Everyone Asks

In Lesson 2 you learned to break the ice and make small talk.

Then comes the big question: "So, what do you do?"

A good answer has three parts: your role, your company, and your main responsibility.

Thirty seconds of confidence opens every door.

Chapter 3

The Pitch Pattern

Present simple + role, company, responsibility

Pitch Discovery

Listen and Notice

I am an IT Manager at SPF Group.

We distribute Nike and Centauro products.

I oversee all technology projects.

I lead a team of twelve people.

What tense are the verbs in? What three things does the pitch tell us?

Pitch Rule

The Three-Part Pitch

PartPatternExample
1. RoleI am a / an + position + at + company"I am an IT Manager at SPF Group."
2. CompanyWe + verb + what the company does"We distribute Nike and Centauro products."
3. ResponsibilityI oversee / I lead / My main responsibility is..."I oversee all technology projects."

All in the present simple, because it is true every day: I oversee, we distribute, I lead.

Common Mistake

Wrong Form vs Right Form

✗ WRONG

"I am leading a team of twelve people."

✓ RIGHT

"I lead a team of twelve people." (daily job = present simple)

✗ WRONG

"I responsible for the IT infrastructure."

✓ RIGHT

"I am responsible for the IT infrastructure." (need "am")

For your daily job, use the present simple (I lead, I oversee). With "responsible", always add "am/is/are".

Meeting a Contact

Daniela and Marcus introduce their jobs

Dialogue

M
Hi, I am Marcus. So, Daniela, what do you do?
D
I am an IT Manager at SPF Group, here in São Paulo.
M
Nice. And what does SPF Group do?
D
We distribute Nike and Centauro products to stores across Brazil. We work in retail.
M
And what is your main responsibility there?

I oversee all technology projects, and I lead a team of twelve people.
M
That is great. We implement similar systems in the US.

Perfect. I am responsible for the infrastructure too. We should talk more.
Comprehension

Did You Catch It?

1. What is Marcus's job?
He is also an IT manager — he works in retail technology and implements similar systems in the US.
2. What does SPF Group do?
It distributes Nike and Centauro products across Brazil; it works in retail.
3. What two responsibilities did Daniela mention?
She oversees all technology projects and leads a team of twelve people; she is also responsible for the infrastructure.
