Grammar Rule

Direction Commands

CommandMeaningExample
Go straightcontinue aheadGo straight for two minutes.
Turn leftgo to the leftTurn left at the corner.
Turn rightgo to the rightTurn right after the bank.

Key idea: to give a direction, start with the base verb -- Go, Turn, Take. We do not say "you" and we do not use "to". Just "Go straight" and "Turn left".

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"Turn to left at the corner."
"Turn left at the corner."

After turn, say left or right directly. Do not use "to". Say "Turn left" and "Turn right".
