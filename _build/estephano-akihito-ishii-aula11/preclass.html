Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Bus -- a big vehicle that carries many people on the road
"I go to school by bus." (ônibus)
Train -- a long vehicle that runs on rails
"I take the train to work." (trem)
Subway -- a train that runs under the ground
"The subway is fast." (metrô)
Ticket -- a small paper you buy to travel
"I buy a ticket." (passagem / bilhete)
Station -- a place where trains and buses stop
"I wait at the station." (estação)
Left -- the opposite of right
"Turn left at the corner." (esquerda)
Right -- the opposite of left
"Turn right after the bank." (direita)
Straight -- ahead; not turning left or right
"Go straight for two minutes." (em frente / reto)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Giving Directions

GRAMMAR

Dar direcções com comandos simples. / Giving directions with simple commands.

Command / ComandoMeaning / SignificadoExample
Go straightSiga em frenteGo straight for two minutes.
Turn leftVire à esquerdaTurn left at the corner.
Turn rightVire à direitaTurn right after the bank.
Atenção: para dar uma direção, comece com o verbo base (Go, Turn, Take). Não use "you" e não use "to": "Turn left" (nunca "Turn to left") e "Go straight" (nunca "Go to straight").
