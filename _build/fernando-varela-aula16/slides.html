Chapter 3

The Action-Item Code

I am sending · We are meeting · I'll prepare

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I am sending the minutes tomorrow."

"We are meeting on Friday."

"I'll prepare the recap."

"I will assign the task to Maria."

StructureUseExample
am/is/are + verb-ingfuture plan, already arrangedI am sending the minutes tomorrow
am/is/are + verb-ing + dayarranged meetingwe are meeting on Friday
will / I'll + verboffer or decision nowI'll prepare the recap
will + verbI will assign the task (a decision)
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"I am send the minutes tomorrow."

✓ RIGHT

"I am sending the minutes tomorrow."

✗ WRONG

"We are meet on Friday."

✓ RIGHT

"We are meeting on Friday."

✗ WRONG

"I prepare the recap." (an offer now)

✓ RIGHT

"I'll prepare the recap."

For an arranged plan, use am/is/are + verb-ing. For an offer or a decision now, use will (I'll).
