Aula 16 -- Pre-class

Follow-up & Action Items

The meeting ends: wrap up, recap the decisions and assign the action items (who does what, by when). Key words: task, assign, minutes, owner, due, deliverable, prioritize, recap, wrap up, action point. Structure: present continuous for future arrangements (I am sending the minutes tomorrow; we are meeting on Friday) and will for offers and decisions (I'll prepare the recap).

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para escutar.

Task -- tarefa (a piece of work to do)
"This task is for you."
Assign -- atribuir / designar (to give a task to a person)
"I assign the task to Maria."
Minutes -- ata (the written notes of a meeting)
"I am sending the minutes."
Owner -- responsavel (the person responsible for a task)
"Who is the owner of this task?"
Due -- com prazo / a vencer (the time when something must be ready)
"The report is due on Friday."
Deliverable -- entregavel (a result you must produce)
"The deliverable is the budget."
Prioritize -- priorizar (to put the most important first)
"We prioritize the report."
Recap -- resumo / recapitulacao (a short summary of the main points)
"Here is a quick recap."
Wrap up -- encerrar / finalizar (to finish or close something)
"Let us wrap up the meeting."
Action point -- ponto de acao / item de acao (a task decided in a meeting)
"We have three action points."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

The meeting is over. Fernando and Sarah wrap up and recap the main points. There are three action points. The first task is the report, and Fernando is the owner. "I am sending the minutes tomorrow," he says. The second deliverable is the budget; it is due on Friday. "OK, I'll prepare the recap for the team," Fernando offers. "Let us prioritize the report. We are meeting again next week."

1. Por que dizemos "I am sending the minutes tomorrow" e nao "I send"?
A Porque a acao acontece agora.
B Present continuous (am + verb-ing) expressa um plano futuro ja combinado.
C Porque "minutes" e plural.
2. "I'll prepare the recap" usa will porque:
A E um plano antigo, ja combinado.
B E uma oferta/decisao feita no momento da fala.
C E uma acao no passado.
3. Which sentence is correct?
A "We are meet on Friday."
B "We are meeting on Friday."
C "We am meeting on Friday."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Plans & Offers

GRAMMAR

Como falar de um plano futuro e como oferecer ajuda (explicacao em ingles e portugues).

StructureUse / UsoExample
am / is / are + verb-ingPlano futuro ja combinadoI am sending the minutes tomorrow

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

C "I am tired."

A "I am send the minutes tomorrow."

Stage 5: Free Production

Reflection
