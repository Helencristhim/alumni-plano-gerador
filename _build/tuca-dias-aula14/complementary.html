Lesson 14 -- Making Plans: Reservations & Bookings

Series / Travel
Wolters World -- "How to Book a Hotel & Avoid Booking Mistakes" (YouTube)

A friendly traveler explains how to make a reservation, check availability, read the cancellation policy, and avoid booking mistakes. Full of natural booking vocabulary in real situations.

Watch one video with English subtitles. Write three sentences reporting what the video said, using "He said that..." and "He told us to...".

Podcast
All Ears English -- "Booking a Hotel and Making Reservations in English"

A short, clear episode about making plans in English: booking a room, asking about availability, and confirming or cancelling. You will hear the words reservation, deposit, and refund used naturally.

After listening, record a one-minute voice note reporting what the hosts said. Use said, told, and asked, plus the words booking, deposit, and deadline.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Reported Speech: said, told and backshift"

A practical grammar video that explains how to tell someone what another person said, with said, told, and the backshift of tenses. Exactly today's grammar focus.

After watching, write four reported sentences about a phone call, each one moving the verb one step back (have to had, will to would).
