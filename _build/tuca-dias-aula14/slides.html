Chapter 3 -- Reported Speech

She Said That...

Telling someone what another person said

Listen & Repeat

Reported Speech in Action

"The hotel said that they had availability."

"She told me that the deposit was fifty dollars."

"He said the voucher would arrive by email."

"She asked me to confirm the booking by Friday."

Grammar Rule

Reported Speech: Said / Told, Backshift

DirectReportedChange
"We have a room."She said they had a room.have → had (no person after said)
"I will send it."He told me he would send it.will → would; told + person
"Confirm by Friday."She asked me to confirm.request: asked + person + to + verb

Key idea: in reported speech the verb usually shifts one step back: have → had, will → would, can → could. Use said with no person, or told + a person. For a request, use asked + person + to + verb.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"She said me that the room was free."
"She told me that the room was free."

Use said with no person ("she said that..."), or told + a person ("she told me that...").

Grammar Practice

Complete the Sentence

"The hotel _____said that they had a room." (report, no person)
"She _____told me that the deposit was small." (report + person)
"He said the voucher _____would come by email." (will changes to this)
"She asked me _____to confirm by Friday." (a request)
