What is the job of each orange phrase in a talk?

Grammar Rule

The Linking Toolkit

JobLinking language
Signpost the partsFirst, ... Next, ... Finally, ...
Give cause and effect..., as a result, ... / ..., therefore, ...
SynthesizeTo synthesize, ... / To sum up, ...
Close the talkIn my closing remarks, ...

Key idea: A strong talk does not jump between ideas. Use linking language to integrate the parts: signpost (first / next / finally), connect cause and effect (as a result / therefore), synthesize (to synthesize / to sum up), and finish with closing remarks. This is how you bring the whole journey together.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"The sample was large. The data is robust. The end."
"The sample was large, as a result, the data is robust. To synthesize, the message is clear."

Use linking language to integrate your ideas. A talk with no connectors sounds like a list; one with signposting and cause-effect flows as a story.
