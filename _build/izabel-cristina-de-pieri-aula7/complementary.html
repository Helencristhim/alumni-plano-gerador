Aula 7 -- The Road Ahead -- Talking About Plans and the Future

Series
Billions -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

Hedge-fund and corporate leaders spend every scene talking about the road ahead: the next move, the forecast, the plan. Listen for future forms in action -- "We are going to build this," "I will handle it," "We are meeting them Thursday." It is exactly the language you use when the board asks about your roadmap.

Tip: watch one strategy scene with English subtitles. Write down five future sentences and mark whether each one is "will", "going to", or present continuous.

"Will, going to, present continuous -- 3 English future forms" -- Simple English Videos

A clear lesson on the three ways English talks about the future: "will" for instant decisions and predictions, "be going to" for decided plans, and the present continuous for fixed arrangements. Exactly the code you practiced in Lesson 7 -- and why "I meet them Tuesday" should be "I am meeting them on Tuesday".

Tip: pause after each example and say your own version about AVRT. Then record a 90-second roadmap for the next quarter using all three forms.
