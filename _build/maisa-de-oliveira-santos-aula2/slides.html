Chapter 4: The Code

Questions & This is...

What · Where · How + This is

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"What is your name? My name is Maísa."

"Where are you from? I am from São Paulo."

"How are you? I am great, thank you."

"David, this is Paula. She is my colleague."

Question wordAsks aboutExample
Whata thing / a nameWhat is your name?
Wherea placeWhere are you from?
Howa feeling / a wayHow are you?

The order is: question word + is / are + person. To introduce someone, use This is + name.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

Where you from?
Where are you from?
This my colleague, Paula.
This is my colleague, Paula.

A WH question needs are / is: "Where are you from?". And never drop is: "This is my colleague."
