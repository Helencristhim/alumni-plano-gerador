Hello, I'm Maísa

Apresentar-se com confiança e apresentar OUTRAS pessoas: How are you?, Where are you from?, This is..., pleased to meet you, the team, the manager, Welcome!, How do you do?. Gramática: perguntas WH (What / Where / How + to be) + "This is..." para apresentar alguém. Missão: receber e apresentar a equipe.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada expressão e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

How are you? -- a friendly question about someone's day
"Hi, David. How are you today?" (Como vai você?)
Where are you from? -- a question about your city or country
"Where are you from? I am from São Paulo." (De onde você é?)
This is... -- you use this to introduce a person
"David, this is Paula. She is my colleague." (Este/Esta é...)
Pleased to meet you -- a polite phrase when you meet someone new
"Pleased to meet you, David." (Prazer em conhecê-lo)
The team -- a group of people who work together
"Welcome to the finance team." (O time / a equipe)
The manager -- the person who leads a team
"Sarah is the manager of the finance team." (O gerente)
Welcome! -- what you say to receive a new person
"Welcome to the company, Maísa!" (Bem-vindo(a)!)
How do you do? -- a formal way to say hello
"How do you do? Pleased to meet you." (Muito prazer / Como vai?)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- WH questions & This is

GRAMMAR

Perguntas com WH usam a ordem: palavra-pergunta + is/are + pessoa. / WH word + is/are + person.

Palavra / WordPergunta sobre / Asks aboutExemplo / Example
What (o quê)uma coisa / um nomeWhat is your name?
Where (onde)um lugarWhere are you from?
How (como)um sentimento / um modoHow are you?
This is (este/esta é)apresentar alguémThis is my colleague, Paula.

Atenção: a pergunta WH precisa do verbo to be — "Where are you from?", não "Where you from?". E nunca omita o is em "This is my colleague".
