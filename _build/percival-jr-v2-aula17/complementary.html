Aula 17 -- Handling Difficult Emails

Tema da aula: handling difficult emails -- making requests politely (I was wondering if you could / Would it be possible to), refusing politely with a reason (I am afraid we cannot...), and softening the message. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"How to write a polite request in English" -- business email video

A short, clear video about making polite requests and saying no without sounding rude. Listen for "I was wondering if you could...", "Would it be possible to...?" and "I am afraid we cannot..., as...".

Dica: anote duas formas de pedir um favor com educação e duas de recusar com uma razão.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "Polite requests & indirect questions"

Short lessons and quizzes on softening language: turning orders into indirect questions ("Could you possibly...?", "I was wondering if you could...") and refusing politely. Perfect for difficult work emails.

Dica: escreva cinco ordens diretas e transforme cada uma em um pedido educado.

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about saying no or being diplomatic

A short, friendly podcast for B1 learners. Listen for how people ask for favours, refuse politely and give a reason without sounding harsh.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote uma forma educada de recusar um pedido.
