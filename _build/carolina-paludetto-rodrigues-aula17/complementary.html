Aula 17 -- Giving Advice (should & shouldn't)

Tema da aula: Giving Advice -- helping a friend with should and shouldn't. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
Giving Advice in English -- should and shouldn't

Watch a short, clear video about giving advice. Listen for should + base verb (You should rest), the negative shouldn't (You shouldn't worry), questions with Should I...?, and soft advice with Why don't you...?

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote cinco conselhos com should e dois com shouldn't.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- modals: should for advice

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about should and shouldn't: how to use should + base verb, the negative shouldn't, questions with Should I...?, and how to soften advice.

Dica: escreva seis conselhos para um amigo estressado. Sublinhe should e shouldn't, e circule o verbo na forma base.

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about stress, study tips or friendship

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen to people talk about problems, stress and how to feel better -- great input to review today's words and advice with should and shouldn't.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote todo conselho com should (you should...) e toda palavra nova (advice, stressed, worried, focus, calm).
