Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Opinion -- what a person thinks about something
"What is your opinion on the new timeline?" (Opinião)
Point of view -- the way a person sees a situation
"From my point of view, quality comes first." (Ponto de vista)
Agree -- to have the same opinion
"I agree with your point of view." (Concordar)
Disagree -- to have a different opinion
"I disagree, because the deadline is too tight." (Discordar)
Persuade -- to make someone want to do something
"We need to persuade the client with a clear plan." (Persuadir)
Convince -- to make someone believe something is true
"Good reasons will convince the team." (Convencer)
Argument -- a reason that supports an opinion
"My main argument is that quality matters most." (Argumento)
Emphasize -- to give a point special importance
"I want to emphasize one key point." (Enfatizar)
Stance -- a clear position on a topic
"We should present one united stance." (Posição)
Common ground -- an idea two sides both agree on
"We found common ground on a new plan." (Base comum)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Opinion Phrases

GRAMMAR

Para dar opiniao, use I think (neutro) ou In my view (mais formal), seguidos de uma ideia completa (sujeito + verbo). Para reagir, use I agree / I disagree; para discordar com educacao, I see your point, but... Lembre: agree e verbo, entao nunca diga "I am agree". / To give an opinion, use I think (neutral) or In my view (more formal) plus a full idea (subject + verb). To react, use I agree / I disagree; to disagree politely, I see your point, but... Remember: agree is a verb, so never say "I am agree".

PhraseUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
I think ...Give your opinion (neutral) -- opiniao neutra"I think we should wait."
In my view, ...Give your opinion (formal) -- opiniao formal"In my view, the risk is high."
I agree / I disagreeReact to an opinion -- reagir"I agree with you."
Regra de ourofrase de opiniao + ideia completa (sujeito + verbo). Nunca "I am agree" -- diga "I agree".
