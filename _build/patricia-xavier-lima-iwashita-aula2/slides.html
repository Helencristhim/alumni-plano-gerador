Chapter 3: The Code

Finished vs Up to Now

I joined · I have worked

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I joined Salesforce in 2019."

"Last year, I led a global rollout."

"I have worked with teams in India and the US."

"I have handled many difficult clients."

What is different about the green verbs?

FormUseExample
Past Simple
verb + ed / irregular		A finished action with a clear past timeI joined in 2019.
Present Perfect
have / has + past participle		Experience up to now, time not finishedI have worked here since 2019.
Signal wordsin 2019, last year, ago → past simple · for, since, so far, recently, ever, never → present perfect

The past participle of lead is led; of handle is handled; of be is been.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

"I work here since 2019."
"I have worked here since 2019."
"I have joined the team last year."
"I joined the team last year."

Use the Present Perfect with for and since (time up to now). Use the Past Simple with a finished time like last year or in 2019.
