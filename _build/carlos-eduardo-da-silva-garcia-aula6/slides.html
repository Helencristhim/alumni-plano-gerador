Chapter 4: Have & Has

Have & Has

I have · the farm has · it doesn't have

Discover the Grammar

Read and Notice

"I have access to the IMS."

"The farm has a certificate."

"It doesn't have a plan."

"Do you have the spreadsheet?"

SubjectVerbExample
I / you / we / theyhaveI have access.
he / she / ithasThe farm has a certificate.
Negativedon't / doesn't haveIt doesn't have a plan.
QuestionDo you have it? Does the farm have it?

He / she / it = has. Negative and question use do / does + have. / Com he/she/it use has; negativa e pergunta usam do/does + have.

Language Focus

Have or Be?

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"The farm have a certificate."

✓ RIGHT

"The farm has a certificate."

✗ WRONG

"I am six documents."

✓ RIGHT

"I have six documents."

With he/she/it use has. Use have for possession, to be for descriptions. / he/she/it = has; have = posse, to be = descricao.

E
Hi Carlos. Does Fazenda Sao Joao have all the required documents?
C
It has an Impact certificate, but it doesn't have a corrective action plan.
E
And does the warehouse have the updated checklist?
C
Yes, it has the new version of the checklist in the database.
E
Do you have access to all the producer files?
C
Yes, I have full access to the IMS. Every producer has a file there.
E
Good. Which farm doesn't have IMS access yet?
C
Fazenda Boa Vista doesn't have access yet. I help the producer this week.
