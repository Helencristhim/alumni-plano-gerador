Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Revise cada palavra-chave do bloco. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Review each key word from the block.

Reservation -- a room you booked before you arrive (reserva)
"I have a reservation." (Eu tenho uma reserva.)
Elevator -- the machine that takes you up and down (elevador)
"Where is the elevator?" (Onde fica o elevador?)
Corner -- the place where two streets meet (esquina)
"The cafe is on the corner." (O café fica na esquina.)
Crosswalk -- the white lines where you cross the street (faixa de pedestres)
"Cross at the crosswalk." (Atravesse na faixa de pedestres.)
Cash -- paper money and coins (dinheiro em espécie)
"I will pay with cash." (Vou pagar em dinheiro.)
Discount -- money off the price (desconto)
"There is a discount today." (Hoje tem desconto.)
Menu -- the list of food and prices (cardápio)
"Could I see the menu, please?" (Posso ver o cardápio, por favor?)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas. / Read the text and answer the questions.

Marlene has a busy day. First, she says, "I have a reservation," and takes the elevator to her room. Then she goes out. "Go straight and turn left at the corner," a local says. Marlene crosses at the crosswalk. At the market, she asks, "How much is this?" There is a discount, so she pays with cash. For lunch, she reads the menu and orders some water. (Marlene faz o check-in, pede direções, faz compras e almoça.)

1. How does Marlene go to her room?
A She takes the elevator.
B She takes a taxi.
C She takes the menu.
2. How does she pay at the market?
A With her passport.
B With cash, because there is a discount.
C With the elevator.
3. What does she do for lunch?
A She reads the menu and orders some water.
B She goes back to the hotel.
C She asks for the boarding pass.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Block 3 Summary

GRAMMAR

Revisão dos quatro padrões do bloco: presente contínuo (I am staying) para agora; comandos (Go straight) para direções; How much is/are para preços; a/some/any para pedir comida. / Review of the four block patterns.

PatternExample (EN)Tradução (PT)
Present continuousI am staying here.Estou ficando aqui.
DirectionsGo straight, turn left.Siga em frente, vire à esquerda.
How muchHow much is this?Quanto custa isto?
a / some / anyCould I have some water?Pode me trazer um pouco de água?

Key point / Ponto-chave: um dia, quatro padrões. I am ...ing para agora; Go/Turn para direções; How much is/are para preços; a/some/any para pedir comida.
