The Summary

Block 3 Grammar

PatternUse it forExample
Present continuoussomething happening nowI am staying here.
Directions (command)telling the wayGo straight, turn left.
How much is / areasking a priceHow much is this?
a / some / anyordering food and drinksCould I have some water?

Key idea: one day, four patterns. I am ...ing for now; Go/Turn for directions; How much is/are for prices; a/some/any for ordering.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"How much is these shoes?"
"How much are these shoes?"

Remember the block: many things = are; one thing = is. And use some water, not "a water".
