Stage 1.1: Plane & Arrival Vocabulary

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Estas palavras ajudam voce no voo e na chegada em outro pais.

Cabin crew -- tripulacao (quem ajuda no aviao)
"The cabin crew welcomed us on board."
Seatbelt -- cinto de seguranca
"Please fasten your seatbelt for take-off."
Tray table -- mesinha (na frente do assento)
"Please close your tray table before landing."
Landing -- pouso (quando o aviao chega ao chao)
"The landing was smooth and we arrived on time."
Turbulence -- turbulencia (quando o aviao balanca)
"There was some turbulence, but it was fine."
Immigration -- imigracao (controle de passaporte)
"At immigration, the officer checked my passport."
Customs -- alfandega (controle do que voce traz)
"At customs, I had nothing to declare."
Declare -- declarar (dizer o que voce traz)
"Do you have anything to declare?"

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia a situacao e responda as perguntas.

On the plane, Roberto listens to the cabin crew. Before the plane takes off, he fastens his seatbelt. After it takes off, he opens his tray table. When there is turbulence, he stays in his seat. Before the plane lands, he closes the tray table. After he leaves the plane, he goes to immigration, and the officer checks his passport. At customs, he has nothing to declare, so he walks straight through.

1. What does Roberto do before the plane takes off?
A He opens his tray table.
B He fastens his seatbelt.
C He goes to customs.
2. Where does he go after he leaves the plane?
A Back to his seat.
B To immigration.
C To the tray table.
3. After "before", "after" and "when", which verb form is correct?
A will + verb (will land).
B the present simple (lands).
C the past simple (landed).

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- When, After & Before

Grammar

Use when, after e before para colocar duas acoes em ordem. Depois dessas palavras, use o presente simples, mesmo falando do futuro: "When the plane lands..." (NUNCA "when the plane will land"). "Before" = isso acontece primeiro; "after" = isso acontece depois; "when" = naquele momento. Voce pode comecar com a palavra de tempo ou coloca-la no meio: "I fasten my seatbelt when there is turbulence." / Use when, after and before to order two actions. After these words, use the present simple, even about the future.

Palavra / WordExemplo / ExampleUso / Use
when + presenteWhen the plane lands, you turn on your phone.naquele momento / sempre que
after + presenteAfter you leave the plane, you go to immigration.isso acontece depois
before + presenteBefore you land, you close the tray table.isso acontece primeiro
no meio da fraseI fasten my seatbelt when there is turbulence.mesma ideia, ordem trocada

Stage 3: Pronunciation

Speaking

Ouca cada frase e depois grave-se dizendo. Atencao ao "t" MUDO de "fasten" e ao som de "turbulence".

Quiz

A "When there is turbulence, I fasten my seatbelt."
