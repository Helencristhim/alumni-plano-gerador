Weather and Seasons -- How Is the Sky Today?

Fale sobre o tempo e as estações: weather, sunny, rain, cloudy, windy, hot, cold, season. Gramática: present continuous (It is raining now, the sun is shining) para dizer o que acontece AGORA. Aprenda o "small talk" do dia a dia.

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. Veja o present continuous (am/is/are + verbo com -ing) em ação.

Good morning! Look out the window. The weather is not good today. It is raining now, and the sky is grey and cloudy. The wind is cold and the day is windy. I am wearing a warm jacket, and I am drinking a hot coffee. My daughter is reading a book on the sofa. It is not a sunny day, but my favorite season is spring, and spring is near!

1. "It is raining now." Is this about now or about every day?
A About now (the present continuous: is + raining).
B About every day (a habit).
C About the past.
2. "I am wearing a jacket." Why is there "am" before "wearing"?
A Because "wearing" is a noun.
B Because the present continuous needs am/is/are + verb-ing.
C Because it is a question.
3. How is the weather in the text?
A Sunny and hot.
B Rainy, cloudy and cold.
C Hot and windy.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Continuous (now)

GRAMMAR

Para dizer o que acontece AGORA, usamos o present continuous: am/is/are + verbo com -ing (It is raining = está chovendo). O português não usa o verbo "ser/estar" do mesmo jeito, então lembre do am/is/are. / To say what is happening NOW, use the present continuous: am/is/are + verb-ing.

Sujeitobe + verbo-ing (agora)
II am wearing a jacket — estou usando uma jaqueta
it / he / she / the sunit is raining — está chovendo
we / you / theythey are playing — eles estão brincando
negativothe sun is not shining — o sol não está brilhando
