Chapter 3

The Now Code

Present continuous · happening now

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"It is raining now."

"The sun is shining."

"I am wearing a warm jacket."

"The children are playing outside."

Subjectbe + verb-ing (now)
II am wearing a jacket
it / the sunit is raining · the sun is shining
we / they / the childrenthey are playing outside
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"It rain now."

✓ RIGHT

"It is raining now."

✗ WRONG

"The sun shining."

✓ RIGHT

"The sun is shining."

✗ WRONG

"I wearing a jacket."

✓ RIGHT

"I am wearing a jacket."

For something happening NOW, you need two parts: am, is or are + the verb with -ing. It is raining. The sun is shining. I am wearing.
