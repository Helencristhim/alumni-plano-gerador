Aula 15 -- Present Perfect in Business

Tema da aula: present your track record -- present perfect for experience and results (I have closed three deals this quarter) vs simple past for finished, dated events (I led the restructuring in 2019). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

TED Talk
TED -- Leaders telling their career stories

Watch how senior leaders present their own track record on stage. Listen for the present perfect for experience and ongoing results, and the simple past for the specific projects they finished.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote três frases no present perfect (I have...) e uma no simple past com data (in 2019 / last year).

Podcast
NPR -- "How I Built This" (Guy Raz)

Founders and executives describe how they built their companies. A perfect input for present perfect vs simple past: their journey so far (have built, have grown) and the dated turning points (in that year, we launched...).

Dica: ouça um episódio e separe duas falas: uma de experiência/resultado (present perfect) e uma de evento datado (simple past).

YouTube
"How to write a LinkedIn summary" (career coaches)

Short videos on writing a professional summary in English. Notice how strong summaries use the present perfect for a track record and the simple past for specific, dated achievements -- exactly your homework.

Dica: escreva o seu próprio resumo de LinkedIn com três frases no present perfect e uma no simple past com data. Leia em voz alta.
