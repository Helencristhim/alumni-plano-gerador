Aula 15 -- Present Perfect in Business -- Experience & Results

Tema da aula: tell your professional story like a seasoned executive -- the present perfect for experience and results in an open period (I have managed this holding for over ten years; we have closed three deals this quarter), the simple past for finished, dated milestones (I led the restructuring in 2019), and the passive voice for results (the target was exceeded; the groundwork has been laid). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Talk
TED Talk -- a leader telling their career story

Search TED for a CEO or founder telling the story of a business they turned around. Listen for how they describe their experience in the present perfect ("I have spent twenty years building this") and report dated moments in the simple past ("in 2015, we launched..."), plus results in the passive ("the company was saved", "targets have been surpassed"). The exact tense-switching of this lesson.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote duas frases no present perfect (experiência/resultado) e duas no simple past (eventos com data).
