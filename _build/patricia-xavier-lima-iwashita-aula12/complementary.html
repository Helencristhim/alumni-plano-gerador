Silicon Valley (HBO) -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Minimum Viable Product"

A small tech team plans what to build first and what to leave for later. Listen for how they talk about features, releases and roadmaps, and how they decide what to prioritize.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down three sentences about future plans, then rewrite each one in a different future form ("we are going to build" / "we are launching" / "it will help").
