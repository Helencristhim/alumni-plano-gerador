Chapter 4: The Code

How much / How many + There is/are

Prices · numbers · quantities

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"How much is the report? The price is 2,500 reais."

"How many reports are there? There are four."

"There is a discount of 10 percent."

"There are two dates: July 15 and August 15."

StructureUse it forExample
How muchprice / a thing you cannot countHow much is it?
How manythings you can countHow many reports?
There isone thing (singular)There is one meeting.
There aremany things (plural)There are two dates.

Use How much for a price and How many for a number of things. Use There is for one, and There are for two or more.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

How much people?
How many people?
There is two dates.
There are two dates.

Count people, reports and dates with How many. Use There are when the number is two or more: "There are two dates."
