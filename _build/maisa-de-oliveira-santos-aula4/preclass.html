Aula 04 -- Pre-class

Your World in Numbers

Preços, datas, números e small talk com clientes: How much?, price, expensive, cheap, a budget, a discount, percent, a date. Gramática: números, preços e datas + How much / How many + There is / There are (quantidades). Missão: dar o preço, o desconto e as datas de pagamento a um cliente.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

How much? -- you ask this to know the price of something
"How much is the report? It is 2,500 reais." (Quanto custa?)
Price -- how much money something costs
"The price is 2,500 reais." (o preço)
Expensive -- it costs a lot of money
"A new car is expensive." (caro)
Cheap -- it costs little money
"This pen is cheap. It is only 2 reais." (barato)
A budget -- the money you have to spend on something
"Our budget this year is 100,000 reais." (o orçamento)
A discount -- money off the normal price
"There is a discount of 10 percent for you." (o desconto)
Percent -- a part of one hundred, the sign is %
"Ten percent of 100 is 10." (por cento)
A date -- a day on the calendar
"The payment date is July 15." (a data)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

Tuesday, July 8. Maísa has a call with a client, David. First, small talk: "How are you? How is the weather in Rio?" Then David asks: "How much is the new report?" Maísa answers: "The price is 2,500 reais. But there is a discount of 10 percent for you. The final price is 2,250 reais." David is happy: "That is not expensive. It is a good price." Maísa adds: "There are two payment dates: July 15 and August 15." David asks: "How many reports are there this year?" "Four reports, one every three months," she says.

1. Como o David pergunta o preço? / How does David ask the price?
A "How many is the report?"
B "How much is the report?"
C "How much reports?"
2. Quantas datas de pagamento existem? / How many payment dates are there?
A There are two: July 15 and August 15.
B There is one: July 8.
C There are ten.
3. O David acha o preço caro? / Does David think the price is expensive?
A Yes, it is very expensive.
B No, he says it is not expensive.
C He does not say.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- How much / How many & There is / There are

GRAMMAR

Use How much para preço; How many para coisas contáveis. There is para singular; There are para plural. / How much for price; How many for countable things; There is + singular; There are + plural.

Estrutura / StructureUsar para / Use forExemplo / Example
How much (quanto)preço / coisa não contávelHow much is it?
How many (quantos)coisas que se contamHow many reports?
There is (há - singular)uma coisaThere is one meeting.
There are (há - plural)duas ou mais coisasThere are two dates.

Atenção: coisas contáveis (people, reports, dates) usam How many, não "How much". E com plural use there are — "There are two dates", nunca "There is two dates".
