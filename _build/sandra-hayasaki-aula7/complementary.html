Aula 7 — Comparing & Choosing

Series
Tidying Up with Marie Kondo — any episode

People decide what to keep and what to let go. Listen for how they weigh options: "this one is better", "the most useful", "is it worth keeping?". A gentle show full of choices and comparisons in clear English.

Dica: escolha uma cena curta e anote uma frase com "better than" e uma com "the best". / Pick one short scene and write one sentence with "better than" and one with "the best".

Podcast
The Allusionist — episodes that compare words and choices

A clear, friendly podcast where the host compares ideas and explains which option works best, in natural English. A great place to hear comparatives and superlatives used in real conversation.

Dica: ouça um episódio e anote três comparativos ou superlativos que você ouviu. / Listen to one episode and note three comparatives or superlatives you heard.
