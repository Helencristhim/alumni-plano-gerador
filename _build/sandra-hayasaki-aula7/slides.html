Chapter 3 -- The Code

Colors and Order

Colors

Six Colors

Look at the orange words. Where is the color -- before or after the clothing?

"I am wearing a red dress."

"She is wearing blue shoes."

"He has a green jacket."

"There is a black hat."

Grammar Rule

Color Before Clothing

FormStructureExample
One (singular)a + color + nouna red dress
Pluralcolor + nounblack shoes
I wearI am wearing...I am wearing a blue shirt.
Wrongnoun + colora dress red

Key idea: say the color FIRST, then the clothing: "a red shirt", not "a shirt red". Colors: red, blue, green, black, white, yellow.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I am wearing a dress red."
"I am wearing a red dress."

The color comes before the clothing, not after.

Grammar Practice

Put the Words in Order

dress / red / a  =  a red dress
shoes / black  =  black shoes
shirt / a / blue  =  a blue shirt
jacket / green / a  =  a green jacket
