Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Shirt -- clothing for the top of your body, with buttons
"I am wearing a blue shirt." (camisa)
T-shirt -- a simple shirt with no buttons
"There is a white t-shirt." (camiseta)
Pants -- clothing for your legs
"I am wearing black pants." (calça)
Dress -- one piece of clothing for women
"She wears a red dress." (vestido)
Skirt -- clothing for the legs, often for women
"There is a yellow skirt." (saia)
Shoes -- you wear them on your feet
"I am wearing white shoes." (sapatos)
Jacket -- warm clothing for cold days
"He has a green jacket." (jaqueta)
Hat -- clothing for your head
"There is a black hat." (chapéu)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Hello! My name is Lucy. Today I am wearing a red dress and white shoes. Red is my favorite color. I also have a yellow skirt and a white t-shirt. I do not wear a jacket today because it is warm. I love bright colors. Notice: the color comes before the clothing -- a red dress, not "a dress red".

1. What color is Lucy's dress?
A Yellow.
B Red.
C White.
2. What is Lucy wearing on her feet?
A White shoes.
B A yellow skirt.
C Black pants.
3. Which sentence is correct?
A "I am wearing a dress red."
B "I am wearing a red dress."
C "I am wearing red a dress."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- adjective before noun

GRAMMAR

A cor (adjetivo) vem ANTES da roupa (substantivo). / The color (adjective) comes BEFORE the clothing (noun).

Form / FormaStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Color + clothing / cor + roupaa + color + nouna red dress
Plural clothing / roupa pluralcolor + nounblack shoes
I wear / eu vistoI am wearing...I am wearing a blue shirt.
Wrong / erradonoun + colora dress red
Key point / Ponto-chaveDiga a cor PRIMEIRO: "a red shirt", não "a shirt red". Colors: red, blue, green, black, white, yellow.
