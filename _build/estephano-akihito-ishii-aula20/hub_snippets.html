Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Win -- to be the best and finish first in the game
"My team wins the match." (ganhar / vencer)
Lose -- the opposite of win: the other team is better
"We sometimes lose the match." (perder)
Attack -- to move forward and fight the enemy
"I usually attack the enemy." (atacar)
Defend -- to protect your base from the enemy
"My friends defend the base." (defender)
Score -- your number of points in the game
"I always check my score after the match." (pontuação / placar)
Level -- a stage in the game -- it gets harder each time
"Last week, I finished level ten." (nível / fase)
Player -- a person who plays the game
"I am a strong player." (jogador)
Team -- a group of players who play together
"I play with my team." (time / equipe)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Action Verbs

GRAMMAR

Como usar os verbos de ação no jogo. / How to use action verbs in the game.

Form / FormaUse / UsoExample
I / We / They + verbSEM -sI attack. We win.
The team / He + verb-sverbo + -s (he, she, it)The team wins. He defends.
do not / does not + verbNegativoI do not lose. He does not lose.
Do / Does + verb...?PerguntaDo you play? Does your team win?
Atenção (lembra da Aula 18): com the team, he ou it, o verbo ganha um -s.
