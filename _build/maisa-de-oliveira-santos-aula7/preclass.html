Aula 07 -- Pre-class

Scheduling a Meeting

Marcar e remarcar reuniões e chamadas: schedule, available, appointment, confirm, postpone, reschedule, book, invite. Gramática: preposições de tempo (on Monday / at 3 PM / in the morning) e como fazer planos (Are you free...? / Can we meet...? / Let's...). Missão: marcar uma reunião do zero com o David.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

Schedule -- to plan a time for a meeting or call
"Can we schedule a meeting this week?" (Agendar / marcar)
Available -- free and able to meet
"I am available on Tuesday afternoon." (Disponível)
Appointment -- a fixed time to meet someone
"I have an appointment at nine." (Compromisso / horário marcado)
Confirm -- to say a time is final and certain
"Please confirm the new time by email." (Confirmar)
Postpone -- to move something to a later time
"Let's postpone the meeting to Friday." (Adiar)
Reschedule -- to change to a new day or time
"Can we reschedule to Thursday?" (Remarcar)
Book -- to reserve a room or a time
"I will book the meeting room." (Reservar)
Invite -- to ask someone to come
"I invite you to a call next week." (Convidar)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

Maísa wants to schedule a meeting with David. She sends him an invite. "Are you free on Monday?" she asks. David is not available on Monday morning. He has an appointment at nine. So they postpone the meeting. "Can we meet on Tuesday, at three in the afternoon?" says David. Maísa confirms the new time. She books a meeting room for the call. On Tuesday, at three PM, they finally meet!

1. Qual preposição usamos com dias? / Which preposition do we use with days?
A "at Monday"
B "on Monday"
C "in Monday"
2. Qual preposição usamos com horas? / Which preposition do we use with clock times?
A "on three PM"
B "at three PM"
C "in three PM"
3. O que a Maísa faz com o novo horário? / What does Maísa do with the new time?
A She cancels the meeting.
B She confirms it and books a room.
C She forgets it.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- On / At / In

GRAMMAR

Usamos on, at e in para falar de tempo. Cada uma tem o seu uso. / We use on, at and in to talk about time.

PreposiçãoUso / UseExemplo / Example
ondias / dayson Monday, on Friday
athoras / clock timesat three PM, at noon
inpartes do dia / parts of the dayin the morning, in the afternoon

Atenção: dia leva on (on Monday), hora leva at (at three PM), parte do dia leva in (in the morning). Para fazer planos: Are you free...?, Can we meet...?, Let's... (Let's + verbo, sem "to": "Let's confirm", nunca "Let's to confirm").
