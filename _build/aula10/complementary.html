Supplementary Materials -- Lesson 10

Lesson theme: Block 2 review -- the board meeting. Watch and listen for everything from Block 2 together: chairing and sequencing, agreeing and disagreeing, negotiating, and handling objections.

Series
Succession -- Season 2, Episode 1 ("The Summer Palace")

A full board-level meeting: a chair runs the room, members debate, deals are negotiated, and objections fly. Watch how power, persuasion, and concession all happen at one table -- the whole of Block 2 in action.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Spot one example of each Block 2 move: chairing, disagreeing, negotiating, handling an objection.

Podcast
Masters of Scale with Reid Hoffman -- board & strategy decisions

Founders and leaders describe the big decisions made around the board table: presenting results, debating strategy, negotiating, and answering tough objections. A great model for an integrated board meeting.

Tip: pick an episode about a hard decision. Note how the speaker opens, argues, concedes, and closes.
