Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Ouca cada verbo e leia o exemplo. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Check -- conferir (olhar para ver se esta certo)
"I check the farm checklists every Monday." (Confiro as listas toda segunda.)
Verify -- verificar (confirmar que algo e verdadeiro)
"I verify supplier compliance every month." (Verifico a conformidade dos fornecedores.)
Send -- enviar (mandar uma mensagem ou documento)
"I send reports to the Rwanda team." (Envio relatorios para o time de Ruanda.)
Attend -- participar de / comparecer a (uma reuniao). Atencao: NAO e "atender"
"I attend meetings with the European team." (Participo de reunioes com o time europeu.)
Report -- reportar / relatar (contar os resultados)
"I report the results to my manager." (Reporto os resultados a minha gerente.)
Review -- revisar (ler de novo com cuidado para melhorar)
"My manager reviews the documents." (Minha gerente revisa os documentos.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

Here is my week at Sucafina. On Mondays, I check the farm checklists. I verify supplier compliance every month, and I always report the results to my manager. I often attend alignment meetings with the Rwanda team. My manager reviews the reports, and she sends feedback to the team. I do not write long documents in English alone yet, but I try every day.

1. Why do we say "My manager reviewS"? (Por que "reviews" com -s?)
A Because "my manager" is he/she (add -s). (Ele/ela = verbo + s.)
B Because it is plural. (Porque e plural.)
C Because it is a question. (Porque e pergunta.)
2. What does "I do not write" mean? (O que significa "do not"?)
A A question. (Uma pergunta.)
B A negative: he does NOT do it. (Uma negativa.)
C The past. (O passado.)
3. When does Carlos check the checklists? (Quando ele confere as listas?)
A On Fridays. (As sextas.)
B On Mondays. (As segundas.)
C Never. (Nunca.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- The Present Simple

Grammar

Como falar da rotina em ingles (present simple). / How to talk about your routine.

Subject / SujeitoVerb / VerboExample / Exemplo
I / you / we / theycheck (forma base)I check the checklists. (Eu confiro as listas.)
he / she / itchecks (verbo + s)She checks the report. (Ela confere o relatorio.)
Negative / Negativado not / does notI do not write. He does not write. (don't / doesn't)
Question / PerguntaDo / Does + verboDo you attend meetings? Does she review reports?
How often / Frequenciaalways (sempre), usually (geralmente), often (frequentemente), sometimes (as vezes), never (nunca). Vem ANTES do verbo: I often attend. / They go before the verb.
