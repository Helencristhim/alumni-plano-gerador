Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

Hello! Let me tell you about my job. I work at Biodelta, in a big office. Every day, I start at eight o'clock. I send many emails and I answer the phone. I write a report every week. My colleague David works with me. He manages a new project this month. I finish at five o'clock. I love my job, because Biodelta is a great company.

1. "I start at eight o'clock." What time does the day begin?

B 5:00

A Because the verb is in the past.
B Because the subject is "he" (David).
C Because it is a question.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- The Simple Present at Work

GRAMMAR

Como falar do trabalho no presente simples: he/she/it ganha um S. Perguntas usam do/does. / How to talk about work in the simple present: he/she/it adds an S. Questions use do/does.

FormaExemplo
I / You / We (afirmativo)I work, you start — eu trabalho, você começa (sem S)
He / She / It (afirmativo)she works, he starts, he finishes — ela trabalha, ele começa (com S)
NegativoI don't work / she doesn't work — eu não trabalho / ela não trabalha
PerguntaWhat do you do? / What does she do? — o que você faz? / o que ela faz?
