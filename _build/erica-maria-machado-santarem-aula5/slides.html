Chapter 3

The Work Code

Simple present: I work, she works

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I work in an office."

"She works in an office."

"He starts work at eight."

"What do you do?"

SubjectVerb
I / You / Wework, start, finish (no S)
He / She / Itworks, starts, finishes (add S)
Question (you)What do you do?
Question (she/he)What does she do?
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"He work in an office."

✓ RIGHT

"He works in an office."

✗ WRONG

"She don't send emails."

✓ RIGHT

"She doesn't send emails."

✗ WRONG

"What you do at work?"

✓ RIGHT

"What do you do at work?"

With he, she and it, the verb gets an S: he works, she sends. For negatives and questions we use do and does.
