Aula 3 -- Your Field — Valuation & Appraisal

Series
Grand Designs (Channel 4)

In each episode, experts inspect properties and discuss what they are worth, in clear English. Listen to how they describe a building, its condition, and its value to a general audience. Direct connection to Lesson 3: explaining property and value in simple words.

Tip: note how the experts explain value WITHOUT heavy jargon. Write 5 plain-English phrases they use.

Podcast
Planet Money (NPR) -- episodes on how things are priced

Planet Money explains complex economic and pricing ideas in simple, everyday English. Many episodes describe step-by-step how the value of something is decided. Direct connection to Lesson 3: turning a technical process into a clear, jargon-free explanation.

Tip: pick one episode. Summarize the process in 4 steps using first, then, after that, finally.

YouTube
"How to Explain Your Job in English (Describing a Process)" -- Business English Pod

A practical lesson on describing what you do and how a process works, step by step, using sequencing words and present simple. Connection to Lesson 3: the exact language Marcos needs to explain valuation to non-experts at the congress.

Tip: pause and repeat each step aloud. Then describe your own four-step process from memory.
