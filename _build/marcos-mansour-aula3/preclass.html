Stage 3.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para escutar.

Value -- avaliar, valorar (to decide how much something is worth)
"We value buildings, land, and machines."
Asset -- ativo, bem (something valuable a company or person owns)
"A building is an asset; so is a machine."
Property -- imóvel, propriedade (a building or piece of land)
"We value commercial and industrial property."
Estimate -- estimar (to make a careful, informed guess about an amount)
"We estimate the value based on the market."
Inspection -- vistoria, inspeção (a careful, official check of a property)
"The first step is an inspection of the site."
Market value -- valor de mercado (the price an asset would sell for today)
"We compare similar sales to find the market value."
Accurate -- preciso, exato (correct and exact, with no mistakes)
"An accurate value protects the client."
Standard -- norma, padrão (an official rule everyone must follow)
"We follow international valuation standards."
Reliable -- confiável (something you can trust and depend on)
"Banks need a reliable valuation before a loan."
Figure -- cifra, número (a specific number or amount)
"The final figure goes into the report."

Stage 3.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas.

"In simple words, our firm values assets," Marcos explains to a new contact. "The process has four steps. First, we do an inspection of the property. Then we compare it with similar sales to find the market value. After that, we estimate a fair figure. Finally, we write a clear report for the client." He adds: "The value must be accurate and reliable, because banks use it before a loan. We always follow strict standards."

1. Por que dizemos "our firm values" com -s no final?
A Porque a ação está no passado.
B Porque "firm" é he/she/it (3ª pessoa) no present simple.
C Porque "values" é plural.
2. Quais palavras mostram a ORDEM dos passos do processo?
A "must, use, follow"
B "first, then, after that, finally"
C "assets, property, report"
3. Why does Marcos say "in simple words"?
A Because he forgot the technical terms.
B To explain his field clearly to a non-expert, without jargon.
C Because the contact does not speak English.

Stage 3.4: Grammar Tip -- Describing a Process

GRAMMAR

Como descrever um processo de forma clara (explicação em inglês e português).

ToolUse / UsoExample
we + verbo (present simple)Descrever um processo de rotinaWe inspect, we compare.
it / the value + verbo+s3ª pessoa leva -s (the value means...)The value means the price.
first / then / after that / finallyMostrar a ordem dos passosFirst... then... finally...
In simple words, ... / Basically, ...Explicar para um leigo, sem jargão: In simple words, we find a fair price for the asset.
