Complementary Materials — Lesson 6

Series
Ted Lasso — Season 1, Episode 1

A sports comedy full of reporters, press conferences, and rumours about a football club. Perfect for hearing reported speech ("He said that...", "The coach claimed...") in a fun context.

Tip: every time a reporter talks about a player, write down what they said using "He/She said that...".

Podcast
6 Minute English — BBC: "Fake news and rumours"

A short, clear podcast about how news spreads and how people claim, deny, and exaggerate stories. Great B2 listening that reuses today's vocabulary.

Tip: listen once for the gist, then again and note three reporting verbs you hear (claim, deny, reveal...).

YouTube
BBC Learning English — "Reported speech: say and tell"

A short, clear video that explains backshift and the difference between say and tell, with plenty of examples to copy out loud.

Tip: after watching, report five things people said to you today using "said that" and "told me".
