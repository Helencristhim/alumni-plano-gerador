Chapter 3 -- The Code

Grammar Discovery

Reported speech: say, tell + backshift

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

What changes when we report the words?

"He said that he was tired." (from: I am tired)

"She said that they would win." (from: We will win)

"He told me that he could play." (from: I can play)

"They said that the game had started." (from: The game has started)

Grammar Rule

Say, Tell & Backshift

Direct (the words)Reported (one step back)
"I am tired"He said he was tired
"We will win"She said they would win
"I can play"He said he could play
"The game has started"They said it had started

Key idea: use say with no person (He said that...) and tell with a person (He told me that...). The tense moves one step back, and pronouns change (I to he, we to they).

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"He said me that he was late."
"He told me that he was late."

Use tell with a person (tell me, tell us) and say with no person (say that...). Never "say me".

Artefact

The Front Page

THE MORUMBI TIMES

City News -- Today

NEW SPORTS CENTRE FOR THE CITY

By the Sports Desk

The mayor announced yesterday that a new sports centre would open next year. A spokesperson revealed that the building work would begin next month.

One local witness claimed that nobody in the area had heard about the plan. However, the council denied that the project had been secret.

Officials asked people not to exaggerate or spread gossip. According to the report, the centre will cost far less than the rumour online.
