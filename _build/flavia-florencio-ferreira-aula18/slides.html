Chapter 4: Beginning, Middle, Now

started · over time · right now · our goal is

Order your story in time

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

We started the project in 2020.

Over time, the project grew into many cities.

Right now, we are in the expansion phase.

Our goal is to reach every state by 2027.

StageMarkerExample
Beginningstarted / at firstWe started the project in 2020.
Developmentover time / thenOver time, the project grew.
Nowright now / currentlyRight now, we are expanding.
Goalour goal isOur goal is to reach every state.

Use the past (started, grew) for the beginning and development. Use the present (right now, we are growing) for the current status. Use "our goal is" for the future.

Language Focus

Complete the Story

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"We are start the project in 2020."

✓ RIGHT

"We started the project in 2020."

✗ WRONG

"Right now, the project grew."

✓ RIGHT

"Right now, the project is growing."

Use the past (started, grew) for the beginning and development. Use the present (right now, is growing) for the current status. Do not mix past and present in the same step.

Dialogue

Flavia and Thomas

T
Good morning, Flavia. I would like to know more about your project.
F
Of course. We started this project in 2020, with a small framework and a clear scope.
T
Interesting. And how did it develop?
F
Over time, the project grew. We followed the timeline, and each phase brought good progress.
T
What was the first outcome?
F
The first outcome was strong. More than two hundred small businesses joined us.
T
And where is the project right now?
F
Right now, we are in the expansion phase. Our goal is a national launch, to reach every state by 2027.
