Chapter 3 -- Reported Speech

Said & Told

How to report what someone said in a meeting

Listen and Notice

What Do They Share?

Look at the highlighted words. Where is "said" or "told", and what happens to the verb after?

"The partner said the price was final."

"Daniel told me the deal would close on Friday."

"The client said she was happy with the terms."

"Daniel told me two witnesses would be present."

The Reported Speech Code

Reported Speech

DirectChangeReported
"The price is final."is → wassaid the price was final
"We will sign Friday."will → wouldtold me they would sign Friday
"I can attend."can → couldsaid she could attend
said vs toldtold + persontold me / said (no person)

Key idea: use said or told, and move the verb one step back: is → was, will → would, can → could. Use told + a person (told me), and said with no person (said that).

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"The partner said the price is final. ... Daniel said me the deal would close."
"The partner said the price was final. ... Daniel told me the deal would close."

Move the verb one step back (is → was) and use told + a person (told me), never "said me".

Practice

Report the Words

Direct: "The price is final." → The partner _____said the price was final. (said + is → was)
Direct: "The deal will close on Friday." → Daniel _____told me the deal would close on Friday. (told + will → would)
Direct: "I am happy with the terms." → The client _____said she was happy with the terms. (said + am → was)
Direct: "Two witnesses will be present." → Daniel _____told me two witnesses would be present. (told + will → would)

Artefact

The Closing Agenda

CLOSING AGENDA -- FRIDAY

SERVICE AGREEMENT

1 -- Before the closing

The price is finalized and the board ratifies the deal. The effective date is the first of next month, so completion takes place then.

2 -- The signing

Both teams sign the agreement. Two witnesses are present, and each party countersigns the last page. The client attends in person.

3 -- After the closing

The closing is complete, and the proceeds are transferred the same day. Daniel sends the executed copies to both teams.

Note: the partner said the price was final, and the client said she would attend.
