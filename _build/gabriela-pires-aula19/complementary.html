Lesson 19 -- Making Plans with Friends

Series
Heartstopper -- the friends making weekend plans

Watch the scenes where the teenagers arrange to meet up and hang out. Notice how they say "Are you free?", "Let's meet up" and "I'm meeting...".

Tip: Write down three sentences they use to make a plan, with a time and a place.

Podcast
Culips ESL -- "Making Plans with Friends"

A short, clear episode about arranging to meet up in English: how to ask "Are you free?", how to suggest with "Let's" and "Why don't we", and how to talk about a plan with "I'm meeting...".

Tip: Record yourself making a plan: use "I'm meeting" once, "Let's" once, and "on time" once.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Present continuous for future plans"

A focused lesson on using "I'm meeting" and "We're going" to talk about arranged future plans, plus "Let's" and "Why don't we" for suggestions. It reinforces today's grammar.

Tip: After watching, write six sentences about your weekend plans with "be + verb-ing".
