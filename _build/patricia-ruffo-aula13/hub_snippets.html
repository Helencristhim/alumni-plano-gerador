Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Conduct -- to carry out or run a study, test or experiment
"The study was conducted over six months." (Conduzir / realizar)
Measure -- to find the size, amount or value of something
"Each participant was measured at the start." (Medir)
Procedure -- a fixed series of steps done in a set order
"The same procedure was repeated every month." (Procedimento)
Protocol -- the official, written set of rules for a study
"The protocol was approved by the ethics committee." (Protocolo)
Obtain -- to get something, such as a result or data
"The results were obtained within forty-eight hours." (Obter)
Standardize -- to make a method or measurement the same for everyone
"Every measurement was standardized for accuracy." (Padronizar)
Dataset -- a complete, organized collection of data
"The dataset was reviewed by a statistician." (Conjunto de dados)
Analyze -- to examine data carefully in order to understand it
"The samples were analyzed in the central laboratory." (Analisar)

GRAMMAR

At the team meeting, Patricia explains how a six-month study was conducted. First, the protocol was approved by the ethics committee. Then two hundred participants were recruited, and each one was measured at the start. The same procedure was repeated every month, so nothing changed between visits. The blood samples were collected by trained nurses and stored carefully. After that, the samples were analyzed in the central laboratory, and the results were obtained within forty-eight hours. Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- The passive voice

GRAMMAR

Para explicar um método, usamos a voz passiva: o foco fica na ação, não em quem a fez. / To explain a method, we use the passive voice: the focus is on the action, not on who did it.

Function / FunçãoStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Singularwas + past participle"The sample was analyzed."
Pluralwere + past participle"The data were obtained."
Negativewas/were + not + p.p."The data were not changed."
Say who (optional)... by + agent"It was reviewed by a statistician."

Key point / Ponto-chave: A passiva é was (singular) ou were (plural) + o past participle (analyzed, obtained, collected). Ela coloca o foco na ação e no método, não na pessoa. Em uma seção de métodos, geralmente omitimos quem fez a ação. Lembre: data é plural, então use were.
