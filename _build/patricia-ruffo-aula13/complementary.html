Lesson 13 -- Explaining Methods & Processes

Documentary
Explained (Netflix) -- any science or health episode

Watch how a documentary narrates how research is done: how a study was conducted, how samples were collected and how data were analyzed.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down three passive sentences you hear (was conducted, were collected, was analyzed) and the process word in each.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Using the Passive Voice in English"

A focused episode on the passive voice and the language of processes: how to describe a procedure, a protocol and the steps of a method clearly.

Record a 2-minute explanation of a simple method using only the passive: how it was conducted, how the samples were measured, and how the data were analyzed.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "The passive voice in English"

A clear lesson on forming the passive: was and were plus the past participle, and when to leave out the person who did the action.

After watching, write six passive sentences about a method, using procedure, protocol, dataset, conduct, obtain and analyze.
