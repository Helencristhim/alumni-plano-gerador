Deadlines & Promises

Falar sobre o futuro no trabalho: promise, on time, by Friday, a delay, make sure, as soon as, a plan, due. Gramática: futuro com will (promessas e decisões na hora) e going to (planos), forma negativa (will not / am not going to). Missão: assumir um prazo e fazer uma promessa real ao David.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

Promise -- to say you will really do something
"I promise to send the report today." (prometer)
On time -- not late; at the right time
"I will finish the work on time." (na hora certa)
By Friday -- before or on Friday; the time limit
"I will send it by Friday." (até sexta)
A delay -- when something is late or takes more time
"Sorry, there is a small delay." (um atraso)
Make sure -- to check that something is really done
"I will make sure the report is ready." (garantir)
As soon as -- immediately when something happens
"I will call you as soon as I finish." (assim que)
A plan -- the things you are going to do
"I have a plan for the report." (um plano)
Due -- the day something must be ready
"The report is due tomorrow." (com prazo para)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

The client needs the report soon, so there is a deadline. Maísa has a plan and makes a promise to David. "I will send the report by Friday," she says. "Tonight, I am going to check all the numbers. Tomorrow, I am going to write the summary." David asks her to make sure everything is correct and to send it on time. Maísa answers, "There is no delay. I will call you as soon as it is ready. I promise."

1. Quando a Maísa vai enviar o relatório? / When will Maísa send the report?
A Next month.
B By Friday.
C On the weekend.
2. O que ela vai fazer hoje à noite? / What is she going to do tonight?
A She is going to check all the numbers.
B She is going to call the client.
C She is going to rest at home.
3. O que o David pede a ela? / What does David ask her to do?
A To start a new project.
B To make sure it is correct and send it on time.
C To postpone the deadline.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Future (Will / Going To)

GRAMMAR

Use will para uma promessa ou decisão na hora (I will help you). Use going to para um plano que você já pensou antes (I am going to finish tomorrow). Depois de will, use o verbo base — sem "to". Antes de "going to", use sempre am / is / are. / Use will for a promise or a decision now. Use going to for a plan. After will, use the base verb; before "going to", use am / is / are.

Uso / UseEstrutura / PatternExemplo / Example
Promessa / Promisewill + verbo baseI will send it by Friday. (vou enviar)
Plano / Planam/is/are going to + verbo baseI am going to check the numbers. (vou conferir)
Negativa / Negativewill not / am not going toI will not be late. (não vou me atrasar)

Atenção: não use to depois de will — diga "I will send", nunca "I will to send". E use sempre am / is / are antes de "going to": "I am going to finish", nunca "I going to finish".

Reflection
