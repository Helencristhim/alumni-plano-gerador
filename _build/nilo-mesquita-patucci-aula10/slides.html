Chapter 3 -- The Code

Grammar Discovery

Reported speech: said, told, would, could

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

What happens to the verb when we report what someone said?

"She said that she was the host." (am to was)

"She said that the call would start at three." (will to would)

"He said that he could join by phone." (can to could)

"She told me that she worked with FIFA." (work to worked)

Grammar Rule

Reported Speech

Direct speechReported speechExample
am / iswas"I am busy" to He said he was busy.
arewere"They are online" to She said they were online.
present (work)past (worked)"I work late" to He said he worked late.
willwould"I will call" to She said she would call.
cancould"I can join" to He said he could join.

Key idea: use say with no person after it (she said that...), and tell with a person (she told me that...). Never say "she told that" or "she said me that".

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"She told that she would call back."
"She told me that she would call back."

Use tell with a person (told me, told us) and say without one (said that). The same rule works for every report.
