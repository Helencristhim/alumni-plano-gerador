Aula 13 -- Making & Responding to Suggestions

Series
Friends (Max/streaming) -- Any episode where they plan an outing

The characters are constantly suggesting plans and reacting to each other's ideas in casual, everyday English. Direct connection to Lesson 13: a natural model of "Why don't we...?", "How about...?" and friendly ways to agree or disagree.

Tip: when a character makes a plan, pause and say your own version: "Why don't we..." or "How about..."

Podcast
All Ears English -- Any episode on making plans or invitations

A friendly podcast for B1/B2 learners focused on real conversation and social English. Direct connection to Lesson 13: natural phrases for suggesting, accepting, and politely declining plans.

Tip: after listening, record 60 seconds suggesting a plan to a friend using "How about" and "What if we".

YouTube
"How to Make Suggestions in English" -- BBC Learning English / Speak English With Vanessa

A clear lesson on suggestion phrases (Why don't we, How about, What if we, We could) with everyday examples. Direct connection to Lesson 13: exactly the structures we practiced, modeled by native speakers.

Tip: pause after each example and make a parallel suggestion about your own week.
