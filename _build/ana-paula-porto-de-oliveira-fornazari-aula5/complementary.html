Portugal's Heartland -- Rick Steves' Europe

A calm, beautiful travel episode through small towns, the coast and breathtaking scenery. Rick Steves narrates his journey in clear, natural English. Connection to Lesson 5: listen for the travel words (journey, scenery, coast, landscape) and for stories told in the past.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write three sentences about the trip using "while I was watching" plus a past simple action.

TED Talk
The Value of Travel -- Rick Steves (TEDxRainier)

A warm, personal talk about why a memorable trip changes the way we see the world. The speaker tells travel stories in simple, everyday English. Connection to Lesson 5: notice how he moves between what happened (past simple) and what was happening around him (past continuous).

Tip: pause after each story and retell it in one sentence, using "while" plus "when".

YouTube
Past Simple and Past Continuous -- 6 Minute Grammar (BBC Learning English)

A short, clear video on the exact grammar from today: when to use the past simple and when to use the past continuous, with "when" and "while". Connection to Lesson 5: this is the difference between "I was walking" and "I saw".

Tip: pause after each example and make one true sentence about a trip -- one with the past continuous, one with the past simple.
