Film
The Founder (2016)

Ray Kroc reads the McDonald's market better than the founders who built it: he spots the niche, understands the margins and overhead, and finds the competitive edge that lets him scale. A perfect illustration of Lesson 10 -- reading a market, not just running a business. Watch how each character is described by what they do and own (relative clauses everywhere).

Tip: after a scene, describe one character in a single sentence using a relative clause -- "Ray, who sees the real business, buys the name."
