Lesson 18 -- At the Hotel -- Checking In

Series
Easy English -- "Checking Into a Hotel" (slow, clear)

A short, slow video showing how to check in at a hotel: talking to the receptionist at reception, saying you have a reservation, and asking for your room. Real English -- perfect for today's words.

Watch with English subtitles. Each time you hear a hotel word (reservation, reception, double room, floor, lift), say it out loud.

Podcast
Speak English Now -- "At the Hotel Reception"

A slow, clear podcast about checking in at a hotel: the receptionist's questions, how to say you have a reservation, and how to ask for a room politely with "I would like...".

Repeat each phrase out loud. Then practice: "Yes, I have a reservation." / "I would like a double room." / "Do you have breakfast?"

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Hotel Check-in: What to Say"

A short, practical lesson on today's language: how to check in at reception, ask "Do you have a room?" and request a room with "I would like...".

After watching, say the five Survival Card phrases out loud. Then imagine you arrive at a hotel and check in.
