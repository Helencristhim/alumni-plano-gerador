Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Reservation -- a room you book before you arrive
"I have a reservation for one week." (Reserva)
Reception -- the desk where you check in at a hotel
"Please go to reception." (Recepção)
Single room -- a room for one person
"I would like a single room." (Quarto de solteiro)
Double room -- a room for two people
"I have a double room for one week." (Quarto de casal)
Floor -- a level of a building
"My room is on the third floor." (Andar)
Luggage -- your bags and suitcase when you travel
"My luggage is very heavy." (Bagagem)
Lift -- a small room that takes you up and down
"I take the lift to my floor." (Elevador)
Check out -- to leave the hotel on your last day
"I check out on Sunday morning." (Fazer o check-out / sair do hotel)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas. / Read the text and answer the questions.

Gabriela arrives at her hotel in London. Now she is at reception. The receptionist asks: "Do you have a reservation?" Gabriela says: "Yes, I have a reservation for a double room." Then she says: "I would like the room for one week." The receptionist gives her a key card and says: "Your room is on the third floor. The lift is over there." Gabriela says thank you and takes the lift to her room.

1. Where is Gabriela now?
A At reception.
B At passport control.
C At the gate.
2. What room does she have?
A A single room.
B A double room.
C No room.
3. Which floor is her room on?
A The first floor.
B The third floor.
C The ground floor.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Do you have...? / I would like...

GRAMMAR

No hotel, o recepcionista PERGUNTA com "Do you have...?" e você RESPONDE com "Yes, I have...". Para pedir algo de forma educada, use "I would like...". / At the hotel, the receptionist ASKS with "Do you have...?" and you ANSWER with "Yes, I have...". To request something politely, use "I would like...".

Function / FunçãoStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Receptionist asks / Recepcionista perguntaDo you have + a + coisa?Do you have a reservation?
You answer / Você respondeYes, I have + a + coisa.Yes, I have a reservation.
You request / Você pede (educado)I would like + a + coisa.I would like a double room.

Key point / Ponto-chave: para perguntar, comece com Do you have...?; para pedir com educação, diga I would like... (mais gentil que "I want"). / To ask, start with "Do you have...?"; to request politely, say "I would like..." (softer than "I want").
