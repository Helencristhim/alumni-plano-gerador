Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Opinion -- what you think or believe about something
"In my opinion, this is the best option." (opinião)
Agree -- to have the same idea or opinion as someone
"I totally agree with you." (concordar)
Disagree -- to have a different idea or opinion
"I'm sorry, but I disagree with that." (discordar)
Honestly -- in a true, sincere way; to tell the truth
"Honestly, I prefer the first idea." (honestamente)
Depend -- to change because of another thing or situation
"It depends on the situation." (depender)
Point of view -- the way a person sees or understands a situation
"I understand your point of view." (ponto de vista)
Totally -- completely; one hundred percent
"I totally agree with that idea." (totalmente)
Actually -- in fact; the truth is (often a polite correction)
"Actually, I think the second one is better." (na verdade)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Hi, I'm Mark. People often ask me about working from home, so let me share my point of view. Honestly, I think it depends on the person. In my opinion, it's great for focus, but it can feel a little lonely. Some of my colleagues totally disagree with me — they say the office is better for teamwork. Actually, I understand their point of view, and I partly agree. So my honest opinion is simple: a mix of both is the best option.

1. What is Mark's main opinion about working from home?
A A mix of both is the best option.
B The office is always better.
C Nobody should work from home.
2. How does Mark feel about his colleagues' point of view?
A He thinks they are completely wrong.
B He understands it and partly agrees.
C He never listens to them.
3. Which sentence is correct?
A "I am agree with you."
B "I agree with you."
C "I am agree of you."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Giving & Reacting to Opinions

GRAMMAR

Frases úteis para dar a sua opinião e reagir. / Useful phrases to give your opinion and react.

Function / FunçãoUseful phrases / Frases úteis
Give an opinion / dar opiniãoIn my opinion, ... / I think ... / Honestly, ...
Agree / concordarI totally agree. / That's a good point. / Exactly.
Disagree politely / discordarI see your point, but ... / I'm not sure I agree. / Actually, ...
It changes / dependeIt depends on ... / from my point of view, ...
Key point / Ponto-chaveAgree é verbo: diga "I agree" / "I disagree", nunca "I am agree". E usamos "depend on" e "disagree with".
