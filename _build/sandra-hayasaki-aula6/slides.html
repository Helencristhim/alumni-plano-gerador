Grammar Rule

There is and There are

FormStructureExample
One (singular)There isThere is a sofa.
Two or more (plural)There areThere are two bedrooms.
Question (?)Is there...?Is there a table?
Negative (-)There is noThere is no table.

Key idea: use there is for one thing and there are for two or more. Say "There are two bedrooms", not "There is two bedrooms".

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"There is two bedrooms."
"There are two bedrooms."

For two or more, use there are, not "there is".
