Lesson 15 -- A Day at the Museum

Series
Night at the Museum -- the museum tour scenes

Watch the scenes inside the museum. Notice the words for the galleries, paintings and sculptures, and how the characters describe what they see with "There is" and "There are".

Tip: Write down five museum words you hear and one sentence with "There is" and one with "There are".

Podcast
Culips ESL -- "Visiting a Museum"

A short, clear episode about a day at a museum in English: buying a ticket, asking how much it costs, finding the entrance, and talking about the exhibition and the art inside.

Tip: Record yourself buying a ticket and describing a gallery: ask "How much is", then use "There is" and "There are".

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "At the museum"

A focused lesson on visiting a museum: how to ask prices with "How much is" and "How much are", and how to describe an exhibition with "There is" and "There are". It reinforces today's grammar.

Tip: After watching, write six sentences: two questions with "How much is/are", and four descriptions with "There is/are".
