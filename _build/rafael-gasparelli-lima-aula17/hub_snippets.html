Legal Basics -- Contracts and Terms

Aprenda as palavras-chave de um contrato (contract, clause, party, obligation, breach, agreement, binding, terminate) e os modais juridicos: shall e must para dever, may para permissao e must not para o que e proibido. Sempre modal + verbo na base (must pay, shall deliver), sem "to" e sem "-s".

Stage 1.1: Key Words

Vocabulary

Eight words that appear in almost every contract. Listen to each one and notice how it is used.

Contract -- contrato (acordo legal por escrito)
"Both sides signed the contract today."
Agreement -- acordo (promessa que ambas as partes aceitam)
"The agreement is valid for two years."
Clause -- cláusula (uma regra/parte do contrato)
"Clause five is about payment."
Party -- parte (pessoa ou empresa que assina)
"Each party must follow the rules."
Obligation -- obrigação / dever
"Payment is an obligation of the client."
Breach -- quebra / violação de uma cláusula
"A late payment is a breach of contract."
Binding -- vinculante (de cumprimento obrigatório por lei)
"A signed contract is binding."
Terminate -- encerrar / rescindir um contrato
"Either party may terminate the agreement."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Shall, Must, May

Grammar

Em contratos, usamos modais para dizer o que cada parte deve, pode ou não pode fazer. Shall e must indicam um dever/obrigação (the supplier shall deliver, the client must pay). May indica permissão, não obrigação (either party may terminate). Must not indica algo proibido (the parties must not share the data). Depois do modal, sempre use o verbo na forma base (deliver, pay, share), nunca "to" e nunca "-s". / In contracts, shall/must = duty, may = permission, must not = forbidden. After the modal, always use the base verb.

ModalEnglishPortuguês
shall (dever, legal)The supplier shall deliver the goods.O fornecedor deverá entregar as mercadorias.
must (obrigação)The client must pay each invoice.O cliente deve pagar cada fatura.
may (permissão)Either party may terminate the agreement.Qualquer parte pode rescindir o acordo.
must not (proibido)The parties must not share the data.As partes não podem compartilhar os dados.

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Choose the best clause for each situation.

You want to write that the client has a duty to pay. What is correct?
A "The client must to pay the invoice."
B "The client must pay the invoice."
C "The client must pays the invoice."
You want to give a party permission to end the contract. What do you write?
A "Either party may terminate the agreement."
B "Either party must terminate the agreement."
C "Either party may terminates the agreement."
Sharing the data is forbidden. How do you write that rule?
A "The parties may share the data."
B "The parties must not share the data."
C "The parties not must share the data."
