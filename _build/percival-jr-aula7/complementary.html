Aula 7 -- Telling the Story

Tema da aula: Telling the Story -- narrating a trip from start to end with the past simple, time markers and sequencers. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"Sequencing words" -- first, then, after that, finally

Watch a short, clear video about sequencers and time markers in a story. Listen for first, then, after that, later and finally, and notice how every verb stays in the past simple.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote os cinco sequenciadores na ordem em que aparecem.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "Telling a story in the past"

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about narrating past events: how to order actions with sequencers and keep the past simple from start to end. Perfect to review today's grammar.

Dica: escreva um pequeno relato da sua última viagem -- cinco frases na ordem, usando first, then, after that, later e finally.

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about travel or a trip

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen for how people tell the story of a journey: the flight, the schedule, who they met, and the highlight of the trip.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote os marcadores de tempo e sequenciadores que ouvir (yesterday, first, then, finally).
