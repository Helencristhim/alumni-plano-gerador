Aula 17 — Block 3: Closing the Deal

Series
Suits / Billions — the moment a deal is closed

Watch a scene where a deal is finally closed. Notice how the strongest negotiators recap the outcome in one line, name what is still outstanding, and agree the next steps. Listen for verb patterns -- "we agreed to proceed", "we look forward to working together".

Tip: write the recap line you hear, then add one next step using "plan to + verb" and one using "look forward to + ing".
