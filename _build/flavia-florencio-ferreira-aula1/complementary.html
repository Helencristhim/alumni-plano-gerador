Complementary Activities

Extra material to reinforce your learning outside class. Mark each one as done after you watch or listen.

Lesson 1 -- Who Is Flavia?

Grammar Video
"Present Simple and Present Continuous" -- BBC Learning English (6 Minute Grammar)

A short, clear lesson on the present simple: how to talk about your job and your routine. Connection to Lesson 1: the grammar that lets you say "I work at Sebrae" and "she works with local products" -- and remember the little s.

Tip: pause after each example and say one true sentence about your own work.

Listening
"Participating in Meetings 1" -- Business English

Real meeting language: how people introduce themselves, ask questions, and talk about their work. Connection to Lesson 1: exactly the moment when you meet an international partner and say who you are and what you do.

Tip: listen once for the general idea, then again and write down 3 phrases you can use to introduce yourself.

Talk
"How to Stay Calm When You Know You'll Be Stressed" -- Daniel Levitin, TED

A calm, well-narrated talk about wellness and staying calm under pressure -- one of your favorite themes. Connection to Lesson 1: listen to how a native speaker tells a clear story, the same skill you are building to speak with confidence in meetings.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Note 5 words you already know, and say each one out loud.
