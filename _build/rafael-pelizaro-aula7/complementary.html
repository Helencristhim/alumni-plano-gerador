Lesson 7 -- The Board Presentation

Series
Mad Men -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Smoke Gets in Your Eyes"

Watch the final pitch scene, where Don Draper gives a short, confident presentation to a board of clients. Notice the clear overview, the single takeaway, and the calm, formal tone.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down the one takeaway he leaves the clients with. Then rewrite it as a board update for your own project.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Presenting in English: The Executive Summary"

A clear episode on how to open a presentation with a short overview, link ideas with connectors, and close with a single takeaway for a senior audience.

Record a 90-second board update on your project. Include an overview, two highlights, one bottleneck with a result connector, and a final takeaway.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Linking words and connectors"

A focused lesson on connectors for contrast, result, addition, and summary (however, therefore, in addition, to summarize). Directly reinforces today's grammar discovery.

After watching, write six sentences about your project, each with a different connector, for example "Costs are low. In addition, quality is high." Read them aloud in a formal tone.
