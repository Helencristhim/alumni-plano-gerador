Chapter 3 -- The Code

Grammar Discovery

Connectors and a formal register

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

What do the orange words do between the two ideas?

"The login feature is ready. However, the testing phase was slow."

"The costs stayed in budget. As a result, the board was happy."

"We found a bottleneck in testing. Therefore, the next sprint starts late."

"The numbers are strong. In addition, the client is satisfied."

Grammar Rule

Connectors + Formal Register

FunctionConnectorExample
ContrastHowever / On the other handIt is ready. However, testing was slow.
ResultTherefore / As a resultWe found a delay. Therefore, dates change.
AdditionIn addition / MoreoverCosts are low. In addition, quality is high.
SummaryTo summarize / In shortTo summarize, the project is healthy.

Key idea: A connector links two ideas and shows the relationship. In a formal board update, start with the connector and add a comma: "However, ..." / "Therefore, ..." / "To summarize, ..."

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"Yeah, so the costs went up, so we need more time and stuff."
"The costs increased. As a result, we need more time."

In a formal update, drop fillers like yeah and and stuff. Use a clear connector such as however, therefore, or as a result with a comma.

Grammar Practice

Choose the Connector

"The feature is ready. _____However, testing was slow." (contrast)
"We found a bottleneck. _____Therefore, the dates change." (result)
"Costs are low. _____In addition, quality is high." (addition)
"_____To summarize, the project is healthy and on plan." (summary)

QUARTERLY BOARD UPDATE

Q2 -- Project Login

OVERVIEW

The login feature was delivered this quarter. Overall, the outcome is positive and on plan.

HIGHLIGHTS

  • Delivered on time. In addition, costs stayed inside the budget.
  • Quality improved against our benchmark from the last project.

BOTTLENECK & ROADMAP

Testing was the main bottleneck. Therefore, the next sprint starts two days late. The roadmap shows the new dates and the expected outcome.

TAKEAWAY

To summarize, the project is healthy, the risk is small, and the plan is realistic.
