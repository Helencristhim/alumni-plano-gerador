Aula 17 -- Pre-class

Around Town -- Invitations and Suggestions (would you like / let's)

Aprenda a convidar alguém e fazer planos juntos: cinema, concert, theater, accept, refuse, busy, sounds good, join. Gramática nova: Would you like to + verbo (convite) e Let's + verbo (sugestão), com aceitar (That sounds good!) e recusar (Sorry, I am busy). Ritmo lento e claro.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Invitations & Suggestions

GRAMMAR

Como convidar e sugerir. Para convidar, use Would you like to + verbo na base (Would you like to go?). Para sugerir com "nós", use Let's + verbo na base, sem "to" (Let's have dinner). Para aceitar: "Yes, I'd love to. That sounds good!" Para recusar com educação: "Sorry, I am busy. Maybe next time." / To invite: Would you like to + base verb. To suggest: Let's + base verb.

Tipo / TypeEstrutura / Structure
Convite / InviteWould you like to + verbo base? — Would you like to go to the cinema?
Sugestão / SuggestLet's + verbo base (sem "to") — Let's have dinner.
Aceitar / AcceptYes, I'd love to. That sounds good!
Recusar / RefuseSorry, I am busy. Maybe next time.
Lembre-seDepois de Would you like usa-se to + verbo. Depois de Let's, o verbo fica na BASE, sem "to".
