Aula 11 -- Pre-class

Paying the Bill

Ask for the bill, understand numbers, and pay politely: bill, cash, card, change, receipt, total, split, cents. Structures: "How much is it?", "Can I pay by card?", "Could I have the bill, please?" and "Can we split the bill?"

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Bill -- the paper that shows the money you must pay
"Could I have the bill, please?" (A conta)
Cash -- money in coins and paper notes
"Can I pay with cash?" (Dinheiro)
Card -- a small plastic card you use to pay
"Can I pay by card?" (Cartão)
Change -- the money you get back when you pay too much
"Keep the change." (Troco)
Receipt -- the paper proof that you paid
"Could I have a receipt, please?" (Recibo)
Total -- the full amount of money you must pay
"The total is forty-two dollars." (Total)
Split -- to divide the bill between people
"Can we split the bill?" (Dividir)
Cents -- small money: 100 of these make one dollar
"It is twelve dollars and fifty cents." (Centavos)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Elaine finishes her dinner. The waiter comes and she says, Could I have the bill, please? The waiter brings the bill. The total is forty-two dollars. How much is the tip? she asks. About twenty percent, says the waiter. Can I pay by card? she asks. Yes, of course. She pays by card, asks for a receipt, and says, keep the change. The next day, at a cafe with a friend, they split the bill.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Asking & Paying

GRAMMAR

Três estruturas para pagar a conta. / Three structures to pay the bill.

Structure / Estrutura Use / Uso Example / Exemplo
How much is it? Perguntar o preço ou o total / Ask the price or total How much is it?
Can I pay by + card/cash? Perguntar como pagar / Ask how to pay Can I pay by card?
Could I have + the bill / a receipt? Pedir educadamente (revisão Aula 9) / Ask politely (review L9) Could I have the bill?
