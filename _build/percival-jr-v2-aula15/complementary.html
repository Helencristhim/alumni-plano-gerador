Aula 15 -- Writing Professional Emails

Tema da aula: writing clear, polite work emails -- structure, tone and the present perfect (I have attached the report / Have you received my email? / Yours sincerely). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"How to write a professional email" -- business English video

A short, clear video about email structure: subject line, greeting, body and sign off. Listen for "I am writing regarding...", "I have attached..." and "Yours sincerely".

Dica: anote a estrutura completa de um e-mail e duas frases no present perfect.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "The present perfect"

Short lessons and quizzes on have/has + past participle: I have attached, she has sent, have you received. Perfect to feel confident in work emails.

Dica: escreva três frases sobre o que você já fez hoje no trabalho usando "I have...". Confira os particípios (write -> written, send -> sent).

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about work or communication

A short, friendly podcast for B1 learners. Listen for how people talk about tasks they have done and emails they have sent.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote uma frase no present perfect para usar num e-mail.
