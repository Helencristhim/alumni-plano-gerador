Aula 4 -- Talking About Passion

Documentary
Inspired to Ride (2015)

A documentary that follows riders across America on the Trans Am Bike Race -- the solitude, the endurance, the rough terrain, and the strange freedom of the open road. It is your passion on screen. Connection to Lesson 4: listen for how the riders describe what they love doing (gerunds) and what they had to learn to do (infinitives).

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Afterward, tell your teacher one thing a rider loves doing (love + -ing) and one thing they had to learn (learn + to).

Podcast
The Adventure Podcast -- any episode

Long, honest conversations with cyclists, climbers and explorers about why they chase the hard, demanding trips. The guests talk exactly the way you do -- about the thrill, the solitude, and learning to pace yourself. Natural English at a real, follow-able pace.

Tip: pick one episode. Write down three sentences a guest says with a gerund (love climbing) and two with an infinitive (decided to go).

TED Talk
Why We Do What We Do (Adventure & Motivation) -- TED

A talk about why people push themselves into demanding, exhilarating challenges -- and what the effort gives back. It puts words to the feeling you find hard to explain: the reward is not the finish, it is the journey. Clear, well-paced English on a theme you live.

Tip: watch once, then say out loud how it matches your own reason for riding -- what you love doing and what really drives you.
