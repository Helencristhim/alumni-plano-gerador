Vocabulary

Menu -- the list of food and drinks you can order

Starter -- the small first dish of a meal (appetizer)
"I will have soup as a starter."
Main course -- the biggest, central dish of a meal
"For my main course, the fish, please."
Dessert -- the sweet dish at the end of a meal
"For dessert, the chocolate cake."
Waiter -- the person who takes your order and serves you
"The waiter brought our food."
Allergy -- when your body reacts badly to a food
"I have a nut allergy."
Vegetarian -- a person who does not eat meat
"Do you have a vegetarian option?"
Bill -- the paper that shows how much you must pay

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

The waiter brings the menu. "Could we see the menu, please?" Pricila asks. For her starter, she says, "I will have the soup." For her main course, "I would like the grilled fish." She wants some water, but first she checks: "Is there any nut in the dessert? I am allergic to nuts." Her friend is vegetarian, so Pricila asks if there is a meat-free option. At the end, she says, "Could we have the bill, please?" A polite guest always gets great service.

1. Which sentence orders a dish politely?
A "I will have the soup."
B "Give me soup."
C "Soup now."
2. Why does Pricila use "any" (not "some")?
A Because it is a question.
B Because it is positive.
C There is no rule.
3. How does she explain her allergy?
A "I have allergy of nuts."
B "I am allergic to nuts."
C "I allergic nuts."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Ordering: I will have / I would like + dish (educado, não “I want”)
some / any: some em frases positivas, any em perguntas e negativas
Dietary: I am allergic to... / I cannot eat... / I am vegetarian

UseStructureExample
OrderI will have / I would like"I will have the fish."
some (+)some + noun (positive)"I would like some water."
any (? / −)any + noun (question / negative)"Is there any nut?"
Dietaryallergic to / cannot eat"I am allergic to nuts."

Common Mistake:

"Is there some nuts in this?"

"Is there any nut in this?"

Use "any" em perguntas e negativas; "some" em frases positivas.
