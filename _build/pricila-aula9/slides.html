Grammar Discovery

Spot the Pattern

I will have the soup, please. (order)
Is there any nut in this dish? (question)
I am allergic to nuts. (dietary need)

How do we order and ask about food?

UseStructureExample
OrderI will have / I would like + dish"I will have the fish." / "I would like the soup."
Ask for the menu / billCould we have + ...?"Could we have the menu, please?"
some / anysome in (+), any in (?/−)"I would like some water." / "Is there any nut?"
Dietary needI am allergic to / I cannot eat / I am vegetarian"I am allergic to nuts."

Key: ordenar com I will have ou I would like (educado, não “I want”). Use some em frases positivas e any em perguntas e negativas.

Common Mistake

Watch Out!

"I want the soup." / "Is there some nuts?" / "I have allergy of nuts."
"I would like the soup, please." / "Is there any nut?" / "I am allergic to nuts."

Order politely, use any in questions, and say allergic to.

Chapter 6: Your Turn

Your Turn
to Speak

Three role-plays: guided, semi-free, and free

Guided Role-Play

Level 1: With Keywords

You are at a restaurant in Sydney. Ask for the menu, order a starter and a main course, ask about an allergy, and ask for the bill. Use the keywords below.
Could we see the menu...? I will have... starter / main course Is there any...? allergic to the bill
Semi-Free Role-Play

Level 2: Order for Two

Your friend is vegetarian and allergic to nuts. Order for both of you and check the ingredients. Use these starters:
"For my starter, I will have..."
"My friend is vegetarian. Does she have...?"
"Is there any... in...?"
"She is allergic to..."
Free Role-Play

Level 3: Speak Freely

No keywords. Have dinner from start to finish: ask for the menu, order a full meal, handle an allergy and a vegetarian friend, and ask for the bill — all in English. Be yourself.
