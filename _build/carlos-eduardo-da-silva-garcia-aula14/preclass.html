Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Sao palavras de reuniao de alinhamento. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Agenda -- pauta (a lista de topicos de uma reuniao)
"I prepare a short agenda before the meeting." (Eu preparo uma pauta curta antes da reuniao.)
Topic -- topico / assunto (um assunto sobre o qual voce fala)
"Let us keep everyone on the same topic." (Vamos manter todos no mesmo topico.)
Progress -- progresso (quanto do trabalho esta feito, avancando)
"I ask about the progress of the program." (Eu pergunto sobre o progresso do programa.)
Concern -- preocupacao (uma preocupacao ou problema para conversar)
"I ask about the concerns of each team." (Eu pergunto sobre as preocupacoes de cada time.)
Detail -- detalhe (uma pequena informacao)
"When I need one detail, I ask a question." (Quando preciso de um detalhe, faco uma pergunta.)
Information -- informacao (fatos e dados sobre algo)
"I share the information with my manager." (Eu compartilho a informacao com meu gestor.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto sobre a reuniao de alinhamento e responda as perguntas. (Read the text about the meeting and answer.)

Before every alignment meeting, I prepare an agenda and a list of questions. In the meeting, I ask, "Who is responsible for the farm data?" and "How many farms are in the program?" I also ask, "How often do you verify the properties?" and "Which criteria are the most difficult?" My colleagues ask about my region too: "Where are your farms?" and "Are the producers ready for the audit?" Good questions give me the information I need.

1. Which is a correct WH-question? (Qual e uma WH-question correta?)
A "How often you verify the farms?" (falta o "do")
B "How often do you verify the farms?" (do + verbo base)
C "How often verify you the farms?" (ordem errada)
2. Which question asks about a PERSON? (Qual pergunta e sobre uma pessoa?)
A "Who is responsible for the data?" (Who = pessoa)
B "How many farms are there?" (numero)
C "Where are your farms?" (lugar)
3. Which is a yes/no question? (Qual e uma pergunta sim/nao?)
A "Which criteria are critical?" (escolha)
B "How many farms are there?" (numero)
C "Are the producers ready?" (comeca com Are)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- WH-Questions

Grammar

Como fazer perguntas: WH-question word + is/are/do/does, e perguntas sim/nao. / How to ask questions.

WordAsks about / PerguntaExample / Exemplo
Whoa person / pessoaWho is responsible for the data? (Quem?)
Where / Whatplace / thing (lugar / coisa)Where are your farms? (Onde?)
How many / How oftennumber / frequency (numero / frequencia)How often do you verify? (Com que frequencia?)
Whichchoose from a group / escolherWhich criteria are critical? (Quais?)
Yes / NoComeca com Do/Does ou Is/Are: Are the producers ready? Do most farms comply? Com you/they: do + verbo base. Com he/she/it: does + verbo base.

Speaking

WH-question com do + verbo base (frequencia).

WH-question de escolha (Which + plural).

Pergunta sim/nao (Are + sujeito).

Etapa 4: Quiz Situacional

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao.

You want to know the PERSON responsible for the data. You ask: (Voce quer saber quem.)
A "What is responsible for the data?"
B "Who is responsible for the data?"
C "Where is responsible for the data?"
You ask about frequency. Which is correct? (Voce pergunta com que frequencia.)
A "How often you verify the farms?"
B "How often do you verify the farms?"
C "How often verify you the farms?"
You want a yes or no answer about the audit. You ask: (Voce quer sim ou nao.)
A "Which the producers are ready?"
B "The producers are ready?"
C "Are the producers ready for the audit?"
