Lesson 17 -- Closing the Deal

Series
Suits -- a deal-closing scene (any season)

Watch a scene where characters close a deal and confirm the terms. Notice how they summarize the agreement and report what was decided.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down one line that confirms an agreement and one reported-speech line ("he said... was...").

Podcast
Plain English -- "How to Confirm a Deal Clearly in English"

A practical episode on closing language: confirming terms, summarizing the agreement, and reporting what was decided so nothing is lost.

Record three confirmation lines: one with "as agreed", one with reported speech, and one with "next steps".

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Reported speech"

A focused lesson on reported speech: say and tell, and the backshift of verbs (is to was, will to would, can to could). It directly reinforces today's grammar.

After watching, write six reported-speech sentences about a deal: three with "said" and three with "told me".
