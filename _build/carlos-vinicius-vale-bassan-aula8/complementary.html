Aula 8 — Block 1 Review: Running a Full Meeting

Series
Succession — any boardroom meeting scene

Watch how a chair opens a meeting, sets the agenda, hands the floor to others, and pushes the room to a decision under pressure. Notice the signposting and how action items are assigned. Perfect review of every Block 1 skill in one room.

Tip: pick one meeting scene and write its agenda and minutes in English, with the outcome and two action items.

Tip: note three signposting phrases the host or guest uses to move the conversation forward, then reuse them in your next meeting.
