Your Field — The Oncology Network

Explaining the oncology network and its services to someone outside the field. Key words: treatment, patient, cure, provide, develop, service, process, improve, cost, care. Structure: present simple for processes/services (we provide, it helps, the hospital offers) + sequencers (first, then, next, after that, finally).

Vocabulary

Treatment -- tratamento (the medical care a patient gets)
"We provide treatment to many patients."
Patient -- paciente (a person who gets medical care)
"Every patient gets a care plan."
Cure -- cura, curar (to make a sick person well again)
"New therapies cure more patients."
Provide -- fornecer, oferecer (to give something people need)
"We provide care and treatment."
Develop -- desenvolver (to create something new, step by step)
"We develop new treatments every year."
Service -- serviço (work a company does for people)
"The hospital offers many services."
Process -- processo (the steps to do something)
"The treatment process has four steps."
Improve -- melhorar (to make something better)
"We improve the service every year."
Cost -- custo (the money you pay for something)
"A bigger network lowers the cost."
Care -- cuidado, assistência (help and attention for a sick person)
"We provide modern care for patients."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Simple & Sequencers

GRAMMAR

Como usar o present simple para descrever processos e serviços (explicação em inglês e português).

FormUse / UsoExample
I / We / They + verbo baseProcesso ou serviço que sempre aconteceWe provide care.
He / She / It + verbo + s3a pessoa do singular leva -sThe hospital provides care.
do / does + verbo basePerguntas (o verbo NAO leva -s)Does it provide care?
SequencersDão ordem ao processo: first, then, next, after that, finally
