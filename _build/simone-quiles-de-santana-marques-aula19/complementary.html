Lesson 19 -- Handling Objections

Series
Suits -- Season 2, Episode 6 "All In"

Watch how the lawyers handle objections from clients and opponents: they acknowledge the concern, stay calm and answer with a strong contrast. Listen for how they reassure a reluctant client and overcome the objection instead of arguing.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down 3 moments where a character acknowledges a concern before answering, and underline the connector (however / nevertheless / on the other hand) in each one.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Handling Objections in English"

A practical episode on responding to objections: acknowledging a concern, calling it a valid point, reassuring a reluctant client and answering with concession language. Exactly the phrases you need for client calls and negotiations at the office.

Note 5 phrases the hosts use to acknowledge or answer an objection, and mark which ones use a concession connector (however, nevertheless, on the other hand).

YouTube / BBC
BBC Learning English -- "Linkers of contrast: however, nevertheless, on the other hand"

A short, clear lesson on the grammar from today: how to use concession connectors to join two contrasting ideas formally, and where the comma goes. Directly useful for answering objections professionally.

After watching, write five sentences where you acknowledge a concern and then answer it with a connector. Record yourself reading them with at least four new words from today.
