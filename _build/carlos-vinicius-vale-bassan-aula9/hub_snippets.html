Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Bargain -- to discuss prices or terms until both sides agree
"Let's bargain on the price before we sign." (negociar)
Counteroffer -- a new offer you make in reply to the other side's offer
"Their counteroffer was fair, so we accepted." (contraproposta)
Trade-off -- giving up one thing in order to gain something else
"A lower price for a longer term is a fair trade-off." (troca/compensação)
Common ground -- the interests or points that both sides share
"Let's start from our common ground." (ponto em comum)
Deal-breaker -- a condition that, if not met, ends the negotiation
"No exclusivity is a deal-breaker for us." (impedimento decisivo)
Bottom line -- the lowest price or terms you can still accept
"My bottom line is ninety dollars per share." (limite final)
Terms -- the conditions both sides agree to in a deal
"Let's agree on the terms before Friday." (termos/condições)
Settle -- to reach a final agreement and close the deal
"We settled on a two-year contract." (fechar acordo)

Carlos opened the meeting and looked for common ground: both sides wanted to sign within four weeks. The gap was the price. Carlos made a conditional offer: "If you lower the price, we'll commit to a longer term." It was a fair trade-off. Rachel replied with a counteroffer, a lower price for two years instead of one. Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- The Conditional Offer

GRAMMAR

Numa negociação, você oferece algo só SE o outro lado oferecer algo de volta. Em inglês, isso é a oferta condicional (first conditional): If + presente simples, + will / can. A parte do "if" usa o presente (nunca "will"); a oferta usa will / 'll ou can. "Unless" significa "se não". Use vírgula quando a parte do "if" vem primeiro. / In a negotiation you offer something only IF the other side gives something back. Use If + present simple, then will / can. Never use "will" in the if-part. "Unless" means "if not".

Condition (if + present)Offer (will / can)
"If you lower the price,we 'll sign today."
"If you commit to two years,I can offer a discount."
"Unless you meet our bottom line,we 'll walk away."
