Aula 9 — Block 2: Negotiation in Practice

Series
Billions — any deal-making scene (Axe and Wags)

Watch how the characters open with common ground, make conditional offers, hold a bottom line, and name a deal-breaker before they settle. Notice how often "If you..., we'll..." drives every move at the table.

Tip: pick one negotiation scene and write the term sheet in English: common ground, price, one trade-off, and the deal-breaker.

Podcast
Negotiate Anything — an episode on trade-offs and bottom lines

A negotiation coach and guest model the exact language from this lesson: finding common ground, making conditional offers, and protecting your bottom line without losing the deal. Short, practical episodes in clear American English.

Tip: note three conditional offers ("If you..., we'll...") you hear, then rewrite them for a deal of your own.
