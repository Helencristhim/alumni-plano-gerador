Plans for the Future -- Going to & Will

After the meeting with Robert, talk about your plans for the future. Use going to for a plan you decided before (I am going to prepare a proposal) and will for a promise or a quick decision (I will call you soon). New vocabulary: plan, goal, next, soon, hope, prepare, deadline.

"We will meet next week." (Próximo)
Soon -- in a short time from now
"I will call you soon." (Em breve)
Hope -- to want something good to happen
"I hope to close the deal." (Esperar)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- going to & will

GRAMMAR

Usado para falar sobre o futuro: planos e promessas. / Used to talk about the future: plans and promises.

Form / FormaMeaning / SentidoExample / Exemplo
am / is / are going to + verbUm plano que você já decidiu / A plan you decided before nowI am going to prepare a report.
will + verbPromessa, oferta ou decisão rápida / A promise, offer, or quick decision nowI will call you.
next / soon / tomorrowPalavras de tempo para o futuro / Time words for the futureWe will meet next week.

Key point / Ponto-chave: use going to para planos já decididos e will para promessas. Depois de will, o verbo fica na forma base: will call, não "will to call". Com going, mantenha o to: going to call.
