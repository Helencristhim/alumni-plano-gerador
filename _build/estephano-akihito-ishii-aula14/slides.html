Grammar Rule

Weather Patterns

PatternUseExample
How is the weather?ask about the weatherHow is the weather today?
It is + weather wordsay the weatherIt is sunny. It is cold.
very / a little + weather wordmake it stronger or softerIt is very hot. It is a little windy.

Key idea: always start with It is before the weather word. The word it is always there, even with no real subject: "It is sunny" (never just "Sunny" or "Is sunny").

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"Today sunny."
"It is sunny today."

Always start with It is for the weather. Say "It is sunny", "It is cold", "It is windy".
