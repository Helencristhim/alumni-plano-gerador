"It is rainy in the morning." (chuvoso)
Cloudy -- the sky is gray and there is no sun
"The sky is cloudy." (nublado)
Windy -- the air moves fast
"It is windy outside." (ventando)
Hot -- the temperature is very high
"It is very hot in summer." (quente)

"It is cold in the morning." (frio)
Warm -- a little hot, comfortable
"It is warm and nice." (morno)
Cool -- a little cold, comfortable
"It is cool in the evening." (fresco)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- It Is for the Weather

GRAMMAR

Falar sobre o tempo com It is. / Talking about the weather with It is.

Pattern / EstruturaUse / UsoExample
How is the weather?Perguntar como está o tempoHow is the weather today?
It is + weather wordDizer como está o tempoIt is sunny. It is cold.
very / a little + weather wordMais forte ou mais leveIt is very hot. It is a little windy.
Atenção: comece sempre com It is antes da palavra do tempo. Diga "It is sunny" (nunca "Today sunny" ou "Is sunny"). Em português dizemos "Está sol" sem "it", mas em inglês o it está sempre lá.
