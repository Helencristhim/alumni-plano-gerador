Aula 1 -- Who Is Eliane? -- Rebuilding Your Professional Voice

Series
New Amsterdam -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

A new medical director arrives at a busy hospital and introduces himself to doctors from many specialties. Listen to how the physicians say what they do, name their field, and describe their experience. Direct connection to Lesson 1: professional self-introductions in a real medical setting, close to your own conversations at conferences and executive programs.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Note 3 ways the doctors introduce themselves and their specialty.

Podcast
TED Health -- "How to introduce yourself with confidence"

Short episodes from doctors and researchers who talk about their work in clear, natural English. Listen to how they describe their background, their specialty, and the projects they have been working on. Direct connection to Lesson 1: the real expressions professionals use to present themselves and their careers.

Tip: listen twice. Write down 5 phrases you can use to introduce yourself at a conference or an executive program.

YouTube
"How to Introduce Yourself Professionally in English" -- Business English Pod

A practical lesson on professional self-introductions: your name, role, specialty, and the questions you ask back. Connection to Lesson 1: ready-to-use sentence patterns for networking evenings and meetings, exactly the context you face at Harvard and with American suppliers.

Tip: pause and repeat each introduction aloud. Practice your own version 3 times, focusing on the present perfect continuous.
