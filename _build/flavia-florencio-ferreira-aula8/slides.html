Chapter 4: Three Tools Review

present simple · questions · present continuous

Three tools, one presentation

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

I work with small businesses. (every day)

What does your company do?

Right now, I am presenting my project. (now)

ToolUse it forExample
present simplea routine or a factI work with small businesses.
do / doesto ask a questionWhat does your company do?
present continuousan action happening nowI am presenting my project.

routine = present simple · question = do/does · now = am/is/are + -ing.

Language Focus

Which Tool?

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"Every day I am working with businesses."

✓ RIGHT

"Every day I work with businesses."

✗ WRONG

"What your company do?"

✓ RIGHT

"What does your company do?"

Use the present simple for routines (every day), am/is/are + -ing only for now, and never drop do or does in a question.
