Vocabulary

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia a recepcao do convidado e responda as perguntas. Ela usa Could you...? Do you know...? Would you mind...? (Read and answer.)

Good morning. Welcome to our meeting. Could you tell me your name, please? Do you know how many minutes you need? Would you mind sharing your screen? And would you mind if we record the meeting?

1. Which start is the most polite for a request? (Qual inicio e o mais educado para um pedido?)
A "Tell me your name."
B "Could you tell me your name, please?"
C "Name?"
2. What comes after "Would you mind"? (O que vem depois de "Would you mind"?)
A "to share" (verbo com to)
B "sharing" (verbo com -ing)
C "share" (verbo simples)
3. After "Could you tell me", what word order do we use? (Depois de "Could you tell me", qual ordem usamos?)
A "where is the room" (ordem de pergunta)
B "where the room is" (ordem normal)
C "is the room where"

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Indirect Questions (Could you...? / Do you know...? / Would you mind...?)

Grammar

Como fazer perguntas educadas em ingles. / How to ask polite questions in English.

Inicio educado / Polite startDepois / Then
Could you tell me...?fato em ordem normal: "...where the room is" (onde a sala fica)
Do you know...?fato em ordem normal: "...when the meeting starts" (quando a reuniao comeca)
Would you mind...?verbo + -ing: "...repeating that?" (repetir isso?)
Erro comum / Common mistakeNAO mantenha a ordem de pergunta: "where is the room" (errado) -> "where the room is" (certo). NAO use to depois de mind: "mind to repeat" (errado) -> "mind repeating" (certo).

Inicio educado + ordem normal · Would you mind + -ing. (Polite start + normal word order · Would you mind + -ing.)

Etapa 4: Quiz Situacional

Quiz

Escolha a melhor frase para cada situacao.

A guest joins. You ask for the name politely. You say: (Um convidado entra. Voce pede o nome de forma educada.)
A "What is your name?"
B "Could you tell me your name, please?"
C "Your name."
The speaker talks fast. You ask to repeat. You say: (O palestrante fala rapido. Voce pede para repetir.)
A "Would you mind to repeat that?"
B "Would you mind repeating that?"
C "Repeat, please repeat."
You ask where the room is, politely. You say: (Voce pergunta onde e a sala, de forma educada.)
A "Could you tell me where is the room?"
B "Could you tell me where the room is?"
C "Where the room?"
