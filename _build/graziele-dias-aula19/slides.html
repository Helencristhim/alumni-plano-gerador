Chapter 3: The Polite Code

Ask It Politely

Could you tell me + normal word order

The Tools

Three Polite Starts

Could you tell me + fact · "Could you tell me your name, please?"

Do you know + fact · "Do you know when the meeting starts?"

Would you mind + -ing · "Would you mind repeating that?"

After the polite start, keep normal word order.

Polite start + normal word order — soft and clear

Discover the Pattern

What's the Pattern?

Could you tell me your name, please?

Do you know when the meeting starts?

Would you mind repeating that?

Could you tell me where the room is?

Polite startThen...
Could you tell mea fact, normal order — "...where the room is"
Do you knowa fact, normal order — "...when the meeting starts"
Would you mindverb + -ing — "...repeating that?"

Polite start + normal word order · Would you mind + -ing

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"Could you tell me where is the room?"

✓ RIGHT

"Could you tell me where the room is?"

✗ WRONG

"Would you mind to repeat that?"

✓ RIGHT

"Would you mind repeating that?"

Normal word order after the start. Would you mind + verb-ing.

Chapter 4: At the Meeting

"Welcoming the Speaker"

Ask politely, one question at a time

Dialogue

Graziele Welcomes

G
Good morning. Welcome to our meeting. Could you tell me your name, please?
C
Good morning. I am Doctor Carter, a guest speaker from Toronto.
G
Thank you. Do you know how many minutes you need?
C
About ten minutes. Would you mind sharing your screen?
G
Not at all. I was wondering if you could clarify one point.
C
Of course. Which point would you like me to clarify?
G
Could you tell me the title of your talk?
C
Yes. The title is Safety in the ICU.
G
Perfect. Would you mind if we record the meeting?
C
No problem. Please go ahead.
