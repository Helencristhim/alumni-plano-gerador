Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Casual vs Professional Register

GRAMMAR

Same idea, two levels. At a professional event, choose the professional register.

FunctionCasualProfessional
Greeting"Hi! How's it going?""Good morning. It is a pleasure to be here."
Introducing"I'm Flavia.""I am Flavia Ferreira, a consultant at Sebrae-SP."
Offering help"Ask me stuff.""I would be happy to answer your questions."
Closing"See ya!""Thank you for your time."

The professional register is formal and concise. It builds your credibility and shows respect. Be assertive but polite, and keep a friendly tone to build rapport.
