Chapter 4: Casual vs Professional

casual · vs · professional

Choose the right register for the event

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

Good morning. It is a pleasure to be here.

I am a consultant at Sebrae-SP.

I would be happy to answer your questions.

Thank you for your time.

FunctionCasualProfessional
GreetingHi! How's it going?Good morning. It is a pleasure to be here.
IntroducingI'm Flavia.I am Flavia Ferreira, a consultant at Sebrae-SP.
Offering helpAsk me stuff.I would be happy to answer your questions.
ClosingSee ya!Thank you for your time.

At a professional event, choose the professional register. It is formal and concise, and it builds your credibility. Be assertive but polite.

Dialogue

Flavia and Marco

M
Good morning, Flavia. Welcome to the event. Could you tell us about your work?
F
Good morning. It is a pleasure to be here. I am Flavia Ferreira, a consultant at Sebrae-SP.
M
Wonderful. What does Sebrae do?
F
Let me give you a concise summary. We help small businesses and protect geographic indications.
M
And what is a geographic indication, exactly?
F
It is a certification that protects products from a special region. It gives our products more credibility.
M
Impressive. I would love to hear about your mission to Italy.
F
Of course. I am happy to build rapport with our Italian partners and share the value of Brazilian products.
