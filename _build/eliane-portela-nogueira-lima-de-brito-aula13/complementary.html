Shark Tank (2009 - )

Founders get three minutes to pitch real investors, and the best ones lead with a single differentiator, name one pain point, and show traction. It is Lesson 13 live: watch how the strongest pitches use emphasis ("What sets us apart is...", "It is X that...") to put the spotlight on one idea, and how the sharks ask for the return on investment before they give their buy-in.

Tip: after each pitch, rebuild the founder's opening as a cleft sentence -- "What they offer is...", "It is their traction that..."
