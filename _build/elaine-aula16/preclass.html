Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Elaine has a full day in New York. At the restaurant she reads the menu and says, I would like eggs and coffee. She tells the waiter, I am allergic to nuts. After breakfast she asks, Could I have the bill, please? Later she is lost, so she asks a man, Where is the museum? It is far, so she takes the subway. She asks, Which line goes to Times Square, and how much is a ticket? In the afternoon she goes shopping and asks, Do you have this in blue? Is there a discount?

1. Como Elaine pede a comida? / How does Elaine order food?
A I want eggs.
B I would like eggs and coffee.
C Give eggs me.
2. Como ela avisa sobre a alergia? / How does she mention her allergy?
A I am allergic of nuts.
B I am allergic to nuts.
C I have allergic nuts.
3. Como ela pergunta o caminho? / How does she ask for the way?
A Where is the museum?
B Where the museum is?
C Museum where?
