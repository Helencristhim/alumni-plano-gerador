Aula 9 -- Agreeing and Disagreeing

Tema da aula: Agreeing and Disagreeing -- professional debates, with linking words for contrast (however, although, on the other hand). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"How to Disagree Respectfully" -- TED-Ed / Harvard Business Review

Watch a short, clear video about disagreeing well at work: concede valid points, give your objection with a reason, and look for common ground. Notice the linking words speakers use to show contrast.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote uma frase com however e uma com although.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- Linking Words: however, although, on the other hand

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about linking words for contrast and concession. Perfect to review when to use however (two sentences) and although (start of a clause).

Dica: escreva três frases sobre uma decisão no trabalho -- uma com however, uma com although e uma com on the other hand.

Podcast
"Business English Pod" -- Meetings: Agreeing and Disagreeing

A friendly business podcast for B1-B2 learners. Listen to professionals agree, disagree and reach a consensus in real meetings -- great input to review standpoint, counterargument, objection and compromise in context.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote toda expressão usada para discordar com respeito.
