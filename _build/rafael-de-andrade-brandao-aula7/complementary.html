Lesson 7 -- Meeting Clients for the First Time

Series
Suits -- Season 1, Episode 2: "Errors and Omissions"

Watch how the characters welcome clients to the office, offer them a seat and a drink, and use polite requests. Notice the soft forms: "Would you like...?", "Could you...?", and "May I...?"

Write down every polite request and offer you hear. Pause and repeat each one, then say it out loud to an imaginary client.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- Episode: "Welcoming Clients and Visitors"

Practical tips for receiving a client: how to welcome them at the reception, offer a refreshment, make polite requests, and show a brochure. Listen for the new words: appointment, reception, welcome, refreshment, and agenda.

Note 3 polite offers the host makes. Practice each one with Would you like or May I.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Polite Requests: Could you, Would you, May I"

A short, clear lesson on polite requests and offers in English: how to ask for things, offer help, and welcome a guest without sounding direct. Perfect for receiving a client for the first time.

After watching, record a 60-second client welcome out loud. Use at least three new words and two polite forms.
