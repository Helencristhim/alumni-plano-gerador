Aula 7 -- Experiences & Life So Far

Series
Chef's Table (Netflix) -- Any episode

Documentary-style episodes where chefs reflect on their life journeys and what they have achieved. Rich, clear English with lots of present perfect. Direct connection to Lesson 7: listen for how people talk about experiences over a lifetime.

Tip: write down 5 present-perfect sentences you hear ("I have...", "I've never...").

Podcast
How I Built This (NPR) -- Any founder story

Founders tell the story of what they have built and the challenges they have overcome. Clear American English, full of experience language. Direct connection to Lesson 7: present perfect and simple past working together in real stories.

Tip: notice when the speaker switches from "I have..." to a specific past time.

YouTube
"Present Perfect vs Past Simple" -- BBC Learning English

A clear lesson comparing present perfect and simple past, with native-speaker examples. Direct connection to Lesson 7: exactly the contrast we practiced, in a short and friendly format.

Tip: after watching, say one true present-perfect sentence and one true simple-past sentence about your life.
