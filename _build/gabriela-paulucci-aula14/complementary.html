Lesson 14 -- My Daily Routine

Series
Peppa Pig -- "Daddy's Movie Camera" / "The Time Capsule"

Short, slow episodes that follow a daily routine: getting up, having breakfast, going out, and going to bed. Simple sentences and clear pronunciation -- perfect for the routine verbs and times from today's lesson.

Watch with English subtitles. Each time you hear a routine verb (get up, go to work, have dinner, go to bed), say it out loud with a time: "I get up at seven."

Podcast
Speak English Now -- "My Daily Routine and Telling the Time"

A slow, clear podcast about daily routines: the verbs for get up, go to work and go to bed, and how to tell the time with "o'clock" and ask "What time is it?".

Repeat each phrase out loud. Then record a short description of your own morning: say what time you get up and what you do first.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Telling the Time and Daily Routines"

A short, practical lesson on today's language: how to tell the time with "o'clock", ask "What time is it?", and describe a daily routine with "I get up at...", in clear American English.

After watching, write five sentences about your day: "I get up at seven. I go to work at eight..." Then read them aloud and check your pronunciation.
