Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Get up -- you leave your bed and stand up in the morning
"I get up at seven o'clock." (Levantar da cama)
Have a shower -- you wash your whole body with water
"First I have a shower." (Tomar banho)
Brush my teeth -- you clean your teeth in the bathroom
"I brush my teeth after breakfast." (Escovar os dentes)
Go to work -- you leave home to do your job
"I go to work at eight o'clock." (Ir para o trabalho)
Come home -- you go back to your house after work
"I come home at six o'clock." (Voltar para casa)
Have dinner -- the meal you eat in the evening
"I have dinner at eight o'clock." (Jantar)
Go to bed -- you lie down to sleep at night
"I go to bed at eleven o'clock." (Ir dormir)
O'clock -- a way to say the time on the hour: seven, eight, nine
"It is seven o'clock." (Em ponto / horas)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas. / Read the text and answer the questions.

Gabriela has a busy day. She gets up at seven o'clock. First she has a shower and brushes her teeth. Then she goes to work at eight o'clock. She comes home at six o'clock in the evening, and she has dinner at eight. After dinner she reads a book, and she goes to bed at eleven o'clock. When her friend asks "What time do you get up?", she says: "At seven o'clock."

3. How does she ask about the time?

B "How much is it?"

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Telling the Time

GRAMMAR

Perguntamos a hora com What time is it? e respondemos com It is + número + o'clock. Dizemos quando fazemos algo com at + horário. / We ask the time with What time is it? and answer It is + number + o'clock. We say when with at + the time.

Form / FormaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
What time is it?Para perguntar a hora / To ask the timeWhat time is it?
It is + número + o'clockPara dizer a hora cheia / To say the time on the hourIt is seven o'clock.
I + verbo + at + horárioPara dizer quando você faz algo / To say when you do somethingI get up at seven.
What time do you + verbo?Para perguntar quando alguém faz algo / To ask whenWhat time do you go to bed?

Key point / Ponto-chave: use at para um horário (at seven o'clock) e in para uma parte do dia (in the morning, in the evening). / Use at for a clock time, and in for a part of the day.
