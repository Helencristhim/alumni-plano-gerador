The Night Of (HBO) — Season 1, Episode 2: “Subtle Beast”

A near-documentary look at criminal procedure: the arrest, the warrant, the bail hearing, the remand into custody. Narration and police reports are saturated with passive constructions (“the suspect was detained”, “bail was set”), exactly the structures from tonight’s lesson.

More Perfect (WNYC Studios) — episode on a landmark criminal appeal

Long-form legal journalism that narrates how cases move through the system. Reporters lean heavily on the passive when the agent is unknown or unimportant (“the case was remanded”, “the ruling was overturned”), giving B2 listeners a natural model of register.

Dica: Ouça 15 minutos a 1x. Pause sempre que ouvir uma forma passiva e repita a frase em voz alta antes de continuar.
