Aula 19 -- Houston, We Have Culture: US vs Brazil

Tema da aula: compare business and art culture across the US and Brazil -- whereas, while, unlike, similar to, different from; punctuality, hierarchy, directness, etiquette, rapport, mindset. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Série
Ted Lasso (Apple TV+) -- US vs UK culture clashes

Watch an American adapt to a very different work culture. Listen for how characters notice differences in directness, formality, and rapport, and how a warm mindset wins people over.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote três frases que comparam culturas -- uma com whereas ou while, e uma com unlike ou different from.

Podcast
HBR IdeaCast -- "Working Across Cultures"

Leaders discuss how punctuality, hierarchy, and directness differ across countries, and how to build rapport with international partners. Rich input for the language of comparing and contrasting.

Dica: ouça um episódio sobre cultura e separe duas expressões novas (custom, mindset, etiquette, rapport) e como foram usadas em contraste.

YouTube
Museum of Fine Arts, Houston -- gallery walkthrough

Explore the Houston Museum District before your trip. Note how curators describe contemporary and abstract works -- perfect small talk to build rapport with US partners who share your taste in art.

Dica: depois de assistir, grave uma frase comparando o gosto americano e o brasileiro por arte, usando similar to e different from.
