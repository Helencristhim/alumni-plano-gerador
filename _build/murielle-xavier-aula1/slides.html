Chapter 3

The Code

Present simple & WH-questions to introduce yourself

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I work as an HR business partner."

"I am in charge of the HR team."

"My colleague works in the finance team."

"What do you do? Where are you based?"

StructureUseExample
I / You / We workFacts and routines (no -s)I work in HR.
She / He / It worksThird person adds -sShe leads the team.
am / is / areIdentity, role, locationI am a manager.
WH + do/are + youAsk about someoneWhat do you do? Where are you based?
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"My colleague work in finance."

✓ RIGHT

"My colleague works in finance."

✗ WRONG

"I am HR manager."

✓ RIGHT

"I am an HR manager."

✗ WRONG

"What you do?"

✓ RIGHT

"What do you do?"

Add -s after she/he/it. Use "a/an" before a job. In questions, use "do" before you: What do you do?

Chapter 4

Meeting Someone

A first conversation on the global call

Dialogue

On the Call

J
Hi! I don't think we've met. I'm James Carter, the people director here in Australia. Nice to meet you.
M
Nice to meet you too, James. I'm Murielle. I work as an HR business partner.
J
Great! What do you do at Minerva Foods?
M
I'm an HR manager. I'm in charge of a small team for the corporate areas.
J
Nice. Where are you based?
M
Our headquarters is based in São Paulo. We have operations in Australia and Chile too.
J
Wonderful. We work with your team on the headcount review. Let's stay in touch.
M
Thank you, James. Let's keep in touch after the call.
Comprehension

Did You Catch It?

1. What is James's name and his job?
His name is James Carter and he is the people director in Australia.
2. What does James want to do at the end?
He wants to stay in touch with Murielle.
3. What do James's team and Murielle's team work on together?
They work on the headcount review.
