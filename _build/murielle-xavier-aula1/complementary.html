Aula 1 -- Who Is Murielle? -- Your Professional Self-Introduction

Series
Suits -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

Lawyers and executives in a top firm introduce themselves, state their roles, and meet new partners and clients. Observe how they say what they do and where they work. Direct connection to Lesson 1: professional self-introductions in a fast corporate world, close to your meetings with international executives.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Note 3 expressions the characters use to introduce themselves and their roles.

Podcast
The English We Speak (BBC) -- "Introducing Yourself and Others"

Short, clear episodes about everyday professional English. Listen to the natural phrases people use when they meet someone, say their role, and keep in touch. Direct connection to Lesson 1: the real expressions native speakers use for self-introductions and small talk.

Tip: listen twice. Write down 5 phrases you can use to introduce yourself on a global call.

YouTube
"How to Introduce Yourself Professionally in English" -- Business English Pod

A practical lesson on professional self-introductions: name, role, company, and team, plus the questions you ask back. Connection to Lesson 1: ready-to-use sentence patterns for meetings, exactly the context you face with your Australian colleagues.

Tip: pause and repeat each introduction aloud. Practice your own version 3 times, and try one with an Australian-accent video to train your ear.
