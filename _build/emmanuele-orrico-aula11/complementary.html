Lesson 11 -- What's Happening Right Now

Series
Formula 1: Drive to Survive — Season 1, Episode 1 (Netflix)

On the pit wall, the teams describe what is happening on the track in real time: "The car is losing pace", "He is pushing now", "We are changing the strategy". It is the present continuous of a live situation — the same skill you use to report your field to the Global team.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Treasure hunt: write down 3 sentences an engineer says about what is happening right now (am/is/are + -ing). Do not try to understand 100% — look ONLY for the live descriptions.

Business English Pod — BEP 39: Reporting in English, Project Update

A project manager gives his boss a quick update on the status of a project — exactly your live update to David. You hear the natural phrases to open a status, say what is happening now, and report progress in a professional voice.

Tip: listen ONCE on your way to work, with no pauses. Then listen again and repeat out loud every sentence that starts with "Right now, we are..." or "At the moment, we are...". You already listen to podcasts every day — give one of them to your English.
