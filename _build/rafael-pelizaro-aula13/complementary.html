Lesson 13 -- Onboarding a New Vendor

Series
Suits -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

Watch how the lawyers read contracts, question clauses, and negotiate terms. Notice when they explore a hypothetical risk with "if" before they sign anything.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down two clauses they discuss, and rewrite one as a second conditional ("If the client missed the deadline, they would ...").

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Negotiating a Contract in English"

A clear episode on the language of contract negotiation: discussing scope, deadlines, penalties, and liability, and using conditionals to test "what if" situations before you sign off.

Record a 90-second summary of a contract you know: are the terms fair, which clause would you change, and what would happen if the other party broke it?

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "The second conditional: if + past, would"

A focused lesson on the form and meaning of the second conditional, including "If I were you ..." for advice. Directly reinforces today's grammar discovery.

After watching, write six second-conditional sentences about a vendor contract, mixing penalty, liability, scope, and "If I were you ..." advice. Read them aloud.
