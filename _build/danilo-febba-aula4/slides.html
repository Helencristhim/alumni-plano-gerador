Chapter 3

The Grammar

There is / there are + prepositions of place

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"There is a laptop on the desk."

"There are two screens on my desk."

"The keyboard is in front of the screen."

"Is there a whiteboard in the meeting room?"

StructureUseExample
There is + singularOne thingThere is a laptop on the desk.
There are + pluralTwo or more thingsThere are two screens here.
There isn't / There aren'tNegativeThere isn't a printer here.
Is there...? / Are there...?QuestionIs there a meeting room?
Prepositions of placeWhere it ison, in, under, next to, between, behind, in front of

There IS for one. There ARE for many. Say where with a preposition.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"There is two screens on the desk."

✓ RIGHT

"There are two screens on the desk."

✗ WRONG

"Have a laptop on the desk."

✓ RIGHT

"There is a laptop on the desk."

✗ WRONG

"The keyboard is in front the screen."

✓ RIGHT

"The keyboard is in front of the screen."

Use "are" for plural. Say "There is", not "Have". Never drop the "of" in "in front of".
