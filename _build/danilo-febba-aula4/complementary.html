Lesson 4 -- My Office & My Tools

Series
The Office (US) -- Season 2, Episode 1: "The Dundies"

A slow, friendly workplace comedy set entirely in an office. Watch how the characters move around the desks, the meeting room, and the kitchen. Direct connection to Lesson 4: spotting office furniture and rooms, and how people describe where things are.

Tip: pause on an office scene and describe it with "There is / There are" and a preposition (on, under, next to).

Podcast
BBC Learning English -- "English at Work" (My Office)

Short, clear episodes at a friendly speed, perfect for A2. Listen for "there is / there are" and words for the office and desk. Direct connection to Lesson 4: it trains your ear with slow, natural English about the workplace.

Tip: listen twice. Write down 5 things that are in the office and where they are.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "There is / There are + Prepositions of Place"

A short, practical lesson on saying what is in a room and exactly where it is. Connection to Lesson 4: ready-to-use patterns to describe your desk and office, with on, in, under, next to, between, behind, and in front of, at your level.

Tip: pause and describe your own desk aloud after each example. Say 3 sentences with "There is" and 3 with "There are".
