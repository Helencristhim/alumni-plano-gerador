Chapter 4: The Three Moves

Buy Time · Clarify · Redirect

Three moves to handle a hard question

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

That is a great question. Let me think for a moment.

Could you say that again?

It depends on the situation. What I mean is, it can change.

MoveUse it toSay
Buy timeget a moment to thinkLet me think for a moment.
Clarifycheck the questionCould you say that again?
Redirectanswer and explainIt depends... What I mean is...

A short pause is normal. Say "It depends on..." (not "of"), and use "What I mean is..." to make your point clearer.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"It depends of the situation."

✓ RIGHT

"It depends on the situation."

✗ WRONG

"Repeat, please."

✓ RIGHT

"Could you say that again, please?"

Say "It depends on..." (not "of"). Ask for clarification with a polite "Could you...?", not a direct order.

Dialogue

Flavia and Marco

M
Hi Flavia. Quick question: what is the exact price of your program in Italy?
F
That is a great question. Let me think for a moment.
M
Sorry, maybe my question was not clear.
F
No problem. Could you say that again? I want to understand the context.
M
I mean the price for a small company, not a big one.
F
I see. That was a small misunderstanding. It depends on the situation.
M
So there is no fixed price for everyone?
F
What I mean is, small businesses need different support in different markets.
Did You Catch It?

About the Conversation

1. What does Marco ask about at the start?
The exact price of the program in Italy.
2. What price does Marco really mean?
The price for a small company, not a big one.
3. What does Marco want to know at the end?
If there is no fixed price for everyone.
