Lesson 13 -- Numbers Do Not Lie

Talk
Aswath Damodaran -- The Value of Stories in Business (Talks at Google)

The NYU valuation professor argues that a number on its own is worthless -- it only means something inside a story and a trend. Connection to Lesson 13: this is exactly the difference between reading a figure naked and dressing it in direction and comparison.

Tip: every time he pairs a number with a narrative, note the trend verb or comparison he uses to make the figure mean something.

Lecture
Aswath Damodaran -- Valuation in Four Lessons (Talks at Google)

A short, plain-English tour of how a business is really judged: not by one figure but by the way its margins, growth and cash move over time. Connection to Lesson 13: the language of trends and comparison in action, applied to real companies.

Tip: pick one company he discusses and describe its performance out loud using year on year and like-for-like.
