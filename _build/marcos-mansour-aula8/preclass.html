Aula 08 -- Pre-class

Giving a Short Professional Presentation

Structuring a talk with signposting. Key words: outline, overview, agenda, slide, walk you through, highlight, move on to, key takeaway, sum up, wrap up. Structures: signposting (Today I will... / First... / Now let us move on to... / To sum up...).

Stage 8.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para escutar.

Outline -- esboço, roteiro (a short plan of the main points)
"Let me outline what I will cover today."
Overview -- visão geral (a short, general description of the whole)
"First, here is a quick overview of the project."
Agenda -- pauta, agenda (the ordered list of points to cover)
"There are three items on our agenda today."
Slide -- slide (one screen of a presentation)
"On this slide, you can see our results."
Walk you through -- guiar passo a passo (to guide someone step by step)
"Let me walk you through the three main steps."
Highlight -- destacar (to draw attention to the most important part)
"I want to highlight one key result."
Move on to -- passar para (to go from one topic to the next)
"Now let us move on to the results."
Key takeaway -- conclusão principal (the single most important idea)
"The key takeaway is: stay accurate."
Sum up -- resumir (to give a short final summary)
"To sum up, we deliver accurate, reliable reports."
Wrap up -- encerrar, finalizar (to bring a talk to a close)
"Let me wrap up and thank you for your time."

Stage 8.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas.

Marcos gives a short presentation. He opens with a clear signpost: "Today I will give a short overview of our work." Then he sets the structure: "There are three points on the agenda. First, let me walk you through the process." He uses transitions: "Now let us move on to the results." He stresses what matters: "I want to highlight one number." Finally, he closes: "The key takeaway is: accuracy builds trust. To sum up, we deliver reliable reports. Let me wrap up and thank you for your time."

1. O que são "signposting phrases" numa apresentação?
A Frases para encher tempo.
B Frases que guiam a plateia pela estrutura (abertura, ordem, transição, fecho).
C Perguntas para a plateia.
2. Qual frase Marcos usa para FECHAR a apresentação?
A "Today I will..."
B "To sum up... Let me wrap up."
C "First, let me walk you through..."
3. What is the correct phrase to change topic?
A "Let us move to the results."
B "Let us move on to the results."
C "Let us move in the results."

Quiz
