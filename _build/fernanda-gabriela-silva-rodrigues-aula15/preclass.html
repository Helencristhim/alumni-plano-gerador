Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Estas sao as palavras-chave da aula. Ouca cada uma e leia o exemplo. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Opinion -- opiniao / o que voce pensa
"In my opinion, the date is good." (Na minha opiniao, a data e boa.)
Agree -- concordar
"I agree with you." (Eu concordo com voce.)
Disagree -- discordar
"I disagree, but I see your point." (Eu discordo, mas entendo seu ponto.)
Suggest -- sugerir / propor
"I suggest we call the supplier today." (Eu sugiro que liguemos para o fornecedor hoje.)
Decide -- decidir
"Let's decide this together." (Vamos decidir isto juntos.)
Idea -- ideia
"I have a different idea." (Eu tenho uma ideia diferente.)
Point -- ponto / o principal da opiniao
"That is a good point." (Esse e um bom ponto.)
Sure -- certo / sem duvida
"I am not sure about that." (Eu nao tenho certeza disso.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

In the meeting, Daniel asks for opinions. He says: "I think the new date is good." Fernanda says: "I agree with you. That is a good point." Then Daniel suggests a small discount. Fernanda says: "I am not sure about that. I have a different idea. I suggest we pay on time and ask next month." Daniel likes it. They decide the plan together.

1. What does Daniel think about the new date? (O que Daniel acha da nova data?)
A He thinks it is good. (Ele acha que e boa.)
B He thinks it is bad. (Ele acha que e ruim.)
C He has no opinion. (Ele nao tem opiniao.)
2. "I am not sure about that" -- what does it mean? (O que significa?)
A I agree completely. (Concordo totalmente.)
B I have doubt, I disagree politely. (Tenho duvida, discordo com educacao.)
C I am angry. (Estou bravo.)
3. How do they choose the final plan? (Como escolhem o plano final?)
A Only Daniel decides. (So o Daniel decide.)
B Only Fernanda decides. (So a Fernanda decide.)
C They decide together. (Eles decidem juntos.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Giving an Opinion

Grammar

Para dar a sua opiniao, comece com 'I think' + uma frase completa. / To give your opinion, start with 'I think' + a full sentence.

Uso / UseForma / FormExample / Exemplo
Opiniao / OpinionI think (that) + fraseI think the date is good. (Acho que a data e boa.)
Concordar / AgreeI agree (with you)I agree with you. (Concordo com voce.)
Discordar / DisagreeI do not agree / I am not sureI am not sure about that. (Nao tenho certeza disso.)
Sugerir / SuggestI suggest (that) we + verboI suggest we wait. (Sugiro que esperemos.)

Atencao: nunca diga 'I am agree' -- agree e verbo, entao e 'I agree'. E depois de 'I think', use uma frase completa, nao so 'yes'. / Never say 'I am agree' -- agree is a verb, so use 'I agree'. And after 'I think', use a full sentence, not just 'yes'.
