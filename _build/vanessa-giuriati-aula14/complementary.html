Aula 14 -- Talking About Past Experiences

Series
How I Met Your Mother (streaming) -- Any episode where Ted tells a story

The whole show is one long story told in the past, full of "a few years ago", "at first", and "in the end". Direct connection to Lesson 14: a natural model of sequencing a past experience and building it around one moment.

Tip: pause after a scene and retell what happened in the past simple, with time markers.

Podcast
The Moth -- Any "True Stories Told Live" episode

Real people tell real personal stories, on stage, without notes. Direct connection to Lesson 14: hear how good storytellers set the scene, sequence events, and land on one unforgettable moment.

Tip: after one story, record 60 seconds telling your own past experience around a single moment.

YouTube
"Telling a Story in English (Past Simple & Time Markers)" -- BBC Learning English / Speak English With Vanessa

A clear lesson on narrating past experiences: at first, then, suddenly, eventually, in the end, with the past simple. Direct connection to Lesson 14: exactly the sequencing we practiced, modeled by native speakers.

Tip: pause after each example and tell a parallel mini-story about your own week.
