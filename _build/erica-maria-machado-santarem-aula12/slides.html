Chapter 3

The Code Together

Four structures, one conversation

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"Go straight and turn left."

"It is sunny today."

"How much is the coffee?"

"She is tall and has long hair."

TopicStructureExample
Directionsverb firstGo straight. Turn left.
Weatherit is + adjectiveIt is sunny / cloudy.
ShoppingHow much / How manyHow much is it?
Peoplebe + haveShe is tall. She has long hair.
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"How many is the coffee?"

✓ RIGHT

"How much is the coffee?"

✗ WRONG

"She has tall."

✓ RIGHT

"She is tall."

✗ WRONG

"It has cloudy."

✓ RIGHT

"It is cloudy."

Use "how much" for money, "be" for qualities like tall and cloudy, and "have" only for parts like hair.
