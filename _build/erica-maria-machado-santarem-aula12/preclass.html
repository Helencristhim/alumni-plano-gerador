Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. Ele junta tudo das Aulas 8-11.

Today it is sunny, so I walk to the store. To get there, I go straight and then I turn left at the corner. The store is near the station. It is not expensive — it is very cheap. I ask the man: "How much is the coffee?" He is tall and very friendly. He has short hair. Now it is getting cloudy, so I go home.

1. How does the person get to the store?
A Turn right and go back.
B Go straight, then turn left at the corner.
C Take the bus to the airport.
2. To describe the man, the text uses "He is tall" and "He has short hair." Which is correct?
A Use "be" for tall/friendly and "have" for hair.
B Use "have" for tall and "be" for hair.
C Use "be" for everything.
3. "It is getting cloudy" describes the weather happening now. Which question asks the price?
A "How many is the coffee?"
B "How much is the coffee?"
C "How old is the coffee?"

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Putting the Pieces Together

GRAMMAR

As quatro estruturas das Aulas 8-11, juntas num resumo. / The four structures from Lessons 8-11, together in one summary.

TemaEstruturaExample
Directions (Aula 8)imperative (verbo no início)Go straight. Turn left.
Weather (Aula 9)it is + adjetivo / -ing (agora)It is sunny. It is raining now.
Shopping (Aula 10)How much / How manyHow much is it? — It is cheap.
People (Aula 11)be (qualidade) + have (parte)She is tall. She has long hair.
Lembre-se"How much" = dinheiro/incontáveis; "How many" = coisas que você conta.
