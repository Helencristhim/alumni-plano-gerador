Aula 7 -- Procurement & Tenders

Tema da aula: Procurement & Tenders -- how a tender works, with the first conditional and polite requests (could you / would you mind). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"What is Procurement?" -- a clear introduction for professionals

Watch a short, clear video about how procurement works: tenders, bids, vendors and approval. Listen for the words you learned today and notice how buyers and suppliers talk about a deal.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote uma frase com tender e uma com bid.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- First Conditional and polite requests

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about "If + present, will + verb" and polite ways to ask for things at work (Could you...? / Would you mind + -ing?). Perfect to review today's grammar.

Dica: escreva três frases sobre o seu trabalho -- uma com o first conditional, uma com could you e uma com would you mind.

Podcast
"Business English Pod" -- Negotiations and Contracts

A friendly business podcast for B1-B2 learners. Listen to professionals negotiate price and contract terms -- great input to review vendor, clause, compliance and negotiate in a real context.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote toda expressão usada para negociar e fazer pedidos educados.
