Projects in Progress -- Status Updates

Giving a clear status update on a project in progress: stages, deadlines, milestones, on track or behind schedule. Key words: progress, update, stage, deadline, on track, behind schedule, currently, delay, milestone, complete. Structure: present continuous (am/is/are + verb-ing) for actions happening now and current projects.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para escutar.

Progress -- progresso (movement toward a goal)
"We are making good progress."
Update -- atualizacao (new information about a situation)
"Let me give you an update."
Stage -- etapa / fase (one step in a process)
"We are on stage two."
Deadline -- prazo (the day something must be ready)
"The deadline is in March."
On track -- no caminho certo (going well, as planned)
"We are on track."
Behind schedule -- atrasado (later than planned)
"We are not behind schedule."
Currently -- atualmente (right now, at the moment)
"We are currently testing it."
Delay -- atraso (when something is late)
"There is a small delay."
Milestone -- marco (an important point in a project)
"The first milestone is done."
Complete -- concluir / concluido (to finish; finished)
"The first stage is complete."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

Fernando is giving Sarah a quick update on the project. "We are currently on stage two," he says. The team is working on the new center, and they are not behind schedule. "We are on track with the deadline," Fernando adds. "The first milestone is almost complete." Sarah is happy: the project is making good progress.

1. Por que dizemos "The team is working" e nao "The team works"?
A Porque "team" e plural.
B O present continuous (is/are + verb-ing) descreve uma acao acontecendo agora / um projeto atual.
C Porque a acao aconteceu no passado.
2. "We are not behind schedule" significa:
A O projeto NAO esta atrasado.
B O projeto esta cancelado.
C O projeto ja terminou.
3. Which sentence describes an action happening now?
A "We work on stage two."
B "We are currently on stage two."
C "We were on stage two."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Continuous

GRAMMAR

Como falar de acoes acontecendo agora e projetos atuais (explicacao em ingles e portugues).

StructureUse / UsoExample
subject + am/is/are + verb-ingAcao agora / projeto atualWe are expanding the network.
am/is/are + not + verb-ingNegativaThe project is not stopping.
Am/Is/Are + subject + verb-ing?PerguntaAre you working on the budget?
now / currently / at the momentMarcadores do presente continuo — We are currently on stage two.

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao de status update.

You start your status update. You say:
A "Project okay."
B "Let me give you a quick update on the project."
C "I am tired."
The team is working right now. The correct sentence is:
A "The team work on the new center."
B "The team is working on the new center."
C "The team working on the new center."
You want to say the project is going well, as planned. You say:
A "We are on track with the deadline."
B "We are behind schedule."
C "We are a deadline."
You ask the consultant a question about her work now. You say:
A "You are working on the budget?"
B "Are you working on the budget?"
C "Do you working on the budget?"
