Chapter 3

The -ing Code

am · is · are + verb-ing

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"We are building the new Parque Global."

"The team is working on the new center."

"We are currently hiring new doctors."

"I am preparing the report now."

UseStructureExample
Action nowam/is/are + verb-ingI am preparing the report now.
Current projectare + verb-ingWe are building the new center.
Singular subjectis + verb-ingThe team is working on it.
Time markersnow / currently / at the moment — We are currently on stage two.
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"We building the new center."

✓ RIGHT

"We are building the new center."

✗ WRONG

"The team is work on it."

✓ RIGHT

"The team is working on it."

✗ WRONG

"You are working on the budget?"

✓ RIGHT

"Are you working on the budget?"

The present continuous needs to be (am/is/are) + verb-ing. For questions, put am/is/are before the subject.
