Series
The Office (U.S., NBC)

A mockumentary about a paper-company branch and its well-meaning but often terrible manager. It is a masterclass -- by counter-example -- in micromanaging, false empowerment, and how NOT to delegate, alongside genuine moments of mentoring and people stepping up. Natural workplace English throughout. Connection to Lesson 18: coaching, delegating, and developing a team.

Tip: pick a scene where Michael delegates something and note whether he truly gives ownership or just micromanages.
