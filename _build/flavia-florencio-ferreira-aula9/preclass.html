Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Want To / Would Like To + Verb

GRAMMAR

How to talk about your goals and plans. Both structures are followed by the base verb.

StructureUseExample
want to + verba strong wish or goalI want to improve my English.
would like to + verba polite, softer wishI would like to work abroad.
he / she / itwant gets an -sShe wants to grow her business. (but: She would like to grow it.)
Negative & QuestionAlways keep to + base verb. Negative: I don't want to wait. / She doesn't want to stop. Question: Do you want to travel? / Would you like to start now?
