Aula 09 — Pronunciation Lab I: Sounds That Trip Up Brazilian Speakers

Conteúdos para reforçar o som do TH, a terminação -ed e os pares mínimos de vogais. Marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
The Crown (Netflix) — any episode

A series with very clear, careful pronunciation. Focus your ear on the th sound and the -ed endings in past tense verbs. Slow, formal speech makes it easy to copy the sounds from Lesson 9.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês. Pause em 5 verbos no passado e repita o som -ed (sem vogal extra).

Podcast
The English We Speak (BBC) — short pronunciation episodes

Short, friendly episodes (3 to 5 minutes) with clear models of difficult sounds. Perfect for practicing the th sound and minimal pairs like ship and sheep at your own pace.

Dica: ouça um episódio e repita cada frase em voz alta logo depois (shadowing).

YouTube
Rachel’s English — "The TH Sound" and "-ED Endings"

Clear tutorials that show exactly where the tongue goes for the th sound and how the three -ed endings work. Maps directly onto Lesson 9, with slow-motion mouth views.

Dica: assista os dois vídeos e pratique no espelho, olhando a posição da língua.
