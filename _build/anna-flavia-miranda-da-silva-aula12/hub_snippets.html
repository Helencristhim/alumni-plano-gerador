Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia a primeira reuniao da Anna e responda as perguntas. / Read Anna's first meeting and answer.

It is Anna's first team meeting. Bruno has an idea: start the meeting at nine. Anna says: "I think that is good." Bruno says: "In my opinion, we need a coffee break." Anna says: "I agree." Then a colleague wants a long meeting. Anna says: "I don't agree. Maybe a short meeting is better." Bruno says: "You are right."

1. What is Bruno's idea?
A To start the meeting at nine.
B To go home early.
C To buy a new phone.
2. How does Anna give her opinion?
A "I am agree."
B "I think that is good."
C "Me think good."
3. How does Anna disagree?
A "I don't agree."
B "I am disagree."
C "No agree."

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Giving Opinions

Grammar

Para dar opiniao, comece com I think + frase, ou In my opinion + frase. Para concordar, use I agree. Para discordar, use I don't agree (nunca "I am agree"). / To give an opinion use I think or In my opinion. To agree, I agree. To disagree, I don't agree.

Para... / To...EnglishPortugues
Dar opiniaoI think it is good.Eu acho que e bom.
Dar opiniaoIn my opinion, the plan is good.Na minha opiniao, o plano e bom.
ConcordarI agree. / You are right.Eu concordo. / Voce esta certo.
DiscordarI don't agree.Eu nao concordo.
Sem certezaMaybe.Talvez.
