Chapter 3: Giving Opinions

How to Speak Up

Two easy ways to share your idea

Discover the Code

Listen and Find the Pattern

"I think it is a good idea."

"In my opinion, the plan is good."

"I agree with you."

"I don't agree."

To do thisSay
Give your opinionI think... / In my opinion,...
AgreeI agree. / You are right.
DisagreeI don't agree. / Maybe, but...

Start an opinion with I think + sentence, or In my opinion + sentence. To say no, use I don't agree (not "I am agree").

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"I am agree."

✓ RIGHT

"I agree."

✗ WRONG

"I no agree."

✓ RIGHT

"I don't agree."

"Agree" is a verb. Do not use "am" with it. To say no, use don't.

Chapter 4: In the Meeting

Ideas at the Table

Anna gives her opinion to Bruno

Dialogue

Anna's First Meeting

B
Good morning, team. I have an idea. We start the meeting at nine o'clock.
A
I think that is a good idea. Nine o'clock is good for me.
B
In my opinion, we also need a short coffee break.
A
I agree. A coffee break is good for the team.
B
Some people want a long meeting. What do you think, Anna?
A
I don't agree. A short meeting is good. Maybe we can finish at ten.
