Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Document -- documento (papel ou arquivo)
"There is a new document." (Ha um documento novo.)
Invoice -- fatura / nota (pedido de pagamento)
"There are two invoices." (Ha duas faturas.)
Warehouse -- armazem / deposito
"The coffee is in the warehouse." (O cafe esta no armazem.)
Certificate -- certificado (documento oficial)
"There is a certificate for the warehouse." (Ha um certificado.)
Reliable -- confiavel (em quem se pode confiar)
"We have a reliable partner." (Temos um parceiro confiavel.)
Urgent -- urgente (precisa ser feito ja)
"The shipment is urgent." (O carregamento e urgente.)
Local -- local (da sua regiao ou pais)
"It is for the local client." (E para o cliente local.)
Complex -- complexo (dificil, com muitas partes)
"It is a complex contract." (E um contrato complexo.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

Today there is a lot on my desk. There is a new document and there are two invoices from the supplier. The shipment is large and urgent. We work with a reliable partner in Colombia. There is also a certificate for the warehouse, and one contract for the local client. The contract is not complex — it is small and simple.

1. "There is a document" vs "There are two invoices". Why different? (Por que a diferenca?)
A "is" = one thing; "are" = many things. (Um vs muitos.)
B They are the same. (Sao iguais.)
C One is the past. (Um e passado.)
2. "a reliable partner". What does "reliable" mean? (O que significa?)
A New and small. (Novo e pequeno.)
B You can trust them. (Confiavel.)
C Far away. (Distante.)
3. "an invoice", but "a document". Why "an"? (Por que "an"?)
A "invoice" starts with a vowel sound. (Som de vogal.)
B It is plural. (E plural.)
C There is no reason. (Sem motivo.)
