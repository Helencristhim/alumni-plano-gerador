Aula 18 -- Chasing a Reply

Tema da aula: making a clear request and chasing a reply -- a polite reminder when no one answers (This is a friendly reminder about... / I am still waiting for your response / Could you reply by Friday? / We kindly ask for a prompt response). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"How to write a polite follow-up / reminder email" -- business English video

Watch a short, clear video about chasing a reply: opening with a friendly reminder, showing you are still waiting, and setting a clear deadline. Listen for "This is a friendly reminder...", "I am still waiting for...", and "Could you reply by...".

Dica: anote uma frase para abrir o lembrete e uma para definir o prazo (Could you reply by Friday?).

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "Following up and reminders at work"

Short lessons and quizzes to practise the language of polite reminders: showing you are waiting, setting a deadline, and asking kindly. Perfect to feel confident chasing a real reply.

Dica: escreva um lembrete curto de três linhas -- abertura, "I am still waiting for...", e um prazo com "by". Confirme que usa "by Friday" e não "until Friday".
