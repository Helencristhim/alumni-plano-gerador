CCQs da Aula 2 (Concept Checking Questions)

  • "Volatility rose sharply" — Is it still rising, or did it finish? (It finished, in the past — past simple.)
  • rose / fell / was — Are these regular verbs? (No — they are irregular; the verb changes shape.)
  • "We did not report the breach" — Why not "did not reported"? (After "did", the verb goes back to base form.)
  • breach — If you breach a limit, do you stay under it or go over it? (Go over it.)
  • default — If a bank defaults, does it pay the loan or fail to pay? (Fails to pay.)
  • provision — Is it money already spent, or money set aside for a possible loss? (Set aside for a possible loss.)

Notas de Condução

  • A leitura é a FORÇA do Sergio: deixe-o ler o relatório em silêncio primeiro, com as perguntas do slide de tarefa em mente (REGRA 2.1/2.2).
  • Correção adiada: na produção livre, fluência primeiro; anote a mistura de tempos verbais para feedback depois.
  • Homework (oral, no último slide): 90s resumindo um trimestre real da XP no past simple.
