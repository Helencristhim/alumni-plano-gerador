"How The Economic Machine Works" -- Ray Dalio

A clear 30-minute animation that explains debt, credit, and market cycles -- exactly the events a risk report describes. Connection to Lesson 2: it tells the story of what happens over time, so notice how the narrator uses the past simple to say what rose, fell, and changed.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down five past-simple verbs you hear (rose, fell, cut, borrowed...) and say one sentence about last quarter with each one.

"Planet Money" -- NPR

Short, story-driven episodes that explain finance and the economy in plain English. Connection to Lesson 2: each episode narrates what happened in a real case, so it is perfect practice for the reading-to-reporting skill and the past simple you used today.

Tip: pick one recent episode, listen once for the main idea, then summarize it out loud in three past-simple sentences: what happened, what people did, and what the result was.
