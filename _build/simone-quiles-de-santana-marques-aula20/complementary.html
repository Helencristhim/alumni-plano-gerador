Lesson 20 -- Social English

Series
Suits -- Season 3, Episode 4 "Conflict of Interest"

Watch the social side of business: lawyers mingle at events and dinners, give compliments, make light small talk and toast to a deal. Listen for how they keep the conversation friendly before any business is discussed.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down 3 social phrases a character uses to compliment someone or open a conversation, and underline any present perfect (have/has + participle).

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Socializing & Networking over Dinner"

A practical episode on social English at business events: how to mingle, give a compliment, ask about experience and join a toast. Exactly the phrases you need for the corporate dinner after a negotiation.

Note 5 social phrases the hosts use, and write 3 present perfect questions you could ask a guest (Have you ever...?).

YouTube / BBC
BBC Learning English -- "The Present Perfect: Have you ever...?"

A short, clear lesson on today's grammar: how to use the present perfect (have/has + past participle) to talk about experience, and how to ask Have you ever...? Directly useful for small talk at a dinner.

After watching, write five present perfect sentences about your own experience, then record yourself making conversation with at least four new words from today.
