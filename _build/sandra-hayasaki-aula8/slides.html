Chapter 3 -- The Code

Frequency and Order

frequency + verb

Frequency

How Often?

Look at the orange words. Where is the frequency word -- before or after the verb?

"I always wake up at seven."

"I usually eat fruit for breakfast."

"She sometimes cooks rice."

"We usually work every day."

Grammar Rule

Frequency Before the Verb

FormStructureExample
Always (100%)I + always + verbI always wake up.
Usually (80%)I + usually + verbI usually work.
With to beI am + alwaysI am always busy.
Wrongverb + frequencyI wake up always

Key idea: the frequency word comes BEFORE the action verb: "I always work", not "I work always". Words: always, usually, sometimes, never.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I wake up always at seven."
"I always wake up at seven."

The frequency word comes before the action verb, not after.

Grammar Practice

Put the Words in Order

wake up / always / I  =  I always wake up
eat fruit / usually / I  =  I usually eat fruit
cook fish / sometimes / she  =  she sometimes cooks fish
am / busy / always / I  =  I am always busy
