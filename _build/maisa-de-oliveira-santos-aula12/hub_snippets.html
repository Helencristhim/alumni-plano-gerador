Last Week's Results

Falar sobre o passado no trabalho: last week, yesterday, started, finished, called, closed a deal, worked on, a result. Gramática: past simple com verbos regulares (verbo + -ed), forma negativa (did not + verbo base) e pergunta (Did you...?). Missão: contar a história da sua semana passada, de segunda a sexta.

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

Last week was very busy for Maísa. On Monday, she started a new project. She worked on the numbers every morning. On Wednesday, she called three clients. On Thursday, she finished a big report. On Friday, her team closed a deal with a new client, and everyone was happy with the result. Maísa did not work on Saturday. She rested at home.

1. O que a Maísa começou na segunda? / What did Maísa start on Monday?
A She finished a report.
B She started a new project.
C She called a client.
2. Quantos clientes ela ligou na quarta? / How many clients did she call on Wednesday?
A Three clients.
B Five clients.
C One client.
3. A Maísa trabalhou no sábado? / Did Maísa work on Saturday?
A Yes, she worked all day.
B No, she did not work. She rested at home.
C Yes, she called clients.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Past Simple (Regular Verbs)

GRAMMAR

Para verbos regulares, o passado é fácil: adicione -ed ao verbo (work → worked, call → called). Na forma negativa e na pergunta, use did / did not com o verbo base. / For regular verbs, add -ed for the past. In the negative and question, use did / did not with the base verb.

Forma / FormEstrutura / PatternExemplo / Example
Afirmativa / Affirmativeverbo + -edI worked late. (eu trabalhei)
Negativa / Negativedid not + verbo baseI did not work on Saturday. (não trabalhei)
Pergunta / QuestionDid + sujeito + verbo base?Did you finish the report? (você terminou?)

Atenção: não use did junto com o verbo no passado — diga "I finished", nunca "I did finished". E com uma expressão de tempo passado (yesterday, last week), lembre do -ed: "Yesterday I worked late.".

Stage 5: Think About It

Reflection

Sem certo ou errado. Pense em inglês, toque no microfone e fale livremente.

Imagine: um colega pergunta como foi a sua semana passada. Conte a história em 4-5 frases: o que você começou, no que trabalhou, quem ligou, o que terminou e o que a sua equipe fechou. Use: Last week..., I started..., I worked on..., I called..., I finished..., My team closed a deal..., I did not work on the weekend.
