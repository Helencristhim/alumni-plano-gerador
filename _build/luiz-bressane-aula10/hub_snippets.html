Série
The Staircase (Netflix) — Episode 3: “A Striking Coincidence”

A documentary trial where witnesses and experts contradict one another. Lawyers constantly relay testimony in reported speech (“the witness said that she had heard…”, “the expert claimed that the wound was…”), the exact structure from tonight’s lesson.

Dica: Assista com legenda em inglês e anote 8 frases em reported speech. Para cada uma, escreva a fala direta original que a pessoa provavelmente disse.

Podcast
Serial (This American Life) — Season 1, a witness-driven murder case

Long-form legal journalism built almost entirely on reporting what people said. The host constantly backshifts: “he told her that he had been at track practice”, “she said that she couldn’t remember”. A natural B2 model of say versus tell and tense shift.

Dica: Ouça 15 minutos a 1x. Pause sempre que ouvir um reporting verb (said, told, claimed, admitted) e repita a frase em voz alta antes de continuar.
