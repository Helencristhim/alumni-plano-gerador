Aula 12 -- Expressing Preferences & Making Choices

Series
Chef's Table (Netflix) -- Any single episode

Chefs constantly explain their choices and what they prefer, with calm, clear English. Direct connection to Lesson 12: a rich model of saying "I prefer..." and "I'd rather..." with a reason behind every decision.

Tip: pause when a chef explains a choice, then say your own version: "I prefer X to Y, because..."

Podcast
Plain English (DEF Media) -- Any episode where two options are compared

Made for B1/B2 learners at a controlled speed. Direct connection to Lesson 12: a natural model of weighing options and stating a preference politely.

Tip: after listening, record 60 seconds saying which side you prefer and why, using "I'd rather".

YouTube
"PREFER, WOULD RATHER, WOULD PREFER" -- BBC Learning English / Learn English with Papa Teach Me

A clear grammar lesson on preference structures with everyday examples. Direct connection to Lesson 12: exactly the patterns we practiced, modeled by native speakers.

Tip: pause after each example and say a parallel sentence about your own preferences.
