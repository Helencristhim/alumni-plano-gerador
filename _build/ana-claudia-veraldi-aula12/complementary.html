Grammar
The Second Conditional -- 6 Minute Grammar (BBC Learning English)

The exact grammar of this class in six short minutes: If + past simple, would + base verb, for imagined situations. It is the clean, neutral British English explanation of the pattern you use to weigh trade-offs and plan for "what if".

Tip: after each example, make your own test-planning sentence, like "If I had more time, I would automate the checkout."
