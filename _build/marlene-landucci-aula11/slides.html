Chapter 3 -- Right Now

Language Discovery

The present continuous -- happening now

Discovery

Listen and Notice

What do the orange words have in common? When is this happening?

"I am staying for three nights."

"She is checking in at the front desk."

"We are traveling in Italy."

"He is waiting in the lobby."

The Rule

Present Continuous

Subjectam / is / areverb + -ing
Iamstaying here tonight.
He / Sheischecking in now.
You / We / Theyaretraveling in Italy.

Key idea: use the present continuous for something happening right now: am/is/are + verb + -ing. For the booking, say "I have a reservation" -- not "I am have".

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I am have a reservation."
"I have a reservation."

Use have for the booking: "I have a reservation." Use the -ing form for now: "I am staying" -- not "I staying".

Practice

Complete the Form

"I _____am staying for three nights." (now)
"She _____is checking in at the front desk." (now)
"We _____are waiting in the lobby." (now)
"I _____have a reservation for tonight." (the booking)
Key Phrase

The First Sentence

"I have a reservation."

Say it at the front desk. Then add your name and your dates: "I am staying for three nights."

Make It Yours
