Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Reservation -- a room you booked before you arrive (reserva)
"I have a reservation for tonight." (Eu tenho uma reserva para hoje à noite.)
Lobby -- the big room at the entrance of a hotel (saguão)
"I am waiting in the lobby." (Estou esperando no saguão.)
Key card -- the small card that opens your room (cartão-chave)
"Here is your key card." (Aqui está o seu cartão-chave.)
Floor -- one level of the hotel (andar)
"My room is on the third floor." (Meu quarto é no terceiro andar.)
Elevator -- the machine that takes you up and down (elevador)
"The elevator is on the right." (O elevador fica à direita.)
View -- what you see from your window (vista)
"My room has a beautiful view." (Meu quarto tem uma vista linda.)
Sign -- to write your name on a form (assinar)
"Please sign here." (Por favor, assine aqui.)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Continuous

GRAMMAR

Usamos o presente contínuo para ações que acontecem AGORA: am/is/are + verbo com -ing. Para a reserva, dizemos "I have a reservation" (não "I am have"). / We use the present continuous for actions happening NOW: am/is/are + verb-ing. For the booking, we say "I have a reservation" (not "I am have").

FormExample (EN)Tradução (PT)
Affirmative (+)I am staying here tonight.Estou ficando aqui hoje à noite.
Affirmative (+)She is checking in now.Ela está fazendo o check-in agora.
Negative (-)We are not leaving today.Não estamos saindo hoje.
Question (?)Are you staying two nights?Você vai ficar duas noites?

Key point / Ponto-chave: presente contínuo = am/is/are + verbo + -ing (I am staying, we are traveling). Para a reserva, use "I have a reservation" -- nunca "I am have a reservation".

Aula 11 -- Hotel Check-in

Tema da aula: fazer o check-in em um hotel ("I have a reservation") e usar o presente contínuo (I am staying, We are traveling). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Film
"The Grand Budapest Hotel" -- the front desk scenes

A beautiful film set in a hotel. Watch the check-in and reception scenes and listen for hotel words: reservation, lobby, key, floor, and room.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote toda palavra de hotel que você ouvir (reservation, lobby, floor, room).

Podcast
"Travel English" -- At the Hotel (beginner episode)

A slow, clear podcast for beginners. Listen to a traveler check into a hotel and practice the phrase "I have a reservation" and present continuous (I am staying here for two nights).

Dica: ouça duas vezes. Na segunda, repita em voz alta "I have a reservation" e "I am staying for two nights".

YouTube
"Checking in at a Hotel -- English Conversation" (English with subtitles)

A short, simple video of a real hotel check-in. A guest says "I have a reservation," gives a name, signs the form, and takes the key card to the elevator and floor.

Dica: pause depois de cada fala do receptionist e repita. Foque nas frases "I am staying for..." e "Here is your key card."
