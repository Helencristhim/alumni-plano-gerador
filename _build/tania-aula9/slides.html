Chapter 3: The Code

Making
Suggestions

Let's · Shall we · Why don't we · How about

Grammar Discovery

Grammar Discovery

Look at the colored words. What do they have in common?

"Let's visit our grandparents."
"Shall we go sightseeing?"
"Why don't we book the hotel?"
"How about visiting the museum?"
StructureFormExample
Let's+ base verbLet's visit our grandparents.
Shall we+ base verb?Shall we go sightseeing?
Why don't we+ base verb?Why don't we book the hotel?
How about+ verb-ing?How about visiting the museum?
Common Mistake

Watch Out!

"Let's to go sightseeing."
"Let's go sightseeing."
"How about visit the museum?"
"How about visiting the museum?"
Practice

Complete the Suggestions

"___Let's visit our grandparents."
"___Shall we go sightseeing on Sunday?"
"___Why don't we book the hotel today?"
"How about ___visiting the museum?"
"Let's ___plan the itinerary together."
Build

Build Your Suggestions

Use Let's, Shall we, Why don't we, How about:

Suggest inviting the relatives: "___Shall we invite all our relatives?"
Suggest visiting grandparents: "___Let's visit our grandparents."
Suggest booking the hotel: "___Why don't we book the hotel?"
Suggest going sightseeing: "___How about going sightseeing?"

T
Good plan. Let's check the schedule for the flights.
S

T

Listening

Listen for Suggestions

Listen again. Count how many suggestions they make.

1. How many suggestions do they make?
Five: "Shall we go in July?", "Let's go in July", "How about a week at the beach?", "Why don't we bring our parents?", "Shall we book two big rooms?"

They check the schedule and plan the itinerary.
