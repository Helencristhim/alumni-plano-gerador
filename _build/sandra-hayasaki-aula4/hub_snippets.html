Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Mother -- a woman who has a child
"This is my mother." (mãe)
Father -- a man who has a child
"My father is at home." (pai)
Sister -- a girl with the same parents as you
"My sister is a teacher." (irmã)
Brother -- a boy with the same parents as you
"My brother is tall." (irmão)
Son -- your male child
"My son is twenty-five years old." (filho)
Daughter -- your female child
"My daughter likes music." (filha)
Grandmother -- the mother of your mother or father
"My grandmother is eighty years old." (avó)
Grandfather -- the father of your mother or father
"My grandfather reads a lot." (avô)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

This is my family. My mother is Maria. My father is Antonio. I have one sister. Her name is Lucia, and she is a teacher. I have one son. His name is Pedro, and he is twenty-five years old. My grandmother lives with us. Her name is Rosa. I love my family very much.

1. Which word do we use for a man or boy?
A her
B his
C your
2. "Her name is Lucia." Who is Lucia?
A The sister.
B The son.
C The father.
3. Which sentence is correct?
A "He is my son. Her name is Pedro."
B "He is my son. His name is Pedro."
C "He is my son. Your name is Pedro."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Possessives

GRAMMAR

Usado para mostrar a quem algo pertence. / Used to show who something belongs to.

Person / PessoaWord / PalavraExample / Exemplo
I / eumyThis is my family.
you / vocêyourWhat is your name?
he / elehisHis name is Paulo.
she / elaherHer name is Lucia.
Key point / Ponto-chaveUse his para homem e her para mulher. A palavra segue o dono, não a coisa.
