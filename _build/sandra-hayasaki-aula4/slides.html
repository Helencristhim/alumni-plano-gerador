Grammar Rule

Possessive Words

PersonPossessiveExample
ImyThis is my family.
youyourWhat is your name?
he (a man)hisHis name is Paulo.
she (a woman)herHer name is Lucia.

Key idea: use his for a man or boy, and her for a woman or girl. The word follows the OWNER, not the thing.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"He is my brother. Her name is Paulo."
"He is my brother. His name is Paulo."

Use his for a man or boy, and her for a woman or girl.
