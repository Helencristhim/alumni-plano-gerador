Ted Lasso (Apple TV+) -- Any episode with a conflict

Ted handles complaints, mistakes, and tension with calm honesty and real apologies. Direct connection to Lesson 19: watch how he takes responsibility instead of making excuses, and listen for moments where someone owns a mistake and offers to make it right.

Tip: pause when a character apologizes and rephrase it with "should have / could have + past participle".

Podcast
Dear HBR (Harvard Business Review) -- Episodes on conflict & difficult conversations

Real workplace problems answered by experts: handling complaints, apologizing, and repairing trust. Direct connection to Lesson 19: hear how professionals take responsibility and turn a complaint into a chance to build trust.

Tip: after one episode, record 60 seconds apologizing for a work problem, using "should have" twice and offering a clear solution.

YouTube
"Should have / Could have / Would have" -- BBC Learning English / English with Lucy

A clear lesson on modal perfects (should have + past participle) for regret, criticism, and apologies, including the "should've" vs "should of" trap. Direct connection to Lesson 19: exactly the grammar we practiced to take responsibility for the past.

Tip: pause after each example and make your own apology sentence with "should have + past participle" out loud.
