Aula 11 -- Conference Calls

Tema da aula: running a video call across time zones -- managing the tech (mute, lag, freeze, dropped calls) and making polite requests with "Could you...?" and "Would you mind + -ing", plus the passive voice for call problems. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
"The Office" (US) -- the video conference episodes

The branch is full of clumsy video calls, frozen screens, and people talking over each other on mute -- a comic but realistic window into conference-call English. Listen for how characters interrupt, ask each other to repeat, and handle the tech.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote três pedidos educados (Could you... / Would you mind...) e duas frases sobre problemas técnicos (cut out, freeze, on mute).

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Telephone & Conference Call English"

Clear, practical episodes on the exact language of calls: joining, asking people to repeat or speak up, handling poor connections, and closing the call. Perfect input to review this lesson's polite requests and call vocabulary.

Dica: ouça um episódio e repita em voz alta cada pedido educado, transformando "Could you...?" em "Would you mind + -ing" e vice-versa.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- Making Requests & the Passive Voice

Short, clear guides to polite requests ("Could you...?", "Would you mind + -ing") and to the passive voice (be + past participle). It also flags the classic mistake "would you mind to do" and shows when to use the passive instead of naming who did something.

Dica: pause após cada exemplo e diga a mesma ideia de duas formas -- um pedido educado e uma frase na voz passiva sobre uma chamada ("the line was dropped").
