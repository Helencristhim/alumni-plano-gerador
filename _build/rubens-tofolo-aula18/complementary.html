Aula 18 -- Hotel Check-in

Tema da aula: Hotel Check-in (check-in na recepção e pedidos educados com I would like... e Would you mind + -ing). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
Any hotel scene -- a guest checking in at the front desk (streaming)

Watch a guest arrive, give their name, and ask for what they need. Listen for words like reservation, front desk, and checkout, and for polite requests with I would like... and Would you mind...?

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote três palavras do hotel e dois pedidos educados.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Would you mind...? -- polite requests"

A short, clear lesson on asking favors with Would you mind + the -ing form -- exactly today's grammar, with natural examples.

Dica: pause após cada exemplo e crie o seu próprio pedido, do tipo "Would you mind sending some extra towels?".

Podcast
Coffee Break English -- "At the hotel and checking in"

A friendly episode about arriving at a hotel, checking in, and asking for amenities. Perfect vocabulary for a doctor who travels abroad for conferences.

Dica: ouça duas vezes e anote cinco palavras do hotel e duas formas de pedir um favor educadamente.
