Something Was Happening -- Past Continuous for Storytelling

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. A tradução em português está entre parênteses. (Listen and read the example.)

Meanwhile -- at the same time (enquanto isso)
"Meanwhile, the team was finishing the report."
Suddenly -- quickly and unexpectedly (de repente)
"Suddenly, the system crashed."
Unfold -- to happen or develop (se desenrolar / acontecer)
"The crisis was unfolding fast."
Simultaneously -- at the same time (simultaneamente)
"Two meetings were running simultaneously."
Gradually -- slowly, little by little (gradualmente)
"The team was gradually calming down."
Hectic -- very busy and chaotic (agitado / caótico)
"It was a hectic morning at the office."
Underway -- already happening (em andamento)
"The audit was already underway."
Midway -- in the middle (no meio / na metade)
"The call dropped midway through the meeting."
Unexpectedly -- in a surprising way (inesperadamente)
"The director arrived unexpectedly."
Interruption -- something that stops an activity (interrupção)
"There was a sudden interruption."
Commotion -- noisy confusion (tumulto / confusão)
"There was a commotion in the corridor."
Chaos -- complete disorder (caos)
"The office was in chaos during the audit."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Past Continuous

GRAMMAR

Use o past continuous (was/were + verbo-ing) para uma ação em andamento no passado. Uma ação longa de fundo pode ser interrompida por uma ação curta no past simple, com while/when. Negativo: was not / were not + -ing. Pergunta: Was/Were + sujeito + -ing?

UsoFormaExemplo
Ação em andamentowas/were + verbo-ingI was preparing the report.
Interrupçãopast continuous + when + past simpleI was talking when he arrived.
Duas ações (while)while + past continuousWhile I was presenting, Ethan was writing.
Negativa / Perguntawas not / were not; Was/Were...?What were you doing?

Atenção: I/he/she/it usam was; you/we/they usam were. Depois de was/were vem o verbo com -ing, não a forma base.

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situação. (Choose the best answer for each situation.)

You describe an action in progress in the past. You say:
A "I was review the report."
B "I was reviewing the report."
C "I reviewing the report."
The recruiters were busy. You say:
A "The recruiters was interviewing candidates."
B "The recruiters were interviewing candidates."
C "The recruiters were interview candidates."
You combine a background action and an interruption. You say:
A "I was presenting when the system crashed."
B "I presented when the system was crashing."
C "I present when the system crashed."
You ask about a past action. You say:
A "What you were doing at 3 p.m.?"
B "What were you doing at 3 p.m.?"
C "What was you doing at 3 p.m.?"
