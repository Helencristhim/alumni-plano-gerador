Chapter 3

Past Continuous

Was/were + verb-ing

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I was reviewing the data when Ethan called."

"The team were preparing the report all morning."

"While I was presenting, the system crashed."

"We were not expecting so many questions."

UseFormExample
Action in progresswas/were + verb-ingI was reviewing the data
Interrupted actionpast continuous + when + past simpleI was talking when he arrived
Two actions (while)while + past continuouswhile I was presenting
Negative / Questionwas not / were not; Was/Were...?We were not expecting that
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

I was review the data.

✓ RIGHT

I was reviewing the data.

✗ WRONG

They was preparing the report.

✓ RIGHT

They were preparing the report.

✗ WRONG

We was not expecting so many questions.

✓ RIGHT

We were not expecting so many questions.

✗ WRONG

What you were doing at 3 p.m.?

✓ RIGHT

What were you doing at 3 p.m.?

Use was with I/he/she/it and were with you/we/they. After was/were, the verb takes -ing. For questions, invert: Was/Were + subject + verb-ing?

Chapter 4

The Story

Recounting a chaotic audit morning

Dialogue

The Hectic Morning

E
Murielle, you look tired. What was happening this morning?
M
It was hectic. I was preparing the audit report when the system suddenly crashed.
E
Oh no. What were the others doing?
M
The team was calling IT, and meanwhile Diana was calming the auditors.
E
Did you finish on time?
M
Almost. The crisis was unfolding fast, but gradually things got back to normal.
E
And the auditors?
M
They arrived unexpectedly, midway through the chaos. But we were still smiling.
