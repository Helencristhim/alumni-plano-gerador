Aula 19 -- Digital Communication

Reinforce short-form professional register: how to keep a Slack or WhatsApp message brief but warm, soften an ask, and read the channel before you send. Watch, read and listen this week.

YouTube
"Slack etiquette & tone at work" -- Business English

A practical guide to professional chat: how to stay brief without sounding cold, when to ping versus message directly, and how to soften a quick ask.

Dica: a cada exemplo, reescreva uma mensagem sua de "ordem seca" adicionando um could you ou um no rush.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- Polite requests & softeners

Refresher on the small softeners that keep short messages warm: "Could you...", "Would you mind...", "when you get a sec", "no rush".

Dica: escreva o mesmo pedido em três registros (email formal / chat com colega / chat com um sênior).

Podcast
Business English Pod -- Communicating on chat & async teams

An episode on the language of remote, async teams: status updates, heads-ups, circling back, and keeping tone friendly when you cannot hear a voice.

Dica: anote cada expressão que cria calor (heads-up, no rush, appreciate it) e adapte ao seu canal na Quinto Andar.
