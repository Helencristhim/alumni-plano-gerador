Chapter 3: Adjectives

Describe the Product

be + adjective & adjective + noun

Discover the Code

Listen and Notice

"Our sugar is white."

"We make sweet sugar."

"The heavy bags are strong."

PatternExample
be + adjectiveThe sugar is sweet.
adjective + nounwhite sugar / heavy bags
no plural -sheavy bags (NOT heavies)

The adjective goes after be or before the noun. It never changes for one or many: one heavy bag, two heavy bags.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"The bags are heavies."

✓ RIGHT

"The bags are heavy."

✗ WRONG

"We make sugar white."

✓ RIGHT

"We make white sugar."

Chapter 4: Showing the Product

A Visitor at Cururique

Describe the sugar and the bags

Dialogue

Showing the Sugar

A
Welcome to Usina Cururique, Mr. Costa. This is our sugar. It is white and very sweet.
C
It looks very clean. Is it a natural product?
A
Yes, it is natural and dry. We make it here from sugarcane.
C
Very good. The bags are big. Are they heavy?
A
Yes, the bags are heavy and strong. They are good for a long trip.
C
Perfect. Your sugar is fresh and clean. I want to buy it.
