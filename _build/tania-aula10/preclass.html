Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Review

Vocabulary

Words you already know — one from each part of your journey. Tap Listen to review the pronunciation.

Passport·passaporte — documento oficial para viajar entre países
"Here is my passport. I am flying to Paris."
Boarding pass·cartão de embarque — o documento que permite entrar no avião
"Could I see your boarding pass, please?"
Reservation·reserva — quando você marca um quarto ou mesa com antecedência
"I would like to check in. I have a reservation."
Directions·direções — instruções de como chegar a um lugar
"Could you give me directions to the museum?"
Menu·cardápio — a lista de comidas e bebidas de um restaurante
"Could I have the menu, please?"
Souvenir·lembrança — um presente que você compra de uma viagem
"How much is this souvenir?"
Landmark·ponto turístico — um lugar famoso ou importante de uma cidade
"I have been to many famous landmarks."
Itinerary·roteiro — o plano dos lugares e horários de uma viagem
"Let's plan the itinerary together."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Read the text and answer the questions.

Tânia is on her last trip of the course. At the airport she says: "Here is my passport. Can you help me with my luggage?" At the hotel: "I would like to check in. I have a reservation." In the city she asks: "Could you tell me the way to the museum?" At the restaurant: "Could I have the menu, please?" She buys a souvenir and asks: "How much is this?" She has been to many landmarks. When she feels sick, she says: "I need to find a pharmacy." Finally she tells her friend: "Let's plan our next trip together!"

1. What does Tânia say at the hotel?
A "Give me a room."
B "I would like to check in. I have a reservation."
C "Where is my passport?"
2. How does Tânia ask for directions?
A "Where museum?"
B "Could you tell me the way to the museum?"
C "I want museum now."
3. What does Tânia say when she feels sick?
A "I want doctor."
B "I need to find a pharmacy."
C "Give me medicine."
