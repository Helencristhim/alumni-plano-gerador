Chapter 3: Your Toolkit

The Right Phrase

One phrase for each job

Recap the Code

Listen and Match the Job

"Can you send me the price by email?"

"Can I speak to the manager, please?"

"I am going to call the buyer tomorrow."

"Our sugar is white and sweet."

JobPhrase
Email requestCan you send me...?
Phone callCan I speak to...?
Future planI am going to...
Describe productIt is + adjective

Each job has its own phrase. For a future plan, always use am / is / are + going to.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"I going to call him."

✓ RIGHT

"I am going to call him."

✗ WRONG

"The bags are heavies."

✓ RIGHT

"The bags are heavy."

Chapter 4: A Day at the Office

A Call from a Buyer

Phone, plan and product together

Dialogue

A Day at Cururique

A
Good morning, Usina Cururique. This is Anna speaking.
C
Hello, this is Mr. Costa. Can I make a plan to visit next week?
A
Of course. I am free on Tuesday. I am going to send you an email with the time.
C
Perfect. And how is your sugar?
A
It is white, clean and very sweet. It is a natural product.
C
Excellent. I am going to call you on Tuesday. Thank you, Anna.
