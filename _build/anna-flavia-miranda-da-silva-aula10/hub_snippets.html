Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o dia de trabalho da Anna e responda as perguntas. / Read Anna's work day and answer.

Anna has a busy day. In the morning she reads her email and she replies to her partners. Then a buyer is on the phone, but her manager is busy, so she takes a message. The buyer wants a meeting, so Anna says: "I am going to send you an email." In the afternoon, a visitor asks about the product, and Anna says: "Our sugar is natural and the bags are strong."

1. What does Anna do in the morning?
A She reads and replies to her email.
B She goes home.
C She makes the bags.
2. Why does Anna take a message?
A Because the buyer is free.
B Because her manager is busy.
C Because the sugar is sweet.
3. How does Anna make a plan for the meeting?
A "I send an email yesterday."
B "I am going to send you an email."
C "I sending an email."

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Your Toolkit

Grammar

Cada tarefa tem a sua frase: email, ligacao, plano e produto. Para um plano futuro, sempre use am / is / are + going to. / Each job has its phrase. For a future plan, always use am/is/are + going to.

Tarefa / JobEnglishPortugues
Email (pedido)Can you send me the price?Voce pode me enviar o preco?
Phone (ligacao)Can I speak to Mr. Costa?Posso falar com o Sr. Costa?
Plan (going to)I am going to send an email.Eu vou enviar um e-mail.
Product (adjetivo)It is white and sweet.E branco e doce.
Pergunta de planoAre you free next week?Voce esta livre na semana que vem?
