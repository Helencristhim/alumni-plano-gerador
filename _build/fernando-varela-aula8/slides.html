Chapter 3

The Future Code

am / is / are · going to · + verb

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"We are going to open the new Parque Global next year."

"We are going to attract patients from Latin America."

"I am going to present the plan to the board."

"It is going to be a big year."

UseStructureExample
Plan / intentionam/is/are + going to + verbWe are going to expand the network.
Negativenot going to + verbWe are not going to stop the project.
QuestionAre you going to + verb?Are you going to launch it soon?
Future markersnext year / next quarter / soon — We are going to launch it next quarter.
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"We are going to expanded the network."

✓ RIGHT

"We are going to expand the network."

✗ WRONG

"We going to invest in research."

✓ RIGHT

"We are going to invest in research."

✗ WRONG

"Are you going open it next year?"

✓ RIGHT

"Are you going to open it next year?"

Use the BASE verb after going to: going to expand, going to open. Always keep be (am/is/are) + going + to.
