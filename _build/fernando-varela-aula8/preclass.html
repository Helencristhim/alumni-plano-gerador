Aula 08 -- Pre-class

Plans for Next Year — Going To

Talking about plans and intentions for the network next year: expand, open, launch, attract, invest. Key words: plan, goal, expand, launch, attract, invest, focus on, soon, next quarter, abroad. Structure: be going to + verb for future plans and predictions (am/is/are going to; not going to; Are you going to...?).

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

Fernando shares his plan for next year with Sarah. "Our main goal is growth," he says. "We are going to expand the network in Latin America. We are going to open the new Parque Global and launch new services." Sarah asks, "How are you going to attract new patients?" Fernando answers, "We are going to invest in research and focus on partners abroad. We start next quarter." "It is going to be a strong year," says Sarah.

1. Por que usamos "We are going to expand" e nao "We are going to expanded"?
A Porque "expand" e plural.
B Depois de "going to" usamos o verbo na forma base (expand), nunca com -ed.
C Porque a acao ja aconteceu.
2. Usamos "be going to + verbo" para:
A Falar de planos e intencoes futuras.
B Falar de algo que terminou ontem.
C Pedir um cafe.
3. Which sentence is a correct question?
A "You going to open it?"
B "Are you going to open it?"
C "Are you going open it?"

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Be Going To

GRAMMAR

Como falar de planos e intencoes futuras (explicacao em ingles e portugues).

StructureUse / UsoExample
am/is/are + going to + verbPlano / intencao futuraWe are going to expand the network.
not going to + verbNegativoWe are not going to stop the project.
Are you going to + verb?PerguntaAre you going to launch it soon?
next year / next quarter / soonMarcadores de futuro — We are going to launch it next quarter.
