Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Comparatives & Superlatives

GRAMMAR

Usamos comparativos para comparar duas coisas (mais... que) e superlativos para o topo de um grupo (o mais...). / We use comparatives to compare two things and superlatives for the top of a group.

TypeComparativeSuperlative
short adj (+er / +est)safe → safer thanthe safest
long adj (more / the most)efficient → more efficient thanthe most efficient
irregulargood → better than / bad → worse thanthe best / the worst

Key point / Ponto-chave: com adjetivos curtos, adicione -er (comparativo) e -est (superlativo): safe → safer → the safest. Com adjetivos longos, use more e the most: more efficient → the most efficient. Use than depois do comparativo e the antes do superlativo. Irregulares: good → better → the best.
