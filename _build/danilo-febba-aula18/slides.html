Chapter 3

The Diary Code

am / is / are + verb-ing + day / time

Discovery

Listen and Notice

What do the green words have in common? When do these things happen?

I am meeting the client on Tuesday.

We are presenting the results on Friday.

Rachel is hosting the workshop on Wednesday.

I am not working on Friday.

FormPatternExample
Positiveam / is / are + verb-ing + day/timeI am meeting the client on Tuesday.
Negativeam / is / are + not + verb-ingI am not working on Friday.
QuestionAm / Is / Are + you + verb-ing?Are you attending the review?

We use the present continuous for a fixed future arrangement — already in the diary, with a day or a time, usually with another person. Same form as "now" (Lesson 12), but here the day/time tells us it is the future.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"I meeting the client on Tuesday."

✓ RIGHT

"I am meeting the client on Tuesday."

✗ WRONG

"We are present on Friday."

✓ RIGHT

"We are presenting on Friday."

✗ WRONG

"You are attending the review?"

✓ RIGHT

"Are you attending the review?"

Always use be (am/is/are) before the verb. After be, the verb takes -ing (meeting, presenting). In a question, be comes before you: Are you attending?

Chapter 4

Arrangements in Action

Arranging the week, line by line

Dialogue

Arranging the Week

R
Hi Danilo. Are you attending the data review on Tuesday?
D
Yes. And on Monday I am meeting two clients. I am arranging it now.
R
Nice. On Wednesday I am hosting a workshop. I am inviting you.
D
Thank you. What time? I want to confirm my diary.
R
At 2 PM. And we are presenting the results on Friday at 3 PM.
D
Friday is hard for me. Can we postpone it to Monday?
R
Sure. Let us not cancel it. Do you have a free slot on Monday?
D
Yes, I have a slot at 11 AM. I am not working in the afternoon.
R
Perfect. So we are presenting on Monday at 11. I am confirming it now.
D
Great. Everything is in the diary. Thank you, Rachel.
Comprehension

Did You Catch It?

1. What is Rachel hosting on Wednesday?
A workshop. She is inviting Danilo.
2. Why does Danilo want to postpone the presentation?
Because Friday is hard for him.
3. When are they presenting the results now?
On Monday at 11 AM.
