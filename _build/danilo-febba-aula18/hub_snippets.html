Making Arrangements -- Present Continuous for Future

Fale dos seus compromissos fixos da semana usando o present continuous para o futuro (I am meeting the client on Tuesday). É um plano FIXO na agenda, com dia/hora — diferente do "agora" (aula 12) e da intenção be going to (aula 17). Palavras novas: arrange, confirm, postpone, cancel, attend, invite, host, slot.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. (Escute cada palavra e leia a tradução.)

Arrange -- to plan something with other people (combinar / organizar)
"We are arranging a meeting for Tuesday."
Confirm -- to make something sure and final (confirmar)
"Please confirm the meeting time."
Postpone -- to move something to a later time (adiar)
"We are postponing the demo to Friday."
Cancel -- to stop a planned thing (cancelar)
"I am canceling the call today."
Attend -- to go to a meeting or event (comparecer / participar)
"I am attending the review on Tuesday."
Invite -- to ask someone to come (convidar)
"I am inviting Rachel to the meeting."
Host -- to organize and receive guests (receber / sediar)
"I am hosting a workshop on Wednesday."
Slot -- a free time in your diary (horário livre / janela)
"I have a free slot on Thursday."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas. (Repare no present continuous para compromissos fixos no futuro.)

Danilo is arranging his week. "On Tuesday, I am meeting the client at 10 AM," he says. "On Wednesday, I am hosting a workshop, and I am inviting Rachel." On Friday, the team is presenting the results. But Danilo has a problem: "Friday is hard for me. Can we postpone it?" Rachel checks her diary. "I have a free slot on Monday. Let us not cancel it — we are just moving it." Danilo replies, "Perfect. I am confirming Monday now." He is not attending the Friday call, but everything else is in the diary.

C passado simples

B porque a sexta é difícil para ele
C porque ele está de férias
3. O que a Rachel oferece para resolver?
A um slot livre na segunda-feira
B cancelar a apresentação
C convidar mais pessoas

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Continuous for Future

GRAMMAR

Usamos o present continuous (am / is / are + verbo-ing) também para falar de compromissos fixos no futuro — algo já combinado, na agenda, com dia/hora e geralmente com outra pessoa. (We use the present continuous for fixed future arrangements: be + verb-ing + day/time.)

FormaEstrutura (EN)
afirmativoI am meeting the client on Tuesday. / She is hosting a workshop.
negativoI am not working on Friday. / They are not presenting today.
perguntaAre you attending the review? / Is he hosting the call?

Agora vs futuro: a mesma forma (am/is/are + verbo-ing) serve para o "agora" (aula 12: "I am working right now") E para um plano fixo no futuro ("I am meeting the client on Tuesday"). O dia ou hora mostra que é futuro. Diferença de be going to (aula 17): "I am going to launch a pipeline" = intenção/plano; "I am meeting the client on Tuesday" = compromisso JÁ combinado, com hora e outra pessoa. Atenção: não esqueça o be (am/is/are) antes do verbo, e use o verbo com -ing (meeting, presenting). Tradução: "I am meeting the client on Tuesday" = "Eu vou me encontrar com o cliente na terça".

Reflection
