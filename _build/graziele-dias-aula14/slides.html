Chapter 3: Email Parts

The Four Parts

Greeting · Opening · Body · Closing

The Forms

Four Email Parts

1. Greeting · "Hello Doctor Lewis,"

2. Opening · "I am writing to invite you."

3. Body · "The meeting is on Friday at ten."

4. Closing · "Sincerely, Graziele."

Greeting → Opening → Body → Closing · always polite

Discover the Pattern

Find the Parts

Hello Doctor Lewis,

I am writing to invite you to our meeting.

The meeting is on Friday. I attach the agenda.

Sincerely, Graziele.

PartExample
Greeting"Hello Doctor Lewis," — at the top
Opening"I am writing to invite you." — why you write
Body"The meeting is on Friday at ten." — the details
Closing"Sincerely, Graziele." — at the end, with your name

Always: Greeting first · Sincerely + your name last · short and polite

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"Hey! Come to the meeting."

✓ RIGHT

"Hello Doctor Lewis, I am writing to invite you."

✗ WRONG

"...see you. Bye."

✓ RIGHT

"...see you. Sincerely, Graziele."
