Aula 20 -- The Big Review: Block 2 Integration

Tema da aula: integrar tudo do Bloco 2 numa negociação completa -- present perfect, modais (shall/must/may, should/had better), trend language e comparação (whereas/unlike/different from); track record, revenue, warranty, trade-off, rapport, mindset. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Série
Industry (HBO) -- deals, terms, and negotiation

Watch how analysts and executives report results, state contract terms, and weigh trade-offs under pressure. A high-stakes showcase of every Block 2 structure in real business English.

Dica: assista a uma cena de negociação e anote uma frase de cada tipo -- present perfect, modal de obrigação, trend language, conselho e comparação.

Podcast
HBR IdeaCast -- "How to Negotiate a Better Deal"

Experts discuss track records, options, and trade-offs, and how to give a clear recommendation. Rich review input for present perfect, modals, and advice language across one conversation.

Dica: ouça um episódio e grave um resumo de 60 segundos usando pelo menos três estruturas do bloco (track record, terms, recommendation).

YouTube
TED -- "The Art of Negotiation" (William Ury)

A clear talk on preparing your position, weighing options and trade-offs, and reaching agreement across cultures. Perfect to close Block 2 and prepare you to lead the meeting in Block 3.

Dica: depois de assistir, escreva cinco frases de auto-avaliação do Bloco 2, uma para cada estrutura, e leia em voz alta.
