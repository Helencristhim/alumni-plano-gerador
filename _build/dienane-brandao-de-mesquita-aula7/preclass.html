Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Dienane works at a big company. She is a manager in the HR department. Every day, she helps the employees and talks with her coworkers. Bruno is a new coworker. He asks: "What do you do? Where do you work?" Dienane answers: "I work in HR. I help people every day." She likes her job, and the company pays a good salary.

1. Which question is correct?
A "What you do?"
B "What do you do?"
C "What does you do?"
2. How do we ask about she or he?
A "Where do she work?"
B "Where does she work?"
C "Where she works?"
3. What is Dienane's job?
A A manager in the HR department.
B A coworker in the Sales department.
C An employee at the bank.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Do and Does

GRAMMAR

Usado para fazer perguntas no presente. / Used to ask questions in the present simple.

Word / PalavraUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
docom I, you, we, theyWhat do you do?
doescom he, she, itWhere does she work?
base verbapós do/does, o verbo fica sem -sDoes he work here?

Key point / Ponto-chave: use do com I, you, we, they e does com he, she, it. Após do ou does, o verbo principal fica na forma base (work, não works).
