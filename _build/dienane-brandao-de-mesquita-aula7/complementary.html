Lesson 7 -- My Job at the Company

Series
Extra English -- Episode 3: "Hector Gets a Job"

A simple comedy made for English learners. In this episode, the characters look for work and talk about jobs and companies in slow, clear English. Perfect for A1 listening.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down every work word you hear (job, company, work). Then say what you do using "I work in..." and "I am a...".

Podcast
Coffee Break English -- "Talking About Your Job"

A short, friendly lesson on work and jobs: how to ask "What do you do?" and "Where do you work?", and how to answer with your job and your company. The hosts explain everything slowly, with many examples you can repeat.

Pause after each example and repeat it out loud. Then describe your own job in three sentences using a new word from today.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Jobs and the Present Simple"

A clear video that teaches work vocabulary (manager, employee, company, department) and present simple questions with do and does, with pictures and simple sentences.

After watching, record a 60-second answer to a job interview: what you do, where you work, and why you like your job. Use at least three new words and a do/does question.
