Chapter 3 -- The Code

Grammar Discovery

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

When do we use do and does?

"What do you do at the company?"

"Where do you work?"

"What does your manager do?"

"Does she work in HR?"

Grammar Rule

Do and Does

WordUseExample
dowith I, you, we, theyWhere do you work?
doeswith he, she, itWhat does she do?
base verbafter do/does, the verb has no -sDoes he work here?

Key idea: use do with I, you, we, they and does with he, she, it. After do or does, the main verb stays in the base form (work, not works).

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"What does she works?"
"What does she do?"

After does, the main verb stays in the base form, so we say does she do, never "does she works".
