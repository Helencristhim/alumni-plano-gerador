Chapter 3: Reporting the News

Telling People What You Heard

Reported speech · said (that) + backshift

Discover the Code

Listen and Find the Pattern

"Your flight is delayed." → She said the flight was delayed.

"We will rebook you." → They said they would rebook me.

"The plane has landed." → He said the plane had landed.

"I can help you." → The agent told me she could help me.

Their exact wordsWhen you report it
present (is / are)→ past (was / were)
past simple (was / landed)→ past perfect (had been / had landed)
present perfect (has done)→ past perfect (had done)
will→ would
can / may→ could / might

Use said (that)... with no person, or told + person (told me, told the passengers). Time and place words shift too: now → then, today → that day, here → there.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"She said me the flight was full."

✓ RIGHT

"She told me the flight was full."

✗ WRONG

"He said the gate is closed." (he said it earlier)

✓ RIGHT

"He said the gate was closed."

Chapter 4: At the Gate

A Problem at the Gate

Pricila handles a missed connection with the ground staff

Dialogue

Rebooking a Missed Connection

P
Excuse me, I think there is a problem. My flight was delayed, and I have just missed my connecting flight to Porto.
G
I am sorry to hear that. Let me check. Yes, I can see the incoming flight arrived late. Don't worry, we will rebook you free of charge.
P
Thank you, that is a relief. Will the next flight have a seat, or will I be on standby?
G
You have a confirmed seat. There is a two-hour layover, but I will give you a voucher for the departure lounge.
P
That is very kind. One more thing -- I am worried the airline might misplace my suitcase during the change.
G
I have tagged it through to Porto, so it will travel with you. Just collect it at baggage claim when you land.

Chapter 6: Your Turn

Handle It Yourself

Guided > Semi-free > Free

Role-Play

At the Service Desk

Scenario: Your flight was delayed and you have missed your connecting flight. Explain the problem to the ground staff (the teacher) and ask to be rebooked.

Key phrases:

I have missed my connecting flight. Could you rebook me? Will I be on standby?
Role-Play

Report It to a Friend

Scenario: You just left the desk. Call a friend and report everything the ground staff told you: the delay, the rebooking, the layover, your bag. Use "they said..." and "she told me...".

Tools:

She told me that... They said they would... He said the flight had...
Free Practice

Your Travel Story

Scenario: Tell the teacher the full story of a trip that went wrong at the airport -- real or imagined -- from the delay to baggage claim. Report what the staff said to you along the way.

No key phrases. Tell the story and report the news, from start to finish!
