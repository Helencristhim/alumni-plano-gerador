Stage 1.1: Words for the Airport

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. These are rich B2 phrases for handling problems at the airport.

Overbooked -- com mais reservas que assentos
"The flight was overbooked, so they asked for volunteers to give up their seats."
A layover -- escala / espera entre voos
"We had a four-hour layover in Madrid before the next flight."
Ground staff -- equipe de terra do aeroporto
"The ground staff at the gate helped me find a new connection."
A connecting flight -- voo de conexao
"If this one is late, I will miss my connecting flight to Rome."
To be rebooked -- ser remarcado em outro voo
"After the delay, I was rebooked onto the morning flight."
To be bumped -- ser retirado do voo por overbooking
"I was bumped from the flight, but they gave me a voucher and a hotel."
Standby -- lista de espera por um assento
"They put me on standby for the next flight, just in case."
To misplace -- extraviar / perder temporariamente
"The airline misplaced my suitcase, but it arrived a day later."
To clear security -- passar pela seguranca do aeroporto
"Once you clear security, the lounge is on your left."
The departure lounge -- sala de embarque
"We relaxed in the departure lounge until they called our gate."
Baggage claim -- esteira de retirada de bagagem
"After we landed, I waited ages at baggage claim for my bag."
Customs -- alfandega
"We had nothing to declare, so customs was quick."
Jet lag -- cansaco do fuso horario
"The jet lag was so bad that I slept all afternoon."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Reported Speech

Grammar

Para contar o que alguem disse, o verbo geralmente "recua" um tempo: is/are -> was/were, will -> would, have/has done -> had done. Use say (sem objeto) ou tell + pessoa. / To report what someone said, the verb usually shifts back one tense: is/are -> was/were, will -> would, have/has done -> had done. Use say (no object) or tell + person.

DirectReportedPortugues
"It is overbooked."She said it was overbooked.Ela disse que estava com overbooking.
"I will rebook you."She said she would rebook me.Ela disse que me remarcaria.
"We have misplaced it."He said they had misplaced it.Ele disse que tinham extraviado.

Common mistakeNOT "She said me." Say "She told me" or "She said that".Nunca "say me".
