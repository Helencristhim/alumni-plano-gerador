Lesson 8 -- What Should Have Happened

Series
Industry -- Season 1, Episode 5: "Learned Behaviour"

Characters review the consequences of past decisions and face performance reviews. Watch how they connect what they did to where they are now -- mixed conditional thinking applied to career situations.

Note 3 moments where characters reflect on past actions and their present consequences. Try converting their reflections into mixed conditionals.

Podcast
Supply Chain Now -- "Post-Mortem: What We Learned from the Container Crisis"

Industry experts conduct a real post-mortem of a major supply chain failure. Listen for root cause analysis language, corrective actions, and how they describe what should have been done differently.

Write down every time a speaker says "If we had..." or "If we were..." and classify each as regular conditional or mixed conditional.

A 12-minute guide on conducting productive failure reviews. Covers root cause analysis, lessons learned documentation, and how to turn failures into organizational improvements.
