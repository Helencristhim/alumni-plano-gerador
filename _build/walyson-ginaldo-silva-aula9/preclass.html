Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Strength -- a thing you are good at; a strong point
"My biggest strength is writing clear emails." (Ponto forte)
Weakness -- a thing you are not good at yet; a weak point
"My main weakness is speaking too quickly." (Ponto fraco)
Improve -- to get better at something over time
"I want to improve my speaking this year." (Melhorar)
Struggle -- to find something hard and try with effort
"I still struggle with the past tense." (Ter dificuldade)
Confident -- feeling sure about yourself and your skills
"Now I feel confident on the phone." (Confiante)
Fluent -- able to speak a language smoothly and easily
"I speak more fluently than before." (Fluente)
Review -- to look at or study something again
"I review one lesson every week." (Revisar)
Assess -- to judge or measure how good something is
"Today you assess your own progress." (Avaliar)
Goal -- something you want to reach or achieve
"My goal is to speak in longer sentences." (Meta)
Feedback -- helpful comments about your work or progress
"The feedback from my teacher helped me a lot." (Retorno / feedback)
Overcome -- to deal with and beat a problem or fear
"I want to overcome my fear of mistakes." (Superar)
Reflect -- to think carefully about your experience
"I reflect on my progress every month." (Refletir)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- used to / would

GRAMMAR

Usamos used to e would para comparar o passado com o presente. / We use used to and would to compare the past with now.

Form / FormaStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Past habit / stateused to + base verbI used to feel nervous.
Past repeated actionwould + base verbI would practice every morning.
Negativedid not + use to + base verbI did not use to feel confident.
Now (present)present simpleNow I feel confident.

Key point / Ponto-chave: use used to + verbo base para hábitos e estados do passado que mudaram. Use would apenas para ações repetidas, não para estados (diga "I used to be", não "I would be"). No negativo e na pergunta, tire o -d: use to.
