Lesson 9 -- Looking Back, Moving Forward

Series
The Last Dance -- Episode 1 (documentary)

Watch how an elite team reflects on its journey, talks about strengths and weaknesses, and sets goals for the future. Notice how people compare how things used to be with how they are now.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down three sentences with used to or would, and one strength and one weakness you hear.

Podcast
All Ears English -- "How to Talk About Your Progress and Goals"

A clear episode on reflecting in English: describing your strengths and weaknesses, giving and receiving feedback, and setting goals to improve.

Record a 2-minute self-assessment: how you used to speak, how you speak now, your strength, your weakness, and one goal for the next block.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "used to, would and the past"

A focused lesson on used to and would for past habits and states. It directly reinforces today's grammar for comparing the past with now.

After watching, write six sentences about your English journey: three with used to and three with would.
