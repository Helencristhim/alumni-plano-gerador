Complementary Activities

Optional extras to enjoy outside class. Mark each one as done after you watch or listen to it.

Lesson 1 -- Who Is Ana, Really?

Series
The Repair Shop -- Season 1, Episode 1 (full episode)

People bring broken family treasures to a barn full of craftspeople, who bring them back to life: furniture, clocks, old toys. Every object has a story behind it. If you love restoring things, this is your world in English -- and the restorers explain exactly what they are doing while they do it.

Tip: listen for the present continuous. The restorers narrate their own work: "I am taking this apart", "I am sanding it down". Say one sentence about what you are fixing at home this week.

Podcast
Urban Exodus, E69 -- Academics leave city life for a farm in the Australian bush

Louise and David sold a comfortable city life in Sydney and moved 27 kilometers from the nearest town. They talk about what they gained, what they miss, and what nobody tells you about the silence. It is the same decision you made, told by other people -- in Australian English.

Tip: this is a different accent from the American English you hear in class. Listen once for the general story only, without trying to catch every word. That is the skill we are training.

Listen on Spotify ↗
Documentary
Inside the Mind of a Dog (Netflix)

What dogs actually think, how they read us, and why a rescued street dog bonds the way it does. Narrated clearly and slowly, with scientists explaining their work. Seventy-five minutes about the animals who wake you up every morning.

Tip: watch with English subtitles the first time, then rewatch one favorite scene with the subtitles off and see how much you still catch.
