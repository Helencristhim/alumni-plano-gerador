Lesson 13 -- Negotiating a Deal

Series
Suits -- a negotiation scene (any season)

Watch a scene where two sides bargain over terms. Notice how each side states a bottom line, uses leverage, and offers a trade-off before they meet halfway.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down one conditional offer ("If you..., I will...") and one deal-breaker you hear.

Podcast
Negotiate Anything -- "Conditional Offers and Trade-offs"

A practical episode on how to bargain: knowing your bottom line, using leverage, making offers with conditions, and closing in good faith without reaching a deadlock.

Record three conditional offers for a coffee contract: one with "if", one with "unless", and one with "provided".

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Conditionals: if, unless and provided"

A focused lesson on the first conditional and the words unless and provided. It explains the structure (present simple in the condition, will in the result) and directly reinforces today's grammar.

After watching, write six sentences: two with "if", two with "unless", and two with "provided", all about a price negotiation.
