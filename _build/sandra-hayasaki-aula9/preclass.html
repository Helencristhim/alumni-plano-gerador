Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

Reliable -- always there when you need them; you can trust them
"He is a reliable friend who never lets you down." (confiável)
Generous -- happy to give time, help, or things to others
"She is so generous that she always shares everything." (generoso/a)
Charming -- very pleasant, friendly, and easy to like
"He is a charming man who makes everyone smile." (charmoso/encantador)
Downtown -- the central, busy part of a city
"My office is downtown, near the main square." (centro da cidade)
Crowded -- full of people; with very little free space
"The square was crowded with families." (lotado/cheio de gente)
Peaceful -- calm, quiet, and relaxing
"In the morning, the beach is so peaceful." (tranquilo/sereno)
Stunning -- extremely beautiful; amazing to look at
"The sunset from the hill was absolutely stunning." (deslumbrante)
View -- what you can see from a certain place
"Our room had a beautiful view of the sea." (vista)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Relative Clauses (who / which / that)

GRAMMAR

Para descrever melhor, juntamos duas ideias numa frase só. Usamos who para pessoas, which para coisas e lugares, e that para os dois. A cláusula vem logo depois do substantivo, e não repetimos o sujeito (he/she/it) depois do who/which/that. / To describe better, we join two ideas into one sentence. Use who for people, which for things and places, and that for both. The clause comes right after the noun, and we do not repeat the subject after it.

WordUse it forExample
whopeople"a friend who helps me"
whichthings & places"a town which is quiet"
thatpeople, things, places"the person that helped me"
