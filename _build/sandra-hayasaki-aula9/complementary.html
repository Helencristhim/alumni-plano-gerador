Aula 9 — Describing People & Places

Series
Friends — any episode (describing the people you love)

The whole show is six friends describing each other and their favorite places. Listen for relative clauses ("She is the one who...", "It is the café that...") and describing words like charming and reliable.

Dica: escolha uma cena e anote uma frase com "who" sobre uma pessoa e uma com "which/that" sobre um lugar. / Pick a scene and write one sentence with "who" about a person and one with "which/that" about a place.

Podcast
The Allusionist — "Places" episodes

Clear, gentle English about cities and places: what makes a place charming, peaceful, or crowded. A great way to hear describing words used naturally about real locations.

Dica: ouça uma vez só e anote três palavras que descrevem lugares. / Listen once and note three words that describe places.
