Lesson 10 -- The Scientific Debate

Series
The Crown -- any cabinet or debate scene

Watch how speakers articulate a clear argument, hear a counterpoint, and rebut it without attacking the other person. Notice how the most persuasive speaker stays calm.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down one persuasive argument and one counterpoint that you hear.

Podcast
Intelligence Squared (IQ2) Debates

A real debate podcast: a moderator reads the motion, two sides argue, and the audience decides which case was more persuasive. Perfect to hear rebuttals and counterpoints in action.

Pick one episode. Note how each speaker rebuts a counterpoint with evidence, then say the argument again in your own words.

YouTube
TED-Ed -- "How to argue like an expert"

A short, clear video on how to build a valid, persuasive argument and how to rebut a counterpoint politely. It reinforces every skill from Block 2.

After watching, record a 2-minute mock debate: read a motion, give one argument with evidence, and rebut one counterpoint.
