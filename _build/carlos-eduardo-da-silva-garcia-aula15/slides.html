A professional email has five parts, always in this order. Ask politely: Could you please...? / Um email profissional tem cinco partes, sempre nesta ordem. Peca com educacao: Could you please...?

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"I want you to send me the data."

✓ RIGHT

"Could you please send me the data?"

✗ WRONG

"I am writing for request information."

✓ RIGHT

"I am writing to request information."

In a professional email, ask politely (Could you please...?) and use "I am writing to request...". / Num email profissional, peca com educacao (Could you please...?) e use "I am writing to request...".

Chapter 4: A Real E-mail

One First-Contact E-mail

Read the email line by line, from greeting to signature

Real Artifact

First-Contact E-mail

New MessageSucafina Brazil
Subject: Regarding your farm registration
Dear Ms. Nakato, I hope this email finds you well.
My name is Carlos Eduardo. I am a Sustainability Analyst at Sucafina Brazil.
I am writing to request your farm registration data. Could you please send me the documents?
Thank you in advance. Best regards, Carlos Eduardo, Sustainability Analyst.
