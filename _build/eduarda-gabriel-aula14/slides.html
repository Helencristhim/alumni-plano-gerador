Chapter 3 — The Code

Have Been -ing

Look at the purple words. How do we report an action that started in the past and is still going on?

We have been monitoring the covenants since the closing.
The borrower has been complying with every milestone.
We have been following up with their finance team for two days.
How long have you been tracking the debt levels?
PartFormExample
have / has been+ verb-ing"We have been monitoring..."
Durationfor / since"...since the closing / for two days."
QuestionHow long have/has + been + -ing"How long have you been tracking it?"
Grammar Rule

Present Perfect Continuous

FunctionPatternExample
Report an ongoing actionhave / has been + verb-ing"We have been monitoring the deal."
Show duration (start point)... since + point in time"...since the closing."
Show duration (period)... for + period"...for three weeks."
Ask about durationHow long have/has + subject + been + -ing"How long have you been tracking it?"
Common Mistake

Watch Out!

"We monitor the covenants since the closing."
"We have been monitoring the covenants since the closing."

For an action that started in the past and is still going on, use have / has been + -ing — not the present simple.

The Compliance Review

J
Eduarda, the deal closed a month ago. How has the borrower been doing on compliance?
E
We have been monitoring the covenants since the closing, and they have been hitting every milestone.
J
Good. Have you flagged anything at all?
E
Just one item. Their reporting package arrived late, so I have been following up with their finance team.
J
Have you escalated it, or is it still at your level?
E
I have already escalated it to the credit committee, and we have agreed on a remediation plan.
J
Excellent work. So overall, how is their track record looking?
E
Very solid. They have been complying consistently for a month, so I am comfortable with the position.

Re: Vertex Industries — Monthly Compliance Report

We have been monitoring all financial covenants since the closing. The borrower has been complying fully and has hit every milestone on schedule.

One item has been flagged: the reporting package arrived two days late. We have been following up with the finance team, and the issue has already been escalated to the credit committee.

A remediation plan has been agreed. Overall, the borrower's track record has been solid, and no covenant breach has been recorded this month.
