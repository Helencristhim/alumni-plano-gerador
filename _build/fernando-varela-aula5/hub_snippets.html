Aula 05 -- Pre-class

Introducing & Presenting Other People

Welcoming an international partner and presenting a colleague or director (this is..., have you met...?, talking about a third person). Key words: introduce, this is, have you met, host, guest, director, team, leads, responsible for, pleasure. Structure: third person present (he works / she leads / he is responsible for, with -s) + introduction formulas (This is... / I'd like you to meet... / Have you met...?).

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para escutar.

Introduce -- apresentar alguem (to present a person to another)
"Let me introduce my team."
This is... -- este(a) e... (words to present a person)
"This is Dr. Lima, our director."
Have you met...? -- voce ja conhece...? (a question to ask if you know someone)
"Have you met Sarah, our guest?"
Host -- anfitriao (the person who receives the guests)
"I am your host today."
Guest -- convidado, visitante (a visitor you welcome)
"Sarah is our guest from Chicago."
Director -- diretor(a) (a person who leads a department)
"He is our research director."
Team -- equipe (a group of people who work together)
"This is my team."
Leads -- lidera (he/she leads = guides a group)
"She leads the oncology team."
Responsible for -- responsavel por (in charge of a task or area)
"He is responsible for research."
Pleasure -- prazer (a nice, happy feeling)
"It is a pleasure to meet you."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

At the Einstein, Fernando is the host of an international visit. Sarah is his guest. "It's a pleasure to have you here," he says. "Let me introduce our team. This is Dr. Lima, our research director. He leads the clinical studies. He is responsible for our CAR-T research." "Wonderful," says Sarah. "Have you met the new partner from Boston?" "Not yet," Fernando answers.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Third Person & Introductions

GRAMMAR

Como apresentar pessoas e falar de uma terceira pessoa na 3a pessoa do singular (explicacao em ingles e portugues).

StructureUse / UsoExample
This is + nameApresentar uma pessoaThis is Dr. Lima.
He / She + verb + sDizer o que a pessoa faz (3a pessoa, +s)She leads the team.
He / She is responsible forDizer pelo que e responsavelHe is responsible for research.
Have you met + name?Perguntar se ja conhece — ou "I'd like you to meet..."

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

A partner arrives at the Einstein. You welcome the guest:
A "Okay."
B "Welcome! It's a pleasure to have you here."
C "I am tired."
You present your director to the partner. You say:
A "This is Dr. Lima, our research director."
B "I present you Dr. Lima."
C "Here Dr. Lima."
You describe what she does. The correct sentence is:
A "She lead the oncology team."
B "She leads the oncology team."
C "She is lead the oncology team."
You want to ask if the partner knows your colleague Ana. You ask:
A "You know Ana?"
B "Have you met my colleague Ana?"
C "Ana is who?"
