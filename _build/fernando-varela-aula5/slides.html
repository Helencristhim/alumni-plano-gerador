Chapter 3

The -s Code

He works · She leads · He is responsible for

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"This is Dr. Lima. He leads our team."

"She works in oncology."

"He is responsible for research."

"Have you met our director?"

UseStructureExample
Present a personThis is + nameThis is Dr. Lima.
Say what they doHe / She + verb + sShe leads the team.
Be responsibleHe / She is responsible forHe is responsible for research.
Ask if they knowHave you met + name? — I'd like you to meet...
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"He work in oncology."

✓ RIGHT

"He works in oncology."

✗ WRONG

"She lead the team."

✓ RIGHT

"She leads the team."

✗ WRONG

"I present you Dr. Lima."

✓ RIGHT

"I'd like you to meet Dr. Lima."

For he or she (one person), the verb takes an -s: he works, she leads. To present a person, say "This is..." or "I'd like you to meet...".

Wonderful. Your team is great, Fernando.
