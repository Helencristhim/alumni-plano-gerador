Lesson 5 — Introducing & Presenting Other People

Series
The Office (US) -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

A manager hosts a visitor and introduces the team in the office. Listen for "This is...", "He works in...", and "She is responsible for...". Direct connection to Lesson 5: presenting other people and saying what each person does.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down 3 sentences that present a person (This is... / He leads...).
