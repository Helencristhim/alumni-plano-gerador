Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

Karina has a big day today. David, a client from Canada, visits her company. Karina is ready: "My name is Karina Macedo. I am from São Paulo, Brazil. I have ten years of experience." At the office, she presents AeroBras: "We have partners in many countries. We offer excellent service." David asks, "How much does the small jet cost?" Karina smiles: "It costs four million dollars."

1. Como Karina fala a idade da empresa dela no mercado? / How does Karina talk about her experience?
A "I am ten years of experience."
B "I have ten years of experience."
C "I do ten years of experience."
2. Como David pergunta o preço? / How does David ask the price?
A "How much costs the small jet?"
B "How many is the small jet?"
C "How much does the small jet cost?"
3. O que a AeroBras oferece? / What does AeroBras offer?
A Excellent service.
B Cheap aircraft.
C Free delivery only in Brazil.
