Aula 5 -- What Do You Recommend? -- Making Recommendations

Series
Suits -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

Lawyers at a New York firm advise clients and each other all day long. Listen for constant recommendations: "You should take the deal," "I would advise against that," "We had better move fast." It is exactly the language you use when the board asks for your view.

Tip: watch one advising scene with English subtitles. Write down five sentences with should, ought to, had better, or "I would recommend / advise".
