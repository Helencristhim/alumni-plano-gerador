Chapter 3

Phone Phrases

Starting a call politely

Discover the Rule

Listen and Notice

"Hello, this is Elaine."

"Can I speak to the manager?"

"Could you take a message?"

"Can I leave a message?"

PhraseUseExample
This is...say your nameHello, this is Elaine.
Can I speak to...?ask for a personCan I speak to the manager?
Can I / Could you... a message?leave or take a messageCan I leave a message?

On the phone, say "this is" (not "I am"). Ask for a person with "Can I speak to...?" and use "message" when no one can answer.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"Hello, I am Elaine."

✓ RIGHT

"Hello, this is Elaine."

✗ WRONG

"Can I speak the manager?"

✓ RIGHT

"Can I speak to the manager?"

On the phone, say "this is" + your name. Always use "speak to" + a person.

ELAINE'S TRAVEL NOTES

How to Make a Call

1.Say hello: "Hello, this is Elaine."
2.Ask for a person: "Can I speak to...?"
3.If busy: "Could you take a message?"
4.Give your number clearly.
5.End: "Thank you. Goodbye!"

Tip: speak slowly and clearly on the phone
