Succession — any boardroom or deal-structuring scene

Watch how the characters present a merger or acquisition to the board: they name the structure, explain the synergies, defend the valuation, and report what has already been done. Notice how often the present perfect ("We have...", "The board has...") frames the status of the deal.

Tip: pick one boardroom scene and write a one-page memo in English: structure, valuation, one synergy, the equity, and the due-diligence status.
