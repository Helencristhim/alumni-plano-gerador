Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Negotiate -- to talk to agree on a price or a deal
"Let's negotiate the price together." (Negociar)
Deal -- an agreement to buy or sell something
"This is a very good deal." (Negócio / Acordo)
Propose -- to present an idea or a plan
"I propose a lower price." (Propor)
Budget -- the money you have to spend
"My budget is ready for the deal." (Orçamento)
Discount -- a lower price than normal
"I can give you a small discount." (Desconto)
Counter -- a different offer you give back
"I have a counter idea for you." (Contraproposta)
Accept -- to say yes to an offer or a deal
"I accept the deal." (Aceitar)
Terms -- the conditions of a deal
"The terms are fair for both." (Condições / Termos)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Making Offers & Comparatives

GRAMMAR

Como fazer uma oferta e comparar opções. / How to make an offer and compare options.

Phrase / FraseMeaning / SentidoExample / Exemplo
I would like to offer...Fazer uma oferta / Make an offerI would like to offer you a good price.
cheaper / bigger thanComparar (adj curto + -er + than) / CompareThis one is cheaper than the old one.
better thanComparar (forma irregular) / Compare (irregular)The new deal is better than the old one.
What about...?Fazer uma contraproposta / Make a counter offerWhat about a bigger discount?

Key point / Ponto-chave: com adjetivos curtos, adicione -er e use than: cheap → cheaper than, big → bigger than, fast → faster than. Good é irregular: good → better than (não "gooder"). Para uma contraproposta, diga What about...?
