Lesson 16 -- Negotiation Basics: Making Offers

Series
Shark Tank (US) -- any pitch and negotiation scene

Business owners make offers and negotiate deals with investors. You hear real offers, counter offers, discounts, and comparatives like "cheaper" and "better than".

Watch with English subtitles. Write down one offer ("I would like to offer...") and one counter offer ("What about...?") that you hear.

Podcast
Speak English Now -- "Making Offers and Negotiating a Deal"

A slow, clear podcast about negotiation: how to make an offer, compare options with comparatives, ask "What about...?", and accept a deal politely.

Repeat each phrase out loud. Then record a short offer for an aircraft, using a comparative like "cheaper than".

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Comparatives and making offers in English"

A short, practical lesson on today's language: making offers with "I would like to offer...", comparing options (cheaper, bigger, better than), and counter offers with "What about...?".

After watching, write five sentences to negotiate a deal: make an offer, compare two options, give a discount, ask "What about...?", and accept. Then read them aloud.
