Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Business Toolbox

GRAMMAR

Revisão do bloco. Present continuous: am/is/are + verbo-ing para o que acontece agora (I am calling). Going to: am/is/are going to + verbo na base para planos futuros (we are going to launch). Comparativo: adjetivo curto + -er + than, ou more + adjetivo longo + than (faster than, more reliable than) -- sempre THAN, nunca "then". Superlativo: the + adjetivo + -est, ou the most + adjetivo longo (the cheapest, the most popular). Present perfect: have/has + particio para resultados (we have reached). / Block review. Present continuous for now; going to for future plans; comparatives (with than) to compare two; superlatives (with the) for the best; present perfect (have/has + participle) for results.

ToolExample
Now -- present continuousI am calling a customer now.
Future plan -- going toWe are going to launch a service.
Compare two -- comparative (+ than)It is more reliable than the old one.
The best -- superlative (the ...)It is the most popular choice.
Result -- present perfectWe have reached our target.
