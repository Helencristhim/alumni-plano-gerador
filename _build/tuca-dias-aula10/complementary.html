Lesson 10 -- The Story So Far: Progress Check

Series / Documentary
Down to Earth with Zac Efron (Netflix)

A relaxed travel series where the host reflects on what he has learned in each place he visits. Perfect for hearing real people mix past, present perfect, and future as they talk about their journeys.

Watch one episode with English subtitles. Write three sentences about it: one with the past simple, one with the present perfect, and one with going to or will.

Podcast
The Fluent Show -- "How to Track Your Language Progress"

A clear, encouraging episode about measuring your progress, setting goals, and staying confident as a language learner. Full of useful reflection language for talking about how far you have come.

After listening, record a one-minute voice note about your biggest achievement and your next goal in English. Use the words progress, improve, and confident.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "English Tenses Review"

A practical video that reviews the present simple, past simple, present perfect, and future together, with clear examples of when to use each one. Exactly today's grammar focus.

After watching, write four short sentences about your own story, one in each tense, and read them aloud while recording yourself.
