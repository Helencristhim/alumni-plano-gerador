Aula 13 — Block 3: Measuring the Change & Reporting to the Board

Series
Industry (HBO) — any board-review or performance-update scene

Watch how leaders report numbers to a board: they lead with the results, show the metrics against a baseline, compare to the benchmark, and are honest about what is behind. Notice how often they say "has been" and "have been" to show what has been happening over time.

Tip: pick one review scene and write five sentences in the present perfect continuous about how the metrics have been moving (has been rising, have been tracking, has been climbing).
