Flavia is preparing for her speech, because she speaks in ten minutes. She is a little nervous, but she is ready. Everyone is working hard, and the day is going well.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Continuous (am / is / are + -ing)

GRAMMAR

How to describe an action that is happening right now.

SubjectbeExample
IamI am waiting at the booth.
he / she / itisShe is presenting on the stage.
you / we / theyareThey are waiting for us.
The -ing formmost verbs: + ing (wait → waiting) · verbs with -e: drop the e (prepare → preparing) · short vowel + consonant: double it (get → getting). Negative: I am not waiting. Question: Are you waiting?
