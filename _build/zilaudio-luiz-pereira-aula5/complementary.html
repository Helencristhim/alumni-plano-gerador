Polite English: How to Ask People to Repeat Themselves — engVid

This lesson is your exact problem: someone speaks and you miss it. It shows you the polite ways to ask again — "Sorry?", "I didn't catch that", "Could you speak more slowly, please?" — so a short "What?" never sounds rude again.

Tip: take the quiz at the end of the page. Then write down the three phrases you like most and keep them next to your computer for your next call.
