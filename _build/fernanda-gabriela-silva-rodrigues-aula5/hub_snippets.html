Vocabulary

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

A good email has four parts. First, a clear subject line. Then a greeting: Dear Carlos. In the body, I write: "I am writing to schedule a call regarding a coffee shipment." I also attach the contract. Please reply soon. Finally, a closing: Best regards, and my signature.

1. We start the body with "I am writing to...". Why? (Por que "I am writing to..."?)
A It is a polite way to say the email's purpose. (Forma educada de dar o objetivo.)
B It is a closing. (E um fechamento.)
C It is the subject line. (E o assunto.)
2. What does "please find attached" mean? (O que significa?)
A Please reply fast. (Responda rapido.)
B There is a file with the email. (Ha um arquivo anexo.)
C Forward this email. (Encaminhe.)
3. Which is a good closing? (Qual e um bom fechamento?)
A Dear Carlos. (Caro Carlos.)
B Best regards. (Atenciosamente.)
C Subject: shipment. (Assunto: carregamento.)

Etapa 4: Quiz Situacional

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao profissional.

You open an email to a supplier. You write: (Voce abre um email.)
A "Hey, listen up."
B "Dear Carlos, I am writing regarding the shipment."
C "Carlos! Email me!"
You want to send a file with the email. You write: (Voce quer anexar um arquivo.)
A "The contract is in the email here."
B "Please find attached the contract."
C "Contract I put now."
You close a professional email. You write: (Voce fecha o email.)
A "Bye bye now."
B "Best regards, Fernanda."
C "End of email Fernanda."

Etapa 5: Producao Livre

Reflection

Grave voce respondendo a pergunta abaixo. Nao ha resposta certa ou errada.

Say a short email out loud to a new supplier. Use the four parts: a subject line, a greeting (Dear...), a body ("I am writing to..."), and a closing (Best regards). Mention you attach the contract. Take your time. (Diga em voz alta um email curto a um fornecedor: assunto, saudacao, corpo e fechamento.)
