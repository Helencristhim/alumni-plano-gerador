Chapter 3: The Code

The Four Parts

subject · greeting · body · closing

Discover the Grammar

Notice the Order

1. Subject: Coffee shipment - call request

2. Greeting: Dear Carlos,

3. Body: I am writing to schedule a call.

4. Closing: Best regards, Fernanda

PartPhrase
GreetingDear + name,
Body (purpose)I am writing to + verb
ClosingBest regards, + name

"I am writing to..." gives the purpose. / da o objetivo.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"I writing to schedule a call."

✓ RIGHT

"I am writing to schedule a call."

✗ WRONG

"Hey, send the contract."

✓ RIGHT

"Dear Carlos, please send the contract."

Chapter 4: Writing It

Writing the Email

A real email, line by line

Artifact

Fernanda's Email

Subject: Coffee shipment - call request

Dear Carlos,

I am writing to schedule a call regarding a coffee shipment. We have a delivery on March 15th. Please find attached the contract. Please reply when you can.

Best regards,
Fernanda Rodrigues
Sucafina - Trading Department
