A series full of high-stakes business dinners, invitations and social networking. Direct connection to Lesson 15: notice how characters invite each other, accept and decline, and build relationships outside the meeting room.

Practical episodes on inviting people, accepting warmly and declining politely in professional settings. Direct connection to Lesson 15: Would you like to...? / How about...? and I'd love to / I'm afraid I can't.
