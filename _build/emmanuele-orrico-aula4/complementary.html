Lesson 04 -- Breaking the Ice at a Global Event

Series
The Office (US) — Season 2, Episode 5: "Halloween" (Netflix)

Do not watch it for the comedy — watch it for the small talk. The whole office is made of hallway conversations: people introducing themselves, asking what the other person is doing, reacting. It is the exact mechanic of your lesson 4, in natural American English and in short sentences.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Treasure hunt: write down 3 sentences that start with "I am working on..." or "We are..." (the structure of the NOW). Do not try to understand 100% — look ONLY for the structure.
