Aula 18 -- Pre-class

Closing the Deal -- Confirming the Agreement

Close a deal and confirm an agreement. Say the deal is done ("We have a deal."), confirm the details ("Let's confirm the details."), agree to the terms ("I agree to the terms."), and sign the contract ("Please sign here."). New vocabulary: agreement, contract, sign, condition, payment, delivery, guarantee, satisfied.

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Karina was happy. "We have a deal, Robert!" she said. "Let's confirm the details of the agreement." The payment was ready, the delivery was next month, and the guarantee was one year. Robert read the contract. The conditions were clear, and he was very satisfied. "I agree to the terms," he said. Karina smiled: "Please sign here." Robert signed, and the deal was done.

2. Why is Robert satisfied?

B The conditions are clear and the terms are fair.
C There is no contract.
3. What does Karina ask Robert to do?

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Closing & Confirming

GRAMMAR

Como fechar um negócio e confirmar um acordo. / How to close a deal and confirm an agreement.

Phrase / FraseMeaning / SentidoExample / Exemplo
We have a deal.Dizer que o negócio está fechado / Say the deal is doneWe have a deal! Thank you.
Let's confirm the details.Revisar os detalhes / Check the detailsLet's confirm the details of the agreement.
I agree to the terms.Concordar (verbo: não "I am agree") / Say yesThe terms are fair. I agree to the terms.
Please sign here.Pedir a assinatura / Ask for the signatureHere is the contract. Please sign here.

Key point / Ponto-chave: para fechar um negócio, use frases fixas: We have a deal (com a deal), Let's confirm the details e Please sign here. Para concordar, agree é um verbo: I agree to the terms (não "I am agree").
