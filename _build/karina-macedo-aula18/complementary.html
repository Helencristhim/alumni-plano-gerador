Lesson 18 -- Closing the Deal: Confirming the Agreement

Series
Suits -- any deal-signing or contract scene

Lawyers and business people confirm agreements and sign contracts. You hear real phrases like "We have a deal", "I agree to the terms", and "Please sign here".

Watch with English subtitles. Write down one phrase that closes a deal and one that asks for a signature.

Podcast
Speak English Now -- "Closing a Deal and Signing a Contract"

A slow, clear podcast about closing a deal: how to confirm the details, agree to the terms, and sign a contract politely.

Repeat each phrase out loud. Then record a short closing for a deal, using "We have a deal" and "I agree to the terms".

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Confirming and agreeing in English"

A short, practical lesson on today's language: closing a deal with "We have a deal", confirming details, agreeing with "I agree to the terms", and signing with "Please sign here".

After watching, write five sentences to close a deal: say the deal is done, confirm the details, agree to the terms, ask to sign, and end politely. Then read them aloud.
