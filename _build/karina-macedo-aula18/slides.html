Chapter 3 -- Confirm and Agree

Grammar Discovery

We have a deal -- I agree to the terms

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

How do we confirm a deal and agree to the terms?

"We have a deal. Thank you, Robert."

"Let's confirm the details of the agreement."

"I agree to the terms."

"Please sign here on the contract."

Grammar Rule

Confirming & Agreeing

PhraseWhen we use it
We have a deal.Say the deal is done
Let's confirm the details.Check the details together
I agree to the terms.Say yes (not "I am agree")
Please sign here.Ask for the signature

Key idea: to close a deal use fixed phrases: We have a deal (with a deal), Let's confirm the details, and Please sign here. To say yes, agree is a verb: I agree to the terms -- never "I am agree".

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I am agree to the terms. We have deal."
"I agree to the terms. We have a deal."

agree is a verb: I agree (not "I am agree"). Say a deal with a.
