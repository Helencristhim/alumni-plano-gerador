Lesson 16 -- Negotiating by Email

Series
Suits -- a price or deal negotiation scene (any season)

Watch a scene where a character negotiates terms and counters an offer. Notice how they justify a position, make a counterproposal, and reach a final figure.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down one counterproposal and one "final offer" line you hear.

Podcast
Plain English -- "How to Negotiate Politely in English"

A practical episode on bargaining language: making offers and counteroffers, using conditional sentences, and closing on a fair figure by email.

Record three counterproposal lines with the second conditional: "If you raised..., I would...", "If you covered..., I would...".

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "The second conditional"

A focused lesson on the second conditional: if + past simple, would + base verb, for imaginary situations. It directly reinforces today's grammar.

After watching, write six second-conditional sentences to negotiate a price: three offers and three counteroffers.
