Complementary Materials — Lesson 9

Series
Abstract: The Art of Design — Netflix: any episode

Each designer presents their work in clear, structured talks. Great for hearing how confident speakers open, transition between ideas and recap a key takeaway.

Tip: every time a speaker changes topic, note the transition they use, then copy it for your own outline.

Podcast
TED Talks Daily — any short talk under 8 minutes

Short TED talks are perfect models of mini-presentations: a clear outline, signpost phrases and one strong takeaway, all at a B2-friendly pace.

Tip: write down the speaker's outline (three main points) and their final takeaway, then say them out loud.

YouTube
BBC Learning English — "Signposting language for presentations"

A clear, short video that teaches the exact signpost phrases from this lesson: how to open, move on, highlight a point and sum up with a takeaway.

Tip: after watching, record a one-minute talk using "Let me start with", "Moving on to" and "To sum up".
