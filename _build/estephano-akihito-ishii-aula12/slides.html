Grammar Rule

Request Patterns

PatternUseExample
Can you + verb?ask a person to do somethingCan you help me?
Could you + verb?the same, but more politeCould you send me the file?
Can I + verb?ask for something for youCan I borrow your pen?

Key idea: after can or could, use the base verb -- help, send, open. We do not use "to" and we do not add "s". Add please to be more polite.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"Can you to help me?"
"Can you help me?"

After can or could, use the base verb directly. Do not use "to". Say "Can you help me" and "Could you open the door".
