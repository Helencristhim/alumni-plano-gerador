Lesson 16 -- Out and About: Shopping & Returns

Series / Travel
English with Lucy -- "Shopping for Clothes in English: Sizes, Fitting Rooms & Returns" (YouTube)

A clear, friendly guide to shopping for clothes in English: asking for sizes, trying things on in the fitting room, talking about fit, and making a return. Full of natural shopping vocabulary.

Watch with English subtitles. Then write four sentences about clothes that do not fit, two with "too" and two with "not ... enough".

Podcast
All Ears English -- "At the Shops: Buying Clothes and Making Returns in English"

A short, practical episode on shopping: asking for a size, trying things on, asking about a discount, and returning a faulty item for an exchange or refund. You will hear words like size, fit, and discount used naturally.

After listening, record a one-minute voice note about a shopping trip. Use too, not enough, and at least four shopping words.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "too and enough: How to Use Them Correctly"

A practical grammar video on too + adjective and not + adjective + enough, with clear examples about sizes and fit. Exactly today's grammar focus.

After watching, write four sentences about clothes, then change each "too small" into "not big enough" (remember: enough goes after the adjective).
