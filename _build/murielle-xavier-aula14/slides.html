UseStructureExample
Decision nowwill + base verbI will step in.
Offer / promisewill + base verbI will confirm the room.
Negativewill not (won't)I will not cancel it.
Prediction (opinion)I think + willThey will be satisfied.
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"I will handling it."

✓ RIGHT

"I will handle it."

✗ WRONG

"She will leads the panel."

✓ RIGHT

"She will lead the panel."

✗ WRONG

"I not will cancel it."

✓ RIGHT

"I will not cancel it."

After "will", the verb stays in the base form (no -s, no -ing). The negative is "will not". For questions, invert: "Will you...?"

Chapter 4

The Sudden Request

Offering help when a problem appears

Dialogue

Sofia Is Sick

E
Murielle, I just heard that Sofia is sick and cannot run the interviews tomorrow.
M
Oh no. That is a problem. Who will lead the panel?
E
Do not worry. I will step in and lead the panel myself.
M
That is a relief. Will you confirm the room and notify the candidates?
E
Yes. I will confirm the room this afternoon, and I will notify the candidates promptly. I promise I will not cancel anything.
M
Thank you. And if a candidate does not show up?
E
I will reassure the team and reschedule quickly. I guarantee we will cope well; we are reliable.
M
Perfect. I will send you the final list tonight.
