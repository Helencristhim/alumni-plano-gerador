Lesson 13 -- Getting Around the City

Series
Emily in Paris -- moving around the city scenes (Season 1)

Watch the scenes where Emily takes the metro and walks around Paris. Notice how people ask for directions with "Where is...?" and "How do I get to...?", and how they give directions with go straight and turn left.

Tip: Write down five city words you hear and one direction sentence with an imperative (for example, with go straight or turn left).

Podcast
Culips ESL -- "Asking for and Giving Directions"

A short, clear episode about getting around a city in English: asking the way, taking the subway and the bus, and following directions to find a place.

Tip: Record yourself giving directions from your home to your school: go straight, turn left or right, and get off at a stop.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Asking for directions"

A focused lesson on directions: how to ask "Where is...?" and "How do I get to...?", and how to give directions with the imperative (go straight, turn left, turn right). It reinforces today's grammar for finding your way.

Tip: After watching, write six sentences: three questions to ask the way, and three directions with go straight, turn left or get off.
