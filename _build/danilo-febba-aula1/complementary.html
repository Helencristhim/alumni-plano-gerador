Lesson 1 -- Introducing Yourself Professionally

Series
Silicon Valley (HBO) -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Minimum Viable Product"

Engineers and founders constantly introduce themselves, explain what they build, and pitch at tech events. Notice how they say their role, name their company, and talk about what they are working on. Direct connection to Lesson 1: professional self-introductions and talking about your current project in a tech environment.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Note 3 different ways the characters introduce themselves and their work.

Podcast
BBC Learning English -- "The English We Speak"

Short, clear episodes about everyday and professional English. Listen for natural phrases used when meeting people, stating your role, and keeping in touch. Direct connection to Lesson 1: the real expressions native speakers use for self-introductions and small talk — and a gentle warm-up for your listening, which is our main focus.

Tip: listen twice. Write down 5 phrases you could use to introduce yourself at a summit.

YouTube
Business English Pod -- "How to Introduce Yourself in English at Work"

A practical lesson on professional self-introductions: name, role, company, and field, plus the questions you ask back. Connection to Lesson 1: ready-to-use sentence patterns for networking at summits and meetings — exactly the situations where you tend to freeze.

Tip: pause and repeat each introduction aloud. Practice saying your own version 3 times.
