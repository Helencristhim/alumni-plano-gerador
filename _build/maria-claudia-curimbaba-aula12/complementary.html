Aula 12 -- Fiscal and Tax Talk

Tema da aula: talking to accountants about the fiscal year -- tax returns, deadlines, audits and what is deductible -- with the present perfect + for/since and the passive voice for fiscal facts. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
"Succession" -- the boardroom and accountant scenes

A family holding, advisors, and constant talk of the books, audits, liabilities and compliance -- exactly your world. Listen for how executives discuss what "has been done" and how long things "have been" a certain way, in fast, natural Business English.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote três frases no present perfect com for/since e duas frases na voz passiva sobre números ou auditoria.
