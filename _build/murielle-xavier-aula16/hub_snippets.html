Naming the very best of a group with superlative adjectives: short (the highest), long (the most efficient), irregular (the best/the worst), and one of the + superlative. Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. A tradução em português está entre parênteses. (Listen and read the example.)

Outstanding -- extremely good; much better than others (excepcional / notável)
"She is our most outstanding manager."
Exceptional -- unusually good (excepcional)
"This was an exceptional quarter for the team."
Leading -- most important or most successful (líder / principal)
"We are the leading employer in the region."
Remarkable -- so good it deserves attention (notável / impressionante)
"The team made remarkable progress."
Unmatched -- better than anything else; with no equal (inigualável / sem igual)
"Her track record is unmatched."
Foremost -- the most important; the best-known (principal / mais importante)
"He is our foremost expert on compliance."
Prominent -- important and well-known (proeminente / de destaque)
"She is a prominent leader in the company."
Prestigious -- respected and admired (prestigioso / de prestígio)
"It is the most prestigious award in the sector."
Flagship -- the best and most important of a group (carro-chefe / principal)
"This is our flagship program."
Standout -- clearly the best (destaque)
"She was the standout candidate."
Peak -- the highest point or level (pico / auge)
"Engagement reached its peak this year."
Utmost -- the greatest possible (máximo / maior possível)
"We treat safety with the utmost care."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Superlative Adjectives

GRAMMAR

Use superlativos para o melhor ou o pior de um grupo. Adjetivo curto: the + adjetivo + -est (the highest). Adjetivo longo: the most + adjetivo (the most efficient). Irregulares: good → the best, bad → the worst. Use sempre the antes do superlativo.

TipoFormaExemplo
Adjetivo curtothe + adj + -estThe North has the highest retention.
Adjetivo longothe most + adjShe is the most efficient manager.
Irregularthe best / the worstIt was the best quarter of the year.
Um dos melhoresone of the + superlative + pluralShe is one of the most reliable leaders.

Atenção: adjetivos curtos usam -est (não the most big). Adjetivos longos usam the most, nunca -est. Sempre use the antes do superlativo.
