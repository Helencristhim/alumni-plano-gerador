Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Oito palavras novas para fazer compras. Ouça cada uma e repita. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

Store -- loja — the place where you buy things
"I buy my clothes at this store."
Buy -- comprar — to give money and take a thing home
"I want to buy a new dress."
Price -- preço — how much money a thing costs
"What is the price of this shirt?"
Money -- dinheiro — the coins and notes you pay with
"How much money do you have?"
Cheap -- barato — it costs little money, a low price
"This shirt is cheap, only ten reais."
Expensive -- caro — it costs a lot of money, a high price
"The red dress is very expensive."
Size -- tamanho — how big a thing is: small, medium or large
"What size do you want?"
Pay -- pagar — to give your money for a thing
"Can I pay with card?"

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. Veja How much e How many em ação.

Érica is at a clothes store. She finds a nice blue shirt and asks: "How much is this shirt?" The shirt is forty reais — a good price, very cheap. She also asks: "How many colors do you have?" The store has four colors. The red dress is beautiful, but it is expensive. Érica wants a medium size, and she can pay with card or money. She is happy to buy two shirts today.

1. "How much is this shirt?" Why "how much"?
A Because it asks about the price.
B Because shirts are countable.
C Because it is about the past.
2. "How many colors do you have?" Why "how many"?
A Because colors cost money.
B Because colors are things you can count.
C Because it is a price.
3. Is the red dress cheap or expensive?
A Cheap.
B Expensive.
C The text does not say.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- How Much / How Many

GRAMMAR

Para perguntar quantidade ou preço, use How much ou How many. How much é para preço e dinheiro (coisas que não se contam: How much is it?). How many é para coisas que você conta, no plural (How many shirts?). / Use How much for price and uncountable nouns; use How many for countable nouns in the plural.

PerguntaQuando usar (agora)
How much...?preço e dinheiro — How much is it? (quanto custa?) · How much money? (quanto de dinheiro?)
How many...?coisas que se contam (plural -s) — How many shirts? (quantas camisas?)
resposta de preçoit is forty reais — custa quarenta reais; não se diz "how much cost"

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situação.

You want to know the price of a shirt. Which is correct?
A "How many is this shirt?"
B "How much is this shirt?"
C "How much cost this shirt?"
You want to know the number of colors. Which is correct?
A "How many colors do you have?"
B "How much colors do you have?"
C "How many color you have?"
The shirt costs only ten reais. You say:
A "This shirt is expensive."
B "This shirt is cheap."
C "This shirt is a price."
You give money for the shirt. The correct verb is:
A "I want to size the shirt."
B "I want to pay for the shirt."
C "I want to store the shirt."
