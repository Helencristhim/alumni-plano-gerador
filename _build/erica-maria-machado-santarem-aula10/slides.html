Chapter 3

The Question Code

How much · How many

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"How much is this shirt?"

"How much money do you have?"

"How many shirts do you want?"

"How many colors are there?"

QuestionWhen to use it
How much...?price & money (you cannot count): how much is it? how much money?
How many...?things you can count (plural -s): how many shirts? how many colors?
price answerit is forty reais · the shirt is cheap
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"How many money?"

✓ RIGHT

"How much money?"

✗ WRONG

"How much shirts?"

✓ RIGHT

"How many shirts?"

✗ WRONG

"How much cost this?"

✓ RIGHT

"How much is this?"

Use how much for price and money. Use how many for things you can count (shirts, colors). For the price, say "How much is it?", not "How much cost it?".

How Much Is This?

D
Hello, welcome to the store! Can I help you?
E
Yes, please. How much is this blue shirt? What is the price?
D
The blue shirt is forty reais. It is a good price, very cheap.
E
That is cheap! How many colors do you have?
D
We have four colors. What size do you want?
E
I want a medium size. Can I pay with card?

Yes, you can pay with card or money. How many shirts do you want?

Two shirts, please. I am happy to buy them today.
