Welcoming Guests: Greetings & Small Talk

Receba um convidado numa chamada: cumprimente pela hora do dia (Good morning / afternoon / evening), pergunte "How are you?", faca uma pequena conversa (How was your weekend?) e use frases de recepcao -- "Welcome! Please have a seat. Would you like a coffee?"

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

It is 9 a.m. and our guest joins the Zoom call. I start with a greeting: "Good morning, Doctor. Welcome. It's a pleasure to meet you." Then I ask, "How are you?" and make small talk: "How was your weekend? How is the weather?" Finally, I am polite and warm: "Please have a seat. Would you like a coffee?" Now the guest feels welcome and we can start the meeting.

1. At 9 a.m., how do you greet the guest? (As 9h, como cumprimentar?)
A "Good morning, Doctor." (Bom dia, Doutor.)
B "Good evening, Doctor." (Boa noite, Doutor.)
C "How do you spell it?" (Como se escreve?)
2. How do you offer the guest a drink? (Como oferecer uma bebida?)
A "You want a coffee?" (Voce quer um cafe?)
B "Would you like a coffee?" (Voce gostaria de um cafe?)
C "Give me a coffee." (Me de um cafe.)
3. What is a good small-talk question? (Qual e uma boa pergunta de small talk?)
A "How do you spell your surname?" (Como se escreve seu sobrenome?)
B "How was your weekend?" (Como foi seu fim de semana?)
C "What time is the meeting?" (Que horas e a reuniao?)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Greeting & Welcoming

Grammar

Como cumprimentar e receber um convidado. / How to greet and welcome a guest.

To do this / Para istoSay / Diga
Cumprimentar (pela hora)Good morning / afternoon / evening
Perguntar como estaHow are you? -- I'm fine, thank you. And you?
Receber o convidadoWelcome! It's a pleasure to meet you.
Oferecer assento / cafePlease have a seat. Would you like a coffee?
Small talkHow was your weekend?

morning = manha · afternoon = tarde · evening = noite. / "Would you like...?" = Voce gostaria de...? (oferta educada) · "It's a pleasure to meet you." = E um prazer conhece-lo.
