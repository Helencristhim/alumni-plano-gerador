Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"How are you?" — "I have fine."

✓ RIGHT

"How are you?" — "I'm fine, thank you."

✗ WRONG

"You want a coffee?"

✓ RIGHT

"Would you like a coffee?"
