Chapter 3: Reading a Form

The Code

Please + verb. Read each field.

Discover the Code

What Does the Form Ask?

Please fill in the date.

Please check the quantity.

Please sign at the bottom.

What is the weight?

InstructionMeaning
Please fill in...write in the blank space
Please check...look and confirm it is correct
Please sign...write your name (signature)
What is...?read a field: the weight, the date

A form gives instructions with Please + verb (fill in, check, sign). To read a field, ask What is the...?

Common Mistakes

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"Please fill the form."

✓ RIGHT

"Please fill in the form."

✗ WRONG

"Please do a signature here."

✓ RIGHT

"Please sign here."

✗ WRONG

"Which is the weight?"

✓ RIGHT

"What is the weight?"

Remember: fill in (with "in"), sign (one verb), and What is the...? to read a field.
