Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o que a Anna ve no documento e responda. / Read what Anna sees on the document and answer.

Anna reads the export document. There is an invoice on the desk. There are two copies of the form. The shipment is sugar. The weight is twenty tons and the quantity is correct. Anna fills in the date and puts her signature at the bottom. Then the form goes to customs.

A There is an invoice.
B There is a coffee.
C There is a menu.
2. What is the weight of the shipment?
A Two tons.
B Twenty tons.
C Ten tons.
3. Where does the form go?
A To customs.
B To the restaurant.
C To the lobby.

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- There is / There are

Grammar

Para dizer o que existe num documento, use There is (1 coisa, singular) e There are (2+ coisas, plural). / Use There is for one thing and There are for many things.

EstruturaEnglishPortugues
There is + 1 (singular)There is an invoice.Ha uma fatura.
There are + 2+ (plural)There are two forms.Ha dois formularios.
NegativoThere is no signature.Nao ha assinatura.
PerguntaIs there an invoice?Ha uma fatura?
