Lesson 7 -- Culture Curious: Asking Deep Questions

Series
Somebody Feed Phil -- any episode (Netflix)

Phil travels the world and is genuinely curious, constantly asking locals about their food, traditions, and background. A perfect model for friendly culture interviews and past-simple questions.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down three questions Phil asks a local, and rewrite each one in the past simple with "did". Then say them aloud.

Podcast
Cultures & Traditions -- "Asking About Where You Are From"

A slow, clear lesson about talking across cultures: customs, heritage, etiquette, and how to ask respectful questions about a person's background and past.

Pause after each question and repeat it aloud. Note three culture words and compare them with the questions Tuca asked Kenji in today's interview.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Past simple questions"

A clear, practical video on forming questions about the past: question word, "did", subject, and the base verb. Directly useful for interviewing people and avoiding the most common mistakes.

After watching, record a 60-second culture interview with an imaginary foreigner. Use at least four new words and four past-simple questions.
