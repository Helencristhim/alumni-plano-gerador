Chapter 3

The Question Code

Open vs. closed questions & follow-ups

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"How was your trip?"

"What do you think of the congress?"

"Where are you from?"

"I'm from Brazil. What about you?"

TypeStarts withExample
Open (long answer)How / What / Where / WhyHow was your trip?
Closed (yes / no)Do / Did / Are / IsDid you enjoy it?
Follow-upask it backWhat about you? / And you?
ConnectorsSo, ... · By the way · Actually
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"Where you are from?"

✓ RIGHT

"Where are you from?"

✗ WRONG

"What you think of the talk?"

✓ RIGHT

"What do you think of the talk?"

✗ WRONG

"How was your travel?"

✓ RIGHT

"How was your trip?"

In open questions, the question word comes first, then the verb: Where are you from? What do you think? For a journey, we say "trip", not "travel".
