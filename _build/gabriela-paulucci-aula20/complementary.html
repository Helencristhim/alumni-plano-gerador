Lesson 20 -- Eating Out -- At the Café Counter

Easy English -- "Ordering at a Café" (slow, clear)

A short, slow video showing how to order a snack and a drink at a café counter: saying what you want, answering "Anything else?" and "For here or to go?", and paying. Real English -- perfect for today's words.

Watch with English subtitles. Each time you hear a café word (sandwich, cookie, muffin, soda, counter), say it out loud.

Speak English Now -- "At the Café Counter"

A slow, clear podcast about ordering a quick snack: asking with "A sandwich, please", finishing with "That is all, thanks", and choosing "for here or to go".

Repeat each phrase out loud. Then practice: "A sandwich, please." / "That is all, thanks." / "For here, please."
