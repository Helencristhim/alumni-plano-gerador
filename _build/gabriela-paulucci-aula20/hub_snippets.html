Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Café -- a small place for snacks and drinks
"I go to a small café for a snack." (Café/Lanchonete)
Counter -- the long table where you order and pay
"I walk to the counter to order." (Balcão)
Sandwich -- bread with food inside
"A sandwich and a soda, please." (Sanduíche)
Cookie -- a small, round sweet snack
"I would love a cookie with my drink." (Biscoito/Cookie)
Muffin -- a small, soft sweet cake
"The muffin is warm and fresh." (Muffin/Bolinho)
Soda -- a sweet, cold drink with bubbles
"I would like a cold soda, please." (Refrigerante)
Snack -- a small thing you eat between meals
"I want a quick snack at the café." (Lanche)
Takeaway -- food you take out to eat somewhere else
"A sandwich for takeaway, please." (Para viagem/Para levar)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas. / Read the text and answer the questions.

Gabriela is tired after her trip, so she goes to a small café. She walks to the counter and says: "A sandwich, please." The server asks: "Anything else?" Gabriela says: "Yes, a cookie and a soda, please. That is all, thanks." The server asks: "For here or to go?" Gabriela says: "For here, please." She pays and gets her change. Then she sits down and enjoys her snack.

1. What does Gabriela order first?
A A sandwich.
B A train ticket.
C A hotel room.
2. How does she finish her order?
A Goodbye.
B That is all, thanks.
C How much is it.
3. Does she eat in the café or take it away?
A For here -- she eats in the café.
B To go -- she takes it away.
C She does not eat.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- A ..., please. / That is all, thanks.

GRAMMAR

Para pedir um lanche no balcão, use "A + lanche + please". Para terminar o pedido, diga "That is all, thanks". O atendente pergunta "Anything else?" (mais alguma coisa?) e "For here or to go?" (para comer aqui ou para levar?). / To order a snack at the counter, use "A + snack + please". To finish, say "That is all, thanks". The server asks "Anything else?" and "For here or to go?".

Function / FunçãoStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Order / PedirA + lanche + please.A sandwich, please.
Finish / TerminarThat is all, thanks.That is all, thanks.
Server asks / Atendente perguntaAnything else? / For here or to go?Anything else? -- For here or to go?

Key point / Ponto-chave: para UM lanche, use a: a sandwich, a cookie, a muffin, a soda. E sempre adicione please. / For ONE snack, use "a" (a sandwich), and always add "please".
