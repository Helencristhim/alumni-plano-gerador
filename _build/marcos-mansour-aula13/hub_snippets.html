Stage 13.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para escutar.

Menu -- cardápio (the list of food and drinks)
"Could I see the menu, please?"
Appetizer -- entrada (a small dish before the main course)
"To start, I would like the soup as an appetizer."
Main course -- prato principal (the largest dish of the meal)
"For the main course, I will have the salmon."
Dessert -- sobremesa (a sweet dish at the end of a meal)
"For dessert, I think I will have the fruit tart."
Beverage -- bebida (a drink)
"Can I get you a beverage while you look at the menu?"
Server -- garçom/garçonete (the person who takes your order)
"The server told us about the specials."
Bill -- conta (the paper that shows what you owe)
"Could we have the bill, please?"
Tip -- gorjeta (extra money for good service)
"In the US, a tip of about twenty percent is normal."
Recommend -- recomendar (to suggest a good dish)
"What do you recommend, the salmon or the chicken?"
Allergy -- alergia (a bad reaction to a certain food)
"I have a mild allergy to nuts."

Stage 13.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas.

After the congress, Marcos has dinner at a restaurant. The server brings the menu and describes the specials. Marcos orders politely. To start, he says: "I would like the tomato soup as an appetizer." For the main course: "I will have the grilled salmon." He makes a small change: "Could I get the vegetables instead of the potatoes?" He also mentions an allergy: "I have a mild allergy to nuts." For dessert: "I think I will have the fruit tart." At the end, he asks: "Could we have the bill, please?" and leaves a tip for good service.

1. Quais frases são usadas para fazer um pedido educado?
A "Give me food."
B "I would like... / I will have... / Could I get...?"
C "Bring everything."
2. Como Marcos pede uma troca no prato?
A "No potatoes."
B "Could I get the vegetables instead of the potatoes?"
C "Change it."
3. Which is the correct way to order the main course?
A "I will having the salmon."
B "I will have the salmon."
C "I would like have the salmon."

Stage 13.4: Grammar Tip -- Making Orders

GRAMMAR

Como fazer um pedido em um restaurante (explicação em inglês e português).

PhraseFollowed by / Seguido deExample
I would like...+ noun (o prato)I would like the soup.
I'll have...+ nounI'll have the salmon.
Could I get...?+ nounCould I get the vegetables?
DicaUse instead of para trocar (vegetables instead of potatoes). I'll have = I will have. Adicione please.

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada momento no restaurante.

The server is ready to take your order. You start with:
A "Give me soup."
B "To start, I would like the tomato soup."
C "Soup now."
You order the main course. You say:
A "I will have the grilled salmon."
B "I am having salmon yesterday."
C "Salmon, fast."
You want to change a side dish. You say:
A "No potatoes, vegetables."
B "Could I get the vegetables instead of the potatoes?"
C "Change my food."
You finished your meal and want to pay. You say:
A "Could we have the bill, please?"
B "Money now."
C "I pay where?"
