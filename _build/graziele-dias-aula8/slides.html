Chapter 3: Asking with Can

Can You...?

Polite requests with can

Requests

Ask with Can

"Can you help me?" · ask for help

"Can you repeat, please?" · ask to say again

"Can you speak slowly?" · ask to slow down

"Can I ask a question?" → ask permission

Can you...? = Voce pode...? · Can I...? = Posso...? · please = por favor

Discover the Pattern

Ask Politely with Can

"Can you help me?"

"Can you repeat, please?"

"Can you speak slowly?"

"Can I ask a question?"

To do thisSay
Ask for helpCan you help me?
Ask to say againCan you repeat, please?
Ask to slow downCan you speak slowly?
Ask permissionCan I ask a question?

Can + you/I + verbo base (sem "to"). · Can you help me? (NAO "Can you to help me?")
