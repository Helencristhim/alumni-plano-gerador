Aula 18 -- Pre-class

Sending a Postcard

At the post office in Paris: mail vocabulary (stamp, envelope, mailbox, address, parcel) and the short words to say who you send things to ("send it to her", "send them to him"). New vocabulary: post office, stamp, envelope, address, mailbox, abroad, by air mail, parcel, weigh.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Post office -- a place where you send letters, postcards and parcels
"I went to the post office to send my postcards." (Correio / agência dos correios)
Stamp -- a small paper you stick on mail to pay for sending it
"The stamp for Brazil is one euro eighty." (Selo)
Envelope -- a flat paper cover that you put a letter inside
"I put the letter in an envelope and wrote the address." (Envelope)
Address -- the place where someone lives, that you write on the mail
"I copied my grandmother's address on the postcard." (Endereço)
Mailbox -- a box on the street where you put letters to send them
"I put the postcard in the yellow mailbox." (Caixa de correio)
Abroad -- in or to another country, far from home
"To send a postcard abroad, you need a bigger stamp." (Para o exterior)
By air mail -- when a letter or postcard travels to another country by plane
"I sent it to Brazil by air mail." (Por via aérea)
Parcel -- a box or package that you send to someone by mail
"The clerk weighed a small parcel for my mom." (Encomenda / pacote)
Weigh -- to measure how heavy something is, often on a scale
"The clerk will weigh your parcel to find the price." (Pesar)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- it / them / her / him

GRAMMAR

Para não repetir o nome, usamos uma palavra curta depois do verbo: "it" para uma coisa, "them" para várias, "her" para uma mulher, "him" para um homem. / To avoid repeating the name, we use a short word after the verb.

Instead of / Em vez deWe say / DizemosExample / Exemplo
the postcard (uma coisa)itI'll send it.
two postcards (várias coisas)themI'll send them.
my mom (mulher)hersend it to her.
my dad (homem)himsend it to him.
my friends (pessoas)themwrite to them.

Key point / Ponto-chave: depois do verbo, use a palavra curta para não repetir o nome: it (uma coisa), them (várias coisas ou pessoas), her (mulher), him (homem). Estrutura: send + it/them + to + her/him/them.
