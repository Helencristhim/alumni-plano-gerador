"This is for my mom. I'll send it to her."

"This is for my dad. I'll send it to him."

Grammar Rule

Short words: it / them / her / him

Instead of...We sayExample
the postcard (one thing)itI'll send it.
two postcards (many things)themI'll send them.
my mom (a woman)hersend it to her.
my dad (a man)himsend it to him.
my friends (people)themwrite to them.

Key idea: after the verb, use a short word so you do not repeat the name: it = one thing, them = many things or people, her = a woman, him = a man. Pattern: send + it/them + to + her/him/them.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I'll send the postcard to my mom. I'll send it to him."
"I'll send it to my mom. I'll send it to her."

Use it for the thing and her for a woman, him for a man.
