Lesson 18 -- Sending a Postcard

Series
Emily in Paris -- the letter and message scenes

Watch a scene where a character writes a message or sends mail. Notice how people use short words like "it", "them", "her" and "him" instead of repeating the name.

Tip: Write down three sentences you hear with "it" or "them", and say who each one is about.

Podcast
Culips ESL -- "At the Post Office"

A short, clear episode about sending mail in English: how to ask for a stamp, talk about sending things abroad by air mail, and use "send it to her / him".

Tip: Record yourself sending three things: use "it" once, "them" once, and "her" or "him" once.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "object pronouns: it, them, her, him"

A focused lesson on object pronouns: when to use "it" for one thing, "them" for many, "her" for a woman and "him" for a man. It reinforces today's grammar.

Tip: After watching, write six sentences about sending mail, one for each: it, them, her, him.
