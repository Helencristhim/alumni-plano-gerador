Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Auction -- a public sale where pieces go to the person who offers the most
"The painting goes to auction next Thursday in New York." (Leilão)
Bid -- an offer of a specific price to buy something at an auction
"I placed a bid of two million on the sculpture." (Lance)
Appraisal -- a professional estimate of how much a piece is worth
"Always get an independent appraisal before you buy." (Avaliação)
Provenance -- the record of ownership and origin of a work of art
"The provenance proves the piece is genuine." (Procedência)
Masterpiece -- an artist's finest and most important work
"This canvas is considered her masterpiece." (Obra-prima)
Soar -- to rise very quickly to a high level
"Prices for her work have soared this year." (Disparar)
Boom -- a time of strong, fast growth in a market
"The contemporary art market is in a boom." (Auge)
Sought-after -- wanted by many people; in high demand
"She is the most sought-after artist of the decade." (Cobiçado)
Emerging -- new and starting to become known and successful
"Emerging artists are the fastest-growing segment." (Emergente)
Blue-chip -- a safe, established name with reliably high value
"Blue-chip names hold their value in any market." (Ativo de primeira linha)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Superlatives & Trend Language

GRAMMAR

Use superlativos para RANQUEAR valor (the most ___, the ___-est) e trend language para descrever MOVIMENTO (prices are rising = agora; values have soared = mudança até agora). / Use superlatives to RANK value (the most ___, the ___-est) and trend language to describe MOVEMENT (prices are rising = now; values have soared = change up to now).

FunctionPatternExample
Short superlativethe + adjective + -est"the highest price", "the fastest-growing segment"
Long superlativethe most + adjective"the most valuable lot", "the most sought-after artist"
Trend nowis/are + verb-ing"prices are rising", "the market is booming"
Trend up to nowhas/have + past participle"demand has grown", "values have soared"

Key point / Ponto-chave: use -est para adjetivos curtos (high, fast, low) e the most para os longos (valuable, expensive, sought-after). Nunca os dois juntos (NUNCA "the most expensivest"). Irregular: good → the best, bad → the worst. Para movimento agora use present continuous (are rising); para a mudança até agora use present perfect (have soared).
