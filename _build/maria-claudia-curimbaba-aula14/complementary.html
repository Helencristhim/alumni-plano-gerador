Aula 14 -- The Art Market

Tema da aula: reading the art market -- trends, artists and value (superlatives: the most sought-after artist, the highest price; trend language: prices are rising, values have soared). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Documentary
"The Price of Everything" (HBO documentary)

A sharp look inside the contemporary art market -- auctions, blue-chip names, emerging artists and soaring values. Listen for how dealers and collectors talk about what is most sought-after and how prices rise.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote três superlativos (the most ___, the ___-est) e duas frases de trend (prices are rising, values have soared).

Podcast
NPR -- "Planet Money" / "The Art Market" episodes

Clear, well-produced episodes on how art is valued, auctioned, and traded. Great input to review appraisal, provenance, bid, and how the market booms -- exactly the vocabulary of this lesson.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote cada palavra usada para falar de valor e de leilão (auction, bid, appraisal, provenance).

YouTube
Sotheby's & Christie's -- "Inside the Auction Room"

Official channel videos showing real evening sales: how lots are presented, how bids climb, and how an auctioneer describes the most valuable masterpiece of the night. Watch the language of value and trends in action.

Dica: pause em cada lote e descreva-o em voz alta com um superlativo e uma frase de trend (por exemplo, "This is the most sought-after lot, and prices are rising").
