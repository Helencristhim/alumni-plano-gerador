Lesson 15 -- Milestone Review: From Maceio to Seoul

TED Talk
Your Body Language May Shape Who You Are — Amy Cuddy (TED)

This talk is for the moment before you walk into the reception in Seoul. Amy Cuddy shows that how you stand and breathe can change how confident you feel — before you say a single word. It is the perfect message for a leader who speaks English with courage, even when the interpreter is not there.

Tip: turn on the English subtitles. Do not try to understand every word — listen for the main idea and for one sentence you can repeat. Then try her "power pose" for two minutes before your next call.

Business English Pod — BEP 27: Meetings, Giving Opinions in English

This is your round table, in a lesson. It teaches the exact phrases you used today: how to give your opinion ("in my opinion...", "I think...") and how to agree and disagree politely in a meeting. Short and clear — made for a leader who has to speak up with respect.

Tip: listen ONCE on your way to the university, with no pauses. Then listen again and write down every phrase for giving an opinion. Practice one out loud: "In my opinion, we should..."
