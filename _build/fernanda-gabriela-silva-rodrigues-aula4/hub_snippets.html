Vocabulary

"Sucafina is located in Switzerland." (Localizada na Suica.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

At the conference desk, the host asks for my full name. I spell my last name: R-O-D-R-I-G-U-E-S. Then I say my position. I work for the trading department. Sucafina is a global company. Our headquarters is located in Switzerland, and we have a branch in Brazil.

1. Why do we say "a global company" but "an office"? (Por que "a" e "an"?)
A "an" before a vowel sound (a, e, i, o, u). (an antes de som de vogal.)
B "a" is for plural. (a e plural.)
C They are the same. (Sao iguais.)
2. What is "headquarters"? (O que e headquarters?)
A A small office in another city. (Filial pequena.)
B The main office of the company. (Escritorio principal.)
C A coffee shipment. (Um carregamento.)
3. "I spell my last name." What do you do? (O que voce faz?)
A Say the letters one by one. (Digo as letras uma a uma.)
B Write a long email. (Escrevo um email.)
C Count the numbers. (Conto os numeros.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- A / An & Spelling

Grammar

Quando usar a / an e como soletrar. / When to use a/an and how to spell.

Use / UsoPattern / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Consonant sound / Som de consoantea + worda company, a branch
Vowel sound / Som de vogalan + wordan office, an email
Spelling / Soletrarsay each letter"R-O-D-R-I-G-U-E-S"
Key idea / Ideia-chavea antes de consoante; an antes de vogal (a, e, i, o, u). / a = consonant, an = vowel.

Etapa 4: Quiz Situacional

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao profissional.

A host asks you to spell your last name. You say: (Soletre seu sobrenome.)
A "My last name Rodrigues fast."
B "My last name is spelled R-O-D-R-I-G-U-E-S."
C "Rodrigues, the name is."
You describe the main office. You say: (Voce descreve a sede.)
A "Our headquarters in Switzerland are."
B "Our headquarters is located in Switzerland."
C "We headquarters Switzerland."
You choose a / an correctly. You say: (Escolha a ou an.)
A "Sucafina is an global company."
B "Sucafina is a global company with an office in Brazil."
C "Sucafina is a office company."
