Chapter 4: The Code

was testing · crashed

Past continuous and past simple, together

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I was testing the checkout when the page crashed."

"She was adding items when the total froze."

"While the app was loading, the error appeared."

"The page crashed when I clicked Pay now."

UseFormExample
Action in progresswas / were + -ingI was testing the checkout.
Sudden, completed eventpast simpleThe page crashed.
Background + interruptionpast continuous + when + past simpleI was testing when it crashed.
Two background actionswhile + past continuous (While the app was loading, I was watching the log.)

Past continuous = was/were + -ing for the longer action in progress. Past simple for the short, completed event that interrupts it.

Language Focus

Past Simple or Past Continuous?

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"I was test the checkout when it crashed."

✓ RIGHT

"I was testing the checkout when it crashed."

✗ WRONG

"The page was crashing when I clicked Pay now."

✓ RIGHT

"The page crashed when I clicked Pay now."

Use was/were + the -ing form for the action in progress. Use the past simple for a short, sudden event, not the past continuous.

Dialogue

Lucas Asks About the Bug

L
Hi Ana. I saw your message. What happened with the checkout?
A
I was testing the payment page when it crashed. I clicked Pay now, and the whole screen went blank.
L
Okay. What were you doing right before the crash?
A
I was adding three items to the cart, and the total was updating fine. The problem started at Pay now.
L
Got it. Is it intermittent, or does it happen every time?
A
It happens every time. It is not intermittent, so it is easy to reproduce.
L
Perfect. Can you send the error log and a screenshot?
A
I already attached both to the report. The severity is critical, because no one can pay.
