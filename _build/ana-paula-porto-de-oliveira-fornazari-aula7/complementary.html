City Life vs Countryside Living -- Which Is Better? (Speak English With Nora)

A calm, slow-English podcast that compares living in the city and living in the countryside. The speaker weighs the advantages and drawbacks of each option in clear, natural language. Connection to Lesson 7: listen for comparatives and superlatives -- "more affordable", "quieter", "the best place to live".

Tip: pause after each point and repeat it as a comparison, using "more ... than" or "the most ...".

Is It Better to Live in the City or Countryside? -- English on the Go! (Beginner)

A short, easy listening episode that compares the city and the countryside as two places to live. It is spoken slowly and repeats the key comparisons, so it is a friendly way to hear today's grammar in action. Connection to Lesson 7: every point is a comparison of two options -- exactly what you practiced.

Tip: after listening, choose two places you know and compare them out loud in three sentences.

Grammar Video
Comparatives and Superlatives -- 6 Minute Grammar (BBC Learning English)

A short, clear video on the exact grammar from today: how to build comparatives (cheaper, more modern) and superlatives (the cheapest, the most modern), including the irregular forms good/better/best. Connection to Lesson 7: this is the difference between "cheaper than" and "the cheapest".

Tip: pause after each rule and make one true sentence comparing two things you own or two places you know.
