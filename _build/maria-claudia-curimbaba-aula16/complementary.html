Aula 16 -- Contracts & Agreements -- Legal English

Tema da aula: read and discuss a contract like a lawyer -- obligations with shall (each party shall deliver the goods), requirements with must (the warranty must cover twelve months), permissions with may (either party may terminate), prohibitions with shall not / must not / may not, and the passive voice for every term (the goods shall be delivered; confidential information must not be disclosed). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.
