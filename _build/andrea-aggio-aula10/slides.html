Chapter 4: The Second Conditional

The Second Conditional

if + past simple · would / could + verb

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"If I were you, I would book the low season."

"If you had more time, you could visit both cities."

"If the budget allowed it, I would upgrade the rooms."

Are these situations real or imaginary? What tense follows "if", and what follows "would"?

FormUseExample
If + past simpleAn unreal or hypothetical conditionIf I were you,
would / could + verbThe imagined result or adviceI would recommend it.
Always 'were'Use 'were' for I / he / she in this structureIf she were here (not 'was')

Second conditional = an imaginary situation. If + past simple, ... would/could + verb. Use 'were' for all persons.

Language Focus

Complete the Advice

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"If I was you, I will recommend the inland hotel."

✓ RIGHT

If I were you, I would recommend the inland hotel.

✗ WRONG

"If you had more time, you can visit both cities."

✓ RIGHT

If you had more time, you could visit both cities.

In the second conditional, use the past simple after 'if' (and 'were' for all persons), and 'would' or 'could' -- never 'will' or 'can' -- in the result.

Dialogue

The Tricky Request

S
Hi Andrea. My group wants a beach resort in August, but the budget is really tight. What would you do?
A
If I were you, I would look at the low season instead. The same resort would cost much less.
S
But the group insists on August. Is there any alternative?
A
If you booked a resort slightly inland, you would get the beach nearby and a better rate. It is a good trade-off.
S
Interesting. And what if they want everything included?
A
If they chose a tailor-made package, I could add transfers and dinners. It would give them peace of mind.
S
That sounds worthwhile. Honestly, I am having second thoughts about the beach idea now.
A
If I were in your position, I would recommend the inland option. It is flexible, and your group would still be happy.
Did You Catch It?

About Stefan

1. What does Stefan's group want?
A beach resort in August, on a tight budget.
2. What alternative does Andrea suggest for the same budget?
A resort slightly inland, near the beach but at a better rate.
3. What is Stefan having second thoughts about?
The beach idea / booking a beach resort in August.
