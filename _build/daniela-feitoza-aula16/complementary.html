Aula 16 — Sequencing the Past (Past Perfect)

Conteúdos para reforçar como ordenar uma história — past perfect para a ação mais antiga, sequenciadores para a ordem. Marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
The Crowded Room or any whodunit — a scene that reconstructs a timeline

Investigators piece together what had happened before a key moment. Listen for the past perfect ("she had already left") and sequencers (first, then, after that) — the exact tools from Lesson 16.

Dica: anote uma frase com "had + past participle" e diga qual ação aconteceu primeiro.

Podcast
The Downtime Project — postmortem episodes with full timelines

Engineers reconstruct exactly what had happened before each step of an incident. A great model of the past perfect plus sequencers, used to narrate a real postmortem in order.

Dica: pause e repita cada frase com "by the time..." ou "had already..." que você ouvir.

YouTube
"Past Perfect explained — had + past participle for the earlier action" (English lesson)

A short, clear video on using the past perfect for the earlier of two past events, plus sequencers to order a story. Maps directly onto Daniela’s postmortem with Marcus in Lesson 16.

Dica: depois do vídeo, narre o timeline de um incidente seu em 4 frases, usando o past perfect.
