Aula 13 -- Making Proposals

Tema da aula: pitching an idea to the partners -- rationale, scope, a low-risk pilot and a clear value proposition -- with proposing frames (I would like to suggest that..., What if we...?, I propose that we...) and the passive voice for the outcome (the proposal was approved). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
"Shark Tank" / "Dragons' Den" -- the pitch scenes

Founders pitch ideas to investors in minutes: rationale first, then scope, numbers and a value proposition, while the panel pushes back. Exactly the structure you use with the board. Listen for how they open ("What if you...", "I would suggest...") and how deals "are done" and proposals "are accepted".

Dica: assista a um pitch com legenda em inglês e anote três frames de proposta e duas frases na voz passiva sobre o resultado (a deal was made, the offer was accepted).
