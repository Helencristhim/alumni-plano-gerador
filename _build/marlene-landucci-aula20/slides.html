The Map

Four Ways to Speak

LessonPatternExample
SightseeingI want to + verbI want to visit the museum.
ShoppingI would like to + verbI would like to try on this dress.
HealthMy + part + hurtsMy stomach hurts.
EmergencyVerb + the/an...Call an ambulance!

Key idea: you now have four ways to speak on a trip -- plan (I want to), ask politely (I would like to), say what hurts (My ... hurts), and give an urgent order (Call...!).

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I am headache."
"I have a headache."

Remember the patterns: "I have a headache", "I want to visit", "Call an ambulance".
