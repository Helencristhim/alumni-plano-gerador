Lesson 20 -- My Paris Trip So Far

Series
Emily in Paris -- the travel moments

Watch the scenes where characters talk about what they have done and seen in Paris. Notice the present perfect: "I have visited...", "Have you ever...?".

Tip: Write down three sentences you hear with "have" + verb, then say one about your own week.

Podcast
Culips ESL -- "Talking About Experiences"

A short, clear episode about the present perfect: how to use "already", "yet", "just" and "ever" to talk about what you have done in your life and on a trip.

Tip: Record yourself saying three things you have already done today and one thing you have not done yet.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Present Perfect: already, yet, just"

A focused lesson on the present perfect with "already", "yet" and "just", plus "ever" for life experiences. It reinforces today's grammar.

Tip: After watching, write six sentences about your trip or week: two with "already", two with "yet", two with "just".
