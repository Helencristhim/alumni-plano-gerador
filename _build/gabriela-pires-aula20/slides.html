Grammar Rule

Talking with have + verb

UseExample
Done (positive)I have already visited the museum.
Not done (yet)I have not seen the tower yet.
A moment ago (just)I have just written a postcard.
Ask (ever)Have you ever tried a croissant?
Formhave / has + visited, seen, written, been, done

Key idea: use have + verb to talk about your experiences until now. Add already (done), yet (not done), just (a moment ago) and ever (in your life, in questions).

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I have visit the museum. I have not see the tower."
"I have visited the museum. I have not seen the tower yet."

After have, use the third form: visited, seen, gone, written, been.
