Responding to Questions -- Q&A Skills

Answer a question clearly. Start your answer ("Let me explain..."), give a reason ("The reason is..."), give an example ("For example..."), and check understanding ("Does that answer your question?"). New vocabulary: explain, reason, repeat, clarify, suppose, certainly, exactly, instance.

Explain -- to make something clear

Reason -- why something is true
"The reason is the new engine." (Motivo / Razão)
Repeat -- to say something again
"Let me repeat the main points." (Repetir)
Clarify -- to make something easier to understand
"Let me clarify the discount." (Esclarecer)
Suppose -- to think something is probably true
"I suppose you want a clear answer." (Supor)
Certainly -- yes, of course (sure)
"Certainly, I can explain that." (Certamente)
Exactly -- perfectly correct
"Yes, that is exactly right." (Exatamente)
Instance -- an example
"For instance, you save on fuel." (Exemplo / Caso)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Robert had a question about the price. Karina said, "Let me explain. The reason is the new engine. It is faster and safer. For instance, you save money on fuel." Robert wanted an exact number, so she clarified the discount: "It is five percent this month." She also said, "Certainly, I can repeat the main terms." At the end, Karina asked, "Does that answer your question?" Robert said, "Yes, that is exactly what I wanted to know."

1. How does Karina start her answer?
A "Let me explain. The reason is the new engine."
B "What about a bigger discount?"
C "Please have a seat."
2. What example does Karina give?
A The aircraft is older.
B You save money on fuel.
C The office is closed.
3. What does Karina ask at the end?
A "Does that answer your question?"
B "Would you like a coffee?"
C "What is your name?"

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Answering Questions Clearly

GRAMMAR

Como responder a uma pergunta com clareza. / How to answer a question clearly.

Phrase / FraseMeaning / SentidoExample / Exemplo
Let me explain...Comecar a resposta / Start your answerLet me explain the price.
The reason is...Dar o motivo / Give the reasonThe reason is the new engine.
For example / For instance...Dar um exemplo / Give an exampleFor example, you save on fuel.
Does that answer your question?Checar se entendeu / Check understandingDoes that answer your question?

Key point / Ponto-chave: comece a resposta com Let me explain, dê o motivo com The reason is (não "The reason because"), dê um exemplo com For example / For instance, e cheque a compreensão com Does that answer your question?
