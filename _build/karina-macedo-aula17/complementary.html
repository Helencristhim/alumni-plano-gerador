Lesson 17 -- Responding to Questions: Q&A Skills

Series
Shark Tank (US) -- any Q&A scene with investors

After a pitch, investors ask hard questions and the owner answers them. You hear real answers that start with "Let me explain..." and give reasons and examples.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down one answer that starts with "Let me explain..." and one reason ("The reason is...") that you hear.

Podcast
Speak English Now -- "Answering Questions Clearly"

A slow, clear podcast about Q&A skills: how to start an answer, give a reason, give an example, and check that the other person understands.

Repeat each phrase out loud. Then record a short answer that uses "The reason is..." and "For example...".

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "How to answer questions in English"

A short, practical lesson on today's language: answering with "Let me explain...", giving reasons with "The reason is...", giving examples with "For example...", and checking with "Does that answer your question?".

After watching, write five sentences to answer a question: start the answer, give the reason, give an example, clarify a detail, and check understanding. Then read them aloud.
