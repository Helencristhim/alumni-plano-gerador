Chapter 3 -- Answer Clearly

Grammar Discovery

Let me explain -- The reason is

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

How do we answer a question clearly?

"Let me explain the price."

"The reason is the new engine."

"For example, it is faster."

"Does that answer your question?"

Grammar Rule

Answering Clearly

PhraseWhen we use it
Let me explain...Start your answer
The reason is...Give the reason
For example / For instance...Give an example
Does that answer your question?Check the person understands

Key idea: to answer clearly, start with Let me explain, give the reason with The reason is, add an example with For example, and check with Does that answer your question?

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I explain you the price. The reason because the engine."
"Let me explain the price. The reason is the engine."

Say Let me explain (not "I explain you"). Say The reason is (not "The reason because").
