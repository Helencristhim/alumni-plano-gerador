ToolWhen to useExample
Can / Could you...?a polite requestCould you repeat that?
...-er than / more ... thancompare two thingsThis quarter is better than the last.
somepositive sentencesI would like some water.
anyquestions & negativesIs there any news?

Three tools you already use every day: ask politely with Could you, compare with than, and pick some or any.

Wrong vs Right

"Could repeat that, please?"

"Could you repeat that, please?"

"This is more better."

Dialogue

Confirming the Trip

R
Good morning. I have a reservation for Monday, and I want to confirm the details.
C
Of course, Mr. Gasparelli. How many nights are you staying with us?
R
Three nights. Could you speak more slowly, please? Is there any breakfast included?
C
Yes, breakfast is free for all guests. I will also send the confirmation to your email as an attachment.
R
Thank you. And how much is the room per night?
C
It is 180 dollars per night. Have a safe flight, Mr. Gasparelli!
Comprehension

About the Call

1. What does the clerk say about breakfast?
It is free for all guests.
2. How will the clerk send the confirmation?
To his email, as an attachment.
3. How much is the room per night?
It is 180 dollars per night.
