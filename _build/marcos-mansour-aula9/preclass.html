Trends -- AI in Valuation

Describing trends and changes with present continuous. Key words: trend, emerging, automate, algorithm, data-driven, efficiency, adopt, replace, insight, forecast. Structure: present continuous for change in progress (Technology is changing... / More firms are adopting...).

Stage 9.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para escutar.

Trend -- tendência (a general direction of change over time)
"Automation is the biggest trend in our industry."
Emerging -- emergente (new and starting to grow or appear)
"Artificial intelligence is an emerging technology in valuation."
Automate -- automatizar (to make a task run by machine, not by hand)
"We are starting to automate the slow tasks."
Algorithm -- algoritmo (a set of rules a computer follows to solve a problem)
"The algorithm compares thousands of properties in seconds."
Data-driven -- baseado em dados (guided by data, not by opinion)
"The market is becoming more data-driven every year."
Efficiency -- eficiência (doing a job well with less time or effort)
"These tools are improving our efficiency."
Adopt -- adotar (to start using something new)
"More firms are adopting new software."
Replace -- substituir (to put something new in place of the old)
"AI is not replacing the appraiser; it is supporting us."
Insight -- percepção, análise (a clear, useful understanding of something)
"The data gives us new insight into the market."
Forecast -- previsão, prever (to predict a future value or result)
"The software helps us forecast values more quickly."

Stage 9.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas.

Marcos describes the trends in his field. "Technology is changing the way we work," he says. "Right now, more firms are adopting artificial intelligence. The tools are automating the slow tasks, so our teams are spending less time on spreadsheets. The market is becoming more data-driven, and clients are asking for faster reports. AI is not replacing the appraiser — it is supporting us. The industry is moving fast, and the firms that are adapting now are gaining an advantage." He uses the present continuous because these changes are happening now, in progress.

1. Por que Marcos usa o present continuous (is/are + -ing)?
A Para falar de hábitos fixos e rotinas.
B Para descrever mudanças e tendências que estão acontecendo agora.
C Para falar do passado.
2. Segundo Marcos, a IA está substituindo o avaliador?
A Sim, está substituindo todos os avaliadores.
B Não — ela está apoiando o avaliador, não substituindo.
C O texto não fala sobre isso.
3. Which sentence is correct present continuous?
A "More firms adopting AI."
B "More firms are adopting AI."
C "More firms is adopt AI."

Stage 9.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Continuous for Trends

GRAMMAR

Como descrever mudanças em progresso (explicação em inglês e português).

Form / FormaStructureExample
Affirmative / Afirmativais / are + verb-ingTechnology is changing our work.
Negative / Negativais / are + not + verb-ingAI is not replacing us.
Question / PerguntaIs / Are + subject + verb-ing?Are firms adopting AI?
When / Quando usarPara mudanças e tendências em progresso agora (use sinais como right now, these days, currently). Não para rotinas fixas (essas usam present simple).
