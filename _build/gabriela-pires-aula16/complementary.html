Lesson 16 -- Asking About the City

Series
Emily in Paris -- the sightseeing scenes

Watch the scenes where Emily explores Paris and visits the landmarks. Notice how people ask for information and talk about the tour, the view, and the places nearby.

Tip: Write down five tour words you hear and one question that starts with "Can you tell me".

Podcast
Culips ESL -- "Asking for Information"

A short, clear episode about asking for information politely in English: how to start with "Can you tell me", how to ask if something exists with "Is there", and how to talk about a tour.

Tip: Record yourself asking three polite questions on a city tour: use "Can you tell me" twice and "Is there" once.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Asking for information"

A focused lesson on getting information politely: how to use "Can you tell me..." with where, what time and how long, and how to ask "Is there...?" It reinforces today's grammar.

Tip: After watching, write six questions: four with "Can you tell me" and two with "Is there".
