Aula 14 -- On the Phone

Tema da aula: phone calls -- starting and ending a call and taking a message (Could I speak to Karen? / Can I take a message? / Could you tell her that Percival called?). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"Telephone English -- taking and leaving a message" -- business English video

Watch a short, clear video about handling a phone call: asking for someone, offering to take a message, and passing it on. Listen for "Could I speak to...", "Can I take a message?" and "Could you tell her that...".

Dica: anote duas frases para oferecer anotar um recado e uma para passar a informação adiante.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "Telephone phrases"

Short lessons and quizzes to practise polite phone language: speak to, hold on, call back, leave a message. Perfect to feel confident on a real work call.

Dica: escreva três frases -- pedir a pessoa, oferecer anotar e passar o recado. Confirme que usa "speak to" e "tell her that".

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about communication or working remotely

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen for how people talk on the phone, leave a message, and arrange a call back.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote uma expressão nova para usar numa ligação.
