Chapter 3 -- The Code

Grammar Discovery

Asking in a store: how much and how many

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

When do we use how much and how many?

"How much is the shirt?"

"How much money do you have?"

"How many shirts do you want?"

"How many bags do you need?"

Grammar Rule

How Much and How Many

QuestionUseExample
How muchprice and things you cannot count (money)How much is the shirt?
How manythings you can count, in the pluralHow many bags do you want?
plural nounafter how many, the noun is pluralHow many shirts do you need?

Key idea: use how much for the price and for things you cannot count, and how many for things you can count, like shirts and bags.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"How much shirts do you want?"
"How many shirts do you want?"

For things we count, like shirts and bags, we use how many, never "how much".
