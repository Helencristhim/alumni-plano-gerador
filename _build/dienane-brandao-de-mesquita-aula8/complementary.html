Lesson 8 -- Shopping Basics

Series
Extra English -- Episode 5: "Bridget and Annie Go Shopping"

A simple comedy made for English learners. In this episode, the characters go to stores, look at prices, and buy clothes in slow, clear English. Perfect for A1 listening about shopping.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down every shopping word you hear (store, price, money). Then ask out loud: "How much is it?" and "How many do you have?".

Podcast
Coffee Break English -- "Shopping and Prices"

A short, friendly lesson on shopping: how to ask "How much is it?" and "How many do you have?", and how to talk about cheap and expensive things. The hosts explain everything slowly, with many examples you can repeat.

Pause after each example and repeat it out loud. Then describe one thing you want to buy and ask its price in three sentences.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "How Much and How Many"

A clear video that teaches shopping vocabulary (store, price, cheap, expensive) and the questions how much and how many, with pictures and simple sentences.

After watching, record a 60-second shopping scene: ask for a price, ask how many they have, and pay. Use at least three new words and one how much / how many question.
