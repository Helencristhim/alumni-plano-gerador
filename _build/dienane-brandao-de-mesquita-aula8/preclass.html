Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Dienane goes to a store near her house. She looks at a nice shirt and asks: "How much is the shirt?" The clerk says the price is twenty reais. It is cheap! Then she asks: "How many bags do you have?" The clerk has many bags. Dienane pays with her money, and the clerk gives her a receipt.

1. How do we ask about a price?
A "How many is the shirt?"
B "How much is the shirt?"
C "How price the shirt?"
2. How do we count things, like bags?
A "How much bags do you have?"
B "How many bags do you have?"
C "How many bag do you have?"
3. Is the shirt cheap or expensive?
A It is cheap, only twenty reais.
B It is very expensive.
C It has no price.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- How Much and How Many

GRAMMAR

Usado para perguntar sobre preço e quantidade. / Used to ask about price and quantity.

Question / PerguntaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
how muchpreço e coisas que não contamos (money)How much is the shirt?
how manycoisas que contamos, no pluralHow many bags do you want?
plural nounapós how many, o substantivo fica no pluralHow many shirts?

Key point / Ponto-chave: use how much para preço e coisas que não se contam, e how many para coisas que se contam, como shirts e bags.
