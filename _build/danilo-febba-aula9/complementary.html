Lesson 9 -- Skills & Abilities

Series
Silicon Valley (HBO) -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Minimum Viable Product"

A tech team builds a product. The engineers talk about what they can and can't do: "I can code", "I can build it", "I can't design that". Connection to Lesson 9: a real work setting full of can / can't for ability, close to your field.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down 5 sentences with "can" and 2 with "can't" that you hear.

Podcast
BBC Learning English -- "6 Minute English: The skills you need for the future"

A short, clear episode at A2 speed. The hosts talk about job skills and abilities people can and can't learn. Connection to Lesson 9: it reinforces talking about skills with can / can't in natural speech.

Tip: listen twice. The second time, repeat any sentence with "can" or "can't" out loud.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Can and can't for ability"

A short, practical lesson on can and can't for ability: positive, negative, questions, and short answers (Yes, I can / No, I can't), including the third person (she can speak). Connection to Lesson 9: exactly the grammar you studied today, at your level.

Tip: pause and say each example aloud. Then make your own version about your work 3 times.
