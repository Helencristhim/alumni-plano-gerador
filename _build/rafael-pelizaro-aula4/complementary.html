Shark Tank (ABC) -- any episode of a pitch and negotiation

Founders pitch a proposal and then negotiate terms live with the investors. Listen for how each side asks for a discount, makes a counteroffer, and trades one term for another to reach an agreement.

Watch one negotiation with English subtitles. Write down three offers, and rewrite each one politely with would ("Would you take 10% for a longer commitment?").

A focused lesson on using would to sound polite and soft: "Would you consider...?", "We would accept...". Directly reinforces today's grammar on making proposals and counteroffers.
