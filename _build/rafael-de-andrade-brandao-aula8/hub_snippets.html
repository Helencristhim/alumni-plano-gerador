Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Need -- a thing that is necessary
"Tell me about your main need." (Necessidade)
Challenge -- a difficult problem to solve
"What is your biggest challenge this year?" (Desafio)
Goal -- something you want to achieve
"Our main goal is to grow our sales." (Objetivo / meta)
Budget -- the money available to spend
"What is your budget for this project?" (Orçamento)
Priority -- the most important thing first
"Your priority is faster delivery." (Prioridade)
Concern -- a worry or a problem
"Do you have any concerns about the price?" (Preocupação)
Clarify -- to make something clear
"Could you clarify that point for me?" (Esclarecer)
Expectation -- what you hope will happen
"Tell me about your expectations for the service." (Expectativa)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

In the meeting, Rafael wants to understand Diana's needs. He asks open questions: "What is your main goal?" and "What is your biggest challenge?" Diana says her goal is faster delivery, and her main challenge is the busy season. Rafael also asks: "What is your budget?" Then he uses active listening to confirm: "So your top priority is speed. Is that correct?" Diana feels he understands her very well.

B "Goal?"

B "Priority now."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Open Wh- Questions

GRAMMAR

Usado para descobrir as necessidades de um cliente. / Used to discover a client's needs.

Form / FormaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
What/Why + is/are + substantivoPergunta aberta / An open questionWhat is your main goal?
What/Why + do/does + sujeito + verboPergunta aberta com verbo / Open question with a verbWhat do you need from us?
How many + substantivo + arePerguntar quantidade / Ask about quantityHow many people are on your team?
Key point / Ponto-chaveA pergunta aberta usa Wh- word + auxiliar (is/are/do/does) + sujeito + verbo. O auxiliar vem ANTES do sujeito: "Why is delivery slow?", não "Why delivery is slow?".
