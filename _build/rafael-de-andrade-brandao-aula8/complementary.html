Lesson 8 -- Understanding Client Needs

Series
The Office (US) -- Season 2, Episode 19: "Michael's Birthday"

Watch how the sales team asks clients about their goals, challenges, and budgets before they offer a deal. Notice the open Wh- questions: "What do you need?", "What is your main goal?"

Write down every Wh- question you hear. Pause and repeat each one, then ask it to an imaginary client.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- Episode: "Needs Analysis and Active Listening"

Practical tips for a discovery meeting: how to ask open questions about a client's goal, challenge, budget, and priority, and how to confirm with active listening. Listen for the new words: need, challenge, goal, budget, priority, and concern.

Note 3 open Wh- questions the host asks. Practice each one out loud with What, Why, or How many.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Wh- Questions: What, Why, When, Where, How"

A short, clear lesson on open Wh- questions in English: how to form them with the auxiliary before the subject, and how to use them to get long, useful answers. Perfect for a client needs meeting.

After watching, record yourself asking five open questions a real client could answer. Use at least three new words from this lesson.
