Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Agenda -- the list of topics for a meeting
"We have three topics on the agenda." (Pauta)
Topic -- one subject you talk about
"Let's start with the first topic." (Tópico)
Opinion -- what you think about something
"In my opinion, this topic is urgent." (Opinião)
Suggest -- to offer an idea for others to consider
"I suggest we start with the budget." (Sugerir)
Agree -- to have the same opinion as someone
"I agree with your idea." (Concordar)
Disagree -- to have a different opinion
"I disagree, but I see your point." (Discordar)
Clarify -- to make something clearer or easier to understand
"Could you clarify the start time?" (Esclarecer)
Decision -- a choice the group makes together
"At the end we make a decision." (Decisão)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Opinions & Suggestions

GRAMMAR

Para conduzir uma reunião você precisa de três funções: PEDIR uma opinião, DAR a sua opinião e fazer uma SUGESTÃO. / To run a meeting you need three functions: to ASK for an opinion, to GIVE your opinion, and to make a SUGGESTION.

Função / FunctionPatternExample
Pedir opinião / AskWhat do you think (about ...)? / Do you agree?What do you think about the plan?
Dar opinião / GiveI think (that) ... / In my opinion, ...I think we should start now.
Sugerir / SuggestLet's + verb / How about + verb-ing?Let's start. / How about starting now?
Concordar / AgreeI agree (with you). / I disagree.I agree with you.

Key point / Ponto-chave: use What do you think? para pedir, I think / In my opinion para dar sua opinião, e Let's ou How about + -ing para sugerir. Dizemos I agree (nunca "I am agree"). / Use What do you think? to ask, I think / In my opinion to give your view, and Let's / How about + -ing to suggest. Say I agree, never "I am agree".

Aula 11 -- Opening the Meeting

Tema da aula: meeting skills -- open a meeting, set the agenda, ask for opinions and make suggestions (What do you think? / I think / Let's / I agree). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.
