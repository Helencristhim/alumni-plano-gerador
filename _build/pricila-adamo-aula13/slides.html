Chapter 3: Inviting & Suggesting

Ways to Invite

Invite · suggest · accept or decline

Discover the Code

Listen and Find the Pattern

"Would you like to join us for lunch?"

"How about meeting at ten?"

"Why don't we visit the museum together?"

"That sounds great, but I'm afraid I can't make it."

FunctionPhraseExample
InviteWould you like to + base verbWould you like to join us?
SuggestHow about + verb-ingHow about meeting at ten?
SuggestWhy don't we + base verbWhy don't we walk there?
AcceptSounds good / I'd love toSounds good -- count me in!
Decline (soft)I'm afraid I can't make itI'm afraid I can't make it today.

Use to + base verb after "Would you like", and the -ing form after "How about". Accept warmly or decline softly with "I'm afraid..."

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"How about to meet at ten?"

✓ RIGHT

"How about meeting at ten?"

✗ WRONG

"No. I don't want."

✓ RIGHT

"I'm afraid I can't make it, but thank you."

Chapter 4: A Day Out

Let's Plan a Day Together

Pricila and a traveler arrange to meet

Dialogue

A Traveler Invites Pricila

M
Hi Pricila! A few of us are going to the old harbor tomorrow. Would you like to join us?
P
I'd love to! That sounds wonderful. What time are you planning to meet up?
M
How about meeting at the hotel lobby at ten? Does that work for you?
P
Could we reschedule for half past ten? I'd like a slow breakfast first.
M
Of course, half past ten works for everyone. Why don't we have lunch by the water after the walk?
P
Sounds good -- count me in! I can't wait. See you tomorrow!
Listening

A Voice Message

Listen first. Sound first, no text.

0:000:00
1. Where does Mark want to meet Pricila?
At the hotel lobby, around ten in the morning.
2. What are the three parts of the plan?
Walk to the old harbor, have lunch by the water, and visit the little museum.
3. What does Mark say if Pricila wants to start later?
Nothing is fixed yet, so she can just let him know.
Comprehension

About the Plan

1. What does Mark invite Pricila to do?
To join the group at the old harbor tomorrow.
2. Why does Pricila ask to reschedule?
She would like a slow breakfast first.
3. What new time do they agree on?
Half past ten.
