Lesson 9 -- On the Trail: Mont Blanc Preparation

Series / Documentary
14 Peaks: Nothing Is Impossible (Netflix)

A breathtaking documentary about high-altitude mountaineering. Perfect for hearing how climbers describe their gear, the trail, the summit, and how they plan and predict the future on the mountain.

Watch with English subtitles. Write two sentences with going to (their plans) and two with will (their predictions) about what you watched.

Podcast
The Tough Girl Podcast -- "Preparing for a Big Trek"

A clear, slow episode about getting ready for the mountains: choosing gear, packing a backpack, checking the forecast, and staying safe at altitude. Full of useful trekking vocabulary.

Pause and repeat each trekking phrase aloud. Note three new words and use each one in a sentence about your own future adventure.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "will vs going to"

A clear, practical video on the difference between will and going to: plans and evidence versus quick decisions, offers, and opinions. Exactly today's grammar.

After watching, record a 60-second plan for a future trip: what you are going to do, and what you think will happen. Mix both forms.
