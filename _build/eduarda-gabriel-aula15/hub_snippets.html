Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

These are the key review and lessons-learned terms for this lesson. Tap Listen to hear the pronunciation.

Post-Mortem -- a review held after a project or deal is finished
"We held a post-mortem the week after the deal closed."
Outcome -- the final result of a process or deal
"The outcome of the restructuring was positive overall."
Root Cause -- the deepest underlying reason for a problem
"The root cause of the delay was a late report."
Hindsight -- understanding something only after it has happened
"In hindsight, we should have acted faster."
Overlook -- to fail to notice or consider something important
"We overlooked an early warning sign in the reports."
Recur -- to happen again, often more than once
"We updated the checklist so the issue will not recur."
Downturn -- a period when the economy or market gets weaker
"A sharp downturn could have changed the outcome."
Debrief -- a meeting to review what happened after an event
"Let us walk through the deal in the debrief."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Grammar

Use the third conditional to imagine a different past — one that did not happen. Form: If + had + past participle (the imagined condition), would / could have + past participle (the imagined result). Usamos isso para falar de hindsight: o que TERIA acontecido. O passado nao pode mudar. Compare com a Aula 13 (first conditional, futuro real): aqui e um passado imaginario.

PartFormExample
Imagined conditionIf + had + past participle"If we had caught it sooner..."
Imagined resultwould have + past participle"...we would have avoided the delay."
Softer resultcould have + past participle"...it could have been worse."

Common Mistake:

"If we would have caught it sooner, we would have avoided the delay."

"If we had caught it sooner, we would have avoided the delay."

A if-clause leva had + participio — nunca "would have". So o resultado usa would/could have.

Stage 5: Think About It

Reflection
Think of a deal or project that has finished. In 3-4 sentences, run a mini post-mortem: name the outcome and the root cause, and use the third conditional to say what would have been different in hindsight ("If we had..., we would have..."). Use at least two key terms.

Survival Card -- Lesson 15

1 Overall, the outcome was positive, but there is one clear lesson. No geral, o resultado foi positivo, mas há uma lição clara.
2 The root cause was a gap we overlooked early on. A causa raiz foi uma falha que deixamos passar no começo.
3 In hindsight, if we had caught it sooner, we would have avoided the delay. Em retrospecto, se tivéssemos notado antes, teríamos evitado o atraso.
