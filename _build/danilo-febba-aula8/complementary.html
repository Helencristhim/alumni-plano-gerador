Complementary Activities

Extra materials to reinforce your learning outside class. Mark as done after watching or listening.

Lesson 8 -- Review: Lessons 1-7

Series
The Office (US) -- Season 1, Episode 6: "Hot Girl"

Characters talk about their daily routine (present simple) and about what happened (past simple) in the same scenes. Connection to Lesson 8: listen for the switch between "every day" routines and "yesterday / last week" stories.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write 3 present sentences and 3 past sentences you hear.

Podcast
BBC Learning English -- "6 Minute English: How do you learn a language?"

A short, clear episode at A2 speed. The hosts talk about learning, practice, mistakes, and progress. Connection to Lesson 8: it reinforces your new words (improve, practice, mistake, progress) in natural speech.

Tip: listen twice. The second time, repeat any sentence with "practice" or "improve" out loud.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Present Simple vs Past Simple"

A short, practical lesson that compares the present simple (every day) with the past simple (yesterday), including irregular verbs like go/went and have/had. Connection to Lesson 8: exactly the two tenses you reviewed today, at your level.

Tip: pause and say each example aloud. Then make your own version about your work week 3 times.
