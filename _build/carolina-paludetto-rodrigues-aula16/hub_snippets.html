Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Predict -- to say what you think will happen in the future
"I predict that English will be my best skill." (Prever / fazer uma previsão)
Future -- the time that comes after now, like next year
"In the future, I want to study abroad." (O futuro)
Maybe -- possible, but not sure; it can happen or not
"Maybe I will start a podcast next year." (Talvez)
Probably -- almost sure; very likely to happen
"I will probably join the science club." (Provavelmente)
Sure -- you have no doubt; you know it will happen
"I am sure I will work harder next year." (Certo / ter certeza)
Hope -- to want something good to happen in the future
"I hope I will study abroad one day." (Esperar / torcer por)
Dream -- a big wish or goal you really want for your future
"My dream is to become a writer." (Sonho)
Technology -- modern machines and tools, like computers and robots
"Technology will change our classrooms." (Tecnologia)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- will for predictions

GRAMMAR

Usamos will + verbo na forma base para prever o futuro. O negativo é won't. Quando não temos certeza total, usamos probably (quase certo) ou maybe (talvez). / We use will + base verb to predict the future. The negative is won't. When we are not totally sure, we use probably (almost sure) or maybe (not sure).

TypeRuleExample
Positivewill + base verbI will study harder
Negativewon't + base verbI won't give up
QuestionWill + subject + verb?Will you join the club?
Almost surewill + probablyI will probably join
Not sureMaybe + subject + willMaybe I will join

Key point / Ponto-chave: depois de will, o verbo fica sempre na forma base: she will study (não "she will studies"), I will travel (não "I will to travel"). O negativo é won't (= will not). Para suavizar, use probably depois de will (I will probably) ou maybe no começo (Maybe I will). Diferença da aula 12: lá usávamos going to/will para PLANOS do fim de semana; aqui usamos will para PREVISÕES e sonhos sobre o futuro.
