Aula 16 -- Looking Ahead (Predictions with will)

Tema da aula: Looking Ahead -- making predictions about the future with will, won't, probably and maybe. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
Will for Predictions in English -- I will, I won't, probably, maybe

Watch a short, clear video about will for the future. Listen for will + base verb (I will study), the negative won't (I won't give up), and how probably and maybe show how sure we are.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote cinco previsões com will. Marque onde aparece probably e onde aparece maybe.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- the future with will

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about will for predictions: how to use will + base verb, the negative won't, questions with will, and the adverbs probably and maybe.

Dica: escreva seis frases sobre o seu futuro. Sublinhe will e won't, e circule probably e maybe.

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about the future, technology or careers

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen to people talk about the future, new technology and their dreams -- great input to review today's words and predictions with will.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote toda previsão com will (will change, will grow) e toda palavra nova (future, dream, technology, probably).
