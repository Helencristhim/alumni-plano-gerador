Suits — Season 1, Episode 1 (Netflix)

A busy firm where people present results, review their progress and talk about targets and challenges — the same professional English you use with the Global team. A good mirror for your midpoint review.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Treasure hunt: write down 3 sentences that compare or review — one with a comparative ("stronger than", "better than") and two about results or a challenge. Do not try to understand 100%.
