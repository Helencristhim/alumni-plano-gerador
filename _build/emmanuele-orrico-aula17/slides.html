Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

"I am work on the Rain Plan."
"I am working on the Rain Plan."
"This year is more strong than last year."
"This year is stronger than last year."
"Yesterday I present the results."
"Yesterday I presented the results."

Keep the -ing for what is happening now ("am working"). Short adjectives take -er ("stronger"). And use the past for finished actions ("presented", not "present").
