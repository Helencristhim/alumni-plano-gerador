Stage 1.1: Trip Story Vocabulary

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Estas palavras ajudam voce a descrever e contar a historia de uma viagem.

Amazing -- incrivel, surpreendente
"The trip was amazing from start to finish."
Journey -- viagem (o trajeto)
"The journey was long, but the scenery was beautiful."
Scenery -- paisagem (natural)
"The scenery on the road was wonderful."
Sunset -- por do sol
"We watched the sunset from the beach."
Beach -- praia
"We relaxed on the beach all afternoon."
To explore -- explorar (descobrir um lugar)
"I explored the old town all day."
To relax -- relaxar, descansar
"On the last day, I relaxed by the sea."
Exhausting -- cansativo, exaustivo
"The long walk was exhausting, but fun."
Memorable -- memoravel, inesquecivel
"It was a memorable week with my family."
Highlight -- ponto alto, melhor parte
"The sunset on the beach was the highlight of my trip."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- The Past Simple

Grammar

Para falar de uma viagem que ja terminou, use o past simple. A maioria dos verbos ganha -ed (walk → walked, explore → explored, relax → relaxed). Alguns verbos sao irregulares e mudam de forma (go → went, see → saw, have → had, be → was/were). Use palavras de tempo como yesterday, last week, two days ago. / To talk about a trip that is already finished, use the past simple. Most verbs add -ed (walk → walked). Some verbs are irregular and change shape (go → went, see → saw). Use time words like yesterday, last week, two days ago.

Stage 3: Pronunciation

Speaking

Ouca cada frase e depois grave-se dizendo. Atencao ao som do "-ed" no fim dos verbos regulares.

The journey was long, but the scenery was amazing.
A viagem foi longa, mas a paisagem foi incrivel.
I explored the old town all day.
Eu explorei a cidade velha o dia todo.
One evening I saw an amazing sunset.
Uma noite eu vi um por do sol incrivel.
It was a memorable, amazing trip.
Foi uma viagem memoravel e incrivel.

Quiz
