Lesson 3 -- Talking About My Team

Series
The Office (US) -- Season 1, Episode 2: "Diversity Day"

A slow-paced workplace comedy where the characters talk about their roles, their team members, and who does what. Direct connection to Lesson 3: describing the people on a team, their skills and responsibilities, with clear American English.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Note 3 ways the characters describe a colleague's job or skill.

Podcast
BBC Learning English -- "6 Minute English: Working in a team"

Short, clear episodes at a friendly speed, perfect for A2. Listen for plurals and words for people at work. Direct connection to Lesson 3: it trains your ear with slow, natural English about teams, roles, and skills.

Tip: listen twice. Write down 5 sentences describing the people on your team.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Plurals and Possessives in English"

A short, practical lesson on making plurals (-s, person → people) and possessives ('s and his/her/their). Connection to Lesson 3: the exact grammar you need to describe your team, at your level.

Tip: pause and repeat each example aloud. Then say 3 sentences about your own team using 's.
