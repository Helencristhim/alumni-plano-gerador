Podcast — listening
Talk About Your Job — English Conversation for Beginners (A1-A2)

Slow, clear listening practice about jobs and daily work — perfect for your two hours of daily immersion. Understanding real speech is the hardest skill, and the only way to train the ear is to use it, a little every day.

Tip: listen ONCE without stopping — don't worry about the words you miss. Then write down three verbs with -s that you hear (works, studies, lives...). Bring them to the next class.
