Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- The Third Conditional & "wish"

Grammar

O terceiro condicional fala de um passado IMAGINARIO (que nao aconteceu): if + had + particio passado, would have + particio passado. "If I had known, I would have booked." NUNCA use "would" depois de "if" (NAO "if I would have known"). Para arrependimentos, use "I wish I had..." ou "If only I had...". / The third conditional talks about an unreal past: if + had + past participle, would have + past participle. Never use "would" in the if-clause. For regrets, use "I wish I had..." or "If only I had...".

Funcao / FunctionEnglishPortugues
Unreal past resultIf I had known, I would have booked earlier.Se eu soubesse, eu teria reservado antes.
Possible past resultIf we had saved, we could have gone to Japan.Se tivessemos economizado, poderiamos ter ido ao Japao.
Past regret (wish)I wish I had learned English sooner.Eu queria ter aprendido ingles mais cedo.
Strong regret (if only)If only I had taken that trip.Se eu ao menos tivesse feito aquela viagem.
Common mistakeNOT "If I would have known" / "would of"Nunca "would" no "if"; e "would have", nao "would of".
