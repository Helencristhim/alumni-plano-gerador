Lesson 20 -- The Confident Traveler: Integration

Series / Travel
Somebody Feed Phil (Netflix) -- any episode

Phil immerses himself in a new culture in every episode, staying spontaneous and open. A great model of the confident, resilient traveler mindset in action.

Watch one episode with English subtitles. Note three moments where he adapts or embraces a surprise, then describe them using used to and now I can.

Podcast
The Thoughtful Travel Podcast -- "How Travel Changes You"

Travelers reflect on how trips broadened their view and built a resilient mindset. Perfect listening for today's reflective language: used to, now I can, and I have learned to.

After listening, record a one-minute voice note about how you have changed, using used to, now I can, and four new mindset words.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "used to: talking about the past"

A clear lesson on using "used to" to contrast a past habit with the present. Exactly today's grammar focus, with plenty of natural examples.

After watching, write four sentences about your journey: two with I used to and two with Now I can, then read them aloud.
