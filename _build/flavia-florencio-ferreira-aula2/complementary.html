Lesson 2 -- The Team

Grammar Video
"Present Simple and Present Continuous" -- BBC Learning English (6 Minute Grammar)

A short, clear lesson on the present simple. Connection to Lesson 2: this is the tense you add adverbs of frequency to when you say "we always meet on Monday" or "she often works from home".

Tip: after each example, say one true sentence about your own week with always, usually, often, sometimes, or never.
