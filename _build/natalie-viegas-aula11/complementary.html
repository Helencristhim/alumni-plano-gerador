Aula 11 -- Building Trust

Tema da aula: Networking and professional relationships, building trust with question tags (don't you?, haven't we?). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"How to Network Effectively" -- Harvard Business Review

A short, clear video on building genuine professional relationships: how to start a conversation, find a mutual interest, and follow up to build trust and credibility over time.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote duas frases com question tags (por exemplo, "..., don't you?" e "..., haven't we?").

Practice
BBC Learning English -- Question Tags

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about question tags. Perfect to review how a positive sentence takes a negative tag, how the tag matches the auxiliary (be, have, do), and how to use tags to confirm and connect.

Dica: pegue cinco frases sobre seus contatos e adicione um question tag a cada uma; confira o auxiliar e o pronome.

Podcast
"Business English Pod" -- Networking and Building Relationships

A friendly business podcast for B1-B2 learners. Listen to professionals network at events, build rapport and offer referrals -- great input to review rapport, mutual, reliable, credibility and nurture in context.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote toda expressão usada para iniciar uma conversa e manter contato.
