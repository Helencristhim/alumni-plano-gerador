Lesson 6 -- Executive Introduction Mastery

Series
Suits -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

Watch how the lawyers introduce themselves with confidence. Notice how a short, concise pitch builds rapport in seconds. Listen for present perfect lines like "I have worked on cases like this."

Write down two self-introductions you hear. Rewrite them with your own role at Oracle and say them out loud.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- Episode: "Introducing Yourself with Confidence"

Practical tips for a memorable self-introduction: your role, one strong project, and a friendly question to build rapport. Review of the words you learned: summarize, highlight, confident, strength.

Note the structure the host suggests. Record your own thirty-second pitch using present simple and present perfect.

YouTube
TED -- "How to Introduce Yourself Like a Leader"

A short, clear talk on the perfect professional introduction: be concise, highlight one strength, and finish with a question. Great for practicing a confident first impression in real meetings.

After watching, record a sixty-second introduction. Use at least three new words and one present perfect sentence.
