Aula 20 -- Shopping for Food

Tema da aula: Shopping for Food -- asking prices at the supermarket with How much is it?, and pointing with this, that, these and those. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
At the Supermarket -- How much is it? in English

Watch a short, clear video about shopping for food. Listen for How much is it? and How much are they?, and for this, that, these and those when people point at items and ask the price.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote cinco perguntas sobre preços: três com How much is it? (uma coisa) e duas com How much are they? (varias coisas).

Practice
BBC Learning English -- this, that, these, those

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about pointing words and prices: this apple, that bag, these apples, those bananas, and How much is it? / How much are they?

Dica: escreva seis frases sobre compras. Sublinhe is e are, e circule cada palavra nova (price, cheap, expensive, cashier, receipt, customer, cart, change).

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about shopping or money

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen to people talk about shopping, prices and money -- great input to review today's words and the patterns How much is it? and this / that / these / those.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote toda frase com How much e toda palavra nova (price, cheap, expensive, cashier, receipt, change).
