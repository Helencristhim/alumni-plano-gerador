Grammar Tip — Possessives + have/has

Grammar

Os possessivos dizem DE QUEM é. E have/has diz o que alguém TEM. Veja como funciona:

Pessoa Possessivo have/has Exemplo
I (eu)my (meu/minha)haveI have a son. My son is tall.
you (você)your (seu/sua)haveYour family is lovely.
he (ele)his (dele)hasHe has a daughter. His daughter is Sofia.
she (ela)her (dela)hasShe has a nice kitchen. Her kitchen is sunny.
we (nós)our (nosso/a)haveOur neighborhood is quiet.
they (eles/elas)their (deles/delas)haveTheir apartment is big.

Regra de ouro: he/she usa has (não "have"). (Ele tem = He has / Ela tem = She has)

Cuidado com his/her: his = dele, her = dela. O possessivo combina com o DONO, não com a coisa: o filho DELA = her son.

Exemplo: "David has two children. His son is ten. Érica loves her kitchen."
