Podcast — listening
My Daily Routine — Easy English Listening Practice, A2 Level (English Phrases Hub)

Slow, clear listening at your exact level: a person describes a daily routine in the present simple, and what is happening now in the present continuous. Understanding by ear is your hardest skill, and the only way to train it is to use it, a little every day.

Tip: listen ONCE without stopping — don't worry about the words you miss. Then write down two sentences: one habit you heard (She wakes up...) and one action now (She is cooking...). Bring them to class.
