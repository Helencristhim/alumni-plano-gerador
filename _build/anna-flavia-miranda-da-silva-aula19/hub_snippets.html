Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Can / Can't

Grammar

Use can + verbo quando e possivel e can't + verbo quando nao e possivel. O verbo nao muda (sem -s, sem to). / Use can for possible and can't for not possible. The verb never changes.

EstruturaEnglishPortugues
can + verboI can hear you.Eu consigo ouvir voce.
can't + verboI can't see your screen.Eu nao consigo ver sua tela.
Can + you + verbo?Can you hear me?Voce consegue me ouvir?
can + I + verbo?Can I share my screen?Posso compartilhar minha tela?
