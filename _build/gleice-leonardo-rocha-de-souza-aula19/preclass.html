Aula 19 -- Pre-class

Job Interviews -- answering "Tell me about yourself"

Be ready for a job interview in English. Answer the big question -- "Tell me about yourself" -- using the present perfect you already know (I have worked, I have managed). Talk about your strengths and weaknesses, stay confident and honest, and answer questions about your qualification and salary. New words: interview, candidate, strength, weakness, confident, honest, qualification, salary.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Learn the eight new words about job interviews. Tap Listen to hear each one.

Interview -- a meeting where a company asks you questions about a job
"I have an interview on Monday." (entrevista)
Candidate -- a person who wants the job and goes to the interview
"I am the right candidate for this team." (candidato)
Strength -- something you are very good at
"My biggest strength is communication." (ponto forte)
Weakness -- something you are working to improve
"One weakness is that I am too dedicated." (ponto fraco)
Confident -- sure of yourself; calm and positive
"Stay calm and confident in the interview." (confiante)
Honest -- telling the truth; not lying
"Be honest about your strengths." (honesto)
Qualification -- the study, training, or experience that makes you right for a job
"I have a good qualification for this role." (qualificação)
Salary -- the money a company pays you for your work
"My salary expectation is fair and open." (salário)

GRAMMAR

"Thank you for this interview. Let me tell you about myself. My background is in biology, and I have worked in the pharmaceutical field for ten years. In my last position, I have managed a large sales team. My biggest strength is communication, and I am a very confident and honest person. One weakness is that I am too dedicated. I have a good qualification for this role, and I really want this job. I believe I am the right candidate for your team."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Answering "Tell me about yourself"

GRAMMAR

Numa entrevista, a pergunta mais importante é "Tell me about yourself" (Fale sobre você). Use o present perfect que você já conhece (have/has + particípio) para falar da sua experiência: I have worked in this field for ten years. I have managed a sales team. Fale do seu ponto forte (My biggest strength is...) e de um ponto fraco (One weakness is...). Termine com confiança: I am the right candidate for this job. NUNCA responda só com "I am Gleice" -- conte sua história profissional. / Use the present perfect to answer interview questions about your experience, then share a strength, a weakness, and why you are the right candidate.
