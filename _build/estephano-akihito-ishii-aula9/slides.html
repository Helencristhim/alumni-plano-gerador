Chapter 3 -- The Code

Two Key Questions

Can I have...? / How much...?

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

Look at the orange words. One question asks for a thing. One question asks the price.

"Can I have this shirt?"

"How much is it?"

"How much are they?"

"Can I pay here?"

Grammar Rule

Asking and Paying

QuestionUseAnswer
Can I have this shirt?ask for a thingYes, of course.
How much is it?price (one thing)It is forty reais.
How much are they?price (many things)They are ten reais.

Key idea: use Can I have to ask for a thing. Use How much is it for one thing and How much are they for many things.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"How much it is?"
"How much is it?"

In a question, the verb is comes before it. Say "How much is it?"
