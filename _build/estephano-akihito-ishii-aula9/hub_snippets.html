Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Store -- a place where you buy things
"I go to the store." (loja)
Buy -- to get a thing and give money for it
"I want to buy a shirt." (comprar)
Price -- the money you pay for one thing
"The price is forty reais." (preço)
Money -- coins and bills you use to buy things
"I have money in my bag." (dinheiro)
Pay -- to give money for a thing
"I pay with cash." (pagar)
Cheap -- low price, not a lot of money
"This shirt is cheap." (barato)
Expensive -- high price, a lot of money
"That car is expensive." (caro)
Size -- how big a thing is: small, medium, large
"I need a medium size." (tamanho)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Estephano goes to a store. He sees a blue shirt and asks: "Can I have this shirt?" The shop assistant says: "Of course. What size do you need?" Estephano says: "Medium, please. How much is it?" She says: "It is forty reais. It is very cheap today!" Estephano is happy. He gives his money and asks: "Can I pay here?"

1. What does Estephano say to ask for the shirt?
A "Can I have this shirt?"
B "Where are you from?"
C "What is your name?"
2. How much is the shirt?
A One hundred reais.
B Forty reais.
C Twenty reais.
3. Which question asks the price?
A "Can I have this shirt?"
B "How much is it?"
C "What size do you need?"

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Can I have...? / How much...?

GRAMMAR

Pedir uma coisa e perguntar o preço em uma loja. / Asking for a thing and asking the price.

Question / PerguntaUse / UsoAnswer / Resposta
Can I have this shirt?Pedir uma coisaYes, of course.
How much is it?Preço de UMA coisaIt is forty reais.
How much are they?Preço de VÁRIAS coisasThey are ten reais.
Atenção: a ordem é How much + is/are (nunca "How much it is"). O verbo vem antes: "How much is it?"
