Arnel's Everyday English — Indirect (Embedded) Questions

This is tonight's grammar, explained slowly and clearly by a teacher whose accent is easy to follow. She shows exactly how the soft opener works and why the word order changes after it — "where the elevator is", not "where is the elevator". Watch it before your lesson and the whole thing will click.

Tip: keep a pen. Every time she gives a direct question, pause and try to turn it into an indirect one yourself before she does. Then check whether you kept the normal word order.
