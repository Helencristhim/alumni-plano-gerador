Metodologia

1. Discovery-Based Grammar

Victor discovers grammar patterns through real corporate examples before seeing the rule. This leverages his strong comprehension and analytical MBA mindset.

2. Stakeholder-Driven Content

Every lesson uses Brink's context: board presentations, KPI discussions, cross-regional meetings. Victor practices what he will actually do at work.

3. Multi-Accent Exposure

Progressive exposure to American, British, Indian, and Australian accents. Brink's operates in 50+ countries, so accent flexibility is critical.

4. Production Over Comprehension

Victor understands more than he produces. Every lesson pushes production: role-plays, presentations, elevator pitches. Target: close the comprehension-production gap.

5. Spanish as Accelerator

Leverage Victor's fluent Spanish for cognate vocabulary (strategy/estrategia, performance/performance). Monitor for false friends and interference patterns.
