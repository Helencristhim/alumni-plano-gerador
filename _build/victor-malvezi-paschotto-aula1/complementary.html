Aula 1 -- Diagnostic + Personal Baseline

Series
Industry (HBO) -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Sesh"

Young professionals navigating the corporate world at a top London investment bank. Observe how they introduce themselves to senior colleagues, describe their roles, and use corporate vocabulary under pressure. Direct connection to Lesson 1: professional self-introductions, navigating hierarchy, and making a first impression in a multinational environment.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Note 3 expressions the characters use when describing their roles or responsibilities to senior staff.

Podcast
Harvard Business Review IdeaCast -- Episode 932: "What It Takes to Lead Growth"

Growth strategy experts discuss what separates companies that scale successfully from those that stall. Vocabulary about KPIs, stakeholder alignment, and performance metrics in context. Direct connection to Victor's role: growth management language used by real executives and consultants in a natural, unscripted format.

Tip: listen to the first 25 minutes. Write down 5 expressions related to growth strategy that you hear.

YouTube
TED Talk -- Simon Sinek: "How Great Leaders Inspire Action"

Simon Sinek's iconic talk about the "Golden Circle" and why great leaders start with WHY. Relevant for Victor's growth role: how to articulate vision and strategy to stakeholders. Connection to the lesson: professional language for describing purpose, motivating teams, and communicating results to leadership.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Practice repeating 3 phrases Sinek uses when explaining how leaders communicate their vision.
