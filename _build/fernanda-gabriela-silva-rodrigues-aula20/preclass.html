Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Estas sao palavras-chave do Bloco 2 que voce ja aprendeu. Ouca cada uma e leia o exemplo para revisar. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Arrange -- marcar, organizar (uma reuniao)
"Let me arrange a call with the supplier." (Deixe-me marcar uma ligacao com o fornecedor.)
Reschedule -- remarcar para outro horario
"We can reschedule the call to Friday." (Podemos remarcar a ligacao para sexta.)
Support -- ajudar, apoiar (um colega)
"I can support you with the report." (Posso te ajudar com o relatorio.)
Compare -- comparar (duas opcoes)
"Let me compare the two suppliers." (Deixe-me comparar os dois fornecedores.)
Progress -- progresso, andamento
"Here is the progress on the order." (Aqui esta o andamento do pedido.)
Complete -- completo, pronto (100%)
"That part is complete now." (Essa parte esta completa agora.)
On track -- no caminho certo, no prazo
"We are still on track for the deadline." (Ainda estamos no prazo.)
Choose -- escolher (uma opcao)
"I think we should choose this supplier." (Acho que devemos escolher este fornecedor.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

At Sucafina, Daniel and Fernanda plan the week. Daniel says: "Could you set up a call with the supplier?" They are going to confirm the price on Thursday. Fernanda compares the two suppliers: the Andino one is cheaper than the other, so they will choose it. Fernanda can help the new colleague with the report. For the progress: they have already sent the contract, but the deposit has not arrived yet. They are back on track for the deadline.

1. What are they going to do on Thursday? (O que eles vao fazer na quinta?)
A They are going to confirm the price. (Confirmar o preco.)
B They are going to sign the deposit. (Assinar o deposito.)
C They are going to cancel the order. (Cancelar o pedido.)
2. Why do they choose the Andino supplier? (Por que escolhem o Andino?)
A Because it is more expensive. (Porque e mais caro.)
B Because it is cheaper than the other. (Porque e mais barato.)
C Because it is far away. (Porque e longe.)
3. What has not arrived yet? (O que ainda nao chegou?)
A The contract. (O contrato.)
B The deposit. (O deposito.)
C The report. (O relatorio.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Your Block 2 Toolkit

Grammar

Esta aula reune as cinco estruturas-chave do Bloco 2. Uma conversa de trabalho real usa todas elas juntas: pedido educado, plano, oferta de ajuda, comparacao e follow-up. / This lesson brings together the five core structures of Block 2. A real work conversation uses them all together.

Uso / UseForma / FormExample / Exemplo
Pedido educado / Polite requestCould you + verbo?Could you set up a call? (Voce poderia marcar uma ligacao?)
Plano / Planbe going to + verboWe are going to confirm the price. (Vamos confirmar o preco.)
Oferecer ajuda / Offer helpcan + verboI can help you. (Eu posso te ajudar.)
Comparar / Comparecheaper/faster + thanAndino is cheaper than the other. (E mais barato que o outro.)
Follow-up / Done?have/has + participioWe have already sent it. (Ja enviamos.)

Atencao: 'be' + going to para planos; 'cheaper than' para comparar (nunca 'more cheap that'); 'have' com I/you/we/they e 'has' com he/she/it. / Remember: 'be' + going to for plans; 'cheaper than' for comparisons; 'have' with I/you/we/they.
