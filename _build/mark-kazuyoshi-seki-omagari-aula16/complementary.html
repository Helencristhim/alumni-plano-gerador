Complementary Materials — Lesson 16

Series
Hot Ones — any celebrity interview episode

The host asks sharp, unexpected questions and the guests answer between spicy wings. Perfect for noticing how a question is asked, then how you would retell it later as a reported question.

Tip: pause after each question and retell it: "He asked whether...", "She wondered what...".

Podcast
The Tim Ferriss Show — long-form interviews

An interviewer who probes deeply and asks careful follow-up questions. Notice how he inquires, wonders and refers to earlier answers, and report three questions in your own words.

Tip: write three reported questions from the episode, one yes/no with "whether".

YouTube
BBC Learning English — "reported questions"

A short, clear video on exactly this lesson's grammar: how to report yes/no questions with if/whether and wh-questions, using statement order and no question mark.

Tip: after watching, report five questions someone asked you this week.
