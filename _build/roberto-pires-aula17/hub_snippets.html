Future Travel Plans: Talking About Your Next Trip

Stage 1.1: Trip Planning Vocabulary

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Estas palavras ajudam voce a planejar e descrever a sua proxima viagem.

Destination -- destino (lugar para onde voce vai)
"My next destination is a city by the sea."
Itinerary -- roteiro (plano dia a dia)
"My itinerary has sightseeing in the morning."
Budget -- orcamento (dinheiro planejado)
"My budget for this trip is small."
Accommodation -- hospedagem (onde ficar)
"I am going to book accommodation near the center."
Sightseeing -- passeio turistico
"We are going to do sightseeing every morning."
Abroad -- no exterior, em outro pais
"Next year I will travel abroad."
To plan -- planejar, decidir o que fazer
"I am going to plan my whole trip this week."
In advance -- com antecedencia, cedo
"I will book a flight in advance to save money."
Adventure -- aventura (experiencia animadora)
"It is going to be an amazing adventure abroad."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia a situacao e responda as perguntas.

Next month, Roberto is going to take a big trip. His destination is a city abroad, by the sea. He already booked a flight in advance, so the ticket was cheap. He is going to plan his accommodation near the center. Each day, he will follow a simple itinerary, with time for sightseeing and time to rest. His budget is small, but he thinks it will be an amazing adventure.

1. When is Roberto going to take his trip?
A Last month.
B Next month.
C Yesterday.
2. Why was his flight cheap?
A Because the budget was big.
B Because he booked it in advance.
C Because he stayed near the center.
3. Which form shows a plan he already has?
A He went to a city.
B He is going to plan his accommodation.
C He plans every day.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Going To & Will

Grammar

Para falar do futuro, use going to para um plano que voce ja decidiu (I am going to book a flight) e will para uma decisao na hora, uma promessa ou uma previsao (I will help you / The trip will be great). Atencao: depois de "going" sempre vem "to" (going to book), e depois de "will" o verbo fica na forma base, sem "to" (will book, NUNCA will to book). / To talk about the future, use going to for a plan you already decided, and will for a decision now, a promise, or a prediction. After "going" always use "to"; after "will" use the base verb (no "to").

Forma / FormExemplo / ExampleUso / Use
am/is/are going to + verboI am going to visit Italy.plano que voce ja tem
going to (negativo)I am not going to fly today.plano que NAO vai acontecer
will + verbo baseI will help you pack.decisao agora / promessa
will + verbo baseThe trip will be amazing.previsao (o que voce acha)
will not (won't)It won't be expensive.previsao negativa

Stage 2: Order the Trip Plan

Order

Ouca primeiro, depois coloque os passos do plano na ordem certa.

?Finally, he is going to check his budget before the big adventure abroad.

Stage 3: Pronunciation

Speaking

Ouca cada frase e depois grave-se dizendo. Atencao a "going to" (fala-se "gonna" no dia a dia) e ao "ll" de "I'll".

My next destination is going to be abroad.
Meu proximo destino vai ser no exterior.
I am going to book a flight in advance.
Eu vou comprar uma passagem com antecedencia.
I will write a simple itinerary for each day.
Eu vou escrever um roteiro simples para cada dia.
It will be an amazing adventure.
Vai ser uma aventura incrivel.

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao.

A friend asks: "What are your plans for next month?" What is the best answer?
A "I am going to travel abroad."
B "I am going travel abroad."
C "I going to travel abroad."
Your friend looks worried about planning. You want to make a promise. What do you say?
A "I will to help you."
B "Don't worry, I will help you plan."
C "I helped you yesterday."
Which sentence is a correct prediction in the future?
A "I think the trip is amazing yesterday."
B "I think the trip will to be amazing."
C "I think the trip will be amazing."

Stage 5: Free Production

Reflection

Grave-se respondendo a pergunta. Nao ha resposta certa ou errada.

Think about your next trip. Make the plan in the future: say where you are going to go, how you are going to book your flight and accommodation, what your itinerary and budget will be, and why it will be a great adventure.
