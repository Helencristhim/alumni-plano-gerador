This is exactly the journey of lesson 6: the flight, the departure, the arrival. Lucy shows the real words you need at the airport — check-in, boarding, gates, luggage — in clear, slow, American-friendly English.

Podcast — listening
Traveling Soon: English Phrases for the Airport (All Ears English, ep. 2297)

Natural American conversation about getting ready to travel — the plan, the check-in, the departure — perfect for your two hours of daily immersion. Understanding real speech by ear is the hardest skill, and the only way to train it is a little every day.

Tip: listen ONCE without stopping — don't worry about the words you miss. Then write down three "going to" sentences you can use at an airport (I am going to check in, they are going to board, we are going to depart). Bring them to the next class.
