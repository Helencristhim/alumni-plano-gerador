Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"She have a colleague."

✓ RIGHT

"She has a colleague."

✗ WRONG

"I have 55 years old."

✓ RIGHT

"I am 55 years old."

✗ WRONG

"I wake up always early."

✓ RIGHT

"I always wake up early."

He, she and it use has. Use the verb to be for age. Frequency words (always, usually, never) go before the main verb.
