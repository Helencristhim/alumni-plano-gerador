A clear documentary about how phones, notifications and apps have been changing the way we live. Perfect to hear people describe what technology has been doing to our attention.

All Ears English — "Present Perfect Continuous" episodes

Clear, friendly English about how to talk about actions that started before and are still going on, with have been and has been. A great way to hear this tense used naturally in real conversation.
