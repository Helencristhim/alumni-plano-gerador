Aula 15 — Block 3: Presenting the Business Case: Delivering the Recommendation

Series
Shark Tank / Dragons' Den — any two-minute investment pitch

Watch how the best founders deliver a recommendation: they open with the ask, give a concise rationale, advocate for one option, and close with a one-line upshot. Notice the emphasis frames they use to point at the one number that matters ("What you need to know is...").

Tip: pick one pitch and write its structure -- recommendation, rationale, emphasis frame, upshot -- in five lines.
