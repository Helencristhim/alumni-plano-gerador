Composure -- the ability to stay calm and in control under pressure
"The director praised my composure during the hard questions." (Compostura / calma)
Walkthrough -- a clear, step-by-step explanation of how something was done
"I gave the director a walkthrough of the project from start to finish." (Explicação passo a passo)
Articulate -- to express an idea clearly and simply
"He articulates complex ideas in a simple way." (Expressar com clareza)
Anecdote -- a short, true story you tell to make a point
"I shared a quick anecdote about a project I rescued." (Caso / historinha real)
Showcase -- to present your skills or results clearly
"The interview is your chance to showcase your track record." (Evidenciar / exibir)
Debrief -- a short meeting to review something after it happened
"After the panel, the team had a debrief and made a decision." (Reunião de balanço)

For the final interview, Rafael rehearsed his answers so he could keep his composure. He opened with his experience up to now in the present perfect: "I have led delivery teams for six years, and I have managed eight major releases." Then he gave the director a clear walkthrough of one project using the STAR method, in the past simple, because the story was finished: "When I joined, the release was eight weeks late. After a quick debrief, the panel decided to offer him the role.

A He did not prepare at all.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- The STAR Method & Tenses

GRAMMAR

Use o STAR para estruturar a resposta: Situation, Task, Action, Result. Abra com a experiência até agora no present perfect e conte UMA história terminada no past simple. / Use STAR to structure your answer: Situation, Task, Action, Result. Open with your experience up to now in the present perfect, then tell ONE finished story in the past simple.

Step / PassoIt answers / RespondeExample / Exemplo
S -- Situationo contexto (onde você estava)The release was eight weeks late.
T -- Taskseu objetivo ou o problemaMy task was to get it back on track.
A -- Action (past simple)o que você FEZI rebuilt the plan and aligned the team.
R -- Result (com número)o desfechoWe shipped on time and the client renewed.

Key point / Ponto-chave: abra mostrando sua carreira até agora no present perfect ("I have led eight releases"); depois conte UMA história terminada no past simple seguindo o STAR. O Result sempre traz um número real (on time, two years), porque um número soa concreto. Evite respostas que são só opinião, sem história nem resultado.

Stage 2: Put the STAR Steps in Order

Order

Listen first, then put the STAR steps of a strong answer in the correct order.

?Then describe the action: what did you actually do, step by step?
?First, set the situation: where were you and what was the context?
?Finally, add one short takeaway: what did you learn from it?
?Next, explain the task: what was your goal or the problem to solve?
?After that, share the result with a real number, like on time or two years.

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Choose the best response for each interview situation.

The director asks for a walkthrough of one project. The best opening is:
A "When I joined, the release was eight weeks late, so I rebuilt the plan."
B "When I joined, I have rebuilt the plan eight weeks late."
C "I am good with late projects, trust me."
You want a strong Result step. The clearest sentence is:
A "The result was good and everyone was happy."
B "We shipped on time, and the client renewed the contract for two years."
C "The result will be great next year."
The director asks how you keep your composure. The best answer is:
A "I never feel pressure, so it is easy."
B "My mindset is to stay calm, communicate clearly, and own the outcome."
C "I will rehearse it later, I promise."
