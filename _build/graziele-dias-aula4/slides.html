Chapter 4: Scheduling with Dr. Carter

"Can We Meet?"

Set the day and the time

Dialogue

Setting the Meeting

C
Hi Graziele, can we schedule a meeting this week?
G
Of course, Dr. Carter. Let me check my calendar. The meeting room is free on Monday.
C
Monday is good. What time?
G
Can we meet at ten o'clock?
C
Ten o'clock is a little early. Can we meet at half past two?
G
Yes, half past two on Monday is perfect. The meeting is one hour.
C
Great. Please put the appointment in the calendar.
G
Done. The meeting is on Monday, the third of June, at half past two.

One hour.
