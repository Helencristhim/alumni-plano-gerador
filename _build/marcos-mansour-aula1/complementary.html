Aula 1 -- Introducing Yourself Professionally

Series
Suits -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

Professionals at a top New York law firm constantly introduce themselves, state their roles, and exchange contacts. Observe how they say what they do, name their company, and make a strong first impression. Direct connection to Lesson 1: professional self-introductions and networking in a high-stakes environment.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Note 3 expressions the characters use to introduce themselves and their roles.

Podcast
The English We Speak (BBC) -- "Introducing Yourself and Others"

Short, clear episodes about everyday professional English. Listen to natural phrases used when meeting people, stating your role, and keeping in touch. Direct connection to Lesson 1: the real expressions native speakers use for self-introductions and small talk.

Tip: listen twice. Write down 5 phrases you can use to introduce yourself at the congress.

YouTube
"How to Introduce Yourself Professionally in English" -- Business English Pod

A practical lesson on professional self-introductions: name, role, company, and field, plus the questions you ask back. Connection to Lesson 1: ready-to-use sentence patterns for networking events and congresses, exactly the context Marcos will face in Washington.

Tip: pause and repeat each introduction aloud. Practice saying your own version 3 times.
