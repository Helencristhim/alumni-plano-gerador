Aula 17 -- Data-Driven -- Describing Trends & Numbers

Tema da aula: read a chart like an analyst -- compare figures with comparatives (Q3 was higher than Q2) and superlatives (the highest figure of the year), show proportion (twice as large as), tune the size with degree modifiers (significantly, considerably, slightly, marginally), and report every number in the passive voice (the target was exceeded; the figures were reported by finance). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.
