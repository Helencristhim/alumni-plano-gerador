Aula 16 -- The Follow-Up Email

Tema da aula: follow-up emails -- writing a clear email after a meeting (subject, recap, action items, Please find attached... / I would be grateful if you could...). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"How to write a professional follow-up email" -- business English video

Watch a short, clear video about writing a follow-up email after a meeting: the subject line, the greeting, a quick recap, and a polite closing. Listen for "Please find attached..." and "I would be grateful if you could...".

Dica: anote uma frase para apontar o anexo (Please find attached...) e uma para pedir com educação (I would be grateful if you could...).

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "Writing emails at work"

Short lessons and quizzes to practise the language of professional emails: subject lines, greetings, attaching files, polite requests, and closings. Perfect to feel confident writing a real work email.

Dica: escreva um email curto de três linhas -- recap, anexo, e um pedido educado. Confirme que usa "Please find attached" e "Best regards".

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about business communication or writing

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen for how people talk about clear writing, polite requests, and professional emails at work.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote uma expressão nova para usar no seu próximo email de trabalho.
