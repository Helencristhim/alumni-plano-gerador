Lesson 12 -- Closing the Deal

Series
Suits -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

Watch how lawyers negotiate terms and close a deal. Notice the offers with conditions: "If you do X, we will do Y." Listen for words like agreement, terms, and deal.

Write down every sentence with "If... will...". Pause and repeat each one, then negotiate an imaginary deal out loud.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- Episode: "Negotiating and Closing the Deal"

Practical phrases for negotiations: how to ask for a discount, make an offer with a condition, and close a deal. Listen for the new words: negotiate, discount, agreement, terms, deposit, warranty, sign, and deal.

Note 3 sentences that use the first conditional ("If... will..."). Practice each one out loud and change the condition.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "The first conditional: real possibilities"

A short, clear lesson on the first conditional: if + present simple, will + base verb. Perfect for making offers with conditions when you close a deal.

After watching, record yourself closing a contract meeting. Use the first conditional and at least three new words from this lesson.
