Grammar Rule

Block 2 in One Table

FormUseExample
Wh- questionsAsk about needsWhat are your priorities?
ComparativesCompare two optionsmore reliable than
should / wouldGive advice, recommendI would recommend the pro plan
will / going toDecisions and plansWe are going to sign it
First conditionalAn offer with a conditionIf you sign today, we will give a discount

Key idea: To close a deal, use the first conditional: If + present simple, will + base verb. It connects a condition to an offer.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"If you will sign today, we will give a discount."
"If you sign today, we will give a discount."

In the if-part, use the present simple. Use will only in the result part.
