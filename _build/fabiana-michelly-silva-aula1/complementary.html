Lesson 1 -- Diagnostic + Who Is Fabiana?

Series
Industry -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Sesh"

Young graduates navigating the corporate world in a global finance company. The high-pressure environment mirrors Bloomberg's pace, and the financial vocabulary is directly applicable to your context.

Watch with English subtitles. Pay attention to how characters introduce themselves and describe their roles in the first 15 minutes.

Podcast
Supply Chain Now -- "What Does a Supply Chain Professional Actually Do?"

A 25-minute episode where real supply chain professionals describe their daily routines, challenges, and vocabulary. Excellent for hearing familiar terms used by native speakers in your exact field.

Listen at 1x speed first. Write down 5 words or expressions you already know and 3 that are new to you.

YouTube
Bloomberg Originals -- "How Bloomberg Terminal Works"

A 10-minute explainer about the Bloomberg Terminal, the core product your company provides. Hearing your own company's product described in English builds confidence and activates passive vocabulary you already know.

Watch once for fun, then watch again and pause every time you hear a word from today's lesson (supply chain, vendor, procurement, etc.).
