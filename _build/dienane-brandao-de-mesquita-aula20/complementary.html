Lesson 20 -- On the Phone

Series
Extra English -- Episode 7 (a phone call)

A simple comedy made for English learners. The friends make phone calls in slow, clear English -- Can I speak to...?, This is ... speaking, Hold on, please. Perfect for A1 listening about phone phrases.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down every phone phrase that you hear. Then say out loud: "Can I speak to...?" and "This is ... speaking.".

Podcast
Coffee Break English -- "On the Telephone"

A short, friendly lesson on phone calls: how to use phrases like "Can I speak to...?", "This is ... speaking", and "Can I take a message?". The hosts explain the phrases slowly, with many examples you can repeat.

Pause after each example and repeat it out loud. Then make a short phone call: say hello, say who you are, and ask to speak to someone.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Telephone English"

A clear video that teaches phone vocabulary (call, message, speak, caller, wrong number, hold on) and the phone phrases, like "Can I speak to the manager?" and "Hold on, please", with pictures and simple sentences.

After watching, record a short voice message: make a work phone call -- say hello, who you are, ask to speak to someone, and leave a message.
