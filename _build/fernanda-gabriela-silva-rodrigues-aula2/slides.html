Chapter 3: The Code

Present Simple

I work · she works · we work

Discover the Grammar

Find the S

"I work with suppliers."

"She works in the office."

"Daniel handles the clients."

"We work from many countries."

SubjectVerbExample
I / You / We / Theywork (no s)We work with suppliers.
He / She / Itwork + sShe works in the office.
Negativedo not / does notWe do not work in one office.

Only he / she / it add an s. / So he/she/it ganha "s".

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"Daniel work with clients."

✓ RIGHT

"Daniel works with clients."

✗ WRONG

"We not work in one office."

✓ RIGHT

"We do not work in one office."

Exactly. I love my new role. Thank you, Daniel!
