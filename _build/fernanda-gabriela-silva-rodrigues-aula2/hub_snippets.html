Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Role -- funcao / papel (o que voce faz no trabalho)
"My role is in the trading office." (Minha funcao e no escritorio.)
Office -- escritorio (lugar de trabalho)
"I work in the trading office." (Trabalho no escritorio.)
Remote -- remoto (trabalho de casa, a distancia)
"My team is remote." (Minha equipe e remota.)
Global -- global (do mundo todo)
"We work with global clients." (Clientes globais.)
Supplier -- fornecedor (quem vende o cafe para a Sucafina)
"We work with coffee suppliers." (Fornecedores de cafe.)
Export -- exportar / exportacao (vender para outros paises)
"I help with export contracts." (Contratos de exportacao.)
Commodity -- commodity (produto basico, como o cafe)
"Coffee is a global commodity." (O cafe e uma commodity.)
Agriculture -- agricultura (cultivo no campo)
"Coffee comes from agriculture." (O cafe vem da agricultura.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

I work at Sucafina. My role is in the trading office. I work with global suppliers, and I help with export contracts. Daniel works with me. He handles the new clients. My team is remote, so we work from many countries. Coffee is a global commodity, and it comes from agriculture.

1. Why do we say "I work" but "Daniel works"? (Por que "works" com Daniel?)
A Because Daniel is far away. (Porque ele esta longe.)
B Because he / she / it adds "s" to the verb. (He/she/it leva "s".)
C Because it is the past. (Porque e passado.)
2. "My team is remote." What does this mean? (O que significa remote?)
A They work in one office together. (Juntos num escritorio.)
B They work from many places, far apart. (De varios lugares.)
C They do not work. (Eles nao trabalham.)
3. What is a "supplier"? (O que e um supplier?)
A A company that sells coffee to Sucafina. (Quem vende cafe para a Sucafina.)
B A coffee drink. (Uma bebida.)
C An office room. (Uma sala.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Simple

Grammar

Como dizer o que voce e os outros fazem (present simple). / How to say what people do.

Subject / SujeitoVerb / VerboExample / Exemplo
I / You / We / Theywork (sem "s")I work with suppliers. (Eu trabalho.)
He / She / Itworks (com "s")She works in the office. (Ela trabalha.)
Negative / Negativodo not / does not + verboWe do not work in one office. (Nao trabalhamos.)
Key idea / Ideia-chaveSo he / she / it ganha "s" no verbo. / Only he/she/it adds "s".

Etapa 4: Quiz Situacional

Quiz

B "My role is in the trading office. I work with suppliers."
C "I office trading role."
You describe Daniel's job. You say: (Voce fala do trabalho do Daniel.)
A "Daniel work with clients."
B "Daniel works with clients."
C "Daniel working clients."
You explain your remote team. You say: (Voce explica a equipe remota.)
