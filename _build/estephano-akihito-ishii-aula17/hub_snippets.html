Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Computer -- the machine you use to work and study
"There is a computer on my desk." (computador)
Screen -- the flat part you look at to see images
"There is a big screen on my desk." (tela)
Keyboard -- you press the keys on it to type
"There is a keyboard in front of the screen." (teclado)
Mouse -- you move it to click on the screen
"There is a mouse next to the keyboard." (mouse)
Cable -- it connects two devices together
"There are two cables behind the screen." (cabo)
Charger -- you use it to give power to your phone
"There is one charger for my phone." (carregador)
Headphones -- you wear them to listen on a call
"There are headphones for my video calls." (fones de ouvido)
Folder -- you keep your files inside it on the computer
"There is a folder with all my projects." (pasta)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- There is / There are

GRAMMAR

Como usar There is e There are. / How to use There is and There are.

Form / FormaUse / UsoExample
There isUMA coisa (singular)There is a computer.
There areDUAS ou mais coisas (plural)There are two cables.
There is no...NegativoThere is no printer.
Is there...? / Are there...?PerguntaIs there a mouse? Are there cables?
Atenção: conte as coisas. UMA = is; DUAS ou mais = are. NUNCA diga "There is two cables".
