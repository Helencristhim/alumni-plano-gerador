Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

These are the key words for this lesson. Tap Listen to hear the pronunciation.

Recap -- to say the main points again at the end
"Let me recap the decisions before we finish." (Recapitular / resumir)
Action item -- a task someone must do after the meeting
"We have three action items today." (Tarefa / item de ação)
Assign -- to give a task to a person
"I will assign this task to Karen." (Atribuir / designar)
Owner -- the person responsible for one task
"Karen is the owner of the report." (Responsável)
Follow up -- to check again later if a task is done
"I will follow up with you on Tuesday." (Dar seguimento / acompanhar)
Next steps -- the things we will do after the meeting
"These are our next steps for the week." (Próximos passos)
Takeaway -- the most important thing to remember
"The main takeaway is to start in October." (Conclusão / ponto principal)
Confirm -- to say something is true and sure
"Can you confirm the budget by Monday?" (Confirmar)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Will + Verb & By + Day

GRAMMAR

Para atribuir tarefas você usa duas estruturas: WILL + verbo base mostra um compromisso, e BY + um dia mostra o prazo. / To assign tasks you use two structures: WILL + base verb shows a commitment, and BY + a day shows the deadline.

Função / FunctionPatternExample
Atribuir tarefa / Assignperson + will + base verbKaren will send the report.
Definir prazo / Deadline... by + day... by Friday.
Perguntar o responsável / Ask ownerWho will + base verb ...?Who will confirm the budget?
Oferecer-se / OfferI will. / I'll do it.I will. I'll do it by Monday.

Key point / Ponto-chave: use will + verbo base para um compromisso (Karen will send, nunca "will sends"), e by + um dia para o prazo (by Friday = na sexta ou antes). Pergunte o responsável com Who will...? / Use will + base verb for a commitment, and by + a day for the deadline. By means on that day or before.

Aula 13 -- Closing the Meeting

Tema da aula: closing a meeting -- recap decisions and assign action items (who does what by when), with will + by (Karen will send the report by Friday / Who will be the owner? / I will follow up). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.
