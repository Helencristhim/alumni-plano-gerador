Aula 14 -- At the Restaurant

Tema da aula: pedir comida e bebida em um restaurante ("Could I have...?") e usar a / some / any. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
"Chef's Table" -- the restaurant scenes

A beautiful series about food and restaurants. Watch the dining scenes and listen for restaurant words: menu, dish, waiter, drink, and dessert.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote toda palavra de restaurante que você ouvir (menu, dish, dessert, bill).

Podcast
"Travel English" -- At the Restaurant (beginner episode)

A slow, clear podcast for beginners. Listen to a traveler order dinner and practice "Could I have...?" with a, some, and any (a coffee, some water, any dessert).

Dica: ouça duas vezes. Na segunda, repita em voz alta "Could I have the fish, please?" e "Could I have some water?".

YouTube
"Ordering Food in a Restaurant -- English Conversation" (English with subtitles)

A short, simple video of a real restaurant order. A customer reads the menu, says "Could I have the fish, please?", asks for some water, and asks for the bill at the end.

Dica: pause depois de cada fala do waiter e repita. Foque nas frases "Could I have...?" e "Do you have any dessert?".
