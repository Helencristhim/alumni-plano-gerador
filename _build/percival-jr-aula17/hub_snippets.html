Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

These are the key words for this lesson. Tap Listen to hear the pronunciation.

Complaint -- when a customer says something is wrong
"We received a complaint about the late order." (Reclamação)
Acknowledge -- to confirm you received an email or message
"Thank you for your email. We acknowledge your complaint." (Confirmar o recebimento)
Apologize -- to say you are sorry for a problem
"We apologize for the late delivery." (Pedir desculpas)
Inconvenience -- a problem that causes trouble or delay
"We are sorry for the inconvenience." (Transtorno / incômodo)
Resolve -- to fix a problem completely
"We will resolve this problem by Friday." (Resolver)
Replacement -- a new item that takes the place of a damaged one
"We will send a replacement part this week." (Reposição / item de substituição)
Refund -- money given back to the customer
"He did not ask for a full refund." (Reembolso)
Delivery -- when the order arrives at the customer
"The delivery arrived two days late." (Entrega)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Apologize & Offer a Solution

GRAMMAR

Numa resposta a um cliente você pede desculpas com We apologize for / We are sorry for + substantivo (the delay, the inconvenience) e oferece a solução com We will + verbo base ou We can offer + substantivo. Depois de "will" usamos o verbo base, sem "to". / In a reply to a customer you apologize with We apologize for / We are sorry for + a noun, and you offer the solution with We will + the base verb or We can offer + a noun.

Função / FunctionPatternExample
Pedir desculpas / ApologizeWe apologize for + nounWe apologize for the delay.
Desculpa mais suave / Softer apologyWe are sorry for + nounWe are sorry for the inconvenience.
Prometer ação / Promise an actionWe will + base verbWe will resolve this by Friday.
Oferecer solução / Offer a solutionWe can offer + nounWe can offer a replacement part.

Key point / Ponto-chave: dizemos apologize for + substantivo (não "apologize about") e, depois de will, usamos o verbo base: send, resolve, offer (não "will to send"). / We say apologize for + a noun, and after will we use the base verb.
