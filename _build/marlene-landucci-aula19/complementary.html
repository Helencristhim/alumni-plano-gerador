Aula 19 -- Calling for Help

Tema da aula: chamar ajuda numa emergência ("This is an emergency", "Call the police!", "There is an accident"). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
"9-1-1" -- a simple emergency call scene

A drama about emergency calls. Watch a short scene where someone calls for help. Listen for words: emergency, accident, ambulance, police, hospital.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote toda palavra de emergência que você ouvir (emergency, police, ambulance).

Podcast
"Travel English" -- Emergencies & Calling for Help (beginner episode)

A slow, clear podcast for beginners. Listen to a traveler call an operator and practice "This is an emergency" and "Call an ambulance!".

Dica: ouça duas vezes. Na segunda, repita em voz alta "Call the police!" e "There is an accident".

YouTube
"Emergency English -- How to Call for Help" (English with subtitles)

A short, simple video of a real emergency call. A traveler says "This is an emergency", gives an address, and asks for an ambulance.

Dica: pause depois de cada fala do operador e repita. Foque nas frases "This is an emergency" e "What is your address?".
