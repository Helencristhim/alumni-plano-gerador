Lesson 16 -- At the Airport -- Part 1

Series
Peppa Pig -- "The Airport" / "Flying on Holiday"

A short, slow episode at the airport: the family goes to check-in, shows their passports, takes a suitcase, and looks for the gate. Simple sentences and clear pronunciation -- perfect for today's airport words.

Watch with English subtitles. Each time you hear an airport word (passport, suitcase, gate, flight), say it out loud and point to it on the screen.

Podcast
Speak English Now -- "At the Airport"

A slow, clear podcast about the airport: how to do check-in, show your passport and boarding pass, and find your gate, in simple American English.

Repeat each phrase out loud. Then practice the check-in conversation: "Can I see your passport?" / "Here is my passport."

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "At the Airport: Check-in"

A short, practical lesson on today's language: the words you need at the airport and the check-in conversation, with clear examples of "Can I see your...?" and "Here is my...".

After watching, say the five Survival Card phrases out loud. Then imagine your own trip and describe what you do at check-in.
