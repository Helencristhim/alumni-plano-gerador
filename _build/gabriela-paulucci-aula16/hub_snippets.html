Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Passport -- a small book with your name and photo for travel
"Here is my passport." (Passaporte)
Ticket -- the paper that says you can take a flight
"I have my ticket to London." (Passagem)
Suitcase -- a big bag for your clothes on a trip
"My suitcase is very heavy." (Mala)
Gate -- the door where you get on your flight
"My flight leaves from gate twelve." (Portão)
Flight -- a trip in a plane from one city to another
"My flight is at three o'clock." (Voo)
Boarding pass -- the paper with your seat and gate number
"Here is your boarding pass." (Cartão de embarque)
Check-in -- the desk where you show your passport and bag
"I go to check-in first." (Check-in)
Departures -- the part of the airport where flights leave
"The sign says Departures." (Embarque / Saídas)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Can I see...? / Here is...

GRAMMAR

No check-in, o agente PEDE um documento com "Can I see your...?" e você MOSTRA o documento com "Here is my...". / At check-in, the agent ASKS for a document with "Can I see your...?" and you SHOW the document with "Here is my...".

Function / FunçãoStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Ask / PedirCan I see your + documento?Can I see your passport?
Show / MostrarHere is my + documento.Here is my ticket.
Give / EntregarHere is your + documento.Here is your boarding pass.
Thank / AgradecerThank you.Thank you very much.

Key point / Ponto-chave: use my para o seu documento (Here is my passport) e your para o documento da outra pessoa (Can I see your passport?). / Use my for your own document and your for the other person's.
