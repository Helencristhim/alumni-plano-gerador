"Giving Advice & Recommendations -- should, could, why don't you" -- BBC Learning English

A clear, practical lesson on exactly today's grammar: how to make a recommendation with "should", "could", "I'd recommend", and "why don't you". Watch for the base verb after the modal (no "to") and the "-ing" form after "recommend". Connection to Lesson 7: this is the grammar that makes your recommendations on calls sound clear and correct.

Tip: pause after each example and say it out loud, using a real recommendation you need to make to a customer this week.
