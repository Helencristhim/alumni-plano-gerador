Aula 10 -- Pre-class

Confirming Appointments

Confirme, mude ou cancele uma reuniao numa chamada: diga se voce esta disponivel, ocupada ou livre (available / busy / free) e use as preposicoes de tempo at / on / in -- "at three o'clock, on Monday, in the morning". Confirme com seguranca: "I confirm the meeting on Monday at three."

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

Dr. Carter calls to confirm our meeting. I ask, "Are you available on Monday?" He says he is busy in the morning. No problem. He is free at three o'clock, so we change the meeting to the afternoon. Finally I say, "I confirm the meeting on Monday at three." We do not cancel it. Everything is ready!

1. When is Dr. Carter busy? (Quando o Dr. Carter esta ocupado?)
A In the morning. (De manha.)
B At three o'clock. (As tres horas.)
C On Sunday. (No domingo.)
2. How do you say the new time? (Como dizer o novo horario?)
A "on three o'clock" (na tres horas)
B "at three o'clock" (as tres horas)
C "in three o'clock" (em tres horas)
3. What does Graziele do at the end? (O que a Graziele faz no fim?)
A She cancels the meeting. (Ela cancela a reuniao.)
B She confirms the meeting. (Ela confirma a reuniao.)
C She is busy. (Ela esta ocupada.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Prepositions at / on / in

Grammar

Como dizer o horario com at, on, in. / How to say the time with at, on, in.

Use / UseExample / Exemplo
at + horaat three o'clock (as tres horas)
on + dia/dataon Monday (na segunda-feira)
in + mesin May (em maio)
in + parte do diain the morning (de manha)
ConfirmarI confirm the meeting on Monday at three.

at = hora · on = dia / data · in = mes ou parte do dia. / Errado: "in Monday" → Certo: "on Monday". Errado: "on three o'clock" → Certo: "at three o'clock".
