Chapter 4: The Code

Relative Clauses

who · which · that · where · whose — defining and non-defining

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"AVRT is the company that makes the sensors."

"The founder who started it is Izabel."

"AVRT, which was founded in 2015, is growing."

"The city where AVRT is based is Valinhos."

Three clauses define which one we mean (no commas). One just adds extra information (commas). Which one, and why?

TypeWordsExample
Defining (no commas)who (people), which/that (things), where (places), whose (possession)The engineer who leads research.
Non-defining (commas)who or which only — never thatAVRT, which was founded in 2015, is growing.
Dropping the pronounonly in defining clauses, when it is the objectThe deal (that) we signed.
Language Focus

Which Word?

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"AVRT is the company what makes sensors."

✓ RIGHT

"AVRT is the company that makes sensors."

✗ WRONG

"AVRT, that was founded in 2015, is growing."

✓ RIGHT

"AVRT, which was founded in 2015, is growing."

✗ WRONG

"The engineer which leads research won two awards."

✓ RIGHT

"The engineer who leads research won two awards."

Never use "what" as a relative pronoun. In non-defining clauses (with commas) use "which" or "who", never "that". Use "who" for people and "which/that" for things.

Dialogue

Introducing AVRT to a New Board Member

A
Izabel, I am briefing a new board member tomorrow. In a few sentences, how would you describe AVRT?
I
AVRT, which I founded in Valinhos in 2015, is a company that designs wireless sensors. Our flagship product, the sensor that made our name, is used by factories that track equipment in real time.
A
Good. And who are the key people they should know?
I
The engineer who leads research, and whose designs won two awards, built the team. And there is a mentor who advised me early on and still shapes how we think.
A
And the market? Where does AVRT actually compete?
I
We serve a niche which most large firms ignore: clients who need cutting-edge sensors and the proprietary software that runs them. The headquarters where we operate sits just outside Valinhos.
A
And the competition? Who is the one to watch?
I
The incumbent that once dominated the segment, a firm whose prices were always lower, is the one we are catching. We recently opened a subsidiary, which handles North American sales, and our track record is what clients trust.
