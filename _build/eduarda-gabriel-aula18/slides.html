Chapter 3 — The Backshift

Reported Speech

Left: what was said live. Right: how the minutes record it. What shifts?

"The deal is on track" → She said the deal was on track.
"We will close on Friday" → He said they would close on Friday.
"We can proceed" → She said they could proceed.
"I agree with the terms" → He said he agreed with the terms.

Grammar Rule

Live vs Recorded

What was said (live)The minute (reported)
"The deal is on track.""He said the deal was on track."
"We will close on Friday.""She said they would close on Friday."
"We can proceed.""They said they could proceed."
"I agree with the resolution.""He said he agreed with the resolution."
Common Mistake

Watch Out!

"He says the deal is on track and we will close on Friday."
"He said the deal was on track and they would close on Friday."

Minutes are written after the meeting, so every quote backshifts: is → was, will → would, can → could.

Practice

Shift to the Past

"The deal is on track" → She said the deal ___was on track.

"The figures are final" → She said the figures ___were final.

Dialogue

The Minutes Review

J
Eduarda, did you start the minutes from this morning's board call?
E
Yes. I listed the attendees and noted the apologies from the two directors who were absent.
J
Good. Was there a quorum for the vote?
E
Yes. So I recorded the decision as a resolution. The chair said the deal was on track, so I wrote it in the past.
J
Exactly. And the action items — did each one get an owner?
E
Every action has an owner and a deadline. One item was not resolved, so I marked it as tabled for next week.
J
Perfect. How did you close it?
E
I wrote that the chair adjourned the meeting at noon. I will send you the draft before I circulate it.
Comprehension

About James

1. What does James ask about first?
Whether she started the minutes from the board call.
2. What does James check about the vote?
Whether there was a quorum.
3. What does James ask about the action items?
Whether each one got an owner.

Meeting: Vertex Deal — Board Call

Resolution 2: The CFO said they would close on Friday.

Actions:

• Lender confirmation — Owner: James — Due Friday.
