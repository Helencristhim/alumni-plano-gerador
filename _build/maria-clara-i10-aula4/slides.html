Chapter 4: The Code

The Present Continuous

I am working · she is arriving · are you presenting?

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I am traveling to Seoul this week. The delegation is arriving tomorrow."

"We are meeting the rector this afternoon. She is presenting the agenda."

"I am not working today. I am flying to Korea right now."

"Are you preparing the conference? Yes, I am."

Subject+ Yes- No? Question
II am workingI am not workingAm I working?
he / she / itShe is workingShe is not workingIs she working?
you / we / theyWe are workingWe are not workingAre we working?

Present continuous = be (am / is / are) + verb-ing, for something happening right now or around now (this week). Spelling: make → making (drop the e), sit → sitting (double the consonant).

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

"I working in Seoul now."
"I am working in Seoul now."
"She is arrive tomorrow."
"She is arriving tomorrow."

The present continuous always has two parts: be (am / is / are) + verb-ing — never just one.

Chapter 5: Arriving in Seoul

At the Airport

You just landed. Someone asks: "Are you here for the conference?" — and this time, you answer

Dialogue

Are You Here for the Conference?

D
Good afternoon, and welcome to Seoul. Are you here for the education conference?
M
Yes, good afternoon. I am arriving now for the conference. My name is Maria Clara.
D
Wonderful. I am the university liaison. The delegation is waiting in the lobby. Are you traveling alone?
M
Yes, I am traveling alone this time. I am speaking English on my own.
D
That is great. Right now, the team is preparing the conference room. What are you looking forward to most?
M
I am looking forward to presenting my work tomorrow. I am a little nervous, but I am ready.
D
You are doing very well. The rector is announcing the agenda in one hour. Are you participating in the round table too?
M
Yes, I am participating in the round table on Friday. Once in a while, I still forget a word, but I keep going.
D
That is the right attitude. The car is departing now. Let me help you with your suitcase.
M
Thank you very much. I am following you now.
