Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Dienane is at a restaurant. The waiter brings the menu. Dienane says: "Could I have the fish dish, please?" Then she asks: "Could you bring some water, please?" For dessert, she says: "Could I have the chocolate cake?" At the end, she asks: "Could I have the bill, please?" In English, to ask for a thing politely, we use Could I have + thing + please. To ask for an action, we use Could you + verb + please. Could is more polite than can.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Could I have...?

GRAMMAR

Em inglês, pedimos com educação usando Could I have (uma coisa) e Could you (uma ação). / In English, we ask politely with Could I have (a thing) and Could you (an action).

You want / Você querStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
uma coisaCould I have + coisa, please?Could I have the menu, please?
um pratoCould I have + prato, please?Could I have the fish, please?
uma açãoCould you + verbo, please?Could you bring water, please?
a contaCould I have + the bill, please?Could I have the bill, please?

Key point / Ponto-chave: para pedir uma coisa, use Could I have + coisa + please. Para pedir uma ação, use Could you + verbo + please. Could é mais educado que can. Sempre acrescente please.
