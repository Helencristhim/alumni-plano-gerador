Toolkit Summary

Four Tools, One Talk

TenseWe use it forExample
Present simpleroutines and factsI work in São Paulo.
Present perfectlife so far, experiencesI have lived here for years.
Past cont. + past simpletelling a storyI was working when it happened.
going to / willplans and predictionsNext year I 'm going to travel.

Key idea: a great "life in 3 minutes" moves through time -- your routine now, your story so far, and your plans ahead. Use one tool for each part.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I work here for ten years."
"I have worked here for ten years."

For something that started in the past and is still true now, use the present perfect (have + past participle), not the present simple.
