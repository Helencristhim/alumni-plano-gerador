Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o email curto e responda as perguntas. Ele junta as quatro ferramentas das aulas 11-14. (Read and answer.)

Hello Doctor Lewis,
On Monday I worked the morning shift. The team meeting was busy but good. Could you send me the agenda, please? I am writing to invite you to our next meeting.
Best regards, Graziele.

1. Which word is in the past? (Qual palavra esta no passado?)
A "worked" (trabalhou)
B "send" (enviar)
C "invite" (convidar)
2. Which line is the polite request? (Qual linha e o pedido educado?)
A "Best regards, Graziele." (Atenciosamente, Graziele.)
B "Could you send me the agenda, please?" (Voce poderia enviar a pauta?)
C "On Monday I worked." (Na segunda eu trabalhei.)
3. How does the email finish? (Como o email termina?)
A "Hey, bye!" (Ei, tchau!)
B "Best regards, Graziele." (Atenciosamente, Graziele.)
C "The meeting was good." (A reuniao foi boa.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Your Four Tools (Lessons 11-14)

Grammar

As quatro ferramentas das aulas 11 a 14, juntas. / Your four tools from Lessons 11-14.

Ferramenta / ToolExemplo / Example
1. Past (passado)"I worked late." -- was/were + verbo-ed (verb-ed)
2. Request (pedido)"Could you help me?" -- Can/Could you + verbo base, SEM "to" (no "to")
3. Connector (conector)"busy but good" -- and / but / because (e / mas / porque)
4. Email"Hello ... Best regards" -- saudacao + fechamento (greeting + closing)
Dica / TipLembre: passado leva -ed; pedido nao leva "to"; email comeca e termina educado. (Past takes -ed; requests have no "to"; emails open and close politely.)

Quatro ferramentas, quatro aulas -- voce ja usa todas. (Four tools, four lessons -- you already use them all.)
