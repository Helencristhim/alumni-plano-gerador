Aula 9 -- Gallery Night

Tema da aula: socializing at a professional event -- networking and small talk, plus an opinion toolkit that goes beyond "I think" (From my perspective..., I'd argue that..., I'm inclined to think..., What's your take on...?). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
"Emily in Paris" -- gallery openings & party scenes

The show is full of vernissages, launch parties, and social mixers where characters mingle, break the ice, and share quick opinions about art and brands. Great input for networking small talk and reacting to what you see.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote três formas diferentes de dar uma opinião (não só "I think") e duas frases de socialização (mingle, small talk, break the ice).

Podcast
The Art Newspaper -- "The Week in Art"

Curators, collectors, and critics discuss new exhibitions and give candid impressions of the work. Perfect input to review this lesson's vocabulary -- curator, collector, compelling, thought-provoking, impression -- and to hear opinions framed in many ways.

Dica: ouça um episódio e anote cada vez que alguém introduz uma opinião ("I'd argue that...", "From my perspective...", "I'm inclined to think...") e como convidam a opinião do outro.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- Giving Your Opinion

A short, clear guide to phrases for giving opinions at different strengths -- confident, neutral, and tentative -- and for agreeing and disagreeing politely. It also covers the classic mistakes "according to me" and "I am agree".

Dica: pause após cada exemplo e diga a mesma opinião de três formas -- forte, neutra e hesitante: "I'd argue that... / From my perspective... / I'm inclined to think...".
