Chapter 3: Numbers & Dates

In, On, At

in December · on March 15th · at 9 AM

Discover the Grammar

Listen and Notice

"The audit is in December."

"The deadline is on December 30th."

"The call is at 9 AM."

"In 2023, we audited 150 farms."

PrepositionUse it forExample
inmonths and yearsin December, in 2024
ondays and dateson Monday, on December 30th
atclock timesat 9 AM, at 3 PM

Date order (American): month + day + year -- December 30th, 2024. / A ordem e mes, dia, ano.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"The deadline is 30 of December."

✓ RIGHT

"The deadline is December 30th."

✗ WRONG

"We meet in Monday at December."

✓ RIGHT

"We meet on Monday in December."

American dates go month first: December 30th. Use on for a day, in for a month. / Data: mes primeiro. on para dia, in para mes.
