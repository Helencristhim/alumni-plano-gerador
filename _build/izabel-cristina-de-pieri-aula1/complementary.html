Aula 1 -- Mapping Your Executive Voice

Series
Industry (HBO) -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Induction"

Young professionals introduce themselves, state their roles, and describe their responsibilities in a high-pressure finance world. Notice how they say what they are in charge of, who they report to, and what they deal with on a daily basis -- exactly your Thursday-call vocabulary.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Note 3 expressions the characters use to describe their roles and responsibilities.
