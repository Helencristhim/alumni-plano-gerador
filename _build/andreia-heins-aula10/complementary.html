Lesson 10 -- Block 1 Review: My Professional Identity

Series
The Good Doctor -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Burnt Food"

A young surgeon introduces himself to a hospital board and presents his story: who he is, how he started, and what he wants to accomplish. Listen for how he uses different tenses to present his professional identity.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down five sentences he uses about himself and label the tense of each (present, past, present perfect, present continuous, future).

Podcast
HBR IdeaCast -- "How to Introduce Yourself with Confidence"

The hosts explain how to give a strong self-presentation: open with an overview, highlight your expertise and strengths, and end with your aspiration. Full of natural language for presenting your professional identity.

Note three tips for introducing yourself. Then record a two-minute self-presentation, using at least four new words from today, such as introduce, overview, expertise, and aspiration.

YouTube
English with Lucy -- "Talk About Yourself in English (All Tenses)"

A clear, practical lesson on exactly today's grammar: which tense to use for who you are, how you started, what you have done, what you are doing now, and where you are going.

After watching, record your three-minute self-presentation. Use one sentence in each tense, highlight one strength, and summarize your professional identity at the end.
