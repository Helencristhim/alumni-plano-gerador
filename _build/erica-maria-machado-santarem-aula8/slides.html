Chapter 3

The Map Code

Imperatives · prepositions of place

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"Turn left at the corner."

"Go straight and take the bus."

"The bank is next to the station."

"The cafe is in front of the office."

ToolExample
imperative (order)Turn left · Go straight · Take the bus (no subject)
next to / nearthe bank is next to the station
in front of / behindthe cafe is in front of the office
on the cornerthe shop is on the corner
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"Turn to left."

✓ RIGHT

"Turn left."

✗ WRONG

"Go to straight."

✓ RIGHT

"Go straight."

✗ WRONG

"It is in front the bank."

✓ RIGHT

"It is in front of the bank."

An imperative is just the verb: Turn, Go, Take. No "to" before left, right or straight. Remember the "of" in "in front of".
