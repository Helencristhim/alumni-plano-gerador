Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Would like to & Hope to

Grammar

Para falar de um sonho ou desejo para o futuro, use would like to, hope to ou would love to e depois um verbo no infinitivo (to + verbo): "I would like to travel", "I hope to learn". NUNCA esqueça o "to" e NUNCA use -ing depois. / To talk about a future dream or wish, use would like to, hope to, or would love to + to + base verb ("I would like to travel"). Always keep the "to"; never use -ing after it.

Estrutura / StructureEnglishPortuguês
would like to + verbI would like to travel.Eu gostaria de viajar.
hope to + verbI hope to learn English.Eu espero aprender inglês.
would love to + verbI would love to live abroad.Eu adoraria morar no exterior.
wish to + verbI wish to start a business.Eu desejo abrir um negócio.
