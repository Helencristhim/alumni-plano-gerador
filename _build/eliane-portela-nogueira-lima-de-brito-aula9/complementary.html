Series
The Newsroom -- Season 1, Episode 1

A drama set in a TV newsroom, where the entire job is relaying accurately what people said. You will hear constant reported speech: "He said that...", "She told us that...", "They confirmed that...". Watch how professionals recap, flag, and paraphrase under pressure. Direct connection to Lesson 9: reporting back what was said, without changing the meaning.

Tip: pick one scene and write down three reported-speech sentences you hear, then turn them back into the speaker's direct words.

Podcast
HBR IdeaCast -- Harvard Business Review on management and communication

Harvard Business Review's weekly podcast, where the hosts constantly relay and recap what researchers and executives said: "She explained that...", "They found that...", "He argued that...". You will hear reported speech and reporting verbs used naturally by native speakers, all in a business context. Direct connection to Lesson 9: the language of relaying and recapping decisions at work.

Tip: after one episode, recap it to a colleague in three sentences using "He said that...", "She explained that...", and "They found that...".
