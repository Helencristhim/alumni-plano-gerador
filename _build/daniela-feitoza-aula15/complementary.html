Aula 15 — Setting the Scene (Past Continuous)

Conteúdos para reforçar como narrar um incidente — past continuous para a cena, past simple para o evento. Marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
Mr. Robot — any server outage or incident-response scene

The characters narrate what was happening when systems went down. Listen for the scene-and-surprise pattern: "I was monitoring... when it crashed" — the exact structure from Lesson 15.

Dica: anote uma frase com was/were + -ing e o evento em past simple que a interrompe.

Podcast
The Downtime Project — engineers tell the story of real outages

Each episode walks through an incident: what the team was doing, what suddenly broke, how they recovered and the root cause. A perfect model of the past continuous setting the scene.

Dica: pause e repita cada frase com "while we were..." que você ouvir.

YouTube
"Past Continuous vs Past Simple — when and while" (English lesson)

A short, clear video on using the past continuous for the scene and the past simple for the interrupting event. Maps directly onto Daniela’s outage review with Marcus in Lesson 15.

Dica: depois do vídeo, narre um incidente seu em 4 frases, usando past continuous e past simple.
