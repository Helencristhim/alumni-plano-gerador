Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

Introduce -- to tell someone your name for the first time
"Let me introduce myself. I am Helen." (Apresentar)
Brand -- the name and personality of a company
"Our brand is young and modern." (Marca)
Campaign -- a plan of ads and posts to sell an idea
"The new campaign starts in May." (Campanha)

"James is a partner from New York." (Parceiro)
Audience -- the group of people you want to reach
"Our audience is young professionals." (Público)
Launch -- to put a new product in the market
"We launch the new app in June." (Lançamento / lançar)
Business card -- a small card with your name and company
"Here is my business card." (Cartão de visita)
Goal -- what you want to achieve
"Our goal is to grow the brand." (Meta / objetivo)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Quem você é

GRAMMAR

As três ferramentas da apresentação. / The three tools of an introduction.

Structure / EstruturaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
I am / he is / we areNome, cidade, profissãoI am Helen. I am from São Paulo.
I work / he worksSeu trabalho e áreaI work in marketing. He works in sales.
we have / she hasProjetos e coisasWe have a new campaign.

Atenção: idade usa BE ("I am 29 years old") — nunca "I have 29 years". E he/she/it ganha o -s mágico: works, has, is.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

Schedule -- the plan of your day, hour by hour
"My schedule is full on Mondays." (Agenda)
Deadline -- the day the work must be ready
"The deadline for the report is Friday." (Prazo)
Task -- one piece of work to do
"My first task is to check the emails." (Tarefa)
Report -- a document with numbers and results
"I write the campaign report every month." (Relatório)
Meeting -- when the team talks together, at a time
"We have a team meeting at nine." (Reunião)
Priority -- the most important thing first
"The launch is our priority this week." (Prioridade)
Inbox -- where your emails arrive
"My inbox is full every morning." (Caixa de entrada)
Break -- a short pause with a coffee
"I take a break at noon." (Pausa / intervalo)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- O ritmo do dia

GRAMMAR

Advérbios de frequência: com que frequência você faz cada coisa. / How often you do each thing.

AdvérbioFrequênciaExample / Exemplo
always100% (sempre)I always check my inbox at eight.
usually / often80% / 60% (geralmente / frequentemente)We usually meet at nine.
sometimes40% (às vezes)I sometimes work late.
never0% (nunca)I never skip my break.

Posição: ANTES do verbo principal (I always check) — mas DEPOIS do to be (I am always busy).
