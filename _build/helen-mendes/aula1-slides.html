Chapter 3: The Code

Who You Are

I am · I work · We have

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I am Helen. I am from São Paulo."

"I work in marketing. I am a manager."

"James is a partner. He works in New York."

"We have a new campaign this year."

StructureUseExample
I am / he is / we areName, city, professionI am a marketing manager.
I work / he worksYour job and areaI work in marketing. He works in sales.
we have / she hasThings and projectsWe have a new campaign.

he / she / it adds the magic -s: works, has (irregular), is.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"I have 38 years."

✓ RIGHT

"I am 38 years old."

✗ WRONG

"She have a new campaign."

✓ RIGHT

"She has a new campaign."

Meeting James

Dialogue

Hi, I Don't Think We've Met

J
Hi! I don't think we've met. I'm James, from the New York office.
H
Nice to meet you, James! Let me introduce myself: I am Helen Mendes.
J
Great to meet you, Helen. What do you do?
H
I am a marketing manager. I work with the brand team here in São Paulo.
J
Excellent! I work with digital campaigns. Are you here for the full summit?
H
Yes, I am! We have a new launch this year, so I am here to learn.
J
A launch! What is the audience for the new product?
H
Young professionals. Our goal is to grow the brand in Brazil.
J
We should talk more. Here is my business card.
H
Thank you! And here is mine. It is a pleasure, James.
