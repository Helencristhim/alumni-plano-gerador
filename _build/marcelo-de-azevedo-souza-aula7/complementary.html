Aula 7 -- Navigating Real Places

Series
The White Lotus (HBO) -- Season 1, Episode 1

The entire show lives at a hotel front desk: guests arrive tired, reservations go wrong, and everyone negotiates for the room, the upgrade, and the apology they think they deserve. It is the exact world of this lesson -- watch how guests complain (well and badly) and how the staff stay calm and polite. Connection to Lesson 7: listen for requests, complaints, and the difference between the guest who shouts and the guest who asks nicely.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down one polite request and one complaint you hear, and rewrite the complaint the calm, firm way.

Podcast
Zero To Travel Podcast -- any episode

Long, relaxed conversations about real travel: the trips that went wrong, the problems solved on the road, and the people met along the way. Clear, natural English at a comfortable pace, full of the exact language you practiced tonight -- requests, plans, and what you would do if things did not go as planned.

Tip: pick one episode. Pause twice and say out loud one polite request (Is there any chance you could...?) you would make in that situation.

Hotel Problems in English: How to Complain Politely

A short, practical video that walks through a hotel stay where everything goes wrong -- the wrong room, the noise, the missing booking -- and shows exactly how to complain politely and get the problem solved. It is this lesson in ten minutes: real requests, real solutions, clear English.
