Aula 14 — If This, Then That

Series
Race Across the World (BBC) — real plans and what-ifs

A travel show where real people plan, prepare and make quick decisions. Perfect to hear people say what they will do if a plan changes, with if, unless and as long as.

Dica: anote duas frases com "if ..., I will ..." sobre os planos das pessoas. / Note two sentences with "if ..., I will ..." about the people's plans.

Podcast
All Ears English — "First Conditional" episodes

Clear, friendly English about how to talk about real, possible situations with if + present, will + verb. A great way to hear this structure used naturally in real conversation.

Dica: ouça uma vez só e anote três frases com "if", "unless" ou "as long as". / Listen once and note three sentences with "if", "unless" or "as long as".
