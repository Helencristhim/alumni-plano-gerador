Aula 16 — What Would You Do?

Series
The Good Place — characters imagining "what if" scenarios

A warm, funny show full of people imagining different choices and what they would do. Great to hear the second conditional ("If I were...", "I would...") used naturally.

Dica: anote duas frases com "if + past, would". / Note two sentences with "if + past, would".

Podcast
All Ears English — "The second conditional: what would you do?"

Clear, friendly English about imagining different situations and using "would". A great way to hear "if I had", "I would" and "I would rather" in real conversation.

Dica: ouça uma vez só e anote três frases imaginárias. / Listen once and note three imaginary sentences.
