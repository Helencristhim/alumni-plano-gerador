Stage 1.1: Check-in & Boarding Vocabulary

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Estas palavras ajudam voce no balcao de check-in e na hora de embarcar.

Aisle seat -- assento do corredor (no aviao)
"I prefer an aisle seat, so I can stand up easily."
Window seat -- assento da janela (no aviao)
"I chose a window seat to see the clouds."
Overhead bin -- compartimento acima do assento
"You have to put your small bag in the overhead bin."
Terminal -- terminal (predio do aeroporto)
"My flight leaves from Terminal 2."
Layover -- conexao (parada entre dois voos)
"My trip has a short layover in Madrid."
Connecting flight -- voo de conexao (o segundo aviao)
"I have a connecting flight to Lisbon after Madrid."
Priority boarding -- embarque prioritario (entrar antes)
"With priority boarding, you board first."
Boarding group -- grupo de embarque (seu numero)
"I am in boarding group three, so I wait for my turn."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia a situacao e responda as perguntas.

Roberto arrives at the airport early because he has to check in two hours before his flight. At the counter, he chooses a window seat. He has to check his big bag, but he does not have to pay for it. He can put his small bag in the overhead bin. His trip has a short layover in Madrid, so he has a connecting flight. He does not have to collect his bag there. He only needs to wait at the gate in the terminal for his boarding group.

1. Why does Roberto arrive early?
A Because the flight is cheap.
B Because he has to check in two hours before.
C Because he likes the terminal.
2. Does he have to pay for his big bag?
A Yes, he has to pay a lot.
B No, he does not have to pay for it.
C No, he does not check it.
3. What does he have to do during the layover?
A Collect his bag in Madrid.

C Leave the terminal and go to a hotel.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Have To & Need To

Grammar

Para falar de regras e coisas necessarias, use have to e need to + verbo (I have to show my pass). Para he/she/it, use has to e needs to (She has to check her bag). Do not have to quer dizer que NAO e necessario, voce pode escolher (You do not have to pay). Perguntas comecam com Do / Does ... have to? Atencao: depois de "have to" sempre vem o verbo na forma base (have to show, NUNCA have show). / Use have to and need to for necessary things (a rule). For he/she/it, use has to / needs to. Do not have to means it is NOT necessary. Questions start with Do / Does ... have to?

Forma / FormExemplo / ExampleUso / Use
I/you/we have to + verboI have to show my pass.e necessario (regra)
he/she/it has to + verboShe has to check her bag.necessario, 3a pessoa
do not have to + verboYou do not have to pay.NAO e necessario (escolha)
Do I have to...?Do I have to check this bag?perguntar sobre uma regra
need to + verboI need to wait at the gate.e necessario (igual a have to)
