Chapter 3: The Rules Code

Have To & Need To

"I have to..." / "I need to..."

Discover the Code

Listen and Find the Pattern

"I have to show my boarding pass at the gate."

"You need to arrive two hours early."

"You do not have to pay for that bag."

"Do I have to check this bag?"

FormExampleUse
have to / need to + verbI have to show my pass.it is necessary (a rule)
has to (he / she / it)She has to check her bag.necessary, third person
do not have to + verbYou do not have to pay.it is NOT necessary (free choice)
Do I have to...?Do I have to check this bag?asking about a rule

Use have to and need to for things that are necessary (a rule). For he/she/it, use has to and needs to. Do not have to means it is NOT necessary — you can choose. Questions start with Do / Does ... have to?

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"I have show my passport."

✓ RIGHT

"I have to show my passport."

✗ WRONG

"I has to wait at the gate."

✓ RIGHT

"I have to wait at the gate."

Practice

Complete the Rule

Say it first, then click to check

"At check-in, I (necessary: show) ___have to show my passport."
"She (necessary: check) ___has to check her big bag."
"You (not necessary: print) ___do not have to print your boarding pass."
"(question: I / wait) ___Do I have to wait for my boarding group?"
"For my layover, I (necessary: stay) ___need to stay in the terminal."

Dialogue

Roberto Checks In

A
Good afternoon. May I see your passport, please? Do you want a window seat or an aisle seat?
R
Here it is. A window seat, please. Do I have to check this bag?
A
The big one, yes. But you do not have to pay for it. You can put the small bag in the overhead bin.
R
Perfect. I have a connecting flight in Madrid. Do I have to collect my bag there?
A
No, you do not. It is a short layover, so you only need to wait at the gate in the terminal.
R
Great. So I have to listen for my boarding group and keep my boarding pass ready. Thank you!
Listening

The Boarding Call

Listen first. Sound first, no text.

0:000:00
1. Where do you have to put a large bag?
In the overhead bin above your seat.
2. What do you have to have ready at the gate?
Your boarding pass.
3. Do passengers with a connecting flight have to collect their luggage here?
No, they do not have to.
Comprehension

About the Counter

1. Does Roberto have to pay for his big bag?
No, he does not have to pay for it.
2. Where does the agent say he can put the small bag?
In the overhead bin.
3. Does he have to collect his bag during the layover in Madrid?
No. He only needs to wait at the gate in the terminal.
