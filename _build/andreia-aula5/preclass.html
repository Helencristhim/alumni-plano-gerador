Aula 05 -- Pre-class

Talking About Experience -- Have You Ever...?

Present perfect para experiência profissional: procedure, certification, simulation, mentor, hands-on, milestone, supervise, cutting-edge. Estruturas: "Have you ever...?", "I have + particípio (+ número)", "I have never...". Missão: apresentar sua experiência para o Dr. Adams.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear the pronunciation.

Procedure -- a medical operation or formal process
"I have assisted in fifty robotic procedures." (Procedimento)
Certification -- an official document that proves your skill
"I have completed two international certifications." (Certificação)
Simulation -- a safe practice of a real situation
"New nurses start with simulations." (Simulação)
Mentor -- an experienced person who guides you
"Dr. Ferraz has been my mentor for years."
Hands-on -- practical, doing the real work yourself
"The training is very hands-on." (Prático / mão na massa)
Milestone -- an important moment of progress
"My first robotic case was a milestone." (Marco)
Supervise -- to watch and guide other people's work
"I have supervised many simulation sessions."
Cutting-edge -- the most modern and advanced of its kind
"Robotic surgery is cutting-edge technology." (De ponta)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

GRAMMAR

Present perfect para experiência: have/has + particípio. A carreira inteira, sem data. / Your whole career, no specific date.

Structure / EstruturaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
Have you ever + particípio?Perguntar sobre a carreira todaHave you ever supervised a team?
I have + particípio (+ número)Sua experiência, com númerosI have completed two certifications.
I have never + particípioNunca, até agoraI have never presented abroad.
Data específica? Past simple!Em 2019, ontem, na sexta...I completed the course in 2023.
