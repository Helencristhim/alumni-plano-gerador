Chapter 3: The Code

Grammar Discovery

Which is a routine, and which is happening now?

I usually work in the ICU.
This month, I am training on the new robot.
Right now, we are implementing the Da Vinci system.
These days, the program is expanding.
TenseUseExample
Present Simpleroutine / factI work in the ICU.
Present Continuousnow / temporary / changingI am training this month.
Signal wordsnow, right now, at the moment, these daysRight now, we are implementing...
Common Mistake

Watch Out!

"At the moment, I adapt to the new role."
"At the moment, I am adapting to the new role."

For something happening now or temporarily, use the present continuous (am/is/are + -ing).
