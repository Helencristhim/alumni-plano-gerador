Aula 1 -- Introducing Yourself & Your Role

Series
Modern Family -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

The characters introduce themselves and explain who they are and what they are doing right now — perfect natural present simple vs present continuous. You already love this show, so use it to learn. Connection to Lesson 1: real self-introductions and the difference between routine ("I work...") and current actions ("I'm doing...").

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Note 3 times a character says what they are doing right now (am/is/are + verb-ing).

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Introductions & Talking About Your Job"

Short, clear episodes about real professional English. Listen to the natural phrases people use on a first call to say their role, what they are responsible for, and what they are working on. Connection to Lesson 1: the exact expressions you need with your global teams.

Tip: listen twice. Write down 5 phrases you can use to introduce yourself and your role on a call.

YouTube
"Present Simple vs Present Continuous" -- BBC Learning English

A clear, practical lesson on the exact contrast from today: routine duties (present simple) versus what is happening now (present continuous). Connection to Lesson 1: reinforces the grammar that will make your status updates correct and confident on every call.

Tip: pause and say one sentence about your routine and one about your current project after each example.
