Chapter 4: In Context

Your 60-Second Pitch Script

ELEVATOR PITCH — EDUARDO CHIBA

Hi, I'm Eduardo Chiba, Head of B2B at Quinto Andar. We connect real estate agencies to millions of renters through a digital marketplace. Our platform is faster and more scalable than traditional agencies — agencies can list properties in twenty-four hours. We have grown our partner network by forty percent this year, well above the industry benchmark. Could we schedule a call to discuss a partnership?

Which sentence is the value proposition?
"We connect real estate agencies to millions of renters through a digital marketplace."
Which sentence is the proof (results)?
"We have grown our partner network by forty percent this year."
What is the ask at the end?
To schedule a call to discuss a partnership

Chapter 5: Practice

Build It Block by Block

Block 1 — Say who you are and your role.
"I'm Eduardo Chiba, Head of B2B at Quinto Andar. We connect agencies to millions of renters."
Block 2 — State your value proposition.
"Our value proposition is speed: agencies can list properties in twenty-four hours."
Block 2 — Say what makes you better (comparative).
"Our platform is faster and more scalable than traditional agencies."
Block 4 — Give the proof (present perfect + data).
"We have grown our partner network by forty percent this year, well above the benchmark."
Block 3 — Make the ask (polite request).
"Could we schedule a call this week to discuss a partnership?"
Close — Offer a follow-up.
"I'll send a follow-up email today with all the details."
