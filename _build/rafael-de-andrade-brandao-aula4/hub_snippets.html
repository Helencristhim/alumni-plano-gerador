Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Small talk -- easy, friendly conversation about light topics
"We made small talk before the meeting." (Conversa informal)
Weather -- the condition of the air outside, like sun or rain
"The weather is sunny and warm today." (Clima)
Weekend -- Saturday and Sunday, when most people rest
"What are your plans for the weekend?" (Fim de semana)
Traffic -- many cars on the road, often moving slowly
"The traffic was heavy this morning." (Trânsito)
Hobby -- an activity you enjoy in your free time
"My favorite hobby is playing tennis." (Passatempo)
Conference -- a large professional event where people meet and learn
"We are at a technology conference." (Conferência)
Networking -- meeting new people to build professional contacts
"She is doing some networking at the event." (Fazer contatos)
Trip -- a short journey to a place and back
"How was your trip to the conference?" (Viagem)
Coffee break -- a short rest to drink coffee and relax
"Let's talk during the coffee break." (Pausa para o café)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

It is a busy morning at the Tech Connect conference. Right now, Rafael is enjoying a coffee break between sessions. He is making small talk with a new contact named Diana. They are talking about the weather and their weekend plans. Diana is doing some networking because she loves meeting new people. Rafael says his favorite hobby is tennis. The morning traffic was heavy, but now he is calm and ready. After a long trip to the city, he is happy to relax and talk.

1. What is Rafael doing right now?
A He is sleeping at home.
B He is enjoying a coffee break and making small talk.
C He is driving in heavy traffic.
2. Which sentence is in the present continuous?
A "They are talking about the weekend."
B "He likes tennis."
C "She met new people yesterday."
3. Why is Diana doing some networking?
A Because she loves meeting new people.
B Because she is tired.
C Because the traffic is heavy.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Continuous

GRAMMAR

Usado para acoes acontecendo agora. / Used for actions happening right now.

Form / FormaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
am/is/are + verb-ingAção acontecendo agora / Action happening nowI am meeting a new contact.
is/are not + verb-ingNegativa / Not happening nowShe is not working today.
be + subject + verb-ingPergunta sim/não / Yes-No questionAre you enjoying the event?
Wh + be + subject + verb-ingPergunta aberta / Wh- questionWhat are you doing this weekend?
Key point / Ponto-chaveSempre use o verbo "be" (am/is/are) antes do verbo com -ing. Em perguntas, "be" vem antes do sujeito.

Lesson 4 -- Small Talk That Opens Doors

Series
Ted Lasso -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

Ted is a master of small talk. Watch how he breaks the ice with strangers using friendly comments about the weather, simple questions, and warm energy. Notice how easy openers build trust fast.

Write down every small talk opener you hear. Pause and repeat each one aloud, then create your own version for a conference.

Podcast
The Art of Charm -- Episode: "How to Make Small Talk and Network"

Practical tips on starting conversations at professional events: good openers, easy topics like the weather and weekends, and how to keep a chat going. Listen for the new words: small talk, networking, conference, and hobby.

Note 3 openers the hosts suggest. Practice asking each one as a present continuous question, like "What are you working on?"

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Small Talk: Making Conversation in English"

A short, clear lesson on safe small talk topics and friendly questions. Perfect for breaking the ice before a meeting and for practicing the present continuous in real situations.
