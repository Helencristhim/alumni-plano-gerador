Lesson 4 -- Small Talk That Opens Doors

Series
Ted Lasso -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

Ted is a master of small talk. Watch how he breaks the ice with strangers using friendly comments about the weather, simple questions, and warm energy. Notice how easy openers build trust fast.

Write down every small talk opener you hear. Pause and repeat each one aloud, then create your own version for a conference.

Podcast
The Art of Charm -- Episode: "How to Make Small Talk and Network"

Practical tips on starting conversations at professional events: good openers, easy topics like the weather and weekends, and how to keep a chat going. Listen for the new words: small talk, networking, conference, and hobby.

Note 3 openers the hosts suggest. Practice asking each one as a present continuous question, like "What are you working on?"

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Small Talk: Making Conversation in English"

A short, clear lesson on safe small talk topics and friendly questions. Perfect for breaking the ice before a meeting and for practicing the present continuous in real situations.

After watching, record a 60-second small talk opener for a conference. Use at least three new words and two questions in the present continuous.
