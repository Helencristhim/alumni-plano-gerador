Chapter 3: Polite Invitations

The Code

Verb first. Add "please".

Discover the Code

What Comes First?

Come in, please.

Have a seat, please.

Follow me, please.

Let me introduce you to my team.

InvitationExample
Verb + (please)Come in, please. / Have a seat.
Follow meFollow me, please. (= come with me)
Let me + verbLet me introduce you to my team.
Would you like...?Would you like a coffee?

To invite, start with the verb (Come, Have, Follow). Add please to be polite. Use Let me... to offer help and Would you like...? to offer something.

Common Mistakes

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"You sit here."

✓ RIGHT

"Please, have a seat."

✗ WRONG

"Come, please."

✓ RIGHT

"Come in, please."

✗ WRONG

"You want a coffee?"

✓ RIGHT

"Would you like a coffee?"

Remember: invite with the verb first (Have a seat, Come in), and offer with Would you like...?
