Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia como a Anna recebe um visitante e responda as perguntas. / Read how Anna welcomes a visitor and answer.

A visitor arrives at the reception. Anna is the host. She says: "Welcome. Please come in." She takes the guest to the lobby and says: "Please, have a seat. Would you like a coffee?" Then she takes the elevator to the third floor and says: "Let me introduce you to my team."

1. Where does the visitor arrive?
A At the reception.
B At the restaurant.
C At home.
2. What does Anna offer the guest?
A A menu.
B A coffee.
C A report.
3. How does Anna invite the guest in?
A "Welcome. Please come in."
B "You come now."
C "Go away."

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Polite Invitations

Grammar

Para convidar de forma educada, comece com o verbo (Come, Have, Follow) e adicione please. Use Let me... para oferecer ajuda e Would you like...? para oferecer algo. / Start with the verb, add please. Use Let me... and Would you like...?

EstruturaEnglishPortugues
Verb + pleaseCome in, please.Entre, por favor.
Have a seatPlease, have a seat.Por favor, sente-se.
Let me + verbLet me introduce you to my team.Deixe-me apresentar voce a minha equipe.
Would you like...?Would you like a coffee?Voce gostaria de um cafe?
