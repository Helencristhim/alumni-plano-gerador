Chapter 3

The Engine

Connecting ideas for smooth, natural speech

Grammar Discovery

Contrast, Result, Addition

"I was shy. However, I kept going."

"Although it was hard, I improved."

"I practiced daily. As a result, I am faster."

"It is useful. In addition, it is fun."

JobConnectors
ContrastHowever, ... / Although ... / On the other hand, ...
ResultAs a result, ... / Because of that, ...
AdditionIn addition, ... / Also, ...
ConcedeThat said, ... (I accept that, but...)
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"I am tired however I will finish."

✓ RIGHT

"I am tired. However, I will finish."

✗ WRONG

"Although it was hard, but I improved."

✓ RIGHT

"Although it was hard, I improved."

✗ WRONG

"In addition of that, it is cheap."

✓ RIGHT

"In addition to that, it is cheap."

"However" links two sentences (use a period or semicolon before it). With "although", do not add "but". Say "in addition to that", not "in addition of".
