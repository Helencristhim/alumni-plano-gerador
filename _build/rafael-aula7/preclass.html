Vocabulary Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Estude cada palavra de e-mail, ouça a pronúncia e leia o exemplo.

Attachment•anexo
A file you send with an email
"Please find the report in the attachment."
Recipient•destinatário
The person who receives the email
"Add the recipient before you send it."
Subject line•linha de assunto
The title that says what the email is about
"Write 'Contract Review' in the subject line."
To reply•responder
To answer an email
"I will reply to your message today."
To forward•encaminhar
To send a received email to someone else
"I will forward it to my legal team."
Regards•atenciosamente
A polite word to close an email
"Best regards, Rafael."
Draft•rascunho
A version of an email before you send it
"I will write a draft and check it first."
To follow up•dar seguimento
To send another message to check progress
"I will follow up next week."

Grammar Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Leia o e-mail. As frases de e-mail estão em negrito. Depois, responda as perguntas.

Dear Ms. Collins,

I am writing to share my comments on the contract. Please find my notes attached. Could you forward this email to your legal team? Please let me know if you have any questions.

I look forward to your reply.

Best regards,
Rafael Gasparello Lima
1. Why is Rafael writing this email?
A To cancel a meeting
B To share his comments on the contract
C To ask for a job
2. Which phrase sends a file?
A "Please find my notes attached."
B "I look forward to your reply."
C "Best regards."
3. How does Rafael close the email?
A "Bye!"
B "Best regards, Rafael Gasparello Lima"
C "See you"

Grammar Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

As partes de um e-mail profissional e o registro formal.

Email Phrases & Register
PARTE FRASE (FORMAL)
Greeting
(saudação)		Dear Mr. / Ms. + sobrenome,
Reason
(motivo)		I am writing to + verbo...
Request
(pedido)		Please find... / Could you...?
Close
(encerramento)		I look forward to your reply.
Best regards, + nome

✗ "I wait your reply." (tradução literal)

✓ "I look forward to your reply." (frase fixa correta)

Quiz Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situação de e-mail.

You want to send a contract with your email. What do you write?
A "I send you the contract in attachment."
B "Please find the contract attached."
C "Contract is here."
How do you politely ask a partner to answer by Friday?
A "Could you reply by Friday, please?"
B "Reply Friday."
C "You must answer Friday."
Which is the best formal closing?
A "Bye, Rafael"
B "Best regards, Rafael Gasparello Lima"
C "Thx"
You have not received a reply. What do you write?
A "Why you not answer me?"
B "I am writing to follow up on my last email."
C "Answer please now."

Reflection Stage 5: Free Production

Grave sua resposta livremente. Não há certo ou errado — o objetivo é praticar.

Record a short email out loud in English to an international partner. Include: a greeting (Dear...), your reason for writing (I am writing to...), one request (Could you... / Please find... attached), and a professional close (I look forward to your reply. Best regards...).
