Aula 17 -- At the Airport

Tema da aula: At the Airport (check-in, encontrar o portão e fazer pedidos educados com Could I...? e Where can I...?). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
Departures or any travel documentary -- an airport check-in scene (streaming)

Watch travellers check in, drop off bags, and go through security. Listen for words like boarding pass, gate, and carry-on, and for polite requests with Could I...? and Where can I...?

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote três palavras do aeroporto e dois pedidos educados.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Polite requests: Could you...?"

A short, clear lesson on making polite requests with Could I, Can I and Could you -- exactly today's grammar, with natural examples.

Dica: pause após cada exemplo e crie o seu próprio pedido, do tipo "Could you tell me where the gate is?".

Podcast
Coffee Break English -- "At the airport and travelling by plane"

A friendly episode about checking in, going through security, and finding your gate. Perfect vocabulary for a doctor who travels abroad for conferences.

Dica: ouça duas vezes e anote cinco palavras do aeroporto e duas formas de pedir ajuda educadamente.
