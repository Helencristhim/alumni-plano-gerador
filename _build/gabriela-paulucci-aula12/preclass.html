Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Sunny -- the sun is in the sky and it is bright
"It is sunny today." (Ensolarado)
Rainy -- water falls from the clouds
"It is rainy, so I take my umbrella." (Chuvoso)
Cloudy -- the sky is grey and you cannot see the sun
"It is cloudy and grey today." (Nublado)
Windy -- the air moves fast and strong
"It is windy, so hold your hat." (Ventando)
Hot -- the temperature is very high; you feel warm
"In summer it is very hot." (Quente)
Cold -- the temperature is very low; you feel chilly
"In winter it is cold in the morning." (Frio)
Umbrella -- you hold this over your head when it rains
"When it is rainy, I take my umbrella." (Guarda-chuva)
Coat -- warm clothing you wear over your shirt in winter
"When it is cold, I wear a coat." (Casaco)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas. / Read the text and answer the questions.

Welcome to the weather in São Paulo. In spring it is often sunny and warm. In summer it is very hot, and sometimes it is rainy, so I take my umbrella. In autumn the sky is grey, it is cloudy, and the air is windy. In winter it is cold in the morning, so I wear a warm coat. My favorite season is spring, because it is bright and sunny.

1. What is the weather like in summer?
A It is very hot, and sometimes it is rainy.
B It is cold and grey.
C It is windy and snowy.
2. What does the speaker wear in winter?
A An umbrella.
B A warm coat.
C A light shirt.
3. Why is spring the favorite season?
A Because it is bright and sunny.
B Because it is cold and rainy.
C Because it is cloudy and windy.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- What's the Weather Like?

GRAMMAR

Perguntamos com What is the weather like? e respondemos com It is + palavra do tempo. / We ask with What is the weather like? and answer with It is + a weather word.

Form / FormaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
What is the weather like?Para perguntar / To ask about the weatherWhat is the weather like?
It is + palavra do tempoPara responder / To answerIt is sunny.
Is it...?Pergunta sim/não / Yes/no questionIs it cold?
I wear + roupaPara dizer o que você veste / To say what you put onI wear a coat.

Key point / Ponto-chave: use It is para o tempo, não There is. Diga It is sunny, It is cold. / Use It is for the weather, not There is. Say It is sunny, It is cold.
