Lesson 12 -- What's the Weather Like?

Series
Peppa Pig -- "Windy Castle" / "Rainy Day"

Short, slow episodes where the characters talk about the weather: it is sunny, it is rainy, it is windy. Simple sentences and clear pronunciation -- perfect for the words from today's lesson.

Watch with English subtitles. Each time you hear a weather word (sunny, rainy, windy, cold), say it out loud and add "It is...".

Podcast
Speak English Now -- "Talking About the Weather and the Seasons"

A slow, clear podcast about the weather and the four seasons: the words for sunny, cloudy, rainy and cold, and how to ask "What is the weather like?" and answer with "It is...".

Repeat each phrase out loud. Then record a short report of today's weather in São Paulo, one sentence at a time.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Weather: It's sunny, it's raining"

A short, practical lesson on today's language: the weather words and how to use "It is + weather" to describe the day, with real examples and clear American English.

After watching, write five sentences about the weather this week: "On Monday it is...". Then read them aloud and check your pronunciation.
