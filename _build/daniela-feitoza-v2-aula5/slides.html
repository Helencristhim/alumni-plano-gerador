Chapter 3

The Superlative Rule

How to say your company is number one

Superlative Discovery

Listen and Notice

Centauro is the largest retailer in Latin America.

It is the biggest sportswear group in the region.

We have the most complete supply chain in the market.

Centauro is the most important brand in our group.

When do we add -est? When do we use "the most"?

Superlative Rule

-est vs the most

FormUseExample
the + adjective + -estShort adjectives (one syllable)"the largest", "the biggest", "the fastest"
the most + adjectiveLong adjectives (two or more syllables)"the most important", "the most complete"
irregularSpecial forms to memorizegood → "the best"; bad → "the worst"

Always use the before a superlative: "the largest retailer", never "largest retailer".

Common Mistake

Wrong Form vs Right Form

✗ WRONG

"Centauro is the most big retailer."

✓ RIGHT

"Centauro is the biggest retailer." (short = -est)

✗ WRONG

"It is the more largest group in Brazil."

✓ RIGHT

"It is the largest group in Brazil." (never more + -est)

Short adjectives take -est. Long adjectives take "the most". Never mix "more" with "-est".
