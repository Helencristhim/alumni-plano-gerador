Aula 13 -- Negotiation Basics

Tema da aula: Negotiation Basics -- finding common ground, com o segundo condicional (if + past simple, would + base verb) e o vocabulário de negociação (leverage, trade-off, bottom line, concession, win-win). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"How to Negotiate -- Finding a Win-Win" -- Harvard Business Review

A short video on principled negotiation: how to look past the price, discover the other side's priorities, and propose a trade-off that works for both sides. Perfect input to review leverage, bottom line, concession, and common ground in real context.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote uma frase no segundo condicional ("If they..., I would...") e dois termos novos de negociação.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- The Second Conditional

Short lessons and quizzes on the second conditional: the form (if + past simple, would + base verb), the use for imaginary situations, and the special case "If I were you". Great to consolidate the grammar of this lesson.

Dica: escreva cinco frases sobre uma negociação na Microsoft -- todas no segundo condicional -- e confira que você nunca usou "would" depois de "if".

Podcast
"Business English Pod" -- Negotiation Skills

A friendly business podcast for B1-B2 learners. Listen to professionals open negotiations, make counteroffers, break deadlocks, and settle on win-win deals -- great input to review trade-off, terms, concession, and walk away in real conversation.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote toda frase usada para fazer uma contraproposta e para fechar o acordo.
