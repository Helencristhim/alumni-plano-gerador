Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

Schedule -- the list of your activities and times
"My schedule is full on Monday." (Agenda)
Meeting -- when people work together in a room, at a time
"The meeting is at ten o'clock." (Reunião)
Appointment -- a fixed time with a doctor or at work
"I have an appointment at the bank." (Hora marcada / compromisso)
Calendar -- the paper or app with all the days of the year
"Let me check my calendar." (Calendário)
Available -- free to do something at that time
"Are you available on Thursday?" (Disponível)
Busy -- with many things to do, not free
"I am busy in the morning." (Ocupado/a)
Early -- before the normal time
"The meeting is early, at eight o'clock." (Cedo)
Late -- after the normal time
"Sorry, I am late!" (Atrasado/a, tarde)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

Dienane works in Human Resources. Today she calls Mr. Costa, a candidate. "Are you available for an interview this week?" she asks. She checks her calendar: she is busy on Tuesday, but Thursday is free. "The interview is on Thursday, at half past nine," she says. Mr. Costa writes it in his schedule. "What time?" he asks again. "Nine thirty!" The meeting room is on the second floor.

1. Como a Dienane pergunta se ele está livre? / How does Dienane ask if he is free?
A "Are you available for an interview?"
B "Are you busy forever?"
C "What hours are you?"
2. Que horas é a entrevista? / What time is the interview?
A At nine o'clock.
B At half past nine (9:30).
C At a quarter past nine.
3. Quando a Dienane está ocupada? / When is Dienane busy?
A On Thursday.
B On Tuesday.
C On Monday.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- As horas em inglês

GRAMMAR

Duas formas de dizer as horas — as duas estão certas! / Two ways to tell the time — both correct!

RelógioClássico / ClassicFácil / Easy
9:00nine o'clocknine a.m.
9:30half past nine (e meia)nine thirty
9:15a quarter past nine (e quinze)nine fifteen
9:45a quarter to ten (quinze para as dez)nine forty-five

Pergunta: What time is it? (Que horas são?) — Dias usam on (on Thursday), horas usam at (at nine o'clock).
