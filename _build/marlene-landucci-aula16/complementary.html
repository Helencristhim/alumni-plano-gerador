Aula 16 -- Sightseeing

Tema da aula: planejar um dia de passeio e dizer o que quer visitar ("I want to visit...", "I would like to see..."). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
"Rick Steves' Europe" -- a guided city tour episode

A classic travel series. Watch a guided tour of a European city and listen for sightseeing words: museum, cathedral, monument, statue, and guide.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote toda palavra de passeio que você ouvir (museum, cathedral, tour, guide).

Podcast
"Travel English" -- Sightseeing & Attractions (beginner episode)

A slow, clear podcast for beginners. Listen to a traveler plan a day of sightseeing and practice "I want to visit..." and "I would like to see...".

Dica: ouça duas vezes. Na segunda, repita em voz alta "I want to visit the museum" e "I would like to see the statue".

YouTube
"Sightseeing in English -- Conversation at a Tourist Site" (English with subtitles)

A short, simple video of a real sightseeing conversation. A tourist says "I want to visit the cathedral", buys a ticket, and joins a tour with a guide.

Dica: pause depois de cada fala do guia e repita. Foque nas frases "I want to visit..." e "I need a ticket, please".
