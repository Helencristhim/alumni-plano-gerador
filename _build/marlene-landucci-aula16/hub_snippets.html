Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Museum -- a building with art and history (museu)
"I want to visit the museum." (Eu quero visitar o museu.)
Cathedral -- a large and important old church (catedral)
"The cathedral is beautiful inside." (A catedral é linda por dentro.)
Ticket -- the paper you buy to enter a place (ingresso)
"I need a ticket for the museum." (Preciso de um ingresso para o museu.)
Tour -- an organized visit to see a place (passeio, tour)
"We will join a city tour." (Vamos fazer um passeio pela cidade.)
Guide -- the person who shows you around (guia)
"The guide tells the history." (O guia conta a história.)
Monument -- a big structure to remember a person or event (monumento)
"The monument is in the square." (O monumento fica na praça.)
Statue -- a figure of a person in stone or metal (estátua)
"The famous statue is in the museum." (A estátua famosa fica no museu.)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- I want to / I would like to

GRAMMAR

Para dizer o que você planeja, use I want to + verbo (mais informal) ou I would like to + verbo (mais educado). O verbo depois de to nunca muda (visit, see, take, buy). / To say your plan, use "I want to + verb" or, more politely, "I would like to + verb". The verb after "to" never changes.

FormExample (EN)Tradução (PT)
I want to (+)I want to visit the cathedral.Eu quero visitar a catedral.
I would like to (polite)I would like to see the statue.Eu gostaria de ver a estátua.
Question (?)Do you want to take a tour?Você quer fazer um passeio?
Negative (-)I do not want to wait.Não quero esperar.

Key point / Ponto-chave: sempre use to antes do verbo: "I want to visit", "I would like to see". Nunca "I want visit".
