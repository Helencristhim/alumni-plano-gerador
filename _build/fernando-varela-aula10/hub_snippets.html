Conference Calls with Partners

Running a conference call with international partners: opening the call, the agenda, the connection, and the follow-up. Key words: call, agenda, hold on, connection, follow up, summary, mute, dial, speak up, attend. Structure: present perfect (have/has + past participle) to check what is done -- "Have you sent the agenda?", "I have finished the summary.", with already / yet.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para escutar.

Call -- ligacao / chamada (a phone or video conversation)
"We have a call at ten."
Agenda -- pauta (the list of topics for a meeting)
"Here is the agenda for the call."
Hold on -- aguarde (wait a moment)
"Hold on, please."
Connection -- conexao (the link between phones or computers)
"The connection is weak."
Follow up -- dar continuidade (to do more after a meeting)
"Let us follow up by email."
Summary -- resumo (a short version of the main points)
"I will send a summary."
Mute -- mudo / silenciar (to turn off your microphone)
"Please put your mic on mute."
Dial -- discar (to call a number)
"Dial the number to join."
Speak up -- falar mais alto (to talk louder)
"Could you speak up, please?"
Attend -- participar / comparecer (to be present at a meeting)
"Thank you for attending the call."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

On the call, the connection is weak, so Sofia asks Fernando to speak up. "Have you received the agenda?" he asks. "Yes, I have," she says. "Have you sent the summary?" Fernando answers: "I have finished the summary, and I am going to send it now." The team has joined the call too. At the end, they follow up on the new project, and Fernando says: "Thank you for attending the call."

1. Por que dizemos "Have you sent...?" e nao "Have you send...?"
A Porque "send" e plural.
B Depois de have/has usamos o particípio (sent), nao a forma base (send).
C Porque a acao e no futuro.
2. Usamos "I have finished the summary" para:
A Dizer que algo esta concluído e importa agora.
B Pedir um cafe.
C Falar de uma rotina diaria.
3. Which short answer is correct for "Have you received the agenda?"
A "Yes, I received."
B "Yes, I have."
C "Yes, I am."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Perfect

GRAMMAR

Como falar de uma acao concluída que importa agora (explicacao em ingles e portugues).

StructureUse / UsoExample
have/has + past participleAcao concluída ligada ao agoraI have sent the email.
Have you + past participle?Pergunta (ja fez?)Have you finished the summary?
already / yetja / aindaWe have already started. / Not yet.
Short answersYes, I have. / No, I have not. (NAO "I have send")
