Chapter 3

The Present Perfect Code

Have you sent...? · I have finished... · already / yet

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"Have you sent the agenda?"

"I have finished the summary."

"We have already started the project."

"The partner has joined the call."

UseStructureExample
Finished, matters nowhave/has + past participleI have sent the email.
QuestionHave you + past participle?Have you finished the summary?
already / yetalready (done) / not yetWe have already started. / Not yet.
Short answersYes, I have. / No, I have not.
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"I have send the report."

✓ RIGHT

"I have sent the report."

✗ WRONG

"Have you finish the summary?"

✓ RIGHT

"Have you finished the summary?"

✗ WRONG

"The partner has join the call."

✓ RIGHT

"The partner has joined the call."

After have or has, use the past participle (sent, finished, joined), not the base verb (send, finish, join).

Chapter 4

Running the Call

A conference call with a partner

Dialogue

The Conference Call

S
Hi Fernando, can you hear me? Please speak up, the connection is weak.
F
Hi Sofia. Now it is better. Have you received the agenda?
S
Yes, I have. Have you sent the summary?
F
I have finished the summary, and I am going to send it now.
S
Great. Hold on, let me put the team on the call.
F
Sure. Have they joined yet?
S
Yes, they have. Let us follow up on the new project.
F
Perfect. Thank you for attending the call, Sofia.
