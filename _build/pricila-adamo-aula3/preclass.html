Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Future Continuous & Future Perfect

Grammar

Para uma acao que estara EM ANDAMENTO num momento futuro, use o future continuous: will be + verbo-ing. Para uma acao que estara CONCLUIDA antes de um momento futuro, use o future perfect: will have + particicio. / For an action IN PROGRESS at a future moment, use the future continuous (will be + verb-ing). For an action COMPLETED before a future point, use the future perfect (will have + past participle).

Forma / FormEnglishPortugues
Future continuous (in progress)This time next year, I will be travelling.No ano que vem, eu estarei viajando.
Future perfect (completed)By next year, I will have reached my goal.Ate o ano que vem, eu ja terei alcancado minha meta.
NegativeI will not have finished the trip by June.Nao terei terminado a viagem ate junho.
InterrogativeWill you be living abroad this time next year?Voce estara morando fora no ano que vem?
Common mistakeNOT "This time next year I will travel." Say "I will be travelling" (in progress).Acao em andamento no futuro = will be + -ing.
