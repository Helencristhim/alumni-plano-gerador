Aula 9 -- The What-If Room -- Weighing Hypotheticals with the Second Conditional

Series
Succession -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Celebration"

The Roy family fights for control of a media empire, and every scene is a what-if: "If we sold now, we would lose control," "If I were you, I would not sign." It is the second conditional in a boardroom — leverage, concessions, walking away, and weighing every trade-off out loud, exactly like the what-if room in Lesson 9.

Tip: watch one negotiation scene with English subtitles. Write down three hypothetical sentences ("If they offered X, I would...") and check that the if-clause is past simple and the result uses "would".
