Aula 16 -- Handling Uncertainty & Buying Time

Series
The Office (US) -- Any meeting scene

Watch how characters react when they are put on the spot and don't have an answer. Direct connection to Lesson 16: notice who freezes and who buys time, and listen for fillers ("let me think...", "honestly...") and the natural question tags that keep conversations friendly.

Tip: pause on a tense moment and say out loud how you would buy time and answer calmly with an estimate.

Podcast
WorkLife with Adam Grant -- Any interview episode

A workplace podcast where guests are asked tough questions in real time. Direct connection to Lesson 16: hear how confident professionals buy time, give honest estimates ("roughly", "I'd estimate"), and admit uncertainty without losing authority.

Tip: after one episode, record 60 seconds answering a hard question, buying time first and ending with a question tag.

YouTube
"Question Tags in English" -- BBC Learning English / English with Lucy

A clear lesson on forming question tags with the mirror rule (positive sentence + negative tag, and the reverse) and choosing the right auxiliary. Direct connection to Lesson 16: exactly the grammar we practiced for keeping conversations soft and friendly.

Tip: pause after each example and make your own sentence with a correct question tag out loud.
