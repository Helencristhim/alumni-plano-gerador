Stage 1.1: Hotel & City Vocabulary

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Estas palavras ajudam voce no hotel e na cidade nova.

Front desk -- recepcao (onde voce faz o check-in)
"Please go to the front desk to check in."
Key card -- cartao-chave (abre a porta do quarto)
"Here is your key card for room 402."
Porter -- carregador (leva as malas ao quarto)
"The porter brought our bags up to the room."
Wake-up call -- chamada para acordar (de manha)
"Could I have a wake-up call at six?"
Late check-out -- saida tardia (deixar o quarto mais tarde)
"Could I have a late check-out, please?"
Vacancy -- vaga (quarto livre no hotel)
"Do you have a vacancy for tonight?"
Within walking distance -- a pe (perto o suficiente para ir andando)
"The museum is within walking distance."
Amenities -- comodidades (academia, cafe da manha...)
"The hotel has great amenities for guests."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia a situacao e responda as perguntas.

At the hotel, Roberto goes to the front desk. "Could I have a quiet room, please?" he asks. The receptionist gives him his key card and says, "Would you like the porter to bring your bags up?" Roberto says yes, and adds, "Could you give me a wake-up call at six?" The hotel has nice amenities, and the old square is within walking distance. Before he leaves, he says, "I'd like a late check-out, please."

1. How does Roberto ask for a quiet room?
A "I want a quiet room."
B "Could I have a quiet room, please?"
C "Give me a quiet room."
2. What does the receptionist offer?
A A late check-out.
B The porter to bring his bags up.
C A taxi to the airport.
3. After "Could I", "Could you" and "Would you like", which verb form is correct?
A to + verb (to have).
B the base verb (have).
C verb + ing (having).

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Polite Requests & Offers

Grammar

Use Could I + verbo para pedir algo para voce ("Could I have a key card?"), Could you + verbo para pedir que alguem faca algo ("Could you call a taxi?"), Would you like + substantivo para oferecer ("Would you like breakfast?") e I'd like + substantivo para dizer o que voce quer de forma educada ("I'd like a late check-out"). Depois de Could I / Could you / Would you like, use o verbo na forma base, SEM "to". I'd like e a forma curta de I would like. Adicione please para soar ainda mais educado. / Use these polite forms to ask and offer; after Could I / Could you / Would you like, use the base verb (no "to").

Quiz

B "Could I have my key card, please?"
