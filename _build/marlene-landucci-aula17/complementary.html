Aula 17 -- Shopping for Style

Tema da aula: comprar roupas e descrever cor e tamanho (a small blue dress). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
"The Bold Type" -- the clothing and fashion scenes

A series about fashion and style. Watch the store and styling scenes and listen for clothes words: dress, shirt, jacket, color, and size.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote toda palavra de roupa e cor que você ouvir (dress, jacket, blue, red, size).

Podcast
"Travel English" -- Shopping for Clothes (beginner episode)

A slow, clear podcast for beginners. Listen to a traveler shop for clothes and practice describing color and size, like "a small blue dress".

Dica: ouça duas vezes. Na segunda, repita em voz alta "I want a red dress" e "Can I try this on?".

YouTube
"Shopping for Clothes in English -- Conversation" (English with subtitles)

A short, simple video of a real clothing store conversation. A customer asks for a color and size, tries clothes on, and decides what to buy.

Dica: pause depois de cada fala do assistant e repita. Foque nas frases "I want a red dress" e "What size do you have?".
