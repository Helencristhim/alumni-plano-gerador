Problem-Solving Meetings

There is a problem in the study process: the samples arrived late. Fernando and Sarah hold a problem-solving meeting and agree a solution. Key words: issue, solution, solve, cause, suggest, option, decide, fix, challenge, concern. Structure: should / shouldn't for advice and making suggestions (Why don't we...? / We could... / How about ...ing?).

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para escutar.

Issue -- problema / questao (a problem to talk about)
"We have an issue with the study."
Solution -- solucao (the answer to a problem)
"We need a solution today."
Solve -- resolver (to find the answer to a problem)
"Let us solve this together."
Cause -- causa (the reason a problem happens)
"The cause is a delay at customs."
Suggest -- sugerir (to give an idea)
"I suggest a new plan."
Option -- opcao (one choice you can take)
"That is a good option."
Decide -- decidir (to make a final choice)
"We decide today."
Fix -- consertar / corrigir (to repair or correct a problem)
"Let us fix the delay."
Challenge -- desafio (a hard task you must face)
"This is a big challenge."
Concern -- preocupacao (a worry about something)
"My main concern is the deadline."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

There is an issue with the study: the samples arrived late. The cause is a delay at customs. Fernando calls a meeting. "We should find a solution today," he says. Sarah has a suggestion: "Why don't we use the local lab this week?" Fernando likes the idea. "That is a good option. We could also call the supplier to fix the delay. How about sending the samples by express next time?" Together they decide on the plan. The challenge is solved.

1. Por que dizemos "we should meet" e nao "we should to meet"?
A Porque "meet" e plural.
B Depois de should usamos o verbo na forma base, sem "to".
C Porque a acao e no passado.
2. "Why don't we use the local lab?" e:
A uma frase negativa de verdade.
B uma sugestao (uma forma educada de propor uma ideia).
C uma ordem.
3. Which sentence is correct?
A "How about to send the samples by express?"
B "How about sending the samples by express?"
C "How about we sending the samples by express?"

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Should & Suggestions

GRAMMAR

Como dar conselho e fazer sugestoes (explicacao em ingles e portugues).

StructureUse / UsoExample
should + verbconselho (deveriamos)we should meet today
shouldn't + verbconselho negativo (nao deveriamos)we shouldn't wait
Why don't we + verb?sugestao (por que nao...?)Why don't we use the lab?
We could + verbsugestao suave (poderiamos)We could call the supplier
How about + verb-ing?How about sending it by express? (NAO "how about to send")

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao na reuniao.

You open the meeting and name the problem. You say:
A "Problem here."
B "We have an issue with the study."
C "I am happy."
You give advice to act now. The correct sentence is:
A "We should to find a solution."
B "We should find a solution."
C "We find should a solution."
You want to suggest using the local lab. You say:
A "Why don't we use the local lab?"
B "Why we don't use the local lab?"
C "Why not we use the local lab?"
You suggest a faster method next time. You say:
A "How about to send it by express?"
