Chapter 3

The Suggestion Code

We should · Why don't we · We could · How about ...ing

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"We should meet today to find a solution."

"Why don't we use the local lab?"

"We could call the supplier."

"How about sending the samples by express?"

StructureUseExample
should / shouldn't + verbadvicewe should meet today
Why don't we + verb?suggestionWhy don't we use the lab?
We could + verbsoft suggestionWe could call the supplier
How about + verb-ing?How about sending it by express?
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"We should to meet today."

✓ RIGHT

"We should meet today."

✗ WRONG

"Why we don't use the lab?"

✓ RIGHT

"Why don't we use the lab?"

✗ WRONG

"How about to send it by express?"

✓ RIGHT

"How about sending it by express?"

After should and could, use the base verb (no "to"). Why don't WE comes before the verb. After "how about", use verb + ing.

The Meeting

Dialogue

Solving It Together

S
Hi Fernando. You said we have an issue with the study. What is the problem?
F
The samples arrived late. This is a real problem for the team.
S
What is the cause of the delay?
F
A delay at customs. We should find a solution today.
S
I have a suggestion. Why don't we use the local lab this week?
F
That is a good option. We could also call the supplier to fix the delay.
S
Good idea. How about sending the samples by express next time?
F
I agree. Let us decide on this plan. The challenge is solved.
