Lesson 13 -- Change and Adaptation

Grammar Video
Used To, Be Used To, Get Used To (Arnel's Everyday English)

A clear, natural explanation of the exact three structures from tonight, with plenty of real examples. Listen for how a native speaker moves between a past habit ("I used to...") and being or becoming accustomed ("I'm used to..." / "I'm getting used to...").

Tip: after the video, say one sentence of each type about your own work out loud.

Language Skill
Used To, Get Used To, Be Used To in English (Lingportal)

A second, slower walk-through that focuses on the two classic mistakes to avoid: dropping the "-d" in "used to", and using a base verb instead of "-ing" after "be/get used to". Great reinforcement before you speak in the lesson.

Tip: pause after each example and predict the correct form before the video says it.
