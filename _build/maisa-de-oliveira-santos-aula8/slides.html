Chapter 4: The Code

Polite Phone Questions

Can I...? · Could you...? · Would you...? · Who...?

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"Can I speak to David, please?"

"Who is calling, please?"

"Could you call back later?"

"Would you like to leave a message?"

QuestionUse it forExample
Can I speak to ...?ask for a personCan I speak to David?
Who is calling?ask who is on the lineWho is calling, please?
Could you ...?ask politelyCould you call back later?
Would you like to ...?offer or inviteWould you like to leave a message?

On the phone, start with a polite word — Can, Could, Would or Who — BEFORE the subject. After Could you and Can I, use the base verb: "Could you call."

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

Who are you?
Who is calling, please?
Can I speak David?
Can I speak to David?

On the phone, ask "Who is calling?", not "Who are you?". And do not forget to: "Can I speak to David?"
