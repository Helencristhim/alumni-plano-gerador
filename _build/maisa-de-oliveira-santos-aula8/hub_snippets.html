On the Phone

Atender e fazer ligações de trabalho em inglês: answer the phone, speaking, hold on, leave a message, call back, hang up, the line is busy, wrong number. Gramática: question forms para telefone (Can I speak to...? / Who is calling? / Could you...? / Would you like to leave a message?). Missão: conduzir uma ligação de trabalho e anotar um recado.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

Answer the phone -- to pick up a call when the phone rings
"I answer the phone at work." (atender o telefone)
Speaking -- a word to say who you are on a call
"Maísa speaking. How can I help you?" (quem fala, ao telefone)
Hold on -- to wait for a short moment on the line
"Hold on, please. One moment." (espere um momento)
Leave a message -- to say words for someone who is not there
"Would you like to leave a message?" (deixar um recado)
Call back -- to call someone again later
"Could you call me back later?" (retornar a ligação)
Hang up -- to end a call and put the phone down
"I say goodbye and hang up." (desligar)
The line is busy -- the other person is already on a call
"Sorry, the line is busy right now." (a linha está ocupada)
Wrong number -- when you call the incorrect number
"Sorry, this is the wrong number." (número errado)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

The phone rings at the finance team. Maísa answers the phone. She says: "Maísa speaking." A man asks: "Can I speak to David, please?" David is not there, so Maísa says: "Hold on. Would you like to leave a message?" The man says: "Yes. Could you ask David to call me back?" Maísa writes the message and says goodbye. Then she hangs up.

1. O que a Maísa diz quando atende? / What does Maísa say when she answers?
A "Who are you?"
B "Maísa speaking."
C "I am busy now."
2. David não está. O que a Maísa oferece? / David is not there. What does Maísa offer?
A "Would you like to leave a message?"
B "This is the wrong number."
C "Please hang up."
3. O que o homem pede? / What does the man ask?
A "Could you call me back?" — no, ele nunca liga.
B "Could you ask David to call me back?"
C "Who is calling?"

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Question Forms for the Phone

GRAMMAR

Ao telefone, comece com uma palavra polida (Can, Could, Would, Who) ANTES do sujeito. Depois de "Can I" e "Could you", use o verbo na forma base. / On the phone, start with a polite word before the subject; after "Can I" and "Could you", use the base verb.

Pergunta / QuestionUsar para / Use forExemplo / Example
Can I speak to ...?pedir por uma pessoaCan I speak to David?
Who is calling?perguntar quem está na linhaWho is calling, please?
Could you ...?pedir com educaçãoCould you call back later?
Would you like to ...?oferecer ou convidarWould you like to leave a message?

Atenção: ao telefone, pergunte "Who is calling?", nunca "Who are you?". E não esqueça o to: "Can I speak to David?", nunca "Can I speak David?".

Quiz

Stage 5: Think About It

Reflection

Sem certo ou errado. Pense em inglês, toque no microfone e fale livremente.

Imagine: você atende uma ligação de trabalho e a pessoa procurada não está. Conduza a ligação em 4-5 frases: atenda, diga que a pessoa não está, ofereça um recado, pergunte quem está ligando e despeça-se. Use: Maísa speaking..., Hold on..., Would you like to leave a message?, Who is calling?, I will ask him to call you back.
