Chapter 3 -- The -ed Code

The Simple Past

The Past Code

Add -ed

RuleBasePast
most verbs: + edstart, joinstarted, joined
ends in e: + dmove, graduatemoved, graduated
consonant + y: y to iedstudy, applystudied, applied
Golden rulethe past is the SAME for I, you, he, she, we, they: "She started", "We started".

Key idea: regular past verbs end in -ed, and the form never changes with the subject. One ending, one past.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I studyed at night. ... She move to Sao Paulo in 2014."
"I studied at night. ... She moved to Sao Paulo in 2014."

For study, change y to i: studied. After a past date, the verb must be in the past.
