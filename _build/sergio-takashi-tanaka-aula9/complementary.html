"10 ways to have a better conversation" -- Celeste Headlee (TED)

A clear, practical talk on how to communicate so people actually listen -- be honest, be brief, and say what you mean. Connection to Lesson 9: a strong email follows the same rules. When you write to a partner bank, you keep it formal and concise, one idea at a time, exactly as she describes for speaking.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Then write one short, formal email in three sentences -- open the topic, make one point with a connector, and close. Read it aloud to check it is concise.
