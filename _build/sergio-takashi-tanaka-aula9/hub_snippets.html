Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

This morning a partner bank sent an inquiry regarding the quarterly figures. I opened my reply with the topic, and then I answered each point. I confirmed the total exposure. In addition, I attached the updated limit table. One number was still unclear; however, I explained what I could and promised the rest by Friday. The committee meets that day; therefore, a prompt, formal reply mattered. I kept it concise and closed with "I look forward to hearing from you."

1. Why does the writer use "In addition" before "I attached the updated limit table"?
A To add a second point to the first one.
B To show a contrast with the first point.
C To show the result of the first point.
2. What does "however" show in "One number was still unclear; however, I explained what I could"?
A An extra point in the same direction.
B A contrast or a problem.
C The topic of the email.
3. Why does the writer use "therefore" in "The committee meets that day; therefore, a prompt reply mattered"?
A It shows a consequence of the first idea.
B It introduces the subject of the email.
C It adds a point with no relation.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Formal Register & Connectors

GRAMMAR

How connectors link your ideas, and how a formal register keeps an email polite.

ConnectorWhat it showsExample
In addition / MoreoverAdds another point.In addition, we have updated the limits.
HoweverShows a contrast or a problem (formal "but").The control works. However, one point is open.
Therefore / As a resultShows a consequence.The figures are late; therefore, we will wait.
Register & punctuationOpen the topic with regarding ("I am writing regarding your inquiry"), use the purpose form to + verb ("I am writing to confirm..."), and soften commands ("Could you please send it?"). A connector at the start of a sentence takes a comma after it.
