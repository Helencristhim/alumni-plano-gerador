Documentary
Downfall: The Case Against Boeing (Netflix)

The most expensive case of missing psychological safety ever documented: the engineers saw the problem, they said so, and the company culture taught everyone that speaking up came at a cost. Connection to Lesson 2: this is your cultural diagnosis at scale — disengagement, exit interviews nobody read, and an elephant in the room nobody named until it was too late.

Tip: watch with English subtitles only. Write down THREE third conditional sentences about what leadership should have done (“If they had..., they would have...”).
