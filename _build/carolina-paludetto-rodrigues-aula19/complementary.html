Aula 19 -- Food & Healthy Eating

Tema da aula: Food & Healthy Eating -- talking about meals and quantities with how much, how many, some and any. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
Countable & Uncountable Nouns -- how much vs how many

Watch a short, clear video about food quantities in English. Listen for How much...? and How many...?, and for some, any, a lot of, a little and a few with food words.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote cinco perguntas sobre comida: três com How many (contaveis) e duas com How much (nao contaveis).

Practice
BBC Learning English -- food and quantities

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about food vocabulary and quantifiers: How much sugar?, How many apples?, some/any, a lot of, a little and a few.

Dica: escreva seis frases sobre comida. Sublinhe how much e how many, e circule cada palavra nova (vegetable, fruit, breakfast, snack, hungry, thirsty, sugar, fresh).

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about food, diet or healthy eating

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen to people talk about food, meals and healthy eating -- great input to review today's words and the patterns how much / how many and a lot of / a little.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote toda frase com how much / how many e toda palavra nova (fruit, vegetable, snack, fresh, sugar).
