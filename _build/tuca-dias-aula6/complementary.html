Lesson 6 -- Small Talk: Starting Conversations

Series
Friends -- any scene at Central Perk

The classic sitcom is set largely in a coffee shop, where the characters constantly make small talk. Perfect for hearing natural icebreakers, follow-up questions, and casual chat in English.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down three ways a character starts a conversation, and one question with "Have you ever...?" Then try saying them aloud.

Podcast
Coffee Break English -- "Making Small Talk"

A short, slow lesson dedicated to conversation language: icebreakers, safe topics, asking "Have you ever...?", and keeping a chat going with follow-up questions.

Pause after each phrase and repeat it aloud. Note three icebreakers and compare them with how Tuca started her chat with Daniel in today's dialogue.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "How to make small talk"

A clear, practical video on starting conversations in English: choosing safe topics, using the present perfect to ask about experiences, and avoiding awkward silences. Directly useful for travelling alone.

After watching, record a 60-second small-talk conversation with an imaginary stranger. Use at least four new words and two questions with "Have you ever...?"
