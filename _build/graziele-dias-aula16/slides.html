Chapter 3: Suggestion Tools

Make a Suggestion

Let's · Shall we...? · Why don't we...?

The Tools

Three Suggestion Tools

Let's + verb · "Let's start the meeting."

Shall we + verb? · "Shall we begin?"

Why don't we + verb · "Why don't we discuss it?"

All three use the base verb, with no "to".

Let's · Shall we...? · Why don't we...? — three ways to suggest

Discover the Pattern

What Comes Next?

Let's start the meeting.

Shall we discuss the schedule?

Why don't we decide today?

Let's suggest a short break.

ToolPattern
Let'sLet's + base verb — "Let's start."
Shall we...?Shall we + base verb + ? — "Shall we begin?"
Why don't we...?Why don't we + base verb — "Why don't we decide?"

Always the base verb · never "to" after them

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"Let's to start."

✓ RIGHT

"Let's start."

✗ WRONG

"Shall we to begin?"

✓ RIGHT

"Shall we begin?"

After Let's and Shall we, use the base verb. No "to".

The first topic is the schedule. Let's discuss it.

Good idea. I suggest we move the morning shift.

I agree. Shall we decide now?

Yes. We decide the new schedule today.
G
Why don't we discuss the supplies next?

Perfect. You lead very well today.
