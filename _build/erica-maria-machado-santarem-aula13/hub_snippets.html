Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. Ele usa like + -ing e want + to.

In my free time, I like reading books and listening to music. On Saturday, I enjoy painting flowers. On Sunday, I love gardening. This weekend, I want to swim in the morning, and I want to learn to dance. My free time is calm and happy.

1. What does the person like doing in their free time?
A Working and cleaning the house.
B Reading books and listening to music.
C Driving to the office.
2. The text says "I like reading" and "I want to swim." Which form is correct?
A After "like" use -ing; after "want" use "to".
B After "like" use "to"; after "want" use -ing.
C Always use the base verb.
3. What does the person want to learn this weekend?
A They want to learn to drive.
B They want to learn to dance.
C They want to learn to cook.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Like + -ing / Want + to

GRAMMAR

Duas estruturas para falar do que você gosta e quer fazer. / Two patterns to talk about what you like and want to do.

VerboEstruturaExample
like / love / enjoy+ verbo + -ingI like reading. / I enjoy painting.
want+ to + verboI want to walk. / I want to swim.
PerguntaWhat do you like doing?— I like cooking.
Lembre-seDepois de like = -ing. Depois de want = to. Nunca os dois juntos.

Stage 5: Free Production

Reflection

Talk about your free time. Say two or three things you like doing (use "I like + -ing", for example: I like reading, I enjoy gardening). Then say one thing you want to do this weekend (use "I want to...", for example: I want to walk in the park). Take your time and speak calmly.
