Aula 18 -- Closing the Deal: Confirming Terms & the Follow-up

Tema da aula: Closing the Deal (fechar o acordo e escrever um follow-up curto, confirmando o que foi acordado e feito com o present perfect -- we have agreed on the terms; I have attached the contract). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
Suits (Netflix) -- any scene where a deal is closed and confirmed

Watch how lawyers close a deal and recap what has been agreed: "We have reached an agreement", "I have sent the contract". A great model of confident, professional closing language in fast business English.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote cada frase com "have / has + particípio". Quantas palavras de hoje (finalize, agreement, sign-off, deliverable) você reconhece?

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Present perfect" (6 Minute Grammar / English In A Minute)

Short, clear videos explaining the present perfect: have / has + past participle, for finished actions with a result now -- exactly the grammar of today. Perfect review for recapping a deal in a follow-up email.

Dica: pause após cada exemplo e repita em voz alta. Depois grave-se recapitulando três coisas que você "have done" hoje usando o present perfect.
