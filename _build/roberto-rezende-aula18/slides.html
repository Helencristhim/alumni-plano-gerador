Chapter 3 -- The Code

Present perfect: have + past participle

Grammar Rule

The Present Perfect

Subject + have / haspast participle + ...
We haveagreed on the price.
We havereached an agreement.
I haveattached the contract.
The client hasconfirmed the order.

Key idea: the present perfect links a finished action to its result now. Use have with I / you / we / they, and has with he / she / it. After have or has, use the past participle (agreed, reached, attached, confirmed). It is perfect for recapping a deal, because the decision is done and still true today.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"We have agree on the price and I have attach the file."
"We have agreed on the price and I have attached the file."

After have or has, use the past participle (agreed, attached), never the base verb. Use the present perfect for a result that matters now.

About the Briefing

1. What grammar does Amy say is the best tool?

The present perfect: have plus the past participle (we have agreed, we have reached).

2. What three things should the follow-up include?

A thank you, a recap of the agreed terms, and the clear next steps.

3. When should Roberto use the past simple instead?

Only when he gives a specific time, like "we agreed yesterday".

Artefact

The Follow-up Email

From: Roberto Rezende · Sales, Resende

Subject: Engine Order -- Confirmation & Next Steps

Dear Mr. Lima,

Thank you for your time today. It was a pleasure to close this deal with you.

Just to confirm what we have agreed: we have agreed on a five percent discount, and we have reached an agreement on delivery in March.

I have attached the draft contract for your legal team, and I have confirmed the payment terms with finance.

Next steps: your team will review the draft this week, and we will proceed to sign-off on Friday.

I look forward to finalizing the agreement. Thank you for your commitment.

Best regards,
Roberto Rezende

Dialogue

The Confirmation Call

C

"Roberto, good news. We have decided to go ahead. The board has given sign-off on the proposal."

R

"That is wonderful. Thank you for your commitment. Just to confirm, we have agreed on a five percent discount and delivery in March."

C

"Exactly. Could you send the contract so our legal team can review it?"

R

"Of course. I have attached the draft to my follow-up email, and I have confirmed the payment terms with finance."

C

"Perfect. So what are the next steps from here?"

R

"Your team reviews the draft this week, and we proceed to sign-off on Friday. Once you confirm, we can finalize the agreement."
