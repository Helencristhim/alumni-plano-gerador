Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Subway -- the fast train under the ground in a city
"Take the subway to Times Square." (Metrô)
Bus -- a big road vehicle for many people
"Does this bus go to the park?" (Ônibus)
Ticket -- the paper or card you buy to travel
"How much is a ticket?" (Bilhete / passagem)
Stop -- the place where the bus or train waits for you
"It is four stops from here." (Parada)
Platform -- the long place where you wait for the train
"The platform is on your left." (Plataforma)
Line -- one route of the subway (red, blue...)
"The red line goes to Times Square." (Linha)
Transfer -- to change from one line to another
"Do I need to transfer?" (Baldeação / trocar)
Schedule -- the list of times when the train comes
"Check the schedule on the screen." (Horário)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Elaine wants to go to Times Square. At the station she asks, Which subway goes to Times Square? The red line, says the clerk. A ticket is three dollars. Do I need to transfer? No, the line is direct. Elaine goes to the platform and waits. It is six stops. She watches the schedule on the screen. Soon the train arrives.

1. Como Elaine pergunta a linha? / How does Elaine ask about the line?
A Which subway go to Times Square?
B Which subway goes to Times Square?
C What subway is Times Square?
2. Ela precisa fazer baldeacao? / Does she need to transfer?
A No, the line is direct.
B Yes, three times.
C Yes, on the bus.
3. Quantas paradas ate Times Square? / How many stops to Times Square?
A Six stops.
B Sixty stops.
C No stops.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Asking About Transport

GRAMMAR

Tres estruturas para o transporte. / Three structures for transport.

Structure / EstruturaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
Which + noun + goes to...?Escolher entre opcoes / Choose between optionsWhich bus goes to the park?
Where is the nearest + place?Achar o mais perto / Find the closestWhere is the nearest subway?
Do I need to + verb?Perguntar se e necessario / Ask if necessaryDo I need to transfer?

Quiz

A "Which subway goes to Times Square?"
B "Subway Times Square?"
C "Times Square is subway?"

C "Ticket how?"
