Lesson 17 -- Talking About Experiences

Series / Interview
A job interview scene -- talking about experiences

Choose a scene where someone talks about their career experiences. You will hear the present perfect for experiences (I have worked, I have travelled) and the past simple for finished moments (last year, I went). Notice when they use each tense.

Watch with English subtitles. Write 3 present perfect sentences (experiences) and 2 past simple sentences (finished moments). Then say them out loud about yourself.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Talking About Your Experience"

The speakers describe their work experiences and trips abroad. A perfect model for talking about clients you have visited, contracts you have negotiated, and conferences you went to.

Choose one short episode. Note 2 present perfect phrases (experiences) and 1 past simple phrase (a finished moment). Compare them with David's story from class.

YouTube / BBC
BBC Learning English -- "Present perfect vs past simple"

A short, clear lesson that compares the present perfect (I have visited) for experiences with the past simple (I visited last year) for finished moments. It also shows ever, never, already, and yet in action.

After watching, record a 90-second career story: three experiences with the present perfect and one finished moment with the past simple.
