Lesson 13 -- Clothes & Shopping for the Season

Series
Peppa Pig -- "New Shoes" / "Daddy Gets Fit"

Short, slow episodes where the characters talk about clothes and shoes: a jacket, boots, a dress. Simple sentences and clear pronunciation -- perfect for the words from today's lesson.

Watch with English subtitles. Each time you hear a clothes word (jacket, boots, dress, shoes), say it out loud and add "I wear...".

Podcast
Speak English Now -- "Clothes and Shopping for Clothes"

A slow, clear podcast about clothes and shopping: the words for jacket, dress and boots, and how to ask "How much is it?" and "Can I try it on?" in a store.

Repeat each phrase out loud. Then imagine you are in a store and record a short dialogue: ask the price and the size of one item.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Shopping for Clothes: How much is it?"

A short, practical lesson on today's language: clothes words and how to shop in English -- ask the price with "How much is it?", ask for a size, and try clothes on, with clear American English.

After watching, write five sentences about what you wear in each season: "In winter I wear...". Then read them aloud and check your pronunciation.
