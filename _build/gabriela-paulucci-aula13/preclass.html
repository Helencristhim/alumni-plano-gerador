Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Jacket -- a short coat you wear over your shirt
"It is cold, so I wear a jacket." (Jaqueta)
Boots -- tall shoes that keep your feet warm and dry
"In winter I wear my boots." (Botas)
Dress -- one piece of clothing that women often wear
"In summer she wears a light dress." (Vestido)
Shirt -- clothing for the top of your body, with buttons
"I wear a blue shirt to work." (Camisa)
Shoes -- you wear these on your feet to walk outside
"I wear comfortable shoes every day." (Sapatos)
Scarf -- soft cloth you wear around your neck when it is cold
"It is windy, so I wear a scarf." (Cachecol)
Gloves -- you wear these on your hands to keep them warm
"In winter I wear gloves on my hands." (Luvas)
T-shirt -- a simple, light shirt with short sleeves
"When it is hot, I wear a T-shirt." (Camiseta)
Size -- how big or small the clothing is: small, medium, large
"What size is this dress?" (Tamanho)

GRAMMAR

Gabriela goes to a clothes store. In summer she wears a light dress and comfortable shoes. In winter it is cold, so she wears a warm jacket, her boots, and gloves. Today she needs a new scarf, so she asks the assistant: "How much is this scarf?" The assistant says: "It is thirty reais. What size do you need?" Gabriela says: "Medium, please. Can I try it on?"

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Clothes & Shopping

GRAMMAR

Dizemos o que vestimos com I wear / I need + roupa, e perguntamos o preço com How much is it?. / We say what we put on with I wear / I need + clothing, and ask the price with How much is it?

Form / FormaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
I wear + roupaPara dizer o que você veste / To say what you put onI wear a jacket.
I need + roupaPara dizer o que você quer comprar / To say what you want to buyI need new boots.
How much is it?Para perguntar o preço / To ask the priceHow much is this scarf?
Can I try it on?Para experimentar a roupa / To try the clothing onCan I try it on?

Key point / Ponto-chave: use How much is it? para o preço de uma peça, e What size? para o tamanho. / Use How much is it? for the price of one item, and What size? for the size.
