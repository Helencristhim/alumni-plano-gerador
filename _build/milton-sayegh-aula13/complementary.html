Lesson 13 -- Closing the Deal: Negotiating Price & Terms

Series / Film
Shark Tank (ABC) -- any pitch with a back-and-forth on price or equity

A live masterclass in negotiation: counteroffers, concessions, leverage and the moment two sides either compromise or hit a deadlock. Watch how the investors and founders soften their asks and trade one thing for another to settle a deal.

Tip: pause on one offer and rephrase it as a polite indirect question: "Could you tell me...?", "Would you be open to...?".

Podcast
Negotiate Anything (Kwame Christian) -- any episode on price or "asking with confidence"

Practical, conversational advice on how to make a counteroffer, give a concession without losing leverage, and break a deadlock — exactly the toolkit from this class, modeled in real business language.

Tip: write down one negotiation phrase you hear, then say it back as a softened, indirect question.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Indirect questions" / polite question forms

A short, clear lesson on exactly the grammar from this class: how to soften a question with "Could you tell me...", "Do you know...", and "I was wondering if...", keeping statement order and dropping "do/does/did". Native speakers model it in natural contexts.

Tip: after watching, turn five direct questions about your own deals into polite, indirect ones out loud.
