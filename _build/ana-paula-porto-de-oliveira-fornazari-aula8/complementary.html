Lesson 8 -- Midpoint Review: How Far Has Ana Paula Come?

TED Talk
Grit: The Power of Passion and Perseverance -- Angela Lee Duckworth (TED)

A short, clear talk about why effort and perseverance matter more than talent when we learn something hard. It is full of the words from today: challenge, overcome, achieve, improve. Connection to Lesson 8: it is the perfect story about staying on a long journey and not giving up.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down three sentences she uses about effort and progress, then say your own version.

TEDx Talk
The First 20 Hours -- How to Learn Anything -- Josh Kaufman (TEDxCSU)

A friendly speaker explains how a small daily routine and the right habits help you improve fast at the start of any journey -- exactly like your English. Connection to Lesson 8: it uses simple, clear English about routines, habits, and early milestones.

Tip: pause and describe your own daily English routine in three or four sentences, using the present simple.

TED Talk
Try Something New for 30 Days -- Matt Cutts (TED)

A very short, easy talk about how a small new habit for thirty days can become an unforgettable turning point. It is slow, clear, and perfect for B1. Connection to Lesson 8: it is a real-life example of building a habit and reaching a personal milestone.

Tip: after watching, choose one new English habit for the second half of the course, and say why in one or two sentences.
