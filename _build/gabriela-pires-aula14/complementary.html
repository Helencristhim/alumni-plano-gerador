Lesson 14 -- Eating Out Like a Local

Series
Emily in Paris -- restaurant and dinner scenes (Season 1)

Watch the scenes where the characters eat out in Paris. Notice how people order food with "I would like" and "Could I have", and how they ask the waiter to recommend a dish.

Tip: Write down five restaurant words you hear and one polite order with "I would like" or "Could I have".

Podcast
Culips ESL -- "Ordering Food at a Restaurant"

A short, clear episode about eating out in English: booking a table, asking the waiter to recommend a dish, ordering an appetizer and a main course, and paying the check.

Tip: Record yourself ordering a full dinner: an appetizer, a main course, and ask for the check at the end.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "At a restaurant"

A focused lesson on eating out: how to order politely with "I would like" and "Could I have", and how to ask the waiter to recommend something. It reinforces today's polite ordering language.

Tip: After watching, write six sentences: three polite orders with "I would like", and three requests with "Could I have" or "Can you recommend".
