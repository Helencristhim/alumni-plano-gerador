Talking About Your Career Story -- present perfect with for & since

Tell your career story and say HOW LONG you have done something. Use the present perfect (have/has + past participle) with FOR + a period of time (I have worked here for twelve years) and SINCE + a start point (I have been a manager since 2018). Ask "How long have you...?" New words: position, hire, skill, background, field, senior, loyal, dedicated.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- For & Since

GRAMMAR

Para dizer HA QUANTO TEMPO, use o present perfect (have/has + particio) com FOR ou SINCE. FOR + um periodo de tempo (for five years, for two months) -- I have worked here for twelve years. SINCE + um ponto de inicio (since 2015, since March, since I joined) -- I have been a manager since 2018. Pergunta: How long have you + particio? -- How long have you worked at Sanofi? NUNCA "since five years" nem "for 2015". / Present perfect + FOR (a period) or SINCE (a start point) to say how long.

WordExample
FOR + a period of timeI have worked here for twelve years.
SINCE + a start point (year)I have been a manager since 2018.
SINCE + a start point (month)I have lived here since March.
FOR + a short periodI have known him for two months.
Question -- how longHow long have you worked at Sanofi?
