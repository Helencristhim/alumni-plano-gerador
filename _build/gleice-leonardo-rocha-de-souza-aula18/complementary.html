Lesson 18 -- Talking About Your Career Story

Series / Interview
A job interview scene -- telling your career story

Choose a scene where someone tells how long they have done their job. You will hear the present perfect with for (I have worked here for ten years) and since (I have been a manager since 2018). Notice when they use for and when they use since.

Watch with English subtitles. Write 3 sentences with for and 2 sentences with since. Then say them out loud about your own career.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Talking About Your Career"

The speakers describe their career history: how long they have worked in their field, since when they have held their position, and their background. A perfect model for telling your own story at Sanofi.

Choose one short episode. Note 2 phrases with for (a period) and 1 phrase with since (a start point). Compare them with David's career story from class.

YouTube / BBC
BBC Learning English -- "For and since with the present perfect"

A short, clear lesson on how to say how long with the present perfect: for + a period of time (for five years) and since + a start point (since 2015). It shows many examples for your career story.

After watching, record a 90-second career story: how long you have worked here (for), since when you have been in your position (since), and your background and field.
