ToolUseExample
Abilitycan / can't + verbI can answer questions.
Polite requestCould you + verb?Could you send the file?
Nowam / is / are + verb-ingI am writing a report.
Likeslike / enjoy + verb-ingI like working here.
Orderfirst / then / next / finallyFirst, I check the agenda.

Nothing new today — these are your five tools from Lessons 9 to 15. A workday uses all of them together.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"Right now I write a report."

✓ RIGHT

"Right now I am writing a report."

✗ WRONG

"Can you to send the file?"

✓ RIGHT

"Could you send the file?"

✗ WRONG

"I am can answer questions."

✓ RIGHT

"I can answer questions."

Use am/is/are + -ing for "right now". For a polite request, say "Could you" + verb (no "to"). With "can", use no "am" and no "to".

Dialogue

Planning the Day

R
Good morning, Danilo. What is on your agenda today?
D
Hi Rachel. First, I check the data report. It is my top priority.
R
Can you finish it before noon?
D
Yes, I can. Right now, I am preparing the data.
R
Great. Could you send me a short summary after that?
D
Of course. I will wrap up the report and send the summary.
R
Perfect. I am on a call at ten, so let's catch up at lunch.
D
Sounds good. I like talking about the project with you.
R
Me too. Let's sort out the new tasks together this afternoon.
D
Good plan. Finally, we can review everything at five.
