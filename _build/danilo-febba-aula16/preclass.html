GRAMMAR

Danilo and Rachel plan the day. Rachel asks, "What is on your agenda today?" Danilo says, "First, I check the data report. It is my top priority." Rachel asks, "Can you finish it before noon?" Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Your Workday Toolkit (Lessons 9-15)

GRAMMAR

Hoje não tem gramática nova: você junta as cinco ferramentas que aprendeu nas aulas 9-15 para descrever um dia de trabalho inteiro. (Today there is no new grammar: you combine the five tools from Lessons 9-15 to describe a whole workday.)

Ferramenta (PT)Estrutura (EN)
habilidadecan / can't + verbo: I can answer questions.
pedido educadoCould you + verbo? Could you send the file?
agoraam / is / are + verbo-ing: I am writing a report.
gostoslike / enjoy + verbo-ing: I like working here.
ordemfirst / then / next / finally + frase. Horários com at: at nine.

Atenção (erros comuns): para "agora", use present continuous (I am writing, não "I write"). Pedido educado é Could you + verbo (sem "to"). Com can, não use "am" nem "to" (I can answer, não "I am can" / "I can to"). Horários pedem at (at nine o'clock).
