Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Diana likes the solution, but she has too many options and a limited budget. She asks Rafael for his advice. He decides to propose two options. He says she should choose the pro plan, because it is more reliable. As an alternative, they could improve the old system first and consider an upgrade later. In the end, Rafael says he would recommend the pro plan, and he asks her to consider it carefully.

1. What does Diana ask Rafael for?
A His advice.
B A bigger office.
C A new color.
2. Which plan does Rafael recommend?
A The pro plan.
B No plan.
C The old system.
3. Which suggestion is correct?
A "You should choose the pro plan."
B "You should to choose the pro plan."
C "You should choosing the pro plan."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Should / Could / Would

GRAMMAR

Usado para fazer sugestões e recomendar uma solução. / Used to make suggestions and recommend a solution.

Form / FormaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
should + verbo baseSugestão forte / conselho / Strong suggestion or adviceYou should choose the pro plan
could + verbo baseOpção mais suave / A soft optionWe could improve it first
would + recommendRecomendação educada / A polite recommendationI would recommend the new one
Key point / Ponto-chaveUse o verbo base depois do modal. NUNCA use to e NUNCA adicione -s: diga "should choose", não "should to choose".
