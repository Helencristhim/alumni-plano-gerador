Chapter 3: When & While

Connect the Actions

while + was -ing · when + past simple · as soon as

Discover the Code

Listen and Find the Pattern

While I was waiting, it happened. (while + was -ing)

I was reading when a man sat down. (when + past simple)

As soon as she spoke, I knew her. (as soon as + past)

I was walking when it started to rain. (long + short)

FormUseExample
while + was/were -inga long background actionWhile I was waiting...
when + past simplea short action that interrupts...when a man sat down.
as soon as + past simplethe moment one thing led to anotherAs soon as she spoke, I knew.

The long action (while + was -ing) is the background. The short action (when + past simple) interrupts it.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"While I waiting, it happened."

✓ RIGHT

"While I was waiting, it happened."

✗ WRONG

"I was reading when he was sitting down."

✓ RIGHT

"I was reading when he sat down."

Chapter 4: Building the Story

Tânia Tells a Story

Something that happened at the airport

Dialogue

What Happened?

F
You look happy, Tânia! Did something happen on your trip?
T
Yes! Let me tell you a funny anecdote. While I was waiting for my flight, something incredible happened.
F
Really? What was it?
T
I was reading a magazine when a man sat next to me. As soon as he spoke, I realized he was from my hometown.
F
What an unbelievable coincidence! Was it embarrassing?
T
A little, at first! But luckily we talked for hours, and eventually he became a good friend.
