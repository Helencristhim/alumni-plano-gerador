Presenting Data and Insights -- Numbers & Trends

Present numbers, trends, and a clear recommendation to a C-level audience. New vocabulary: trend, surge, decline, peak, forecast, highlight, margin, breakdown. Two ways to describe change: verb + adverb ("revenue rose sharply") and "there was a + adjective + noun" ("there was a sharp rise"). Prepositions: rose by 8M (size of change), to 40M (final value).

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Trend -- the general direction in which something changes over time
"Sales follow an upward trend this year." (tendência)
Surge -- a sudden and large increase
"We saw a surge in digital demand." (disparada / aumento súbito)
Decline -- a steady decrease over a period of time
"The budget segment is in slow decline." (queda / declínio)
Peak -- the highest point a value reaches
"Revenue reached a new peak this quarter." (pico / auge)
Forecast -- a statement about what will happen, based on data
"Our forecast for next quarter is cautious." (previsão)
Highlight -- to draw attention to the most important point
"Let me highlight one key result." (destacar)
Margin -- the difference between revenue and cost, often as a percentage
"Our profit margin improved by three points." (margem)
Breakdown -- a detailed split of a number into its parts
"Here is the breakdown by region." (detalhamento)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Describing Trends

GRAMMAR

Há duas formas de descrever a mesma mudança. (1) VERBO + ADVÉRBIO: "revenue rose sharply". (2) "there was a + ADJETIVO + SUBSTANTIVO": "there was a sharp rise". Preposições: use by para o tamanho da mudança ("rose by 8M") e to para o valor final ("rose to 40M"). / There are two ways to describe the same change: verb + adverb, or "there was a + adjective + noun". Use by for the size of the change and to for the final value.

Verb + adverbThere was a + adjective + noun
Revenue rose sharply.There was a sharp rise in revenue.
Costs fell slightly.There was a slight fall in costs.
Demand grew steadily.There was steady growth in demand.
Regra de ouroby = tamanho da mudança (rose by 8M); to = valor final (rose to 40M).
