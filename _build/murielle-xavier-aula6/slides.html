Chapter 3

Going To

Decided plans and predictions based on evidence

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"We are going to expand the team next quarter."

"Applications are up, so hiring is going to be strong."

"We are not going to hire externally for senior roles."

"Are you going to launch it in March?"

FormUseExample
am / is / are + going to + base verbPlan already decidedWe are going to expand the team.
going to (prediction)Prediction from evidence nowApplications are up, so hiring is going to be strong.
negative: am / is / are + not + going toNegative planWe are not going to hire externally.
question: Am / Is / Are + subject + going toAsk about a planAre you going to launch it in March?
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"We will going to expand the team."

✓ RIGHT

"We are going to expand the team."

✗ WRONG

"We going to set up a new program."

✓ RIGHT

"We are going to set up a new program."

✗ WRONG

"They are going to expanding the market."

✓ RIGHT

"They are going to expand the market."

Use be (am/is/are) + going to + the base verb. Never mix "will" with "going to", and never add -ing after "going to".

Chapter 4

In the Meeting

Aligning on the plans and goals for next quarter

Dialogue

Aligning on Next Quarter

L
Hi Murielle. Before the board meeting, tell me: what is your main goal for next quarter?
M
Our main goal is faster hiring. We are going to expand the team by ten people, and the budget is already approved.
L
Great. Are you going to hire externally, or promote from inside?
M
For senior roles, we are not going to hire externally. Our strategy is to promote from inside and set up a faster onboarding program.
L
Sounds solid. What is your target for the onboarding time?
M
We aim to have every new hire ready in two weeks. That is our target, and I really think this plan is going to work.
L
I agree. When are you going to send me the roadmap?
M
I am going to send you the full roadmap on Friday. Please look at the priorities before our call.
Comprehension

Did You Catch It?

1. What is Murielle's main goal for next quarter?
Faster hiring; the team is going to expand by ten people.
2. Are they going to hire externally for senior roles?
No. Their strategy is to promote from inside and set up a faster onboarding program.
3. When is Murielle going to send the roadmap?
On Friday, so Liam can look at the priorities before the call.
