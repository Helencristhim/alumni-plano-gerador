A documentary about looking back on a long journey and taking stock of progress, milestones, and setbacks at the highest level. Watch how the athletes and coaches reflect on the past, the present, and what is coming next. Connection to Lesson 8: describing a journey using past, present, and future.

A clear review of the exact grammar of this lesson: past simple for finished actions, present continuous for now, and going to / will for the future. Connection to Lesson 8: telling your story across all three time frames.
