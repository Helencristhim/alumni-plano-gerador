Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Relative Clauses

Grammar

Clausula defining da informacao essencial: sem virgulas, com who (pessoas), that/which (coisas), where (lugares). Clausula non-defining da um extra: com virgulas e who/which (NUNCA that). / A defining clause gives essential information: no commas, with who (people), that/which (things), where (places). A non-defining clause adds extra information: with commas and who/which (NEVER that).

Tipo / TypeEnglishPortugues
Defining (people)The driver who knew a shortcut.O motorista que sabia um atalho.
Defining (things)The route that avoids the centre.A rota que evita o centro.
Non-defining (commas)The taxi, which was expensive, hit gridlock.O taxi, que era caro, pegou engarrafamento.
Place (where)The corner where we pulled over.A esquina onde encostamos.
Common mistakeNOT "The taxi, that was expensive..." Use which with commas.Non-defining nao usa that.
