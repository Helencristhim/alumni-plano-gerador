Aula 3 -- The Clinic Is Happening -- Plans, Timelines & Business Vision

Series
The Dropout -- Season 1, Episode 1: "I'm in a Hurry"

A founder pitches a state-of-the-art medical venture to investors, describing her timeline, milestones and vision. Listen for the future forms she uses to sell the plan: "we are going to...", "we are building...", "the plan is to...", and "it will change...". Direct connection to Lesson 3: talking about your business vision and timeline with confidence.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Note 3 sentences where a character talks about the future with "going to" or "will".

"WILL vs BE GOING TO" -- BBC Learning English

A clear lesson on when to use "will" (predictions and spontaneous decisions) and when to use "going to" (plans you have decided), plus a note on the present continuous for arrangements. Connection to Lesson 3: the exact grammar behind pitching your plans and timeline.
