Numbers, Budget & Forecast

Presenting financial numbers clearly in a strategic meeting: revenue, budget, forecast and targets. Key words: budget, forecast, revenue, growth, increase, decrease, percent, amount, spend/spent, target. Structure: comparatives for trends (higher than, lower than, more than) + talking about a change (increased / decreased).

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para escutar.

Budget -- orcamento (money planned for a period)
"Our budget is for one year."
Forecast -- previsao (what we expect for the future)
"The forecast for next year is strong."
Revenue -- receita (the money a business earns)
"Our revenue is higher this year."
Growth -- crescimento (when something gets bigger)
"We expect strong growth."
Increase -- aumentar / aumento (to go up)
"Revenue increased 12 percent."
Decrease -- diminuir / queda (to go down)
"Costs decreased this year."
Percent -- por cento (a part of one hundred)
"Revenue grew 12 percent."
Amount -- quantia / montante (how much money or quantity)
"The amount is higher than last year."
Spend / Spent -- gastar (to use money; spent = passado)
"We spent less this year."
Target -- meta / alvo (the goal you want to reach)
"Our target is 20 percent growth."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

In the semester meeting, Fernando presents the numbers to Sarah. "Our revenue is higher than last year," he says. "It increased 12 percent." Then he talks about costs: "We spent less. Our costs are lower than the budget." Finally, he gives the plan: "The forecast for next year is strong. Our target is 20 percent growth." "The numbers look very good," says Sarah.

1. Por que dizemos "higher than" e nao "more high than"?
A Porque "high" e plural.
B Adjetivos curtos formam o comparativo com -er + than (nunca "more").
C Porque a acao e no futuro.
2. Usamos "Revenue increased 12 percent" para:
A Falar de uma mudanca (a receita subiu).
B Pedir um cafe.
C Apresentar uma pessoa.
3. Which sentence says costs went down?
A "Costs are higher than the budget."
B "Our costs are lower than the budget."
C "Costs are the budget."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Comparatives & Trends

GRAMMAR

Como comparar numeros e mostrar uma tendencia (explicacao em ingles e portugues).

StructureUse / UsoExample
adjective + -er + thanComparar para cimaRevenue is higher than last year.
lower thanComparar para baixoCosts are lower than the budget.
increased / decreasedUma mudanca (subiu / caiu, +d)Revenue increased 12 percent.
more + nounMais quantidade — We have more growth this year. (NAO "more higher")
