Aula 13 -- Last Vacation

Tema da aula: Last Vacation -- telling travel stories with the past simple. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
Past Simple -- regular and irregular verbs in English

Watch a short, clear video about the past simple. Listen for regular verbs that end in -ed (traveled, walked, packed) and irregular verbs that change form (went, saw, ate, took, swam).

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote cinco verbos irregulares no passado sobre uma viagem sua.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "past simple" and irregular verbs

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about the past simple: how to add -ed to regular verbs, the most common irregular verbs, and how to make questions and negatives with did and did not.

Dica: escreva a história de uma viagem em seis frases no passado. Sublinhe os verbos regulares e os irregulares.

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about travel or holidays

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen to people tell stories about trips and vacations -- great input to review today's travel words and the past simple.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote toda frase no passado e toda palavra sobre viagem (beach, trip, souvenir, sunset, postcard).
