Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Log in -- acessar / entrar (no sistema)
"I log in every morning." (Acesso toda manha.)
Attend -- participar / comparecer (a uma reuniao)
"I am attending a meeting." (Estou em uma reuniao.)
Review -- revisar / analisar (documentos)
"I am reviewing the contract." (Estou revisando.)
Coordinate -- coordenar / alinhar (com outras pessoas)
"I coordinate with the team." (Coordeno com a equipe.)
Spreadsheet -- planilha (tabela no computador)
"I am updating the spreadsheet." (Atualizando a planilha.)
Follow up -- dar seguimento / cobrar retorno
"I am following up on two emails." (Dando seguimento.)
Currently -- atualmente / no momento
"I'm currently reviewing the order." (No momento, revisando.)
Still -- ainda (algo que continua)
"We are still waiting." (Ainda estamos esperando.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

Every morning I log in and check my emails. But right now, things are busy. I am reviewing the new contract. I am coordinating with the export team. We are still waiting for the prices. I am updating the spreadsheet, and I am following up on two emails. I am not attending the afternoon call today.

1. "Every morning I log in" vs "Right now I am reviewing". Why different? (Por que a diferenca?)
A Routine = simple; happening now = continuous (-ing). (Rotina vs agora.)
B They are the same. (Sao iguais.)
C One is the past. (Um e passado.)
2. "We are still waiting." What does "still" tell us? (O que "still" diz?)
A It is finished. (Acabou.)
B It continues, not finished yet. (Continua, ainda nao acabou.)
C It is in the past. (E passado.)
3. "I am following up on two emails." What are you doing? (O que voce faz?)
A Checking and asking for a reply. (Cobrando retorno.)
B Deleting them. (Apagando.)
C Printing them. (Imprimindo.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Continuous

Grammar

Como dizer o que esta acontecendo agora. / How to say what is happening now.

Form / FormaPattern / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Affirmative / Afirmativoam/is/are + verb-ingI am reviewing the contract.
Negative / Negativoam/is/are + not + verb-ingWe are not closing today.
Question / PerguntaAm/Is/Are + subject + verb-ingAre you joining the meeting?
Key idea / Ideia-chaveAgora = am/is/are + verbo-ing. Rotina = present simple. / Now vs routine.

Etapa 4: Quiz Situacional

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao profissional.

Your manager asks what you are doing now. You say: (O que faz agora?)
A "I review the contract every day now."
B "I am currently reviewing the contract."
C "I reviewing contract."
You report a pending task. You say: (Tarefa pendente.)
A "We still wait the prices."
B "We are still waiting for the prices."
C "We waiting prices still."
You ask if a colleague joins the call now. You ask: (Pergunta sobre agora.)
A "You joining the call?"
B "Are you joining the call?"
C "Do you joining the call?"
