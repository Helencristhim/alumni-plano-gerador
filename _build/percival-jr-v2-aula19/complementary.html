Aula 19 -- Presenting Your Report

Tema da aula: presenting your report aloud -- deliver your findings to an audience, walk them through the sections, point out the key figures, emphasise what matters, and recommend an action with the first conditional (if we approve this control, the risk will drop). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Short lessons and quizzes on the first conditional: if + present simple, will + verb. Perfect for making recommendations -- "if we approve this control, the risk will drop".
