Aula 19 -- Making & Changing an Appointment

Tema da aula: making and changing an appointment -- booking a time slot and politely rescheduling when plans change (Are you free on...? / Would Thursday be convenient? / I am afraid we have to reschedule / Could we move it to Thursday?). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"Making appointments & rescheduling in English" -- business English video

Watch a short, clear video about booking a meeting and changing it. Listen for "Are you free on...?", "Would that be convenient?", "I am afraid we have to reschedule", and "Could we move it to...?".

Dica: anote uma frase para marcar (Are you free on...?) e uma para remarcar (Could we move it to...?).

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "Arranging and changing meetings at work"

Short lessons and quizzes to practise the language of appointments: asking if someone is free, confirming a convenient time, and rescheduling politely when there is a clash.

Dica: escreva um convite curto -- marque um horário, depois remarque. Confirme que usa "on Wednesday" (não "in") e "move it to Thursday" (não "for").

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen for how people book times, talk about being free or busy, and move a meeting when something clashes.
