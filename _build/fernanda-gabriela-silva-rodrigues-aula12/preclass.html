Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Estas sao as palavras-chave da aula. Ouca cada uma e leia o exemplo. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Arrange -- marcar / combinar
"Let's arrange a call for Monday." (Vamos marcar uma ligacao para segunda.)
Reschedule -- remarcar (para novo horario)
"We need to reschedule the call." (Precisamos remarcar a ligacao.)
Postpone -- adiar (para mais tarde)
"Let's postpone the visit to next week." (Vamos adiar a visita para a proxima semana.)
Appointment -- compromisso / hora marcada
"I have an appointment at three." (Tenho um compromisso as tres.)
Agenda -- pauta / temas da reuniao
"I will send the agenda before the call." (Vou enviar a pauta antes da ligacao.)
Slot -- horario livre / espaco na agenda
"Is there a free slot at ten?" (Ha um horario livre as dez?)
Set up -- preparar / organizar
"Could you set up the online room?" (Voce poderia preparar a sala online?)
Plan ahead -- planejar com antecedencia
"We plan ahead for the busy quarter." (Planejamos com antecedencia para o trimestre cheio.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

Daniel wants to arrange the plan for next week. He says: "On Monday, we are going to confirm the shipment. On Wednesday, I am going to meet the supplier. But I am not going to be here on Friday." Fernanda opens the agenda. She says: "Let's reschedule our call. Is there a free slot on Thursday?" They plan ahead and choose a new appointment: Thursday at ten. Fernanda is going to set up the room.

1. "We are going to confirm..." -- what is this about? (Sobre o que e?)
A A plan for the future. (Um plano para o futuro.)
B Something that happened in the past. (Algo do passado.)
C Something happening right now. (Algo agora.)
2. Why do they reschedule the call? (Por que remarcam?)
A The supplier is late. (O fornecedor esta atrasado.)
B Daniel is not going to be there on Friday. (Daniel nao estara la na sexta.)
C The agenda is wrong. (A pauta esta errada.)
3. After "going to", how is the verb? (Como fica o verbo?)
A With -s: "going to sends". (Com -s.)
B No -s: "going to send". (Sem -s.)
C In the past: "going to sent". (No passado.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Be + Going To

Grammar

Como falar de planos futuros. / How to talk about future plans.

Form / FormaStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Afirmativoam/is/are + going to + verboI am going to send it.
Negativoam/is/are + not + going toI am not going to be here.
InterrogativoAm/Is/Are + ... + going to?Are you going to join?
verbo sem -sdepois de going toShe is going to send (NAO sends)
Usoplanos / intencoes do futurotomorrow, next week, on Monday
