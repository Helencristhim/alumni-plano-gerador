Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Frequency Expressions

GRAMMAR

How to say how often you do something.

ExpressionUse it forExample
once / twice / three times + a + perioda number of times in a periodI walk twice a day.
every + morning / day / weekeach one, with no breakI sleep well every night.
positionusually at the end of the sentenceI do exercise three times a week.

Remember: once = 1 · twice = 2 · three times = 3, then "a" + day / week / month. Use every + a singular word: every day, every morning, every night.
