What Happened?

Contando uma história curta de trabalho no passado. Verbos-chave (irregulares): go/went, have/had, make/made, find/found, get/got, write/wrote, take/took, meet/met. Gramática: past simple irregular + was/were + did/didn't para perguntas e negativas.

Vocabulary

Go -- to move to a place / past: went (ir)
"I went to the meeting."
Have -- to possess or experience / past: had (ter)
"We had a problem on Monday."
Make -- to create or produce / past: made (fazer / criar)
"I made a small mistake."
Find -- to discover something / past: found (achar / descobrir)
"She found the error fast."
Get -- to receive or obtain / past: got (obter / receber)
"We got good results."
Write -- to put words on a page or screen / past: wrote (escrever)
"I wrote a short note."
Take -- to need time, or to carry / past: took (levar / tomar)
"It took two days."
Meet -- to come together with someone / past: met (conhecer / encontrar)
"I met the new client."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia a história e responda às perguntas. (Repare nos verbos no passado em negrito.)

Last Monday, Danilo had a hard day. The main system went down in the morning. The team met in a call, and they found the cause fast: a bad update. They made a small fix. It took two hours, but they got the system back. After that, Danilo wrote a short report. "Did you fix everything?" his manager asked. "Yes, we were ready by noon," Danilo said. It was a hard day, but the team worked well.

1. Por que dizemos "went" e não "goed"?
A Porque a ação ainda não terminou.
B Porque "go" é irregular: muda de forma no passado.
C Porque todo verbo no passado recebe -ed.
2. Depois de "did" (em perguntas e negativas), qual forma do verbo usamos?
A A forma base: "Did you fix?" / "I didn't go."
B A forma do passado: "Did you fixed?"
C O verbo com -ing: "Did you fixing?"
3. Com "we", "you" e "they", usamos "was" ou "were"?
A "was" (We was ready).
B "were" (We were ready).
C Os dois valem igual.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Irregular Past Simple

GRAMMAR

Como usar o past simple com verbos irregulares, was/were e did.

FormUse (PT)Example
Irregular verb (no -ed)Muda de forma: go→went, have→had, make→madeI went to the office.
More irregularsfind→found, get→got, write→wrote, take→took, meet→metWe found the error.
was / were (to be)I/he/she/it = was; you/we/they = wereI was late. They were ready.
did not (didn't) + base verbNegativo (forma base, sem mudar)I didn't go home.
did + you + base verbPergunta: Did you go? / What did you do?

Atenção (erro comum): Verbos irregulares NÃO recebem -ed. Diga "I went" (não "I goed"). Depois de did, use a forma base: "Did you go?" (não "Did you went?"). E com we/you/they use were: "We were busy" (não "We was busy").
