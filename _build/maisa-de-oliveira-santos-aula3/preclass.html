Falar sobre o seu trabalho sem travar: manage, analyze, report, client, responsible for, deadline, spreadsheet, task. Gramática: Present Simple (I work / she works; perguntas com do / does). Missão: descrever o próprio trabalho quando o David perguntar "What do you do?".

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

Manage -- to lead people or a project
"I manage a small team." (Gerenciar / administrar)
Analyze -- to study numbers or data carefully
"I analyze the numbers every day." (Analisar)
Report -- a document with information or results
"I write a report for my manager." (Relatório)
Client -- a person or company that buys your work
"I am responsible for two big clients." (Cliente)
Responsible for -- the job or duty that is yours
"I am responsible for the big accounts." (Responsável por)
Deadline -- the last day to finish something
"I always have a deadline on Friday." (Prazo)
Spreadsheet -- a table of numbers on the computer
"My spreadsheet is my best friend at work." (Planilha)
Task -- a small piece of work to do
"I have many tasks every day." (Tarefa)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Simple

GRAMMAR

O Present Simple fala do que fazemos SEMPRE — a rotina de trabalho. / We use it for routines and facts.

Sujeito / SubjectAfirmativo (+)Negativo (-)Pergunta (?)
I / you / we / theyI work in finance.I do not work on Sunday.Do you work here?
he / she / itShe works in finance.She does not work on Sunday.Does she work here?

Atenção: com he / she / it, o verbo ganha -s (she works, he analyzes). Em perguntas e frases negativas, use do (I/you/we/they) ou does (he/she/it) — e o verbo principal perde o -s: "Does she work?" (nunca "Does she works?").
