Lesson 20 -- Saying Goodbye

Series
Suits -- a client farewell or send-off scene (any season)

Watch a scene where a business relationship is closed warmly. Notice how people thank each other, talk about future plans, and agree to keep in touch.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down two sentences with "going to" and one phrase used to say goodbye.

Podcast
Plain English -- "Saying Goodbye and Staying in Touch in English"

A practical episode on farewells: how to thank a host, talk about future plans, and keep a professional relationship alive after a visit.

Record three lines: one "I am going to..." plan, one thank-you, and one way to stay in touch.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Talking about the future: be going to"

A focused lesson on "be going to" for plans and intentions. It directly reinforces today's grammar for talking about what you are going to do next.

After watching, write six sentences about your real plans for next week, all with "be going to".
