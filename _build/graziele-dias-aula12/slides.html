Chapter 3: Was / Were & -ed

Talk About the Past

I was · we were · verb + -ed

Past Forms

Two Ways to the Past

"I worked as a nurse." · verb + -ed

"I was a student in 2014." · to be (I)

"We were a small team." · to be (we)

"Last year, I studied and I became a coordinator."

verb + -ed = passado · was = era/estava (eu) · were = eram/estavam (nós)

Discover the Pattern

Find the Past

"I studied nursing at the university."

"I graduated in 2015."

"I was a nurse for ten years."

"We were a big team last year."

NowPast
work / study / join+ -ed — worked, studied, joined
I am / he iswas — "I was a nurse."
we are / they arewere — "We were a team."
become (special)became — no -ed: "I became a coordinator."

Maioria dos verbos: + -ed · was = eu/ele/ela · were = nós/eles · become → became (especial)

Wrong vs Right
