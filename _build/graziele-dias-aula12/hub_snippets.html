Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Sao verbos no passado. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Graduated -- formei / formou (passado de graduate)
"I graduated in 2015." (Eu me formei em 2015.)
Worked -- trabalhei / trabalhou (passado de work)
"I worked as a nurse." (Eu trabalhei como enfermeira.)
Trained -- treinei / treinou (passado de train)
"I trained new nurses." (Eu treinei novas enfermeiras.)
Joined -- entrei (em) / juntei-me (passado de join)
"I joined Hospital Morumbi." (Eu entrei no Hospital Morumbi.)
Became -- tornei-me / virei (passado especial de become)
"I became a coordinator." (Eu me tornei coordenadora.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

This is my story. I studied nursing at the university. I graduated in 2015. Then I worked as a nurse for many years. We were a big team, and I trained new nurses. Two years ago, I joined Hospital Morumbi. Last year, I became a coordinator. I was very proud!

1. What did Graziele study? (O que a Graziele estudou?)
A Nursing. (Enfermagem.)
B Medicine. (Medicina.)
C Law. (Direito.)
2. Which word is the past of "be" with "we"? (Qual e o passado de "be" com "we"?)
A was (era - eu)
B were (eram - nos)
C are (sao - agora)
3. What did she become last year? (O que ela se tornou no ano passado?)
A A student. (Uma estudante.)
B A coordinator. (Uma coordenadora.)
C A doctor. (Uma medica.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- O Passado Simples

Grammar

Como falar do passado. / How to talk about the past.

Forma / FormUso / Use
verb + -edmaioria dos verbos no passado -- work → worked, study → studied, join → joined
waspassado de am/is (eu, ele, ela) -- "I was a nurse." (Eu era enfermeira.)
werepassado de are (voce, nos, eles) -- "We were a team." (Nos eramos uma equipe.)
became (especial)passado de become, SEM -ed -- "I became a coordinator." (Eu me tornei coordenadora.)
Time markersyesterday (ontem), last year (ano passado), in 2015 (em 2015), two years ago (dois anos atras)

A maioria dos verbos: + -ed. O verbo "to be": was (eu/ele/ela) e were (nos/eles). "become" e especial: vira "became".
