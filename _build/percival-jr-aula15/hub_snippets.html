Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

These are the key words for this lesson. Tap Listen to hear the pronunciation.

Join -- to enter and connect to the call
"Click the link to join the meeting." (Entrar / participar)
Log in -- to enter with your name or account
"Please log in with your full name." (Fazer login / entrar)
Mute -- to turn off your microphone
"Please mute when you are not speaking." (Mutar / silenciar)
Unmute -- to turn your microphone back on
"Can you unmute? I cannot hear you." (Desmutar / reativar o som)
Share screen -- to show the others what is on your screen
"I will share my screen to show the report." (Compartilhar a tela)
Camera -- the device that shows your video
"Please turn on your camera so we can see you." (Câmera)
Freeze -- when the picture stops moving
"Your video froze. Can you turn it off and on?" (Travar / congelar)
Echo -- when you hear the sound repeated
"There is an echo. Can you mute, please?" (Eco)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Sound & Screen Problems

GRAMMAR

Numa video call você descreve o problema com I can't + verbo (I can't hear you) e pede a solução com Can you / Could you + verbo (Can you unmute?). Depois de can't e can you usamos o verbo na forma base, sem "to" e sem "-ing". / On a video call you say the problem with I can't + verb, and ask for the fix with Can you / Could you + verb. After can't and can you we use the base verb.

Função / FunctionPatternExample
Dizer o problema / Say the problemI can't + verbI can't hear you.
Dizer o problema / Say the problemI can't + verbI can't see you.
Pedir a solução / Ask for the fixCan you + verb?Can you unmute?
Pedir com educação / Ask politelyCould you + verb, please?Could you share your screen, please?

Key point / Ponto-chave: depois de can't e can you usamos o verbo base, sem "to" e sem "-ing": I can't hear you (não "I can't to hear"), Can you unmute? (não "Can you to unmute?"). / After can't and can you we use the base verb, with no "to" and no "-ing".

Aula 15 -- On a Video Call

Tema da aula: video calls -- joining, sharing your screen, and fixing sound problems (I can't hear you / Can you unmute? / I will share my screen). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.
