Aula 15 -- On a Video Call

Tema da aula: video calls -- joining, sharing your screen, and fixing sound problems (I can't hear you / Can you unmute? / I will share my screen). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"Video call English -- joining, sharing your screen and sound problems" -- business English video

Watch a short, clear video about being on a video meeting: how to join, how to ask people to mute or unmute, and what to say when you cannot hear or see someone. Listen for "Can you unmute?", "I can't hear you", and "I will share my screen".

Dica: anote duas frases para dizer o problema (I can't...) e duas para pedir a solução (Can you...?).

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "Talking about technology problems"

Short lessons and quizzes to practise the language of video calls and tech problems: mute, unmute, share screen, freeze, echo. Perfect to feel confident in a real online meeting.

Dica: escreva três frases -- dizer o problema, pedir a solução com Can you, e pedir com educação com Could you, please.

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about working remotely or online meetings

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen for how people talk on video calls: joining, sharing screens, and fixing sound and camera problems.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote uma expressão nova para usar na sua próxima video call.
