Complementary Materials — Lesson 8

Series
The Repair Shop — BBC: any episode

Real experts repair and restore broken objects for their owners. Perfect for hearing the language of services: repair, replace, restore and inspect, used naturally.

Tip: every time an owner gets something fixed, say it with the causative: "She had her clock repaired."

Podcast
6 Minute English — BBC: "The right to repair"

A short, clear B2 podcast about repairing things instead of replacing them. It reuses today's service vocabulary in a real-world topic for teenagers.

Tip: write down four service words you hear (repair, replace, fix, technician), then make a causative sentence with each.

BBC Learning English — "Causative: have/get something done"

A short, clear grammar video that shows exactly how to use have and get something done, with plenty of service examples to copy out loud.

Tip: after watching, describe five things you had done this month using have or get.
