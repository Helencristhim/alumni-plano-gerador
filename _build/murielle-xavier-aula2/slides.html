Chapter 3

Routines & Frequency

Present simple for habits, routines and facts

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I oversee a team of business partners."

"My team handles compensation."

"I usually start the week with a call."

"Does your team handle salaries?"

StructureUseExample
I / You / We + verbRoutines (no -s)I oversee the team.
She / He / It + verb+sThird person adds -sShe handles salaries.
Adverb + verbFrequency before the verbI usually start at nine.
Do / Does + subject + verbQuestion formDoes your team handle salaries?
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"My team handle compensation."

✓ RIGHT

"My team handles compensation."

✗ WRONG

"Does she handles salaries?"

✓ RIGHT

"Does she handle salaries?"

✗ WRONG

"I start usually at nine."

✓ RIGHT

"I usually start at nine."

Add -s after she/he/it. After "does", use the base verb. Put frequency adverbs before the main verb.

Chapter 4

Explaining Your Role

Onboarding a new international colleague

Dialogue

Liam's First Day

L
Hi Murielle, I'm Liam, from the Australia office. So, what does your team actually do here?
M
Hi Liam, welcome! I oversee the HR business partner team. We support the corporate areas.
L
Nice. Who do you report to?
M
I report to the people director. I also liaise with the managers in Brazil and Chile.
L
Got it. Does your team handle compensation?
M
Yes, we handle compensation, and we also recruit and retain talent.
L
That's a lot. Is it always the same process?
M
It depends on the business unit. Let's talk again this week.
