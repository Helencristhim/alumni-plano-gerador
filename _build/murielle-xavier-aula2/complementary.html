Lesson 2 -- This Is What I Do -- Describing Your HR Role

Podcast
WorkLife with Adam Grant (TED) -- any episode on teams and management

An organizational psychologist explores how people work, lead teams, and build culture. Notice how guests describe what they do and how their teams operate, all in natural present simple. Direct connection to Lesson 2: real professionals explaining their roles and routines.

Tip: write down 5 present simple sentences a guest uses to describe their work, and copy the structure for your own role.

Series
The Office (US) -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

A workplace comedy where every character explains their role and daily routine to the camera. Listen for how they describe what they do, who they report to, and what their department handles, all in the present simple. Direct connection to Lesson 2: describing roles, teams, and routines at work.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Note 3 sentences where a character describes their job with the present simple.

View on IMDb ↗
YouTube
"Talking About Your Job in English" -- BBC Learning English

A clear lesson on describing your job, your responsibilities, and your daily routine, with the present simple and frequency adverbs. Connection to Lesson 2: the exact structures you use to explain your HR role to an international colleague.

Tip: pause and build your own sentences with oversee, liaise with, handle, and report to. Try one with an Australian-accent clip to train your ear.
