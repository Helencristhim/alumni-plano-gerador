Aula 16 — Used To: Past Habits and Routines

Conteúdos para reforçar como falar de hábitos do passado — used to + verbo base, com nowadays para contrastar com hoje. Marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
Halt and Catch Fire — any "how we used to build it" scene

Characters look back on old technology and how the work routine changed over the years. Listen for "we used to...", "did you use to...?" and the contrast with how things are done now — the exact forms from Lesson 16.

Dica: anote uma frase com "used to" (o hábito antigo) e uma com "nowadays" (hoje).

Podcast
Darknet Diaries / Soft Skills Engineering — "how we used to do things" episodes

Engineers compare the old manual way of working with the automated way today. Hear "used to" for the past routine and the present simple plus "nowadays" for the current reality.

Dica: pause e repita cada frase com "used to" que você ouvir.

YouTube
"USED TO — past habits (affirmative, negative, questions)" (English lesson)

A short, clear video on "used to" for past habits, including the negative and the question where the "d" disappears. Maps directly onto Daniela’s migration review with Marcus in Lesson 16.

Dica: depois do vídeo, descreva uma rotina sua em 4 frases, usando used to e nowadays.
