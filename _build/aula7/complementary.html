Supplementary Materials -- Lesson 7

Lesson theme: making your case. Watch and listen for how people give an opinion, agree, disagree politely, and argue consequences with the first conditional ("If we do X, we'll get Y").

Series
The Newsroom -- Season 1, Episode 1 ("We Just Decided To")

The famous opening debate is a masterclass in making a case: stating a strong opinion, backing it with reasons, and handling pushback. Notice the opinion frames and the if/then arguments.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down 3 phrases used to state an opinion or disagree, then reuse them in a Quinto Andar strategy debate.

Podcast
WorkLife with Adam Grant -- on disagreeing well

Adam Grant on how to argue productively and disagree without damaging relationships. Listen for diplomatic disagreement language and how speakers find common ground.

Tip: pick an episode on conflict or persuasion. Note 5 phrases used to disagree politely or to build on someone's idea.

A focused lesson with 14 professional phrases to agree and disagree in meetings, from strong agreement to polite disagreement. Exactly the opinion frames of this lesson.
