Lesson 13 -- On the Phone

Series
The Office (US) -- Season 2, Episode 2: "The Dundies" (phone scenes)

Office workers answer and make calls all day. Listen for the phone phrases: "This is...", "Can I speak to...?", "Could you hold on?", "Can I take a message?". Connection to Lesson 13: real office phone talk at work.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down 5 phone phrases you hear and say them aloud.

A short, clear episode at A2-B1 speed about answering and making calls at work. Connection to Lesson 13: it reinforces the fixed phone phrases and polite requests with can / could.
