Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. A tradução em português está ao lado. Toque em Listen.

Request -- to politely ask for something (pedido/solicitar)
"I have a small request, if that's okay."
Offer -- to say you will help or give something (oferecer)
"Can I offer you some help?"
Reschedule -- to change a meeting to a new time (remarcar)
"Could we reschedule for Thursday?"
Clarify -- to make something clearer (esclarecer)
"Could you clarify what you mean?"
Misunderstanding -- when people understand something differently (mal-entendido)
"I think there's a small misunderstanding."
Permission -- when someone lets you do something (permissão)
"May I have your permission to leave early?"
Favor -- a kind act you ask someone to do (favor)
"Could you do me a favor?"
Reach out -- to contact someone (entrar em contato)
"I'll reach out to you by email."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas.

Camila needed to change a meeting. She wrote a polite message: "Hi, I have a small request. Could we possibly reschedule our call to Thursday?" Her client replied kindly and offered to help. During the call, the client spoke quickly, so Camila said, "Sorry, I didn't catch that. Could you repeat it, please?" There was a small misunderstanding about the time, so she asked, "What do you mean by 'early afternoon'?" They cleared it up. Camila then said, "I'll reach out by email to confirm." She handled the whole situation politely and professionally.

1. How did Camila make her request polite?
A She used "Could we possibly reschedule...?"
B She said "Change the meeting."
C She did not ask, she just decided.
2. What did Camila do when the client spoke too fast?
A She pretended to understand.
B She asked him to repeat it politely.
C She ended the call.
3. How did Camila handle the misunderstanding about the time?
A She asked, "What do you mean by 'early afternoon'?"
B She got angry.
C She ignored it.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Requests, Offers & Clarification

GRAMMAR

Modais e níveis de formalidade. / Modals and levels of formality.

Job / FunçãoEnglishPortuguês
Request (casual → formal)Can you...? / Could you...? / Would you be able to...?Você pode...? / Você poderia...? / Você teria como...?
OfferCan I help you with...? / Would you like...?Posso te ajudar com...? / Você gostaria de...?
PermissionCan I...? / Could I...? / May I...? (formal)Posso...? / Eu poderia...? / Permite que eu...?
ClarifyCould you repeat that? / What do you mean by...?Pode repetir? / O que você quer dizer com...?
⚠ CarefulWould you mind + verb-ing (Would you mind helping?), not "to help". After "did not", use the base verb.

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situação.

You ask a client for a document. The most polite version is:
A "Could you possibly send me the document?"
B "Send the document."
C "I want the document."
Which sentence is correct?
A "Would you mind to wait?"
B "Would you mind waiting?"
C "Would you mind wait?"
You did not understand. The best response is:
A "Sorry, I didn't catch that. Could you say it again?"
B (nod and pretend)
C "Yes." (without understanding)
You want to offer help. You say:
A "Let me know if you need anything."
B "You need help, give it to me."
C "Help yourself."

Reflection
