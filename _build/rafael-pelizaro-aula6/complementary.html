Lesson 6 -- Handling Pushback

Series
The Office (US) -- Season 3, Episode 23: "Beach Games"

Managers and team members disagree about decisions, push back on plans, and try to reach a compromise. Listen for how characters raise concerns and object without becoming aggressive.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down two moments of pushback. Then rewrite one rude line to make it professional, using "I see your point, but...".

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Disagreeing Diplomatically"

A clear episode on how to disagree politely at work: softening phrases, raising a concern, and proposing a compromise instead of saying no directly.

Record a 90-second audio where you disagree with a stakeholder. Include an opinion frame, a first conditional, an objection, and a compromise.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "The First Conditional"

A focused lesson on the first conditional (If + present, ... will / may + verb). Directly reinforces today's grammar discovery for explaining consequences.

After watching, write six first-conditional sentences about your project, for example "If the vendor is late, we will need a backup plan." Read them aloud.
