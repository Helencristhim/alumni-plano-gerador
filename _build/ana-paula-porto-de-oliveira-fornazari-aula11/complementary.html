Lesson 11 -- Predictions & Promises

Podcast
6 Minute English -- Will robots take our jobs? (BBC Learning English)

Two hosts make predictions about the future of work: what jobs will change, what will probably disappear, and what will stay. The English is clear and the key words are explained as they go. Connection to Lesson 11: it is full of predictions with will, likely, and expect -- exactly today's grammar.

Tip: every time you hear a prediction, pause and repeat it as your own sentence with "I think it will ..." or "I doubt it will ...".

How to Use Will Future for Predictions (OnScreen Academy)

A slow, friendly B1 lesson that shows exactly when and how to use will for predictions, with many simple examples about the weather, sport, and daily life. It repeats the base-verb pattern clearly. Connection to Lesson 11: it is the grammar you practiced today, explained step by step.

Tip: pause after each example and make one true prediction about your own week with will or won't.

Grammar Video
WILL / WON'T = Decision, Offer, Promise (Teachers Mark and Matt)

A short, clear video on the other side of today's grammar: using will for a decision you make right now, an offer to help, and a promise. It gives natural, everyday examples and simple explanations. Connection to Lesson 11: this is the "I'll help you" and "I promise I will" part of the lesson.

Tip: after the video, imagine a friend needs help and make three quick offers out loud, each starting with "I'll ...".
