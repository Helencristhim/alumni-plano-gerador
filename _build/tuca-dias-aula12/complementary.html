Lesson 12 -- Emergency! Health & Safety Abroad

Series / Travel
Wolters World -- "Travel Health: Staying Safe Abroad" (YouTube)

A friendly traveler explains how to find a pharmacy, describe your symptoms, and what to do in an emergency in another country. Full of natural first conditional and must / have to.

Watch one video with English subtitles. Write three sentences with if + will about staying healthy on a trip.

Podcast
All Ears English -- "At the Doctor and Pharmacy in English"

A short, clear episode about describing how you feel and asking for medicine in English. You will hear the words fever, painkiller, and symptom used in real situations.

After listening, record a one-minute voice note describing symptoms and asking a pharmacist for help. Use the words pharmacy, fever, painkiller, and medicine.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "The First Conditional: if + present, will"

A practical grammar video that explains how to talk about real future possibilities and their results, with clear examples. Exactly today's grammar focus.

After watching, write four sentences about your own travel plans, each using if + present, will + base verb.
