Chapter 3: Asking Questions

How to Ask

Small talk is made of questions

Discover the Code

Listen and Find the Pattern

"What do you do?"

"Where do you work?"

"Where are you from?"

"Do you like coffee?"

Question wordAsk aboutExample
Whata thing / a jobWhat do you do?
Wherea placeWhere do you work?
Howa way / a feelingHow are you?
Do youyes / no answerDo you like coffee?

With most verbs, use do you + verb. With the verb be, use are you (Where are you from?).

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"Where you work?"

✓ RIGHT

"Where do you work?"

✗ WRONG

"What you do?"

✓ RIGHT

"What do you do?"

Chapter 4: Meeting the Team

A Hello at the Door

Anna meets Bruno, a colleague

Dialogue

Anna Meets Bruno

B
Good morning! Welcome to the office. My name is Bruno.
A
Hello, Bruno. Nice to meet you. I am Anna, from the financial team.
B
Nice to meet you too. Where are you from, Anna?
A
I am from Minas Gerais. What do you do here?
B
I am an analyst. Our team is on the third floor. Do you like coffee?
A
Yes, I do! Thank you, Bruno. I am glad to be here.
