Etapa 1.1: Palavras do primeiro dia

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra nova e leia o exemplo. Estas palavras servem para conhecer a equipe. / Listen to each new word and read the example.

Colleague -- colega de trabalho
"Bruno is my new colleague."
Team -- equipe / time
"My team is small and friendly."
Welcome -- bem-vindo / bem-vinda
"Welcome to the office, Anna!"
Floor -- andar (do predio)
"Our team is on the third floor."
Coffee -- cafe
"We have coffee together at nine."
Department -- departamento
"I work in the financial department."
Glad -- contente / feliz
"I am glad to meet you."

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o primeiro dia da Anna e responda as perguntas. / Read Anna's first day and answer.

It is Anna's first day at the office. A colleague says: "Welcome to the team!" His name is Bruno. He asks: "Where are you from?" Anna says: "I am from Minas Gerais." Then she asks: "What do you do?" Bruno is an analyst on the third floor. He asks: "Do you like coffee?" Anna is glad to be there.

1. What does Bruno say to Anna first?
A "Welcome to the team!"
B "Goodbye."
C "The bags are heavy."
2. How does Bruno ask about Anna's city?
A "Where you from?"
B "Where are you from?"
C "You from where?"
3. How does Anna ask about Bruno's job?
A "What you do?"
B "What do you do?"
C "You do what?"

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Asking Questions

Grammar

Para fazer perguntas, use uma palavra de pergunta + do you + verbo. Com o verbo be, use are you. / To ask questions, use a question word + do you + verb. With the verb be, use are you.

Palavra / WordEnglishPortugues
What (coisa)What do you do?O que voce faz?
Where (lugar)Where do you work?Onde voce trabalha?
Where + beWhere are you from?De onde voce e?
How (modo)How are you?Como voce esta?
Do you (sim/nao)Do you like coffee?Voce gosta de cafe?

Etapa 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao.

You meet a new colleague. What do you say?
A "Hello, nice to meet you."
B "The bags are heavy."
C "Goodbye."
You want to know your colleague's job. What do you ask?
A "What you do?"
B "What do you do?"
C "Where is the floor?"
A colleague offers you coffee. What do you say?
A "I am busy."
B "Yes, please. Thank you!"
C "I take a message."
