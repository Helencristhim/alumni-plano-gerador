Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia como a Anna negocia com um comprador e responda as perguntas. / Read how Anna negotiates with a buyer and answer.

A buyer says the price is high. Anna makes an offer. She says: "If you buy more, I can give a discount." Then she asks: "Would you accept this offer?" They talk about the terms. The buyer says yes and they accept the deal. Anna sends the agreement.

1. What does the buyer think about the price?
A It is high.
B It is low.
C It is free.
2. When does Anna give a discount?
A If you pay late.
B If you buy more.
C Never.
3. What does Anna send at the end?
A The agreement.
B A menu.
C A coffee.

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Offers & Conditions

Grammar

Para uma condicao, comece com If + presente (you buy) e use can ou will no resultado. Para oferecer de forma educada, use Would you...? / Start with If + present, then use can or will. Offer politely with Would you...?

EstruturaEnglishPortugues
If + present, can + verbIf you buy more, I can give a discount.Se voce comprar mais, posso dar um desconto.
If + present, will + verbIf you pay now, we will deliver fast.Se voce pagar agora, entregaremos rapido.
Would you...?Would you accept this offer?Voce aceitaria esta oferta?
We have a deal.We have a deal.Temos um acordo / negocio fechado.

Etapa 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao.

The buyer wants a lower price. What do you say?
A "If you buy more, I can give a discount."
B "No price."
C "You buy now."
You want the buyer to accept. How do you ask politely?
A "You accept?"
B "Would you accept this offer?"
C "Accept now."
The buyer says yes. How do you close?
A "Maybe later deal."
B "We have a deal. Let me send the agreement."
C "Deal no."
