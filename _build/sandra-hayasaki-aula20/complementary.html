Aula 20 — Telling Your Story

Series
This Is Us — characters telling the story of their lives

A warm family drama that moves between past and present, with people remembering milestones and reflecting on their journeys. Great for hearing past simple, wishes and regrets in real, emotional speech.

Dica: anote uma frase com "I wish" ou "the turning point". / Note one sentence with "I wish" or "the turning point".

Podcast
The Moth — real people telling true personal stories

Ordinary people share memorable true stories from their lives, with a clear beginning, turning point and reflection. The perfect model for telling your own story out loud.

Dica: ouça uma história e identifique o começo, o ponto de virada e a reflexão. / Listen to one story and find the beginning, the turning point, and the reflection.

YouTube
English with Lucy — how to tell a story in English

A calm, clear lesson on telling a personal story in English — using past tenses, linking words and natural phrases. Great for older learners who like a steady pace.

Dica: depois de assistir, grave um áudio de 3 minutos contando a sua jornada de inglês. / After watching, record a 3-minute audio telling your English journey.
