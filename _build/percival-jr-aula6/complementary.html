Aula 6 -- Reporting Back

Tema da aula: Reporting Back -- talking about what already happened with the past simple. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"Past Simple" -- regular and irregular verbs explained

Watch a short, clear video about the past simple: how to add -ed to regular verbs and the most common irregular verbs (go-went, have-had, make-made). Listen for did and didn't in questions and negatives.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote três verbos regulares e três irregulares no passado.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "Past simple"

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about the past simple, with regular and irregular verbs and the use of did / didn't. Perfect to review when to add -ed and when a verb changes form.

Dica: escreva um pequeno relato do seu dia de ontem -- três ações no passado e uma frase negativa com didn't.

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about work or travel

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen for the past simple when people report what they did at work or on a trip -- great input to review today's grammar and vocabulary.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote toda forma de passado que você ouvir (reviewed, went, had, submitted).
