Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Estas sao as palavras-chave da aula. Ouca cada uma e leia o exemplo. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Request -- pedido / solicitacao
"I have a small request for you." (Tenho um pequeno pedido para voce.)
Offer -- oferta / oferecer ajuda
"Thank you for your kind offer." (Obrigada pela sua gentil oferta.)
Favor -- favor
"Can I ask you a favor?" (Posso te pedir um favor?)
Mind -- importar-se / incomodar
"Would you mind helping me?" (Voce se importaria de me ajudar?)
Appreciate -- agradecer / valorizar
"I really appreciate your help." (Eu realmente agradeco sua ajuda.)
Prefer -- preferir
"I prefer to call, it is faster." (Eu prefiro ligar, e mais rapido.)
Kindly -- gentilmente / por gentileza
"Could you kindly send the list?" (Voce poderia, por gentileza, enviar a lista?)
Right away -- imediatamente / agora mesmo
"I can send it right away." (Posso enviar agora mesmo.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

Daniel has a request for Fernanda. He says: "Could you send the price list, please? And would you mind checking the delivery date?" Fernanda is happy to help. She says: "Of course. I can do it right away. I prefer to call them, because it is faster." Daniel says: "I really appreciate it." A kind offer and a polite favor make work easier for everyone.

1. "Could you send..." and "Would you check...". What are these? (O que sao?)
A Polite requests. (Pedidos educados.)
B Things that happened in the past. (Coisas do passado.)
C Orders that are rude. (Ordens rudes.)
2. Fernanda says "I can do it right away." What is she doing? (O que ela faz?)
A Asking for a favor. (Pedindo um favor.)
B Offering her help. (Oferecendo sua ajuda.)
C Saying no. (Dizendo nao.)
3. After "could you" and "would you", how is the verb? (Como fica o verbo?)
A With -s: "Could you sends". (Com -s.)
B No -s: "Could you send". (Sem -s.)
C In the past: "Could you sent". (No passado.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Can, Could & Would

Grammar

Como pedir e oferecer com educacao. / How to ask and offer politely.

Form / FormaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
Can you...?pedido educado / friendlyCan you help me?
Could you...?mais educado / more politeCould you send it, please?
Would you...?muito educado / very politeWould you check the date?
I can...oferecer ajuda / offer helpI can do it right away.
verbo sem -sdepois de can/could/wouldCould you send (NAO sends)

Etapa 4: Quiz Situacional

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao profissional.

You ask a colleague to send a document, politely. You say: (Voce pede educadamente.)
A "Send me the document now."
B "Could you send me the document, please?"
C "Could you sends the document?"
Daniel asks for help. You say yes and offer to do it now. You say: (Voce aceita e oferece.)
A "Maybe later, I am busy."
B "Of course. I can do it right away."
C "I can to do it right away."
A colleague helped you with a report. You thank them. You say: (Voce agradece.)
A "It was your job."
B "I really appreciate your help."
C "You appreciate me."
