Chapter 3: The Code

Can, Could & Would

the magic words of polite English

Discover the Grammar

How Do We Ask Politely?

"Can you help me, please?"

"Could you send the list, please?"

"Would you check the date for me?"

WordUseExample
Can you...?polite request (friendly)Can you help me?
Could you...?more polite requestCould you send it?
Would you...?very polite requestWould you check it?
I can...offer your helpI can do it now.

can/could/would + verbo no infinitivo (sem -s). Could/Would = mais educado. Adicione "please".

Say Yes Nicely

ToolUseExample

Of course / Suresay yes politelyOf course, no problem.

Sure. I can send it right away.

A Real Message

A Polite Request

Subject: Quick Favor — Price List & Delivery Date

Hello Daniel,

Could you send me the new price list, please? The supplier in Colombia asked for it.

Would you also check the delivery date for me? I can call them this afternoon if you prefer.

I really appreciate your help. I will send a short message when it is done.

Best regards,

Fernanda Gabriela · Sucafina
