Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

Every day, Fernanda checks her email. This morning, a supplier in Switzerland sent a message. They want an update on the coffee shipment. The order was confirmed last week, but the deadline is close. Right now, the team is checking the documents. Fernanda is writing a short reply and she will confirm the date soon.

1. "Every day, she checks" but "right now, she is writing". Why different? (Por que a diferenca?)
A "checks" = routine; "is writing" = right now. (Rotina vs agora.)
B They mean the same thing. (Significam o mesmo.)
C "is writing" is the past. (E o passado.)
2. "The order was confirmed last week." When did it happen? (Quando aconteceu?)
A Right now. (Agora.)
B In the past. (No passado.)
C In the future. (No futuro.)
3. The supplier wants an "update". What is that? (O que e?)
A A new contract. (Um novo contrato.)
B New information about the situation. (Informacao nova sobre a situacao.)
C A payment. (Um pagamento.)

Etapa 4: Quiz Situacional

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao profissional.

You describe what you are doing at this moment. You say: (O que voce faz agora.)
A "Right now, I write a reply."
B "Right now, I am writing a reply."
C "Right now, I wrote a reply."
You ask the supplier for the delivery date. You say: (Voce pergunta a data.)
A "When the delivery date is?"
B "When is the delivery date?"
C "The delivery date when is?"
You open a professional email. You write: (Voce abre o e-mail.)
A "Hey! What's up?"
B "Dear partner, thank you for your message."
C "I want the date now."
