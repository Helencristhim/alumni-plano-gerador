Discover the Grammar

Now, Right Now, or Before?

"Every day, I check my emails."

"Right now, I am writing an email."

"Last week, I confirmed the order."

WhenTenseExample
every day / routinepresent simpleI check
right nowam/is/are + -ingI am writing
last week / beforesimple past (-ed / was/were)I confirmed

rotina = presente simples; agora = presente continuo; antes = passado.

A Real Email

Fernanda's Reply

Subject: Coffee Shipment — Delivery Date Update

Dear partner,

Thank you for your message. I am writing about the coffee shipment.

The order was confirmed last week. Right now, our team is checking the delivery date. We are working on it, and I will confirm the date soon.

Please let me know if you need more information.

Best regards,

Fernanda Gabriela · Sucafina
