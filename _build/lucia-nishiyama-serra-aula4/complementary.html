The First Conditional -- 6 Minute Grammar

Six minutes, two presenters, and exactly the pattern you focused on tonight: if + present, will + verb. They say the sentences at natural speed, so your ear learns where "will" falls in a prediction. A short, gentle way to review the grammar of the whole lesson.

Tip: watch it twice. The first time, only listen. The second time, pause after each example and build your own boardroom sentence out loud: "If we enforce this framework, we will mitigate the risk."
