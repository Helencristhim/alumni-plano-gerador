Chapter 4: The Code

Present Simple + Frequency

Routines · how often · times of day

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I always wake up at seven."

"She usually has lunch at noon."

"He often works in the morning."

"We never work at night."

StructureUse it forExample
I / You / We + verbroutines, habitsI work · I wake up
He / She + verb + s3rd person routineShe works · She wakes up
always / usually / often / sometimes / neverhow often (before the verb)I always work
in the morning · at 8 · at nightwhen (the time)I work in the morning

Put always, usually, never BEFORE the main verb. Add -s to the verb with he and she: "She wakes up."

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

I go always to work at 8.
I always go to work at 8.
She wake up at 6.
She wakes up at 6.

Frequency words go before the main verb: "I always go." And with he/she, add -s: "She wakes up."
