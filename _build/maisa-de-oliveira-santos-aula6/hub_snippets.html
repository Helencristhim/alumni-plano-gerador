My Day, My Routine

A rotina diária dela em casa e no trabalho: wake up, get to work, have lunch, a break, go home, usually, always, at night. Gramática: present simple para rotina + advérbios de frequência (always, usually, often, sometimes, never) + horas e partes do dia (in the morning, at 8, at night). Missão: descrever o dia inteiro, da manhã à noite.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

Wake up -- to stop sleeping and open your eyes
"I wake up at 6 o'clock in the morning." (acordar)
Get to work -- to arrive at your job
"I get to work at 8 o'clock." (chegar ao trabalho)
Have lunch -- to eat your meal in the middle of the day
"I have lunch at noon with a colleague." (almoçar)
A break -- a short rest from work
"I take a short break in the afternoon." (uma pausa)
Go home -- to leave and travel to your house
"I go home at 6 o'clock in the evening." (ir para casa)
Usually -- almost every day, most of the time
"I usually have meetings in the morning." (geralmente)
Always -- every time, one hundred percent
"I always wake up early." (sempre)
At night -- the dark part of the day
"At night, I go home and watch TV." (à noite)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

Maísa lives in São Paulo. She has a busy routine. She always wakes up at 6 o'clock in the morning. First, she has a coffee. She gets to work at 8. In the morning, she usually has meetings with her team. At noon, she has lunch with a colleague. In the afternoon, she sometimes takes a short break. She never works late. At night, she goes home and relaxes.

1. Quando a Maísa acorda? / When does Maísa wake up?
A She wake up at 6.
B She always wakes up at 6 in the morning.
C She wakes up always at 6.
2. O que ela faz de manhã no trabalho? / What does she do at work in the morning?
A She usually has meetings with her team.
B She usually go home.
C She never works.
3. Ela trabalha até tarde? / Does she work late?
A Yes, she always works late.
B No, she never works late.
C She works late at night.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Simple & Frequency Adverbs

GRAMMAR

Use o present simple para rotina. Com he/she, o verbo ganha -s. Os advérbios de frequência vêm ANTES do verbo. / Use present simple for routines; add -s with he/she; frequency adverbs go before the verb.

Estrutura / StructureUsar para / Use forExemplo / Example
I / You / We + verborotina, hábitosI work · I wake up
He / She + verbo + srotina na 3ª pessoaShe works · She wakes up
always / usually / often / sometimes / nevercom que frequência (antes do verbo)I always work
in the morning · at 8 · at nightquando (o horário)I work in the morning

Atenção: o advérbio vem ANTES do verbo — "I always go", nunca "I go always". E com he/she adicione -s: "She wakes up", nunca "She wake up".
