Chapter 4

Under the Spotlight

Answering a tough question calmly

Dialogue

Let Me Explain

E
Marcos, how do you make sure a valuation is fair when the market is so unstable?
M
That is a great question. Let me think for a second. To be honest, it depends on the market.
E
I see. Could you explain how exactly?
M
To put it simply, when prices are unstable, we choose the safer value. In other words, we stay conservative.
E
And how long does the whole process take, roughly?
M
Roughly two weeks. I touched on the timeline earlier, but bear with me if you want the exact figure.
E
No need. One last thing -- what is the exact margin of error?
M
I do not have that number with me right now. Let me check and get back to you on that.
Comprehension

Did You Catch It?

2. How does he keep a valuation fair in an unstable market?
