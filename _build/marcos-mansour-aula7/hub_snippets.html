Stage 7.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para escutar.

Rephrase -- reformular (to say the same thing with different words)
"Let me rephrase that more simply."
In other words -- em outras palavras (a phrase to introduce a simpler explanation)
"In other words, we choose the safer value."
Buy time -- ganhar tempo (to gain a few seconds before you answer)
"That is a great question; let me buy a little time."
Roughly -- aproximadamente (not exactly, but about that amount)
"Roughly speaking, it takes two weeks."
To be honest -- para ser honesto (a phrase to introduce a sincere answer)
"To be honest, the answer depends on the market."
Bear with me -- tenha paciência comigo (asking someone to wait a moment)
"Bear with me while I check the figure."
Touch on -- mencionar brevemente (to mention a topic briefly)
"I touched on that point earlier in the talk."
Get back to you -- retornar com a resposta (to answer later, when you have the info)
"Let me check and get back to you on that."
Put it simply -- dizer de forma simples (a phrase to introduce a clear summary)
"To put it simply, we find a fair price."
Depend -- depender (to change according to other conditions)
"It depends on the size of the asset."

Stage 7.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas.

An investor asks Marcos a hard question. He does not panic. First he buys time: "That is a great question. Let me think." Then he answers, and he rephrases it to be clear: "In other words, we choose the safer value." When she asks for a number he is not sure of, he stays honest: "Roughly two weeks, but let me check and get back to you." He never guesses. "To be honest," he adds, "the exact figure depends on the market." A calm, honest answer builds more trust than a fast, nervous one.

1. Por que Marcos diz "That is a great question. Let me think." primeiro?
A Para mudar de assunto.
B Para ganhar tempo e organizar a resposta.
C Porque não entendeu a pergunta.
2. Qual a preposição correta com "depend"?
A depends of
B depends on
C depends in
3. What does Marcos do when he is not sure of a number?
A He invents an exact figure.
B He gives a rough estimate and offers to get back to her.
C He stays silent.

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situação sob pressão.

A hard question surprises you. The best first move is:
A Stay silent for ten seconds.
B "That is a great question. Let me think."
C "I do not want to answer."
You do not know the exact figure. You say:
A "It is exactly 14.73%." (a guess)
B "Roughly fifteen percent; let me get back to you with the exact figure."
C "I do not know, sorry."
Your answer is complex. To make it clear, you say:
A "To put it simply, we find a fair price."
B "It is very technical, never mind."
C "You would not understand."
Which sentence is correct?
A "It depends of the size of the asset."
B "It depends on the size of the asset."
C "It depends in the size of the asset."
