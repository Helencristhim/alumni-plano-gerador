Lesson 8 -- The Floor Is Yours: Expressing Opinions and Ideas

Series
Mad Men -- Season 1: "The Wheel" (pitch scene)

Watch how people share opinions and ideas in a meeting. Notice opinion frames ("In my opinion...", "From my perspective...") and how speakers soften ideas with hedging words.

Write down two opinion phrases you hear. Rewrite them for a real discussion at Oracle and say them out loud.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- Episode: "Giving Opinions in Meetings"

Practical phrases to introduce an opinion, agree, disagree politely, and suggest an idea. Review of today's words: opinion, perspective, suggest, propose.

Note the host's opinion frames. Record your own thirty-second opinion using one frame and one hedging word.

YouTube
"How to Give Your Opinion Politely in English"

A short, clear guide to sharing opinions: introduce your idea, soften it with hedging, and agree or disagree without sounding rude. Great for real discussions at work.

After watching, record a sixty-second opinion on an IT topic. Use at least three new words and one hedging word.
