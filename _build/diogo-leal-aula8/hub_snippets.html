Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Opinion -- a view or belief you have about something
"In my opinion, we should test first." (Opinião)
Perspective -- your way of seeing a situation
"From my perspective, the risk is low." (Perspectiva)
Suggest -- to offer an idea for people to consider
"I suggest we run a pilot." (Sugerir)
Consider -- to think carefully about an idea or option
"Let us consider both options." (Considerar)
Propose -- to formally offer a plan or idea
"I propose a small pilot first." (Propor)
Convince -- to make someone agree with you or believe you
"The data convinced the team." (Convencer)
Tend to -- to usually do or think something
"I tend to agree with Sophia." (Costumar)
Slightly -- a little; a small amount
"I slightly disagree on the budget." (Levemente)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Tomorrow the team discusses a new tool. Diogo prepares his opinion. "In my opinion, it is a good idea," he thinks. "From my perspective, it will save time." He knows the rule: agree is a verb, so he says "I agree," never "I am agree." He also wants to sound polite, so he uses hedging: "Perhaps we could test it," and "It seems slightly risky." He will suggest a small pilot and ask the team to consider the costs. He tends to agree with Sophia, but he is slightly worried about the training. In the end, he will propose a decision together.

1. How does Diogo introduce his opinion?
A With "In my opinion" and "From my perspective".
B With "I am agree".
C With no words at all.
2. Which sentence is correct?
A I agree with Sophia.
B I am agree with Sophia.
C I am agreeing with Sophia.
3. What is Diogo slightly worried about?
A The training.
B The lunch.
C The weather.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Opinion Frames & Hedging

GRAMMAR

Introduzir sua ideia e soar educado. / Introducing your idea and sounding polite.

Tool / FerramentaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
In my opinion / I thinkIntroduzir sua ideia / Introduce your ideaIn my opinion, we should test first.
From my perspectiveDar seu ponto de vista / Give your point of viewFrom my perspective, the risk is low.
perhaps / maybe / mightSuavizar (hedging) / SoftenPerhaps we could run a pilot.
it seems / I tend to / slightlySuavizar (hedging) / SoftenIt seems slightly risky.
Key point / Ponto-chaveAgree e disagree são verbos: diga "I agree" / "I disagree", NUNCA "I am agree". Os opinion frames apresentam sua ideia; as palavras de hedging (perhaps, might, slightly) a deixam mais educada.
