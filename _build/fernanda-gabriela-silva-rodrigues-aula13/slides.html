Chapter 3: The Code

Me, Him, Them

small words that replace names

Discover the Grammar

What Do These Words Replace?

"Can you call me back?"

"I will send it to her."

"Tell them the price."

SubjectObjectExample
ImeCall me back.
youyouI will help you.
hehimTell him the news.
sheherSend it to her.
ititI will send it.
weusCall us later.
theythemTell them the price.

Object pronouns replace a name AFTER the verb or after to/with/for. We use them so we do not repeat the name.

Chapter 4: At Work

A Call at Sucafina

opening, fixing, and closing a call

Dialogue

The Colombia Call

F
Hello, this is Fernanda. How can I help you?
D
Hi Fernanda, this is Daniel. I am calling about the Colombia shipment.
F
Sorry, you are breaking up. Can you hear me?
D
My connection is bad. Hold on, let me move closer. Better now?
F
Yes, perfect. The supplier sent new dates. I will send them to you now.
D
Great. On our video call tomorrow, can you share screen with the agenda?
F
Of course. I will mute the room first, then I will show it to everyone.
D
Perfect. I have to go now. Call me back if you need anything. Talk soon.
A Real Message

The Phone Note

Message for Fernanda · Sucafina — Trading Team

From: Daniel

About: the Colombia shipment — new dates

Also: The team needs the dates — send them today.

Listening

Daniel's Voicemail

He left a message. Sound first, no text.

0:000:00
1. What is Daniel's call about?
The Colombia shipment and the new dates.
2. What does Daniel ask Fernanda to do?
Call him back before five, or leave him a message.
Listening 2

Fernanda's Reply

She returns the call. Sound first, no text.

0:000:00
1. When is Fernanda going to call Daniel back?
At four o'clock.
2. What is she going to do if Daniel cannot hear her?
Share her screen with the agenda.
