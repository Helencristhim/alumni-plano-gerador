Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Estas sao as palavras-chave da aula. Ouca cada uma e leia o exemplo. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Call back -- retornar a ligacao
"I will call you back at four." (Vou te retornar as quatro.)
Hold on -- aguardar um momento
"Hold on, I will find the file." (Aguarde, vou achar o arquivo.)
Message -- recado / mensagem
"Please leave me a message." (Por favor, me deixe um recado.)
Connection -- conexao
"My connection is bad today." (Minha conexao esta ruim hoje.)
Mute -- desligar o microfone / mutar
"Please mute your microphone." (Por favor, mute seu microfone.)
Share screen -- compartilhar a tela
"Let me share my screen with the agenda." (Deixe-me compartilhar a tela com a pauta.)
Hang up -- desligar (a ligacao)
"Do not hang up, I am still here." (Nao desligue, ainda estou aqui.)
Dial -- discar (o numero)
"Dial the number again, please." (Disque o numero de novo, por favor.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

Daniel calls Fernanda about the Colombia shipment. The connection is bad, so Fernanda says: "Sorry, you are breaking up. Can you hear me?" Daniel says: "Hold on, let me move closer." The supplier sent new dates, and Fernanda says: "I will send them to you now." Daniel asks her to share screen on the video call tomorrow. He has to go, so he says: "Call me back if you need anything." Fernanda will leave him a message if he does not answer.

1. "I will send them to you" -- what does "them" mean? (O que e "them"?)
A The new dates. (As novas datas.)
B Daniel. (O Daniel.)
C The connection. (A conexao.)
2. Why does Fernanda say "Can you hear me?" (Por que ela pergunta isso?)
A She wants to hang up. (Ela quer desligar.)
B The connection is bad. (A conexao esta ruim.)
C She is muted. (Ela esta mutada.)
3. "Call me back" -- who is "me"? (Quem e "me"?)
A The supplier. (O fornecedor.)
B Fernanda.
C Daniel (he is speaking). (Daniel, que esta falando.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Object Pronouns

Grammar

Pequenas palavras que substituem um nome depois do verbo. / Small words that replace a name after the verb.

Subject / SujeitoObject / ObjetoExample / Exemplo
I (eu)meCall me back. (Me retorne.)
you (voce)youI will help you. (Vou te ajudar.)
he (ele)himTell him. (Diga a ele.)
she (ela)herSend it to her. (Envie a ela.)
it (isto/coisa)itI will send it. (Vou enviar isto.)
we (nos)usCall us later. (Nos ligue depois.)
they (eles)themTell them. (Diga a eles.)

Usamos o object pronoun depois do verbo ou depois de to/with/for, para nao repetir o nome. / We use the object pronoun after the verb or after to/with/for.
