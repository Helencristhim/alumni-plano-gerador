Chapter 3: The Code

Who You Are

am · is · are

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I am Maria Clara. I am from Maceio."

"Clarinha is my granddaughter. She is three years old."

"We are a big family. We are in Brazil."

"Are you from Brazil? Yes, I am."

Subject+ Yes- No? Question
II amI am notAm I?
he / she / itClarinha isClarinha is notIs Clarinha?
you / we / theyWe areWe are notAre you?

One verb, three shapes: am, is, are. It says your name, your city, and your family.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

"I have 40 years."
"I am 40 years old."
"Clarinha are three years old."
"Clarinha is three years old."

Age uses to be (I am 40), never "have". And she / he / Clarinha always takes is.
