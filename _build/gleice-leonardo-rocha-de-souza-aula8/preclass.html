The Pharma World -- Industry Vocabulary (Present Simple)

Talk about your world at Sanofi. New vocabulary: patient, treatment, product, market, pharmacy, brand, supply, launch. With he, she, it and singular nouns (the company, a patient), the present simple verb adds -s: Sanofi develops, the team supplies. After does, use the base verb (Does the company sell?).

Product -- a thing a company makes and sells

"The market wants new medicines." (mercado)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Simple (he / she / it + -s)

GRAMMAR

Com he, she, it e nomes no singular (the company, a patient), o verbo no presente ganha -s: Sanofi develops, the team supplies. Verbos terminados em consoante + y trocam para -ies: supply vira supplies. Para negativa e pergunta, use does + o verbo na base. / With he, she, it and singular nouns, the present simple verb adds -s. Verbs ending in consonant + y change to -ies. For negatives and questions, use does + the base verb.

BaseHe / She / ItBaseHe / She / It
developdevelopsneedneeds
sellsellsmakemakes
supplysupplieslaunchlaunches
Regra de ouroafirmativa: Sanofi develops. Negativa e pergunta: Sanofi does not develop / Does Sanofi develop? (depois de does, use a base).
