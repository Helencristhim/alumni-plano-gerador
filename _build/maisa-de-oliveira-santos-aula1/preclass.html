Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

Monday morning. Maísa is in the elevator. A man smiles: "Good morning! I am David." Maísa is a little nervous, but she answers: "Nice to meet you. My name is Maísa. I work in finance." David is the new partner at the company. He is from Canada. "We are colleagues, then!" he says. The meeting room is next to the elevator. Maísa is from São Paulo — it is a big city.

B "I am work in finance."
C "Me finance."
2. De onde é o David? / Where is David from?
A From São Paulo.
B From Canada.

3. Qual a forma correta com "we"? / Which form is correct with "we"?
A "We is colleagues."
B "We are colleagues."
C "We am colleagues."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- O verbo TO BE

GRAMMAR

Um verbo, três formas: am / is / are. Ele diz quem você é. / One verb, three shapes.

Sujeito / SubjectAfirmativo (+)Negativo (-)Pergunta (?)
I (eu)I am Maísa.I am not nervous.Am I late?
he / she / it (ele/ela)David is from Canada.David is not late.Is David here?
you / we / they (você/nós)We are colleagues.We are not late.Are you from São Paulo?

Atenção: idade usa TO BE ("I am 35 years old") — nunca "I have 35 years". E não use "am/is/are" antes de outro verbo: diga "I work in finance", não "I am work".
