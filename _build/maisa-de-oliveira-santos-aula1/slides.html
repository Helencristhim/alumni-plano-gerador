Chapter 3: The Code

Who You Are

am · is · are

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I am Maísa. I am from São Paulo."

"David is from Canada. He is our new partner."

"We are colleagues. We are in finance."

"Are you from São Paulo? Yes, I am."

Subject+ Yes- No? Question
II amI am notAm I?
he / she / itDavid isDavid is notIs David?
you / we / theyWe areWe are notAre you?

One verb, three shapes: am, is, are. It says your name, your city, and your job.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

I have 35 years.
I am 35 years old.
David are from Canada.
David is from Canada.

Age uses to be (I am 35), never "have". And he / she / David always takes is.
