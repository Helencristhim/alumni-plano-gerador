Chapter 3: The -est Tool

Pick the Top One

the + -est · the most + ...

The Tools

Two Top-One Tools

Short word + the ...-est · "Reception is the busiest area."

Short word + the ...-est · "The east corridor is the widest corridor."

Long word + the most ... · "The ICU is the most important ward."

Always start with the for the top one.

Short word + -est · long word + the most — always with the

Discover the Pattern

What's the Pattern?

Reception is the busiest area.

The east corridor is the widest corridor.

The ICU is the most important ward.

The new pharmacy is the most modern part.

WordTop one (superlative)
busy (short)the busiest — "the busiest area"
wide (short)the widest — "the widest corridor"
important (long)the most important — "the most important ward"

Short word + -est · long word + the most · always + the

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"the most busiest area"

✓ RIGHT

"the busiest area"

✗ WRONG

"the importantest ward"

✓ RIGHT

"the most important ward"

Short word: just -est. Long word: only the most. Never both.
