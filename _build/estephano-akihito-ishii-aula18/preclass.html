Aula 18 -- Pre-class

Apps and Software -- Present Simple

Aprender as palavras de apps e software e descrever o que eles fazem com o Present Simple (it works, it saves -- verbo + s). Vocabulário novo: app, software, download, install, update, delete, login, file.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

App -- a small program you open on your phone
"My favorite app helps me study." (aplicativo)
Software -- the programs inside a computer or phone
"The software works on my computer." (software)
Download -- to get a file from the internet to your device
"The app downloads a new lesson." (baixar)
Install -- to put a new app on your phone or computer
"I install the app on my new phone." (instalar)
Update -- a new version of the app with new things
"The app updates every week." (atualizar)
Delete -- to remove a file you do not want
"I delete the old files." (apagar)
Login -- to enter an app with your email and password
"I login with my email." (entrar / fazer login)
File -- a photo, a document, or a video on a device
"The app saves my file in a folder." (arquivo)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Simple (it works)

GRAMMAR

Como descrever o que um app faz. / How to describe what an app does.

Form / FormaUse / UsoExample
I / You / They + verbSEM -sI download a lesson.
It / The app + verb-sverbo + -s (he, she, it)It works. It saves.
It does not + verbNegativo (verbo SEM -s)It does not delete my files.
Does it + verb...?PerguntaDoes it work on my phone?
Atenção: com it ou the app, o verbo ganha um -s. NUNCA diga "The app work".
