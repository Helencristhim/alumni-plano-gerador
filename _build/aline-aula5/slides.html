Chapter 3

The Life Question

Have you ever...? · I have... · I have never...

Discover the Rule

Listen and Notice

"Have you ever been abroad?"

"Yes, I have. I have visited Argentina twice."

"I have never tried Spanish food."

"When did you go? — In 2023." (specific time = past simple)

StructureUseExample
Have you ever + past participle?Ask about the whole lifeHave you ever been to Spain?
I have + past participleYes — at some point in lifeI have visited Chile.
I have never + past participleNot once in your lifeI have never been to Asia.
When did you...? (past simple)Specific time follow-upWhen did you go? In 2023.

The experience matters, not the date. For the date, switch to past simple.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"Have you ever went to Spain?"

✓ RIGHT

"Have you ever been to Spain?"

✗ WRONG

"Did you ever been abroad?"

✓ RIGHT

"Have you ever been abroad?"
