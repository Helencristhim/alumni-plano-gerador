"Sequencing Words -- First, Then, Next, After That, Finally" -- BBC Learning English

A clear, practical lesson on exactly today's grammar: the words we use to describe a process in order ("first", "then", "after that", "once", "finally"). Watch for the comma after the connector and the present tense after "once". Connection to Lesson 6: this is the grammar that lets you walk a customer through their onboarding step by step.

Tip: pause after each example and describe one step of your own onboarding out loud, using the sequencing word you just heard.
