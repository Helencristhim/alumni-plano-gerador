Aula 8 -- Expressing Opinions

Series
The Newsroom (HBO) -- Any debate scene

Sharp, articulate characters who state opinions, argue, and disagree. Clear American English at a natural pace. Direct connection to Lesson 8: hear how people give opinions and disagree while staying respectful.

Tip: write down 3 opinion phrases and 2 polite disagreement phrases.

Podcast
TED Radio Hour (NPR) -- Any episode with multiple guests

Thoughtful conversations where people share and compare opinions on big ideas. Clear, well-paced English. Direct connection to Lesson 8: listen for "in my opinion", "from my perspective", and polite disagreement.

Tip: pause and repeat one opinion phrase. Then say your own opinion on the topic.

YouTube
"How to Agree and Disagree in English" -- BBC Learning English / mmmEnglish

A clear lesson with practical phrases for agreeing and disagreeing politely, modeled by native speakers. Direct connection to Lesson 8: exactly the opinion toolkit we practiced.

Tip: choose three phrases and practice them out loud with a real topic.
