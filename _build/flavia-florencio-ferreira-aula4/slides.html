Chapter 4: In, At, On

in · at · on

Three little words for place

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"Our office is in Sao Paulo."

"She works at the branch in Campinas."

"The map is on the wall."

"The meeting is on March 15th."

WordUse it forExample
incities, states, countriesin Sao Paulo, in Brazil
ata specific point or buildingat the branch, at the airport
ona surface, a street, or a dateon the map, on Avenida Paulista, on March 15th

in = big areas · at = a point · on = a surface, a street, or a date.

Language Focus

Which Preposition?

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"Our office is at Sao Paulo."

✓ RIGHT

"Our office is in Sao Paulo."

✗ WRONG

"The office is in Avenida Paulista."

✓ RIGHT

"The office is on Avenida Paulista."

Use in for a city or state (in Sao Paulo), and on for a street (on Avenida Paulista). Use at for a specific point (at the branch).

Dialogue

F

R
How many branches are there in the state?
F

R
Where can I see all the locations?
F
There is a big map on the wall. Every branch is on it.
R
And when is the annual meeting?
F
It is on March 15th, here in the capital.
