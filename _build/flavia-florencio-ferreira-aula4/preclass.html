Lesson 04 -- Pre-class

Numbers, Dates & Regions

Talk about your state, its regions, and the 33 branches of Sebrae-SP. Key words: state, capital, map, branch, distance, border, location, date, highway, number. Structure: prepositions of place (in, at, on) and where we use each one.

State -- a large area with its own government

Capital -- the main city of a state or country
"The capital of the state is Sao Paulo."
Map -- a drawing that shows where places are
"There is a big map on the wall."
Branch -- a local office of a bigger organization
"Sebrae has a branch in each region."
Distance -- how far one place is from another
"The distance to Campinas is one hundred kilometers."
Border -- the line where one area ends and another begins
"Ribeirao Preto is near the border with Minas Gerais."
Location -- the exact place where something or someone is
"Please check the location of your branch."
Date -- a certain day, month, and year
"The date of the annual meeting is March 15th."
Highway -- a big road that connects cities
"We drive on the highway to the north."
Number -- a word or sign that shows how many
"The number of branches is thirty-three."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Flavia works in the state of Sao Paulo. The head office is in the capital. On the wall of her office, there is a big map. Sebrae-SP has thirty-three branches, and each one is in a different city. One branch is at Campinas, about one hundred kilometers from the capital. Ribeirao Preto is in the north, near the border with Minas Gerais. "I always check the location of each branch on the map," she says. "The annual meeting is on March 15th, here in the capital."

1. Why do we say "in the state of Sao Paulo" and not "at the state"?
A We use "in" for cities, states, and countries.
B We use "at" for all big places.
C "In" and "at" mean the same thing.
2. Why do we say "on the map" and "on the wall"?
A We use "on" only for streets.
B We use "on" for a surface, like a map or a wall.
C We use "on" for cities and states.
3. Which sentence is correct?
A "The meeting is in March 15th."
B "The meeting is on March 15th."
C "The meeting is at March 15th."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Prepositions of Place (in, at, on)

GRAMMAR

Which little word to use for a place, a point, a surface, or a date.

WordUse it forExample
incities, states, countries, regionsin Sao Paulo, in the north
ata specific point or buildingat the branch, at the airport
ona surface, a street, or a dateon the map, on March 15th
Sentence typeExample
Big area (in)"Our office is in Sao Paulo."
A point (at)"She works at the branch in Campinas."
Surface / date (on)"The map is on the wall." / "The meeting is on March 15th."

Quiz

A "Our head office is at Sao Paulo."
B "Our head office is in Sao Paulo, the capital of the state."
C "Our head office is on Sao Paulo."
