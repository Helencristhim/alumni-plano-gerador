Chapter 3: Comparing Tools

Make a Comparison

-er + than · more + ... + than

The Tools

Two Comparing Tools

Short word + -er + than · "The ICU is larger than the lab."

Short word + -er + than · "The team is faster than before."

Long word + more ... than · "The wing is more modern than before."

Always use than to compare two things.

Short word + -er · long word + more — always with than

Discover the Pattern

What's the Pattern?

The ICU is larger than the lab.

The lab is smaller than the ICU.

The wing is more modern than before.

Safety is more important than speed.

WordComparison + than
large (short)larger than — "larger than the lab"
fast (short)faster than — "faster than before"
important (long)more important than — "more important than speed"

Short word + -er · long word + more · always + than

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"more larger than"

✓ RIGHT

"larger than"

✗ WRONG

"importanter than"

✓ RIGHT

"more important than"

Short word: just -er. Long word: only more. Never both.
