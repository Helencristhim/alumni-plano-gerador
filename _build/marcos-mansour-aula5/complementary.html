Aula 5 -- Introducing & Presenting Other People

Series
The Office (US) -- introduction scenes

Characters constantly introduce each other and describe what colleagues do. Watch how they use "This is..." and describe roles in the third person. Direct connection to Lesson 5: presenting one person to another and the third person -s.

Tip: note 5 introductions. Write each one as "This is X. He/She + verb + s."

Podcast
How I Built This (NPR) -- guest introductions

At the start of each episode, the host introduces the guest and describes what they do, using clear third-person English. Direct connection to Lesson 5: presenting a person and describing their role to an audience.

Tip: listen to the first two minutes. Copy how the host introduces the guest, then introduce a colleague the same way.

YouTube
"Introducing People in English (Formal & Informal)" -- Business English Pod

A practical lesson on "This is...", "Have you met...?", and replying politely with "It is a pleasure". Connection to Lesson 5: the exact phrases Marcos needs to connect people at the congress.

Tip: pause and repeat each introduction. Then introduce two imaginary colleagues to each other from memory.
