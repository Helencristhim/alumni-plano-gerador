Chapter 3: The Code

Who You Are

my name is · I am · I work

Discover the Grammar

Listen and Notice

"My name is Fernanda."

"I am from Brazil."

"I work at Sucafina."

"I work with international clients."

UsePatternExample
Your nameMy name is + nameMy name is Fernanda.
Where you are fromI am from + placeI am from Brazil.
Your companyI work at + companyI work at Sucafina.
What you work withI work with + people/thingI work with clients.

With I we use am and work (no s). / Com "I": am e work.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"My name Fernanda."

✓ RIGHT

"My name is Fernanda."

✗ WRONG

"I have 35 years."

✓ RIGHT

"I am 35 years old."
