Chapter 3

The Grammar Engine

The verb to be: am, is, are

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I am Erica."

"She is from Sao Paulo."

"Biodelta is a company."

"We are colleagues."

SubjectVerb to beExample
IamI am Erica.
He / She / ItisShe is from Sao Paulo.
You / We / TheyareWe are colleagues.
Present simple verbsI work, I live (no -s). She works, she lives (add -s for he/she/it).
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"I have 55 years old."

✓ RIGHT

"I am 55 years old."

✗ WRONG

"I am work at Biodelta."

✓ RIGHT

"I work at Biodelta."

✗ WRONG

"She work at Biodelta."

✓ RIGHT

"She works at Biodelta."

For age, use the verb to be: "I am 55." Do not put "am" before a main verb. Add -s to the verb for he, she, and it.
