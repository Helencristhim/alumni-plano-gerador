Grammar Discovery

Which one is soft? Which one shows the block? Which one is impossible to ignore?

Grammar Rule

Nudge, Block, Date

MoveStructureExample
Open softJust a quick nudge on / I am checking in on + nounJust a quick nudge on the API document.
Show the blockwait for / rely on / depend on + nounMy team relies on that page. We are waiting for it.
Give a date, and an exitCould you + verb + by + day / If it is bigger, I will helpCould you get it to me by Thursday?

A nudge is not a complaint. It is one specific ask, one clear block, one date -- and an offer to help. It gives the other person an easy way to say yes, and no reason to be offended. Never copy the boss to make a point: that is not a nudge, that is a threat.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I am waiting your answer since Monday."
"I have been waiting for your answer since Monday."
"The tests depend of your file."
"The tests depend on your file."

Em inglês se espera FOR alguma coisa (wait for) e se depende ON alguma coisa (depend on, rely on). As preposições não são as do português.

Chapter 5 -- Talking It Through

The Nudge

Dialogue

I Will Get to It This Week

D

"Camila, just a quick nudge on the API document. I am checking in because we cannot start the tests."

C

"I know, I know. It is on my list. This week, I promise."

D

"You said that last week too. I am not angry -- but my team relies on that page, and we lose a day for every day we wait."

C

"Everyone wants something from me. I have my own deadlines."

D

"Then let me make it small. I do not need the full document. Only the endpoint list -- one page. That would unblock us."

C

"One page I can do. Probably."

D

"Could you get it to me by Thursday? And if it is bigger than we thought, I will send someone from my team to write it with you."

C

"You would do that? Fine. Thursday."

D

"Thursday. I will confirm it in writing, so nobody drops the ball -- including me."

Dialogue Review

About Camila

1. What does Camila promise at the start?
That she will do it this week -- the same thing she said last week.
2. Why does she finally accept?
Because he makes the ask small (one page) and offers to help her.
3. What does Diogo do at the end?
He confirms the date in writing, so nobody forgets.
