Stage 1.1: Words for Polite Disagreement

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. These are rich B2 phrases for disagreeing without conflict.

To beg to differ -- discordar educadamente
"I'm afraid I beg to differ -- the coast is the best part."
A fair point -- um argumento valido e razoavel
"That's a fair point -- the queues really are long."
To push back -- resistir ou questionar com jeito
"She didn't argue, but she pushed back on the price."
Common ground -- um ponto em comum
"We found common ground: we both love good food."
To concede -- admitir / ceder um ponto
"I'll concede the city is crowded in summer."
To be at odds -- estar em desacordo
"We were at odds about the route, but stayed friendly."
To meet halfway -- chegar a um meio-termo
"We met halfway: a morning in the museum, an afternoon by the sea."
To agree to disagree -- aceitar que discordam e seguir
"In the end, we just agreed to disagree about the hotel."
A counterargument -- um contra-argumento
"Her counterargument was that sunsets there are unforgettable."
With all due respect -- com todo o respeito (antes de discordar)
"With all due respect, I think the coast is worth the time."
To take issue with -- discordar de / objetar a algo
"I take issue with the idea that markets are a waste of time."
To play devil's advocate -- bancar o advogado do diabo
"Let me play devil's advocate: what if we stayed one more day?"
With respect, I see it differently -- com respeito, vejo de outra forma
"With respect, I see it differently -- the trip was worth it."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

"My friend loves cities, whereas I much prefer the coast. Although we were at odds about the trip, we stayed friendly and looked for common ground. Even though she made a fair point about the museums, I begged to differ about spending three whole days there. While she pushed back gently, I conceded that the old town was beautiful. In the end, although neither of us fully agreed, we met halfway and agreed to disagree about the rest."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Contrast Conjunctions

Grammar

Although, even though, whereas e while ligam duas oracoes completas (cada uma com sujeito e verbo) para mostrar CONTRASTE. Nao use but junto de although na mesma frase. / Although, even though, whereas and while join two full clauses (each with a subject and verb) to show CONTRAST. Do not use but together with although in the same sentence.

Conjuncao / WordEnglishPortugues
althoughAlthough we disagreed, we stayed friends.Embora discordassemos, continuamos amigos.
even though (stronger)Even though she made a fair point, I disagreed.Mesmo ela tendo razao, discordei.
whereas (comparing)She loves cities, whereas I prefer the coast.Ela ama cidades, ao passo que prefiro o litoral.
while (contrast)While she pushed back, I conceded.Enquanto ela resistia, eu cedia.
Common mistakeNOT "Although we disagreed, but we stayed friends." Drop "but".Nao use but com although.
