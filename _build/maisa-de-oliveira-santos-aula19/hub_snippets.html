Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada expressão e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

I think -- a way to say your idea or opinion
"I think we should start now." (Eu acho / na minha opinião)
In my opinion -- a polite way to give your idea
"In my opinion, the plan is good." (Na minha opinião)
I agree -- I have the same idea as you
"I agree with David." (Eu concordo)
I disagree -- I have a different idea (politely)
"I disagree, but I understand." (Eu discordo)
An agenda -- the list of topics for a meeting
"What is on the agenda today?" (A pauta / a agenda)
A point -- one idea or topic in a meeting
"That is a good point." (Um ponto / uma ideia)
Let's -- let us; a way to suggest an action
"Let's start the meeting." (Vamos)
Move on -- go to the next topic or point
"Let's move on to the next point." (Seguir em frente)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

David starts the meeting with Maísa. "Good morning. Let's start. The first point on the agenda is the budget," he says. "I think we should look at the numbers first," Maísa says. David nods. "That is a good point. In my opinion, the budget is too high." Maísa smiles. "I agree with you. We can find a discount." Then David talks about the deadline. "I disagree a little," Maísa says. "The deadline is too soon, but we can try." David is happy. "Thank you for your opinion. Let's move on to the next point." The meeting is short, and their conversation is clear and polite.

1. Como o David começa a reunião? / How does David start the meeting?
A "Let's start. The first point is the budget."
B "We start meeting the budget now."
C "Start we the first point?"
2. Qual é a opinião do David sobre o orçamento? / What is David's opinion about the budget?
A "In my opinion, the budget is low."
B "In my opinion, the budget is too high."
C "I disagree with the budget."
3. Como a Maísa discorda com educação? / How does Maísa disagree politely?
A "I am agree with the deadline."
B "I disagree a little, but we can try."
C "I no agree the deadline."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Giving Opinions

GRAMMAR

Para dar sua opinião numa reunião, use I think + frase (Eu acho que...) ou In my opinion, + frase (Na minha opinião,...). Para reagir a uma ideia, diga I agree (Eu concordo) ou I disagree (Eu discordo). / To give your opinion in a meeting, use I think + sentence or In my opinion, + sentence. To react to an idea, say I agree or I disagree.

Estrutura / StructureUso / UseExemplo / Example
I think + sentencedar sua opinião / give your opinionI think we should start now.
In my opinion, + sentencedar opinião (formal) / give opinion (formal)In my opinion, the plan is good.
I agree (with + pessoa)concordar / agreeI agree with David.
I disagree, but...discordar com educação / disagree politelyI disagree, but I understand.

Atenção: diga I agree e I disagree, nunca "I am agree" ou "I am disagree" (o verbo já está em agree/disagree). E não junte as duas: use In my opinion, ... OU I think ..., nunca "In my opinion I think".

Stage 5: Think About It

Reflection
