Small Talk -- Meeting People

Meet a new person and make small talk: introduce, hobby, weather, weekend, hometown, job, free time, nice to meet you. Structures: "Where are you from?", "What do you do?", "How about you?"

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Introduce -- to tell someone your name
"Let me introduce myself." (Apresentar(-se))
Hobby -- something you enjoy doing for fun
"My hobby is cooking." (Passatempo / hobby)
Weather -- if it is sunny, rainy, or cold
"The weather is nice today." (Tempo / clima)
Weekend -- Saturday and Sunday
"What did you do on the weekend?" (Fim de semana)
Hometown -- the city where you grew up
"My hometown is Sao Paulo." (Cidade natal)
Job -- the work you do for money
"What is your job?" (Trabalho / emprego)
Free time -- time when you do not work
"What do you do in your free time?" (Tempo livre)
Nice to meet you -- what you say when you meet someone
"Hi, nice to meet you!" (Prazer em conhece-lo(a))

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Elaine meets a new person at an event. She smiles and says, Nice to meet you. Then she asks, Where are you from? The person is from Canada. Elaine asks, What do you do? The person says she is a nurse. Elaine asks about her hobby and her free time. They also talk about the weather and the weekend. At the end, Elaine says it was great talking.

1. Como Elaine pergunta de onde a pessoa e? / How does Elaine ask where the person is from?
A Where you from?
B Where are you from?
C From you where?
2. Como Elaine pergunta sobre o trabalho? / How does Elaine ask about the job?
A What do you do?
B What you do?
C You do what job?
3. Quais sao topicos seguros para small talk? / What are safe small talk topics?
A The weather, the weekend, and hobbies.
B Money and problems.
C Nothing at all.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- WH-Questions for Small Talk

GRAMMAR

Fazer perguntas amigaveis a uma nova pessoa. / Asking a new person friendly questions.

Question word / PalavraUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
WhereLugar / a placeWhere are you from?
WhatCoisa / trabalhoWhat do you do?
How about you?Devolver a pergunta / Return itI am fine. How about you?

Lembre: use are com "you" e um lugar (Where are you from?). Use do you antes de um verbo de acao (What do you do?). Devolva a pergunta com How about you? / Use "are" with a place, "do you" before an action verb, and "How about you?" to return the question.

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Choose the best response for each situation.

You meet a new person. How do you greet them? / Voce conhece alguem novo.
A "Hi, nice to meet you. I am Elaine."
B "You who?"
C "Name me Elaine."
You want to know the person's city. What do you ask? / Voce quer saber a cidade.
A "Where you from?"
B "Where are you from?"
C "You from city what?"
You want to know the person's job. / Voce quer saber o trabalho.
A "What you do?"
B "What do you do?"
C "Your job is do what?"
The person asks "How are you?" You answer and return the question. / Devolva a pergunta.
A "I am fine. How about for you?"
B "I am fine. How about you?"
C "I am fine. You how?"
