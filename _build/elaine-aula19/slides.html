Chapter 3

Asking Questions

Friendly WH-questions

Discover the Rule

Listen and Notice

"Where are you from?"

"What do you do?"

"How do you like the city?"

"What do you do in your free time?"

Question wordAsk aboutExample
Wherea placeWhere are you from?
Whata thing / jobWhat do you do?
Howan opinion / wayHow do you like the city?

Start with Where / What / How. After "do you" use the base verb. Return the question with "How about you?"

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"Where you from?"

✓ RIGHT

"Where are you from?"

✗ WRONG

"What you do?"

✓ RIGHT

"What do you do?"

Use "are" with "you" and a place. Use "do you" before an action verb.

ELAINE'S TRAVEL NOTES

How to Meet a New Person

1.Smile and say "Hi, nice to meet you."
2.Introduce yourself: "I am Elaine."
3.Ask: "Where are you from?"
4.Talk about the weather, weekend, or job.
5.End: "It was great talking to you!"

Safe topics: travel, weather, hobbies
