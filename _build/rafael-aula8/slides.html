Grammar Recap

Can & Could on the Phone

FunctionPhone phrase
Ability / audio check"Can you hear me?"
Polite request"Could you call back later?"
Ask to wait"Could you hold on, please?"
Offer help"Can I take a message?"
Common Mistake

Watch Out!

"Who are you?" (answering the phone)
"Who is calling, please?"
"Wait!" (asking someone to hold)
"Could you hold on a moment, please?"

Use polite phone phrases — not direct commands.

Find the Mistake

"Who are you?" (on the phone)
"Who is calling, please?" (polite phone phrase)
"I will call back you tomorrow."
"I will call you back tomorrow." (call + person + back)
"Can you to hold on, please?"
"Could you hold on, please?" (no 'to' after could)
"The connection are bad."
"The connection is bad." (singular subject)
