Aula 15 — Past Continuous: Setting the Scene

Conteúdos para reforçar como descrever uma cena no passado — was/were + verbo-ing com when e while. Marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
Mr. Robot — any system-outage or incident scene

Characters describe what was happening when a system went down. Listen for "we were monitoring...", "while the server was rebooting..." and the past simple moment that interrupts — the exact forms from Lesson 15.

Dica: anote uma frase com was/were + -ing (a cena) e uma com past simple (o momento).

Podcast
The Changelog — episodes with post-incident or outage stories

Engineers tell the story of an outage: what they were doing, the moment it occurred and how they fixed it. Hear the past continuous for the scene and the past simple for each event.

Dica: pause e repita cada frase com "while" que você ouvir.

YouTube
"Past continuous vs past simple (when / while)" (English lesson)

A short, clear video on the two past tenses and when to use each. Maps directly onto Daniela’s incident review with Marcus in Lesson 15.

Dica: depois do vídeo, descreva um incidente seu em 4 frases, usando as duas formas.
