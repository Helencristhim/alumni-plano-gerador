Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Three Tenses Together

GRAMMAR

Cada tempo tem um uso e palavras-sinal diferentes. As palavras-sinal mostram qual tempo usar. / Each tense has a different use and signal words. The signal words show which tense to use.

TenseSignal wordsExample
Present Simpleevery day, usuallyI study every day.
Present Continuousright now, at the momentI am studying right now.
Past Simpleyesterday, last weekI studied yesterday.

Key point / Ponto-chave: olhe sempre a palavra-sinal. every day → Present Simple; right now → Present Continuous (am/is/are + verbo-ing); yesterday / last week → Past Simple (-ed ou forma irregular: go → went, eat → ate). Always look at the signal word to choose the tense.
