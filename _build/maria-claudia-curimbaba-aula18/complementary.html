Aula 18 -- Decision-Making: I Suggest We...

Tema da aula: recommend and decide in a meeting -- should and ought to for normal advice, had better for strong advice with a consequence; weigh the options, pros and cons, reach a consensus. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Série
Succession (HBO) -- the boardroom decision scenes

Watch the family and the board weigh options under pressure. Listen for how executives recommend a path (we should..., we ought to...) and how they warn about consequences (we had better..., or...).

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote três frases -- uma com should ou ought to (conselho normal) e uma com had better (aviso com consequência).

Podcast
HBR IdeaCast (Harvard Business Review)

Conversations with leaders on how they make hard calls -- weighing pros and cons, trade-offs, and reaching consensus on a team. Rich input for the language of recommending and deciding.

Dica: ouça um episódio sobre decisão e separe duas expressões novas (weigh the options, trade-off, downside, reach a consensus) e como foram usadas.

YouTube
"Should, ought to and had better" (business English channels)

Short lessons that show the difference between normal advice (should / ought to) and strong advice with a consequence (had better), plus the base-verb rule and the negatives.

Dica: pegue uma decisão real do seu trabalho e grave uma recomendação em voz alta: uma frase com should ou ought to, e uma com had better + or...
