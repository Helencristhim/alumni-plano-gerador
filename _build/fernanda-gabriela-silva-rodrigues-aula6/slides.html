Chapter 3: The Code

Do, Does & Wh-

Do you...? · Does it...? · What/Who/When

Discover the Grammar

Do or Does?

"Do you ship coffee?"

"Does the delivery arrive Monday?"

"What do you ship?"

"Who handles the contract?"

SubjectHelperExample
I / you / we / theyDoDo you ship coffee?
he / she / itDoesDoes it arrive Monday?
Wh-What/Who/When + do/doesWhat do you need?

Do = I/you/we/they; Does = he/she/it. / Do vs Does.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"Does the delivery arrives Monday?"

✓ RIGHT

"Does the delivery arrive Monday?"

✗ WRONG

"You ship coffee?"

✓ RIGHT

"Do you ship coffee?"

Chapter 4: On the Call

Asking the Supplier

Five questions to complete the order

Dialogue

Completing the Order

F
Hi, this is Fernanda. I have a few questions about the order. What do you ship this month?
S
Hi Fernanda. We ship 500 tons of coffee this month.
F
Great. When does the delivery arrive, and who handles the contract?
S
It arrives on March 15th. Carlos handles the contract. He is your contact.
F
Thank you. Is there any issue with the schedule?
S
No issue. The status is good. Everything is on schedule.
F
Perfect. Are you available for a quick call on Monday?
S
Yes, I am. I will send a short report with the full status.
