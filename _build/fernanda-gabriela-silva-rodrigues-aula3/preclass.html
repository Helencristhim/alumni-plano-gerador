Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Shipment -- carregamento / remessa (cafe enviado de uma vez)
"We have a shipment of 500 tons." (Um carregamento de 500 toneladas.)
Delivery -- entrega (quando o cafe chega)
"The delivery is on March 15th." (A entrega e dia 15.)
Deadline -- prazo final (data limite)
"The deadline is March 10th." (O prazo e dia 10.)
Schedule -- cronograma / agenda (datas e horarios)
"Please check the schedule." (Confira o cronograma.)
Ton -- tonelada (1000 kg)
"We have 500 tons of coffee." (500 toneladas.)
Kilo -- quilo (kg)
"200 kilos for the first delivery." (200 quilos.)
Quarter -- trimestre (3 meses: Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
"Our quarter ends soon." (O trimestre acaba logo.)
Due date -- data de vencimento (quando algo deve ser feito/pago)
"Confirm the due date." (Confirme a data de vencimento.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

We have a shipment of five hundred tons of coffee. The delivery is on March 15th. The deadline for the documents is March 10th. The meeting is at 10:00 AM. Please check the schedule and confirm the due date. Our quarter ends soon, so the numbers must be ready.

1. We say "March 15th", not "March 15". Why? (Por que "15th"?)
A Dates use ordinal numbers (15th = fifteenth). (Datas usam ordinais.)
B Because it is a quantity. (Porque e quantidade.)
C Because it is plural. (Porque e plural.)
2. What is the "deadline"? (O que e o deadline?)
A The day the coffee arrives. (Quando o cafe chega.)
B The last day to do something. (O ultimo dia para algo.)
C A coffee company. (Uma empresa.)
3. "The meeting is at 10:00 AM." When is it? (Quando e a reuniao?)
A At ten in the morning. (As dez da manha.)
B At ten at night. (As dez da noite.)
C On the tenth. (No dia dez.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Numbers, Dates & Times

Grammar

Como dizer numeros, datas e horas em ingles americano. / Numbers, dates and times.

Use / UsoPattern / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Quantity / Quantidadenumber + noun500 tons / 200 kilos
Date / DataMonth + ordinalMarch 15th (March fifteenth)
Time / Horaat + hour AM/PMat 10:00 AM / at 3:30 PM
Key idea / Ideia-chaveDatas usam ordinais (1st, 2nd, 3rd, 15th); horas usam "at". / Dates = ordinals; times = "at".
