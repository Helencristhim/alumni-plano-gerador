Vocabulary

Tall -- alto(a) — a high person, not short
"My brother is very tall."
Short -- baixo(a) — a low person, not tall
"She is short and very kind."
Young -- jovem — not old, with a small number of years
"My daughter is young."
Hair -- cabelo — it grows on your head, long or short
"She has long brown hair."
Friendly -- simpático(a) — a nice person, easy to talk to
"My colleague is very friendly."
Kind -- gentil — a good person who helps others
"She is kind to everyone."
Funny -- engraçado(a) — a person who makes you laugh
"My brother is very funny."
Smart -- inteligente — an intelligent person who learns fast
"She is smart and a good nurse."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. Veja be e have em ação.

This is Bia, Érica's colleague. Bia is tall and young. She has long brown hair. Bia is very friendly and kind — she helps everyone at work. She is also funny, so people like her. Bia is very smart: she is a good nurse. Next to her is a man. He is short, and he has short black hair.

1. "Bia is tall." Why "is"?
A Because be describes the body.
B Because tall is a feature you have.
C Because it is about the past.
2. "She has long brown hair." Why "has"?
A Because hair is a personality.
B Because have is for features like hair.
C Because hair is a verb.
3. How is Bia's personality?
A Tall and short.
B Friendly, kind, funny and smart.
C The text does not say.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Be / Have

GRAMMAR

Para descrever pessoas, use be ou have. Be é para o corpo e a personalidade (she is tall, he is kind). Have é para as características que a pessoa tem (she has long hair, he has brown eyes). O adjetivo vem antes do nome: brown hair (e não "hair brown"). / Use be for the body and personality; use have for features; put the adjective before the noun.

VerboQuando usar (agora)
She/He is...corpo e personalidade — she is tall (ela é alta) · he is kind (ele é gentil)
She/He has...características — she has long hair (ela tem cabelo longo) · he has brown eyes
ordemadjetivo antes do nome — brown hair (cabelo castanho), não "hair brown"
