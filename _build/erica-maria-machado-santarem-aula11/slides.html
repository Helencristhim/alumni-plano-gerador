Chapter 3

Be and Have

She is · She has

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"She is tall and young."

"She has long brown hair."

"He is friendly and smart."

"He has short black hair."

VerbWhen to use it
She/He is...the body & the personality: she is tall, he is kind, she is funny
She/He has...the features you have: she has long hair, he has brown eyes
orderadjective before the noun: long brown hair (not "hair brown long")
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"She is long hair."

✓ RIGHT

"She has long hair."

✗ WRONG

"She has tall."

✓ RIGHT

"She is tall."

✗ WRONG

"She has hair brown."

✓ RIGHT

"She has brown hair."

Use be for the body and the personality (she is tall, he is kind). Use have for the features (she has long hair). Put the adjective before the noun: brown hair, not hair brown.
