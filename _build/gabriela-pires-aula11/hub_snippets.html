Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Menu -- a list of the food and drinks a restaurant has
"The waiter gives me the menu." (Cardápio)
Waiter -- the person who brings your food in a restaurant
"The waiter is very kind." (Garçom)
Order -- to ask for the food you want to eat
"I order a salad and a juice." (Pedir / fazer o pedido)
Dish -- a type of food that is ready to eat
"Pasta is my favorite dish." (Prato / prato de comida)
Drink -- something you drink, like water or juice
"What drink would you like?" (Bebida)
Dessert -- a sweet food you eat at the end of a meal
"For dessert, I would like the cake." (Sobremesa)
Delicious -- food that tastes very good
"This cake is delicious!" (Delicioso)
Bill -- the paper that shows how much your meal costs
"Can I have the bill, please?" (A conta)
Vegetables -- plants we eat, like carrots and tomatoes
"The fish comes with vegetables." (Legumes / verduras)
Fresh -- new and not old; just made or just picked
"The bread is fresh and warm." (Fresco)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- would like + countable / uncountable

GRAMMAR

Usamos "would like" para pedir comida de forma educada, e "a / an / some / any" com palavras contáveis e incontáveis. / We use "would like" to order food politely, and a / an / some / any with countable and uncountable words.

Form / FormaStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Polite requestI would like + nounI would like a salad.
Countable (one)a / an + singularI would like a juice.
Uncountable / plural (+)some + nounI would like some water.
QuestionWould you like...? / anyWould you like any dessert?

Key point / Ponto-chave: use would like + comida para pedir de forma educada. Use a / an com palavras contáveis no singular (a juice, an apple) e some com incontáveis ou plurais (some water, some vegetables). Em perguntas e frases negativas, use any (Would you like any cake?).
