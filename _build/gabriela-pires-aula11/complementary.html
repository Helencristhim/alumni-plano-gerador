Lesson 11 -- Let's Eat!

Series
Emily in Paris -- restaurant and cafe scenes (Season 1)

Watch the cafe and restaurant scenes. Notice how people read the menu, talk to the waiter, and say "I would like..." to order food and a drink. Listen for dessert and bill at the end.

Tip: Write down five food words you hear and one sentence with "I would like".

Podcast
Culips ESL -- "Ordering Food at a Restaurant"

A short, clear episode about ordering food in English: greeting the waiter, choosing a dish, asking for a drink and dessert, and asking for the bill at the end.

Tip: Record yourself ordering a full meal: a drink, a main dish, and a dessert. Use "I would like".

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "some, any and countable / uncountable nouns"

A focused lesson on countable and uncountable food words and how to use some and any. It reinforces today's grammar for talking about food and drinks at a restaurant.

Tip: After watching, write six sentences about a meal: three with some and three with any.
