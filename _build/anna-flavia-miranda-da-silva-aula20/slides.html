Chapter 3: The Most Polite

The Code

the + -est. the most + word.

Discover the Code

What Goes Around?

Brazil is the biggest country in South America.

He is the most polite partner.

This is the most important custom.

A handshake is the most common custom.

WordSuperlative
short word (big)the biggest
long word (polite)the most polite
long word (important)the most important

The superlative compares one thing to all others. Short words: the ...-est (the biggest). Long words: the most ... (the most polite).

Common Mistakes

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"the most big country"

✓ RIGHT

"the biggest country"

✗ WRONG

"He is most polite."

✓ RIGHT

"He is the most polite."

✗ WRONG

"the importantest custom"

✓ RIGHT

"the most important custom"

Remember: short word + -est (biggest). Long word + the most (the most polite). Always with the.
