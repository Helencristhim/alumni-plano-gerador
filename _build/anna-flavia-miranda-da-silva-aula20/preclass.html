Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Anna learns about business culture in another country. The most important custom is to be on time. A firm handshake is the most common way to say hello. People like formal and polite emails. A small gift is the kindest thing for a first meeting. Anna wants to respect every culture.

1. What is the most important custom?
A To be on time.
B To be late.
C To send a form.
2. What is the most common way to say hello?
A A gift.
B A firm handshake.
C An email.
3. What kind of emails do people like?
A Formal and polite emails.
B Long and slow emails.
C No emails.

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Superlatives

Grammar

O superlativo compara uma coisa com TODAS as outras. Palavra curta: the ...-est (the biggest). Palavra longa: the most ... (the most polite). Sempre com the. / Short word + -est, long word + the most.

EstruturaEnglishPortugues
the + curta-estthe biggest countryo maior pais
the most + longathe most polite partnero parceiro mais educado
the most + longathe most important customo costume mais importante
the most + longathe most common helloa saudacao mais comum
