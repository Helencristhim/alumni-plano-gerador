The First Conditional — BBC Learning English (Georgie)

Tonight's grammar, reviewed in one clear lesson from a trusted teacher. It nails the shape you practiced: if + present simple, will in the result — and the one trap Brazilians fall into, putting will in the if part. Watch it as a quick refresher, not a first lesson: you already know this.

Tip: after each example, pause and build your own about the airport — "If the flight is delayed, I'll...", "If the gate changes, I'll...". Say five before the video ends.
