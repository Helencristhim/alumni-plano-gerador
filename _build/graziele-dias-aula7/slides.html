Chapter 3: Present Simple

I + Verb

Talk about what you do every day

Present Simple

Talk About Your Day

"I arrive at seven." · get to work

"I start my shift." · begin work

"I finish at five." · end work

After I, use the base verb → "I check the schedule."

arrive = chegar · start = comecar · finish = terminar

Discover the Pattern

After "I"

"I arrive at seven."

"I start my shift."

"I check the schedule."

"I finish at five."

To say thisSay
Get to workI arrive at seven.
Begin workI start my shift.
Do a taskI check the schedule.
End workI finish at five.

After "I", use the base verb: no -ing, no -s. / Depois de "I", use o verbo base.

A Day at the Hospital

C
Good morning, Graziele. Can I ask about your day?

C
What time do you arrive at the hospital?

C

G
First, I check the schedule for the doctors.
C
Do you take a break during the day?
G
Yes, I take a short break for lunch.
C
And what time do you finish?
G
I finish my shift at five. It is a busy day!
