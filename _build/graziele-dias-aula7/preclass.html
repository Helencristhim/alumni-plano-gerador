Daily Routine at the Hospital

Conte o seu dia de trabalho no hospital: a que horas voce chega e comeca o turno (I arrive / I start), o que faz primeiro (I check the schedule), a pausa do almoco (I take a break) e a que horas termina (I finish). Gramatica: present simple na primeira pessoa -- I + verbo base.

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

My daily routine at the hospital is busy. I arrive at seven and I start my shift. First, I check the schedule for the doctors. At noon, I take a short break for lunch. In the afternoon, I work with the team. I finish my shift at five. After "I", I use the base verb: I arrive, I start, I finish.

1. What time does Graziele arrive? (A que horas ela chega?)
A "I arrive at seven." (Chego as sete.)
B "I finish at seven." (Termino as sete.)
C "I am seven." (Eu tenho sete.)
2. Which sentence is correct? (Qual frase esta correta?)
A "I working at seven." (errado)
B "I work at seven." (Eu trabalho as sete.)
C "I am work at seven." (errado)
3. When does Graziele take a break? (Quando ela faz a pausa?)
A "At seven, in the morning." (As sete, de manha.)
B "At noon, for lunch." (Ao meio-dia, para o almoco.)
C "At five, in the afternoon." (As cinco, a tarde.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Simple (I + verb)

Grammar

Como falar do que voce faz todo dia. / How to talk about what you do every day.

To say this / Para dizer istoSay / Diga
Chegar ao trabalhoI arrive at seven.
Comecar o turnoI start my shift.
Fazer uma tarefaI check the schedule.
Fazer uma pausaI take a short break.
Terminar o trabalhoI finish at five.

Depois de "I", use o verbo base: sem -ing, sem -s, sem "am". / After "I", use the base verb: I work (NOT "I working", NOT "I am work").
