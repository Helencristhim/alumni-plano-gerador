Aula 18 -- When Things Go Wrong

Tema da aula: pedir ajuda quando se sente mal ("I have a headache", "My stomach hurts", "Can you help me?"). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
"The Good Doctor" -- a simple hospital scene

A hospital drama. Watch a short scene where a patient describes how they feel. Listen for health words: sick, headache, fever, hurt, medicine, doctor.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote toda palavra de saúde que você ouvir (sick, headache, fever, doctor).

Podcast
"Travel English" -- At the Pharmacy & Feeling Sick (beginner episode)

A slow, clear podcast for beginners. Listen to a traveler talk to a pharmacist and practice "I have a headache" and "Can you help me?".

Dica: ouça duas vezes. Na segunda, repita em voz alta "I have a headache" e "My stomach hurts".

YouTube
"At the Pharmacy in English -- Conversation Practice" (English with subtitles)

A short, simple video of a real pharmacy conversation. A traveler says "I feel sick", asks for help, and buys medicine for a headache.

Dica: pause depois de cada fala do farmacêutico e repita. Foque nas frases "Can you help me?" e "I need medicine, please".
