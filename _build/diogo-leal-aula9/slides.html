Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

Where is the cause? Where is the result?

"The license arrived late, so we could not start the migration."

"We are two weeks late because the approval took nine days."

"We underestimated the tests. That is why the date moved."

"We have a plan: we are going to test in parallel."

Grammar Rule

Cause, Result, Plan

MoveUseExample
1. What happenedpast simple + because + causeWe are late because the approval took nine days.
2. What it meansso / that is why + resultThe license arrived late, so the go-live moves to July.
3. What you will dobe going to / will + planWe are going to test in parallel.

Never stop at move one. A cause with no plan sounds like an excuse. A plan with no date sounds like a hope. Cause, impact, plan -- and a date.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"We are late because of the approval was slow."
"We are late because the approval was slow."
"I will send the new plan until Friday."
"I will send the new plan by Friday."

because + sentence, because of + noun. And a deadline is by Friday, never "until" Friday.

The Bad-News Script

Chapter 5 -- Talking It Through

How It Sounds

One dialogue, one model call, and your turn

Dialogue

Telling Sophia First

D

"Sophia, I want to give you a heads-up before the client call. The migration is delayed."

S

"How bad is it?"

D

"Two weeks. The root cause is the license approval -- it arrived nine days late."

S

"Nine days? And we are hearing this now?"

D

"You are right, and I apologize. I made a mistake: I did not tell you last Tuesday."

S

"OK. What is the impact on the client?"

D

"The go-live moves from June 20th to July 5th. Nothing else changes."

S

"And your recovery plan?"

D

"We are going to test in parallel and add one weekend run. If it works, we save three days."

Dialogue Review

About Sophia

1. What does Sophia want to know first?
How bad the delay is.
2. Why does she react badly to "nine days"?
Because she is only hearing about it now, not nine days ago.
3. What does she ask for at the end?
The recovery plan.
