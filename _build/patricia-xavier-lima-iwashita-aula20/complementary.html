Aula 20 -- Capstone: Quarterly Business Review (QBR)

Series
Succession -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Celebration"

Watch how senior executives present results, raise concerns and push for decisions in high-stakes business reviews. Listen for the present perfect ("we have grown"), comparatives ("better than last year") and the way people quote each other (reported speech). Connection to Lesson 20: a QBR is exactly this kind of meeting — results, concerns, and clear recommendations to stakeholders.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Note 3 sentences where someone reports results or quotes another person, and rewrite them as you would in a QBR.
