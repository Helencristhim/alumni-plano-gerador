Aula 19 -- Sightseeing & Tours

Tema da aula: Sightseeing & Tours (descrever lugares e atrações num passeio pela cidade com relative clauses -- who, which, that). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series / Documentary
Any travel show with a city walking tour (streaming)

Watch a host explore a city's attractions with a guide. Listen for sightseeing words like attraction, viewpoint, and gallery, and notice how people describe places with who, which, and that.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote três atrações e duas frases que usem which ou that.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Relative clauses: who, which, that"

A short, clear lesson on joining sentences with who, which, and that to add information -- exactly today's grammar, with natural examples.

Dica: pause após cada exemplo e descreva uma atração, do tipo "It is a market that sells local crafts".

Podcast
Coffee Break English -- "Exploring a city and going on a tour"

A friendly episode about joining a guided tour, visiting landmarks, and describing what you see. Perfect vocabulary for a doctor who keeps a free morning for sightseeing on conference trips.

Dica: ouça duas vezes e anote cinco palavras de sightseeing e duas frases com who ou which.
