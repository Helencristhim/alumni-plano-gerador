Chapter 3

The Clock Code

o'clock, quarter past, half past, quarter to

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"It's seven o'clock." (7:00)

"It's quarter past seven." (7:15)

"It's half past seven." (7:30)

"It's quarter to eight." (7:45)

ClockWe say
7:00seven o'clock
7:15quarter past seven (15 after)
7:30half past seven (30 after)
7:45quarter to eight (15 before the next hour)
Datesuse the ordinal: the first, the second, the third of June
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"It's seven and fifteen."

✓ RIGHT

"It's quarter past seven."

✗ WRONG

"It's half to eight."

✓ RIGHT

"It's half past seven."

✗ WRONG

"Today is the three of June."

✓ RIGHT

"Today is the third of June."

For minutes we say "quarter past" and "quarter to", not "and fifteen". And for dates we use ordinal numbers: first, second, third.
