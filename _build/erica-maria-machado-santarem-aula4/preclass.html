Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

O'clock -- em ponto (hora cheia) — the exact hour, zero minutes
"It's seven o'clock."
Quarter past -- e quinze — fifteen minutes after the hour
"It's quarter past nine."
Half past -- e meia — thirty minutes after the hour
"It's half past eight."
Quarter to -- quinze para — fifteen minutes before the next hour
"It's quarter to ten."
Calendar -- calendário — it shows days, weeks and months
"I look at my calendar every day."
Date -- data — the day, the month and the year
"What is the date today?"
Month -- mês — one of the twelve parts of the year
"May is a beautiful month."
Appointment -- compromisso (hora marcada) — a meeting at a fixed time
"I have an appointment at three."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Telling the Time & Dates

GRAMMAR

Como dizer as horas (past = depois, to = antes) e as datas com números ordinais. / How to tell the time (past = after, to = before) and dates with ordinal numbers.

RelógioWe say
7:00seven o'clock — sete em ponto
7:15quarter past seven — sete e quinze (15 depois)
7:30half past seven — sete e meia (30 depois)
7:45quarter to eight — quinze para as oito (15 antes)
Datasuse o ordinal: the first, the second, the third of June (não "the three of June")
