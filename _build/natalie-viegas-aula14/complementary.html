Aula 14 -- Closing the Deal

Tema da aula: Closing the deal, confirming agreements and reporting what people said with reported speech (said / told + backshift). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"How to Close a Deal" -- Harvard Business Review

A short, clear video on closing a deal professionally: how to confirm the agreed terms, make sure both sides are on the same page, and finalize the paperwork without losing the relationship.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote duas frases que confirmam um acordo (por exemplo, "Let me confirm the terms..." e "Once we sign off...").

Practice
BBC Learning English -- Reported Speech

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about reported speech. Perfect to review how the verb shifts back one tense (is→was, will→would, can→could) and the difference between said and told + person.

Dica: pegue cinco frases que alguém disse hoje e reporte cada uma; confira o backshift e se você usou said ou told corretamente.

Podcast
"Business English Pod" -- Closing Deals and Confirming Agreements

A friendly business podcast for B1-B2 learners. Listen to professionals close deals, confirm terms and finalize contracts -- great input to review finalize, sign off, commitment, paperwork and kick off in context.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote toda expressão usada para confirmar um acordo e combinar o próximo passo.
