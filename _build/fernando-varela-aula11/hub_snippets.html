Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

At the ASCO congress, a visitor comes to the Einstein booth. Fernando is proud to present the oncology network. "We have the most advanced therapies in Latin America," he says. "Einstein is the largest cancer center in the country, and it is one of the leading hospitals worldwide." The visitor smiles: "That is a great achievement. The audience here is large. This is a good opportunity to work together."

1. Por que dizemos "the largest" e nao "the most largest"?
A Porque "large" e plural.
B Adjetivos curtos formam o superlativo com the + -est (nunca misturar com "most").
C Porque a acao e no futuro.
2. Usamos "the most advanced" porque:
A "advanced" e um adjetivo curto.
B "advanced" e um adjetivo longo, entao usamos the most + adjetivo.
C "advanced" e irregular.
3. Which sentence is correct?
A "It is one of the leading hospital worldwide."
B "It is one of the leading hospitals worldwide."
C "It is one of the most leading hospital worldwide."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Superlatives

GRAMMAR

Como dizer o melhor, o maior, o mais avancado (explicacao em ingles e portugues).

StructureUse / UsoExample
the + adjective-estSuperlativo (adjetivo curto)the largest center
the most + adjectiveSuperlativo (adjetivo longo)the most advanced therapy
irregular: good → the best, bad → the worstFormas irregularesthe best team
one of the + superlative + pluralone of the leading hospitals (NAO "the most largest")
