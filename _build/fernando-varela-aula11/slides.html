Chapter 3

The Superlative Code

The largest · The most advanced · The best

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"Einstein is the largest cancer center in Latin America."

"We have the most advanced therapies."

"This is the best moment for the network."

"It is one of the leading hospitals worldwide."

StructureUseExample
the + adjective-estshort adjectivethe largest center
the most + adjectivelong adjectivethe most advanced therapy
irregulargood → the best, bad → the worstthe best team
one of the + superlative + pluralone of the leading hospitals
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"We are the most largest center."

✓ RIGHT

"We are the largest center."

✗ WRONG

"Einstein is largest center."

✓ RIGHT

"Einstein is the largest center."

✗ WRONG

"This is the most good moment."

✓ RIGHT

"This is the best moment."

Never mix -est and "most". Use the largest (short word) or the most advanced (long word). Good becomes the best.
