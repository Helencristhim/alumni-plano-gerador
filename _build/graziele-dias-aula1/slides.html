Chapter 3: The Code

Who You Are

my name is · I am · I work

Discover the Grammar

Listen and Notice

"My name is Graziele."

"I am a nurse."

"I work at the hospital."

"I work with doctors."

UsePatternExample
Your nameMy name is + nameMy name is Graziele.
Your jobI am a + jobI am a nurse.
Your workplaceI work at + placeI work at the hospital.
Who you work withI work with + peopleI work with doctors.

With I we use am and work (no s). / Com "I": am e work.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"My name Graziele."

✓ RIGHT

"My name is Graziele."

✗ WRONG

"I have 33 years."

✓ RIGHT

"I am 33 years old."

Meeting Dr. Carter

The welcome begins

Dialogue

Hello, I Am Early for the Meeting

C
Hello! I am early for the meeting. I am Dr. Carter, a doctor from the United States.
G
Welcome, Dr. Carter! My name is Graziele. Nice to meet you.
C
Nice to meet you too. What is your role here?
G
I am a nurse. I work at the hospital with the medical groups.
C
Great! Do you work with many doctors?
G
Yes. I work with doctors from many teams. I am the coordinator.
C
Wonderful. Thank you for the welcome.
G
You are welcome. Please come in. The meeting starts now.
