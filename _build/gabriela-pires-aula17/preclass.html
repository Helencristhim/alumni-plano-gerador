Buying Gifts & Making Choices

Shopping for gifts in a Paris gift shop: gift vocabulary (gift, present, postcard, keychain, jewelry) and the language to choose between things ("Which one...?", "I'll take the blue one", "I prefer the small ones"). New vocabulary: gift, gift shop, present, postcard, keychain, magnet, jewelry, wallet, wrapping paper.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Gift -- a thing you give to someone, often for a special day
"I bought a gift for my best friend." (Presente)
Gift shop -- a store where you buy presents and souvenirs
"There is a gift shop at the end of the tour." (Loja de presentes)
Present -- another word for a gift: a thing you give to someone
"This scarf is a present for my mom." (Presente)
Postcard -- a small card with a picture that you send by mail
"I sent a postcard of the Eiffel Tower to my friend." (Cartão-postal)
Keychain -- a small ring or chain that you put your keys on
"The blue keychain is very popular." (Chaveiro)
Magnet -- a small object that sticks to metal, often on a fridge
"I bought a fridge magnet for my dad." (Ímã / ímã de geladeira)
Jewelry -- pretty things you wear, like rings, necklaces and earrings
"The silver jewelry is perfect for a present." (Jóias / bijuterias)
Wallet -- a small flat case where you keep your money and cards
"I bought a leather wallet for my brother." (Carteira)
Wrapping paper -- colorful paper you use to cover a present
"The clerk wrapped my gift in nice wrapping paper." (Papel de presente)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- one / ones & Which one

GRAMMAR

Para não repetir o substantivo, usamos "one" para uma coisa e "ones" para várias. Perguntamos "Which one...?" e escolhemos com "I'll take...". / To avoid repeating the noun, we use "one" for one thing and "ones" for many.

Form / FormaStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Ask which (choose)Which one + question?Which one would you like?
One thing (singular)the + adjective + onethe blue one
Many things (plural)the + adjective + onesthe small ones
Buy itI'll take + the one/onesI'll take the silver ones.
PreferI prefer + the one/onesI prefer the small ones.

Key point / Ponto-chave: use one para uma coisa e ones para várias, sem repetir o substantivo: "the blue one" (não "the blue keychain one"). Pergunte com Which one e compre com I'll take.
