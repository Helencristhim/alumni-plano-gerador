Lesson 17 -- Buying Gifts & Making Choices

Series
Gilmore Girls -- the gift shopping scenes

Watch the scenes where the characters buy presents in a store. Notice how they choose between things with "one" and "ones" and ask "Which one?".

Tip: Write down five gift words you hear and one sentence with "I'll take the ... one".

Podcast
Culips ESL -- "Shopping for Gifts"

A short, clear episode about buying gifts in English: how to choose with "one" and "ones", how to ask "Which one?", and how to say "I'll take..." in a shop.

Tip: Record yourself buying three gifts: use "one" once, "ones" once, and "I'll take" once.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "one and ones"

A focused lesson on using "one" and "ones" to avoid repeating the noun, plus "Which one?" for making choices. It reinforces today's grammar.

Tip: After watching, write six sentences: three with "one" and three with "ones".
