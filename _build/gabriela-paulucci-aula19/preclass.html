Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Bus -- a big road vehicle for many people
"I take the bus to the museum." (Ônibus)
Train -- a long vehicle that runs on rails
"The train goes to the city center." (Trem)
Subway -- a fast train under the ground
"The subway is fast in the city." (Metrô)
Taxi -- a car you pay to take you somewhere
"I take a taxi back to my hotel." (Táxi)
Platform -- where you wait for the train at a station
"The train leaves from platform two." (Plataforma)
Stop -- where the bus stops for people
"The bus stop is outside the station." (Ponto de ônibus)
Single ticket -- a ticket for one trip, one way
"I would like a single ticket, please." (Passagem só de ida)
Return ticket -- a ticket for there and back
"I buy a return ticket for the train." (Passagem de ida e volta)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas. / Read the text and answer the questions.

Gabriela leaves her hotel and walks to the station. At the ticket office she asks: "Which train goes to the city center?" The clerk says the train leaves from platform two. Gabriela says: "I would like a return ticket. How much is it?" The ticket is eight pounds. Then she asks: "Which bus goes to the museum?" The clerk says the number ten bus goes there. So Gabriela takes the train, then takes the bus. In the afternoon she takes a taxi back to her hotel.

1. What does Gabriela ask at the ticket office?
A Which train goes to the city center.
B Where is the hotel.
C What time is breakfast.
2. What ticket does she buy?
A A single ticket.
B A return ticket.
C No ticket.
3. How does she go back to her hotel?
A By train.
B By bus.
C By taxi.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Which ... goes to ...? / I take the ...

GRAMMAR

Para perguntar qual transporte ir para um lugar, use "Which + transporte + goes to + lugar?". Para dizer o que você usa, use "I take the + transporte". Para perguntar o preço, use "How much is + a + ticket?". / To ask which transport goes to a place, use "Which + transport + goes to + place?". To say what you use, say "I take the + transport". To ask the price, say "How much is + a + ticket?".

Function / FunçãoStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Ask which one / Perguntar qualWhich + transporte + goes to + lugar?Which bus goes to the museum?
Say what you use / Dizer o que usaI take the + transporte.I take the train.
Ask the price / Perguntar o preçoHow much is + a + ticket?How much is a single ticket?

Key point / Ponto-chave: o transporte leva s: the bus goes, the train goes. E diga I take the bus, não "I take bus". / The transport takes "s" (the bus goes), and say "I take THE bus", not "I take bus".
