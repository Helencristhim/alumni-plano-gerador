Chapter 3: The Code

Numbers and Dates

say each digit, one by one

Discover the Code

Listen and Notice

"My number is two, one, five, five, eight, three."

"The code is four, zero, zero, seven."

"The meeting is on Monday, March the third."

On the phone...How we say it
Phone numberone digit at a time: two - one - five - five
The number 0say "zero" (or "oh")
A datemonth + the + day: March the third
Ask again"Could you repeat that, please?"

Say each digit slowly. Pause after three or four numbers - it is easier to understand.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"My number is twenty-one fifty-five..."

✓ RIGHT

"My number is two, one, five, five."

✗ WRONG

"The meeting is on three March."

✓ RIGHT

"The meeting is on March the third."

Chapter 4: Taking a Call

A Call at Cururique

Answer, listen and take a message

Dialogue

Answering the Phone

A
Good morning, Usina Cururique. Anna speaking. How can I help you?
M
Good morning. This is Mr. Costa from the Sao Paulo office. Can I speak to your manager, please?
A
I am sorry, she is in a meeting. Can I take a message?
M
Yes, please. Could you ask her to call me back? My number is two, one, five, five, eight, three.
A
Of course. Could you repeat the number, please?
M
Sure. Two, one, five, five, eight, three. The meeting is on March the third. Thank you.
Listening

A Voicemail

Listen to the message. Sound first, no text.

0:000:00
1. Who is calling, and from which office?
Mr. Silva, from the Sao Paulo office.
2. When is the meeting?
On Monday, March the third, at ten o'clock.
3. What is his phone number?
Two, one, four, four, seven, six, eight, three.
Comprehension

About the Message

1. Why is Mr. Silva calling?
About a meeting next week.
2. What does he ask Anna to do?
To call him back to confirm.
3. Where can she also leave a message?
On his voicemail.
Listening 2

Anna's Greeting

Anna's voicemail message. Sound first, no text.

0:000:00
1. Whose voicemail is this?
Anna Flavia, at Usina Cururique.
2. What three things should the caller leave?
Their name, their phone number and a short message.
3. What can the caller do if it is urgent?
Send an email.
