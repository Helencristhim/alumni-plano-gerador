Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o recado e responda as perguntas. / Read the message and answer.

Mr. Silva leaves a voicemail: "Hello, this is a message for Anna. The meeting is on Monday, March the third, at ten o'clock. Please call me back. My number is two, one, four, four. You can also leave a voicemail."

1. How does he say his phone number?
A As one big number (two thousand...).
B One digit at a time: two, one, four, four.
C He does not say a number.
2. When is the meeting?
A On Monday, March the third.
B On three March.
C Next year.
3. What does he ask Anna to do?
A Send a package.
B Call him back.
C Visit the office.

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Numbers & Dates

Grammar

No telefone, diga cada digito separado. Para datas, use month + the + day. / On the phone, say each digit; for dates, say month + the + day.

Etapa 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao.

The phone rings at Cururique. What do you say?
A "Who are you?"
B "Good morning, Cururique. Anna speaking."
C "Goodbye."
You did not hear the phone number. What do you say?
A "Could you repeat that, please?"
B "I don't care."
C "Thank you, bye."
Your manager is in a meeting. What do you offer?
A "Call again next year."
B "Can I take a message?"
C "She is not here. Bye."

Etapa 5: Free Production

Reflection
