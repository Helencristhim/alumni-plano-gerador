Chapter 3

How to Ask

Tell your allergy and make a polite request

Discover the Rule

Listen and Notice

"I am allergic to nuts."

"I am allergic to shellfish."

"Can you make it without dairy?"

"Does it have gluten in it?"

StructureUseExample
I am allergic to + foodSay what food is dangerous for youI am allergic to peanuts.
Can you + verb...?Ask the waiter to do somethingCan you make it without nuts?
Does it have + food + in it?Ask about an ingredientDoes it have gluten in it?

After "to" and "without", use the food word. Always be polite with "please".

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"I am allergic of nuts."

✓ RIGHT

"I am allergic to nuts."

✗ WRONG

"Can you to make it without nuts?"

At the Restaurant

A Safe Order

D
"Good evening. Are you ready to order?"
E
"Almost. First, I am allergic to nuts. Does the salad have nuts in it?"
D
"Let me check. Yes, the green salad has nuts in it."
E
"Can you make it without nuts, please?"
D
"Of course. I can make it without nuts."
E
"Thank you. I am also allergic to shellfish. Is there shellfish in the soup?"
D
"No, the tomato soup has no shellfish. It is safe for you."
E
"Good. Can you recommend a main course without dairy?"
D
"Yes. The grilled chicken with vegetables has no dairy."
E
"Is it spicy?"
D
"No, it is not spicy. It is very mild."
E
"Perfect. I would like the grilled chicken, please."
