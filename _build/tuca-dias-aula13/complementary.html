Lesson 13 -- Money Matters: Banks, Cash & Cards

Series / Travel
Wolters World -- "Money Tips for Travelers: Cash, Cards & Exchange" (YouTube)

A friendly traveler explains how to change money, use ATMs abroad, and avoid high fees and bad exchange rates. Full of natural money vocabulary in real situations.

Watch one video with English subtitles. Write three sentences with the second conditional about your dream trip (if + past, would).

Podcast
All Ears English -- "At the Bank and Changing Money in English"

A short, clear episode about handling money in English: changing currency, asking about fees, and withdrawing cash. You will hear the words exchange rate, fee, and balance used naturally.

After listening, record a one-minute voice note about changing money at a bank. Use the words bank, cash, exchange rate, and fee.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "The Second Conditional: if + past, would"

A practical grammar video that explains how to talk about imaginary situations and their results, with clear examples. Exactly today's grammar focus.

After watching, write four sentences about imaginary plans, each using if + past, would + base verb. Try one with "if I were you".
