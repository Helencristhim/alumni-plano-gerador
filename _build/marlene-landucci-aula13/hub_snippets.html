Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir. / Listen to each word and read the example.

Cash -- paper money and coins (dinheiro em espécie)
"I will pay with cash." (Vou pagar em dinheiro.)
Change -- the money you get back after you pay (troco)
"Here is your change." (Aqui está o seu troco.)
Wallet -- a small case where you keep your money (carteira)
"My wallet is in my bag." (Minha carteira está na minha bolsa.)
Expensive -- it costs a lot of money (caro)
"This bag is very expensive." (Esta bolsa é muito cara.)
Cheap -- it costs little money (barato)
"These postcards are cheap." (Estes cartões-postais são baratos.)
Discount -- money off the price (desconto)
"There is a discount today." (Hoje tem desconto.)
Fitting room -- a small room where you try on clothes (provádor)
"The fitting room is over there." (O provádor é ali.)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- How Much & This/That/These/Those

GRAMMAR

Para perguntar o preço de UMA coisa: "How much is this/that?" Para MUITAS coisas: "How much are these/those?" this/these = perto de você; that/those = longe. / To ask the price of ONE thing: "How much is this/that?" For MANY things: "How much are these/those?" this/these = near you; that/those = far.

WordExample (EN)Tradução (PT)
this (one, near)How much is this scarf?Quanto custa este cachecol?
that (one, far)How much is that bag?Quanto custa aquela bolsa?
these (many, near)How much are these postcards?Quanto custam estes postais?
those (many, far)How much are those shoes?Quanto custam aqueles sapatos?

Key point / Ponto-chave: uma coisa = is (How much is this?). Muitas coisas = are (How much are these?). Perto = this/these; longe = that/those.

Aula 13 -- Money and Shopping

Tema da aula: fazer compras e perguntar preços ("How much is this?" / "How much are these?") e usar this / that / these / those. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.
