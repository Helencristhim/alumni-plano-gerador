Aula 13 -- Money and Shopping

Tema da aula: fazer compras e perguntar preços ("How much is this?" / "How much are these?") e usar this / that / these / those. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Film
"Confessions of a Shopaholic" -- the store scenes

A fun film about shopping. Watch the store and checkout scenes and listen for shopping words: how much, price, discount, cash, and change.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote toda palavra de compra que você ouvir (how much, discount, cash, change).

Podcast
"Travel English" -- Shopping & Money (beginner episode)

A slow, clear podcast for beginners. Listen to a traveler buy a gift and practice "How much is this?" and "How much are these?" with this, that, these, and those.

Dica: ouça duas vezes. Na segunda, repita em voz alta "How much is this?" e "I will pay with cash".

YouTube
"Shopping in English -- Conversation at a Store" (English with subtitles)

A short, simple video of a real shopping conversation. A customer asks "How much is this?", learns about a discount, pays with cash, and takes the change and receipt.

Dica: pause depois de cada fala do clerk e repita. Foque nas frases "How much are these?" e "Is there a discount today?".
