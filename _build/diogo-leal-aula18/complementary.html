Lesson 18 -- Review Block 3, Presentation Mastery

Series
Any product keynote on YouTube (Apple, Google, or Microsoft)

Watch the first five minutes of a real keynote. Notice the six skills: the opening, the signposts, the data, the Q&A, and the closing. Listen for "first / then / finally" and how the speaker keeps a calm pace.

Write down one signpost phrase and one data sentence you hear. Then say your own version out loud.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- Presentation skills review episode

A full review of how to open, signpost, present data, handle Q&A, and close. Revisits today's words: signpost, transition, data, recap, takeaway.

Record a five-minute talk about your work and time it. Check that every part has a smooth transition and that you end with one clear takeaway.

YouTube
"How to Structure a Business Presentation in English"

A clear guide to the full structure: opening, signposting, presenting data with "rose" and "dropped by", handling questions, and closing with a recap and a takeaway. Learn to keep the momentum from start to finish.

After watching, build a delivery checklist for one project at Oracle: open, signpost, data, Q&A, close. Rehearse it until it feels natural.
