Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Proof -- a fact or piece of data that shows something is true
"The trial gives us clear proof that the formula works." (Prova / evidência)
Therefore -- for that reason; introduces a result
"The data is strong, therefore we should invest." (Portanto)
Lead to -- to cause or produce a particular result
"A bigger budget would lead to a bigger study." (Levar a / resultar em)
Factor -- one of the things that influences a result
"Cost is the main factor in this decision." (Fator)
Impact -- the strong effect that something has on a result
"The new formula has a real impact on patient care." (Impacto)
Demonstrate -- to show something clearly with facts or examples
"The data demonstrate that the treatment is effective." (Demonstrar)
Conclude -- to end an argument with a final decision or point
"To conclude, I recommend phase two." (Concluir)
Rationale -- the set of reasons that explain a decision
"My rationale is simple: the proof is strong." (Justificativa / raciocínio)

Because the results are so strong, she argues that a larger budget would lead to a larger study, and a larger study would lead to even more reliable results. Her rationale is simple. Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Cause and effect

GRAMMAR

Para argumentar, conectamos causas e efeitos com conectores. / To argue, we connect causes and effects with linking words.

Function / FunçãoStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Give the causebecause + clause"Because the data is strong, we will invest."
Give the result..., therefore / as a result + clause"The data is strong, therefore we will invest."
Connect cause to effectcause + lead to + noun"More funding will lead to a bigger study."
Show with evidencethe data demonstrate (that) ..."The data demonstrate a clear impact."

Key point / Ponto-chave: Use because para a causa, e therefore ou as a result para o efeito. Use lead to + substantivo (nunca + verbo no infinitivo) para conectar uma causa diretamente a um resultado.
