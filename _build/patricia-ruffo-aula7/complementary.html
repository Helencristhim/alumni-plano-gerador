Lesson 7 -- Making the Case

Series
Mad Men -- any client pitch scene

Watch how the team builds a persuasive case: they present proof, explain the impact, and connect cause and effect to convince the client. Notice the structure of a strong argument.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down one piece of proof and one cause-and-effect sentence (because, therefore, as a result) that you hear.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Making a Persuasive Business Case"

A focused episode on building an argument in English: stating a recommendation, presenting proof, and using cause-and-effect language to persuade a decision-maker.

Record a 2-minute pitch: recommend an investment, present your proof, and conclude with your rationale using today's vocabulary.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Linking words: cause and effect"

A clear lesson on the connectors that build an argument (because, therefore, as a result, lead to). It directly reinforces today's cause-and-effect language.

After watching, write six sentences: three with because (cause) and three with therefore or as a result (effect).
