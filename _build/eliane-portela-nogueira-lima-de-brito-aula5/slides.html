Chapter 3

Modal Diplomacy

Could, would, should, might, may — and conditionals to move a deal forward

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"Could you walk me through the pricing model?"

"We might be interested in a pilot program."

"This would work well for our setup."

"If we move forward, we would need faster lead times."

ModalMessageExample
couldA polite request or a soft possibilityCould you send a quote?
wouldA hypothetical result or a soft statementThis would work for us.
shouldA recommendation or an expectationWe should review the warranty.
might / mayA possibility, with no commitmentWe might ask for exclusivity.
If + would / couldConditional to negotiate: If we move forward, we would need faster lead times. Every modal takes the base verb, with no "to".
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

Could you to send the quote?
Could you send the quote?
We might to ask for exclusivity.
We might ask for exclusivity.
This would works well for us.
This would work well for us.

After a modal (could, would, should, might, may), the verb stays in its base form: no "to" and no "-s".

Chapter 4

The Supplier Call

A real negotiation with an American technology supplier

Dialogue

Leading the Negotiation

D
Hi Eliane, thanks for joining. I would love to walk you through what we can offer your new lab.
E
Thank you, Daniel. Before we start, could you walk me through the pricing model for the embryology equipment?
D
Of course. The base package is one hundred twenty thousand dollars, and it includes installation and a five-year warranty.
E
That is helpful. Let me make sure I understand correctly — does that price also cover the lead time and delivery?
D
It does. Our delivery window is eight to ten weeks, and we handle the regulatory approval paperwork with you.
E
Good. We might be interested in a pilot program first. Would you be open to that?
D
Absolutely. A pilot could work well, and if it goes well, we could discuss exclusivity for Brazil.
E
I would like to revisit that point later. For now, what is your best offer if we sign this quarter?
D
If you commit this quarter, we could take ten percent off and extend the warranty. I will send the proposal in writing.
Comprehension

Did You Catch It?

1. What does Daniel include in the base package?
Installation and a five-year warranty.
2. What is Daniel's delivery window?

3. What does Daniel offer if Eliane signs this quarter?
Ten percent off and an extended warranty.
