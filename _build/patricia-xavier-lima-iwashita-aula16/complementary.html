"Comparatives and Superlatives -- Describing Trends & Numbers" -- BBC Learning English

A clear, practical lesson on exactly today's grammar: comparatives (higher than, more efficient than) and the language of trends (increase, decrease, a slight/significant rise). Tip: pause after each example and describe one of your own metrics out loud, comparing it to the target with the structure you just heard.
