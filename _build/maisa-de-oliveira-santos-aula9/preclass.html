Aula 09 -- Pre-class

Asking for Help

Pedir ajuda e favores no trabalho de forma educada: help, explain, repeat, show, favor, check, understand, make sense. Gramática: pedidos com Can e Could (Can you help me? / Could you explain that? / Could you repeat, please?). Missão: pedir ajuda ao David com um relatório, do começo ao fim.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

Help -- to do something for someone who needs it
"Can you help me, please?" (Ajudar)
Explain -- to make something clear and easy to understand
"Could you explain this report?" (Explicar)
Repeat -- to say something again
"Could you repeat that, please?" (Repetir)
Show -- to let someone see how to do something
"Can you show me how it works?" (Mostrar)
Favor -- a kind thing you ask someone to do for you
"Can I ask you a favor?" (Favor)
Check -- to look at something to see if it is correct
"Could you check my email?" (Verificar / conferir)
Understand -- to know what something means
"I do not understand this word." (Entender)
Make sense -- to be clear and easy to understand
"Now it makes sense. Thank you!" (Fazer sentido)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

Maísa has a question about the finance report. She goes to David and says: "Can you help me, please?" David says yes. "I do not understand this number. Could you explain it to me?" David explains, but he speaks fast. "Sorry, could you repeat that, please?" she asks. David repeats slowly and says: "Let me show you on the spreadsheet." Now it makes sense. "Thank you! Can I ask you one more favor? Could you check my email before I send it?" David checks it. "Perfect," he says.

1. Como a Maísa pede ajuda de forma educada? / How does Maísa ask for help politely?
A "You help me now."
B "Can you help me, please?"
C "Help me you."
2. O que a Maísa diz quando não entende? / What does Maísa say when she does not understand?
A "Could you explain it to me?"
B "You explain to me."
C "Explain me it."
3. Qual é o último favor que a Maísa pede? / What is the last favor Maísa asks for?
A To repeat the number.
B To check her email before she sends it.
C To send the report for her.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Can / Could Requests

GRAMMAR

Para pedir ajuda em inglês, usamos Can you...? e Could you...? + verbo. Could é mais educado que Can. Adicione please para ser ainda mais gentil. / To ask for help, use Can you...? or Could you...? + verb.

Estrutura / StructureUso / UseExemplo / Example
Can you + verb?pedido simples / simple requestCan you help me?
Could you + verb?mais educado / more politeCould you explain that?
..., please?bem gentil / very politeCould you repeat, please?
Would you mind + verb-ing?muito educado / very politeWould you mind checking this?

Atenção: depois de Can you / Could you vem o verbo direto, SEM "to". Diga Can you help me? (nunca "Can you to help me?"). Para responder um pedido: "Sure.", "Of course.", "No problem.". Afirmativo: Can you help? Negativo: I cannot help now. Interrogativo: Could you help me?

Stage 5: Think About It

Reflection

Sem certo ou errado. Pense em inglês, toque no microfone e fale livremente.

Imagine: você precisa de ajuda com um relatório no trabalho. Em 4-5 frases, peça ajuda ao David de forma educada: diga que não entende uma parte, peça para ele explicar e repetir, e agradeça. Use: Can you help me...?, Could you explain...?, Could you repeat...?, I do not understand..., Thank you.
