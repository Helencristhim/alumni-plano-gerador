Chapter 3: The Code

Is and Has

describing a person

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"She is friendly. She is smart."

"He has short hair. He has glasses."

"David is a hardworking manager."

"What is she like? She is kind and quiet."

IdeaVerbExample
Personality / looksis + adjectiveShe is friendly. He is tall.
Hair, glasses, a beardhas + nounHe has short hair. She has glasses.
Ask about a personWhat is ... like?What is she like?

For personality and looks, use is + adjective. For hair, glasses or a beard, use has + noun. To ask about someone, say What is he/she like?

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

She is have brown hair.
She has brown hair.
He is friendly person.
He is a friendly person.

Hair and glasses use has, not "is have". And before adjective + noun, add a: "a friendly person".
