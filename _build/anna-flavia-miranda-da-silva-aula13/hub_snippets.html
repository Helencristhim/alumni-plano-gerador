Etapa 1.1: Palavras do relatorio

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra nova e leia o exemplo. Estas palavras servem para apresentar numeros num relatorio. / Listen to each new word and read the example.

Number -- numero
"This number is 100."
Total -- total
"The total of sales is big."
Profit -- lucro
"Our profit is good."
Growth -- crescimento
"The growth is good this year."
Increase -- aumentar
"Our sales increase every year."
Bigger -- maior
"The total is bigger than last month."
Higher -- mais alto
"Our profit is higher this month."

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia a apresentacao da Anna e responda as perguntas. / Read Anna's presentation and answer.

Today Anna presents the numbers to the team. She shows the total of sales. "The total is bigger than last month," she says. The profit is higher too. The growth is good this year, and sugar exports increase every year. Bruno says: "The numbers are great, Anna."

1. How is the total this month?
A Bigger than last month.
B Smaller than last month.
C The same as last month.
2. What does Anna say about the profit?
A The profit is lower.
B The profit is higher.
C There is no profit.
3. What does Bruno think about the numbers?
A The numbers are great.
B The numbers are bad.
C The numbers are small.

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Comparatives

Grammar

Para comparar dois numeros, adicione -er a palavra e use than: big -> bigger than, high -> higher than. Palavras curtas como big dobram a ultima letra (bigger). Nunca use "more big". / To compare two numbers, add -er and use than. Short words double the last letter.

WordComparativePortugues
bigbigger thanmaior que
highhigher thanmais alto que
smallsmaller thanmenor que
ExampleThe total is bigger than last month.O total e maior que o mes passado.
