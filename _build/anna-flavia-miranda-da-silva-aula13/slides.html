Chapter 3: Comparatives

Bigger Than

One easy way to compare two numbers

Discover the Code

Listen and Find the Pattern

"The total is bigger than last month."

"Our profit is higher than last year."

"This number is smaller than that one."

"The growth is faster than in May."

WordComparative
bigbigger than
highhigher than
smallsmaller than

To compare two numbers, add -er to the word and use than. Short words like big double the last letter: bigger.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"The total is more big."

✓ RIGHT

"The total is bigger."

✗ WRONG

"Bigger that last month."

✓ RIGHT

"Bigger than last month."

Short words use -er, not "more". Always use than (not "that") to compare.
