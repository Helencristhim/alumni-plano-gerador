Talking About the Past -- The Last Farm Verification Visit

Relate o que aconteceu na sua ultima visita de verificacao usando o simple past: verbos regulares (visited, checked, walked, recorded) e irregulares (saw, gave, spoke, took) e a negativa did not + verbo base. Seis verbos no passado e um field note modelo para o sistema IMS.

Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Ouca cada verbo no passado e leia o exemplo. Sao acoes de uma visita de verificacao que ja terminou. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Arrived -- cheguei / chegou (passado de arrive)
"I arrived early in the morning." (Eu cheguei cedo de manha.)
Recorded -- registrei / anotei (passado de record)
"I recorded my findings in the system." (Eu registrei minhas constatacoes no sistema.)
Saw -- vi (passado irregular de see)
"I saw the two storage areas." (Eu vi as duas areas de armazenamento.)
Gave -- deu / me deu (passado irregular de give)
"The producer gave me a warm welcome." (O produtor me deu boas-vindas calorosas.)
Spoke -- falei / conversei (passado irregular de speak)
"I spoke with the farm manager." (Eu conversei com o gerente da fazenda.)
Took -- peguei / tirei (passado irregular de take)
"I took some photos of the boundary." (Eu tirei algumas fotos do limite.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o field note sobre a visita da semana passada e responda as perguntas. (Read the field note about last week's visit and answer.)

Last week, I visited a coffee farm in the south of Minas Gerais. I arrived early, and the producer gave me a warm welcome. First, I checked the documents. Then I walked around the property and saw the storage areas. I spoke with the manager and took some photos. In the afternoon, I recorded my findings. The farm did not have any critical issues. It was a good visit.

1. Which sentence is in the simple past? (Qual frase esta no simple past?)
A "I visit a coffee farm every week." (presente)
B "Last week, I visited a coffee farm." (passado: visited)
C "I am visiting a coffee farm now." (present continuous)
2. Which verb is IRREGULAR in the past? (Qual verbo e irregular no passado?)
A "checked" (check + ed = regular)
B "saw" (see muda para saw = irregular)
C "recorded" (record + ed = regular)
3. What is the correct negative? (Qual e a negativa correta?)
A "The farm did not had issues." (did not + had = errado)
B "The farm did not have issues." (did not + verbo base)
C "The farm not have issues." (falta o did)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Simple Past

Grammar

Como falar do passado: verbo regular (base + ed), verbo irregular (muda a palavra), e a negativa did not + verbo base. / How to talk about the past.

Form / FormaHow / ComoExample / Exemplo
Regularbase + edvisit -> visited; check -> checked; record -> recorded
Irregulara palavra mudasee -> saw; give -> gave; speak -> spoke; take -> took
Negative / Negativadid not (didn't) + verbo baseThe farm did not have issues. (nao "did not had")
Time markersyesterday, last week, last month, two days ago, in 2023 (marcadores de tempo passado)

Etapa 3: Pronunciation

Speaking

Ouca cada frase no passado e depois grave voce dizendo. (Listen, then record yourself.)

I checked the registration documents.
Verbo regular no passado (check + ed = checked).
I walked around the property.
Verbo regular no passado (walk + ed = walked).
I found two small problems.
Verbo irregular no passado (find muda para found).
It was a good and productive visit.
Passado do verbo to be (is muda para was).

Etapa 4: Quiz Situacional

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao.

You want to say you got to the farm early. You say: (Voce quer dizer que chegou cedo.)
A "I arrive early yesterday."
B "I arrived early in the morning."
C "I am arriving early."
You talked with the manager. Which is correct? (Voce conversou com o gerente.)
A "I speak with the manager last week."
B "I spoke with the manager last week."
C "I spoked with the manager last week."
You want the negative: no critical issues. You say: (Voce quer a negativa.)
A "The farm did not had critical issues."
B "The farm not have critical issues."
C "The farm did not have critical issues."

Etapa 5: Producao Livre

Reflection

Grave voce respondendo a pergunta abaixo. Nao ha resposta certa ou errada.

Tell the story of your last farm visit (real or realistic), in the past. Say when you arrived, what you checked, what you saw, who you spoke with, what you found, and one thing the farm did not have. Take your time. (Conte a historia da sua ultima visita a fazenda, no passado.)
