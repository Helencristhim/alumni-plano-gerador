Chapter 4: Simple Past

Visited, Saw, Gave, Did Not Have

How do we build the past?

Discover the Grammar

Read and Notice

"I visited a coffee farm last week."

"I saw the two storage areas."

"The producer gave me a warm welcome."

"The farm did not have critical issues."

FormHowExample
Regularbase + edvisit -> visited; record -> recorded
Irregularthe word changessee -> saw; give -> gave; take -> took
Negativedid not + base verbThe farm did not have issues.
Time markersyesterday, last week, last month, two days ago, in 2023

Regular past adds -ed; irregular past changes form. Negative = did not + base verb (not "did not had"). / O passado regular usa -ed; o irregular muda a palavra. Negativa = did not + verbo base.

Language Focus

Regular or Irregular?

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"I go to the farm yesterday."

✓ RIGHT

"I went to the farm yesterday."

✗ WRONG

"The farm did not had issues."

✓ RIGHT

"The farm did not have issues."

With a past time marker, use the past verb (went, not go). In the negative, use did not + base verb (have, not had). / Com marcador de passado, use o verbo no passado; na negativa, use did not + verbo base.

Dialogue

Emma Asks: How Was the Visit?

E
Hi Carlos. How was your verification visit last week?
C
Hi Emma. It was good. I arrived early and the producer gave me a warm welcome.
E
Good. What did you check first?
C
First, I checked the documents. Everything was correct.
E
Did you speak with the producer?
C
Yes, I spoke with the manager and I took some photos of the boundary.
E
And did you find any problems?
C
I found two small problems, but the farm did not have any critical issues. I recorded everything.
