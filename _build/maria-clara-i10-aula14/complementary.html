Lesson 14 -- Verbs That Take 'To' and Verbs That Take '-ing'

Series
BBC 6 Minute Grammar — "Verb Patterns" (BBC Learning English)

This is today's lesson, from the BBC. In six short minutes, two hosts explain the exact code you learned: some verbs take to (want to go, decide to stay) and some take -ing (enjoy reading, keep trying). Slow, clear British English, made for learners like you.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Treasure hunt: write down 3 verbs that take "to" and 3 verbs that take "-ing". Then make one true sentence about your work with each group.
