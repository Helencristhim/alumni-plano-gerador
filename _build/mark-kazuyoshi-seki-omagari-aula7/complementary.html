Complementary Materials — Lesson 7

Series
Brainchild — Netflix: "Social Media"

A fun, fast science show for teenagers that builds clear arguments step by step. Great for hearing how a strong opinion is organized with examples and contrasts.

Tip: pause when they change idea and say which connector fits (however, moreover, for instance, therefore).

Podcast
6 Minute English — BBC: "How to win an argument"

A short, clear podcast about building and connecting ideas in a discussion. Perfect B2 listening that reuses today's discourse markers.

Tip: listen once for the gist, then again and write down four connectors you hear (however, moreover, therefore...).

YouTube
BBC Learning English — "Linking words and connectors"

A short, clear video that explains how to join ideas with however, moreover, therefore and in addition, with plenty of examples to copy out loud.

Tip: after watching, say your opinion about homework using four different connectors.
