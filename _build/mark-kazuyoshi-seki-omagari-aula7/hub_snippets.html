Connecting Ideas — Discourse Markers

Discourse markers to build a structured opinion: adding (moreover, in addition, furthermore), contrasting (however, on the other hand, nevertheless, despite, whereas), example (for instance), result (therefore, as a result) and concluding (in conclusion).

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each connector and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

However — used to introduce a contrasting idea
"I love sports; however, I also enjoy reading." (no entanto)
Moreover — used to add a stronger supporting point
"The plan is cheap; moreover, it is fast." (além disso)
In addition — used to add another related point
"In addition, it helps the environment." (adicionalmente)
Furthermore — used to add a further, more formal point
"Furthermore, students would feel less tired." (ademais)
Therefore — used to show a result or a logical conclusion
"He studied hard; therefore, he passed." (portanto)
On the other hand — used to present the opposite side of an idea
"On the other hand, some parents disagree." (por outro lado)
Nevertheless — used to say something is true despite a problem
"It was hard; nevertheless, we finished." (mesmo assim)
For instance — used to introduce an example
"Many sports help, for instance, swimming." (por exemplo)
As a result — used to show the effect of something
"He trained daily; as a result, he improved." (como resultado)
In conclusion — used to start your final point or summary
"In conclusion, I believe schools should change." (em conclusão)
Despite — used before a noun to show contrast
"Despite the rain, we played outside." (apesar de)
Whereas — used to compare two different things or ideas
"I like tennis, whereas my brother likes football." (enquanto que)
First of all — used to introduce your very first point
"First of all, I want to explain my opinion." (antes de tudo)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the opinion text and answer the questions.

I believe our school should start one hour later. First of all, many teenagers wake up tired. Moreover, students who sleep more often get better grades. For instance, one school tried a later start and saw better results. However, some parents work early. On the other hand, the school could offer a quiet study room. Therefore, the benefits are stronger than the problems. In conclusion, a later start would help students focus.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip — Connectors by Function

GRAMMAR

Conectores organizam a opinião. Escolha pela função: adicionar, contrastar, exemplificar, concluir. / Connectors organize an opinion. Choose by function: add, contrast, give an example, conclude.

Function / FunçãoConnectorsExample
Add / Adicionarmoreover, in addition, furthermoreIt is cheap; moreover, it is fast.
Contrast / Contrastarhowever, on the other hand, whereasI like art; however, I dislike exams.
Example / Exemplificarfor instanceSome sports help, for instance, swimming.
Result / Resultadotherefore, as a resultHe trained daily; therefore, he won.
Conclude / Concluirin conclusionIn conclusion, I support the idea.

Key point / Ponto-chave: use despite antes de um substantivo (Despite the rain), e however no início de uma nova frase com vírgula (However, ...). Nunca escreva "despite of".
