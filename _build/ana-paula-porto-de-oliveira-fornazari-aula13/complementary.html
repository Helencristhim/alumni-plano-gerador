Take a "Tenses Tour" -- 6 Minute Grammar (BBC Learning English)

A short, clear tour through the main English tenses in one place: present, past, present perfect and future. Two hosts explain, in slow and natural English, how to choose the right tense for the time you mean. Connection to Lesson 13: this is exactly today's review, the same tenses you mixed in your own story about your English journey.

Tip: each time they name a tense, pause and make one true sentence about your own English journey.

Grammar Overview
Verb Tenses: Present Simple, Present Continuous, Past Simple, Present Perfect and More (Sanook English)

A friendly overview that puts several tenses side by side with simple example sentences, so you can see how they are different and when to use each one. It is a comfortable way to check that you still remember every tense from the first half of the course. Connection to Lesson 13: it reviews the same forms you practiced today, all in one calm video.

Tip: after each tense, close your eyes and say one sentence about your routine, your past, or your goals.

Story
My First Journey Through New York City -- Learn English Through Story (English With Marco)

A gentle, spoken story about a first trip to a new city, told slowly and clearly. The narrator naturally mixes the past, the present and the future, exactly like you did today when you told your own journey. Connection to Lesson 13: listen to how one story moves between tenses without stopping, and notice the same review words, like journey, routine and highlight.

Tip: every time the tense changes, whisper which one it is (past, present, or future), then keep listening.
