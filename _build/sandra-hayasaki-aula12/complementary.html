Aula 12 — Habits Over Time

Series
This Is Us — any episode (life across the years, then and now)

A warm drama that jumps between the past and the present of one family. Perfect to hear how people talk about what they used to do and how their lives changed over time.

Dica: escolha uma cena do passado e anote uma frase com "used to" e uma com "would". / Pick a past scene and write one sentence with "used to" and one with "would".

Podcast
All Ears English — "Used To and Would" episodes

Clear, friendly English about how to talk about past habits, and the difference between used to and would. A great way to hear these forms used naturally in real conversation.

Dica: ouça uma vez só e anote três hábitos do passado que você ouvir. / Listen once and note three past habits you hear.

English with Lucy — Used To, Would and Past Habits

A calm, clear lesson on used to and would, and why we drop the -d in questions and negatives — the same code you practiced today. Great for older learners who like a steady pace.
