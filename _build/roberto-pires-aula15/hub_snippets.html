Stage 1.1: Small Talk Vocabulary

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Estas palavras ajudam voce a conversar de forma simpatica com os moradores locais numa viagem.

Local -- morador (da cidade)
"A friendly local showed me the best cafe."
Friendly -- simpatico, amigavel
"The people here are very friendly."
Weather -- tempo (clima)
"The weather is lovely today."
Neighborhood -- bairro
"This is a quiet, safe neighborhood."
To chat -- bater papo, conversar
"We love to chat with visitors."
Hometown -- cidade natal
"My hometown is Sao Paulo."
Married -- casado(a)
"Yes, I am married. What about you?"
Nearby -- perto (daqui)
"There is a lovely park nearby."
View -- vista (paisagem)
"The view from the hill is beautiful."
Lovely -- adoravel, muito agradavel
"It was a lovely chat. Thank you!"

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia a situacao e responda as perguntas.

On his last day, Roberto meets a friendly local on the street. "The weather is lovely today!" she says. "Are you enjoying our neighborhood?" Roberto answers, "Yes, I am. What about you? Do you live nearby?" "Yes, I do," she says, and tells him about a park with a beautiful view close by. They chat about his hometown, Sao Paulo. At the end, Roberto says, "It was a lovely chat. Thank you!"

1. What does Roberto say after he answers "Yes, I am"?
A "What about you?"
B "Goodbye."
C "How much is it?"
2. What is the correct short answer to "Do you live nearby?"
A "Yes, I am."
B "Yes, I do."
C "Yes, I live."
3. How does Roberto end the chat?
A He walks away with no words.
B He says it was a lovely chat and thanks her.
C He asks for a discount.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Short Answers & Follow-up Questions

Grammar

Para manter uma conversa viva, faca DUAS coisas: de uma resposta curta e depois devolva a pergunta. Com perguntas "Are/Is...?" responda "Yes, I am / No, I am not". Com "Do...?" responda "Yes, I do / No, I do not" (nunca "Yes, I like"). Depois devolva com "What about you?" ou "And you?". / To keep a conversation going, do TWO things: give a short answer, then send the question back. With "Are/Is...?" answer "Yes, I am / No, I am not". With "Do...?" answer "Yes, I do / No, I do not" (never "Yes, I like"). Then send it back with "What about you?" or "And you?".

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao.

A local asks: "Are you enjoying the city?" What is the best answer?
A "Yes, I am. What about you?"
B "Yes, I do."
C "Yes."
A local asks: "Do you know this neighborhood?" Which short answer is correct?
A "Yes, I am."
B "No, I do not. What about you?"
C "No, I know not."
You want to start a friendly small talk. What do you say first?
A "How much is the bill?"
B "The weather is lovely today, isn't it?"
C "Where is the exit?"
