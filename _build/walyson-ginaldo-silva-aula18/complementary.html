Lesson 18 -- Small Talk Before Business

Series
Ted Lasso -- a "getting to know you" scene (any season)

Watch a scene where characters break the ice and build rapport with small talk before getting to the point. Notice the open questions and the friendly follow-ups.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down two open questions (How / What...?) and one follow-up like "What about you?".

Podcast
Plain English -- "How to Make Small Talk in English"

A practical episode on small talk: how to break the ice, choose safe topics, ask open questions, and find common ground to build rapport before business.

Record three small-talk lines: one icebreaker, one open question about the weekend, and one follow-up.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Open and closed questions"

A focused lesson on open vs. closed questions: how How/What/Why questions invite longer answers, while yes/no questions stop the conversation. It directly reinforces today's grammar.

After watching, turn six closed questions into open questions, and write two follow-ups.
