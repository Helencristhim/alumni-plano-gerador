Aula 8 -- Plans & Predictions

Tema da aula: Plans & Predictions -- talking about the future with going to (plans) and will (predictions). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"Going to vs Will" -- future forms explained

Watch a short, clear video about the difference between going to and will. Listen for how going to is used for plans and will for predictions and quick decisions.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote três frases com going to e três com will.

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about talking about the future: when to use going to for plans and will for predictions. Perfect to review today's grammar.
