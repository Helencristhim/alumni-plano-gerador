Chapter 3 -- The Email Code

Three Parts

Open -- Body -- Close

Listen and Notice

Which Part?

Open, body, or close?

"Dear Mr. Reed, I am writing regarding the NDA."

"Could you please review the attached draft?"

"Please put my manager in CC and forward it to the team."

"I look forward to your reply. Best regards, Simone."

The Email Code

Open -- Body -- Close

PartWhat you doExample
OpeningGreeting and the reason you are writing"Dear Mr. Reed, I am writing regarding the NDA."
BodyOne clear request; mention the attachment"Could you please review the attached draft?"
ClosingNext step, then a polite sign off"I look forward to your reply. Best regards."

Key idea: open with a greeting and your reason, keep the body to one clear request, and close with a next step before you sign off. A clear structure is what makes an email professional.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"Hey! Send me the file now. ... I wait your answer."
"Dear Mr. Reed, could you please send me the file? ... I look forward to your reply."

In a professional email, open with a greeting, soften requests with Could you please...?, and close with I look forward to your reply. A command sounds rude; a polite request keeps the relationship strong.

Artefact

Thomas's Email

SUBJECT: NDA -- Draft for Review

From: Thomas Reed (Outside Counsel)
To: Simone Quiles -- CC: Contracts Team -- Attachment: NDA_Draft_v2.pdf

Opening

Dear Ms. Quiles, I am writing regarding the NDA for the TechFlow deal.

Body

Please find the draft attached. Could you review the term length in clause two and the governing law in clause five, and reply with your comments? Feel free to forward this to your team.

Closing

I look forward to your reply. Best regards, Thomas Reed.

Reminder: a clear subject line and a polite greeting make the email easy to answer. Always check the attachment is really there before you send.

Dialogue

Talking the Email Through

S

"Hi Thomas, I just sent my reply regarding the NDA. Did the attachment come through?"

T

"Hi Simone, yes, I received it. Thanks for the quick reply, and the subject line was very clear."

S

"Good. In the body, I asked you to review clause four. Could you forward my email to your partner?"

T

"Of course. I'll forward it and add him in CC. Should I follow up by Friday?"

S

"Yes, please follow up on Friday. I'll sign off now, but reply if anything is unclear."

T

"Will do. I'll reply by email and put the whole team in CC. Thank you, Simone."
