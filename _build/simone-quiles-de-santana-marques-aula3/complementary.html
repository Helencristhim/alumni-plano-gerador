Lesson 3 -- Email Essentials

Series
Suits -- Season 1, Episode 2: "Errors and Omissions"

A legal drama where lawyers live in their inboxes. Watch how characters open emails, mark a clear subject, attach documents, and forward them to the right people -- the exact email code from today.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down 3 opening or closing lines you hear or read on screen, then reuse one in an email of your own.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Writing Professional Emails"

A short, practical episode on the language of corporate emails: polite greetings, stating your reason with "regarding", making a clear request in the body, and signing off. Perfect for your A2-B1 level.

Note 3 phrases the speakers use to open and close an email. Compare them with the phrases Simone used with Thomas today.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "How to write a formal email"

A clear lesson on professional email structure: subject line, greeting, body, and sign off, plus the difference between formal and informal register. Directly useful for your emails to international counsel.

After watching, write a 5-line email to a fictional counterpart: open, make one request, mention an attachment, and sign off. Then read it out loud.
