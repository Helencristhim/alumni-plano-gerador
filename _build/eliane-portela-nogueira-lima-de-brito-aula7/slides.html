Chapter 3

The Passive Voice

How programs, brochures, and professors describe what is done — and by whom

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"The program is taught by leading faculty."

"Applicants are evaluated on their leadership record."

"Cases are discussed in small groups."

"The capstone is presented at the end of the program."

FormMeaningExample
am / is / are + past participlePresent passive: the action matters more than the doerThe program is taught.
was / were + past participlePast passiveThe keynote was given by an alumna.
... by + doerAdd "by" only when the doer is importantCases are discussed by the cohort.
Why use it?When the doer is unknown, obvious, or less important than the action. The verb ALWAYS needs a form of "be" plus the past participle: are evaluated, not "evaluate".
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

The case was write by the team.
The case was written by the team.
Applicants evaluate on their record.
Applicants are evaluated on their record.
The program is teach by faculty.
The program is taught by faculty.

The passive always needs a form of "be" plus the PAST PARTICIPLE: is taught, are evaluated, was written — never "is teach" or just "evaluate".

Chapter 4

Inside the Program

An admissions conversation about a top executive program

Dialogue

The Admissions Call

D
Hi Eliane, thanks for your time. I direct admissions here, and I would love to tell you how the program is run.
E
Thank you, David. I am a physician opening a fertility clinic. Could you tell me how the curriculum is taught?
D
Most of it is taught through the case method. Real cases are discussed by the whole cohort, and every voice is expected to be heard.
E
That is helpful. And how are admissions decided? I want to know how applicants are evaluated.
D
Applicants are evaluated on their leadership record, not only on scores. A founder and a physician like you is exactly who we look for.
E
Good to hear. Is the faculty drawn only from the business school, or from medicine too?
D
Both. The program is co-taught, and the opening keynote is often given by a physician-entrepreneur. Our accreditation is international, too.
E
One last question. How is the final capstone evaluated?
D
It is presented to the whole cohort and reviewed by a faculty panel. Yours could be your clinic. I hope your application is submitted soon.
Comprehension

Through the case method: real cases are discussed by the whole cohort.

Role-Play

Open the Case

Scenario: It is your first case discussion. Introduce yourself to the cohort, then be the first to comment on the case. State who you are, what you do, and one clear point about the case.

Use these:

Hi, I am Eliane, a physician and founder. In my experience... The case is built around... May I start?
Role-Play

Hold Your Own

Scenario: A peer interrupts and disagrees with you. Politely take the floor back, acknowledge the point, and defend your view with a clear reason from your medical experience.

Use these:

May I finish my point? I see it differently, and here is why. In my field, this is often decided by... That is a fair point, but...
