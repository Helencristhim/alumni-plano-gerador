Aula 6 -- Sports and Handball

Tema da aula: Sports and Handball -- Live Action vs Routine (Present Continuous vs Present Simple). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
Olympic Channel -- "Handball explained" / highlights of a handball match

Watch a real handball game with English commentary. Listen for the live action in the Present Continuous ("she is running, he is throwing") and the rules in the Present Simple ("a team has seven players").

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote toda frase que descreve a ação acontecendo agora. Quantas estão no Present Continuous?

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "Present Simple vs Present Continuous"

Short, clear videos and quizzes that practice the difference between habits (Present Simple) and actions happening now (Present Continuous). Perfect review of today's grammar.

Dica: pause após cada exemplo e diga uma frase sua sobre handebol: uma com hábito e outra com ação agora.

Series / Film
Ted Lasso -- any training or match scene (Apple TV)

A friendly comedy about a coach and a sports team. Listen to the coach giving instructions during the action and the players talking about their weekly routine -- the same two tenses you used today.

Dica: escolha uma cena de treino, ouça duas vezes e anote três frases: hábitos do time e o que está acontecendo agora.
