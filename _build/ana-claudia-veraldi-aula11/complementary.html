Lesson 11 -- If This, Then That

Grammar Video
The First Conditional -- 6 Minute Grammar (BBC Learning English)

A short, clear lesson on the exact structure from tonight: "If it rains, I will take my umbrella." Listen for the pattern -- present simple after "if", "will" in the result -- the same one you use to talk about cause and effect in testing.

Tip: after the video, turn three real risks at your work into first conditional sentences out loud.

Language Skill
First Conditional -- Grammar & Verb Tenses (Ellii, formerly ESL Library)

A second, slower walk-through of the first conditional with more examples of cause and effect. It reinforces the two classic mistakes to avoid: no "will" after "if", and don't forget the third-person "-s" in the present simple.

Tip: pause after each example and predict the result before the video says it.

The cause-and-effect ideas from tonight in your own field: what happens to a system as the load grows, where the bottleneck appears, and why a threshold matters. It is the English of a real load and performance test.
