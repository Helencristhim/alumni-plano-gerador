Aula 8 -- Flavors and Traditions

Tema da aula: Flavors and Traditions -- describing a meal you had with the Past Simple of irregular verbs (ate, drank, had, made, went, gave). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
Food vlog -- "What I ate today" or a simple recipe video

Watch a short food video where someone describes a meal they had. Listen for the Past Simple of irregular verbs ("I ate", "I drank", "I had", "she made", "we went").

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote toda frase no passado com verbo irregular. Quantas você encontrou?

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "Past Simple: irregular verbs"

Short, clear videos and quizzes that practice common irregular verbs (eat-ate, drink-drank, have-had, make-made, go-went, give-gave). Perfect review of today's grammar.

Dica: faça um mini cartão para cada verbo (base + passado) e revise em voz alta antes de dormir.

Series / Film
Ratatouille (film) -- any kitchen or restaurant scene

A friendly story about food, flavor, and tradition in a restaurant. Listen to the characters talk about the dishes they made and the meals they had -- great input for food vocabulary.

Dica: escolha uma cena na cozinha, ouça duas vezes e anote três frases no Past Simple sobre a comida que prepararam.
