Series
The Good Doctor -- Season 1, Episode 2: "Mount Rushmore"

Doctors and managers talk about their past, their first jobs, and how they got to the hospital. Listen for past simple verbs (joined, started, became) and time markers. Direct connection to Lesson 2: telling a career story in a hospital, just like yours at the Einstein.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down 3 past simple verbs the characters use about their careers.

A short, clear episode where people describe their professional journeys in the past simple. Listen to how they say when they started, how they got promotions, and how their roles changed. Direct connection to Lesson 2: the real phrases used to tell a career story.
