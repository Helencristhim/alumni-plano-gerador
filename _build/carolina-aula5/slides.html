Chapter 3 -- The Magic -ing

Right Now

am / is / are + verb-ing

Discover the Rule

Listen and Notice

"The hero is exploring the old library."

"The candles are flickering."

"A shadow is moving behind the shelves."

"I am writing chapter three right now."

Subjectbeverb + -ing
Iamwriting, reading, imagining
he / she / it (the hero, a shadow)isexploring, moving, hiding
we / you / they (the candles)areflickering, watching, running

Present continuous = happening NOW. Signal words: right now, at the moment, look!, listen!

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"She writing a story."

✓ RIGHT

"She is writing a story."

✗ WRONG

"The villain is run away."

✓ RIGHT

"The villain is running away."
