The Writer Inside

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para ouvir.

Character -- a person in a story
"Luna is my favorite character." (Personagem)
Scene -- one moment of the story, in one place
"This scene happens in an old library." (Cena)
Plot -- what happens in the story, step by step
"The plot is full of surprises." (Enredo)
Chapter -- one part of a book, with a number
"I am writing chapter three now." (Capítulo)
Villain -- the bad character of the story
"The villain is hiding in the shadows." (Vilão)
Hero -- the good character who saves the day
"The hero is exploring the castle." (Herói / heroína)
Mystery -- a story with a secret to discover
"I am reading a mystery about a lost book." (Mistério)
Imagination -- the power to create pictures in your mind
"Writers use their imagination every day." (Imaginação)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Continuous

GRAMMAR

am/is/are + verbo-ing = acontecendo AGORA. / Happening right now.

SujeitobeExemplo / Example
IamI am writing chapter three. (Estou escrevendo)
he / she / itisThe hero is exploring. (Está explorando)
we / you / theyareThe candles are flickering. (Estão tremulando)
Pergunta--What are you doing? (O que você está fazendo?)

Sinais de AGORA: right now, at the moment, look!, listen! — Diferença: "I write stories" (sempre) vs "I am writing" (agora).

Stage 5: Think About It

Reflection

Sem certo ou errado. Pense em inglês, toque no microfone e fale livremente.
