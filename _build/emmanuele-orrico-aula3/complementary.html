Lesson 03 -- What Happened Last Quarter?

Series
Painkiller — Episode 1 (Netflix)

A short series about the American pharmaceutical industry. Do not watch it for the story — watch it for the results meetings: targets, quarter numbers, the field team reporting back. It is the vocabulary of your Lesson 3 (target, results, quarter, growth) at the real speed of people who do this every day.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down 3 past sentences where someone talks about a number that ALREADY HAPPENED (look for -ed, "last quarter", "we hit / we missed"). Do not try to understand 100%.

How to present numbers in English: say the target, say the result, compare it with the quarter before. It is exactly your August presentation, with the ready-made phrases the Global team expects to hear.

Tip: listen ONE time on the way to work, with no pauses. Then listen again and say the result phrases out loud ("We achieved...", "We exceeded...", "We missed..."). Your speaking is the bottleneck, not your listening.
