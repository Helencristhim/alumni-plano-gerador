Aula 16 -- Pre-class

Plans for the Weekend -- Talking About the Future (going to)

Aprenda a falar dos seus planos para o futuro: plan, going to, tomorrow, beach, picnic, invite, maybe, together. Gramática nova: be + going to para planos futuros (I am going to relax. Are you going to go out? I am not going to work). Ritmo lento e claro.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Future with "going to"

GRAMMAR

Como falar de planos futuros. Use o verbo be (am / is / are) + going to + o verbo na forma base (relax, go, visit). Negativa: am/is/are + not going to. Pergunta: o be vem primeiro (Are you going to...?). / Future plan: be + going to + base verb.

Tipo / TypeEstrutura / Structure
Afirmativo / PositiveI am / she is / we are going to + verbo base — I am going to relax.
Negativo / NegativeI am not going to + verbo base — I am not going to work.
Pergunta / QuestionAre you going to + verbo base? — Are you going to go out?
Lembre-seSempre use o be: "I am going to go" (não "I going to go"). Depois de going to, o verbo fica na BASE.
