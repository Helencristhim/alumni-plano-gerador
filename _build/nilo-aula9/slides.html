Formal vs Informal

Common Mistake

Watch Out!

"I writing to confirm the meeting."
"I am writing to confirm the meeting."
"Please find attach the report."
"Please find attached the report."

Use "I am writing" (not "I writing"). It is "attached" (not "attach").

The Email

A Formal Email

To: m.klein@partner-federation.org
Subject: Compliance Meeting — Confirmation
Dear Ms. Klein,
I am writing to confirm our compliance meeting on Friday.
Could you please send the agenda? Please find attached the report.
I look forward to hearing from you.
BEST REGARDS, NILO MESQUITA
