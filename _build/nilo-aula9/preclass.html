Vocabulary Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Estude cada palavra de email, ouça a pronúncia e leia o exemplo.

Recipient•destinatário
The person who receives the email
"Add the recipient before you send it."
Subject•assunto
The line that says what the email is about
"The subject is: Meeting Confirmation."
Attachment•anexo
A file you send with an email
"Please find attached the report."
Greeting•saudação
The opening line, like "Dear Ms. Klein"
"A formal greeting starts with Dear."
Request•pedido / solicitação
When you ask for something politely
"Could you please send it? is a polite request."
Reply•resposta
An answer to an email
"I will send a reply this afternoon."
Forward•encaminhar
To send a received email to another person
"I will forward the message to my team."
Sign-off•fecho / despedida
The closing line, like "Best regards"
"Best regards is a professional sign-off."

Grammar Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Leia o email. Os marcadores formais estão em negrito. Depois, responda as perguntas.

Subject: Compliance Meeting — Confirmation

Dear Ms. Klein,

I am writing to confirm our compliance meeting on Friday. Could you please send the agenda before the meeting? Please find attached the compliance report for your review.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Best regards,
Nilo Mesquita
1. Why is Nilo writing the email?
A To cancel the meeting
B To confirm the meeting
C To ask for a new job
2. What does Nilo ask Ms. Klein to do?
A Send the agenda
B Call him on Friday
C Forward the report
3. What did Nilo send with the email?
A Nothing
B The compliance report (an attachment)
C A photo

Grammar Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip

Marcadores de registro formal e como soar profissional.

Formal Register — Email Markers
FUNÇÃO FRASE FORMAL / EXEMPLO
Abrir
(dizer o porquê)		I am writing to confirm... / I am writing regarding...
Pedir educadoCould you please send the agenda?
AnexarPlease find attached the report
FecharI look forward to hearing from you. / Best regards,

✗ "Hi, send me the report." / "I writing to..." / "find attach" (informal/errado)

✓ "Dear Ms. Klein, could you please send the report?" / "I am writing to..." / "find attached" (correto)

Quiz Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situação de email.

You open a formal email to a new partner. How do you greet them?
A "Hi Klein, what's up?"
B "Dear Ms. Klein,"
C "Hey, listen,"
You need the agenda. How do you ask politely?
A "Could you please send the agenda?"
B "Send me the agenda now."
C "I want the agenda."
You are sending a file with the email. What do you write?
A "Please find attach the report."
B "Please find attached the report."
C "The report is here inside."
How do you close a formal email?
A "Bye, see ya."
B "I look forward to hearing from you. Best regards,"
C "Thanks a lot, cheers."

Reflection Stage 5: Free Production

Grave sua resposta livremente. Não há certo ou errado — o objetivo é praticar.

Record a short formal email out loud in English to a partner or federation. Start with a greeting (Dear...), say why you are writing, make one polite request (Could you please...), mention an attachment (Please find attached...), and close with a sign-off (I look forward to... Best regards).
