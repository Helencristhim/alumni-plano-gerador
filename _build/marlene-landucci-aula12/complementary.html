Aula 12 -- Getting Around the City

Tema da aula: pedir e dar direções na cidade (Go straight, Turn left, Cross at the crosswalk) e usar preposições de lugar (next to, across from, on the corner). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Film
"Emily in Paris" -- the street scenes

A light series set in a beautiful city. Watch the street and square scenes and listen for direction words: go straight, turn, corner, square, and fountain.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote toda palavra de direção que você ouvir (turn left, go straight, corner).

Podcast
"Travel English" -- Asking for Directions (beginner episode)

A slow, clear podcast for beginners. Listen to a traveler ask for directions on the street and practice "How do I get to...?" and commands like "Go straight" and "Turn left".

Dica: ouça duas vezes. Na segunda, repita em voz alta "How do I get to the square?" e "Turn left at the corner".

YouTube
"Asking for Directions in English -- Conversation" (English with subtitles)

A short, simple video of a real street conversation. A tourist says "Excuse me, how do I get to...?", and a local answers with go straight, turn left, the crosswalk, and the corner.

Dica: pause depois de cada direção e repita. Foque nas frases "Go straight for two blocks" e "It is across from the fountain".
