Aula 20 -- Year-End Review

Tema da aula: year-end review -- look back on your year and look ahead. Review your milestones and achievements, report what people said with reported speech (she told me that the audit was a success), and share your plans for next year. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"How to do a year-end self-review in English" -- reflect on achievements

A short, clear video on reviewing your year at work: how to talk about your milestones, your biggest achievements and your growth, and how to set goals for next year.

Dica: anote três frases que a pessoa usa para falar de conquistas (achievement, growth, proud) e duas frases de planos para o futuro.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "Reported speech"

Short lessons and quizzes on reported speech: said (that) and told + person (that), with the verb moving one step back (is → was, will → would). Perfect for reporting what people told you.

Dica: escreva cinco frases reportando o que um colega ou chefe te disse esta semana, usando "told me that" e "said that".

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about goals, habits or self-improvement

A short, friendly podcast for B1 learners. Listen for the language a speaker uses to talk about progress, growth and plans, and to report what other people said.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote duas frases sobre crescimento (growth, improvement) e uma frase de reported speech.
