Lesson 19 -- Cross-Cultural Scientific Communication

Series
The Diplomat -- any cross-cultural negotiation scene

Watch how characters from different countries read each other's directness, soften their messages and avoid misunderstandings under pressure. Notice the polite, tentative language.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down one phrase used to soften a disagreement and one used to confirm understanding.

Podcast
HBR IdeaCast -- "Working Across Cultures" (Erin Meyer)

An episode on cultural differences in directness, etiquette and communication style at work, and how to adapt your message to a global team.

Record yourself describing one cultural norm you would adapt to when working with a colleague from another country.

YouTube
Erin Meyer -- "The Culture Map" (talk on cross-cultural communication)

A clear talk on how directness, feedback and etiquette differ across cultures, with practical advice on adapting your message and avoiding misunderstandings.

After watching, note two phrases you could use to confirm understanding and soften a disagreement on a global call.
