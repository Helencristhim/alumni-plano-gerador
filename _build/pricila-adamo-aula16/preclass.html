Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Polite Indirect Questions

Grammar

Para perguntar com educacao, comece com "Could you tell me..." ou "Do you know...". Depois disso, a ordem volta ao normal (sujeito + verbo): "what the rate IS" (NAO "what is the rate"). Para perguntas de sim/nao, acrescente "if" ou "whether" (Do you know IF there is a fee?). / To ask politely, start with "Could you tell me..." or "Do you know...". After that, use normal word order (subject + verb), and add "if / whether" for yes/no questions.

Funcao / FunctionEnglishPortugues
Ask for information (Wh-)Could you tell me what the rate is?Voce pode me dizer qual e a taxa?
Ask a yes/no questionDo you know if there is a fee?Voce sabe se ha uma taxa?
Ask where something isCould you tell me where the ATM is?Voce pode me dizer onde fica o caixa?
Ask whether you can do XI'd like to know whether I can pay contactless.Eu gostaria de saber se posso pagar por aproximacao.
Make a polite requestCould I have a receipt, please?Eu poderia ter um recibo, por favor?

Stage 5: Free Production

Reflection

Record yourself answering the question. There is no right or wrong answer.
