Aula 13 — Expressing Opinions Professionally

Conteúdos para reforçar como dar opiniões com confiança e tato — frases de opinião, hedging e softeners. Marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
The Office (US) — any conference room meeting scene

The team constantly shares opinions and disagrees in meetings. Listen for phrases like "in my view", "I see your point, but", and softeners like "maybe" and "a little" — the exact language from Lesson 13.

Dica: anote três frases usadas para discordar com respeito.

Podcast
HBR IdeaCast — episodes where guests debate a business decision

Experts give opinions, hedge when uncertain, and disagree politely with the host. Hear how professionals say "from my perspective", "it might", and "that is a valid point" in real discussions.

Dica: pause e repita cada softener (might, perhaps, a little) que você ouvir.

YouTube
