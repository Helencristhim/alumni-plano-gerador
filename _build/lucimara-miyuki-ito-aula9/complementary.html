USED TO / USE TO / BE USED TO — English with Lucy

Tonight's grammar, in one clear lesson. It nails the two traps Brazilians fall into: keeping the -d in "used to" for a past habit, and dropping it after did — "Did you use to...?", never "did you used to". It also shows how "used to" is different from "be used to" (being accustomed to something), so you don't mix them up.

Tip: after each example, pause and say your own version out loud about your real shopping life — "I used to..., but now I...". Say five before the video ends.
