Aula 15 -- The Best and the Worst (Superlatives)

Tema da aula: The Best and the Worst -- choosing the top one with superlative adjectives. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
Superlative Adjectives in English -- the biggest, the best, the most interesting

Watch a short, clear video about superlatives. Listen for short adjectives with the ...-est (the hardest, the biggest, the easiest) and long adjectives with the most (the most interesting, the most helpful), and notice the word the before every superlative.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote cinco frases que escolhem o TOP de um grupo com the ...-est ou the most.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- superlative adjectives

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about superlatives: how to add -est to short adjectives, when to use the most with long adjectives, the irregular forms the best and the worst, and why we always use the.

Dica: escreva seis frases escolhendo o melhor e o pior na sua escola. Sublinhe os adjetivos com -est e os com the most.

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about school, friends or success

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen to people talk about the best things at school, favorite people and what makes a great student -- great input to review today's words and superlatives.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote todo superlativo (the best, the most useful, the smartest) e toda palavra nova (favorite, helpful, smart, popular).
