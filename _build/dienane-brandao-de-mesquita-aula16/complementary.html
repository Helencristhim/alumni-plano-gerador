Lesson 16 -- Future Plans

Series
Extra English -- Episode 3 (planning scene)

A simple comedy made for English learners. The friends talk about their future plans in slow, clear English -- I am going to..., What are you going to do? Perfect for A1 listening about the future with going to.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down every sentence with "going to" that you hear. Then say out loud: "Tomorrow I am going to..." and "Next week I am going to...".

Podcast
Coffee Break English -- "Talking About the Future"

A short, friendly lesson on the future: how to use "going to" to talk about plans, like "I am going to travel" and "She is going to visit her family". The hosts explain the structure slowly, with many examples you can repeat.

Pause after each example and repeat it out loud. Then say three plans for next week, using "I am going to" each time.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Going to for Future Plans"

A clear video that teaches future vocabulary (tomorrow, next week, soon, vacation, visit, travel) and how to talk about plans, like "I am going to travel" and "What are you going to do?", with pictures and simple sentences.

After watching, record a short voice message: say three things you are going to do next week, using going to and the new words.
