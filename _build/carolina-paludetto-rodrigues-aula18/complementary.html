Aula 18 -- Talking About Health

Tema da aula: Talking About Health -- saying how you feel and describing symptoms. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
At the Doctor's -- talking about your health in English

Watch a short, clear video about going to the doctor and describing how you feel. Listen for I feel..., I have a... (a headache, a fever), and questions like How do you feel? and What's wrong?

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote cinco frases sobre saúde: como você se sente (I feel...) e sintomas (I have a...).

Practice
BBC Learning English -- health and the body

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about health vocabulary and how to describe symptoms: I feel tired, I am sick, I have a headache, and how to ask How do you feel?

Dica: escreva seis frases sobre saúde. Sublinhe I feel + adjetivo e I have a + substantivo, e circule cada palavra nova (health, healthy, sick, headache, fever, medicine, energy, rest).

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about health, sleep or staying well

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen to people talk about health, feeling sick and how to feel better -- great input to review today's words and the patterns I feel... and I have a...

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote toda frase sobre saúde (I feel..., I have a...) e toda palavra nova (healthy, sick, fever, medicine, energy).
