Aula 18 -- Hypothetical Situations & Giving Advice

Series
Ted Lasso (Apple TV+) -- Any episode

A show full of warm, gentle advice and mentoring. Direct connection to Lesson 18: notice how Ted gives advice without ordering people around, often imagining things from their side ("if I were you...") and letting them choose.

Tip: pause when a character gives advice and rephrase it yourself with the second conditional.

Podcast
Dear Sugars / any advice-column podcast -- Any episode

Hosts listen to a real dilemma and offer thoughtful advice. Direct connection to Lesson 18: a model of imagining yourself in someone's situation and giving gentle, honest suggestions instead of orders.

Tip: pause before the hosts answer and say what you would do with "If I were them, I would...".

YouTube
"The Second Conditional (If I were you...)" -- BBC Learning English / English with Lucy

A clear lesson on the second conditional (if + past simple, would + verb) for hypothetical situations and advice, including "were" for all subjects. Direct connection to Lesson 18: exactly the grammar we practiced for imagining and advising.

Tip: pause after each example and make your own "If I were you, I would..." sentence out loud.
