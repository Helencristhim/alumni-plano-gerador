Stage 12.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para escutar.

Reservation -- reserva (a room booked in advance)
"I have a reservation under the name Mansour."
Check-in -- registro de entrada (when you arrive and get your room)
"Check-in is any time after three p.m."
Receptionist -- recepcionista (the person at the front desk)
"The receptionist gave me my key card."
Lobby -- saguão, recepção (the entrance area of a hotel)
"Breakfast is served in the lobby."
Key card -- cartão-chave (the card that opens your room)
"Here is your key card for room eight-oh-two."
Amenities -- comodidades (the services a hotel offers, like a gym or wifi)
"The hotel amenities include a gym and free wifi."
Request -- pedido, pedir (to politely ask for something)
"I would like to make a request for a quiet room."
Complaint -- reclamação (when you report a problem politely)
"I have a small complaint about the air conditioning."
Upgrade -- melhoria de categoria (a better room than the one booked)
"Could I get an upgrade to a room with a view?"
Vacancy -- vaga, quarto livre (a free room available)
"Do you have any vacancy for tonight?"

Stage 12.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas.

Marcos arrives at the hotel and goes to the receptionist. "I have a reservation under the name Mansour," he says. To make his requests polite, he uses can and could: "Could I have a quiet room on a high floor, please? Would it be possible to get a late check-in next time?" Later he has a small complaint: "Could you send someone to look at the air conditioning?" And a question: "Can you tell me the wifi password?" Polite requests with could and would it be possible sound softer and more professional than direct orders.

1. Por que Marcos usa "Could you...?" em vez de uma ordem direta?
A Porque é mais rápido.
B Porque soa mais educado e profissional.
C Porque é o único jeito de pedir.
2. Como Marcos reporta um problema de forma educada?
A "Fix the air conditioning now."
B "Could you send someone to look at the air conditioning?"
C "The room is bad."
3. Which request is the most polite?
A "Give me a quiet room."
B "Could I have a quiet room, please?"
C "I want quiet room."

Stage 12.4: Grammar Tip -- Polite Requests with Can & Could

GRAMMAR

Como pedir de forma educada (explicação em inglês e português).

PhrasePoliteness / EducaçãoExample
Can I / Can you...?Educado (informal)Can you send extra towels?
Could I / Could you...?Mais educadoCould I have a quiet room, please?
Would it be possible to...?Muito educado (formal)Would it be possible to get a late check-out?
DicaDepois de Can/Could use o verbo base (send, have, tell). Adicione please para soar ainda mais educado.
