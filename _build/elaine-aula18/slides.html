Chapter 3

How to Ask

Polite requests to strangers

Discover the Rule

Listen and Notice

"Excuse me, could you take a photo of us?"

"Could you take one more, please?"

"Would you mind taking a photo?"

"Can you get the view in the background?"

StructureUseExample
Excuse me, ...Get a stranger's attentionExcuse me, could you help?
Could you + verb?A polite requestCould you take a photo?
Would you mind + verb-ing?A very polite requestWould you mind taking a photo?

Start with "Excuse me". Use "Could you...?" to ask politely. After "mind" use verb + ing.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"Take photo me."

✓ RIGHT

"Could you take a photo of me?"

✗ WRONG

"Would you mind to take a photo?"

✓ RIGHT

"Would you mind taking a photo?"

Use "Could you + verb" to ask politely. After "mind" use verb + ing.

How to Get a Great Photo

1.Say "Excuse me" to the stranger.
2.Ask: "Could you take a photo?"
3.Put the view in the background.
4.Stand a little closer and smile.
5.Say "Thank you for your help!"

Always be polite with strangers
