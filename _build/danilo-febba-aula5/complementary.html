Extra materials to reinforce your learning outside class. Mark as done after watching or listening.

Lesson 5 -- Last Week at Work

Series
Silicon Valley -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Minimum Viable Product"

A tech-team comedy where engineers recap what they built and the problems they fixed. Direct connection to Lesson 5: people telling what they did at work, with lots of past simple. Note how they say what they finished, tested, and solved.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down 5 regular past verbs (-ed) you hear.

Podcast
BBC Learning English -- "English at Work"

Short, clear workplace episodes at a friendly speed, perfect for A2. The characters often report what they did and what happened. Direct connection to Lesson 5: it trains your ear with the past simple in real work situations.

Tip: listen twice. Write 5 sentences about your own week using verb + -ed.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "The Past Simple: Regular Verbs (-ed)"

A short, practical lesson on how to form the past simple with -ed and how to pronounce the three endings /t/, /d/, /id/. Connection to Lesson 5: exactly the grammar from class, at your level, ready to copy and repeat.

Tip: pause and repeat each verb aloud. Practice fixed /t/, planned /d/, tested /id/.
