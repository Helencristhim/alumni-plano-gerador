A legal drama full of networking and small talk. Watch how the characters break the ice, ask open questions, find common ground, and keep in touch -- exactly the conversation moves from today's lesson.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down 3 questions characters use to start or build a conversation. Then record yourself reusing one with your own context.
