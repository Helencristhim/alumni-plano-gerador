Aula 14 — Talking About the Future

Conteúdos para reforçar como falar de planos, previsões e compromissos — going to, will e present continuous. Marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
Silicon Valley — any product roadmap or launch-planning scene

The team constantly plans launches, predicts results and sets deadlines. Listen for "we are going to...", "it will...", and present continuous for scheduled meetings — the exact forms from Lesson 14.

Dica: anote uma frase com going to, uma com will e uma com present continuous.

Podcast
HBR IdeaCast — episodes about forecasting and project planning

Leaders talk about roadmaps, forecasts and what they intend to do. Hear how professionals announce plans ("we are going to"), make predictions ("it will") and describe deliverables and contingencies.

Dica: pause e repita cada previsão com "will" que você ouvir.
