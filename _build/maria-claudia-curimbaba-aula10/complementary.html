Aula 10 -- Review & Integration

Tema da aula: consolidar o Block 1 e dominar a língua que conecta ideias -- linking words (firstly, moreover, however, in conclusion) e frases de resumo (to recap, on the whole, the bottom line), além de revisar a voz passiva. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
"Succession" -- boardroom recaps & summaries

Executives constantly recap deals, summarize quarters, and deliver the bottom line under pressure. Listen for how they connect points with linking words and report results in the passive ("the deal was signed", "the offer was rejected").

Dica: assista uma cena de reunião com legenda em inglês e anote três frases de resumo (to sum up, on the whole, the bottom line) e dois exemplos de voz passiva.

Podcast
HBR IdeaCast -- executives summarizing strategy

Leaders are interviewed about quarters, deals, and decisions, and they constantly recap and conclude. Perfect input to review this lesson's phrases -- to recap, moreover, however, in conclusion, all things considered -- and to hear how a clear summary is built.

Dica: ouça um episódio e anote cada vez que alguém abre, adiciona, contrasta ou conclui uma ideia (firstly / moreover / however / in conclusion).

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- Linking Words & the Passive

Short, clear guides to connecting ideas (firstly, moreover, however, in conclusion) and to forming the passive voice (be + past participle). It covers the classic mistakes "was wrote" and "was sended" -- exactly the review focus of this lesson.

Dica: pause após cada exemplo e reformule uma frase ativa como passiva: "the team wrote the report" → "the report was written by the team".
