Aula 20 -- The Communication Audit

Reinforce the whole Writing & Communication block as one audit: the right register, a clear structure, a persuasive case and the right channel. Watch, read and listen this week.

YouTube
"How to write a professional email" -- Harvard Business Review talks

Short, practical clips on matching register and tone to the reader, leading with the bottom line, and keeping email concise.

Dica: pause em cada exemplo e identifique a saudação, o ponto principal e o sign-off.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- Formal vs informal register

Grammar refresher on softening requests (I was wondering if you could...) and choosing the right level of formality.

Dica: pegue uma mensagem casual sua e reescreva-a em registro formal, com saudação e sign-off.

Podcast
Worklife with Adam Grant -- Communicating clearly at work

An episode on writing that persuades: leading with the bottom line, being concise, and adjusting the message to each channel.

Dica: ouça como bons comunicadores deixam o ponto principal claro logo na primeira frase.
