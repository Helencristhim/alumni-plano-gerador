Chapter 3

The Travel-Question Code

Where...? · How long...? · Do you have...?

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"Where are you staying?"

"How long are you staying?"

"Do you have anything to declare?" — "No, I do not."

"Is this your first time?" — "Yes, it is."

QuestionShort answerExample
Where / How long + are + you...?give the informationWhere are you staying?
Do + you + have...?Yes, I do / No, I do notDo you have your passport?
Is this...?Yes, it is / No, it is notIs this your first time?
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"Where you are staying?"

✓ RIGHT

"Where are you staying?"

✗ WRONG

"How long you staying?"

✓ RIGHT

"How long are you staying?"

✗ WRONG

"Do you have a visa?" — "Yes, I have."

✓ RIGHT

"Do you have a visa?" — "Yes, I do."

For a question with be, put are before you. For a Do you...? question, answer Yes, I do / No, I do not.
