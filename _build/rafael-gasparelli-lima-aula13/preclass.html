What Happened? -- Describing Past Events

Aprenda a contar o que sua empresa fez no passado. Oito verbos de ação (lançar, assinar, contratar, entregar...) e o Past Simple dos verbos regulares: a terminação -ed, a negativa com did not e a pergunta com Did.

Stage 1.1: Key Words

Vocabulary

Eight action verbs to tell a business story. Listen to each one and notice the past form with -ed.

Launch -- lançar (produto/serviço)
"We launched a new service in January."
Sign -- assinar (um contrato)
"The board signed the contract in February."
Hire -- contratar (alguém)
"We hired four new lawyers."
Deliver -- entregar (mercadoria/relatório)
"We delivered all the reports on time."
Finish -- terminar / concluir
"We finished every project on time."
Improve -- melhorar
"The numbers improved month after month."
Open -- abrir (escritório/loja)
"We opened a small office in Rio."
Achieve -- alcançar (uma meta)
"We achieved our main goal for the quarter."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Read the story and answer the questions.

Last quarter was a strong one for Rafael's company. In January, they launched a new service for corporate clients. In February, the board signed an important contract, and the team hired four new lawyers. They also opened a small office in Rio. The numbers improved every month. By March, they finished every project and delivered all the reports on time. They did not miss a single deadline, and in the end they achieved their main goal.

1. What did the company launch in January?
A A new service for corporate clients.
B A small office in Rio.
C Four new lawyers.
2. What did the board do in February?
A It finished every project.
B It signed an important contract.
C It delivered the reports.
3. Did they miss any deadline?
A Yes, they missed two deadlines.
B No, they did not miss a single deadline.
C The story does not say.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Past Simple (Regular Verbs)

Grammar

Para falar do passado com verbos regulares, acrescente -ed ao verbo: launch → launched, sign → signed. Na negativa, use did not (didn't) + verbo na forma base (sem -ed): "We did not finish." Na pergunta, use Did + sujeito + verbo base: "Did you deliver the report?". Atenção: depois de did/did not, o verbo SEMPRE volta à forma base. / For the past with regular verbs, add -ed. For negatives, use did not + base verb. For questions, use Did + subject + base verb. After did or did not, the verb always returns to its base form.

Form / FormaEnglishPortuguês
Affirmative (verb + -ed)We launched a new service.Nós lançamos um novo serviço.
Affirmative (verb + -ed)The board signed the contract.A diretoria assinou o contrato.
Negative (did not + base)We did not finish the audit.Nós não terminamos a auditoria.
Question (Did + base)Did you deliver the report?Você entregou o relatório?
Short answerYes, we did. / No, we did not.Sim, entregamos. / Não, não entregamos.

Stage 5: Free Production

Reflection

Record yourself answering the question. There is no right or wrong answer.

Think about your company or your team last quarter. Out loud, in English, describe what happened: what you launched, what you signed, who you hired, what you finished and delivered, and what goal you achieved. Use the past with -ed, and remember the base verb after did and did not.
