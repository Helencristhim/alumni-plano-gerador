Aula 15 — Review -- My World in English

Series
The Great British Bake Off — people summarizing their week

A gentle, friendly show where contestants look back on their challenges and summarize how things went. Great to hear "overall", "on the whole", "the biggest challenge" used naturally.

Dica: anote duas frases em que alguém resume como foi a semana. / Note two sentences where someone summarizes how the week went.

Podcast
All Ears English — "How to summarize and review in English"

Clear, friendly English on how to give the big picture and review your progress. A great way to hear "overall", "in general" and "on the whole" used in real conversation.

Dica: ouça uma vez só e anote três expressões de resumo. / Listen once and note three summarizing expressions.
