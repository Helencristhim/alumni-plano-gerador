Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- The Four Tools (Lessons 6-9)

GRAMMAR

Revisão das quatro estruturas das aulas 6 a 9. / A review of the four structures from Lessons 6 to 9.

Tool / FerramentaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
Scheduling (L6)Marcar uma reunião / Set a meetingCan we schedule a meeting? I am available on Tuesday.
Future (L7)Falar de planos / Talk about plansI am going to visit you. I will bring the aircraft.
Describing (L8)Descrever coisas / Describe thingsIt is modern. It has a powerful engine.
Directions (L9)Dar direções / Give directionsTake me to the office. Go straight. Turn left.

Key point / Ponto-chave: use going to para planos decididos e will para decisões e promessas; is/has para descrever; e o verbo no início (sem "you") para dar direções: Go, Turn, Stop.
