Lesson 10 -- Review + Confidence Check (Lessons 6-9)

Series
The Office (US) -- any business meeting scene

Short workplace scenes where people schedule meetings, talk about plans for the future, and give simple directions. A light way to hear the language from Lessons 6 to 9 in one place.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down one phrase to schedule a meeting and one phrase to give a direction.

Podcast
Speak English Now -- "Making Plans and Appointments"

A slow, clear podcast that reviews how to schedule a meeting, say when you are available, and talk about your plans with going to and will. Perfect to revise Lessons 6 and 7.

Repeat each phrase out loud. Then record a short message to schedule a meeting and say one plan for next week.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Talking about the future and giving directions"

A short, practical review of today's language: making appointments, future forms (going to and will), describing things with adjectives, and giving directions to a taxi driver.

After watching, record a one-minute message: schedule a meeting, say one future plan, describe your aircraft, and give a taxi driver two directions.
