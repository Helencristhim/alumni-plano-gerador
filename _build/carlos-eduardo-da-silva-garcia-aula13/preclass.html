Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Sao palavras de lugar de uma propriedade de cafe. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Property -- propriedade (um terreno, geralmente uma fazenda de cafe)
"There is a large property near Varginha." (Ha uma propriedade grande perto de Varginha.)
Boundary -- limite / divisa (a linha onde a propriedade termina)
"There are tall trees on the boundary of the farm." (Ha arvores altas na divisa da fazenda.)
Storage area -- area de armazenamento (lugar para guardar os sacos de cafe)
"There are two storage areas near the house." (Ha duas areas de armazenamento perto da casa.)
Processing unit -- unidade de processamento (onde o cafe-cereja vira grao verde)
"There is a small processing unit on the property." (Ha uma pequena unidade de processamento na propriedade.)
Municipality -- municipio (uma cidade e a area ao redor)
"The farm is near the municipality of Varginha." (A fazenda fica perto do municipio de Varginha.)
Rural -- rural (no campo, nao na cidade)
"The farm is in a rural area." (A fazenda fica em uma area rural.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto sobre uma propriedade de cafe e responda as perguntas. (Read the text about a coffee property and answer.)

This coffee farm is in a rural area, near the municipality of Varginha. It is a large property, registered and active in the Impact program. On the property, there is a small processing unit, where the coffee cherries become green beans. There are two storage areas near the main house, and between them there is a water tank. There is no certified warehouse yet, but the producer is building one near the boundary.

1. Which sentence is correct for ONE thing? (Qual frase e para UMA coisa?)
A There are a processing unit. (plural com uma coisa)
B There is a processing unit. (there is + uma coisa)
C The property have a processing unit. (have)
2. How many storage areas are there? (Quantas areas de armazenamento ha?)
A There is one storage area. (uma)
B There are two storage areas. (duas)
C There are three storage areas. (tres)
3. Is there a certified warehouse? (Ha um armazem certificado?)
A Yes, there is a certified warehouse. (Sim.)
B No, there is no certified warehouse yet. (Nao, ainda nao.)
C There are two certified warehouses. (Dois.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- There Is / There Are

Grammar

Como dizer o que existe num lugar: there is (uma coisa) / there are (duas ou mais), negativa, pergunta e preposicoes. / How to say what exists in a place.

FormUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
One thingThere is + a / oneThere is a processing unit. (Ha uma unidade.)
Many thingsThere are + number / pluralThere are two storage areas. (Ha duas areas.)
NegativeThere is no / There are noThere is no certified warehouse. (Nao ha armazem.)
QuestionIs there...? / Are there...?Are there storage areas? (Ha areas de armazenamento?)
Where / OndePreposicoes: in a rural area, on the property, near the house, between the areas. Localizacao: The farm is located in the south of Minas Gerais.

Etapa 4: Quiz Situacional

Quiz

C "There have two storage areas."
