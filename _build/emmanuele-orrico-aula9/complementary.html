Lesson 09 -- Networking in English

Series
Suits — Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot" (Netflix)

Harvey Specter walks into any room and opens a conversation with anyone — calm, confident, and always with a question. Watch how he breaks the ice and keeps a conversation alive. It is the social confidence you are building for the Global dinner, in a different world.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Treasure hunt: write down 3 questions a character uses to open or keep a conversation going. Do not try to understand 100% — look ONLY for the conversation openers.

Business English Pod — BEP 155: English for Networking (Part 1)

Two people meet for the first time at a pharmacy conference — exactly your world. The lesson shows how to approach someone, introduce yourself, give a short "elevator pitch" about your job, and talk about your industry. This is the flow of your lesson in the mouth of real professionals.

Tip: listen ONCE on your way to work, with no pauses. Then listen again and repeat out loud every opener you hear. You already listen to podcasts every day — give one of them to your English.
