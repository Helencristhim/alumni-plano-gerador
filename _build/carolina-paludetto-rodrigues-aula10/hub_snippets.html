Every Sunday their neighbor Marina visits, because she is like a relative. Carolina loves her family.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Possessives and have / has

GRAMMAR

Usamos have/has para posse e os possessivos para dizer de quem é algo. / We use have/has for possession and possessives to say whose something is.

FormUseExample
haveI / you / we / theyI have two cousins.
hashe / she / itShe has one brother.
my, your, his, her, our, theirbefore a nounThis is my uncle.
name + 'sto say whoseCarolina's family is big.

Key point / Ponto-chave: use have com I, you, we, they e has com he, she, it. Para dizer de quem é algo, use um possessivo (my, her, their) antes do substantivo, ou acrescente 's ao nome: my brother's dog, Carolina's notebook. Always check the subject before have or has.
