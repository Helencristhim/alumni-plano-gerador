Lesson 3 -- Small Businesses & Local Products

Grammar Video
"There Is and There Are" -- ABA English

A short, clear lesson on there is and there are. Connection to Lesson 3: this is exactly the structure you use to describe your stand -- "there is honey" and "there are two hundred jars".

Tip: after each example, say one true sentence about your own products with there is or there are.

Grammar Video
"Countable and Uncountable Nouns -- Many & Much" -- Arnel's Everyday English

A friendly lesson on countable and uncountable nouns, and when to use how many or how much. Connection to Lesson 3: jars and customers are countable (how many), but honey, coffee, and stock are uncountable (how much).

Tip: make two lists of your products -- one you can count (jars, boxes) and one you cannot (honey, coffee) -- and say a sentence for each.

A clear, animated talk about the journey of coffee from a small farm to your cup -- the suppliers, the products, and the people behind them. Connection to Lesson 3: it is the same world you work in every day, with the vocabulary of products, suppliers, and quality.
