Aula 16 -- Replying to Emails

Tema da aula: replying to work emails -- answering questions, saying yes or no politely (Certainly / Unfortunately, I cannot...), short answers (Yes, I have / No, not yet) and short follow-ups. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"How to reply to a professional email" -- business English video

A short, clear video about replying to emails: how to say yes and no politely, answer a query and write a short follow-up. Listen for "Certainly...", "Unfortunately, I cannot..." and "Please let me know if you need any clarification".

Dica: anote duas frases para dizer sim e duas para dizer não com educação.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "Short answers"

Short lessons and quizzes on short answers that repeat the auxiliary: "Have you...?" -- "Yes, I have." / "Can you...?" -- "Yes, I can." / "Did you...?" -- "No, I did not." Perfect for quick, polite email replies.

Dica: escreva cinco perguntas (Have/Can/Did you...?) e responda cada uma com a resposta curta certa.

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about communication or being polite

A short, friendly podcast for B1 learners. Listen for how people answer questions, say no politely and apologize without sounding rude.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote uma forma educada de dizer "não".
