Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

These are the key words for this lesson. Tap Listen to hear the pronunciation.

Acknowledge -- to tell someone you got their message
"I am writing to acknowledge your email." (Confirmar o recebimento / acusar recebimento)
Apologize -- to say you are sorry
"I apologize for the delay in my reply." (Pedir desculpas / desculpar-se)
Clarification -- extra information that makes something clear
"Please let me know if you need any clarification." (Esclarecimento)
Certainly -- a polite way to say yes
"Certainly, I can send it again." (Com certeza / claro)
Unfortunately -- a polite way to introduce bad news or a no
"Unfortunately, I cannot meet on Monday." (Infelizmente)
Query -- a question, often in writing
"To answer your query, the file is a PDF." (Dúvida / pergunta)
Promptly -- quickly, without delay
"Thank you for answering so promptly." (Prontamente / rapidamente)
Sender -- the person who wrote and sent the email
"The sender is Mr. Costa from the supplier." (Remetente)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Polite Replies & Short Answers

GRAMMAR

Para responder bem a um e-mail, use respostas curtas e palavras educadas. Para SIM: "Yes, of course" / "Certainly". Para NÃO: "Unfortunately, I cannot..." / "I am afraid not". Para respostas curtas, repita o auxiliar: "Have you sent it?" -> "Yes, I have" / "No, not yet". / To reply well, use short answers and polite words. For yes: "Yes, of course" / "Certainly". For no: "Unfortunately..." / "I am afraid not". For short answers, repeat the auxiliary verb.

SituationPolite replyExample
Say yes / Dizer simYes, of course / CertainlyCertainly, I can send it again.
Say no / Dizer nãoUnfortunately, I cannot... / I am afraid notUnfortunately, I cannot meet on Monday.
Short answer (Have you...?)Yes, I have / No, not yet"Have you sent it?" -- "Yes, I have."
Short answer (Can you...?)Yes, I can / I am afraid I cannot"Can you help?" -- "Yes, I can."

Key point / Ponto-chave: a resposta curta repete o auxiliar da pergunta. "Have you...?" -> "Yes, I have." "Can you...?" -> "Yes, I can." "Did you...?" -> "Yes, I did." Nunca repita o verbo principal: NÃO diga "Yes, I have sent" como resposta curta -- diga apenas "Yes, I have." / The short answer repeats the auxiliary, not the main verb.

Aula 16 -- Replying to Emails

Tema da aula: replying to work emails -- answering questions, saying yes or no politely (Certainly / Unfortunately, I cannot...), short answers (Yes, I have / No, not yet) and short follow-ups. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.
