Aula 14 -- School Subjects & Comparatives

Tema da aula: School Subjects & Comparatives -- comparing school subjects with comparative adjectives. Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
Comparative Adjectives in English -- bigger, easier, more interesting

Watch a short, clear video about comparatives. Listen for short adjectives with -er (harder, bigger, easier) and long adjectives with more (more interesting, more difficult), and notice the word than between the two things.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote cinco frases que comparam duas coisas com -er ou more.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- comparative adjectives

Short, clear lessons and quizzes about comparatives: how to add -er to short adjectives, when to use more with long adjectives, the irregular forms better and worse, and how to connect with than.

Dica: escreva seis frases comparando suas matérias da escola. Sublinhe os adjetivos com -er e os com more.

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about school or studying

A short, friendly podcast for A2-B1 learners. Listen to people talk about school, subjects and learning -- great input to review today's school words and comparatives.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote toda comparação (harder than, easier than, more interesting than) e toda palavra sobre a escola (math, science, exam, project).
