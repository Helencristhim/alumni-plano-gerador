Stage 1.1: Phone Vocabulary

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. These words help you handle calls and messages.

To pick up -- atender (o telefone)
"The phone is ringing -- could you pick it up?"
To hang up -- desligar
"Don't hang up yet -- I have one more question."
To call back -- retornar a ligacao
"She's busy now, but she will call you back later."
Voicemail -- caixa postal / mensagem de voz
"No one answered, so I left a voicemail."
To dial -- discar
"Dial zero first to call an outside line."
The line is busy -- a linha esta ocupada
"I tried twice, but the line is busy."
An extension -- um ramal
"For the front desk, dial extension two-oh-one."
To take a message -- anotar um recado
"He's out right now. Can I take a message?"
A missed call -- uma chamada perdida
"I had three missed calls from the hotel."
To text -- mandar mensagem de texto
"If I can't reach you, I'll text you the address."
To get through -- conseguir falar / completar a ligacao
"I finally got through to the reception after ten minutes."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Read the phone call and answer the questions.

The phone rings and Pricila picks it up. "Hello?" A voice says, "Hi, I'm calling to speak to Paulo." Pricila answers, "I'm afraid he's out. Can I take a message?" The caller says, "Yes -- could you tell him that the dinner is now at eight?" Pricila writes it down. "Of course. And should he call you back?" The caller replies, "Please ask him to text me if the time doesn't work." Pricila reads back the number and says, "I'll give him the message."

1. Why is the caller phoning?
A He's calling to speak to Paulo.
B He's calling to speak to Pricila.
C He's calling the hotel.
2. What does Pricila offer to do?
A Hang up the phone.
B Take a message for Paulo.
C Call the caller back herself.
3. What does the caller ask Paulo to do if the time doesn't work?
A Cancel the dinner.
B Come at seven.
C Text him.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Taking & Relaying Messages

Grammar

Para passar uma INFORMACAO de uma pessoa para outra, use "tell" + pessoa + "that" + frase completa (Tell him that the room is ready). Para passar um PEDIDO, use "ask" + pessoa + "to" + verbo na forma base (Ask her to call back). Para comecar uma ligacao dizendo o motivo, use "I'm calling to" + verbo base (I'm calling to confirm). / To pass on information, use "tell + person + that + a full sentence". To pass on a request, use "ask + person + to + base verb". Start a call with "I'm calling to + base verb".
