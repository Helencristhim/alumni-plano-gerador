The Best Choice -- Why We Are the Best (Superlatives)

Recommend a product and say it is the best of all. New vocabulary: recommend, choice, leader, advantage, popular, valuable, option, winner. Use superlatives to say number one: the + short adjective + est (the fastest, the cheapest), the most + adjective for long words (the most reliable, the most popular), and irregular forms (good becomes the best). Never say "the most fast" or "the goodest".

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Superlatives (the + adjective + est / the most + adjective)

GRAMMAR

Para dizer que algo é número um (o melhor de todos), use the + superlativo. Adjetivo CURTO (1 sílaba): the + adjetivo + est (fast → the fastest, cheap → the cheapest). Adjetivo terminado em y: troca por iest (easy → the easiest). Adjetivo LONGO (2+ sílabas): the most + adjetivo (reliable → the most reliable, popular → the most popular, valuable → the most valuable). Irregulares: good → the best, bad → the worst. Nunca esqueça o "the", nunca use the most com adjetivo curto ("the most fast") nem diga "the goodest". / To say number one, use the + superlative. Short adjective: add -est. Long adjective: use the most. Irregular: the best, the worst.

Type of adjectiveThe + superlative
Short (fast, cheap)It is the fastest / the cheapest on the market.
Ends in -y (easy)It is the easiest company to work with.
Long (reliable, popular)It is the most reliable / the most popular of all.
Irregular (good, bad)It is the best / the worst option.
Regra de ourouse the + curto + est, ou the most + longo, nunca os dois: diga the fastest (não "the most fast") e the best (não "the goodest").
