Lesson 14 -- The Best Choice (Superlatives)

Series / Business
Shark Tank -- a pitch for the best product

A short scene shows an entrepreneur recommending a product as the number one choice. Listen for superlatives: "the fastest", "the most reliable", "the best", "the most popular".

Watch with English subtitles. Write down 3 superlative sentences that you hear (for example, "It is the best on the market"). Then recommend a product from your work out loud.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Making a Recommendation"

The speakers recommend products and services: how to say one option is the best choice, the most popular, or the most valuable, and how to give the main advantage to a customer.

Choose one short episode. Note 3 superlative phrases the speaker uses to recommend a product. Compare them with the recommendation from Gleice and David in class.

YouTube / BBC
BBC Learning English -- "Superlatives: -est and the most"

A short, clear lesson on superlatives: the + short adjective + est (the fastest, the cheapest), the most + adjective for long words (the most reliable), and irregular forms (good becomes the best).

After watching, write five superlative sentences about products at work (for example, "Our product is the most reliable on the market"). Then record a one-minute audio: recommend your best product.
