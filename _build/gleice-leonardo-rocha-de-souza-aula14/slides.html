Chapter 3 -- The Best

The Best of All

Superlatives -- the fastest, the most reliable

Listen and Notice

Find the Pattern

Look at the orange words. How do we say one thing is number one?

"Our product is the fastest on the market."

"This device is the most reliable of all."

"It is the cheapest option for our customers."

"Our service is the best choice you can make."

The Rule

The + Superlative

Type of adjectiveThe + superlative
Short (fast, cheap)the fastest, the cheapest
Ends in -y (easy)the easiest
Long (reliable, popular)the most reliable, the most popular
Irregular (good, bad)the best, the worst

Key idea: to say number one, use the + superlative. Short adjective: add -est (the fastest). Long adjective: use the most (the most reliable). Never use both, and never say "the goodest" -- say the best.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"Our product is the most fast. It is the reliablest, and our service is the goodest."
"Our product is the fastest. It is the most reliable, and our service is the best."

Short adjective: the ...est (the fastest). Long adjective: the most (the most reliable). Irregular: the best, not "the goodest".
