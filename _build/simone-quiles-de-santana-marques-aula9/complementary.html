Lesson 9 -- Asking Smart Questions

Series
The Good Wife -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

A legal drama full of smart questioning. Watch how the lawyers ask careful, polite questions to get information from clients and witnesses -- listen for indirect questions like "Could you tell me..." and "Do you know whether...".

Watch with English subtitles. Write down 3 indirect questions you hear. Then turn each one into a direct question and notice the change in word order.

Podcast
HBR IdeaCast -- "The Surprising Power of Questions"

Practical advice on asking better questions at work: how to inquire politely, probe deeper, confirm what you heard, and keep your questions relevant. Listen for how the best questioners follow up without sounding aggressive.

Note 3 phrases the speakers use to ask follow-up questions. Compare them with how Simone questions Daniel in the Q&A call.

YouTube / BBC
BBC Learning English -- "Indirect questions" / "Being polite"

A short, practical lesson on indirect questions: how openers like "Could you tell me" and "Do you know" change the word order and make a question sound polite. Directly useful for the Q&A sessions you run at Telefonica.

After watching, write five indirect questions about a real deal, then record yourself asking them using at least four new words from today.
