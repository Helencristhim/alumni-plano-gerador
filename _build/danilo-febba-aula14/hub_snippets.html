Small Talk with Colleagues -- Chatting at a Coffee Break

Faça um bate-papo curto e natural no cafézinho. Palavras-chave: weekend, hobby, enjoy, chat, free time, relax, weather, favorite. Gramática: like + -ing (I like reading / playing football) para falar do que você gosta de fazer, mais as perguntas de small talk (Do you like...? / What do you do on weekends? / How was your weekend?).

Vocabulary

Weekend -- Saturday and Sunday; the days you do not work (fim de semana)
"How was your weekend?"
Hobby -- an activity you do for fun in your free time (hobby / passatempo)
"My hobby is painting."
Enjoy -- to like doing something; to have a good time (gostar de / curtir)
"I enjoy cooking."
Chat -- to talk in a friendly, relaxed way (conversar / bater papo)
"Let us chat over coffee."
Free time -- time when you do not work; you do what you like (tempo livre)
"What do you do in your free time?"
Relax -- to rest and feel calm (relaxar / descansar)
"I relax on Sundays."
Weather -- sun, rain, hot or cold outside (clima / tempo)
"The weather is nice today."
Favorite -- the one you like the most (favorito / preferido)
"Reading is my favorite."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas. (Repare em like / enjoy + -ing e nas perguntas de small talk.)

It is Monday morning at the coffee machine. Rachel sees Danilo and asks, "How was your weekend?" Danilo says it was great; he relaxed a lot. The weather was nice, so Rachel went for a walk. Then Danilo asks, "Do you have any hobbies?" Rachel loves painting; it is her favorite way to relax. Danilo says, "I like playing football and reading in my free time." Rachel asks, "Do you enjoy cooking too?" Danilo answers, "Yes, I really enjoy cooking on weekends." It is nice to chat before the meeting.

1. Como o Danilo fala do que gosta de fazer?
A "I like read and play."
B "I like playing football and reading."
C "I like to playing football."
2. Qual pergunta a Rachel usa para começar o papo?
A "How was your weekend?"
B "Who is calling, please?"
C "Where is your weekend?"
3. Depois de like, love e enjoy, usamos o verbo em qual forma?
A verbo + to (enjoy to cook)
B verbo + ing (enjoy cooking)
C verbo base (enjoy cook)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- like + -ing & Small-Talk Questions

GRAMMAR

Para dizer do que você gosta de fazer, use like / love / enjoy + verbo-ing (I like reading). Para começar um bate-papo, use perguntas simples de small talk. (To say what you like doing, use like / love / enjoy + verb-ing. To start a chat, use simple small-talk questions.)

Uso (PT)Estrutura (EN)
dizer o que você gosta de fazerI like / love / enjoy + verbo-ing: I like reading.
perguntar sobre gostosDo you like + verbo-ing? Do you like cooking?
perguntar sobre o fim de semanaHow was your weekend? / What do you do on weekends?
perguntar sobre o tempo livreWhat do you do in your free time?
devolver a perguntaHow about you? / What about you?

Atenção (erro comum): depois de like, love, enjoy use o verbo com -ing: I like reading (não "I like read"), I enjoy cooking (não "I enjoy to cook"). Nas perguntas de rotina, não esqueça o do: What do you do on weekends? (não "What you do on weekends?").

Stage 5: Free Production

Reflection

Grave você respondendo à pergunta abaixo. Não existe resposta certa ou errada.

Imagine que você encontra um colega no cafézinho antes da reunião. Grave um bate-papo curto: cumprimente, pergunte como foi o fim de semana, fale de um hobby e do que você gosta de fazer no tempo livre, e devolva a pergunta. Use like / enjoy + verbo-ing. Por exemplo: "Hi! How was your weekend? I like reading and I enjoy cooking. Do you have any hobbies? How about you?" Fale com calma.
