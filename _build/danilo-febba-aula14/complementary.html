Lesson 14 -- Small Talk with Colleagues

Series
The Office (US) -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot" (break room scenes)

Colleagues chat in the break room and by the coffee machine. Listen for small-talk openers: "How was your weekend?", "Do you have any hobbies?", "What do you do in your free time?". Connection to Lesson 14: real office small talk between coworkers.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down 5 small-talk questions you hear and say them aloud.

BBC Learning English -- "The English We Speak: making small talk"

A short, clear episode at A2-B1 speed about friendly chat at work: the weekend, hobbies, and the weather. Connection to Lesson 14: it reinforces small-talk questions and talking about what you like doing.

Tip: listen twice. The second time, pause and answer out loud: "I like reading and I enjoy cooking."
