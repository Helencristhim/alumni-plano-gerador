Chapter 3: Can / Could

Ask and Offer Politely

Can you...? · Could you...? · Can I...?

The Forms

Three Polite Patterns

"Can you bring the documents?" · request

"Could you carry this, please?" · softer

"Can I help you?" · offer

"Could you explain the schedule, please?"

Can/Could + you + verbo (sem "to") · Could = mais educado · Can I...? = oferecer ajuda

Discover the Pattern

Find the Verb

"Can you help me, please?"

"Could you bring the documents?"

"Could you wait a moment?"

"Can I carry that for you?"

PatternExample
Can you + verb?"Can you help me?" — ask for help
Could you + verb?"Could you carry this?" — more polite
Can I + verb?"Can I help you?" — offer help
+ please"Could you wait, please?" — even kinder

Sempre verbo base depois de you/I · NUNCA "to" nem "-ing" · Could = mais educado · please = mais gentil
