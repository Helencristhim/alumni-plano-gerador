Lesson 19 -- Out for Dinner

Series
The Bear -- a restaurant dinner-service scene (any season)

Watch a scene set in a restaurant. Notice how staff and guests order, recommend dishes, and ask for things politely with "Could you...?" and "Would you mind...?".

Watch with English subtitles. Write down two polite requests and one recommendation ("I would recommend...").

Podcast
Plain English -- "Ordering Food and Being Polite in English"

A practical episode on restaurant English: how to read a menu, order a starter and a main course, recommend dishes, and ask for the bill politely.

Record three lines: one recommendation, one "Could you...?" request, and one "Would you mind + -ing" request.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Polite requests: Could you and Would you mind"

A focused lesson on polite requests: how "Could you + base verb" and "Would you mind + -ing" make you sound courteous. It directly reinforces today's grammar.

After watching, turn six direct orders into polite requests, using both forms.
