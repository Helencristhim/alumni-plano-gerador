In the afternoon, she goes to a dance class because she loves dancing. She likes swimming in the river with her children, but she does not like swimming in cold water. In the evening, she listens to music and rests.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Like and Love (+ -ing)

GRAMMAR

Usado para falar das coisas que você gosta de fazer no tempo livre. / Used to talk about the things you enjoy doing in your free time.

Form / FormaStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
afirmativolike / love + verbo + -ingI like reading.
negativodo not (don’t) like + verbo + -ingI don’t like running.
perguntaDo + sujeito + like + verbo + -ing?Do you like dancing?

Key point / Ponto-chave: depois de like e love, o verbo recebe -ing (read → reading, cook → cooking, dance → dancing, swim → swimming). Não use "to": dizemos "I like reading", não "I like to reading".
