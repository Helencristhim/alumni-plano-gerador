Lesson 10 -- Free Time and Hobbies

Series
Extra English -- Episode 7: "A Quiet Weekend at Home"

A simple comedy made for English learners. In this episode the characters talk about their hobbies and free time -- reading, cooking, music, and movies -- in slow, clear English. Perfect for A1 listening about what people like and love to do.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down every free-time word you hear (read, cook, dance, music, movie). Then say out loud: "I like reading" and "I love cooking".

Podcast
Coffee Break English -- "Talking About Hobbies"

A short, friendly lesson on free time: how to say "I like reading" and "I love cooking", and how to ask a friend "Do you like dancing?". The hosts explain the -ing form slowly, with many examples you can repeat.

Pause after each example and repeat it out loud. Then describe your weekend in three sentences using like and love with the -ing form.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Like, Love and Hate + -ing"

A clear video that teaches free-time vocabulary (read, cook, dance, swim, music, movie) and how to use like and love with the -ing form to talk about your hobbies, with pictures and simple sentences.

After watching, record a 60-second message about your free time: say two things you like doing and one thing you do not like doing. Use at least three new words and the -ing form.
