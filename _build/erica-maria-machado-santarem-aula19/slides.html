Chapter 3

The Polite Code

Can I have...? · How much is it?

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"Can I have a painkiller, please?"

"Can I have some pills, please?"

"How much is it?"

"Can I have the label, please?"

FunctionHow to say it
Polite requestCan I have + a/some + thing + please? — Can I have a painkiller, please?
Ask the priceHow much is it? — How much is it?
One thing (a/an)a painkiller, a pill, an allergy (an before a vowel)
More than oneCan I have some pills, please?
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"I want a painkiller."

✓ RIGHT

"Can I have a painkiller, please?"

✗ WRONG

"How many is it?"

✓ RIGHT

"How much is it?"

✗ WRONG

"Can I have painkiller?"

✓ RIGHT

"Can I have a painkiller?"

To ask politely, use "Can I have... please?". To ask the price, use "How much is it?". Use "a/an" before one thing.
