Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. Ele usa Can I have...? para pedir e How much is it? para o preço.

This morning Érica had a headache. She went to the pharmacy near her home. At the counter she said: "Can I have a painkiller, please?" The pharmacist was very kind. She gave Érica a small box of pills. Érica asked: "How much is it?" The pharmacist said: "It is twelve reais." Érica also said: "I have an allergy. Is it safe?" The pharmacist read the label and said: "Yes, it is safe. The dose is one pill a day." Érica did not need a prescription. Now her headache is gone.

B "Can I have a painkiller, please?"

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Polite Requests & Asking the Price

GRAMMAR

Como pedir com educação e perguntar o preço. Para pedir, use Can I have + a/an/some + coisa + please (Can I have a painkiller, please?). Para perguntar o preço, use How much is it? Use a/an antes de uma coisa (a pill, an allergy — "an" antes de som de vogal) e some para mais de uma (some pills). / To request politely: Can I have... please? To ask the price: How much is it?

Função / FunctionEstrutura / Structure
Pedido educado / Polite requestCan I have + coisa + please? — Can I have a painkiller, please?
Perguntar o preço / PriceHow much is it? — How much is it?
Uma coisa / One thinga / an + coisa — a pill, an allergy (an antes de vogal)
Mais de uma / More than onesome + coisa — Can I have some pills, please?
Lembre-se"Can I have...?" é mais educado que "I want...". Sempre termine com please.
