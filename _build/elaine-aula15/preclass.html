Aula 15 -- Pre-class

Shopping -- Stores

Shop for clothes: size, color, try on, fitting room, cheap, expensive, discount, fit. Structures: "Do you have... in...?", "Can I try it on?", "Is it on sale?" and "How much is it?"

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Size -- how big or small (small, medium, large)
"Do you have a bigger size?" (Tamanho)
Color -- red, blue, green, black...
"Do you have it in blue?" (Cor)
Try on -- to put on clothes to see if you like them
"Can I try it on?" (Experimentar)
Fitting room -- the little room where you try on clothes
"Where is the fitting room?" (Provador)
Cheap -- it costs little money
"This shirt is cheap." (Barato)
Expensive -- it costs a lot of money
"This coat is expensive." (Caro)
Discount -- money off the normal price (a sale)
"Is there a discount today?" (Desconto)
Fit -- when clothes are the right size for you
"It fits well." (Caber / servir)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Elaine is in a clothing store. She likes a shirt and asks, Do you have this in a bigger size? Yes, in medium and large. Do you have it in blue? Here it is. Can I try it on? The fitting room is at the back. The shirt fits well. How much is it? It is forty dollars, but today it is on sale for thirty. Elaine loves the discount and buys it.

1. Como Elaine pede outro tamanho? / How does Elaine ask for another size?
A Have you a bigger size?
B Do you have this in a bigger size?
C Bigger size you?
2. Como ela pede para experimentar? / How does she ask to try it on?
A Can I try on it?
B Can I try it on?
C I try it?
3. Quanto custa na promocao? / How much is it on sale?
A Thirty dollars.
B Forty dollars.
C Thirteen dollars.
