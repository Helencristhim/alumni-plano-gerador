Aula 8 -- Strategic Proposals

Tema da aula: weighing strategic proposals with the second conditional (if + past simple, would/could/might + base verb) and proposal language (scenario, trade-off, upside, downside, outweigh, feasible, mitigate). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

Series
"Succession" -- boardroom strategy scenes

Executives weigh hypothetical acquisitions and expansions in almost every episode. Listen for how they propose options ("If we sold the division...") and argue about the upside, the downside, and the trade-off before deciding.

Dica: assista com legenda em inglês e anote três frases no segundo condicional (if + past simple, would/could + verbo) e duas palavras de proposta (scenario, trade-off, upside, downside).

Podcast
HBR IdeaCast -- decision-making & strategic bets

Harvard Business Review episodes on how leaders frame hypothetical scenarios, allocate resources, and mitigate risk. Great input to review feasible, allocate, prioritize, projection, and contingency -- exactly the vocabulary of this lesson.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote cada vez que um líder diz "if we..., we would..." e como descreve o trade-off.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- The Second Conditional

Clear, short explainer on the second conditional for hypothetical and unlikely situations, including "If I were you...". Watch how the if-clause stays in the past simple while the result uses would, could, or might.

Dica: pause após cada exemplo e reformule uma decisão real do seu trabalho como hipótese: "If we acquired the plant, we would double our capacity."
