Lesson 05 -- What Are Your Plans?

Series
The Diplomat — Season 1, Episode 1: "The Cinderella Thing" (Netflix)

Kate Wyler spends the whole series in meeting rooms, deciding what the country is going to do — and saying it out loud, under pressure, to people more powerful than her. It is your call with the Global team, with a different topic: the goal, the strategy, the deadline, and what is already in the calendar.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Treasure hunt: write down 3 sentences that start with "We are going to..." or "I am going to...". Do not try to understand 100% — look ONLY for the plan block.
