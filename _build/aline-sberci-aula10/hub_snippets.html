Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Agree -- to have the same opinion as someone
"I agree with your plan." (Concordar)
Disagree -- to have a different opinion from someone
"I disagree, but I understand you." (Discordar)
Opinion -- what you think or believe about something
"In my opinion, the plan is good." (Opinião)
Suggest -- to offer an idea for the group to think about
"I suggest a later deadline." (Sugerir)
Agenda -- the list of topics to talk about in a meeting
"The agenda has three topics." (Pauta)
Clarify -- to make something clearer with a question
"Could you clarify the budget?" (Esclarecer)
Discuss -- to talk about a topic with other people
"Let us discuss the new plan." (Discutir)
Decision -- a choice the team makes after talking
"We need a clear decision today." (Decisão)
Contribute -- to add your ideas or help to the group
"I want to contribute one idea." (Contribuir)
Summarize -- to give the main points in a short way
"I will summarize the meeting." (Resumir)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Aline joins a team meeting. First, she reads the agenda so she knows the topics. Tom asks the team to discuss the new deadline. In Tom's opinion, the date is fine, but Aline does not agree. She says, "I see your point, but I am not sure." She wants to contribute a better idea, so she asks a question to clarify the budget. Then she suggests two more days. The team makes a decision, and Aline helps summarize the next steps.

1. What does Aline read first?
A The agenda, so she knows the topics.
B The final decision.
C The budget report.
2. Does Aline agree with Tom?
A Yes, she agrees with the date.
B No, she disagrees and suggests more time.
C No, she leaves the meeting.
3. What does Aline do at the end?
A She helps summarize the next steps.
B She forgets the agenda.
C She writes a new email.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Opinion & Agreeing

GRAMMAR

Numa reunião, comece com uma frase antes da sua ideia. / In a meeting, start with a phrase before your idea.

Function / FunçãoPhrase / FraseExample / Exemplo
Opinião (opinion)In my opinion / I thinkIn my opinion, we need more time
Concordar (agree)I agree / a good pointI agree. That is a good point
Discordar (disagree)I see your point, butI see your point, but I am not sure
Sugerir (suggest)Maybe we could / Why notMaybe we could move the deadline

Key point / Ponto-chave: para discordar de forma educada, use I see your point, but em vez de You are wrong. Lembre: I agree with (não "agree of"), e could move (não "could to move").
