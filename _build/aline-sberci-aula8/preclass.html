Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Progress -- the improvement you make over time
"I can see my progress every week." (Progresso)
Skill -- an ability you build with practice
"Speaking is an important skill." (Habilidade)
Strength -- something you do well
"Listening is one of my strengths." (Ponto forte)
Weakness -- something you find difficult and want to improve
"Spelling is my weakness." (Ponto fraco)
Feedback -- helpful comments about your work
"My teacher gives me helpful feedback." (Retorno)
Mistake -- something wrong that you can learn from
"I learn from every mistake." (Erro)
Milestone -- an important point in your progress
"Lesson eight is a big milestone." (Marco)
Habit -- something you do often and regularly
"Studying daily is a good habit." (Hábito)
Effort -- the hard work and energy you give
"Real progress takes effort." (Esforço)
Proud -- feeling happy about something good you did
"I am proud of my progress." (Orgulhoso)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- can / can't for Ability

GRAMMAR

Usado para dizer o que você é capaz de fazer. / Used to say what you are able to do.

Form / FormaMeaning / SentidoExample / Exemplo
can + base verbHabilidade (afirmativo) / AbilityI can give directions
cannot / can't + base verbNegativo / NegativeI can't write emails yet
Can + you + base verb?Pergunta / QuestionCan you describe your routine?
Yes, I can. / No, I can't.Resposta curta / Short answerYes, I can.

Key point / Ponto-chave: depois de can, use o verbo base sem to e sem s (dizemos I can give e she can write, não I can to give nem she can writes).
