Lesson 15 -- Managing Conflict at Work

Series
The Office (US) -- Conflict-resolution episodes

A study in how NOT to manage conflict, and occasionally how to. Watch how characters trigger defensiveness, assign blame, and reach a standoff -- then how a calmer voice de-escalates and rebuilds goodwill.

Pick one tense scene and rewrite two blaming lines as concession sentences (Although..., Despite..., Whereas...).

Podcast
WorkLife with Adam Grant -- "How to Disagree Productively"

An episode on giving and receiving difficult feedback: leading with empathy, staying candid without blame, and turning friction into accountability. Directly relevant to a supply-chain review meeting.

Note two phrases the host uses to acknowledge before countering. Try each one in your next hard conversation at work.

YouTube
"Linking Words: Although, Even Though, Despite, In Spite Of" -- English Grammar

A clear guide to the exact difference that trips up B2 speakers: a clause after although / even though / while / whereas, but a noun or -ing after despite / in spite of.

After watching, write five concession sentences about a real work conflict, using a different linker in each.
