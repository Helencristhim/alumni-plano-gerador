Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Dienane is in a new part of the city, and she is lost. She wants the bank, so she asks a man: "Excuse me, how do I get to the bank?" The man helps her: "Go straight for two blocks. Then turn left at the traffic light and cross the street. The bank is on your right." In English, we ask the way with How do I get to + a place. We answer with direction words: go straight, turn left, turn right, cross. To say where a place is, we use is on your left or is on your right.

1. How do we ask the way in English?
A "How I get to the bank?"
B "How do I get to the bank?"
C "Where is get the bank?"
2. How do we say where the bank is?
A "It is on your right."
B "It is in your right."
C "It is to your right side of."
3. Where does Dienane turn left?
A At the traffic light.
B At her house.
C At the bus stop.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Asking and Giving Directions

GRAMMAR

Em inglês, perguntamos o caminho com How do I get to + lugar, e respondemos com palavras de direção. / In English, we ask the way with How do I get to + place, and we answer with direction words.

Function / FunçãoStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
perguntar o caminhoExcuse me, how do I get to + lugar?How do I get to the bank?
ir em frenteGo straight (+ for ... blocks)Go straight for two blocks.
mudar de direçãoTurn left / Turn right (+ at...)Turn left at the traffic light.
dizer onde ficaIt is on your left / rightThe bank is on your right.

Key point / Ponto-chave: para perguntar, use How do I get to + o lugar. Para responder, use Go straight, Turn left, Turn right, Cross the street. Para dizer onde fica, use on your left ou on your right (não "in your right").
