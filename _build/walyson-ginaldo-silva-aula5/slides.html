Grammar Rule

Hedging: The Polite Request

WhenPolite form
Small requestCould you please send the file?
Bigger requestI was wondering if you could help.
Asking permissionWould it be possible to meet on Friday?
Closing politelyI would appreciate it if you could reply.

Key idea: in a professional email, soften your request. Open with "I am writing regarding...", ask with "could you" or "I was wondering if", and close with "Best regards".

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"Send me the report now."
"Could you please send me the report?"

In a formal email, soften the request with could you please or I would appreciate it if, instead of a bare order.

Grammar Practice

Make It Polite

"_____Could you please confirm the delivery date?" (modal of polite request)
"I _____was wondering if you could send the file." (I ___ wondering if)
"Would it _____be possible to reply by Friday?" (Would it ___ possible to)
"I would _____appreciate it if you could clarify the terms." (I would ___ it if)
