Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada adjetivo e leia o exemplo. A tradução em português está ao lado. Toque em Listen.

Patient -- able to wait calmly without getting upset (paciente)
"My colleague is very patient with new people."
Creative -- full of new ideas and imagination (criativo/criativa)
"She is the most creative person I know."
Direct -- saying clearly what you think (direto/franco)
"He is direct, so you always know what he thinks."
Reliable -- someone you can always trust and count on (confiável)
"She is reliable and never misses a deadline."
Outgoing -- friendly and enjoys being with people (extrovertido/sociável)
"My sister is outgoing and talks to everyone."
Shy -- quiet and a little nervous around new people (tímido/tímida)
"He is a bit shy when he meets new people."
Hardworking -- putting a lot of effort into work (esforçado/trabalhador)
"She is hardworking and studies every day."
Supportive -- helping and encouraging other people (que dá apoio/solidário)
"My manager is supportive when I have a problem."
Easygoing -- relaxed and not easily worried (tranquilo/de boa)
"He is easygoing and rarely gets stressed."
Thoughtful -- caring and considerate of other people (atencioso/cuidadoso)
"She is thoughtful and always remembers my birthday."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas.

My colleague Ana is one of the best people on our team. She is very reliable, and she never misses a deadline. The thing about Ana is that she is always patient with new people. When someone has a question, she helps them with a smile. She is quite direct in meetings, so you always know what she thinks. But she can also be very thoughtful. To be honest, I would say she is both hardworking and supportive. Some people are shy at work, but Ana is outgoing and she talks to everyone.

1. Why does the text say "she helps" and not "she help"?
A Because with he/she/it we add "-s" to the verb in the present simple.
B Because "help" is never correct.
C Because the sentence is a question.
2. What does "She can also be very thoughtful" tell us?
A Ana is only thoughtful and nothing else.
B Ana has another quality besides being direct.
C Ana is never thoughtful.
3. Is Ana shy or outgoing?
A She is shy and quiet.
B She is outgoing and talks to everyone.
C The text does not say.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Describing People (to be + adjective)

GRAMMAR

Como descrever a personalidade de alguém. / How to describe someone's personality.

Form / FormaEnglishPortuguês
to be + adjectiveShe is reliable. They are patient.Ela é confiável. Eles são pacientes.
third-person verb (-s)He works hard. She helps the team.Ele trabalha muito. Ela ajuda a equipe.
ContrastHe is direct, but he can also be thoughtful.Ele é direto, mas também pode ser atencioso.
QuestionWhat is she like? What does he do?Como ela é? O que ele faz?
Soft opinionTo be honest... / I would say she is... / The thing about him is... (Para ser honesta... / Eu diria que ela é... / O que acontece com ele é...)
