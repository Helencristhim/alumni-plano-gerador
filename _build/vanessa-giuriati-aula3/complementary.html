Aula 3 -- Speaking About People You Know

Series
The Intern (2015) -- First 20 minutes

A warm film about people at work, with clear American English at a moderate pace. Characters constantly describe each other's personalities and habits. Direct connection to Lesson 3: listen for adjectives that describe what people are like.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write 3 sentences describing one character ("He is...", "She tends to...").

Podcast
WorkLife with Adam Grant -- Any episode on great colleagues

An accessible business podcast where the host describes the personalities and working styles of real people. Direct connection to Lesson 3: hear how a fluent speaker characterizes someone with adjectives and contrast structures.

Tip: note 5 personality adjectives you hear. Use 3 of them to describe a colleague of yours.

YouTube
"Describing People's Personality in English" -- BBC Learning English

A short, clear lesson presenting common personality adjectives and how to use them in real sentences. Direct connection to Lesson 3: the exact language we practiced, modeled by native speakers at a friendly pace.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Pause and repeat 3 sentences out loud about people you know.
