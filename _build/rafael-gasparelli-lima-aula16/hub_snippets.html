At the Board Table -- Opinions and Decisions

Aprenda a falar em uma reunião de diretoria: dar a sua opinião (I think / In my opinion), concordar e discordar com respeito (I agree / I disagree, nunca "I am agree") e chegar a uma decisão. Vocabulário de decisão: proposal, vote, approve, reject, concern, reasonable, compromise, point of view.

Stage 1.1: Key Words

Vocabulary

Eight words you hear in every board meeting. Listen to each one and notice how it is used.

Proposal -- proposta (ideia para discutir)
"The proposal is to open a second office."
Vote -- voto / votar
"Let us take a vote on the proposal."
Approve -- aprovar (dizer sim)
"The board has approved the new budget."
Reject -- rejeitar (dizer não)
"They rejected the first proposal."
Concern -- preocupação / receio
"I understand the concern about the cost."
Reasonable -- razoável / sensato
"That is a reasonable price for the office."
Compromise -- acordo / meio-termo
"We found a reasonable compromise."
Point of view -- ponto de vista / opinião
"Each director shared a point of view."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Read the board minutes and answer the questions.

At the board meeting, the chair presented a proposal to open a second office in the south. In my opinion, this is the right moment, she said. One director said, I agree, the new clients there need a local office. But another director had a concern about the cost. I see your point, but I disagree, he said. I think the cost is too high this year. After a long discussion, the board found a reasonable compromise: start with a small office. Actually, that is a smart idea, the chair said. Then the directors took a vote, and the majority approved the new plan.

1. What is the proposal at the meeting?
A To open a second office in the south.
B To hire two new managers.
C To close the main office.
2. Why does one director disagree?
A The location is bad.
B He thinks the cost is too high this year.
C There are no clients in the south.
3. What compromise does the board reach?
A They reject the proposal.
B They start with a small office.
C They wait until next year.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Opinions, Agree & Disagree

Grammar

Para dar uma opinião, comece com I think ou In my opinion seguido de uma frase completa (sujeito + verbo): "In my opinion, it is a good plan." Para concordar, diga I agree — NUNCA "I am agree" (agree já é verbo). Para discordar, diga I disagree ou, com mais respeito, I see your point, but... Use Actually para corrigir uma ideia com gentileza. / To give an opinion use I think / In my opinion + full sentence. To agree say I agree (never "I am agree"). To disagree politely use I see your point, but...

Function / FunçãoEnglishPortuguês
Give an opinionIn my opinion, it is a good plan.Na minha opinião, é um bom plano.
Give an opinionI think we should wait.Eu acho que deveríamos esperar.
AgreeI agree with you.Eu concordo com você.
Disagree (polite)I see your point, but I disagree.Entendo seu ponto, mas discordo.
Correct gentlyActually, the cost is lower.Na verdade, o custo é menor.
