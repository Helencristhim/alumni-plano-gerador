Aula 14 -- Pre-class

Company History -- Milestones and Changes

Aprenda a contar a história de uma empresa no passado. Oito verbos de marcos (fundar, crescer, construir, comprar, vencer...) e o Past Simple dos verbos IRREGULARES: formas que mudam (grow → grew, become → became), a negativa com did not e a pergunta com Did.

Stage 1.1: Key Words

Vocabulary

Eight milestone verbs to tell a company's history. Listen to each one and notice the irregular past form.

Found -- fundar (passado: founded)
"Two lawyers founded the firm in 1998."
Grow -- crescer (passado: grew)
"The firm grew slowly in the first years."
Become -- tornar-se (passado: became)
"It became one of the most respected firms."
Build -- construir / desenvolver (passado: built)
"The firm built a strong reputation."
Buy -- comprar (passado: bought)
"In 2005, it bought a new office."
Win -- vencer / ganhar (passado: won)
"The firm won a national award in 2012."
Take over -- assumir o controle (passado: took over)
"It took over two local competitors."
Merge -- fundir / juntar (subst.: merger = fusão)
"After a big merger, it opened offices abroad."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Read the story and answer the questions.

In 1998, two lawyers founded a small firm with a single desk. In the first years, the firm grew slowly, but it built a strong reputation. In 2005, it bought a new office and took over two local competitors. The firm became one of the most respected in the region, and in 2012 it won a national award. After a big merger, the company opened offices in three countries. They did not stop, and they never forgot how it all began.

A Two lawyers, with a single desk.
B Two local competitors.

B It bought a new office and took over two competitors.

3. What happened after the big merger?
A The firm closed two offices.
B The company opened offices in three countries.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Past Simple (Irregular Verbs)

Grammar

Muitos verbos comuns NÃO seguem a regra do -ed: eles têm uma forma própria no passado que você precisa memorizar. Ex: grow → grew, become → became, build → built, buy → bought, win → won. Na negativa e na pergunta, porém, vale a mesma regra dos regulares: use did not / Did + o verbo na forma BASE (sem mudança): "We did not win" / "Did the firm grow?". / Many common verbs do not follow the -ed rule: they have a special past form you must memorize. But for negatives and questions, use did not / Did + the base verb, just like regular verbs.

Form / FormaEnglishPortuguês
Affirmative (irregular form)The firm grew quickly.A firma cresceu rapidamente.
Affirmative (irregular form)It became a market leader.Ela se tornou líder de mercado.
Negative (did not + base)We did not win the first time.Nós não vencemos na primeira vez.
Question (Did + base)Did the firm buy a new office?A firma comprou um novo escritório?
Short answerYes, it did. / No, it did not.Sim, comprou. / Não, não comprou.

Stage 5: Free Production

Reflection

Record yourself answering the question. There is no right or wrong answer.

Think about your company, or a company you admire. Out loud, in English, tell its history: who founded it, how it grew, what it built, what it bought, which firms it took over or merged with, and what it became today. Use the irregular past forms, and remember the base verb after did and did not.
