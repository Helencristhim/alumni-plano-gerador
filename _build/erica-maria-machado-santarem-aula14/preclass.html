Aula 14 -- Pre-class

My Life Story -- Past Experiences

Conte a sua história de vida e fale do seu passado: yesterday, last week, born, ago, childhood, school, trip, remember. Gramática nova: past simple (worked, lived, studied) e verbos irregulares (was, went, had). Ritmo lento e claro.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Palavras novas para falar do passado. Ouça cada uma e repita. Toque em Listen.

Yesterday -- ontem — the day before today
"I worked at home yesterday."
Last week -- semana passada — the week before this one
"Last week, I visited my mother."
Born -- nascido(a) — when you came into the world
"I was born in 1969."
Ago -- atrás (no tempo) — a time in the past, before now
"Ten years ago, I moved to the city."
Childhood -- infância — the time when you were a child
"I had a happy childhood."
School -- escola — the place where children study
"I studied at a small school."
Trip -- viagem — when you travel to a place
"We took a trip to the beach."
Remember -- lembrar — to keep something in your mind
"I remember my old house."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. Ele usa o past simple.

I was born in a small town. When I was a child, I lived near a river. I studied at a small school, and I played every day. Ten years ago, I moved to the city. Last week, I remembered my childhood and I felt happy. I had a good life.

1. Where was the person born?
A In a big city.
B In a small town.
C Near the sea.
2. The text says "I lived" and "I studied." Why do the verbs end in -ed?
A Because the actions are in the past (past simple).
B Because the actions are happening now.
C Because they are future plans.
3. What did the person do ten years ago?
A They started school.
B They moved to the city.
C They took a trip to the beach.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- The Past Simple

GRAMMAR

Como falar de ações no passado. / How to talk about actions in the past.

Tipo / TypeAgora / NowPassado / Past
Regular (+ -ed)work / live / studyworked / lived / studied
Irregulargo / have / dowent / had / did
To beam / is / arewas / were
Lembre-seNegativo: I did not + verbo base. Pergunta: Did you...? Depois de "did", o verbo fica na base.

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situação.

A friend asks: "Where were you born?" You say:
A "I was born in São Paulo."
B "I am born in São Paulo."
C "I borned in São Paulo."
You went to the beach in the week before this one. Which sentence is correct?
A "I goed to the beach last week."
B "I went to the beach last week."
C "I go to the beach last week."
You studied at school when you were young. You say:
A "I studyed at school."
B "I studied at school."
C "I was study at school."
You did not work last weekend. The best sentence is:
A "I did not work last weekend."
B "I did not worked last weekend."
C "I not work last weekend."

Stage 5: Free Production

Reflection

Grave você respondendo. Não há resposta certa ou errada.

Tell your life story. Say where you were born (use "I was born..."). Say two things you did when you were a child (use the past simple, for example: I studied, I played). Then say one thing you did last week (for example: I went to..., I visited...). Take your time and speak calmly.
