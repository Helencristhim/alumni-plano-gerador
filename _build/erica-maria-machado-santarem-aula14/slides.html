Chapter 3

The Code

The Past Simple — verb + -ed

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I worked in a small town."

"I studied at a small school."

"I was a happy child."

"I went to school every day."

TypeNowPast
Regularwork / live / studyworked / lived / studied
Irregulargo / have / dowent / had / did
To beam / is / arewas / were
RememberNegative: I did not + base verb. Question: Did you...?
Common Mistake

Careful Here

"I goed to school."
"I went to school." (go is irregular)
"I did not worked."
"I did not work." (after did not = base verb)
"I was study at school."
"I studied at school." (one past verb, not two)

Regular verbs add -ed. Some verbs are irregular (go → went). After did not, use the base verb.
