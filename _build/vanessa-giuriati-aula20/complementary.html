Aula 20 -- Confidence Checkpoint: Looking Back

Series / Documentary
The Last Dance (Netflix) -- Any episode

Athletes look back on a long journey and reflect on how they used to be versus who they became. Direct connection to Lesson 20: listen for "used to" and the language of progress, pride, and overcoming — reflection on growth, told out loud.

Tip: pause after a reflection and say one sentence about yourself: "I used to..., but now I..."

Podcast
The Happiness Lab (Dr. Laurie Santos) -- "Caring What You're Made Of"

A calm, clear episode about growth, gratitude, and measuring yourself against who you used to be, not against perfect. Direct connection to Lesson 20: rich, natural use of "used to," progress, and grateful — exactly the reflective language we practiced.

Tip: after the episode, record 60 seconds on your English journey using "used to" and three of today's words.

YouTube
"USED TO -- Past Habits in English" -- BBC Learning English / Speak English With Vanessa

A clear lesson on "used to" for past habits and states (used to + base verb) and the negative "didn't use to," modeled by native speakers. Direct connection to Lesson 20: exactly the grammar we used to tell the story of your growth.

Tip: pause after each example and make a parallel sentence about your own English journey.
