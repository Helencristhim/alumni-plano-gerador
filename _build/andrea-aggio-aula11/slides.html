Chapter 4: The Passive Voice

The Passive Voice

be + past participle · calm, professional, solution-focused

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"The room was double-booked by mistake."

"Your refund has been processed."

"The problem is being handled right now."

Who does the action in these sentences? Where is the focus -- on the person, or on the problem and the solution?

FormUseExample
be + past participleThe action is done TO the subjectThe room was booked.
Tense comes from 'be'is / was / has been / will be + p.p.It has been resolved.
by + agent (optional)Name who did it only if it mattersIt was cancelled by the hotel.

Passive voice = be + past participle. Match the tense to 'be' (is / was / has been / will be). It keeps the focus on the solution, not on blame.

Language Focus

Complete the Response

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"The problem is handling right now."

✓ RIGHT

The problem is being handled right now.

✗ WRONG

"Your refund has processed."

✓ RIGHT

Your refund has been processed.

In the passive voice, always use be + past participle. For a present action in progress use 'is being handled' (not 'is handling'); for present perfect use 'has been processed' (not 'has processed').

Chapter 5: Voices

Voices in a Difficult Moment

Listen to a real complaint, then produce the language of recovery yourself.

Register & Tone

Sound Professional

Tap to see the polished, professional version

Instead of: "That is not my problem."
Say: I completely understand, and I will make sure it is resolved.
Instead of: "You booked the wrong dates."
Say: It looks like there may have been a mix-up with the dates -- let me fix it.
Instead of: "Please calm down."
Say: I hear you, and I am going to sort this out for you right now.
Instead of: "We cannot do anything."
Say: Here is what can be done for you today.
Dialogue

The Overbooked Hotel

L
Andrea, I need to make a formal complaint. My group arrived, but the hotel was overbooked and three rooms were not ready.
A
I am so sorry, Lukas. Please accept my apology. This should never have happened, and it is being resolved as we speak.
L
The guests are very upset. This is a serious inconvenience after a ten-hour flight.
A
I completely understand, and I want to reassure you. Three equivalent rooms have already been arranged at a hotel nearby, and the transfers will be covered by us.
L
That is a relief. And the rooms were promised weeks ago, so I hope this will not happen again.
A
You have my word. The issue has been escalated to the hotel manager, and as a goodwill gesture, the first night will be fully refunded.
L
Thank you, Andrea. Honestly, this is exactly why I keep working with you.
A
I appreciate that. Everything I promised will be followed through today, and you will receive written confirmation within the hour.

The Complaint

A Guest Review

Rating: 2 out of 5 stars
Guest: M. Ferreira -- group traveller
Stay: Partner hotel, three nights

We were really looking forward to this trip, but the arrival was a disaster. The hotel was overbooked, and our three rooms were not ready. After a ten-hour flight, we waited two hours in the lobby with no explanation and no apology.

To be fair, the agency handled it far better than the hotel. The next morning we were contacted by Andrea from Escape Turismo. She apologized properly, and by lunchtime new rooms had been arranged at a nicer hotel. Our transfers were covered, and the first night was refunded.

So two stars for the hotel, but honestly four for the agency. The problem was resolved quickly and professionally. If the booking is made through Escape Turismo again, I would feel reassured -- I just hope the hotel itself does better next time.
