Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- How Often (Adverbs of Frequency)

GRAMMAR

Para dizer com que frequência você faz algo, use always, usually, often, sometimes, never. A palavra vem ANTES do verbo. / To say how often you do something, use always, usually, often, sometimes, never. The word goes BEFORE the verb.

Word / PalavraHow much / QuantoExample / Exemplo
always100% / sempreI always relax on Sunday.
usuallyquase sempreI usually sleep in.
oftenmuitas vezesI often go out.
sometimesàs vezesI sometimes go for a walk.
never0% / nuncaI never work on Sunday.

Key point / Ponto-chave: a palavra de frequência vem antes do verbo (I always relax). Para perguntar, use How often do you + verbo? / The frequency word goes before the verb; to ask, use How often do you + verb?
