Chapter 3 -- The Code

Grammar Discovery

How often? -- I always relax on Sunday.

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

How do we say how often we do something?

"I always relax on Sunday."

"I usually sleep in on Saturday."

"I sometimes go for a walk."

"How often do you go out?"

Grammar Rule

How Often

WordHow much?Example
always100% of the timeI always relax on Sunday.
usuallymost of the timeI usually sleep in.
oftenmany timesI often go out.
sometimesnot very muchI sometimes watch a movie.
never0% of the timeI never work on Sunday.

Key idea: the word goes before the verb: I always relax. To ask, use How often do you + verb?

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I relax always on Sunday."
"I always relax on Sunday."

The frequency word (always, usually, never) goes before the verb.
