Chapter 3: This, That, These, Those

Pointing at Things

"This box here / those scarves over there"

Discover the Code

Listen and Find the Pattern

"This box here is handmade."

"That hat over there is too big."

"These postcards here are cheap."

"Those scarves over there are a famous brand."

WordNear or far?One or many?Example
thisnearonethis box
thatfaronethat hat
thesenearmanythese postcards
thosefarmanythose scarves

Use this and these for things NEAR you. Use that and those for things FAR from you. Use this and that for ONE thing, and these and those for MANY things.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"How much is this scarves?"

✓ RIGHT

"How much are these scarves?"

✗ WRONG

"I like those box over there."

✓ RIGHT

"I like that box over there."

Chapter 4: At the Souvenir Shop

Choosing a Gift

Roberto buys a souvenir at the shop

Dialogue

Roberto Buys a Gift

S
Good afternoon. Can I help you find a souvenir or a gift today?
R
Yes, please. This box here is beautiful. Is it handmade? And how much are those scarves over there?
S
Yes, this box is handmade. Those scarves are a famous local brand. Would you like to try on that hat too?
R
The hat is nice, but it does not fit. Do you have a bigger size? And is there a discount if I buy two?
S
Of course. If you buy two, you get a small discount. I can wrap this box as a gift for free.
R
Perfect. I will take this box and those scarves. Here is fifty euros. How much change do I get?
Listening

A Tour of the Shop

Listen first. Sound first, no text.

0:000:00
1. What is special about the small box on the counter?
It is handmade.
2. When do you get a discount?
When you buy two scarves.
3. Does the shop wrap gifts?
Yes, they wrap any gift for free.
Comprehension

About the Gift

1. Is the box handmade?
Yes, the box is handmade.
2. Why does Roberto not buy the hat?
Because it does not fit; he needs a bigger size.
3. What does the shopkeeper offer to do for free?
To wrap the box as a gift.
Listening 2

Roberto's Souvenir Tips

A reflection. Sound first, no text.

0:000:00
1. What problem did Roberto have the first time?
He bought a big gift that did not fit in his suitcase.
2. What three things does he always ask now?
If there is a discount, if they can wrap the gift, and how much change he gets.
3. Does he prefer a famous brand or handmade things?
He prefers handmade things, because they tell the story of the place.
