Stage 1.1: Souvenir Shopping Vocabulary

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Estas palavras ajudam voce a comprar souvenirs e presentes numa viagem.

Souvenir -- lembranca (de viagem)
"I bought a small souvenir from Paris."
Gift -- presente
"This box is a perfect gift for my wife."
Size -- tamanho
"Do you have this hat in a bigger size?"
To fit -- servir (no tamanho)
"This jacket does not fit me; it is too small."
To try on -- experimentar (roupa)
"Can I try on this hat, please?"
Discount -- desconto
"If you buy two, you get a discount."
Change -- troco
"Here is your change: five euros."
To wrap -- embrulhar
"Could you wrap this gift, please?"
Brand -- marca
"These scarves are a famous local brand."
Handmade -- feito a mao
"This box is handmade and very special."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia a situacao e responda as perguntas.

Roberto walks into a souvenir shop on his last day. "This box here is beautiful. Is it handmade?" he asks. The shopkeeper says, "Yes, and those scarves over there are a famous brand." Roberto wants to try on a hat, but it does not fit, so he asks for a bigger size. He asks for a discount on two scarves, and the shopkeeper offers to wrap the gift for free. At the end, Roberto pays and gets his change.

1. Which word does Roberto use for the box near him?
A This.
B Those.
C That.
2. Why does Roberto not buy the hat?
A It is too expensive.
B It does not fit; he needs a bigger size.
C It is not handmade.
3. What does the shopkeeper offer to do for free?
A Give him a free hat.
B Deliver the gift to his hotel.
C Wrap the gift.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- this, that, these & those

Grammar

Usamos "this" e "these" para coisas PERTO de nos, e "that" e "those" para coisas LONGE. Usamos "this" e "that" para UMA coisa (singular), e "these" e "those" para VARIAS coisas (plural). Lembre-se: com o plural, o verbo "to be" tambem muda ("these scarves ARE"). / We use "this" and "these" for things NEAR us, and "that" and "those" for things FAR from us. We use "this" and "that" for ONE thing (singular), and "these" and "those" for MANY things (plural). Remember: with the plural, the verb "to be" changes too ("these scarves ARE").

Palavra / WordEnglishPortugues
this (near, one)This box is handmade.Esta caixa e feita a mao.
that (far, one)That hat is too big.Aquele chapeu e grande demais.
these (near, many)These postcards are cheap.Estes cartoes-postais sao baratos.
those (far, many)Those scarves are a famous brand.Aqueles cachecois sao de uma marca famosa.
question formHow much is this? / How much are these?Quanto e isto? / Quanto sao estes?

Stage 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao.

You point at one box near you and ask the price. What do you say?
A "How much is this box?"
B "How much are this box?"
C "How much are those box?"
Which sentence is correct?
A "I like this scarves over there."
B "I like those scarves over there."
C "I like that scarves over there."
You want to put on a jacket to see if it is right for you. What do you say?
A "Can I wrap this jacket?"
B "Can I try on this jacket?"
C "Is there any jacket?"
