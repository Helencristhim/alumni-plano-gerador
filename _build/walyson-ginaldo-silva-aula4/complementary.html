Lesson 4 -- Meeting the Team

Series
The Office (US) -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

A workplace comedy where colleagues introduce themselves, describe their roles, and talk about what they are doing at work. Listen for introductions and the present continuous.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down five present continuous sentences you hear (for example, "he is working on..."). Then introduce one character: their role and what they are doing.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Introducing Your Team"

A short, clear episode on how to introduce colleagues, describe their roles, and explain what each person is in charge of. Perfect for hearing the team vocabulary and the present continuous used naturally.

Record a 90-second audio introducing your team. Name three people, give each role and department, and say what each one is working on, using the present continuous.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "The Present Continuous"

A focused lesson on how the present continuous is formed (to be + verb-ing) and when to use it for actions happening now. Directly reinforces today's grammar discovery.

After watching, write six true sentences about your team right now, one for each person: who is leading, handling, preparing, setting up, reaching out, and launching. Read them aloud with the correct stress.
