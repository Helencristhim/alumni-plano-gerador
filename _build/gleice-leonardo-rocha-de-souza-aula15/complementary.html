Lesson 15 -- Sharing Good News (Present Perfect)

Series / Business
The Office (US) -- a company announcement scene

A short scene shows a manager announcing results to the team. Listen for the present perfect: "we have reached", "we have done", "the team has worked", and words like result, goal, and success.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down 3 present perfect sentences that you hear (for example, "We have reached our target"). Then announce one real result from your team out loud.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- "Sharing Company News"

The speakers announce results and achievements: how to say what the team has done this year, how to share a milestone, and how to say you are proud of the success.

Choose one short episode. Note 3 present perfect phrases the speaker uses to announce good news. Compare them with the announcement from Gleice and David in class.

YouTube / BBC
BBC Learning English -- "Present Perfect: have and has"

A short, clear lesson on the present perfect: have / has + past participle (we have launched, the team has worked), regular verbs with -ed, and irregular forms (do becomes done, win becomes won).

After watching, write five present perfect sentences about results at work (for example, "We have achieved our main goal"). Then record a one-minute audio: announce your team's good news.
