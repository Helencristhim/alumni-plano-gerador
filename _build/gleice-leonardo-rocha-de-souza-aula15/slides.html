Chapter 3 -- We Have Done It

We Have Done It

Present perfect -- we have launched, we have reached

Listen and Notice

Find the Pattern

Look at the orange words. How do we announce what we have done?

"We have launched two new products this year."

"We have reached our sales target."

"The team has worked very hard."

"She has achieved her main goal."

The Rule

Have / Has + Past Participle

SubjectPresent perfect
I / you / we / theyhave launched, have reached
he / she / the teamhas worked, has achieved
Irregular verbshave done, have won, have grown
Negative / questionwe have not reached / have we reached?

Key idea: to announce a recent result, use have or has + the past participle. Use have with I, you, we, they and has with he, she, the team. Regular verbs add -ed (launched, reached); some verbs are irregular (done, won, grown).

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"We reached our target, and the team have work hard. We have do it."
"We have reached our target, and the team has worked hard. We have done it."

Always use have / has before the verb. Use has with he, she, the team. The verb is the past participle: worked, done (not "do").

Practice

Say the Present Perfect

"We _____have launched two new products." (launch)
"The team _____has reached the sales target." (reach)
"She _____has achieved her main goal." (achieve)
"We _____have done it together." (do)
