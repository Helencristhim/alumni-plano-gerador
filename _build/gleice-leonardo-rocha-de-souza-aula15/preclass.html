Sharing Good News -- We Have Done It (Present Perfect)

Announce results and achievements. New vocabulary: announce, result, goal, target, achieve, success, milestone, proud. Use the present perfect to share recent good news: have / has + past participle (we have launched, we have reached, the team has worked, we have done it). Use have with I, you, we, they and has with he, she, the team.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Perfect (have / has + past participle)

GRAMMAR

Para anunciar um resultado recente (uma boa notícia verdadeira agora), use have / has + partícipio passado. Com I, you, we, they use have (we have launched, we have reached). Com he, she, the team use has (she has achieved, the team has worked). Verbos regulares: o partícipio termina em -ed (launch → launched, reach → reached, achieve → achieved). Verbos irregulares: do → done, win → won, grow → grown. Negativo: we have not reached. Pergunta: have we reached the target? Nunca esqueça o have / has e nunca diga "we have do". / To announce a recent result, use have / has + past participle. Use have with I, you, we, they and has with he, she, the team. Regular verbs add -ed; some verbs are irregular (done, won, grown).

Subject / typePresent perfect
I / you / we / theyWe have launched two new products.
he / she / the teamThe team has reached the sales target.
Irregular verbsWe have done it. We have won an award.
Negative / questionWe have not reached it yet. / Have we achieved it?
Regra de ourosempre use have ou has antes do verbo, no partícipio: diga we have reached (não "we reached") e we have done (não "we have do").
