Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o plano e responda as perguntas. / Read the plan and answer.

Anna writes a note: "Next week is going to be busy. On Monday, I am going to call our partner. On Wednesday, we are going to have a meeting at two o'clock. I am going to send a calendar invite today. I am not going to work on the weekend."

1. What is Anna going to do on Monday?
A Have a meeting.
B Call the partner.
C Send the contract.
2. When is the meeting?
A On Wednesday, at two o'clock.
B On the weekend.
C On Monday morning.
3. What is Anna NOT going to do?
A Send an invite.
B Work on the weekend.
C Call the partner.

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Going To (Future)

Grammar

Para planos futuros, use am / is / are + going to + verbo. O verbo nao muda. / For future plans, use am/is/are + going to + verb. The verb does not change.

FormaEnglishPortugues
Afirmativo (I)I am going to sendeu vou enviar
Afirmativo (she)she is going to callela vai ligar
Afirmativo (we)we are going to meetnos vamos nos reunir
NegativoI am not going to workeu nao vou trabalhar
PerguntaAre you going to join?voce vai participar?

Etapa 4: Situational Quiz

Quiz

Escolha a melhor resposta para cada situacao.

You want to plan a meeting next week. What do you ask?
A "Did you meet yesterday?"
B "Are you free next week?"
C "Where is the report?"
A partner asks what you will do tomorrow. What do you say?
A "I am going to send the report."
B "I going send the report."
C "I sended the report."
You booked a meeting. How do you confirm the time?
A "Maybe sometime."
B "We are going to meet on Wednesday at two o'clock."
C "I am busy."
