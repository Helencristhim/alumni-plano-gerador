Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. Repare nas palavras de frequência: always, usually, sometimes, never.

Ana has a simple routine. She always wakes up early, at six. She usually walks in the park before breakfast. She never checks her phone before her coffee. It is her golden rule. At work, she sometimes has lunch with her sister. In the evening, she cooks dinner and rarely watches TV. She is never late for work, because she always sleeps eight hours.

What does Ana always do?
A She always watches TV at night.
B She always wakes up early, at six.
C She always checks her phone before coffee.

A She never checks her phone.
B She never walks in the park.
C She never has breakfast.
Why is Ana never late for work?
A Because she works at home.
B Because she rarely watches TV.
C Because she always sleeps eight hours.

Grammar Tip — Adverbs of Frequency

Grammar

As palavras de frequência dizem COM QUE FREQUÊNCIA você faz algo. Veja a escada:

Palavra Frequência Tradução Exemplo
always100%sempreI always wake up at seven.
usually90%geralmenteI usually have lunch at one.
often70%frequentementeHe often cooks dinner.
sometimes50%às vezesWe sometimes eat out.
rarely10%raramenteI rarely go out at night.
never0%nuncaI am never late.

Regra de posição: a palavra de frequência vem ANTES do verbo principal. (Eu sempre bebo café = I always drink coffee — nunca "I drink coffee always".)

Exceção — verbo to be: com am/is/are, ela vem DEPOIS. (Ela nunca está atrasada = She is never late.)

Exemplo: "I always wake up early. I am never late. My family usually has dinner together."
