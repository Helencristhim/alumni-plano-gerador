Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas.

Hello! Let me tell you about my daily routine. I always wake up at six o'clock. I usually take a shower, and then I have breakfast. I often drink coffee in the morning. I usually have lunch at noon. In the evening, I sometimes cook dinner at home. I never go to bed late, because I am always tired at night. My routine is calm and simple. I like my mornings.

1. The text says "I always wake up at six." Where is the word "always"?
A After the verb "wake up".
B Before the main verb "wake up".
C At the end of the sentence.
2. "I am always tired at night." With the verb to be, the adverb goes...
A After the verb to be (am).
B Before the verb to be (am).
C It does not matter.
3. Which sentence is correct?
A "She has usually breakfast."
B "She usually has breakfast."
C "She breakfast usually has."

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Adverbs of Frequency

GRAMMAR

Como dizer com que frequência você faz algo, e onde colocar o advérbio. / How to say how often you do something, and where the adverb goes.

AdverbFrequênciaPosition / Example
alwayssempre (100%)I always wake up early. (antes do verbo)
usually / oftengeralmente / frequentementeShe usually has lunch at noon.
sometimesàs vezes (~30%)We sometimes cook dinner.
nevernunca (0%)He never goes to bed late.
Com "to be"O advérbio vem DEPOIS: I am always tired. (não "I always am")
