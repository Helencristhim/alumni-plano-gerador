Chapter 3

The Frequency Code

always, usually, often, sometimes, never

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I always wake up at six."

"She usually has breakfast at home."

"We sometimes cook dinner."

"He never goes to bed late."

AdverbHow oftenPosition
always100%I always wake up early. (before the verb)
usually / often~80% / ~60%She usually has lunch at noon.
sometimes~30%We sometimes cook dinner.
never0%He never goes to bed late.
With "to be"The adverb goes AFTER: I am always tired. (not "I always am")
Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"I wake up always early."

✓ RIGHT

"I always wake up early."

✗ WRONG

"She always is tired."

✓ RIGHT

"She is always tired."

✗ WRONG

"He never go to bed late."

✓ RIGHT

"He never goes to bed late."

The frequency adverb comes before the main verb, but after the verb to be. And remember: with he, she, and it, the verb takes an s.

Talking About Our Day

D

D
I always wake up early, at five thirty. Then I have breakfast. Do you have breakfast at home?
E
Yes, I always have breakfast at home. I often drink coffee.
D
Nice! What do you do in the evening?
E
I sometimes cook dinner, and I never go to bed late.
D
That is a good routine. I usually go to bed at eleven o'clock.
E
My routine is calm. I like my mornings.
