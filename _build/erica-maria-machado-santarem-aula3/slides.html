Chapter 3: The Code

How Often?

always · usually · often · sometimes · rarely · never

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

"I always wake up at seven."

"She usually walks before breakfast."

"We sometimes have dinner out."

"I am never late for work."

WordHow often?Example
always100%I always wake up at seven.
usually / often90% / 70%She usually walks. He often cooks.
sometimes / rarely50% / 10%We sometimes eat out. I rarely go out at night.
never0%I am never late.

Position: BEFORE the main verb (I always wake up) · AFTER am/is/are (I am never late).

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

✗ WRONG

"I drink coffee always."

✓ RIGHT

"I always drink coffee."

✗ WRONG

"She never is late."

✓ RIGHT

"She is never late."

Dialogue

What Is Your Routine?

D
Érica! Join me for lunch? I always eat at this time.
E
Thank you, David! I usually have lunch at one, but today I am early.
D
Tell me your secret. You always have so much energy! What is your routine?
E
I wake up at seven. I have breakfast — coffee and fruit — and I start work at eight.
D
At seven! I never wake up before seven thirty. My routine starts late.
E
And I always sleep early, at ten thirty. Sleep is important, David!
D
I usually sleep after midnight. Maybe that is my problem!
E
Then here is a challenge for you: sleep early tonight!
D
Ha! Challenge accepted! And the weekend? Do you relax?
E
Yes! On Sundays my family usually has lunch together. I love it.
