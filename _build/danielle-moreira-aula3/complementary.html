Series
Succession — Season 1, Episode 1: “Celebration” (HBO Max)

The whole series is a course in executive register in English: people disagreeing with senior leaders inside the room, without apologizing for disagreeing. Connection to Lesson 3: notice HOW they open the sentence before they take down someone else’s idea — it is exactly the “Building on that, my take on this is different” you rehearsed here.

Tip: watch with English subtitles. Write down THREE push-back sentences — disagreeing without attacking — and one sentence that opens a proposal.

Podcast
HBR IdeaCast — “Supercharge Your One-on-One Meetings” (Steven Rogelberg)

The researcher who studied thousands of 1:1s explains, in executive English and at native speed, how you prepare, run and close an individual meeting — and why most of them are a status report in disguise. Connection to Lesson 3: this is exactly the meeting you will run with each Canadian leader from August on, with the vocabulary (milestone, deliverable, accountability, buy-in) in native context.

Tip: listen once, with no pauses. Then summarize the episode out loud in sixty seconds, opening with “Let me cut to the chase” and using at least one mixed conditional. Record it and listen back.
