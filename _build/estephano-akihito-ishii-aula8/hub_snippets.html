Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Meet -- to see and talk to a person for the first time
"Nice to meet you!" (conhecer)
Nice -- kind and friendly
"She is a nice person." (legal / simpático)
Friend -- a person you like and know well
"This is my friend." (amigo)
Hometown -- the city where you are from
"My hometown is Sao Paulo." (cidade natal)
Family -- your mother, father, brothers, and sisters
"My family is big." (família)
Favorite -- the one you like the most
"What is your favorite color?" (favorito)
Spell -- to say the letters of a word
"Can you spell your name?" (soletrar)
Pet -- an animal you keep at home
"I have a pet dog." (animal de estimação)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

Estephano meets a new friend at a party. She says: "Hi! Nice to meet you. What is your name?" Estephano answers: "I am Estephano. Where are you from?" She says: "I am from Curitiba. What do you like doing?" Estephano says: "I like playing games. This is my family, and this is my pet." They are good friends now.

1. What does the friend say first?
A "Nice to meet you. What is your name?"
B "Where do you work?"
C "How much is it?"
2. Where is the friend from?
A Sao Paulo.
B Curitiba.
C Rio.
3. Which question asks about a city?
A "What is your name?"
B "Where are you from?"
C "What do you like doing?"

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Questions to Meet Someone

GRAMMAR

Question / PerguntaStructure / EstruturaAnswer / Resposta
Name / NomeWhat is your name?My name is Estephano.
Origin / OrigemWhere are you from?I am from Sao Paulo.
Work / TrabalhoWhat do you do?I am a student.
Likes / GostosCom to be (am/is/are) para nome e origem; com do para ações: "What do you like doing?" → "I like playing games."
