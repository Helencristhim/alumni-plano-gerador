Lesson 19 -- Following Up: Thank You & Next Steps

Series
The Office (US) -- any "thank you" or follow-up scene

Characters thank clients and arrange next steps. You hear polite follow-up phrases like "Thank you for your business", "It was a pleasure", and "Let's arrange a meeting".

Watch with English subtitles. Write down one thank-you phrase and one phrase that arranges a next step.

Podcast
Speak English Now -- "Following Up and Saying Thank You"

A slow, clear podcast about the follow-up: how to thank a client, make a promise with "I will...", arrange the next meeting, and end a message politely.

Repeat each phrase out loud. Then record a short thank-you message for a client, using "I look forward to working with you".

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Polite follow-up emails and phrases in English"

A short, practical lesson on today's language: thanking a client, making promises with "I will...", arranging next steps with "Let's arrange...", and ending with "Best regards".

After watching, write a five-line follow-up: thank the client, say it was a pleasure, promise to send details, arrange a meeting, and sign off. Then read it aloud.
