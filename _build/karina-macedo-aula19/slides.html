Chapter 3 -- Thank and Arrange

Grammar Discovery

Thank you for your business -- Let's arrange

Grammar Discovery

Listen and Notice

How do we thank a client and arrange the next steps?

"Thank you for your business, Robert."

"It was a pleasure to work with you."

"I will send you the details."

"Let's arrange the next meeting."

Grammar Rule

Thanking & Arranging

PhraseWhen we use it
Thank you for your business.Thank a client
It was a pleasure...Say it was good (past)
I will send you...Make a promise (will + verb)
Let's arrange...Plan the next step

Key idea: to thank say Thank you for your business and It was a pleasure to work with you. For a promise use I will + verb: I will send you the details. To plan say Let's arrange the next meeting. End warmly with I look forward to working with you (working, not work).

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I look forward to work with you, and I will to send the details."
"I look forward to working with you, and I will send the details."

After look forward to use -ing (working). After will use the base verb (will send, not "will to send").
