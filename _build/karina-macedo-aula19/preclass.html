Following Up -- Thank You & Next Steps

Follow up after the deal. Thank a client ("Thank you for your business"), make a promise ("I will send you the details"), and arrange the next steps ("Let's arrange the next meeting"). New vocabulary: grateful, pleasure, arrange, attend, update, progress, contact, regards.

"Please contact me if you have questions." (Contatar)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Thanking & Arranging

GRAMMAR

Como agradecer e combinar os próximos passos. / How to thank and arrange next steps.

Phrase / FraseMeaning / SentidoExample / Exemplo
Thank you for your business.Agradecer ao cliente / Thank a clientThank you for your business, Robert.
It was a pleasure...Dizer que foi bom (passado) / Say it was goodIt was a pleasure to work with you.
I will send you...Fazer uma promessa (will + verbo) / Make a promiseI will send you the details.
Let's arrange...Combinar o próximo passo / Plan the next stepLet's arrange the next meeting.

Key point / Ponto-chave: para uma promessa use will + verbo base: I will send (não "I will to send"). Para terminar de forma calorosa, diga I look forward to working with you (com -ing: working, não "work").
