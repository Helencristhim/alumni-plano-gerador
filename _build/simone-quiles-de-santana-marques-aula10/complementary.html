Lesson 10 -- Travel English I

Series
Up in the Air (2009) -- film

A business traveler who lives in airports and hotels. Watch the check-in, security, boarding and hotel scenes, and listen for the polite requests and travel words you learned: boarding pass, gate, aisle seat, check-in, front desk.

Watch the first 30 minutes with English subtitles. Write down 5 travel words you hear and 3 polite requests with Could I or Could you.

Podcast
The Travel English Podcast -- "At the Airport & Hotel"

Short, practical episodes for travelers: how to check in, ask for an aisle seat, deal with luggage and customs, and confirm a reservation at the front desk. Listen for how speakers make polite requests with Could I, Could you and I would like.

Note 3 phrases the speakers use to ask for things. Compare them with how Simone asks the agent in the check-in dialogue.

YouTube / BBC
BBC Learning English -- "Travel English" / "Polite requests"

A short, practical lesson on making polite requests when you travel: how Could I and Could you make a request sound polite, and the base verb form that comes after them. Directly useful for the business trips you take for closings and signings.

After watching, write five polite requests for a real trip, then record yourself asking them using at least four travel words from today.
