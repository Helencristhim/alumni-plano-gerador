The Request Code

Polite Requests

TypeStructureExample
Ask for yourselfCould I + base verb"Could I have an aisle seat?"
Ask the other personCould you + base verb"Could you check my luggage?"
Polite wishI would like + to + verb / + noun"I would like to confirm my reservation."
Extra politeWould it be possible to + verb"Would it be possible to get a late check-out?"

Key idea: after Could I and Could you, use the BASE verb -- no to, no -ing, no -s. "Could I have a seat?" not "Could I to have a seat?"

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"Could I to have an aisle seat? ... Could you giving me my boarding pass?"
"Could I have an aisle seat? ... Could you give me my boarding pass?"

After Could I and Could you, use the base verb. Never add to, -ing or -s.
