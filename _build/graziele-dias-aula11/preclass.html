Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto e responda as perguntas. (Read the text and answer.)

I write a short email to Dr. Carter. First, the subject: Meeting Monday. Then the greeting: "Dear Dr. Carter, thank you for the call." Now the message: "I confirm our meeting on Monday at three, because it is important. I am free in the afternoon, but not in the morning. Please reply if you have a question." Finally, the closing: "Best regards, Graziele." I send the email now.

1. How do you start a polite email? (Como comecar um e-mail educado?)
A "Dear Dr. Carter," (Prezado Dr. Carter,)
B "Hey you," (Ei voce,)
C "Bye," (Tchau,)
2. Which word shows a contrast? (Qual palavra mostra contraste?)
A "and" (e)
B "but" (mas)
C "dear" (prezado)
3. How do you finish the email? (Como terminar o e-mail?)
A "The subject is Monday." (O assunto e segunda.)
B "Best regards, Graziele." (Atenciosamente, Graziele.)
C "I have a question." (Eu tenho uma pergunta.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- And / But / Because

Grammar

Como unir ideias num e-mail. / How to join ideas in an email.

Word / PalavraUse / Uso
and (e)soma uma ideia (+) -- "Thank you, and see you soon."
but (mas)mostra contraste (diferente) -- "I am free on Monday, but I am busy on Tuesday."
because (porque)da o motivo (why) -- "I confirm the meeting because it is important."
Email partsDear ... (greeting) + message + Best regards (closing)

and = e (soma) · but = mas (diferente) · because = porque (motivo). / Comece com "Dear..." e termine com "Best regards,".
