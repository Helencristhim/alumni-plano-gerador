Chapter 3: And / But / Because

Join the Ideas

and = + · but = different · because = why

Linking Words

Three Little Words

"Thank you, and see you soon." · adds

"I am free on Monday, but busy on Tuesday." · contrast

"I confirm the meeting because it is important." · reason

"I send the email because the meeting is on Monday."

and = e · but = mas · because = porque

Discover the Pattern

Join Ideas in an Email

"Thank you, and see you soon."

"I am free on Monday, but I am busy on Tuesday."

"I confirm the meeting because it is important."

"I reply because I have a question."

WordIt means
and+ (adds an idea) — "Thank you, and see you soon."
butdifferent (contrast) — "I am free on Monday, but busy on Tuesday."
becausewhy (reason) — "I confirm because it is important."

and = e (soma) · but = mas (diferente) · because = porque (motivo)

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"I am free on Monday. Busy on Tuesday."

✓ RIGHT

"I am free on Monday, but I am busy on Tuesday."

✗ WRONG

"I write because thank you."

✓ RIGHT

"Thank you, and see you soon."
