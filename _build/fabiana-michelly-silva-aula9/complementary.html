Lesson 9 -- Habits, Changes, Adaptations

Series
The Morning Show -- Season 1, Episode 4: "That Woman"

A newsroom in transformation. Watch how characters describe "how things used to be" versus "how they are now" as the organization evolves under pressure. The language of institutional change mirrors supply chain evolution.

Note every time a character says "used to", "would", or "got used to". How do they describe the old culture vs the new one?

Podcast
Supply Chain Now -- "Digital Transformation: Before and After"

Industry leaders share how their supply chains evolved from manual to digital. Listen for vocabulary like streamline, automate, transition, and legacy system in real professional contexts.

Write down 5 "before and after" comparisons the speakers make. Rewrite each using "used to" and "now".

YouTube
Bloomberg -- "The Evolution of Financial Technology"

A 10-minute documentary on how Bloomberg's technology has evolved over decades. Hear familiar vocabulary and understand the company's transformation journey from the inside.

After watching, describe 3 changes using: "Bloomberg used to...", "They would...", and "Now they are used to..."
