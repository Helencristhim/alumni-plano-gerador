Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o dia da Anna no escritorio de Sao Paulo e responda as perguntas. / Read Anna's day at the office and answer.

Anna has a full day at the Sao Paulo office. In the morning she meets the team and a new colleague. She asks: "Where is the meeting room?" In the meeting she gives her opinion: "The total is bigger this year." Her colleague says: "I agree." At lunch she reads the menu and says: "I would like the fish, please." It is a full day, all in English.

1. Who does Anna meet in the morning?
A The team and a new colleague.
B A waiter.
C A client only.
2. What is Anna's opinion about the total?
A It is smaller.
B It is bigger this year.
C It is the same.
3. How does Anna order the fish?
A "I would like the fish, please."
B "I want the fish."
C "Give me the fish."

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Your Toolkit

Grammar

Voce tem quatro ferramentas das aulas 11 a 14. Use question words (What/Where) com do/are para perguntar, I think / I agree para opinar, o comparativo -er (bigger, higher) para os numeros, e I would like / Can I have...? com please para pedir. / You have four toolkits: questions, opinions, comparatives, and polite orders.

ToolkitExamplePortugues
Questions (L11)Where do you work?Onde voce trabalha?
Opinions (L12)I think... / I agree.Eu acho... / Eu concordo.
Numbers (L13)The total is bigger.O total e maior.
Orders (L14)I would like the fish, please.Eu gostaria do peixe, por favor.

Etapa 4: Situational Quiz

A "Where is the team, please?"
B "Team where?"
C "You team?"
In the meeting, you want to share what you think. How do you start?
A "I want this."
B "In my opinion, the total is bigger."
C "Total more big."

A "Give me a water."
B "Can I have a water, please?"
