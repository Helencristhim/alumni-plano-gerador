Lesson 6 -- Review & Consolidation 1

Series
Suits -- Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

Watch how the characters greet new contacts, mingle at work events, and exchange business cards. Notice the Wh- questions they use to start a conversation: "What do you do?" and "Where are you from?"

Write down every Wh- question you hear. Pause and repeat each one, then ask it out loud to an imaginary new contact.

Podcast
Business English Pod -- Episode: "Networking and Making Connections"

Practical tips for a networking event: how to greet a host, mingle with guests, ask good questions, and follow up after you meet. Listen for the new words: greet, host, guest, mingle, and connection.

Note 3 questions the host suggests for meeting new people. Practice each one as a Wh- question with the helper verb.

YouTube
BBC Learning English -- "Networking: How to Start a Conversation"

A short, clear lesson on networking small talk: how to greet someone, ask Wh- questions, and exchange contact details. Perfect for reviewing all of Block 1 in a real work situation.

After watching, record a 60-second networking conversation out loud. Use at least three new words and two Wh- questions.
