What Do You Do? -- Your Professional World in Natural English

Key words: to oversee, to ensure, to coordinate, to liaise with, to be in charge of, to deal with, to handle, to report to, supply chain, cold chain, last-mile delivery, time-sensitive. Structures: present simple for facts, roles and routines vs present continuous for projects in progress right now.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Ouça cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Toque em Listen para escutar.

To oversee -- to be in charge of work and watch over it (supervisionar)
"I oversee operations for a pharmaceutical logistics company."
To ensure -- to make certain that something happens (garantir)
"We ensure that every medicine arrives safely and on time."
To coordinate -- to organize people and tasks to work together (coordenar)
"I coordinate teams in different cities every single day."
To liaise with -- to communicate and cooperate with a partner or team (fazer a ponte com)
"I liaise with suppliers and drivers across the country."
To be in charge of -- to have control and responsibility for something (ser responsavel por)
"I am in charge of the whole distribution network."
To deal with -- to take action on a task or a problem (lidar com)
"My team deals with delays before they become problems."
To handle -- to manage or take care of a task (dar conta de, cuidar de)
"One team handles customs, another handles the routes."
To report to -- to answer to a boss or a manager (se reportar a)
"Four managers report to me, and I report to the CEO."
Supply chain -- the whole path of a product from source to customer (cadeia de suprimentos)
"A supply chain has hundreds of moving parts."
Cold chain -- temperature-controlled delivery for sensitive products (cadeia do frio)
"The cold chain keeps vaccines at the right temperature."
Last-mile delivery -- the final step of delivery, to the customer's door (entrega de ultima milha)
"Last-mile delivery is the hardest and most expensive part."
Time-sensitive -- needing to be done within a strict time limit (sensivel ao tempo, com prazo apertado)
"Medicine is time-sensitive; a delay can ruin the product."

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Leia o texto e responda às perguntas.

Marcelo is the Operations Director at a pharmaceutical logistics company. Every day, he oversees a large team and makes sure that medicines travel safely across the country. His team handles the routes, deals with customs, and ensures that the cold chain never breaks. "I coordinate people in five different cities," he says, "and I liaise with suppliers and drivers all day long." But this month is special. Right now, the company is expanding into three new states, so Marcelo is building a whole new last-mile delivery team. "Normally I run the routine," he says, "but at the moment I am leading the biggest project of my career."

1. Por que dizemos "Every day, he oversees a large team"?
A Porque é uma rotina e um fato permanente (present simple).
B Porque ele está supervisionando o time neste exato momento.
C Porque ele supervisionou o time uma vez no passado.
2. "Right now, the company is expanding into three new states" usa o present continuous porque:
A É uma verdade permanente sobre a empresa.
B É um projeto em andamento agora, neste período.
C É algo que acontece todos os dias, sem mudar.
3. Which sentence about his routine is correct?
A He is coordinating people in five cities since always.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Present Simple vs Present Continuous

GRAMMAR

Como falar do seu trabalho com o tempo verbal certo (explicação em inglês e português).

FormUse / UsoExample
Present Simple
I / we + verb (he/she + s)		Fatos, papéis e rotinas sempre verdadeiros. Facts, roles and routines that are always true (every day, usually).I oversee operations.
Present Continuous
am / is / are + verb-ing		Projetos em andamento agora, neste período. Projects in progress right now (now, currently, this month).We are expanding right now.
Time wordsevery day / usually / normally → present simple · right now / at the moment / currently / this month → present continuous
