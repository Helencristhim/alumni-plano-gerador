Aula 2 -- Your Career Journey

Series
The Founder (2016 film)

The story of how a man built a global company step by step. Listen to how the characters describe their past decisions, first jobs, and turning points, all in the past simple. Direct connection to Lesson 2: narrating a career journey and the milestones that shaped it.

Tip: note 5 past-tense verbs (regular and irregular) the characters use to describe how the business grew.

Podcast
How I Built This (NPR) -- any founder episode

Founders tell the story of how they started and grew their companies. Every episode is a career journey in the past simple: where they studied, their first jobs, their setbacks, and their milestones. Direct connection to Lesson 2: real narratives of professional growth.

Tip: pick one founder. Write a 5-step timeline of their career using time markers (then, after that, later).

YouTube
"Talk About Your Career & Work Experience in English" -- Business English Pod

A practical lesson on describing your professional background: education, first jobs, promotions, and achievements, all using the past simple. Connection to Lesson 2: ready-to-use sentence patterns for telling your career story at a congress, exactly what Marcos will do in Washington.

Tip: pause and repeat each example aloud. Then say your own version using your real dates.
