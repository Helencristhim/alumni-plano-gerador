Etapa 1.1: Cartoes de Vocabulario

Vocabulary

Ouca cada palavra e leia o exemplo. Sao verbos no PASSADO. Toque Listen para ouvir. (Listen = Ouvir)

Visited -- visitou/visitei (passado de visit: foi a um lugar para ve-lo)
"Last Monday, I visited a coffee farm." (Na segunda passada, eu visitei uma fazenda de cafe.)
Identified -- identificou/identifiquei (passado de identify: encontrou e nomeou um problema)
"I identified one non-conformity at the farm." (Eu identifiquei uma nao-conformidade na fazenda.)
Completed -- completou/completei (passado de complete: terminou algo 100%)
"I completed the audit checklist." (Eu completei o checklist da auditoria.)
Submitted -- submeteu/enviei (passado de submit: enviou um documento a alguem oficial)
"I submitted the report to the European team." (Eu submeti o relatorio para a equipe europeia.)
Approved -- aprovou (passado de approve: disse sim, esta correto)
"Emma approved the first two farms." (A Emma aprovou as duas primeiras fazendas.)
Rejected -- rejeitou (passado de reject: disse nao, nao esta correto)
"The auditor rejected the old certificate." (O auditor rejeitou o certificado antigo.)

Etapa 1.3: Grammar in Context

Grammar

Leia o texto no passado e responda as perguntas. (Read the past text and answer.)

Last Monday, I visited Fazenda Boa Esperanca. I completed the audit checklist and I checked fifteen criteria. I found one critical problem: the producer did not have a valid certificate. I wrote the corrective action plan and I submitted the report on Tuesday. Emma approved two farms, but she did not approve the third one yet.

1. Which verb is irregular (it does not add -ed)? (Qual verbo e irregular?)
A visited (visit + -ed)
B wrote (write -> wrote). (irregular)
C completed (complete + -ed)
2. Why do we say "did not have" and not "did not had"? (Por que verbo base?)
A After did not we use the base verb (have). (Depois de did not, verbo base.)
B Because "have" is a noun. (Porque have e substantivo.)
C Because it is a question. (Porque e pergunta.)
3. What did Emma do with the third farm? (O que a Emma fez com a terceira fazenda?)
A She approved it. (Ela aprovou.)
B She did not approve it yet. (Ela ainda nao aprovou.)
C She visited it. (Ela visitou.)

Etapa 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Simple Past

Grammar

Como falar do passado: verbos regulares, irregulares, negativa e pergunta. / How to talk about the past.

FormUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
Regularverbo + -edvisit -> visited, complete -> completed (visitei / completei)
Irregularforma especialgo -> went, find -> found, write -> wrote, send -> sent
Negativedid not / didn't + baseThe farm did not send it. (nao enviou -- NAO "sent")
QuestionDid + sujeito + baseDid you submit the report? (Voce enviou o relatorio?)
-ed sons / soundsO -ed tem 3 sons: checked = /t/, issued = /d/, visited = /Id/.
