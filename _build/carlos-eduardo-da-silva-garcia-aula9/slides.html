Chapter 3: Simple Past

I visited. I found.

regular -ed · irregular verbs · did not · Did...?

Discover the Grammar

Listen and Notice

"I visited three farms last week."

"I found one non-conformity."

"The producer did not send the document."

"Did you submit the report?"

FormHowExample
Regularverb + -edvisit -> visited, complete -> completed
Irregularspecial formgo -> went, find -> found, write -> wrote, send -> sent
Negativedid not / didn't + baseThe farm did not send it. (NOT sent)
QuestionDid + subject + baseDid you submit the report?
checked = /t/ issued = /d/ visited = /Id/

The -ed ending has three sounds. After did not and Did, always use the base verb. / Depois de did not e Did, use sempre o verbo base.

Common Mistake

Wrong vs Right

✗ WRONG

"I writed the report."

✓ RIGHT

"I wrote the report."

✗ WRONG

"I didn't submitted the file."

✓ RIGHT

"I didn't submit the file."

Irregular verbs do not add -ed (write -> wrote). After didn't / Did, use the base verb. / Verbo irregular nao usa -ed; depois de didn't use o verbo base.
