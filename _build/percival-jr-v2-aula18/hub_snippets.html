Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- The Passive Voice

GRAMMAR

A voz passiva é was (singular) ou were (plural) + o past participle (completed, analysed, written). Usamos a passiva em relatórios quando a AÇÃO importa mais do que quem fez: "The audit was completed", "The results were analysed". Adicione "by + pessoa" só se o autor for importante. / The passive is was/were + the past participle. Use it in a report when the action matters more than the person.

Active / Ativa (quem fez)Passive / Passiva (a ação importa)
We completed the audit.The audit was completed.
The team analysed the results.The results were analysed.
I wrote the report.The report was written.
We collected the data.The data was collected.

Key point / Ponto-chave: use o past participle, não o passado simples ("was written", não "was wrote"), e nunca esqueça o verbo to be ("the audit was completed", não "the audit completed"). Participios irregulares: write → written, do → done, take → taken. / Use the past participle and never drop the verb to be.
