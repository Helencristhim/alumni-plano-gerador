Aula 18 -- Writing a Short Report

Tema da aula: writing a short report -- structure it with three headings (Objective, Findings, Conclusion), write clear factual sentences, and use the passive voice (the audit was completed, the results were analysed). Reforço fora de aula -- marque como concluído ao terminar.

YouTube
"How to write a business report in English" -- structure & headings

A short, clear video on report structure: how to use the headings Objective, Findings and Conclusion, and how to keep every sentence short and factual.

Dica: anote os três headings e uma frase factual de exemplo para cada um.

Practice
BBC Learning English -- "The passive voice"

Short lessons and quizzes on the passive: was/were + past participle. Perfect for factual report writing -- "the audit was completed", "the results were analysed".

Dica: escreva cinco frases ativas e transforme cada uma na voz passiva.

Podcast
"6 Minute English" -- any episode about data, facts or research

A short, friendly podcast for B1 learners. Listen for factual language and the passive voice used to report findings and figures.

Dica: ouça uma vez sem texto e uma vez com a transcrição. Anote duas frases na voz passiva.
