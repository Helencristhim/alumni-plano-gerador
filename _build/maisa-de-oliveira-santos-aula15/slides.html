Chapter 3: The Code

All Four Together

there is · past · up/down · will & going to

Grammar Discovery

One From Each Lesson

"There is a chart in the report."

"Last week, I finished the summary."

"Sales increased and profits are higher."

"I am going to check it and I will send it Friday."

StructureUse forExample
there is / there aresay what is in the reportThere is a chart. There are three columns.
past simple (-ed)say what you didLast week, I finished and called.
increased / highersay how numbers changedSales increased. Profits are higher.
will / going topromises and next stepsI will send it. I am going to check it.

A full report uses all four: there is to describe it, the past to say what you did, increased / higher for the numbers, and will / going to for your plan.

Common Mistake

Right vs Wrong

There is three columns.
There are three columns.
Sales increased up.
Sales increased.

Use there is for one thing and there are for two or more. And increase already means "go up", so never add "up" after it.

Chapter 4: The Monthly Report

Presenting to David

the report, the past, the numbers, the plan

Dialogue

The Whole Month

M
Good morning, David. Here is my report for last month. There is a new chart on the first page.
D
Thank you, Maísa. Tell me, what did you do last week?
M
Last week, I finished the summary and I called the client.
D
Great. And the numbers? What is the result?
M
Sales increased a lot. Profits are higher than last year.
D
Excellent work. So what is your plan for the final report?
M
I am going to check the total again. I promise to send it by Friday.
D
Perfect, Maísa. That is a great presentation.
