Big & Small

Look at the orange words

It's a green dinosaur.

It's a + word + dinosaur

"It's a" = É um. It's a big / small / green dinosaur!

Common Mistake

Say it the right way

It a big dinosaur.
It's a big dinosaur.

Sempre "It's a" -- É um! Say it 2 times.
