Aula 16 -- Pre-class

Closing a Meeting -- Action Items

Close a meeting well: summarize the main points, agree on the action items, and assign an owner to each task (I will be responsible for sending the email; Bruno will take care of the schedule). New vocabulary: summarize, wrap up, action item, recap, assign, owner, outcome, conclude.

Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Summarize -- to give a short version of the main points
"Let me summarize the main points." (Resumir)
Wrap up -- to finish or end a meeting
"Before we finish, let me wrap up." (Encerrar / finalizar)
Action item -- a clear task to do after the meeting
"The first action item is to send the proposal." (Tarefa / item de ação)
Recap -- a short review of the main points
"I will send a short recap with the deadlines." (Recapitulação / resumo)
Assign -- to give a task to a person
"Let us assign the action items now." (Atribuir / designar)
Owner -- the person responsible for a task
"Each action item has an owner." (Responsável)
Outcome -- the final result of a meeting
"The main outcome is a simple, clear plan." (Resultado / desfecho)
Conclude -- to bring something to an end
"Let us conclude here. Thank you for your time." (Concluir / encerrar)

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- Assigning Tasks

GRAMMAR

Usado para dar tarefas e fechar a reunião. / Used to assign tasks and close a meeting. Depois de "responsible for" use a forma -ing; "take care of" usa substantivo; "the next step is to" usa to + verbo.

Form / FormaUse / UsoExample / Exemplo
responsible for + -ingAssumir uma tarefa / take a taskI am responsible for sending the email.
take care of + nounCuidar de algo / handle somethingBruno will take care of the schedule.
the next step is to + verbDefinir o próximo passo / next stepThe next step is to send the proposal.
Key point / Ponto-chaveDepois de responsible for, use o verbo com -ing (sending), não "to send". Take care of precisa de "of" + substantivo.
