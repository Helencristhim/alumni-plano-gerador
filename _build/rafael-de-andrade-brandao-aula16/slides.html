Chapter 3 -- Assigning Tasks

Who Is Responsible?

Assign each action item to an owner

Discover the Pattern

Listen and Notice

"I will be responsible for sending the email."

"Carla is responsible for preparing the minutes."

"Bruno will take care of the schedule."

"The next step is to send the proposal."

Grammar Rule

Assign the Task

PatternFormExample
be responsible for+ verb-ingI am responsible for sending the email.
take care of+ nounBruno will take care of the schedule.
the next step is+ to + verbThe next step is to send the proposal.
to sum upopener + summaryTo sum up, we agreed on the budget.

Key idea: Use responsible for + -ing (responsible for sending), take care of + a noun (take care of the schedule), and the next step is to + verb (to send). Open your summary with To sum up.

Common Mistake

Watch Out

"I am responsible to send the email."
"I am responsible for sending the email."

After responsible for, use the -ing form. Use take care of + a noun (the schedule), and the next step is to + verb (to send).
