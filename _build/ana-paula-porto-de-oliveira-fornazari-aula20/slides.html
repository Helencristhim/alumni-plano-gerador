Chapter 4: The Fluency Toolkit

Four Moves That Keep You Talking

How to speak spontaneously, without a script

Discover the Toolkit

MovePhraseExample
ParaphraseWhat I mean is... / It is the thing you use to..."It is the thing you use to cook."
Buy a secondGive me a second. / Let me think."That is a good question -- give me a second."
Pass the turnWhat about you? / Tell me more."It was great. What about you?"
ElaborateFor example... / because..."I love it, for example, when it saves time."

None of these need perfect grammar. They just keep you talking -- and that is what fluency really is.

Toolkit Practice

Choose the Right Move

Say the full phrase out loud, then tap to check

"That is a good question -- ___give me a second." (buy time)
"It was a great weekend. What ___about you?" (pass the turn)
"I really like it, for ___example, when it saves me time." (elaborate)
"Anyway, as I was ___saying, the trip was amazing." (keep going after a slip)
Language Focus

What Is Each Phrase Doing?

Common Mistake

Freeze vs Keep Going

✗ FREEZE

[Silence] / switch to Portuguese

✓ KEEP GOING

"Sorry, I forgot the word -- what I mean is, the small kitchen tool."

✗ STOP

"I do not know. End."

✓ PASS THE TURN

"Hmm, I am not sure -- what about you?"

A forgotten word is not the end of a conversation -- it is just a small gap. Describe it, buy a second, or pass the turn, and you never have to stop.

Dialogue

A Real Milestone

A
Hi Greg. You will not believe it -- I spoke English on the phone for ten minutes today and I did not translate once.
G
That is fantastic, Ana Paula! So, how did it feel to just speak freely?
A
Honestly, almost effortless. I forgot one word, but I described it instead of stopping.
G
Perfect. That is exactly the trick. What was the word?
A
The thing you use to measure your temperature. What is it called again?
G
A thermometer! See -- I understood you perfectly without the exact word.
A
Right? A year ago I would have gone tongue-tied and given up.
G
And now you just keep going. What helped you the most, do you think?
A
A calm pace, and not being afraid of mistakes. I express the idea and keep the conversation moving.
G
That is real fluency. I am proud of you -- you got your whole story across beautifully.
