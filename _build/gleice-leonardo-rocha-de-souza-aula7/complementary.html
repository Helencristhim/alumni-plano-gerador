Lesson 7 -- Key Moments

Series
The Founder -- a film about turning points

A real story of the key moments that built a company. Listen for irregular past verbs: went, got, made, left, became, took, won, found. Notice how one big decision changed everything.

Watch with English subtitles. Write down 3 sentences in the simple past with irregular verbs about a turning point. Then record one sentence about a key moment in your own career.

Podcast
How I Built This -- a founder's turning point

People tell the key moments of their career and company: the jobs they got, the decisions they made, the places they went, and the prizes they won. Most of the story is in the irregular simple past, the grammar from today.

Choose one episode. Note 3 irregular past verbs the guest uses to describe a turning point. Compare them with Paulo's and Lucia's stories from class.

YouTube / BBC
BBC Learning English -- "Past simple: irregular verbs"

A short, clear lesson on the simple past of irregular verbs: go to went, get to got, make to made, take to took. It also shows how to make questions with did plus the base verb (Did you go?).

After watching, write five key moments of your career with irregular past verbs, then record a 60-second audio telling your three biggest turning points.
