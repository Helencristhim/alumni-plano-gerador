Aula 10 — Review: A Real Conversation

Series
Friends — any episode (real, everyday conversations)

A warm, easy show built almost entirely on conversation. Listen for how the friends respond, react, and follow up. You will hear opinions, comparisons and advice in natural, relaxed English.

Dica: escolha uma cena curta e anote uma frase em que alguém dá uma opinião e outra em que alguém faz uma pergunta de follow-up. / Pick a short scene and note one opinion and one follow-up question.

Podcast
The British Council — LearnEnglish Podcasts (conversations)

Short, clear conversations between two or more people, at a comfortable pace. Perfect to hear how speakers respond, clarify and keep a conversation going naturally.

Dica: ouça um episódio e anote três perguntas de follow-up que os falantes usaram. / Listen and note three follow-up questions the speakers used.
