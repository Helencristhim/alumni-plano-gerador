Stage 1.1: Vocabulary Cards

Vocabulary

Listen to each word and read the example. Tap Listen to hear it.

Menu -- the list of food and drinks
"The waiter gives me the menu." (cardápio)
Waiter -- the person who brings your food
"The waiter is very kind." (garçom)
Order -- to ask for food in a restaurant
"I order a salad and a coffee." (pedir)
Drink -- something you drink, like water
"I would like a cold drink." (bebida)
Dessert -- sweet food after the meal
"I would like a dessert." (sobremesa)
Bill -- the paper that shows what you pay
"Can I have the bill, please?" (a conta)
Hungry -- when you want to eat
"I am very hungry." (com fome)
Delicious -- very good to eat
"The food is delicious." (delicioso)

Stage 1.3: Grammar in Context

GRAMMAR

Read the text and answer the questions.

I am at a restaurant. I am very hungry. The waiter gives me the menu. I read it and I order my food. To be polite, I say: "I would like a salad, please." The waiter asks: "Would you like a drink?" I say: "Yes, I would like some water." At the end, I ask for the bill. The food is delicious.

1. How do you ask for food in a polite way?
A "Give me a salad."
B "I would like a salad, please."
C "I salad now."
2. How does the waiter offer a drink?
A "Would you like a drink?"
B "You drink now?"
C "How much drink?"
3. What word means "very good to eat"?
A Hungry.
B Delicious.
C Bill.

Stage 1.4: Grammar Tip -- would like

GRAMMAR

Use I WOULD LIKE para pedir algo de forma educada e WOULD YOU LIKE para oferecer. / Use I WOULD LIKE to ask politely and WOULD YOU LIKE to offer.

Use / UsoStructure / EstruturaExample / Exemplo
Pedir / AskI would like + a / someI would like a coffee.
Oferecer / OfferWould you like + a / someWould you like a dessert?
Dizer sim / Say yesYes, please.Yes, please.
Wrong / erradoGive me + foodGive me a coffee.
Key point / Ponto-chavePedir? Use I would like. Oferecer? Use Would you like. Sempre com please.
